1 范围 
本标准规定了高滑动要求之门盖系统的钢
制部件表面厚层磷化处理要求。 
本标准适用于评价高滑动要求之门盖系统
的钢制部件表面厚层磷化处理。 
1  Scope 
This standard specifies the heavy layer 
phosphating requirements for Closure’s steel 
part surface needed for heavy duty sliding.  
The standard is adopted to evaluate the 
heavy layer phosphating for Closure’s steel 
part surface needed for heavy duty sliding.

4.1.3  这种高要求滑动特性，是通过预先的厚
层锌磷化处理以及后续再涂敷含MoS2的润滑脂
来实现的。必要时，也可与本公司的相关部门协
商采用锰磷化处理法。 
4.1.3  Such heavy duty sliding properties are 
realized by the pretreated zinc heavy layer 
phosphating and the sequentially applied lu-
bricating grease that contains MoS2. If neces-
sary, consult our relevant departments to use 
the manganese phosphating method. 
4.1.4  在制定涂敷流程和控制流程时，必须确
保成品的应用特性不受到负面影响。通过专业化
安装以及适合的运输条件，需确保涂层不受到会
导致防护功能降低以及/或者无法满足规定要求
的损伤。 
4.1.4  Coating process and control process 
shall not impair the application performance of 
the finished parts. Make sure that the coating 
will not be subject to damages that may impair 
its protective properties and/or prevent it from 
meeting the specified requirements by profes-
sional installation and proper transportation 
conditions.

4.2.3.1  Type A
a) 
Zinc heavy layer phosphating (5 to 15 
µm); 
b) 
2 layers of lubricating grease con-
taining MoS2 (4 to 9 µm per layer; 
applied in the centrifuge), plastic lin-
ing (without deburring). 
4.2.3.2  方案B 
a) 厚层锌磷化处理（5至15 µm）； 
b) 2 层MoS2润滑脂（每层4 至9µm，在离
心机内涂敷）； 
4.2.3.2  Type B 
a) 
Zinc heavy layer phosphating (5 to 15 
µm); 
b) 2 layers of lubricating grease con-
taining MoS2 (4 to 9 µm per layer; 
applied in the centrifuge). 
4.3  腐蚀试验 
4.3  Corrosion Test 
4.3.1  执行DIN ISO 9227 SS 的盐雾喷射试验。
a) 经过96 h 的试验之后： 试样表面不得
出现可目测的变化，只允许在边角位置
出现个别的基材锈蚀现象； 
 
b) 
经过240 h 的试验之后： 试样表面不
得出现可目测的变化、起泡、基材锈蚀
或剥落等现象。 
4.3.1  Salt Spray Test according to German 
Standard DIN ISO 9227 SS. 
a) 
After testing for 96 hours: The 
specimen surface shall have no 
visually detectable changes. Only 
individual base material rusting at 
corners is permissible; 
b) 
After test for 240 hours: The speci-
men surface shall have no visually 
detectable changes, blisters, base 
material rusting or breakup, etc. 
4.3.2  执行DIN EN ISO 6270-2 的冷凝水恒定
气候试验。 
    经过240 h 的试验之后： 不得出现起
泡和基材锈蚀现象。 
4.3.2  Condensate Water Constant Climate 
Test according to German Standard DIN EN 
ISO 6270-2. 
After test for 240 hours: No blisters or 
base material rusting 
4.3.3  执行SMTC 5 200 010 的循环腐蚀试验。
经过15个试验周期之后： 试样表面不
得出现可目测的变化、起泡、基材锈蚀或剥
落等现象。 
4.3.3  Cyclic Corrosion Test according to 
SMTC 5 200 010. 
After 15 test cycles: The specimen surface 
shall have no visually detectable changes, 
blisters, base material rusting or breakup, etc.
