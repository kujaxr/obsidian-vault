Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 螺栓装配异常，导致缸体内螺纹形成双螺纹，进而引起拉脱、发动机漏液。 
   Abnormal assembly of the bolt results in the formation of double threads in the cylinder, which leads to pull off and 
engine leakage. 
建议Suggestions：  
1.  排查装配过程。 
   Check the assembly process.

形成的。
