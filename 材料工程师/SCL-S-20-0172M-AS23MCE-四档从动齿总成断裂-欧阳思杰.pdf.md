Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
(1) 4档结合齿的限位齿在试验过程中受到冲击载荷作用而过载失效。 
The limit teeth of the 4th gear combination teeth are all overload fracture, which may be due to the impact load during the 
test. 
(2) 失效件与通过试验件的结合齿表面硬度、芯部硬度和硬化层深度均合格，金相组织正常。The surface hardness, 
inner hardness and hardened layer depth of the bonding teeth between the failure part and the test piece are qualified, and 
the metallographic structure is normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 校核限位齿冲击载荷，并优化设计。Check the impact load of the stop gear and optimize design.
