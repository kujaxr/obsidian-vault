Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 高湿条件或喷洒消毒液后零件镀层脱落，伴随有擦拭行为。 
The coating of parts falls off under high humidity or after spraying disinfectant, accompanied by 
wiping behavior.  
建议Suggestions：1. 提醒客户避免用84 消毒液等溶液喷洒屏幕，表面残留水分及时擦拭。 
                   Remind customers to avoid spraying the screen with 84 disinfectant and other solutions, and wipe 
the surface residual moisture in time. 
2.供应商对镀层材料进行优化，改善脱落情况。 
                   The supplier optimizes the coating material to improve the peeling situation.

根据失效形貌，首先推断屏幕镀层脱落，查AS23 天宝屏幕表面处理工艺，为AG&AR&AF coating，
主要包括SiO2&TiO2等层。对比失效&正常位置能谱分析结果，如图2 所示。其中谱图44 为失效位置，
谱图45 为正常位置，对比可知，失效位置不含Si&Ti 元素，镀层已脱落，与推断一致。 
According to the failure morphology, first, we inferred that the screen coating had fallen off, and 
checked screen surface treatment process of Tianbao, which was AG&AR&AF coating, mainly including 
SiO2&TiO2 layers. Compare the energy spectrum analysis results of the failure and normal positions, as 
shown in Figure 2. The spectrum 44 was the failure position, and the spectrum 45 was the normal position. 
The comparison showed that the failure position did not contain Si&Ti elements, and the coating had 
fallen off, which was consistent with the inference.
