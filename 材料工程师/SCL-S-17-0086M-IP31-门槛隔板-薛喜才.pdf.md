Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  门槛隔板冲压时发生塑性变形而开裂。 
    DiaphragmⅡ for sill outer is cracking in drawing process because of seriously deformation.  
2.  隔板材料成分、组织、强度及板厚满足要求。 
    Chemical components、microstructure、strength and plate thickness meet the technical requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化冲压形成工艺如增大翻边圆弧角、改善模具表面质量；同时采取措施改善翻边折弯区域材料堆积的现象。
Optimise the drawing process such as increasing the arc angle、improving surface quality of the die. Take measure to    
reduce the material accumulation near bentch region.
