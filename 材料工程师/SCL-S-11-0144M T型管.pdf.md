Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料不符合图纸要求。 
2. 使用过程中，水管已经发生严重老化，外界突然的振动，极易导致该水管在根部区域（应力集中区）
发生断裂。 
Conclusions: After testing, the material of the T pipe can’t conform to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the T pipe, it can be seen that the T pipe already occurred aging 
during the use. So the T pipe easily be broken at the corner region (stress concentration region) under a severe 
shake.  
建议： 采用图纸要求材料牌号制造该T 型管。 
Suggestions：The material grade required in the drawing should be used in the production of the T pipe.
