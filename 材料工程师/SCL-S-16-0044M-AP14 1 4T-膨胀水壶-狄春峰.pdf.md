Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．失效件受外力，自熔接痕处开裂。2．批量零件存在明显熔接痕，注塑工艺不佳。 
3．零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. Foreign force made part fracture. 2. Injection process of part was bad.  
3.The material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 检查注塑工艺（模具），消除熔接痕。2．针对熔接痕进行风险评估。 
Suggestions: 1. The injection process should be check to solve weld line. 2.Risk assessment should be done.
