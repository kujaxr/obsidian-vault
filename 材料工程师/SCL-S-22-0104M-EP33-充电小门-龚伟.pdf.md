Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件装配扭矩与实际屈服扭矩不匹配，导致装配时压溃。 
The assembly torque didn’t match the actual yield torque, resulting in crushing during assembly. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the parts material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  进行接头试验，锁定合适的装配扭矩/转速。 
Conduct joint test and lock the appropriate assembly torque / speed.

TFJ-22-0287，见附件）
