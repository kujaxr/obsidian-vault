Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  三失效件依次为工艺异常、未明原因、锐物割伤导致失效。 
The three failure parts were caused by abnormal process, unknown cause and sharp object cut.

                    Suppliers check the abnormal welding process and rectify.
