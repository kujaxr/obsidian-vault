Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 异常的载荷导致支架受损发生断裂，并可能引起了1#和2#螺栓松动和损伤。悬臂断裂后，1#和2#螺栓
承受着较大的载荷，发生疲劳断裂。Abnormal load made the bracket was damaged and broken, and at the 
same time, maybe let no. 1 and no. 2 bolt loose and damaged. After the rupture of the bracket, no. 1 and no. 2 
bolt was broken by fatigue because of a big load. 
（2） 铝悬臂的成分，硬度和孔隙符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components, hardness and porosity of the 
bracket meet the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
（3） 螺栓的硬度符合要求；无裂纹和无脱碳；组织为回火索氏体，是正常的。The hardness meets the requirement; 
crack and decarbonization are free on the surface; the microstructure is tempered sorbite, which is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 调查该车辆是否出现过事故。该事故可能导致支架受损。Investigate if a accident happened, which 
maybe caused the engine bracket damaged.
