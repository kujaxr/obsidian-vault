Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属双向疲劳断裂，在疲劳源附近有应力集中现象。 
The failure for the part belong to biaxial fatigue, near the source of fatigue stress concentration  
phenomenon. 
2、失效件的材料强度偏下限，更接近DC05/06。 
The strength of the part failure meet the lower limit, closer DC05/06. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核零件的受力情况，优化设计，提高支架的承载力。 
Please check the forces and optimize the design for parts, improve the bearing capacity of the stent.. 
2、 采购正规 DC01 材料并进行检测，不使用偏软材料。 
DC01 material procurement and regular testing, not using soft materials. 
   3、如应力降低有限，提高材料强度等级，如ST37-2G。 
If stress is reduced is limited, improve material intensity level, such as ST37-2G.
