Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论：断口分析可得，暖风机出水管受到意外损伤，导致破裂泄漏。零件材料性能符合要求。 
Conclusions: Throuth the fracture analysis, we can get the conclusion that the hose was injured accidently by 
external damage which caused the leakage. The material perfomance of the hose conforms to the 
requirments. 
 
建议：注意胶管不受外界损伤。 
Suggestion: Avoid external damage of the hose.

2 断口分析（Analyze）： 
   
 
胶管外层裂纹可以看出，沿着裂纹有两条明显的异常刮痕，其中一条刮痕还有破损情况。胶管内层只有一条裂纹，裂纹附近没有任
何其他破损情况。表明胶管外层曾经受到异常损伤。 
There is two abnormal scratches along the cut on the out layer of the hose, and there is a rupture of one abnormal scratch. There 
is only one cut on the inner layer of the hose, which indicate that the hose had been injured accidently by external damage and 
caused the rupture. 
 
胶管编织层完整，无断裂。裂纹断面没有任何杂质、气泡等缺陷，排除材料问题引起的破裂。 
The fracture braid is not broken of the hose. There are no impurities, bubbles and other defects on the cut. We can get that the 
damage does not caused by the material.

从胶管裂纹切面可以看出，胶管外层的裂纹断面不垂直胶管，而是斜着切入胶管。裂纹从胶管外层生成，扩展到编织层，最终导致
内层破裂。因此内层裂纹垂直胶管。 
From the cut, we can see the cut of the outer layer is not perpendicular to the hose, but obliquely cut the hose. The cut is 
produced on the out layer, expanded to the fracture braid, and last lead to the rupture of the inner layer. So the cut of the inner 
layer is perpendicular to the hose.
