Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 
零件受交变载荷作用产生的多源疲劳导致排气管支架断裂。 The brackets have failed due to fatigue, 
beginning from multiple origins.   
2. 
断口表面疲劳裂纹的比例非常高，表明：相对于材料的拉伸强度，承受的载荷非常低。 The high proportion of 
the surface given to the fatigue cracks is very high, showing that the applied load is very low, compared to the tensile 
strength of the material. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 找到高应力产生的原因，并减少应力。Find the cause of the high load and reduce it. 
2. 改进支架的强度。Increase the strength of the bracket

支架#1  Bracket #1 
 
图3 显示断口的整体概况。由图可见：两个主裂纹源扩展相交于中心，最后过载断裂。  
部分断裂表面（图3 黄色线条所圈区域）被严重损坏，不便分析。 
断口的左半部分非常平坦，轻的扩展纹路显示：顶部中心区域是这条裂纹的起源区（源区1），然而，由于损坏
不能进行分析。 
右上角过渡处有明显的疲劳台阶，表明是多源疲劳（源区2）。然而，该疲劳裂纹没有贯穿整个板厚，沿着上表
面扩展。右侧的中部地区有剪切断裂特征，显示这是支架最后的断裂区。  
源区1 对面有裂纹扩展纹路，表明这是另外一个起源区，仅次于其它两个主裂纹源。  
在电镜下，断口有点儿模糊，因此没有对该案例作进一步的分析。 
Figure 3 shows an overview of the fracture surface.  This shows a clearly divided fracture surface, indicating two main 
cracks which joined together at the centre, which formed the final overload region. 
Parts of the fracture surface were heavily damaged, preventing analysis.  These areas are bound by yellow lines in 
figure 3. 
The left part of the fracture is very flat, and light beachmarks suggest an area at the top-centre, to be the origin of this 
crack (Origin area 1), however, due to damage this area cannot be analysed. 
The far right hand corner clearly shows ratchet marks which indicates multiple origins here (Origin area 2).  However, 
this fatigue crack has not spread deep into the material, but has travelled along the upper surface.  The central area of 
the right hand side has shear fracture characteristics showing that this was the last area to fail. 
An area opposite to Origin area 1 is also bound by a beachmark, suggesting a further origin here, although this is 
secondary to the other two. 
The fracture surface was indistinct under electron microscopy, so further analysis was not carried out on this sample.

支架#2  Bracket #2 
 
其主要特点与支架＃1 断口非常相似（见图4），左边是非常平坦的，右边是的浅平面断口，中心部位是剪切断裂
分离区。受损区域与支架＃1 相同，损坏的右侧区域延伸到右上角，擦掉任何可能已经存在疲劳纹理。  
由于该样品的断口更清晰，用电镜分析可以看出许多断裂的方向，从而更明确地确定起源地区。可确定四个领域，
如图4 所示；并采用SEM 对不同点进行了分析。 
The main features are very similar to Bracket #1 (see figure 4), with a very flat fracture to the left, a shallow flat fracture 
to the right and a separating area of shear fracture in the centre.  Damaged areas are also identical to bracket #1, 
although damage on the right hand side extended to the top corner, obliterating any ratchet marks which may have been 
present.  
Since this fracture was more clear, SEM analysis could show the direction of fracture in many areas, thus identifying the 
origin areas more definitely.  Four areas could be identified, and are shown in figure 4, together with reference to the 
SEM analysed positions. 
 
源区1，疲劳裂纹产生后，向下和向外左侧法兰圆角扩展（通过点A，B，C，D＆E 公司）。因为只提供了一半
的支架，因此不能对向右的扩展裂纹进行分析。  
源区1 对面的表面产生了一些小的疲劳裂纹，与裂纹源1 的裂纹相交，形成台阶（G 点）。值得注意的是，这两
个疲劳裂纹重叠（F 点条纹向下扩展）；由于反向弯曲载荷是如此之低，因此没有导致薄带剪切带产生。源区2
还包含一些小的沿晶断裂区，但是主要断裂机理为疲劳。 
At origin area 1, a fatigue crack was generated, which spread downward and outward to the left and around the flange 
radius (through points A,B,C,D & E).  Any crack exetension to the right cannot be checked, since only half of the 
bracket was returned.  
On the opposite surface to origin 1, a number of small fatigue cracks were generated, forming the stepped appearance of 
origin 2 (Point G).  It is noteworthy that these two fatigue cracks overlap (striations at point F are travelling 
downwards), showing the the reverse bending load was so low that it did not cause the shear fracture of the thin strip of 
material between them. Origin 2 also contains some small areas of intergranular fracture, although the primary 
mechanism is fatigue. 
 
疲劳断口的右边条纹（点I＆J）显示区域3 存在一个疲劳源。与支架＃1 相同，该裂缝平行表面扩展，但未穿透
整个板厚。  
虽然区域4 区（点j）损伤严重，但是可以判断是第四个疲劳源区。  
从疲劳裂纹的大小不同，我们可以得出结论，源区1 和2 是主要裂纹源；由于承载面积的减少，零件刚度的减少，
起源3＆4 受到双向弯曲而产生。 
Fatigue striations on the right hand side of the fracture (points I & J) indicate an origin at area 3.  As in bracket #1, this 
crack travelled primarily along the surface, rather than through the thickness of the material. 
A fourth origin area can be found at area 4 (point J) although this area was quite damaged.    
 
From the different sizes of the fatigue cracks, we can conclude that origin areas 1 & 2 were the primary origins, and 
origins 3 & 4 resulted from reverse bending driven by the loss of stiffness due to the reduced cross-sectional area.
