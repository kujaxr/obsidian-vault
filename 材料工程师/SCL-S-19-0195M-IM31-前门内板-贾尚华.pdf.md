Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  前门内板由于局部应力过大发生了疲劳开裂。 
    Fatigue cracking of front door inner panel due to excessive local stress. 
2.  前门内板的材料成分、硬度均满足要求。 
The material composition and hardness of the front door inner panel meet the requirements. 
. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 降低前门内板的局部应力，比如在安装点处增加垫片。 
Reduce the local stress of the front door inner panel, such as adding gaskets at the installation point
