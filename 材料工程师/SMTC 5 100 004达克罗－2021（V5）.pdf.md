1 范围Scope 
 
 
本标准规定了铁基材上由锌铝薄片（铝含量至
少75%）和面涂组成的保护层性能。 
本标准适用于防腐性能要求较高的零件，但不
适用于接地零件。 
 
This standard defines the surface protection 
requirements for zinc and aluminum flakes (min. 
75%) with an additional top coating. 
 
This standard adapts to the parts which has 
high corrosion resistance performance, but not 
suitable to the conductor parts.

3 基本要求General Requirement 
 
 
3.1  避免有害物质按SMTC 5 500 001。对于出口
零件有害物质按SMTC 5 500 002 满足REACH 法
规标准。完成完整的试验需要10 个零件。零件表
面不允许有可见的外观缺陷。加工及装配过程，不
允许损害零件的防腐性能。在基材的弹性形变下，
不应发生涂层的剥落或裂纹。 
 
3.1 
Avoidance 
of 
hazardous 
substances 
according to SMTC 5 500 001. For the harmful 
substances of exported parts, meet the REACH 
standards according to SMTC 5 500 002.Ten 
parts are needed for a complete test. No visible 
defects are permitted on the surface of the 
parts. In the assembling process, no damage 
which 
will 
lead 
to 
the 
decreasing 
the 
anticorrosion performance is allowed. There 
shall be no flaking or cracks of the coating under 
elastic deformation. 
 
3.2  除非在图纸上指出零件的某些部分不需要表
面保护，否则图纸上标注的类型适用于整个零件表
面。 
 
3.2 If one certain surface protection type is 
defined on the drawing ,that means the whole 
part should treated by this type, unless some 
areas is defined as an exception. 
 
3.3  对于紧固件，适用于端部、头部等非螺纹部分。
对于螺纹部分的考核适当降低要求。 
 
3.3 As to the fasten parts, only the head, the 
wrench bearing surface is evaluated according 
to one certain surface protection type which is 
defined on the drawing. The threaded areas 
usually relatively decrease the requirement. 
 
3.4  通过适当的预处理，这种表面保护类型几乎不
会引起致氢脆，因此这种涂层也可用于高硬度、高
强度的钢铁部件。 
 
3.4 Through suitable pretreatment, this kind 
surface protection type rarely will cause 
hydrogen-induced embrittlement. So this kind 
treatment also can be used on high strength 
steel parts or high surface hardness steel parts.  
 
3.5  该涂层不适合用于起导电作用的零部件（例
如：接地零件）；也不适合用于米制螺纹小于M6 的
螺纹部件；也不适合在温度大于120 ℃时对松动扭
矩有要求的螺纹部件。也不适合作为与镁金属接触
的零件的表面保护方式。 
 
3.5 The coating is not suitable for the parts 
which has electric conductor function (such as 
ground connections), for the threaded parts with 
metric ISO thread < M6, for the parts with a 
loosening torques requirement over 120℃ 
either. In addition, also not suitable as the 
surface treatment on the parts which will contact 
with magnesium components.

5  涂覆层性能Layer Performance 
 
 
5.1  涂覆层膜厚Layer thickness 
 
 
涂覆层最小厚度为8 μm，最大厚度为18 μm。
但因涂覆过程及零件结构的限制，有些位置不能达
到最小厚度，但如能满足防腐及性能要求，也是可
以接受的。 
 
Requirement: minimum coating thickness 8 μm; 
max. Coating thickness 18μm.As limited to the 
process and the structure of the parts, some 
areas may not be able to reach the min 
thickness, but if its corrosion resistance and 
function qualified, also can be permitted.  
5.2  附着力Adhesion 
在零件表面采用十字划格试验，胶带为TESA 
4657。要求：无大面积的脱落，轻微的表层附着于
胶带上是可以接受的。 
 
 
Andrew’s cross cut test, using the tape of TESA 
4657. Requirement: no large scale peeling off, 
but small particles adheres to the tape is 
permissible. 
 
5.3 冷凝水试验Moisture climate condensation test  
根据ISO 6270-2 CH 进行试验240 小时，
如果零件结构允许的话，需要在零件表面按照
DIN EN ISO 7253 对零件的涂覆层进行划线，
并按照ISO 4628-3 进行评价。要求：无起泡，
无金属基体腐蚀。划线处允许金属基体腐蚀，
但该处腐蚀不允许扩散。 
 
Test according to ISO 6270-2 CH for 240h, 
before the test, the part should be scribed 
according to DIN EN ISO 7253, and evaluating 
according to ISO 4628-3.Requiremet: no blister, 
no base metal corrosion, the corrosion on the 
scribing area is permitted, but rust creep is not 
permitted. 
 
5.4 耐腐蚀性能Corrosion resistance performance 
5.4.1 方法A  Method A 
 
 
根据ISO 9227 进行试验。对于Sp-TH07、
Sp-TH27，要求：720 小时盐雾试验后，无金
属基体腐蚀。对于Sp-TH05、Sp-TH25，要求：
480 小时盐雾试验后，无金属基体腐蚀。 
 
Testing according to ISO 9227 .As to Sp-TH07、
Sp-TH27,requirement: 720h, no base metal 
corrosion. As to Sp-TH05、Sp-TH25,requirement: 
480h, no base metal corrosion.
