Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件强度较差，受力后断裂。 
The brittle fracture of the part caused by external force because of low intensity.   
2. 零件材料不符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 责令供应商对材料问题做出整改。 
                    Let the supplier rectify the material problem. 
                 2. 建议增加机油尺侧向受力要求。 
                    Side force should be added.
