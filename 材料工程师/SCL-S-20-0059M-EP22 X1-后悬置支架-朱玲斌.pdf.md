Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 后悬置支架长时间工作后在应力较大位置发生疲劳断裂。The rear mount bracket fatigue fracture occurs in a 
high stress position after long working hours; 
（2） 失效支架材料成分合格，硬度、金相组织和孔隙正常。The chemical composition of failed bracket is qualified, 
the hardness、microstructure and porosity are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 评估台架安装及试验状态是否正常。To assess whether the installation and test conditions of the bench are 
normal; 
（2） 优化开裂区域结构以减少结构应力，提高支架的安全系数。Optimizing the structure of cracking area to reduce 
the structural stess.
