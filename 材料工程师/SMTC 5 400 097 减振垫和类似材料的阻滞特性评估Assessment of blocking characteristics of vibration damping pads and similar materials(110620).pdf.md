1 范围 
本标准规定了评估沥青减振垫和类似材料
与每一种在储存期间堆积的其它材料相粘结的
趋势。 
本标准适用于评估沥青减振垫和类似材料
与每一种在储存期间堆积的其它材料相粘结的
趋势。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the assessing the 
tendency of bituminous vibration damping 
pads and similar materials to adhere to each 
other when stacked during storage. 
This standard applies to assess the ten-
dency of bituminous vibration damping pads 
and similar materials to adhere to each other 
when stacked during storage.

4 程序 
4  Procedure  
4.1 在一个平金属板上堆4 张减振垫，将第二
块金属板放在顶上并在其上加相当于堆放600 
mm 高的重量的一个载荷。总载荷包括第二块平
板的重量。 
4.1  Stack the four pads on a flat metal panel, 
place a second metal plate on the top and ap-
ply a load to the top pad equivalent to the 
weight of a stack 600 mm high. The total load 
to include the weight of the second panel. 
4.2 将堆积组件放入烘箱，并且在规定的时间
里保持规定的温度。 
4.2  Place the stack assembly in the oven and 
maintain at the specified temperature for the 
specified time. 
4.3  在冷却至环境温度后，移去顶板和重物，
并且用剥离的方法分开减振垫。 
4.3  After cooling to ambient temperature, 
remove the top plate and weights and separate 
the pads by peeling. 
5 报告 
报告任何阻滞，如达到有害程度的粘结或溢
出.。 
5  Report 
Report any blocking, i.e. detrimental level 
of adhesion or flow.
