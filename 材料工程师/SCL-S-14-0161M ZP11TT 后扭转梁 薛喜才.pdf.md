Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 扭转梁是由于疲劳而开裂的，裂纹起源于扭转梁落料面近棱边，未见明显缺陷；该区域属于高应力区，容易萌生裂纹；Rear torsion 
beam was broken by fatigue. The crack source region was observed no defects and near the edge, which was the high stress region and 
easily cracking. 
2. 与以往相比，扭转梁落料面撕裂带凹凸不平、相对较差；Comparing with the former, the cutting surface of rear torsion beam was 
rugged and relatively poor. 
3. 内加强板与扭转梁局部焊缝熔深偏低；弹簧盘加强板与扭转梁焊缝熔深、焊缝厚度符合要求；The weld penetration between the inner 
strength plate and Rear torsion beam was low. The weld penetration and welded seam thickness between the spring tie strap and rear 
torsion beam met the requirement. 
4. 零件成分符合要求，组织及硬度正常；Chemical components met the requirement. Microstructure and hardness were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 粘贴应变片，校核开裂部位应力载荷；Check the active stress at cracking region. 
2. 控制扭转梁落料工艺，改善边部质量；Control the cutting process to improve the edge quality.  
3. 加强焊接工艺控制，避免熔深不足；Control the welding process to prevent incomplete penetration.
