Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 
环岸断裂是由于汽缸内不正常燃烧所致。由于未对发动机系统进行全面的分析，所以导致不正常燃烧的原因
还不明确。燃油品质也可以引起异常燃烧，但无法对其进行鉴定。The piston lands have fractured due to 
abnormal combustion in the cylinder.  The contributing factors cannot be discovered without comprehensive 
engine running analysis.  Fuel quality can also influence abnormal combustion, however it is not possible to state 
that this is the root cause.  
2. 
活塞的材料满足要求；在断裂区域没有发现缺陷。The piston material meets the requirements and there are no 
defects in the fractured area.   
 
建议Suggestions: 
1.  更换所有的活塞，检查发动机及其传感器是否正常。Replace all pistons in the engine and check engine and 
sensors are operating correctly. 
2.  用户应去好的加油站加压，以保证汽油的质量。The customer should use high quality fuel from well-known 
service station.

The received parts are is the assembly and connecting rod, showed by fig. 1; other parts of the failed engine

the length of fracture area is about 1/3 circumference; ② there is some accumulated carbon, which is normal; ③
other areas are normal,which shows the matching with cylinder is normal.

（2）断口Fracture Surface 
断口的整体形貌如图2 所示。两环岸的断口形貌相同，裂纹起源于活塞头部的一侧，向另一侧扩展，
最后瞬断。 
   The whole fracture surfaces are showed by fig. 2. The fracture characters of the two fracture surfaces are the 
same. The crack began at the front side of the piston, spread to the other side and then final ruptured. 
扫描电镜下，第一环槽和第二环槽间的环岸断口如3 所示。在裂纹起源的一侧，可以发现一些疲劳台阶，
表明该疲劳断裂是多源的；最后断裂区的断口有大量的损伤。 
The fracture surface of the ring bank between the 1st and 2nd groove is checked by SEM and the photo is fig. 
3. At the side of the crack source, the ratchet marks of fatigue can be found, which showed the break is because of 
multi-origin fatigue; the final rupture area was damaged seriously. 
第二环槽和第三环槽间的断口如图4 所示。在裂纹的起源区也可以看到一些疲劳的台阶；瞬断区断口
表现出脆性断口特征，这与该材料的特性相一致。 
Fig.4 shows the fracture surface of the ring bank between the 2nd and 3rd groove checked by SEM. It is also 
find there are ratchet marks at the crack source region; the final rupture region shows the brittle character, which is 
related with the material property. 
断口在高温下氧化，因此很难对断口作进一步的分析。检测中，未发现材料的缺陷。 
Because of the oxidation under high temperature, it is difficult to further check the fracture. During checking, 
no material defects are found.
