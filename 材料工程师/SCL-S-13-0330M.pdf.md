Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 转向机总成发生两处断裂，即扭杆和齿轮轴，断口1（即齿轮轴）先过载断裂，致使转向机总成工作异常，
导致断口2（扭杆）疲劳断裂。There are two cracks in steering gear, the torsion bar and the gear shaft fracture 1 
(ie, gear shaft) first overload fracture, resulting in abnormal steering gear assembly work, leading to fracture 2 
(torsion) fatigue fracture. 
2、 齿轮轴的齿轮表面硬度超差，这不是引起失效的原因，其他所测量分析零件的材料性能无明显异常。 
Gear shaft gear surface hardness tolerance, other parts of the measurement and analysis of material properties is 
no obvious abnormalities. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 调查失效件的具体使用工况。Check the specific use conditions 
2、 供应商加强过程质量控制。Suppliers to strengthen quality control process.
