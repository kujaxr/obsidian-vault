Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件实际扭矩与推荐扭矩不匹配，导致螺钉脱出/热铆孔开裂。 
The actual torque does not match the recommended torque, causing the screw to escape/hot rivet 
cracking.   
2. 螺栓孔定位面高于螺钉外接面，导致装配应力主要集中于定位孔上，扭矩过大时，螺栓脱出。 
The bolt hole positioning surface is higher than the bolt outer surface, so that the assembly stress 
is mainly concentrated on the positioning hole, and when the torque is too large, the bolt released. 
3. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
                 The performance of the parts material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  短期措施为按照较低的推荐扭矩安装。 
Short-term measure: Install at a lower recommended torque. 
2.  长期措施为通过将螺钉外接面抬高/增加垫片等形式将定位面与螺钉外接面高度差抹平。 
Long-term measure: Smooth the difference between the height of the positioning surface and the 
external surface of the screw by raising the external surface of the screw/increasing the spacer.

1） 失效背景 
临港现场近期发生多起EP21 尾灯安装螺钉脱出问题，平均每天1~2 例；同时，9 月4 日夜间，
发生逾100 例热铆孔开裂情况，委托实验室分析。
