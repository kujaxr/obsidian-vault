Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
 
后悬架螺旋弹簧通过整车腐蚀试验（10 年无故障）。The rear suspension springs passed the Whole Vehicle Corrosion 
test (10 years without failure). 
 
弹簧通过疲劳断裂了，这是由弹簧表面产生严重的点蚀所导致。在腐蚀严重的环境下，测试过程中由于
颠簸造成油漆容易剥落。The springs failed through fatigue, which was initiated from heavy corrosion pitting at the spring 
surface.  The heavy corrosive environment and rough road during testing caused the paint to flake off easily. 
 
油漆厚度不符合图纸要求。The paint thickness does not meet the drawing requirements. 
 
在PPAP 的测试时，防腐性能满足规范要求。During PPAP testing, the corrosion properties met the requirements of the 
specification. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
供应商，增加油漆的厚度。Supplier to improve paint thickness. 
 
要评估风险，检查工程试验后车的状况，确认油漆状况是否正常。 To evaluate risk, check condition of cars after 
Engineering testing, confirm paint condition is OK or not.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
两个弹簧都发生疲劳断裂, 裂纹源区是表面腐蚀很严重位置(见图2).  两面断口都锈 蚀, 表明验一完成弹
簧就快断裂了.  
因为在试验结束后检查的时候(10 年)，未发现弹簧有问题，失效被接受（图3）。 
 
In both cases, the fracture was fatigue, originating from an area of heavy surface corrosion (figure 2).  Both fractures were heavily 
corroded, indicating that they failed very soon after the test was completed.  
Since the springs were OK at the end-of-test inspection (10 years), the failure is accepted (figure 3).

左侧弹簧进行材料分析，结果如下表所示。  
 测量断口附近的油漆厚度, 不符合要求。（图4）  
 可见到深度达0.33 毫米的腐蚀坑，这些坑可以作为严重的应力集中点（图5）  
 钢的材料的性能是可以接受的。 
 
The tops of the springs suffered much higher corrosion than the bottom.  This is due to more movement at the top, and so the paint flakes off 
more easily, exposing the base metal to corrosion. 
The corrosion requirement for the spring does not include a functional test.  The first samples of the part were tested in 2010 for PPAP release, 
the surface protection results were acceptable (SCL-P-10-2062M2). 
 
A materials analysis was carried out on the left side spring, the results are shown in the table below.   
 
The paint thickness was measured in cross-section near to the fracture (figure 4). It does not meet the requirement.   
 
Corrosion pits up to 0.33mm were seen, these can act as severe stress-raisers (figure 5)  
 
The steel material properties were acceptable.
