1 
Scope 
This standard specifies the pattern for the standard designation of screws, bolts, nuts and washers. 
It supplements the contents of DIN 76-1, DIN 962, DIN 267-28, DIN 267-30, DIN 34803, 
DIN EN ISO 4753, DIN EN ISO 7085 and DIN EN ISO 10644. 
VW 60361 (Parts Reduction) shall be taken into consideration. 
2
