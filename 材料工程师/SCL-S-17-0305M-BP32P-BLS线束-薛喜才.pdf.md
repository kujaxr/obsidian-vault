Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 振动载荷较大引发线束发生疲劳失效。 
   Big vibrations result in the wire harness cracking by fatigue. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查周围附件和振动，评估振动载荷是否增加。 
   Check the surrounding part and vibration,evaluate whether the vibration increase. 
2. 排查固定点有双色标的原因。 
  Check the double colour label in fixed point.
