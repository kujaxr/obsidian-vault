Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 大灯&尾灯均为受客户异常使用后开裂。 
The headlamp and tail lamp were cracked after abnormal use by customers.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the parts material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 引导客户合理使用。 
                  Guide customers to use reasonably.
