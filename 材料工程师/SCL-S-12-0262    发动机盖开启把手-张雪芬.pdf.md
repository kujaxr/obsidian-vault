Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件受外力作用导致失效。 
         2. 失效件的材料符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: 1. External force made the part fail.               
2. The material performance of failure part conformed to the drawing requirements. 
建议：1.正常使用零件功能，加强对零件的保护。  
Suggestions: 1. Protection of parts should be improved.
