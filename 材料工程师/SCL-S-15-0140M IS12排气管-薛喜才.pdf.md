Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 排气管属于多源疲劳开裂，裂纹从外表面向内表面扩展。Exhaust catalyst pipe was broken off by fatigue and the 
crack propagated from the outer surface to inner surface. 
2. 排气管与法兰环焊缝中存在熔深不足、未熔合缺陷。Incomplete penetration and lack of fusion were observed 
between exhaust pipe and the flange. 
3. 法兰、排气管材料正常。The materials of the FRT flange and FRT pipe were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
 
1. 评估振动载荷。Evaluate vibrational load. 
2. 供应商加强生产过程监控，确保焊接质量。Supplier controls the process strictly and makes sure the welding quality. 
3. 关注该批车辆，查看失效部位是否存在裂纹。Pay attention to these batch cars and check the crack in failed region.
