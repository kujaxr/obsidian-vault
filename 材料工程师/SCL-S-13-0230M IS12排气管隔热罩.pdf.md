Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 隔热罩是由于多源疲劳而开裂的，开裂原因：①排气歧管(1#位置，见图2)表面较粗糙，造成与其匹配的隔热
罩承压面有压伤；②排气歧管螺纹孔口倒角过大，隔热罩在歧管上实际承压区非常窄，使隔热罩承压面压伤加剧，
裂纹萌生；Exhaust manifold heat shield was broken by fatigue. The mainly reason was: ① the surface of exhaust 
manifold was coarse, which caused the heat shield damaging; ② the chamfering of exhaust manifold is too large, 
resulting in the actual load becoming larger and the heat shield cracking. 
2. 隔热罩材质正常；The material of exhaust manifold heat shield was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 更改排气歧管(1#位置)螺纹口表面为加工面，并在图纸中标示加工余量与粗糙度；Change the threaded surface of 
exhaust manifold for machined surface and mark the machining allowance and roughness on the drawing. 
2. 供应商加强控制，使排气歧管螺纹孔口倒角符合图纸要求；Suppliers must control the threaded chamfering 
meeting the requirements;
