Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1、橡胶材料不符合质量要求。橡胶断裂，导致漏油。 
 
 2、扣压工艺存在问题。 
Conclusions: 1. The material performance of rubber did not conform to requirements. The fracture of rubber led to 
oil leak. 
 
     2. The process of squeeze was bad. 
建议： 1、改进橡胶生产工艺和扣压工艺。 
Suggestions：1. The process of rubber and squeeze should be improved.
