Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 右前门外板在油漆工艺之前发生严重的缩颈和微裂纹。PANEL-FRT S/D OTR happened necking and cracking 
before the oil paint.  
 
2. 零件成分符合要求，硬度及组织正常。Chemical components met the requirement. Hardness and microstructure 
were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查零件的成型工艺。Check the deformation process.
