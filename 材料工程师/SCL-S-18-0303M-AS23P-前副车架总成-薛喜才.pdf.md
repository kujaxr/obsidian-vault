Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  局部应力较高引发套管与上片焊缝焊趾疲劳开裂，并扩展至母材。 
The crack propagated by fatigue the welding seam and the plate because of big load. 
2.  焊缝轨迹不符合图纸要求，但熔深、焊高满足技术要求。 
Weld track don’t meet the requirement and fusion and welding thickness meet the requirement.  
3.  纵臂上片成分、板厚及金相组织正常。 
Chemical components、plate thickness and microstructure are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 重新评估零件的安全系数。 
Evaluate again the safety coefficient. 
2. 供应商应按照图纸要求进行焊接。 
   Supplier weld based on the drawing.
