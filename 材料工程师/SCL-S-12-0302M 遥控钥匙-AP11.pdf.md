Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 回扳钥匙时，未按下限位按钮导致失效。 
      2. 失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The spacing button was not push down when move back the key, which led to the failure. 
 
    2. The material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 引导客户正确使用。 
Suggestions：1. Guide customers use the key properly.
