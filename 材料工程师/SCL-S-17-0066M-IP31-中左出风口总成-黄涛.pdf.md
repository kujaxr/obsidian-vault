Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．零件由于异常磨损，与底座匹配形成较大间隙受异常操作力导致脱落。2.零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. Matching gap caused by the part inordinate wear and abnormal operating force led to the failure.  
2.The material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1.责令供应商解释异常磨损原因。 
Suggestions: 1. The reason of the part inordinate wear should be explained by supplier.

综上，零件由于异常磨损，与底座匹配形成较大间隙，受异常操作力，导致脱落。
