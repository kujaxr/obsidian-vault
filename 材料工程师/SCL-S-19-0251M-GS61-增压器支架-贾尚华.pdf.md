Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  增压器支架发生了疲劳开裂，裂纹起源于应力较高的部位。 
    Fatigue cracking occurred in the bracket of supercharger, which originated in the place with high stress. 
2.  增压器支架的材料的成分不满足SUH409L 的要求，疑似为1.4028（马氏体沉淀硬化不锈钢）；从硬度来看，
其强度与SUH409L 相当。 
The material composition of the supercharger support does not meet the requirements of SUH409L, which is 
suspected to be 1.4028 (martensitic precipitation hardening stainless steel); from the hardness point of view, its 
strength is equivalent to SUH409L. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化设计，降低局部应力。 
Optimize design and reduce local stress.

位置1 和位置2 的微观可见疲劳辉纹，扩展方向如箭头所示。由此可见疲劳起源于断口右上方（应力较高的区域），
