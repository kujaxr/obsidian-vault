Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该复原缓冲簧具有相互垂直的裂纹，均为疲劳断裂。 
The restoring buffer spring has mutually perpendicular cracks, and the cracks were all fatigue fractures. 
（2）材料硬度、金相组织正常，金相组织存在少量部分脱碳。 
The Hardness and metallographic structure were normal, and there was a small amount of decarburization in the 
metallographic structure. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）提供完整的失效件，以便进一步分析。 
Provide complete failed parts for further analysis.
