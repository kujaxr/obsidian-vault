Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 零件装配时受过大外力导致断裂。  
          2．零件注塑工艺不佳。 
Conclusions:  1.The brittle fracture of the part caused by excessive external force during the assembling.  
2. The injection molding process was poor.    
建议： 1.责令供应商对工艺问题进行整改。 
       2．检查装配环节，装配过程按照标准要求操作。 
Suggestions:  1. Let the supplier rectify the technology problems. 
            2. Check the assembly process. Follow the standard requirements of operation during the assembly 
process.
