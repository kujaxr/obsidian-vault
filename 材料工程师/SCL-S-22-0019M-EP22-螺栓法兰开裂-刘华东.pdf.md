Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、螺栓开裂是冷镦裂纹。The bolt crack is cold heading crack. 
2、失效螺栓硬度金相符合技术要求。The hardness and microstructure of failure bolt meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离该批次零件；Isolate this batch of parts. 
2、供应商加强冷镦工艺质量管控，防止不合格品流出。The supplier shall strengthen the quality control of cold heading 
process to prevent the outflow of nonconforming products.
