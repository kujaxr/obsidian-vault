Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1.    凸轮轴目标轮在维修过程中因超载断裂。The camshaft target wheel has fractured due to overload during maintenance.  
2. 因素: The contributing factors are: 
a）孔旁的壁厚太薄（0.8 毫米）。  Too thin wall thickness (0.8mm) beside the hole. 
b）不适当的金相组织使其不能承受拉伸载荷。  Inappropriate microstructure which cannot bear a tensile load. 
c）工具未完全插入孔中，增加了弯矩。Tool not inserted fully into hole, increased bending moment. 
3. 未发现材料缺陷。No material defects were found. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
目标轮的设计并非为维护功能。如果这是必需的，可以考虑增加壁厚, 减少该部位的激冷。The target wheel 
was not designed as a maintenance feature.  If this is required, increase the wall thickness and reduce the chill in this area.

Fracture analysis 
 
 
裂纹起源于顶部边缘，并通过材料的迅速扩展。大多数断口是脆性的过载，一个小的剪切唇在底部边缘，最
后的断裂点（图2c）。  The fracture originated at the top edge and progressed rapidly through the material.  The majority of the fracture is 
brittle overload, a small shear lip is present at the bottom edge, indicating the last point of fracture (figure 2c). 
 
 
钻孔内表面，有一来自于工具的清晰痕迹，工具插入孔大约一半的方式。鉴于外观的磨损建议工具与内孔可
以紧配合（图2b）。  Inside the drilled hole, a clear trace from a tool was seen, the tool was inserted approximately half way into the hole.  
The appearance of the wear suggests the tool was a tight fit inside the hole (figure 2b).   
 
 
断口面上无发现缺陷，如孔隙或夹杂。 No defects such as porosity or inclusions were found on the fracture surface.

Dimension Analysis 
 
这法兰最初被设计为一个目标轮的VCT 系统（尺寸见图3a）。宽度仅8mm ± 0.2，钻孔是6.05 -6.10mm，留
下单边壁厚最大1 毫米。在这种情况下，孔稍微偏离中心一侧的管壁厚度仅为0.8mm。这太薄了，考虑到它的使
用，转配气门机构对其有拉伸负荷。图纸也有相应的要求，但这不可能考虑到薄壁。孔的位置稍微远离要求，但
这个对故障的影响不大（图3b）。  
This flange was originally designed as a target wheel for the VCT system (drawing dimensions see figure 3a).  The width is only 8mm ±0.2, 
where the drilled hole is 6.05-6.10mm, leaving maximum 1mm either side.  In this case the hole is slightly off-centre leaving only 0.8mm wall 
thickness on one side. This is too thin, considering that it’s use to turn the valvetrain requires it to carry a tensile load.   
The drawing also specifies a lead-in, but this would be impossible considering the thin section.  The location of the hole is away from 
specification, but this has had little influence on the failure (figure 3b). 
 
钻孔测量为6.03mm, 不符合要求。但测量断裂的零部件有误差，所以这也不能说明开始时尺寸就不足。 
The hole was measured at 6.03mm which is undersize, however there are errors in measuring a fractured component, so it cannot be said that 
this was undersize from beginning.

Materials Analysis 
 
目标轮缘的金相组织有非常高的碳化物的比例。这是由于需要迅速冷却，以实现凸轮附近的激冷深度，然而这形
成了一个非常脆且缺乏韧性的结构。由于钻孔旁边的壁非常薄，其功能要求是受拉伸负荷，这是不适宜的微观结
构。 
The microstructure of the target wheel flange has a very high proportion of carbides (figure 4).  This is due to the rapid cooling required to 
achieve chill depth on the nearby cam tips. However, it forms a structure which is extremely brittle and lacks toughness.  Given the very thin 
walls beside the drilled hole, and it’s function to carry a tensile load, it is an unsuitable microstructure.
