Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该六销台车支撑方管发生过载断裂，断裂面无明显缺陷。（基于所收到的部分断口） 
The six pin trolley support square tube occurred overload fracture, no defect was found on the fracture 
surface.（Based on the received part fracture surface） 
（2）材料化学成分符合要求，金相、硬度正常。 
Material chemical composition met the requirements, metallographic structure and hardness was normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）排查使用过程，查找过载原因。To check usage process to find the reason of overload.
