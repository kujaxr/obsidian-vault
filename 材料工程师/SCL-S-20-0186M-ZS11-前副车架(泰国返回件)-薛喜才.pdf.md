Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions   
结论Conclusions：  
1. 1#前副车架是由于经常处于涉水、泥沙路况，导致零件焊缝或缝隙区域先发生锈蚀、油漆剥离，最终锈穿。 
1# front subframe was often in the condition of wading in water and sand road conditions, resulting in the welding seam or gap area of 
the parts first rusting and the paint peeling off. It was corroded holes finally. 
2. 2#前副车架锈蚀区域与1#副车架相同，均从焊缝、缝隙区域先锈蚀，进一步发展将与1#件相同，其腐蚀原因也与1#件相同。
The rusting area of 2#front subframe is same with 1# parts and the corrosion occurs from the weld and gap area firstly. The further  
rusting conditions will be the same as that of 1# parts and the corrosion reason is the same as 1# parts. 
3. 两副车架电泳漆层厚度及附着力正常。The thickness and coating adhesion of two parts are normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 追踪车辆的使用信息，并收集其他品牌同型车辆腐蚀情况。Track the usage situation of vehicle and collect the corrosion situation of 
other similar vehicles of other brands. 
2. 为了提高耐腐蚀性，由薄层电泳更改为厚层电泳；未来车型建议将下层钢板更改为镀锌钢板。In order to improve the corrosion 
resistance, change the thin electrophoresis layer to the thick electrophoresis layer and the lower layer steel plate was replaced to galvanized 
steel plate in future models.

小结： 
1#前副车架是由内而外、从零件焊缝、缝隙这些耐腐性能较差区域最先腐蚀。零件电泳漆层厚度以及附
着力正常。 
通过对不同区域腐蚀物进行能谱分析，结果见图6 所示。腐蚀产物以铁、氧、碳元素为主，还含有钙、
硅、铝、镁等元素，而腐蚀性元素硫、氯的含量很低，不足以导致如此严重的腐蚀。对腐蚀物进行酸碱度测
试，结果见图6 所示。腐蚀物PH 值约7-8，未见明显的强酸或强碱特性，属于正常现象。因此，零件是由于
接触到空气、水等介质引起的锈蚀；通过零件外观可知，内腔存在很多类似泥沙类物质，这些物质可能含有
钙、硅、铝、镁等元素，这些物质的存在会加速零件的锈蚀。 
此外，从零件腐蚀位置来看，副车架左、右侧距离地面近，锈蚀情况比中间区域严重；零件内腔比外侧
锈蚀严重(外侧存在额外的防护层)。因此，综合以上分析，推测前副车架是由于经常涉水、泥沙路况，导致

The corrosion of the front subframe is produced from the inner side, especially the welding seams and gap 
firstly which have poor corrosion resistance. The coating thickness and adhesion are normal. 
The energy spectrum analysis of corrosion products in different areas are shown in figure.6. The corrosion 
products are mainly composed of iron, oxygen and carbon and also contain calcium, silicon, aluminum, magnesium 
and other elements. The content of sulfur and chlorine in corrosive elements is very low, which is not enough to 
cause such serious corrosion. The results of acid-base test of corrosives are shown in figure.7. The pH value of 
corrosion products is about 7-8, which should be a normal phenomenon. Therefore, the corrosion of parts is due to 
contact with air, liquid, etc. According to the appearance of the parts, there is much sediment in the inner cavity, 
which may contain calcium, silicon, aluminum, magnesium and other elements. The presence of these substances 
will accelerate the corrosion of parts. 
In addition, from the corrosion location of the parts, the left and right sides of the subframe are close to the 
ground and the corrosion is more serious than the middle area. The corrosion of the inner cavity is more serious than 
that of the outer side (additional protective layer on the outside). Therefore, based on the above analysis, the front 
subframe is due to long-term wading or ponding, which leads to corrosion in the welding seam, gap and other weak 
protection areas of parts and then extends to the bottom and side. Some serious areas are rusted to form the holes.
