Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 失效件密封圈材料为EPDM，不符合图纸材料要求（AEM）。The material of the failure O-ring part is EPDM, 
does not conform to the material requirement of the drawing (AEM). 
2. 失效件密封圈接触变速箱液体后异常溶胀导致破裂。O-ring damage is due to abnormal swell of the O-ring 
after contact with TR fluid. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、确保密封圈材料符合要求。Ensure the O-ring material meets the drawing requirement.
