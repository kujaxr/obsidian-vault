Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该张紧器在高应区发生疲劳断裂，起源区机加工尖角会形成应力集中，降低其疲劳寿命。 
The tensioner experienced fatigue fracture in the high stress zone, the machining of sharp corners in the origin area would 
form stress concentration, reduced its fatigue life. 
（2）材料化学成分主要元素符合要求，Mn含量超标；金相组织和硬度正常。 
The main elements in the chemical composition of the material met the requirements, and the Mn content exceeded the 
standard; Normal metallographic structure and hardness were normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化加工工艺，保证平滑过渡，避免尖角。 
Optimize processing technology to ensure smooth transitions and avoid sharp corners.
