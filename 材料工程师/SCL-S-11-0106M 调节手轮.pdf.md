Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，失效件的材料无异常。 
2. 旋钮装配不到位，使旋钮齿条所能承受的载荷降低，同时齿条高度较低，容易导致齿条拟合时滑脱。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the adjusting knob, it can be seen that the allowable load of 
racks was decreased because the adjusting knob was not installed in place. At the same time, the height of 
racks is low which easily lead the racks to skid. 
建议：保证旋钮安装到位；增加旋钮齿条高度；采用强度和耐温性较好材料制造旋钮。 
Suggestions：The adjusting knob should be installed in place. The height of racks should be increased. A good 
strength and temperature resistance material should be used in the production of adjusting knob.

小结：经测试，失效件和新零件的材料为ABS，材料符合设计要求。新零件的外径偏大，但对匹配和性能影
响不大，可以认为零件尺寸合格。 
Conclusion: The material of the failure part and new part is ABS which conforms to the design requirement. The 
external diameter of new part is little bigger than the requirement, but it don’t have too much influence on the 
matching and mechanical property. So the dimensions of the failure part and new part conform to the design 
requirement.  
Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图4 和图5 的内壁对比可以看出，失效件的内壁齿牙已经被磨平，未出现齿根断裂现象。在图6 的外壁
可以看到有银纹存在。图7 是新件和失效件装配尺寸对比，正常状态装配尺寸为15.4mm，而失效件装配尺寸为
12.4mm，明显低于正常装配尺寸。 
From the comparison of Fig 4 and Fig 5, it can be seen that the racks on inside surface of the failure part have been 
worn down. No fracture occurred at the bottom of racks. A crazy is found on the outside surface of failure part as shown 
in Fig 6. Fig 7 is the match dimension of the new part and failure part, the normal match dimension of new part is about 
15.4mm and the match dimension of failure part is about 12.4mm which is smaller than that of normal requirement.
