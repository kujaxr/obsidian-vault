Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承的失效是由于内圈和钢球的疲劳失效造成的；疲劳剥离的深度和外观显示高应力是导致其失效的原因。
The bearing has failed due to fatigue of the inner raceway and balls. The depth and appearance of the fatigue spalls indicates increased load is the reason. 
（2）钢球的运转轨迹显著地偏向轴承内外圈的一边，表明轴承承受高的轴向载荷；内圈的疲劳剥落也仅仅出现在
这些受载的区域。Running traces are strongly biased to one side of the bearing races, indicating high axial load.  In the inner race, fatigue pitting was 
only found in this area.   
（3）钢球的夹杂物含量未见异常。The inclusion contents are normal. 
（4）润滑不良的迹象（见以前的分析报告：SCL-S-11-0193M）没有发现。 Indications for poor lubrication (seen in previous failure 
SCL-S-11-0193M) were not found. 
（5）硬度和化学成分满足图纸的要求；但硬度处于要求范围的下限。The hardness and chemical composition meet the 
requirements, although hardness was at the low end of the specified range. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）依据设计要求，提高轴承的承载能力。Improve the load-carrying capacity of the bearing, according to the design. 
（2）SQE 获得原料样本进行非金属夹杂物的完整评估。SQE to obtain samples of raw material for complete assessment of non-metallic 
inclusions.
