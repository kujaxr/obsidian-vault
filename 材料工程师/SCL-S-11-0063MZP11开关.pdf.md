Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：失效件的材料为PA66，材料的注塑工艺较好。通过控制杆的工作状态和断口分析可以判定，控制杆的
断裂模式是，在控制杆行程端点，控制杆上端受过大外力，导致控制杆在横截面最小处发生断裂。 
Conclusions: The material of failure part is PA66 and the injection process of the part is good. According to the 
macroscopic observation and the switch structure, it can be seen that the switch lever is broken at the minimum 
cross section by the excessive force while the switch lever movement is limited. 
建议：避免用力扳动开关控制杆；增加开关控制杆的保护措施。 
Suggestions：The excessive force on the switch lever should be forbidden. The protective measures for the switch 
lever should be improved.

小结：经测试，失效件的材料为PA66。失效件断裂处的第一次熔融过程和第二次熔融过程中的熔融焓变化很
小，说明失效件断裂处的注塑工艺参数较好. 
Conclusion: After the tests, we can see that the material of failure part is PA66. The change of ΔH of the failure part 
between the first circle and the second circle is little, so the injection process of the part is good. 
Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图2 的开关构造可以看出，工作时，控制杆沿着旋转轴左右转动，从而触发两侧电源，控制杆行程会被
左右两侧电源开关限制。通过图3 的裂纹倾角可以看出，裂纹面在侧向方向存在近30 度的倾角，根据控制杆的
工作状态，可以判定控制杆断裂方向如图中箭头所示；同时可以看出，断裂发生在控制杆横截面最小处。 
From the switch structure of Fig 2, it can be seen that the left and right power can be turned on according to the left 
and right movement of the switch lever. And the movement range of the switch lever can be limited by the left and right 
power. In Fig 3, an about 30 degree angle of the crack surface is appeared. According to the switch structure and 
movement, it can be seen that the macro-fracture direction is shown by the arrow in Fig 3. At the same time, the crack 
occurs at the minimum cross section of the switch lever.
