Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
小结:  
三个失效密封套材料为NBR，不符合图纸CR 的材料要求。 
三个密封套表面有大量龟裂裂纹和大片异常挤压压痕，表明零件受到异常挤压。 
建议按照图纸要求进行整改。 
 
Conclusion: 
The material of the failed part is NBR which is not meet the requirement of the drawing which is CR. 
There is a large number of cracking and unusual stress indentation on the surface of the three grommet, which 
means the parts have been severely squeezed on the part. 
It is suggest that the supplier should supply the parts following the drawing.
