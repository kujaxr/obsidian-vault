Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 带轮卡簧疲劳断裂失效，疲劳起源于工作应力较大区域。The pulley spring fatigue fractured , which is 
originates from the area of large working stress. 
（2） 失效卡簧和未失效卡簧的脱碳层和硬度合格，金相组织正常。The decarbonization layer and hardness of 
failure spring and unfailed spring are qualified, the microstructure are normal.   
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 评估试验规范的合理性及结构设计的可靠性。Evaluation the rationality of the test specification and the 
reliability of structural design.
