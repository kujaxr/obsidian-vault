Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  法兰轴受到较大扭矩发生剪切过载断裂。 
    The flange is subjected to shear overload fracture due to greater torque. 
 
2.  法兰轴的化学成分、表面硬度、芯部硬度和硬化层深度满足要求，金相组织正常。 
   The chemical compositions，surface hardness ，core hardness and the hardened layer of the flange shaft are meet the 
requirments, the microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查异常受力因素。 
Investigate the cause of abnormal stress.
