Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论：1、稳定杆衬套在实际使用过程中受侧向力挤压，前稳定杆衬套较后稳定杆衬套受损严重，导致不能满 
足实际工况的耐久。 
      2、稳定杆衬套压缩永久变形性能有波动，1#衬套为50.9%，4#衬套为63%左右。 
建议：1、检查稳定杆衬套安装使用工况，改善前后稳定杆衬套的实际工况的受力负荷。 
      2、改善衬套橡胶材料的压缩永久变形性能，提高其耐久使用寿命。 
Conclusion: 1. The stabilizer bar bushings were extruded by the lateral force in actual use, the front stabilizer bar bushings 
were damaged more severely than the rear stabilizer bar bushings, so they can not meet the endurance test 
in actual working conditions. 
          2. The compression set of stabilizer bar bushings are fluctuant, the compression set of No.1 bushing is 
50.9%,but the compression set of No.4 bushing is about 63% . 
Suggestions: 1. Check the stabilizer bar bushing installation work conditions, and to improve the front and rear stabilizer 
bar bushings actual working conditions.  
            2.To improve the compression permanent deformation properties of the rubber material, improve their 
durability life.
