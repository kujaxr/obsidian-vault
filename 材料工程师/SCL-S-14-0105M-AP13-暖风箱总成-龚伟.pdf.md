Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致断裂。  
2. 失效件材料符合技术要求，注塑工艺不佳。  
Conclusions:  1. The brittle fracture of the parts caused by external force. 
2. Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements, with poor injection molding 
process.  
建议： 1. 责令供应商就工艺问题给出解释并整改。 
Suggestions:  1. Let supplier explain to technology problem and rectify.
