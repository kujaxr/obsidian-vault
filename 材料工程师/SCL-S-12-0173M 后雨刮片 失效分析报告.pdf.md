Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 超载（滥用）使角度超过正常的位置，进而是不合理的维修造成弹簧损坏和变形。The spring damage and 
deformation seen was caused by overloading (misuse) by exceeding the normal position, followed by remedial 
repair. 
2. 弹簧材料是可接受。The spring material was acceptable. 
3. 塑料件磨损明显，导致过度间隙，不确定是否是由于滥用直接导致。Wear of the plastic parts was noticable, 
causing excessive play in the mechanism, it is not known whether this is directly resulting from the misuse. 
  
建议Suggestions： 
1. 教育客户，以防止误操作。Educate customers to prevent misuse.  
2. 检查耐久性试验车辆的磨损情况。Check wear condition of durability test vehicles

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
雨刮臂提供的图1 所示。还提供了一个新的零部件进行比较。失效的机制是弹簧变形，如图1a 所示。 The 
wiper arm was supplied as shown in figure 1.  A new part was also supplied for comparison.  The failure 
mechanism was deformation of the spring as shown in figure 1a. 
 
 
图1  a)失效总成  b)失效和新的弹簧：表示变形情况 
Figure 1  a) Failed assembly  b) Failed and New springs, showing deformation 
 
 
弹簧 Spring 
弹簧在每一端内部半径遭受磨损。在变形的结尾也可看到损害，是稍微腐蚀（图2），这种损伤符合使用手动工
具造成的损伤，如钳子，螺丝刀等。弹簧无开裂或其他损害。The spring suffered wear on the internal radii at each end.  
Some damage at the deformed end was also seen, which is slightly corroded (figure 2).  This is consistent with hand 
tool such as pliers, screwdriver etc.  There was no cracking or other damage to the spring.

间隙是由塑料部件有磨损。枢轴和孔显示了轻微的磨损，但外凸出物 磨损显著（图7），宽度比较新件减少了0.2
毫米。This phenomenon is caused by wear of the plastic components.  The pivot pin and bore shows some light wear, 
but the outer projections’ wear is significant (figure 7).  The width has been reduced by 0.2mm compared to the new 
part.
