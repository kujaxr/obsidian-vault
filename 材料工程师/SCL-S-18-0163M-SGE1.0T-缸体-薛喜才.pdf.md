Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  局部应力高、且表面“多肉”导致缸体发生疲劳开裂。 
    Cylinder was cracking by fatigue because of the superfluous material and high stress.  
2.  材料成分满足要求，硬度、组织正常。 
Chemical components meet the requirement. Hardness、microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  关注零件高应力区域的表面质量。 
    Pay attention to the surface quality on high stress region.
