Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions:  
1. 支架由于超载过载失效，装配过程或尺寸不正常。 The bracket has failed through overload, the assembly process 
or dimensions are not normal. 
2. 材料无明显的缺陷，材料的金相组织和硬度是典型的。No obvious defects were found, and the material 
microstructure and hardness are typical. 
 
建议Suggestions:  
1. 检查支架和安装位置的尺寸。Check the dimensions of the bracket and mounting locations 
2. 确保检查装配过程规范。Confirm the assembly procedure is correct

断口分析Fracture Analysis 
 
断裂的外表面进行了检查，没有发现损伤或表面缺陷（图3）。使用光学和电子显微镜检查断裂表面，图4 显示，
断口呈现过载断裂的特称，裂纹起源于臂的下边缘。图5 断裂源附近的SEM 图像显示为穿晶断裂，没有明显的
缺陷。 
The outer surface of the fracture was examined and no damage or surface defects were found (figure 3).  The fracture 
was opened and the surfaces examined using light and electron microscope.  Figure 4 shows the fracture surface, which 
is characteristic of overload fracture, with origin at the lower edge of the arm.  Figure 5 shows SEM images near the 
origin, showing cleavage fracture with no obvious defects.

材料分析Material Analysis 
 
由于时间的限制，没有对零件做材料所有方面的分析。 此外，因为没有图纸，不知道材料方面的所有要求。合
金材料为AlSi9Cu3，但供应商提供的化学成分与EN1706 有点区别（图9）。 所以EN1706 的硬度值是唯一的一
个参考值。样品的孔隙率符合要求，不是导致失效的一个因素。 
Not all material analysis was carried out due to time restraints.  Also, since no drawing was submitted, not all material 
requirements are known.  The alloy is given as AlSi9Cu3, however the chemical composition provided by the supplier 
is slightly different to that given in EN1706 (figure 9), so the hardness value from EN1706 is only a guideline.  The 
general amount of porosity in the sample was small, and was not a contributing factor to the failure.
