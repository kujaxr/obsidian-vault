Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）机油泵严重磨损是由于零件长时间在不良润滑条件下工作所致。Severe wear of the pump is because of working 
for a long time in the bad lubrication condition.  
（2）磨损导致的不良匹配以及外来杂质可能是引起油压波动的原因，但不能排除其它零件引起波动的可能性。
The bad matching caused by serious wear and the impurity is likely the reason for oil fluctuation,but it can’t be eliminated that 
other parts was no abnormal, which cause the fluctuation . 
（3）泵体硬度合格；组织和疏松正常；Cu 含量偏低，但不是导致失效的原因。The hardness of the pump body meets 
the requirement; the microstructure and porosity are normal; the Cu content is on the low side,but this is not the failure cause. 
（4）阀柱塞的直径偏低；成分，表面硬度和渗层深度合格；组织正常。。The dia. of the plunger is on the low side; 
the chemical compositions, surface hardness and hardening depth meet the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）评估首次换油里程数是否合理。Evaluate if the mileage for the first oil change is reasonable. 
（2）检测油泵的其它零件，如弹簧。Check other components of the assy,such as spring. 
（3）控制泵体的成分，确保其符合要求。Control the chemical compositions of the pump body and ensure it is ok. 
（4）关注阀柱塞的直径，确保符合要求。Pay attention to the dia. of the plunger and ensure it meets the requirement.

  正常情况下，阀柱塞与机油泵壳体间出现磨损是不可避免的。影响磨损的因素主要有如下几种： 
      In general, the wear between the plunger and oil pump body is inevitable. There are several main factors 
that affect the wear: 
 
润滑环境  
不同的介质，其润滑效果不同；如果机油中含有杂质，它会成为磨料，夹在两个工作面之间，直接磨
损工作面。杂质在磨损的过程中还会破坏润滑油膜的形成，导致工作面与工作面直接接触，发生磨损。这
些杂质来自于整个系统；新的系统含有的杂质比较多，更换机油后，系统夹杂的含量会有所减少。随着系
统的运转，磨损，系统中会产生新的杂质。容易理解：第一次换油的里程非常重要；过长的里程，对零件
的磨损不利。 
 
lubrication environment  
Different media has different lubrication. If the oil contain impurities, the impurities will become abrasive 
sandwiched between two working face and make wear deteriorate.  At the same time, it will destroy the oil film 
and let the two working surface directly touch and wear each other. The impurities are from the engine system. The 
new system often contains more impurities. After changing oil, the content of the impurities will be reduced. With 
the system going on and wearing, the system will generate new impurities. It is easy to understand that the mileage 
of the first oil change is very important; too long mileage is harmful to the wear of the parts . 
 
载荷   
随着载荷的增加，磨损一般也会增加。失效件严重磨损的区域是载荷较大的区域。 
 
Load   
With the increase of the load, the wear degree will increase. The badly worn area appears at the area with big 
load. 
 
粗糙度和尺寸  
差的表面质量会加速磨损的发生；如果配合尺寸出现问题，可能导致磨损加剧。后续检测结果显示：
关键尺寸和粗糙度正常。

综合分析 Comprehensive Analysis 
 
磨损的表面检测显示磨损有磨料磨损的特征，与润滑油中杂质有关。调查发现：国内和英国在保
养方面有明显的不同。国内换油规定：首次换油的里程数为3000Km；后续每10000km 换油一次；
而英国换油规定：首次换油里程数为24000Km。新的系统内部清洁度相对较差，如果经过很长的
历程换油，会导致系统长时间处于差的润滑条件下。这会加速相关零件的磨损。 
The survey found that Chinese and Britainic maintenance is significantly different. In Chinese test, the mileage 
of the first oil change is 3000km; after it, the oil change is made for every 10000km; but in Britain, first oil 
change mileage is 24,000 km. Generally, new system cleanliness is not very good. A very long mileage for the 
first oil change will cause the system in poor lubrication conditions for a long time. This will accelerate the wear 
of the parts. 
 
上述检测可以发现：材料以及尺寸未见异常，不会引起过度磨损。 
Above tests show: no abnormality is found in the material and dimension, which can’t make the serious 
wear. 
 
该零件在国内的开发中以及售后市场未出现问题。 
The part has not failed in domestic development and the aftermarket  
    基于上述分析，可以判断：泵体的严重磨损是由于零件长时间在不良润滑条件下工作所致。 
Base on the above analysis, it can be judged: severe wear of the pump is because the part worked for a long time in 
the bad lubrication condition. 
   过度磨损导致的不良匹配，以及外来杂质会均会导致柱塞运动受阻，从而产生油压波动。但由于未收到其它
的零件，因此不能排除是否由于其它零件的损坏导致油压的波动，例如弹簧。 
   Both the bad matching caused by serious wear and impurity can blocked the movement of the plunger, which likely 
makes the oil pressure fluctuate.Because other components was not offered, it is not clear if other components was 
normal and cuased the oil pressure flunctuate,such as spring.
