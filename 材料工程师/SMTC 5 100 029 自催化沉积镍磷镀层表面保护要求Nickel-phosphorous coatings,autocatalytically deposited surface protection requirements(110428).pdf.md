3.1 一般要求 
3.1  General requirements 
3.1.1  根据SMTC 90008，避免有害物质。 
3.1.1 
Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 90008. 
3.1.2  进行一次完整的检测需要10 个成品件。
3.1.2
A total of 10 finished parts are required for 
comp lete testing. 
3.1.3  除非图纸上标注零件的某个部分，不需表
面镀层外，零件的整个表面（接触点除外）都必
须符合要求的表面保护类型。 
3.1.3 Unless certain sections of a part that are 
marked in the drawing are excluded from the sur-
face coating, the entire surface of the parts, except 
for the contact points, must comply with the re-
quired surface protection type. 
3.1.4  保护镀层不得有任何气孔、裂纹、损坏或
其他会削弱表面防磨防腐特性的瑕疵。 
3.1.4 The protective coatings must not exhibit any 
pores, cracks, damage, or other flaws impairing the 
surface characteristics regarding wear and corro-
sion resistance. 
3.1.5  镀镍组件的表面应有均匀的光学外观。可
通过热后处理改变镀层的结构和粘附性。 
3.1.5  The surface of the nickel-plated compo-
nents should have a uniform optical appearance. 
The structure and the adhesion of the coatings can 
be changed by a thermal post-treatment. 
3.1.6  图纸中可能指定了镀镍部件的附加涂油。
3.1.6  An additional oiling of the nickel-plated parts 
may be specified in the drawing. 
3.2 基体材料. 
基底材料的类型和组成对于镀层的质量有重
大影响。供应商必须确保表面处理适用于镀层。
3.2   Base material 
The type and composition of the base material 
have a significant effect on the quality of the coat-
ing. The purchaser must ensure that the surface 
finish is suitable for coating. 
3.3 镀层的组成和结构 
3.3  Coating composition and structure 
3.3.1  镀层厚度 
    必须根据 DIN EN ISO 3497、DIN EN ISO 2360、
DIN EN ISO 2178 或 DIN EN ISO 1463 确定镀层
厚度。如有冲突，根据 DIN EN ISO 1463 的规格
适用。对于与功能相关的特殊区域，必须在图纸
中指定测量点。然而，表格1 中列出的参考值可
用作指导。 
3.3.1 Coating thickness
The coating thickness must be determined 
according to DIN EN ISO 3497, DIN EN ISO 2360, 
DIN EN ISO 2178, or DIN EN ISO 1463. In case of 
conflict, the specifications according to DIN EN ISO 
1463 apply. For special areas relevant to the func-
tion, the measuring point must be specified in the 
drawing.However, the reference values listed in 
Table 1 can be used as a guideline.

3.3.2  磷含量 
如果关于磷含量的特定要求对于组件十分必
要，则必须在图纸中明确这些要求。 
3.3.2  Phosphorous content 
If specific requirements regarding the phos-
phorous content are necessary for the component, 
these must be specified in the drawing. 
3.3.3  热处理 
    如果随后的镀层热处理对于组件十分必要，
则必须在图纸中明确说明温度和时长。 
3.3.3  Heat treatment 
If a subsequent heat treatment of the coating is 
necessary for the component, this must be speci-
fied in the drawing by indicating temperature and 
duration. 
3.4 耐蚀性 
根据 DIN EN ISO 6270-2 在CH 测试环境中
进行分类测试如下： 
a) A 类：120h，基底金属腐蚀面积小于 1%。
根据 DIN EN ISO 9227 采用 NSS 测试法
进行测试； 
b) B 类：192h，基底金属腐蚀面积小于 1%；
c) C 类：无要求。 
3.4 Corrosion resistance
Testing in CH test atmosphere according to 
DIN EN ISO 6270-2： 
a) Type A: 120h<1% base metal corrosion on 
substantial surface area. Testing with test 
method NSS according to DIN EN ISO 
9227; 
b) Type B: 192h<1% base metal corrosion on 
substantial surface area; 
c) Type C: no requirement. 
3.5 耐磨性/镀层硬度 
3.5  Wear resistance/coating hardness 
3.5.1  测试：根据 DIN EN ISO 4516 在横向薄
切片上或试样表面上足够厚的镀层的维氏显微硬
度。要求：400 HV -600 HV。 
 
3.5.1  Test: Vickers microhardness according to 
DIN EN ISO 4516 on transverse microsections or 
with sufficiently thick coatings on the specimen 
surface. Requirement: 400-600 HV. 
3.5.2  必须在图纸中说明对于 B 类和 C 类特
别组件的特定偏差。 
3.5.2  Component-specific deviations that may be 
particularly necessary for the types B and C must 
be specified in the drawing. 
3.6 附着力 
根据零件要求，按照DIN EN ISO 2819 进行
弯曲、撞击或热冲击试验来测试镀层的附着力。
要求：镀层无气泡、起泡或剥离等现象。 
3.6  Adhesion 
The adhesion of the coatings is tested based 
on DIN EN ISO 2819 and by a bending, impact or 
heat shock test, depending on the component. 
Requirement: no adhesion loss in the shape of 
bubbles, blisters or detachment of the layer.
