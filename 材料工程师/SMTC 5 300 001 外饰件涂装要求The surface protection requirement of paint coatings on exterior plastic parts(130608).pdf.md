1 范围 
本标准规定了外饰的塑料件（车身附件，
如保险杠、轮罩饰板、后导流板、行李箱盖及
前翼板等等）上的底漆、单层和多层漆的要求。 
本标准适用于外饰涂装件，包括发动机仓
内的塑料涂装件。 
 
1  Scope  
This specification specifies requirements for 
primer coat, single and multi-layer painting on 
exterior plastic parts (body-mounted parts, e.g. 
bumper, wheel cover, rear spoiler, trunk lid, 
fender, etc).  
The standard applies to exterior plastic parts, 
and also to painted components within the 
engine compartment.

3  Requirements 
3.1  避免有害物质按SMTC 5 500 001。完成完
整的试验需要10 个零件，如有必要，再提供6 
块正常生产条件下的测试样板。 
 
3.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances 
based on SMTC 5 500 001. Ten finished parts 
and, if required, six test panels coated under 
standard production conditions are required for 
a complete test.  
 
3.2  零件首次送样认可时，随同零件一起提交
的信息记录必须完整（需按照表1 进行填写）。
此外，零件必须无外观缺陷，且色差及光泽满
足相关部门的发放标准。送样零件的膜厚应在
TDS 规定的范围内。 
 
3.2  For the first sample delivering, the 
information of the coating system and coating 
process must be provided (refer to table 1). The 
parts should be no flaws on the appearance, 
and should also meet the requirement of color 
matching. In addition, the coating’s film 
thickness must be of the TDS scope.

3.3  所有涂装零件须按照SMTC 5 400 001 进
行大气曝露试验（1 年湿热和1 年干热）。由
供货商负责解决试验过程中的质量问题。 
 
3.3  All painted parts are subjected to open-air 
weathering test (1 year in wet heat and 1 year 
in dry heat) based on SMTC 5 400 001. The 
supplier is obligated to resolve complaints 
occurring during this test.  
 
3.4  仅允许在总漆膜厚度不超过150μ m的条
件下进行一次返喷(对于钢琴漆，漆膜厚度根据
图纸及要求)。通常情况下，只允许同色返喷。  
其他色调组合补漆须征得中心实验室的认可。 
 
3.4  Only single regurgitation is allowed, 
provided that the total paint coat thickness is no 
more than 150 µm (in the case of piano 
lacquer, paint coat thickness based on release 
status or drawing). Commonly, the first and 
second coating is of the same color in 
regurgitation. Other color combinations shall be 
agreed upon with SCL.
