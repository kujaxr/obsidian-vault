Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）车轮是由于疲劳而开裂的；其原因是由于冲击导致车轮发生变形和微观损伤。The wheel was cracked through 
fatigue because of an impact which caused the deformation and microcosimic damage. 
（2）材料的成分，机械性能和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical compositions, mechanical properties and 
hardness meet the requirement; the microstructure is normal. 
（3）涉及内缘的相关尺寸符合要求。Some dimensions related with inner edge meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核车轮的抗冲击能力，并考虑是否增加内缘厚度（开发中，行驶19177Km 的车辆出现过类似开裂）。
Check the impact resistance ability and consider whether to increase the thickness of the inner edge（during the 
development, the similar crack happened for one car running 19177Km）. 
（2）按照上汽的标准对车轮进行径向冲击测试，评估其抗冲击性能，并与其它相近车型进行对比。Make a 
radial impact test for the wheel according to the saic related standard to evaluate the impact property and compare 
to other car type with the same level. 
（3）关注售后市场的车轮问题。Pay attention to the wheel after sale.
