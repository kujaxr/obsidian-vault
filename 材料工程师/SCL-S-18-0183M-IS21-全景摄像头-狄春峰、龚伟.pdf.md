Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件安装孔位置打胶后，装配应力增大，导致壳体开裂。 
After the part mounting hole is glued, the assemble stress increases, causing the housing to crack. 
2.  螺钉柱尺寸与推荐值不符。 
    Screw column size does not match recommended value. 
3.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
                    Material performance of part conformed to the requirement.   
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商评估打胶对安装的影响。 
                   Supplier evaluates the impact of glue on installation. 
2.  修改螺钉柱尺寸。 
    Modify the screw column size.
