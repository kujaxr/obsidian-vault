Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）螺母牙型不良导致装配中螺纹咬合异常出现滑牙。The bad tooth shape of the nut caused the bad matching 
between the bolt and nut, which made the screw be stripped. 
（2）1#和2#件的螺栓成分，硬度均符合要求；组织正常；但在负载面的中径区域均有折叠裂纹。The chemical 
components and hardness of the two bolts meet the requirements and the microstructure is normal, but there is a folding 
crack on the working surface. 
（3）螺母的硬度符合要求；组织正常。The hardness of the nut meets the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）加强螺母和螺栓的工艺过程控制，确保产品的质量。Strength to control the process of the nut and bolt to ensure 
the parts' quality. 
（2）抽检已装车螺栓的表面质量，并评估其风险。Check the installed bolt’s surface quality and evaluate the risk.
