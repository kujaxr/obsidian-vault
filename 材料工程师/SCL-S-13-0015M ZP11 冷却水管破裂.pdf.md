Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：胶管材料符合要求。 
       胶管内层受到割伤，并在此受伤点形成疲劳开裂，致使胶管开裂。 
Conclusions：the material of the hose conforms to the standard. 
           The tube of the hose was cut during the use, and the fatigue broken took place at this point which leading the hose broken, 
建议：确保胶管在使用中不受异物损伤。 
Suggestions：assure the hose free of the damage by the abnormal articles during the usage.
