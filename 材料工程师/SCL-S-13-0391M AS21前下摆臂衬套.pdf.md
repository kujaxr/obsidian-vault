Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 衬套是由于过载而开裂；The bush was broken because of the over loading.  
2. 衬套化学成分符合要求，微观组织及硬度正常；孔隙率≤5%，最大孔隙尺寸为1.5mm；Chemical components of 
the bush met the technical requirement. Microstructure and hardness were normal. Porosity was less than 5% and the 
maximum of gas pore was 1.5mm.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核开裂处实际应力载荷；Check the actual load at cracking region. 
2. 建议更改衬套为铸铁件；Suggest changing the bushing for iron casting.
