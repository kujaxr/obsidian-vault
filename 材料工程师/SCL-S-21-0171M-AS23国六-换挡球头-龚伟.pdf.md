Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  摇臂与按键装配应力大，导致失效问题发生。 
The assembly stress of rocker arm and key is large, resulting in failure. 
2. 摇臂材料熔融指数偏高，但不是失效的主要原因。 
    The melt index of rocker arm material is high, but it is not the main reason of failure.   
建议Suggestions：1. 优化零件结构以解决零件应力过大问题。 
                    Optimize the part structure to solve the problem of excessive stress. 
2. 供应商优化注塑工艺以改善熔指偏高问题。 
                    The supplier optimizes the injection molding process to improve the problem of high melt index.
