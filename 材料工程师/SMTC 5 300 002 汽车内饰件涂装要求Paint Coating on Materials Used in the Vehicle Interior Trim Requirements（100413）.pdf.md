1 范围 
本标准规定了对汽车内饰件的涂装要求。 
本标准适用于安装于汽车内部的油漆、胶头印
刷、热压、模转印和水转印方法涂覆的（内部装饰）
金属和非金属材料。 
 
1  Scope  
The standard specify the paint coating 
requirement on vehicle interior trim. 
This Technical Supply Specification  applies 
to painted, pad-printed, heat-sealed, in-mold 
decorated (IMD) and cubic-printed metallic and 
non-metallic materials, which are used in the 
vehicle interior.

3.5 预处理 
    所有有待提供检验的样品必须进行预置： 
    在室温下采用狭窄的空气温度范围（18～28）
℃内存放7 天或在60℃环境内存放48h。 
    每个单项检验后零件必须预置30 min，以便进
行目视评定。 
    按照第3.7 、3.8 条检验必须预置1 h（18～
28）℃。 
    纤维增强、滚珠增强或发泡零件必须在（18～
28）℃下的预置24 h 后进行检验。 
3.5  Conditioning  
All samples delivered for testing must be 
pre-conditioned:  
7 days at room temperature with narrow air 
temperature range (18 to 28) °C or 48 h at 60 °C. 
Before the beginning of the visual evaluation, the 
samples shall be conditioned for a minimum of 30 
min after completion of each individual test. 
Testing acc. to Sections 3.7 and 3.8 shall be 
performed after 1 h of conditioning at (18 bis 28) 
°C. Fiber-, ball-reinforced or foamed components 
shall be tested after 24 h of conditioning at (18 to 
28) °C.

3.7.2  十字切割 
    由于零件结构导致涂层网格测试不能操作时，
用十字切割法。 
    检验：十字切割之后撕除胶带。十字切割需要
用锋利的，薄的刀具，例如，带有梯形刀的安全刀
具。 
    要求：在撕下胶粘带后，不允许有油漆颗粒附
着在胶粘带上。 
3.7.2  Crosscut test (St. Andrew's cross)  
This test shall be performed whenever a normal 
crosscut test cannot be performed for reasons of 
component geometry.  
Test: Crosscut and subsequent removal of 
adhesive tape. For the crosscut test, a knife with 
a sharp, thin blade shall be used, e.g. a safety 
knife with trapezoidal blade.  
Requirement:  
─ No paint particles shall remain on the adhesive 
tape after the tape has been removed. 
3.8 耐刮性能 
 
检验按照SMTC 30510 
 
检验用型号318 的Erichsen-硬度试棒 
试验要求： 
10N负荷 
探针直径为0.75 mm 
 
评判要求： 
    涂装层均无油漆的开裂现象。 
    由负荷造成的基材有一个缺口是允许的。 
3.8  Scratch resistance  
Testing acc. to Test Specification SMTC 30510. 
Test e.g. with the Erichsen hardness tester, type 
318, with a load of 10 N and a test needle acc. 
toBosch (point: 0,75 mm) 
Requirements: 
─No cracking (material removal) of the painting 
─An indentation mark in the substrate material 
resulting from the load is permitted 
 
3.9 散发性能 
    检验按照SMTC 30159。 
    油漆非金属材料的要求依据SMTC 30158。 
 
－溶剂漆系统：对于非油漆部件所要求挥发值
允许超过30µgC/g 。 
 
－水性漆系统：对于非油漆部件所要求的挥发
值允许超过10µgC/g。 
 
－金属材料涂装的要求：油漆部件不能超过
20µgC/g。 
 
3.9  Emission behavior  
Testing acc. to SMTC 30159. The following 
deviating requirements apply to the tests acc. to 
SMTC 30158 contained in SMTC 30159 for 
painted non-metallic materials:  
─Solvent-based paint systems: The emission 
values required for the non-painted components 
may be exceeded by 30 µgC/g. ─Water-based 
paint systems: The emission values required for 
the non-painted components may be exceeded 
by 10 µgC/g. ─Requirement for painted metallic 
materials: The emission values of the painted 
component shall not exceed a value of 20 µgC/g. 
 
3.10 耐热性 
3.10  Temperature resistance 
3.10.1  高温老化 
检验：在循环风动烘箱中存放 
——在太阳非直接照射范围内的构件：置放
240 h，置放温度（90＋2）℃。 
——在太阳直接照射范围内的构件：（例如除
霜口，仪表板上部范围）存放时间500 h，存放温
度（120＋2）℃。 
——在太阳照射过渡区内的构件（例如换气
口，方向盘），存放时间500 h，存放温度105＋2℃。
要求：相对于供货状态无外观和手感变化。必
须同时满足第3.7条和3.8条的要求（注意第3.5条）。
 
3.10.1  Aging at elevated temperature  
Test: Aging in mechanical circulation oven: 
─Components exposed to indirect sunlight: aging 
duration 240 h; aging temperature (90 + 2) °C  
─Components exposed to direct sunlight (e. g. 
defroster duct, upper area of dashboard): aging  
duration 500 h; aging temperature (120 + 2) °C
