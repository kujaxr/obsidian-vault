Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于凹坑的应力集中引起的疲劳断裂，凹坑形成于表面保护涂层之前。 
The failure is caused by fatigue fracture due to stress concentration of the dimples, the dimples 
formed on the surface protective layer before. 
2、零件的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The material properties of the parts met the technical requirements.  
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查弹簧整个生产过程的质量控制（包括原材料）。 
Check spring quality control for the production process (including raw materials).
