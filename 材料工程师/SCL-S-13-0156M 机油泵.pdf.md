Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 1#失效件：由于从动齿与从动轴发生粘着磨损，从动齿剥离物粘在从动轴表面，从而导致两摩擦副之间卡死(从
动齿与从动轴设计匹配间隙为0.01mm-0.046mm，从动轴粘着物厚度约为0.2mm)。The 1# driven shaft and gear was 
seized, due to the adhesive wear between the driven shaft and gear (the design matching space was 0.01mm-0.046mm, 
the adhesions thickness of driven shaft is about 0.2mm). 
2. 2#失效件：从动轴与从动齿磨损形貌与1#失效件相似，也是粘着磨损引起两摩擦副间卡死；后期导致驱动轴
与泵体间也发生异常磨损。The 2# driven shaft and gear wear morphology was similar with 1# failure parts. The 
adhesive wear resulted in the 2# driven shaft and gear seized and the driver shaft and gear abnormal wear later. 
3. 1#失效件从动轴微观组织无异常，硬度、硬化层深度、化学成分及直径符合图纸要求；从动齿微观组织无异常，
硬度、孔径符合图纸要求。Microstructure of 1#driven shaft was normal, hardness, harden case, chemical components 
and its diameter met the technical requirement. Microstructure of 1# gear was normal, hardness and aperture met the 
requirement. 
4. 2#失效件从动轴微观组织无异常，硬度、硬化层深度及直径符合要求；从动齿硬度及孔径符合图纸要求
Microstructure of 2# driven shaft was normal, hardness, harden case, and its diameter met the technical requirement. 
Hardness and aperture of2# gear met the requirement 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 评估从动轴与从动齿，从动轴与驱动轴，驱动齿与从动齿及驱动轴与泵体轴孔设计间隙的合理性。
Evaluated the design space of driver shaft and driven shaft, driver gear and driven gear, driven shaft and gear, 
driver shaft and pump. 
2. 检查整个系统的清洁度，是否符合要求。Check the cleaness of the whole system.
