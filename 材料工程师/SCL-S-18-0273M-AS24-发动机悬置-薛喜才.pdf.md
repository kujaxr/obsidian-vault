Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  失效件为冷模阶段的零件，铸件内含有大量的氧化物，导致强度降低，装配时发生断裂。 
   The failed part is cold model part, there is lots of oxygen in casting, resulting in the strength reducing and fracturing in 
assembly. 
2.  材料成分满足A380 技术要求，硬度略低。 
Chemical components meet the requirements. Hardness is a little low. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  建议铸件供应商加强冷模件的质量管控。 
Suggest the casting supplier strengthens monitoring the quality of cold model parts.
