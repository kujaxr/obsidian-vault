Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 飞轮轴扭转剪切过载断裂失效。The flywheel shaft overload torsional shear fractured. 
（2） 失效飞轮轴材料化学成分、表面硬度、芯部硬度和硬化层深度合格，金相组织正常。The chemical 
compositions、surface hardness 、core hardness and the hardened layer of failure flywheel shaft are qualified, the 
microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 通过后台对失效车辆的运行数据进行分析，查找可能的异常的共振工况点并通过试验进行验证。The 
operation data of the vehicle are analyzed in the background to find out the possible abnormal resonance working 
conditions and verify by the experiment.

 此前工程试验时，曾出现相似断裂案例，见报告SCL-S-17-0069M。
