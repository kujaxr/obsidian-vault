Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 下系杆是由于冷隔缺陷引起的过载断裂。The TER LWR was overload fracture due to the cold shut defects. 
2、 下系杆化学成分符合要求，硬度和孔隙度未见异常。The chemical composition of the TER LWR meets the 
requirements, hardness and porosity have no abnormal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、要求供应商加强零件质量管控，尤其对冷模件进行控制。Require the supplier to strengthen the quality control of 
parts, especially control the cold die parts. 
2、对同批次已装车零件进行风险评估。Evaluate the risk of the cars which have assembled the same batch parts.

四、综合分析  Comprehensive Analysis 
失效件断裂起源位置有明显冷隔缺陷，且零件韧性较差，很可能在车辆行驶过程中受到较大外力，发生过载断裂；
也可能在零件装配过程中在冷隔缺陷位置先发生开裂，然后在后面使用过程中再全部断开。 
The crack source of failure part has obvious cold shut defects, and it has poor toughness. and the overload fracture occurs 
when subjected to larger external force. The force is likely to come from the bolt assembly, also may be caused by the 
abnormal external force on the car driving. The parts may be overload fracture when subjected to abnormal external 
force on the car driving. It also may be cracking in the cold shut defect position during assembly in first, then all 
disconnected in the using.
