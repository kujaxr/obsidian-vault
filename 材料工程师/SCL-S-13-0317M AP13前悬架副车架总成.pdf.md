Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 支架是由于多源疲劳而开裂的，开裂位置见图3，开裂原因与应力载荷较大有关；The bracket was broken because 
of multi-source and the crack was shown in figure3. The cracking reason was relate to a big stress load. 
2. 焊缝长度符合图纸要求，部分偏下限；局部焊接熔深偏低；The length of weld seam met the technical 
requirement, parts was low. Local welding penetration was observed.  
3. 副车架本体与支架的材质正常，符合SAPH440 要求；The material of sub-frame and bracket met the 
requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核开裂处应力；Check the active stress at cracking region. 
2. 关注焊接质量，避免出现熔深偏低；Pay attention to the quality of welding, avoid the insufficient penetration.
