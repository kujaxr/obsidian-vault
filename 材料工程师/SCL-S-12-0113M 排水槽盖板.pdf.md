Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件受异常外力导致失效。 
      2. 失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
Conclusions:  1. External force made the part fail. 
 
  2. The material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1.注意在物流，装配等环节对零件的保护。 
Suggestions：1. More attention should be paid to the protection of parts during logistics, assembly and etc.
