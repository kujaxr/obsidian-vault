Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论：1、防尘罩外表面受外物磨损，受力作用下产生细小裂口，裂口扩展，并发生由橡胶外表面向内层撕裂扩展。 
2、失效零件与新件材料密度一致，性能接近。 
建议：1、检查零件使用工况，避免零件在使用过程中发生干涉或磨损。 
 
Conclusion: 1.The outside surface of dust had been worn by other substances and lead to some small cracks under the 
action of force, the cracks become larger by tearing from outside to inner layer. 
2. The density of failure part and the new part are the same, the former material’s properties is close to the 
latter’s. 
Suggestion: 1. Check the working condition of the part and avoid interference or wear in normal use.

小结：1、防尘罩外表面受外物磨损，受力作用下产生细小裂口，裂口不断扩展，导致大裂口产生，并发生由防尘
