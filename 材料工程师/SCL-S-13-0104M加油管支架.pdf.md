Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 加油管支架断裂的根本原因是R 弧处应力载荷过大，导致其发生疲劳断裂。The fatigue fracture of the filler pipe 
bracket resulted from the great tress concentration. 
2. 金相正常，化学成分、硬度符合要求。Microstructure was normal, chemical components and hardness met the 
requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 确认试验时工装条件符合要求。Making sure the experiment condition met the requirement.  
2 优化R弧设计，如增大R弧半径、添加加强筋、增加板材厚度。Optimizing the design such as increasing the R radius, 
adding the strength tendon and increasing the plate thickness.
