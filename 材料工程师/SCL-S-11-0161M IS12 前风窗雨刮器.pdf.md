Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）球头成形过程中形成的表面裂纹，一方面降低了镍层的附着力，导致镍层的剥离；另一方面，缺陷处的金属
在工作中，容易磨损，从基体上脱落。剥离的镍层和基体金属恶化磨损条件，导致了产品出现严重磨损而脱落。
Surface defects caused during manufacture reduces the adhesion force of the Ni coating and leads to delamination. Additionally the metal in the 
defect position is easily worn and separates from the matrix. The separated particles of Ni coating and metal worsened the abrasion condition and 
caused the serious wear and consequent failure.   
（2） 球头的材料成分，硬度和组织正常。The material chemical components, hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）建议供应商做好球头成形过程的质量控制或者改善工艺，避免有表面缺陷的产品出现并流入下一道工序。The 
supplier should improve and control the manufacturing process, avoid the defects and prevent defective parts to flow into the next process.
