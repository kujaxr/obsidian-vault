Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
1.  Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
2.  失效件长时间接触醇类溶剂所致。 
2.  The failure part was caused by exposure to alcohol solvents for long time.   
建议Suggestions： 1. 此零件避免长时间接触醇类溶剂。一旦接触醇类溶剂，请及时用湿毛巾擦拭。 
1. This part should not expose to alcohol solvents for a long time. Once exposed to alcohol solvents, 
please wipe with wet towel in time.

采用毛毡浸泡在特定溶剂48 h 模拟失效情况。毛毡浸泡在酒精和玻璃清洁剂48 h 后，毛毡出现了不同程
度的剥落，与失效件类似。由于玻璃清洁剂的成分中含有甲醇及丙醇等醇类，所以推测失效件是由于接触了
醇类溶剂所致。毛毡浸泡在甲醇和正丙醇48 h 后，毛毡植绒出现明显剥落。 
In order to simulating the failure part, the peels were immersed into specific solvent after 48 h. The peels appear 
certain degree of shedding, which is similar to failure parts. The main component of glass detergent contain methanol 
and/or propanol, so we deduce that the part was exposed to alcohol solvents resulted the Failure, such ethanol or 
propanol. The peels appear heavily shedding after immersing methanol and propanol for 48h.
