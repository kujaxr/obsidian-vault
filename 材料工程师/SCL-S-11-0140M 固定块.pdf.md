Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 在发动机饰盖装配过程中，轴销与橡胶垫轴孔轴线位置匹配存在较大误差，轴销顶到橡胶垫的过度
面而不是轴孔，用力下压饰盖入位时，导致橡胶垫发生撕裂。 
Conclusions: After testing, the materials of the rubber cushion conform to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the rubber cushion, it can be seen that the axis position of rubber 
cushion can’t match the pin on engine. The top of pin touch on the surface of cushion not the rubber cushion 
central hole. Then the rubber cushion was broken by the pin while pressing the engine plastic cover in place. 
建议：装配前百分百检查饰盖上橡胶垫是否装配到位；装配时保证轴销与橡胶垫轴孔位置匹配。 
Suggestions：One hundred percent inspect if the rubber cushions are installed in place. The axis positions of rubber 
cushions central holes should match the pins positions during assembly.

图2 和图3 分别是失效橡胶垫和正常装配橡胶垫俯视图，失效橡胶垫表面存在明显断裂，橡胶垫轴孔轴线方
向发生改变。图4 和图5 分别是失效橡胶垫和正常装配橡胶垫侧视图，可以看出失效橡胶垫的下部已经歪斜。

during assembly are found around the crack which also be found on the normal rubber cushion as shown in Fig 8. In Fig 9, 
the crack surface is coarse and the orientation crack caused by the crack ductile propagation appeared on the crack 
surface.
