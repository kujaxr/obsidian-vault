Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）限位板在螺栓拧紧过程中由于过载而发生扭转剪切断裂。The limit plate was sheared and fractured due to 
overload during bolt tightening; 
（2）限位板材料成分和金相组织满足标准要求，强度正常；板厚及断裂处的宽度尺寸符合图纸要求。The 
composition meet requirements, the strength is normal; the thickness of the plate and the width of the fracture meet the 
requirements of the drawing. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化限位板卡脚大小。Optimize the size of the stop plate pin;
