Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 支架属疲劳断裂，疲劳源起始于翻边的R 角中心内侧位置。Bracket belongs to fatigue fracture , fatigue source 
starting at the center of R angle flanging. 
2、 R 角过小不符合图纸技术要求。 R angle is too small does not meet the technical requirements of drawings 
3、 支架材料性能符合提供的技术要求。Material properties meet the technical requirements 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 供应商严格控制实物质量（如毛刺、裂纹、R 角等）。Suppliers strict control of the physical quality (such as 
glitches, crack, R angle, etc.). 
2、 校核支架翻边处R 角处的实际载荷，对弯折处的内R 角明确技术要求。Check the actual load for the gear sets 
R corner. clear technical requirements within for the angles the R in bent. 
3、 请关注稳定杆橡胶衬套的性能。Concerned about the performance of the rubber bushing stabilizer bar.
