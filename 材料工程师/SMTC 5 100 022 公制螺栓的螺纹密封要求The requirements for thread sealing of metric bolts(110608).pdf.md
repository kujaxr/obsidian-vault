1 范围 
本标准规定了对使用单层涂料（A 类适用于具
有整体轮罩的盘轮）或多层涂料（无索引类，内部
生产）的钢质车轮进行的装饰性腐蚀保护的具体要
求。 
     
本标准适用于单层涂料或多层涂料的钢质车
轮装饰性腐蚀保护。 
1  Scope  
This Standard specifies the requirements of 
decorative corrosion protection for steel disk 
wheels that are produced using single coat paint 
(Type A for wheels with full wheel covers) or mul-
ticoat paint (Type without index, produced 
in-house). 
The standard applies to The requirements of 
decorative corrosion protection for steel wheels 
that are produced using single coat paint or mul-
ticoat paint.

Requirements 
If the following requirements for specific 
components or parts of components do not apply 
completely or not at all, this shall be coordinated 
with the responsible engineering departments and 
a special note shall be added to the drawing 
3.1 基本规范 
按照 SMTC 90008 的要求避免有害物质。完整
的试验需要用到四种零部件，必要时，还要用到四
块测试板。要在图纸上正确标明零件不需要进行试
验的部位。安装时，在可见前表面上，零件外观必
须按照图纸和发布样品的说明，具有统一形态。不
允许出现对指定形态不利的孔洞、粗大裂纹，以及
其他瑕疵。 
 
3.1 Basic specifications
Avoidance of hazardous substances accord-
ing to SMTC 90008. 4 Parts and, if necessary, 4 
test panels are required for a complete test. All 
locations on the part for which the following re-
quirements do not apply shall be identified appro-
priately on the drawing. On the front surface that 
is visible when installed, the parts must have a 
uniform appearance according to the drawing and 
the released sample. Pores, coarse cracks and 
other flaws that adversely affect the specified 
appearance are not permissible. 
3.2 调节 
    在试验前，被测零件必须放置在 60 ℃环境中 
24 h。 
3.2 Conditioning
The parts shall be conditioned 24 h at 60 °C 
before the tests. 
3.3 对测量结果的评估 
在每次测量中都得出所需数值。 
 
3.3  Evaluation of the measuring results 
The required numerical values apply to each 
individual measurement.
