Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
小结:  
O 型圈受到异常挤压，导致密封圈破裂。 
零件材料为氟橡胶，符合图纸要求。 
建议在使用过程中，避免使O 型圈受到异常挤压。 
 
Conclusion: 
The O ring had been severely squeezed and leading to the rupture. 
The material of the part is FKM which meets the drawing. 
It is suggest that the seal should avoid having unusual stress during the use.

different from the smooth part. So we can get the conclusion that: the O ring had been severely squeezed which 
caused the cracks and gaps.
