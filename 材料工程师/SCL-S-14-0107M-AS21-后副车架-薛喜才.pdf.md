Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 后副车架是由于疲劳而开裂的，裂纹起源于母材热影响区，最后扩展至焊缝；Sub-frame failed though fatigue. The 
crack origin was at HAZ and propagated at the welding seam at last. 
2. 材料成分符合要求，金相及硬度正常；Chemical components met the requirement. Microstructure and hardness were 
normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核载荷; Check the active stress load.
