Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  变速箱悬置发生过载断裂，其与匹配间隙、铸件冷隔缺陷有关。Transmission suspension fractured because of big 
load，which is related to the matching dimension and slight cold lap. 
2.  材料成分、硬度、孔隙率均满足要求。Chemical components、hardness、porosity meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查变速箱悬置、纵梁匹配间隙。Check the matching dimension between transmission suspension and 
longitudinal beam. 
2. 供应商提高铸件质量。Supply improves the casting quality.
