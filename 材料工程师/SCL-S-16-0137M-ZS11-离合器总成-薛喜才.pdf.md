Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
(1) 离合器压盘属于热疲劳失效。Clutch pressure plate failed though thermal fatigue. 
 
(2) 压盘边缘区域以E/D 型石墨为主，且石墨大小及所占区域均不符合技术要求。Graphite shape in edge region is the  
type E/D and the length、occupying region don’t meet the technical requirement.  
 
(3) 珠光体含量、碳化物含量及硬度满足技术要求。Pearlite and cementite content and hardness meet the technical  
requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
(1) 供应商加强质量监管，改善铸件质量。Strengthen the quality supervision to improve the casting quality.
