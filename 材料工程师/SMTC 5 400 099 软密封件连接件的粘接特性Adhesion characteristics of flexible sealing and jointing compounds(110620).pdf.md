1 范围 
本标准规定了评价软密封件/连接件的粘接
特性的步骤。 
本标准适用于可形成粘结片的任何材料。 
1 
Scope  
This standard specifies the procedure for 
assessing the adhesion characteristics of 
flexible sealing and jointing compounds. 
This standard is suitable for use with any 
material from which adherent beads can be 
formed. 
2 设备 
2  Apparatus 
2.1 应用于车身的钢板或铝合金金属板，根据
相关的产品应用或相关材料标准的其它指定进
行处理。板的尺寸应是150 mm×100 mm，厚大
约0.8 mm。 
2.1  Metal panels of Autobody sheet steel or 
aluminium alloy as applicable, finished in ac-
cordance with the production application, or as 
otherwise specified in the relevant Material 
Standard. The panel dimensions will be 150 
mm x 100 mm x approximately 0.8 mm. 
2.2 适合的设备用于密封材料密封片的施用。
2.2 Suitable equipment for application of a 
bead of sealer material. 
2.3 必要的材料的状态调节和烘干方法。 
2.3  Means of conditioning and curing the 
material as necessary. 
2.4 满足指定的环境老化程序的环境箱。 
2.4
Environmental chambers suitable for the 
specified environmental ageing procedure. 
3 试样准备 
3  Specimen preparation 
3.1 根据在车辆上的应用和相关材料标准的规
定准备样板。 
3.1
Prepare the panels in accordance with 
the proposed application on the vehicle and as 
stated in the relevant Material Standard. 
3.2 施用两片密封材料到末道漆上，密封片的
最终尺寸为125 mm 长、12 mm-15 mm 宽、3 mm 
厚，并和面板的径向轴平行，离每个边缘20 mm 
并且中间的空间大约40 mm 宽。注：在施用密
封片之前，横向施用宽度大约为20 mm 的带子到
板上，然后按图1 所示将密封片的一端放置在上
面，这样评定粘结时开始剥离时容易一些。带子
应能够经受的住固化和环境老化条件。 
3.2
Apply two beads of the material to finish 
up with final wet dimensions of 125 mm long 
and between 12 mm – 15 mm wide x 3 mm 
thick, parallel with the longitudinal axis of the 
panel 20 mm from each edge and spaced ap-
proximately 40 mm apart. Note: Before apply-
ing the bead apply tape of width approximately 
20 mm across the panel and lay start of bead 
on top as shown in figure 1, this will facilitate 
the start of peel to assess adhesion. The tape 
must be able to withstand the curing and en-
vironmental ageing conditions.

3.3 将板水平放置，密封片在最上面进行指定
的调节或固化程序。 
3.3  With the panel in a horizontal plane, 
beads uppermost, subject the material to the 
specified conditioning or curing. 
4 步骤/试验 
4  Procedure / Test 
4.1 在进行完试验状态调节之后应立即进行试
验。 
4.1
The test must be carried out immediately 
on removal from the test condition. 
4.2  用适合的方法例如用老虎钳或手将密封片
从底材上剥离。 
4.2  Using a suitable method e.g. pliers or by 
hand, peel the bead from the substrate. 
5 报告 
5 
Report  
检查材料以评价密封片试验的效果。报告试
验结果以确认相关材料标准的要求。 
Examine the material to assess the effect 
the test has had on the beads of sealer. Report 
the results of the test to confirm the require-
ments of the relevant material Standard.
