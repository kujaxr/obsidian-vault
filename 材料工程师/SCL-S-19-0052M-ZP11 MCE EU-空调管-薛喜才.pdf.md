Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 空调管发生疲劳断裂与振动载荷较大有关。 
   Hose pipe fractured by fatigue because of big vibration load. 
2. 材料成分满足要求。 
Chemical components meet the requirement.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 评估该故障车振动。 
   Evaluate the vibration load.
