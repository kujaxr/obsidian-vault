Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 隔热罩支架由于受到较大的振动载荷而发生疲劳开裂。 
Heat shield-cylinder head at the bracket was broken off by fatigue because of the big vibrating load. 
2. 隔热罩支架结构偏离原设计要求，且避让缺口偏大，冲孔边缘有毛刺。 
The shape deviated from the original design position. The gap was large and the burrs were observed in the hole. 
3. 支架材料、板厚符合技术要求。Material and thickness of the bracket met the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商应提供符合要求的零件，并减少冲孔边缘毛刺。 
Supplier provides the eligible parts and reduces the burrs.  
2. 评估其承受载荷。 
Evaluate the load.
