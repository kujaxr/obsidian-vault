Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.屏幕被有机溶剂腐蚀后在高温作用下，形成花斑。 
After the screen was corroded by organic solvent, it formed speckles under high temperature.  
建议Suggestions：1.PMMA 材料零件表面应避免喷洒酒精等有机溶剂。 
                   Avoid spraying organic solvents such as alcohol on the surface of PMMA material parts.
