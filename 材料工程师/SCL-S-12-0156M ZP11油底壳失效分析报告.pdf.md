Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于机油滤清器安装凸台有明显的孔隙，这严重削弱了零件的强度。在正常维护时，剩余截面由于过载而断
裂。Significant porosity was present in the oil filter mounting boss, which seriously weakened the part.  The 
remainder of the cross-section failed through overload, during normal maintenance. 
2. 硬度和金相组织正常。Hardness and microstructure are normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商必须加强铸造工艺和检测技术，以防止这种缺陷的产品流传到客户。The supplier must strengthen the 
casting process and inspection techniques to prevent this type of defect getting to the customer.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
收到的油底壳铸件如图1 和2 所示。容易看出：断口的很大一部分不正常（图2b）。 用电镜检查异常面积，发
现有大量的缩孔（图3）。这大减少了凸台的强度。拆卸机油过滤器时，余下的面积由于过载而断裂。  
The sump casting was received as shown in figures 1 & 2.  It was immediately clear that a large part of the fracture was 
abnormal (figure 2b).  Using SEM to examine this area, extensive shrinkage porosity was found (figure 3).  This has 
significantly reduced the strength of the boss.  The remaining area fractured through overload, when disassembling the 
filter.
