Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 燃油箱钢带是局部应力大引起的双向疲劳断裂；The straps are bidirectional fatigue fracture; 
2、 钢带化学成分符合标准要求，硬度和金相组织未见异常。The chemical composition of straps meets the standard 
requirement; the hardness and microstructure are not abnormal. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、重新校核钢带载荷，优化设计，提高零件疲劳强度；如增加板厚、增加螺栓法兰面直径等。Recheck the load on 
the strap and optimize the design to improve the strength of strap, such as adding the thickness or increasing the diameter 
of bolt flange and so on.
