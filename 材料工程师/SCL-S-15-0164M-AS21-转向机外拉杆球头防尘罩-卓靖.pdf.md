Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论：1、防尘罩开裂方向由外到内，受外物刺破所致。 
2、失效零件材料拉伸强度、断裂伸长率等性能满足要求。 
建议：1、检查零件使用工况，避免零件在使用过程中被外物损伤。 
 
Conclusion: 1.The cracking direction of dust was from outside to inner layer, it was punctured by other substances.  
2. The tensile strength and elongation at break of failure part can meet the requirements. 
Suggestion: 1. Check the working condition of the part and avoid damaging in normal use.
