Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1）螺栓松动引发悬置发生早期疲劳开裂。 
Bracket is cracking early by fatigue because of the blot loosing. 
（2）材料成分、硬度满足技术要求，孔隙及组织正常。 
Chemical components and hardness meet the technical requirement. Porosity and microstructure are normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 检查工装夹具的装配扭矩。Check the torque of the clamp.
