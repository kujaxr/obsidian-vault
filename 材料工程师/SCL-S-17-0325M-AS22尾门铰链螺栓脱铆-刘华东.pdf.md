Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 尾门铰链螺栓脱铆是由于拆解过程中受力异常引起的。The failure of bolt is caused by abnormal force during the 
disassembly process; 
2、 螺栓铆接正常；化学成分、硬度和金相组织均符合要求，螺纹存在折叠缺陷，但不是失效原因。The Bolt riveting 
normal. The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure of bolt meet the requirements. The bolt has folds, 
but they are not the reason of failure.  
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、关注拆解过程。Pay attention to the disassembly process. 
2、隔离同批次零件，并评估已装车风险。Isolate the parts of the same batch and evaluate the risk of assembled vehicles. 
3、供应商应加强零件质量控制。Supplier should strengthen control the parts quality.
