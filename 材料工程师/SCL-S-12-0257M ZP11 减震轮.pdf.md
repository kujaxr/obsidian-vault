Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）皮带由于抽丝和撕裂而失效。The belt is failed because the cord was took out and belt was tore. 
（2）皮带受到外物的扎伤，但这并不是导致失效的原因。The belt was punctured by external things, but this is not the 
reason led the failure. 
（3）减震轮出现异常磨损；这种异常磨损可能与系统的匹配尺寸有关，也有可能是导致皮带失效的原因。The 
damping wheel was abnormal worn, which is possible because of the system matching dimensions. Maybe this is the 
failure cause. 
（4）张紧轮的材料符合图纸要求。The material of the tension wheel meets the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）建议对与皮带相连接的系统进行尺寸检查。Suggest checking the matching dimensions of the system including 
the belt and wheel.
