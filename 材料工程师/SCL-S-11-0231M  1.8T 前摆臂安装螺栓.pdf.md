Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是在装配过程中过载引起的失效，引起过载的原因可能是扭矩过大, 或涂装了润滑剂(螺纹或法兰面处)如油或油脂。 
The bolt has failed due to overloading during tightening.  Possible causes are too high torque, or additional 
lubricant, eg oil or grease in thread or under-head. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核螺栓装配工艺及装配过程控制。 
Please check the PAM for the bolt and assembly process control. 
     2、确保螺栓和内螺纹清洁，无附加的润滑脂。 
        Ensure bolt and internal thread are clear and without additional lubricant.
