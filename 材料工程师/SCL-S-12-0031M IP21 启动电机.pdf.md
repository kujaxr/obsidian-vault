Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）启动电机的壳体是由于过载开裂，而非疲劳；开裂后，轴承脱离，导致轴与壳体的匹配面相互接触磨损，
损伤加剧。The shells of the start motor were broken because of overload. After break, the bearing was lost. This led to 
serious wear between the axle and shell matching surface.  
（2）材料的成分，组织和硬度正常。The chemical composition, microstructure and hardness of the material are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）遇到类似的事情，咨询客户了解更多的信息，同时尽可能获得所有的失效部件，确定启动电机出现过载的
原因；这有利于我们了解产品，提升产品的质量。On meeting the same thing, ask the customer to get more information 
and at the same time, get all the failure components; find out the root cause of overload. Therefore learn more about the 
part and improve the quality. 
（2）对工程车辆的轴承和壳体的磨损情况进行检查。Check wear condition of bearings & shells from Engineering 
vehicles.
