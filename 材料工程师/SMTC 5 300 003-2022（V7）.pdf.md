3.4.1  外观 Appearance 
通过仪器测量、目视比较或两者结合，零件
的光泽和颜色必须与已认可的原型样品板或样
件一致。必要时由上海汽车来规定极限样品。特
别需要说明的是，对于MCA 该柔光电镀色号，目
前仅安美特和麦德美的珍珠镍获得该色号的认
可（其他珍珠镍药水牌号必须提前制板，证明其
能够做到和设计部发布的标准样板一致的情况
下，才可能被批准在特定零件上使用）。电镀的
接触点应当选择在安装状态下不可见的位置。重
要面不允许有影响防腐或外观的明显可见的电
镀缺陷，如鼓泡、凹坑、粗糙、裂纹、漏镀、污
渍、变色等。重要面通常是可见的，已完成装配
的车上零件直接或通过反射可见的部分，或其腐
蚀产物会污损整车上可见面。 
 
 
The compliance of the component with the 
released original sample chart or the original 
sample part in terms of gloss and color shall be 
verifiable using measuring instruments and/or 
shall be visually verifiable. If necessary, 
maximum deviation samples must be specified 
by SAIC Motor. The contact points for 
electroplating shall be positioned in a way that 
flaws cannot be recognized in the installed 
condition. Over the significant surface, there 
shall be no clearly visible plating defects such 
as blisters, pits, roughness, cracks, non-plated 
areas, stains, discolorations or other damage 
that impairs the corrosion protection or 
specified appearance. Significant surfaces are 
those normally visible, directly or by reflection, 
on the finished part when assembled in vehicle 
position or which can be the source of 
corrosion deposits that deface visible surfaces 
on the assembled vehicles. 
3.4.2  镀层厚度 Plating thickness 
确定每一独特零件的镀层分布情况是供应
商的责任。为了所有零件重要面的最小镀层厚度
能持续地满足规定要求，挂具可能需要采用许多
特殊加工工艺，比如，采用辅助阳极和（或）遮
蔽措施，减少每挂的零件数量等。可以用切片法
（ISO 1463）或库仑法（ISO 2177）来测量镀层
厚度。如果双方测试结果存在分歧， 切片法应
该作为测量镍和铜层的首选方法。X 射线测厚法
(ISO 3497)可以使用，但在铜层和镍层厚度太高
的情况下只限于测量镀铬层的厚度。 
 
 
It is the supplier’s responsibility to identify the 
plating thickness distribution for each unique 
part. In order to consistently meet the specified 
minimum plating thickness on all significant 
surfaces, plating racks may be required to 
employ many special processing techniques, 
e.g., the use of auxiliary anodes and/or 
shields, reducing the number of parts per rack 
etc.The plating thickness shall be determined 
by the microsection method (ISO 1463) or 
coulometric method (ISO 2177). In case of 
disagreements 
between 
purchaser 
and 
supplier, the microsection method shall be the 
preferred method for Ni and Cu layers. X-Ray 
(ISO 3497) may be used but usage may be 
limited to chrome layer thickness if Copper and

3.4.3  电镀过程 Electroplating process 
 
当供货条件符合要求值时，制造商可锁定电
镀工艺参数（包括电镀时间、电流、装载量等）。
批产产品必须在电镀程序和镀层厚度上与认可

When supply conditions conform to the 
requirements, the manufacturer can lock the 
plating process parameters (including plating 
time current, load etc). Standard-production 
parts shall correspond to released first-sample 
parts in the coating procedure and coating 
thickness.
