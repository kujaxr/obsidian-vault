Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件焊接位置可见撬动痕迹，零件受力后开裂。 
Prying marks can be seen at the welding position of the failure parts, and the parts are cracked 
after being stressed.  
2. 供应商焊接工艺不佳。 
The welding process was poor. 
3. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 避免对零件的撬动或敲击。 
                 Avoid prying or knocking the parts. 
2. 优化焊接工艺。 
                 Optimize welding process.
