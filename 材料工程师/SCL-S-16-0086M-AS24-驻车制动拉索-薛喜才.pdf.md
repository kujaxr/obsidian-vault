Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 驻车主动拉索由于受到较大的外力作用，导致套管率先断裂；套管断裂后拉索在工作时受到弯曲载荷作用引发
疲劳断裂。The cannula was cracking firstly because of the external force. Parking brake cable was cracking by bending 
fatigue after the cannula failed. 
2. 将失效件与未失效件材料进行对比，二者材料硬度、组织无明显差异。Compared with the material of the failure and 
normal part, hardness and microstructure were not significantly different. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查试验过程，调查此外力的来源。Check the testing process to investigate the resource of the external force.
