Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： O ring 材料为HNBR，硬度符合要求。 
O ring 密封面发现有磨损，挤压和破裂现象，致使零件失效。 
Conclusion: The material and Hardness of the O ring conforms to the standard. We discover the scratch, abrasion and crack on the O ring, 
which lead the part fail. 
  建议： 保证安装中O ring 不受损伤，同时核查对手件是否存在毛刺等致使O ring 破裂的因素。 
Suggestion: .Assure the O ring free of damage during the install. Check the opponent pieces with glitch or any other failure which will 
lead O ring crack.
