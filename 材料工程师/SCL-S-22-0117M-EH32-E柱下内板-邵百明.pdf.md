Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）E 柱下内板由于局部应力高导致路试后疲劳开裂。The e post inner plate after the road test was cracked by 
fatigue ,which caused by the high local stress. 
（2）E柱下内板的板厚符合要求；硬度和组织正常。The thickness of the e post inner plate meets the requirement and 
the hardness and microstructure are normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化设计、降低局部应力。Optimize the design and reduce the local stress.
