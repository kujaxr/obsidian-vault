Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 断口具有疲劳特征。The fracture belongs to fatigue fracture. 
2. 硬度符合技术要求。The hardness accords with the requirement. 
3.失效件金相组织不合格，疲劳裂纹起始于铁素体组织。改进后的零件金相组织合格。The microstructure for failure part 
doesn’t meet the requirement. The fatigue crack can initiate at the softer ferrite grain. The  microstructure  for 
improved part accord with the requirement. 
4.失效件（锁槽部位）的机加工表面质量较差，会影响锁槽部位的疲劳寿命。The machining quality of the bar is not very good .The 
rough machining groove can affect fatigue life. 
建议Suggestions： 
1.请进一步分析使用情况。Please check working condition including condition of matching part and installation condition. 
2.将金相组织的技术要求加入图纸的技术要求中。Add microstructure requirement to the drawing. 
3.提高机加工质量。Improve machining quality.

     1、断口宏观形貌，见图3；断裂起始区域已严重磨损，见图5；扩展区有明显的疲劳辉纹，见图4。

结论：合格。              详见附表1
