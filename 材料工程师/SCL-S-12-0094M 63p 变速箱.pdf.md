Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）两例变速箱差速器的失效是由于载荷过大所致。The two differential mechanisms of the gearboxes failed because 
of overload. 
（2）两例行星齿轮和半轴齿轮的组织和硬度符合要求；渗碳层深度高于上限或处于上限，但这不会导致其失效。
The microstructure and hardness of the planet gear and driveshaft gear meet the requirement; the carburization layer 
depths are over or on the top limit, but this can’t cause the failure. 
（3）1#变速箱半轴齿轮的成分正常。The chemical composition of the driveshaft gear is normal (gearbox 1). 
（4）失效卡簧的硬度和组织正常。The microstructure and hardness of the clamp is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检核载荷。Check the load. 
（2）控制好工艺参数，保证渗碳层深度符合技术要求。Control the process and assure the carburization layer depth 
meets the requirement.
