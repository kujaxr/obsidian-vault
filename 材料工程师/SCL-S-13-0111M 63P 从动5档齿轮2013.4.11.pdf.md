Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）齿轮是由于疲劳而开裂。这是由于返工中，盖板背面粘附有螺纹锁固密封胶，使得局部凸起，与齿轮端面
摩擦，出现高温，最终导致齿根萌生裂纹源，出现断裂。The gear was broken by fatigue. During reworking, the 
adhesive adhered to the surface of the cover，which made the local protuberance, and then the side surface of the gear 
rubbed with the cover and generated high temperature. This made the crack origin come into existence and finally the 
gear was failed. 
（2）齿轮材料的成分，表面硬度，芯部硬度以及淬硬层厚度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical composition, surface 
hardness, core hardness and the hardening depth meet the requirement; the microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）修订PFMEA 文件，避免类似事情的发生。Emend PFMEA to avoid the similar case happening. 
（2）制定并执行合理的返工程序。Establish and carry out the reasonable rework process .
