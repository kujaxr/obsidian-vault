Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）支架是由于疲劳而断裂的。The bracket was broken through fatigue. 
（2）材料的成分和强度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical compositions and strength meet the requirement and the 
microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核支架的载荷。Check the load of the bracket. 
（2）检查装配点是否松动。Check if the point of the assembly was loose.
