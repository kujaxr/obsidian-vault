Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件受高温后皮纹消失，受异物朝同一方向刮动后形成变形形貌。 
After the parts were exposed to high temperature, the dermatoglyph disappeared, and the 
deformation morphology was formed when the unknown object scraped in the same direction. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement.
