Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受异常外力导致断裂。  
2. 失效件材料符合技术要求，注塑工艺不佳。  
Conclusions:  1. The brittle fracture of the parts caused by abnormal force. 
2. Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements, with poor injection molding 
process.  
建议： 1. 供应商改善注塑工艺。 
Suggestions:  1. Supplier improves the injection molding process.
