Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件系供应商试制件异常流出所致，其中钥匙垫材料不符合技术要求。 
The failure parts were caused by the abnormal outflow of the supplier's trial parts, in which the 
key pad material does not confirm to requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions：1.责令供应商对试制件流出问题做出解释并整改。 
                   Let the supplier explain and rectify the outflow of trial parts.
