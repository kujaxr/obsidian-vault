Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1．失效扭转梁属疲劳失效，裂源位于横梁内侧边缘高应力区域，裂源位置有磕碰凹坑；failure part is fatigue fracture 
started at torsion beam inside edge where endure high stress; 
2．失效后桥弹簧盘加强板与横梁本体焊缝收弧段延伸方式不符合图纸要求；the carter area of the weld-seam 
between spring reinforcement plate and torsion beam did not conform to drawing requirement; 
3．横梁开裂侧内加强板与横梁本体焊缝、弹簧盘加强板与横梁焊缝熔深、焊高等检测结果合格；related weld-seam 
penetration test result OK; 
4．失效后桥横梁材料正常；failure torsion beam material chemical composition mechanical properties test result OK; 
建议Suggestions： 
1．避免横梁高应力区域磕碰伤，加强横梁边缘质量检查；avoid knock defect in high stress area of the torsion beam; 
2．供应商需要采取措施保证焊接工艺和焊接质量符合图纸要求；Supplier should guarantee the weld process and 
quality conform to drawing requirement; 
3．工程分析弹簧盘加强板与横梁本体焊缝收弧段延伸方式对横梁应力及载荷分布的影响；Engineering should 
make CAE analysis for the loading difference between different weld-seam extend process;
