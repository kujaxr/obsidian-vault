Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 后扭转梁是由于疲劳而开裂的，局部应力较大是导致其开裂的主要原因。Rear torsion beam was broken by 
fatigue and the big stress was the main reason. 
2. 失效件横梁与加强板焊缝延伸长度及角度均不符合图纸要求。The length and angle of welding seam between the 
beam and the strength plate didn’t meet the technical requirement. 
3. 零件成分、板厚符合要求，组织及硬度正常。Chemical components and the plate thickness met the requirement. 
Microstructure and hardness were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 追溯售后市场与失效件同批次或临近几次批次的产品，评估其风险；Trace the same batch with the failled parts or 
near several batches of the products after sale market and evaluate the risk. 
2. 检查当时的生产记录，确认焊缝与图纸不符的原因；Check the process record and make sure the reason that the 
welding seam didn’t meet the drawing.
