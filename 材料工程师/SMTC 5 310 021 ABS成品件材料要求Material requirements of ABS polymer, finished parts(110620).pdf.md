enable SMTC to effectively control material  
requirements of ABS polymer, finished 
parts, and provide material basis for the 
communication and quality evaluation of 
design, quality control, purchasers, and 
suppliers of passenger cars of SMTC.

     高冲击韧性，适用于易于注塑的零件，例

    High impact resistant ABS graft polymer, 
for injection molded parts which are easy to 
manufacture, e.g. seat side trim panels.

stability up to 90 °C for parts that are subject

sign, e.g. radiator guard.

盖板。 
ABS graft polymer with dimensional 
stability up to 100 °C for parts the fitting po-
sition of which requires greater dimensional 
stability under heat, e.g. glove compartment 
lid.

4  General requirements 
4.1 外观要求 
4.1
Appearance requirements
成品件表面与内部必须完全没有像流线、裂
纹、缩孔等类似空缺位置和制造缺陷。只有当凹
陷不影响功能与外观和/或可安装性时，才允许
在筋条与加强筋的安装范围内有凹陷。成品件必
须可以保障无缺陷安装。在仓储与使用后，不允
许出现非抗静电的塑料所特有的积尘现象。 
    Surface and interior of the finished parts 
shall be free of flaws and processing defects 
such as flowlines, voids, cracks or similar 
faults. Sink marks at the base of ribs and 
reinforcements are permissible only if they 
do not adversely affect either the function or 
appearance and/or assembly of the part. The 
finished parts shall permit proper assembly. 
After storage and use, they shall not exhibit 
the dust accumulation which is characteristic 
for non-antistatic plastics.

6.4  耐光照性能 
 
6.4
Light fastness
    如果在图纸中对光照周期数目未作规定，则
适用于下列规定： 
a)  在非阳光直接照射区域中的构件为三  
个光照周期； 
b)  在阳光直接照射区域中的构件为五个
光照周期（例如：车门胸区）； 
c)  阳光强烈照射区域中的构件为十个光
照周期（例如：后搁板）。 
 
    If the number of exposure periods is not 
defined in the drawing, the following rule ap-
plies: 
a)  3 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to indirect sun radia-
tion; 
b)  5 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to direct sun radiation 
(e.g., door upper trim); 
c) 10 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to the highest sun 
radiation (e.g., rear shelf).
