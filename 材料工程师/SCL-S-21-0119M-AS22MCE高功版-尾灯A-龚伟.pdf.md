Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 零件受高温后鼓泡失效。 
The failure part bubbled under high temperature.  
2. 失效件材料不符合技术要求，但不是失效的主要原因。 
The performance of the failure part material didn’t conform to requirement, but it is not the main 
reason of failure. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 调查高温来源。 
                 Check the source of high temperature. 
2. 供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
                 Suppliers explain material problems and rectify.
