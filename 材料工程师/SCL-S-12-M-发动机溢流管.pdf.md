Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论：零件材料性能符合要求。断口分析可得，发动机溢流管受到意外损伤，导致破裂泄漏。  
Conclusions: The material perfomance of the hose conforms to the requirments.Throuth the fracture analysis, 
we can get the conclusion that the hose was injured accidently by external damage which caused the leakage. 
 
建议：在胶管运输和安装过程中，注意胶管不受外界损伤。 
Suggestion: Avoid external damage on the transport and installation process of the hose.

2 断口分析（Analyze）： 
对胶管裂纹进行分析： 
 
断口表面橡胶基体平整，无杂质、气泡等异常现象。异常裂纹纹路笔直，结合故障公里数，可排除材料问题引起的破裂。 
The fracture surface of the rubber is smooth. There are no impurities, bubbles and other anomalies on the fracture. Considering 
the car mileage of the failure and the straight abnormal crack, we can get that the damage dose not caused by the material. 
 
 
胶管中间编织层完好，没有破损，没有出现被冷却液浸泡后出现的发黑现象，排除内层胶管爆裂引起的破裂。 
The middle woven layer does not broken and appears black which is caused by immersed by coolant. So the damage does not 
caused by the burst of the inner hose.
