Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1）经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
      2）装配时，螺栓安装倾斜，致使零件熔合区最薄弱部位受力开裂 
Conclusions： After the tests，the materials of the retainer assy sunvisor conforms to the drawing requirement. 
According to the macroscopic observation and material test of retainer assy sunvisor，the bolt was not 
installed in place.So the failture occurred at the weld zone（the weakest part）. 
    建议： 改善装配和注塑工艺 
Suggestions：Improve the assembly and injection molding process
