1 范围 
本标准规定了在螺钉、螺栓、螺母，以及类
似螺纹部件上进行薄层锌磷化的要求。 
本标准适用于评估螺钉、螺栓、螺母，以及
类似螺纹部件上的薄层锌磷化状态。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the surface pro-
tection requirements for thin-layer zinc phos-
phating on screws, bolts, nuts and similar 
threaded parts. 
This standard is adopted to evaluate the 
surface protection situation for thin-layer zinc 
phosphating on screws, bolts, nuts and similar 
threaded parts.

3.2 外观与结构 
3.2  Appearance and structure 
深灰色至黑色。薄层锌磷化必须是均一的结
晶结构，具有很强的附着性。不允许存在盐以及
粉末残留物。 
Dark gray to black. The thin-layer zinc 
phosphating must be uniform and crystalline in 
structure and exhibit firm adherence. Salt and 
powder residues are not permitted. 
3.3 后处理 
3.3  Post-treatment 
为提高防腐保护以及摩擦性能，锌磷化部件
可进行后处理。 
To improve the corrosion protection and 
friction properties, the zinc phosphated parts 
can be posttreated.

3.5 耐腐蚀性 
3.5  Corrosion resistance 
根据标准ISO 9227-NSS 进行试验。要求：
在无螺纹表面试验6 h后无基体金属腐蚀或者在
螺纹区域出现最大1%的金属腐蚀。 
Testing according to ISO 9227-NSS. Re-
quirement: 6 hour with no base metal corrosion 
on thread-free surfaces and/or max. 1% base 
metal corrosion in the threaded area.
