Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是过载引起的断裂。The failure for the part duo to overloading. 
2、 零件材料性能符合技术要求。The parts material properties met the technical requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 进行新部件的螺栓和螺纹尺寸研究。Carry out dimension study of new parts’ bolt and thread. 
2、 请检查装配、拆解时载荷情况。Check the loading conditions during the assembly and dismantling.
