Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1、零件受外力超过负荷导致失效。 
       2、驾驶员安全带高度调节器失效件的材料符合质量要求。新件不符合原材料厂商质量要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The part was overloaded, which led to the failure. 
2.The material performance of the failure seat belt height adjuster conformed to requirements，while the 
new parts didn’t. 
建议： 对供应商使用的原料和牌号进行核查。  
Suggestions：The raw material and trademark used by supplier might be checked.
