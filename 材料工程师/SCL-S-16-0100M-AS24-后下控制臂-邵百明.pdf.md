Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）后下控制由于局部应力较大而发生双向多源疲劳开裂。The rear control arm was cracked by bending multi source fatigue 
because of a big local stress. 
（2）后下控制臂成分，焊缝和板厚均符合要求；下表面起源处的材料组织和硬度与基材接近，无明显变化。The 
chemical components, weld and thickness meet the requirements; the lower surface origin microstructure and hardness is 
close to the base material and without obvious change.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化局部设计，降低应力，如延长焊缝，避免焊趾处于应力集中的部位。Optimize the local design to reduce the 
stress, such as the extension of the weld to avoid welding toe in the stress concentration location.
