Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 下系杆开裂（SVG309）属于过载开裂，引起过载开裂的主要原因是原材料存在分层缺陷，层与层之间无
冶金结合，且骨架强度不足；造成强度不足和不均的原因是原材料热处理不当引起的。The bowstring 
cracking (SVG311) belongs to the overload fracture, mainly caused by overload fracture is raw material has no 
delamination defects, metallurgical bonding between layers,the strength for the part is insufficient. The cause of 
insufficient strength and uneven raw materials caused by improper heat treatment. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 隔离本批次零件。The isolation of this batch of parts. 
2、 供应商完善过程质量控制。The supplier to improve the process quality control.  
3、 请校核失效件的实际工况载荷。To check the actual load of the failure parts
