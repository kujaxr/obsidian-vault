Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 四件锁扣杆的硬度不符图纸合要求（太低）。 The hardness of the four components’ pin does not meet the 
requirements of the drawing (too low). 
2. 杆A 和C 硬度不符合供应商的硬度规范 （太高）。杆B 和D 符合供应商的规范。 The hardness of pins A & C 
do not meet the supplier’s hardness specification. Pins B & D do meet the supplier’s specification. 
3. 和A，B，C 件相同 （SCL-S-12-0135M），D 件的杆板接触区域也表现出明显地微动磨损。 As with parts A, B 
& C (SCL-S-12-0135M), Part D also shows significant fretting wear at the pin-plate contact area. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 为了减少磨损，应增加杆和底板的硬度和接触面积和。To reduce wear, the surface hardness and contact area of the 
pin and plate should be increased. 
2. 零件图纸必须包含硬度规范。The relevent hardness specification must be included on the component drawing.

材料分析Material analysis 
 
根据图纸，材料的硬度应符合标准G4304 SUS302 或304。但是，供应商采用低的硬度范围标准。  
对四个杆的维氏硬度进行了测试，并使用ISO 18265，转换成HRB。结果见下面的表格。 
注：由于材料的不均匀性，测量位置的不同，硬度结果会有所不同。硬度的测量点为杆部的中心：A 是纵截面的
中心；B，C 及D 是横截面的中心。 
 
According to the drawing, the material hardness should be in line with SUS302 or SUS304 in standard G4304.  
However, the supplier uses an internal standard with a much lower hardness range. 
The vickers hardness of the four pins were tested and converted to HRB using ISO 18265.  The results are shown in the 
table below.  
Note: Due to the inhomogenous nature of the material, the hardness results will vary depending on where on the part the 
measurement was taken.  The following measurements were taken in cross section, in the central shaft of the pin.  A 
was longitudinal cross section, B, C & D transverse cross-section.
