Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）由于过热，5#瓦盖螺栓受到工作应力而发生松脱，使得后续6#，7#，8#瓦盖和3 根螺栓在较大载荷的情况
下依次发生断裂，最终导致凸轮轴在较大载荷下断裂。Because of overheating, the bolts of bearing caps #5 was pulled from the 
cylinder head. Consequently, bearing caps 6#, 7#, 8# and 3 bolts broke in turn under the abnormal load and finally the camshaft was also broken.  
（2）凸轮轴断裂部位的石墨形态和基体组织不满足图纸的要求，但不是导致凸轮轴断裂的原因。The graphite shape 
and the matrix microstructure of the camshaft close to the fracture doesn’t meet the drawing requirement, but this didn’t influence the failure. 
（3）凸轮轴的成分和断裂部位基体硬度合格。The chemical components and hardness of the camshaft is good. 
（4）螺栓的成分，硬度满足产品要求；组织正常。The chemical components and the hardness of the bolt meets the technical 
requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）DRE 和实验工程师从实验过程以及产品的角度进一步查找导致油压下降的原因。DRE and testt engineer further look 
for the reason for oil pressure decreasing. 
（2）让凸轮轴供应商对工艺过程进行分析，查找材料组织不合格的原因，并在生产中进行严格控制，保证材料的
组织满足要求。Let the supplier of the camshaft analyize the reason basing on the manufacturing process , controls the process seriously and ensures the 
micostructure meets the requirement.        
（3）对于将来的发动机失效产品，需要让试验室检查整个发动机，这样能获得更多重要的信息，如疲劳断裂的方
向，相关部件的磨损情况。 For future engine failures, the laboratory must be allowed to examine the whole engine.  This can give much 
more important information, such as direction of fatigue fractures, wear patterns on related components etc.
