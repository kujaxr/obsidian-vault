Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 横梁上存在纵向原始缺陷引发疲劳失效。 
   Rear torsion beam was cracking though fatigue because of longitudinal primitive defect. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查横梁焊管焊接拉拔、液压成型过程，查找产生缺陷的原因、避免再次发生。 
  Check the forming process such as welding、drawing、forming and find the reason of defect to avoid happen 
again.
