Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件内应力过大，使用中开裂。 
The internal stress of the failed parts was too large, cracking during using.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement.. 
建议Suggestions： 1.供应商对灯罩应力过大原因作出解释并整改。 
                 The supplier explains the reason for the excessive stress of the lampshade and rectifies.
