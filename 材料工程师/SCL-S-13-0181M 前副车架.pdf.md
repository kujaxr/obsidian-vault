Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 前副车架总成开裂位置中，左纵臂与套管焊接热影响区先断裂后，引起右纵臂与套管焊缝处载荷和振动加剧，
使其发生疲劳断裂；The front subframe assembly failed due to the left longitudinal arm and tube first broken at welding, 
resulted in the load and shake grave on welding seam of the right and fatigue fracture later.  
2. 前副车架总成上的左、右纵臂与套管焊缝数量及布置不符合图纸要求，且焊缝中存在熔深不够的现象；此可
能降低该焊缝抵抗开裂的能力；The quantity and distribution of welding seam didn’t meet the drawing requirement and 
inadequate penetration existed, which could reduce the ability of the weld cracking resistance. 
3. 右支架上裂纹在副车架总成失效之前就已存在，可能是支架成型过程中冲压变形所致；The crack at the 
bracket existed before the front subframe failure, might result from the stamping deformation. 
4. 右支架结构最大减薄率为45%，不符合图纸要求(≤18%)；The maximum thinning rate is 45%, which didn’t 
meet the requirement(≤18%). 
5. 套管的微观组织无异常，化学成分不符合图纸要求；副车架板材微观组织和抗拉强度无异常，化学成分、厚
度符合要求；Microstructure of tube was normal, chemical components didn’t meet the requirement; Microstructure and 
tensile strength of subframe were normal, chemical components and thickness met the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商应严格按照图纸进行焊接，并保证焊接质量符合要求；The supplier should weld according to the 
drawing. Made sue the welding quality met the requirement. 
2. 工程校核实际载荷，优化焊接结构；Check the welding stress and optimized welding strength. 
3. 建议供应商优化其支架成形工艺；Advice that the suppliers optimized the forming process of bracket. 
4. 确认实际使用的套管材质与图纸一致；Make sure the actual tube according the drawing.
