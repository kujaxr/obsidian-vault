Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是原材料裂纹引起的断裂。 
The failure caused by the crack of raw materials. 
2、失效件的化学成份、硬度不符合技术要求。 
The chemical composition and hardness for the failure did not meet the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 立即停止使用本批次螺栓。 
Immediately stop using the batch bolt. 
   2、供应商提交分析、整改措施。 
Suppliers to submit analysis, corrective measures.
