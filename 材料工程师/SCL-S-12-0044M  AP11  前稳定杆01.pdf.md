Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是从角内的凹坑产生应力集中引起的疲劳断裂，瞬断区约占断口区域的一半，表明稳定杆受到的
载荷比较高。Failure is fatigue fracture beginning from stress concentration at a depression at the inside bend.  
The final fracture area is almost half of the shaft diameter, indicating that the applied load is relatively high.  
2、 失效件材料性能符合技术要求。The material performance for the failed part meet the technical requirements. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强过程质量控制，如热成型，使圆角过度平滑。Strengthen the process of quality control, such as hot 
forming process, to make the radius smooth. 
2、 利用CAE 对稳定杆进行受力分析。Check applied stress by CAE.
