Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、由于超载使排气门发生缩颈，在高温和应力作用下最终由外向内发生沿晶蠕变断裂；The Exhaust Valves 
necking due to overload, final occurrence intergranular creep rupture from outside to inside under the high 
temperature and stress; 
2、排气门在高温工作后，盘杆部局部区域晶界上析出的过多颗粒状、层片状碳化物，使钢材的热强性和韧性发生
下降。After high temperature work, the local region have serious particulate, lamellar carbides gathered 
precipitation of the Exhaust Valves panels and lever, the hot strength and toughness of steel decline. 
3、排气门化学成分、硬度符合技术要求。the chemical composition, hardness of the Exhaust Valve conform to the 
technical requirements 
 
建议Suggestions: 
1、 检查产生过大轴向力的原因。Check the reason of large axial force.

结论：1、从断面形貌可以确定，该排气门杆部为沿晶断裂；From the fracture surface can determine, the Exhaust 
Valves Lever Crossing-Section is intergranular fracture; 
      2、结合图2 与3-E 可以判断，较大的轴向力使排气门缩颈（该力已超过材料的屈服强度），最终由外向内
发生沿晶蠕变断裂。Binding to Fig2&Fig3-E can be judged, the large axial force prompted the Exhaust Valves 
necking（The force has to exceed the yield strength of the material）, final occurrence the intergranular creep 
rupture from outside to inside.

三、材料分析Materials Analysis 
1、 排气门杆部硬度符合图纸技术要求（见下表）；The hardness of Exhaust valve lever conform to the drawing 
technical requirements（see table below）; 
2、 排气门盘部化学成分、硬度、显微组织（非工作区）符合技术要求（见下表）；The chemical composition, 
hardness, Microstructure （non workspace ）of the Exhaust Valve panels conform to the technical 
requirements（see table below）; 
3、 排气门盘部堆焊合金层厚度符合图纸技术要求（见下表及图14）；The surfacing alloy layer thickness of the 
Exhaust Valve panels conform to the drawing technical requirements（see table below and Fig14）; 
4、 排气门杆部（图5/6/8）、排气门盘锥部（图10/11/12/13）的显微组织均主要为奥氏体+深色铬氮化合物，同时
局部区域有严重的颗粒状、层片状碳化物聚集析出，为高温工作后组织。The microstructure of the Exhaust 
Valves lever（Fig5/6/8）, panels mainly Austenite and Dark Cr-N compound, and the local region have 
serious particulate, lamellar carbides gathered precipitation, the organization for after high temperature 
work. 
 
结论：从显微组织来看，在高温工作后，排气门盘杆部晶界有严重的颗粒状、层片状碳化物聚集析出，使钢
材的热强性和韧性发生下降。Conclusion: From the microstructure, after high temperature work, the local region 
have serious particulate, lamellar carbides gathered precipitation of the Exhaust Valves panels and lever, the 
hot strength and toughness of steel decline.
