Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1）前门上部框架局部拉延区域出现集中性塑性变形而开裂。The local of the front door upper frame was cracked 
because of the concentration of the plastic deformation. 
（2）失效件的材料成分和性能符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and properties meet the requirements 
and the microstructure is normal. 
（3）由于返工的焊缝较短，局部还存在裂纹。Because the rework weld is short, there is a short crack at the local. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查模具（如过渡圆角）和冲压工艺，查找集中塑性变形的原因。Check the mold (such as the transition fillet) 
and stamping process to find the reasons for the concentration of the plastic deformation. 
（2）延长修补的焊缝，覆盖拉延区及其过渡区。Extend the weld to cover the extension zone and transition zone. 
（3）请工程评估返工件的风险。Please DRE evaluate the risk for the rework parts.
