Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 点火开关ST 铜片发生电化学腐蚀，导致接触不良，电化学腐蚀原因与壳体材料、零件结构有关。 
   ST copper in the ignition switch happen electrochemical corrosion, results in poor contact. The cause of 
electrochemical corrosion is related to shell material and structure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化壳体结构或更改壳体材料，提高可靠性并更换售后市场零件。 
Optimise the structure or change the shell material to inprove reliability and replace after-sale’s parts.
