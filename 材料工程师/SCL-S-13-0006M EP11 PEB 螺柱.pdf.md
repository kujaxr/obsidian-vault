Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 失效螺柱属于过载断裂。 
The bolts all failed due to overloading. 
2. 失效螺柱材质不符合要求，仅相当于15 钢；强度低于供应商报告中样品测试值。 
The material of bolts is wrong，which equal to 15#；And the tensile strength is below the value from supplier’s 
report. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 请立即封存隔离该批零件并进行追溯排查。 
Please seal、isolate、trace and investigate on the bolts immediately.  
2. 供应商选用正确的材料，保证螺柱强度。 
Suppliers choose the right material and ensure the bolt strength. 
3. 图纸上对螺柱的强度等级、摩擦系数等要求定义明确。 
Make clear the requirement such as bolt grade、friction factor and so on. 
4. 按照图纸要求扭矩进行装配。 
Assembly accord to the torque requirements from drawings.
