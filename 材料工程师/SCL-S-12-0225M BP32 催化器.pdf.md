Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）催化器是由于疲劳而开裂；裂纹源产生于法兰的焊缝焊指处，然后向热影响区扩展。The catalytic convertor 
failed through fatigue. The crack origin was generated at the weld toe of the flange and spread to the HAZ.  
（2）材料成分，组织，硬度以及厚度正常。The chemical composition, microstructure, hardness and thickness are 
normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核载荷；Check the applied load and vibration. 
（2）检测温度；Check the temperature.
