Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions: 
1、 漏油是由于异物存在导致密封不严所致。Oil spills are due to a foreign body lead Mifengbuyan exists. 
2、 螺栓下法兰面的压痕与垫片的压痕痕迹形状、大小基本一致，系同一异物所致。因该异物已丢失，故
无法判断其具体成份和来源。The indentation of the bolt indentation and spacer traces shape, size, consistent 。
Have been lost because of the foreign body, it is unable to determine the specific ingredients and sources 
3、 通过能谱分析，异物来源于螺栓表面涂覆之后，装配之前。By EDS analysis, foreign matter from the bolt 
surface coating before assembly.
