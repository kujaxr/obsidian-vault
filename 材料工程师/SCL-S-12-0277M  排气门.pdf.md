Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 气门盘锥因冲蚀而出现失效。 The valve has failed through erosion of valve seat material. 
2、 气门表面大量的堆积物中的Ca、Zn、P 来自于机油燃烧。 The buildup of the valve surface of Ca, Zn, P may 
be derived from oil combustion. 
3、 气门材料性能无异常。The material properties for the part is normal. 
议Suggestions： 
1、 检查发动机烧机油的原因。To find the reasons for the combustion engine oil. 
2、 请提供更全面的零部件以利于失效分析。Please provide more comprehensive parts and components in order to 
facilitate failure analysis.
