Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、堵塞断裂由冷镦裂纹引起的。The Plug fracture is caused by cold heading crack. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离该批次零件；Isolate this batch of parts. 
2、供应商加强冷镦工艺质量管控，防止不合格品流出。The supplier shall strengthen the quality control of cold heading 
process to prevent the outflow of nonconforming products.
