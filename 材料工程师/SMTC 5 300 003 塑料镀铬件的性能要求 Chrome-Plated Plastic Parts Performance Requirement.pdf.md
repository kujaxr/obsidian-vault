前言 
为规范塑料上电镀装饰性铜/镍/铬电镀层的
要求，特制定本标准。 
本标准是对SMTC 5 300 003—2011（V1）
的修订,自本标准实施之日起废除 SMTC 5 300 
003—2011（V1）。 
本标准与SMTC 5 300 003—2011（V1）相
比主要变化如下： 
——变更了规范性引用文件 
——修改了镀铬层厚度 
——修改了镀镍层最小厚度要求，增加了各
镍层占总镍层百分比的要求

——用反向锯割试验替代划格试验

electrodeposited Cu-Ni-Cr coatings that are 
applied to plastics, this standard is formulated.

– 2011 (V1). This standard will replace SMTC 
5 300 003 – 2011 (V1) from the implementation 
date. 
The main changes between this standard and 
SMTC 5 300 003 – 2011 (V1) are as follows: 
- 
modified normative documents 
- 
changed the requirement of chrome 
layer thickness 
- 
modified the minimum thickness re-
quirement of nickel layer, and increased 
requirement of each special nickel per-
centage of the total nickel 
- 
limited the potential range among nickel 
layers 
- 
improved the requirement of micropores 
- 
modified the storage temperature ac-
cording to the type of substrate 
- 
replaced the cross-cut test by back saw- 
grind test

displaced NSS test by CASS test

3.3  注塑件 
 
3.3  Injection molding parts 
3.3.1  基材 
只有上海汽车指定或批准的电镀级塑料才能
用于电镀。制造商选择合适的电镀级塑料作为电
镀基材应按照表1 的规定。电镀零件不允许使用
回收料。 
 
3.3.1  Substrate material 
Only plating grade plastics designated and ap-
proved by SAIC Motor for chrome plating shall 
be used. Plating glade plastics used by the 
substrate manufacturer as material suitable for 
electroplating according to Table1. No regrind is 
allowed in plated parts. 
3.3.2  素材 
素材表面光洁，不应出现可能影响外观的流
纹、裂纹、毛刺、缩印、基材分层。电镀之前，
素材必须已通过合适的方式充分释放应力。注塑
商和电镀商必须通过合适的措施来保证素材无应
力。不允许使用脱模剂。表面也不应含有油脂和
汗渍。没有上海汽车的书面同意，电镀前不允许
对零件进行机械抛光、砂纸打磨等方式对素材进
行处理。如有偏差必须与上海汽车相关职能部门
商定。 
 
3.3.2  Molded part 
The surface of molded parts shall smooth, free 
of flow lines, cracks, burr, sink marks, craters or 
substrate separations, which may influence the 
appearance. Molded parts must be stress re-
lieved prior to plating. The molded parts are free 
of stress that shall be ensured by the person 
responsible for injection molding and the person 
responsible for electroplating. Do not use mold 
parting agents. Surface shall also contain no 
oil/fat or any human sweat. Rework of substrate 
surfaces by mechanically polishing, sanding or 
other means prior to coating is prohibited with-
out written consent from SAIC Motor. If there is 
any deviation must be agreed with SAIC Motor 
related functional departments. 
3.4   成品件 
供货商对成品件的质量责任。 
 
3.4   Finished part  
The supplier is responsible for the quality of the 
finished parts.  
3.4.1  外观 
通过仪器测量、目视比较或两者结合，零件
的光泽和颜色必须与已认可的原型样品板或样件
一致。必要时由上海汽车来规定极限样品。电镀
的接触点应当选择在安装状态下不可见的位置。
重要面不允许有影响防腐或外观的明显可见的电
镀缺陷，如鼓泡、凹坑、粗糙、裂纹、漏镀、污
渍、变色等。重要面通常是可见的，已完成装配
的车上零件直接或通过反射可见的部分，或其腐
蚀产物会污损整车上可见面。 
 
3.4.1  Appearance 
The compliance of the component with the re-
leased original sample chart or the original 
sample part in terms of gloss and color shall be 
verifiable using measuring instruments and/or 
shall be visually verifiable. If necessary, maxi-
mum deviation samples must be specified by 
SAIC Motor. The contact points for electroplat-
ing shall be positioned in a way that flaws can-
not be recognized in the installed condition. 
Over the significant surface, there shall be no 
clearly visible plating defects such as blisters, 
pits, roughness, cracks, non-plated areas,

3.4.2  镀层厚度 
确定每一独特零件的镀层分布情况是供应商
的责任。为了所有零件重要面的最小镀层厚度能
持续地满足规定要求，挂具可能需要采用许多特
殊加工工艺，比如，采用辅助阳极和（或）遮蔽
措施，减少每挂的零件数量等。可以用切片法（ISO 
1463）或库仑法（ISO 2177）来测量镀层厚度。
如果买方和供应商之间存在分歧，切片法应该作
为测量镍和铜层的首选方法。X 射线测厚法(ISO 
3497)可以使用，但在铜层和镍层厚度太高的情况
下可能只限于测量镀铬层的厚度。 
 
3.4.2  Plating thickness 
It is the supplier’s responsibility to identify the 
plating thickness distribution for each unique 
part. In order to consistently meet the specified 
minimum plating thickness on all significant 
surfaces, plating racks may be required to em-
ploy many special processing techniques, e.g., 
the use of auxiliary anodes and/or shields, re-
ducing the number of parts per rack etc. The 
plating thickness shall be determined by the 
microsection method (ISO 1463) or coulometric 
method (ISO 2177). In case of disagreements 
between purchaser and supplier, the microsec-
tion method shall be the preferred method for Ni 
and Cu layers. X-Ray (ISO 3497) may be used 
but usage may be limited to chrome layer 
thickness if Copper and Nickel layer thickness is 
too high. 
3.4.3  镀层结合力 
 
镀层之间以及镀层与基材之间的结合力须满
足表3 的要求。推荐用总成件进行试验。如果条
件限制，不方便采用总成件进行试验，可以用完
整的零件进行试验。不允许对零件进行分割。在
开始试验前，零件必须在室温下存放24 h。试验
结束后，零件在室温存放1h 后再进行评定。 
 
3.4.3  Electroplating adhesion 
Adhesion between plated layers or plated layers 
and substrate must conform to the requirements 
in Table 3. Using assembly is recommended for 
tests. For certain constraints, it is not convenient 
to use assembly to test, complete parts are al-
lowed. The parts are not allowed to cut. Prior to 
testing, the parts shall be aged at room temper-
ature for 24 hours. After testing, the parts are 
stored at room temperature for 1h and then as-
sessed.

3.4.5  电镀过程 
 
零件满足供货要求后，制造商可锁定电镀工
艺参数（包括电镀时间、电流、装载量等）。批产
产品必须在电镀程序和镀层厚度上与认可的首批
样品一致。 
 
3.4.5  Electroplating process  
When the parts meet the supply requirements, 
the manufacturer can lock the plating process 
parameters (including plating time current, load 
etc). Standard-production parts shall correspond 
to released first-sample parts in the coating 
procedure and coating thickness.  
3.4.6  电镀零件的工艺要求 
电镀零件的工艺要求见表3。零件应该根据表
3 的要求进行PPAP 测试。每一个测试项目至少需
要3 个零件，分别代表挂具上低、中、高电流密
度区域。挂具的右上方、中间和左下方的零件应
优先被检测。表3 中规定的最小镀层厚度适用于
所有重要面。

electroplating part  
Mechanical requirements for the electroplating 
to see Table 3. Parts shall be tested according 
to the PPAP test definition in Table 3. For each 
test a minimum of 3 parts representing low,

transversely over the rack, e.g. right top, center,

surfaces.
