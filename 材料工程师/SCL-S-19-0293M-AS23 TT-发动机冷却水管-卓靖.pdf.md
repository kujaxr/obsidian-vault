Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
Conclusion：1、胶管硫化工艺中芯棒对胶管内壁形成损伤，后伤痕贯穿胶管后导致泄漏。 
The failure part was damaged by the mandrel from the inner side during the hose vulcanization process, 
then the crack propagates through the hose wall and the leak occurred. 
 
Suggestion：1、加强硫化工艺过程中胶管上下芯棒工序的管控。 
Strengthen the control of the mandrel input and the mandrel extraction during the hose vulcanization 
process.

IV. 测试结果 Test Results  
1 外观分析Appearance analysis： 
如图1 所示，失效件开裂区域外表面一处开裂，裂口周围无其他异常损伤；内表面可见两处裂口，有多处损
伤（沿胶管内壁一圈），并有明显的划伤痕贯穿裂口，初步判断胶管首先受到内部损伤，裂纹扩展方向由内及外。 
As shown in Fig. 1, a crack can be observed on the outer surface of the failed part and no other abnormal damage 
around the crack can be seen. Two cracks, multiple scratch around the inner wall of the hose, and scratches across the 
crack can be observed on the inner surface. It can be preliminarily determined that the cracks were caused initially on the 
inner surface of the part, and then propagate from inner surface to outer surface.

crack is caused by the scratch on the inner side of the hose.

对损伤区域进行分析，如图3 所示，可见大小不同的凹坑，沿损伤区域一周分布，且凹陷的区域断面光滑，
主要为受外力使得橡胶快速剥离所致。结合零件工艺过程，推断凹坑及损伤应为胶管硫化时芯棒对内壁形成损伤
所致。 
Then the damage area is examined. As shown in Fig. 3, multiple pits with varied size can be seen around the 
damaged area. The surface of the concave area is smooth, which is mainly caused by the rapid peeling of rubber caused by 
external force. According to the part manufacturing process, it is inferred that the damage must be caused by the mandrel 
during hose vulcanization process.
