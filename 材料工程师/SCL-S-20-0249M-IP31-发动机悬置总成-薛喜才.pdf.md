Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1.  铸件内存在热态裂纹，在受到冲击后发生过载断裂。Hot cracks are observed in castings and fractured though 
overload after impact.  
2.  材料成分、硬度满足要求，孔隙正常。Chemical components、hardness meet the requirements. Porosity is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查热态裂纹产生的原因并采取有效控制措施。Investigate the reason of hot cracks in the casting process 
and take effective control measures.
