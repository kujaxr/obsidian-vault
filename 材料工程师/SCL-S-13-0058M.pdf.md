Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 螺栓是由于过载引起的断裂。 
The failure of the parts due to overload.  
2、零件的材料性能符合图纸技术要求。 
The parts meet the technical requirements of the drawing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 校核装配工艺和检查装配过程。 
        Check PAM and the assembly process. 
2、 建议使用螺栓类的专用冷镦钢，如SCM435、ML35CrMo 等材料。 
    Recommended to use a bolt the dedicated cold heading steel, as the SCM435, ML35CrMo. 
3、 供应商应提高过程质量控制，如表面与芯部硬度差和螺牙缺陷。 
Suppliers should improve process quality control, such as hardenss difference for surface and core  
 and thread defects.
