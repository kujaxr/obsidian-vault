Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致脆断。  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求,含水率偏下限。  
Conclusions:  1.The brittle fracture of the part caused by external force.  
2. Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements; water content closed to the lower limit.     
建议： 1.对尼龙零件增加调湿工序。 
Suggestions:  1.Add humidity step of the nylon parts.
