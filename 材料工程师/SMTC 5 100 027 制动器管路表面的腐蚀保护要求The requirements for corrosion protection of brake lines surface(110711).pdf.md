1  Scope  
This standard specifies the requirements 
for corrosion protection of brake lines surface. 
This standard describes the requirements for 
the four different types of surface protection for 
lines in the hydraulic brake system: 
SMTC 5 100 027, type B Zinc / passiva-
tion / polyvinyl fluoride (PVF) coating, 
SMTC 5 100 027, type D Aluminum / 
passivation / polyamide (PA11) coating, 
SMTC 5 100 027, type E Zinc/ passiva-
tion/ polyamide (PA12) coating, 
SMTC 5 100 027, type F Aluminum / pas-
sivation / polyamide (PA12) coating. 
This standard is adopted to evaluate the 
corrosion protection of brake lines surface.

遵守国外相关法规，保护镀膜应完全满足该国家

ing to this standard shall completely fulfill the

testing. The protective coatings shall be free 
from pores, coarse cracks, damage or other 
flaws impairing the corrosion protection of the 
lines. Damage, folds and cracks in the plastic 
coating, particularly within the bend radii, are 
not permissible.

4 测试备注 
 
4  Notes on testing 
4.1 盐雾测试 
使用标准工具（弯曲装置）在安装前或一次
绕心轴（塑料或木，Ø 18 mm 至 20 mm）缠绕（360°，
节距约 120 mm）后在弯曲条件下测试。将试样悬
挂在测试室内进行老化。 
 
4.1 Salt spray fog test
Testing in bent condition ready for instal-
lation or after winding once (360°, pitch ap-
prox. 120 mm) around a mandrel (plastic or 
wood, Ø 18 mm to 20 mm) using a standard 
tool (bending device). The specimens are 
aged suspended in the test chamber. 
4.2 划痕线 
划痕线根据 DIN EN ISO 7253，第 8.5 节。
测试最好使用长度约为 300 mm 的直线部分。划痕
线长度应大约为 75 mm，并平行与所用试样的纵
轴。试样应放入测试室，与水平面成 60° 至 
75° 角，使划痕线朝上。如果没有足够长度的直
线部分可用或者弯曲区域，请将划痕线延长到外
径。应根据 DIN EN ISO 4628-8 在潮湿条件下评
估。 
 
 
4.2 Scribing line
Scribing line according to DIN EN ISO 
7253, Section 8.5. Straight line sections ap-
prox. 300 mm in length shall preferably be 
used for the test. The scribing line shall be 
approx. 75 mm in length and applied parallel to 
the longitudinal axis of the specimens. The 
specimens shall be placed in the test chamber 
at an angle of around 60° to 75° from the ho-
rizontal with the scribing line facing upwards. 
The scribing line shall be applied to the ex-
ternal radius if there are no straight line sec-
tions of sufficient length available or the bend 
area is to be checked. The evaluation shall be 
performed according to DIN EN ISO 4628-8 in 
moist condition. 
4.3 石头冲击测试 
根据 DIN 55996-1，方法 C 测试，但两个冲
击应力阶段之间无盐雾测试。 
 
 
4.3 Stone impact test
Testing according to DIN 55996-1, me-
thod C, but without salt spray test between the 
two impact stress phases.
