Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂。 
The SHIELD-DISS FRONT BRAKE have failed due to fatigue. 
    2、材料性能符合技术要求。The meterial meet the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查使用情况。Please check working condition 
2、 考虑提高设计强度。  Consider improving the design strength
