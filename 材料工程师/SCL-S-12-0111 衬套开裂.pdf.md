3 支架尺寸测量 (The dimension measurement of the bracket) 
 
 
从测试结果来看，失效件发生的左侧孔的尺寸存在偏小迹象，要求：20±0.2mm，实测：19.81，19.67，19.48，
19.75；同衬套上发现的异常压痕相吻合。 
From the result, the dimension of the left hole of the bracket is less than the requirement: requirement: 20±0.2mm，result：19.81，19.67，19.48，19.75；
which corresponds to the abnormal indentation on the bush. 
 
小结 (Conclusion)： 
1 衬套材料未发现异常。 
The material of the bush conforms to the requirement. 
2 失效衬套发现多处异常压痕，经检验，衬套支架尺寸存在偏小现象，同出现异常压痕现象吻合。可推断衬
套由于支架孔的尺寸偏小导致衬套收到过大压力，最终发生断裂。 
There is a lot of abnormal indentation is observed on the failure bush. After measurement, the dimension of the hole is less than the requirement, which 
corresponds to the abnormal indentation. We can deduce that the wrong dimension of the bracket hole caused the bush endure too big force in the use, which 
lead to the bush cracked.
