Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件的材料、硬度符合设计要求。 
      2．通过宏观分析和材料分析可以判定，饰条断裂的主要原因是装配时饰条唇边被弯折，在大气条件下
经历一段时间存放后，在弯折处发生应力开裂。 
Conclusions: 1. The material and hardness of the failure pipe conform to the design requirement. 
2. According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the hardtop seal, it can be seen that the 
hardtop seal was bended during assembly. After a period of use time, the hardtop seal occurred inner stress 
break at atmospheric environment.
