Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于弹簧次表面的大颗粒非金属夹杂物引起的疲劳断裂。 
Failure for the part belongs to fatigue fracture, due to the large non-metallic inclusions in second surface. 
2、 零件的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The material properties of parts meet the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强原材料质量控制。 
To strengthen the quality control for raw materials.
