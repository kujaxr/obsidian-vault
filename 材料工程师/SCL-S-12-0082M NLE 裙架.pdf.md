Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 搭子是由于过载而断裂的。The tightening point was broken because of overload. 
（2） 螺纹存在缺陷，但是基于来样无法确定其产生的原因。 The screw thread has some abnormality, but the reason 
is not clear based on the sample. 
（3）组织和硬度正常。The microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）DRE 应对其它部分的螺纹进行检查，并确认缺陷产生的原因。DRE should check other screw thread from the 
other parts and find out the reason. 
（2）校核设计强度。 Check the design strength.
