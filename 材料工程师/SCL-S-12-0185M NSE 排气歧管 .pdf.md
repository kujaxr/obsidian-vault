Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）排气歧管是由于局部热应力过大而疲劳开裂的。The exhaust manifold fatigue cracking is due to high local 
thermal stress.  
（2）组织符合技术要求；疏松正常。Microstructure meets the technical requirements; porosity normal. 
（3）硬度稍高于上限。Hardness is slightly higher than the upper limit. 
（4）壁厚不符合要求。The thickness doesn’t meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化排气歧管的设计，降低开裂处的应力。Optimize the design and reduce the stress of the crack region. 
（2）控制工艺过程，确保尺寸符合要求。Control the process and ensure the dimension meets the requirement.
