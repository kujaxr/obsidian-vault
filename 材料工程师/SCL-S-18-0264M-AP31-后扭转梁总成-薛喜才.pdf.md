Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 焊枪偏向后扭转梁侧，造成左后减震器安装支架侧焊高、熔深不足，焊缝强度减弱，较早发生断裂失效。 
The welding gun was partial to the side of the rear torsion beam, resulting in insufficient penetration and 
welding height and strength reducing of the left rear shock absorber mounting bracket, and early fracture failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 调整焊接角度，并加强对补焊工艺的监控。 
  Adjust the welding gun angle and strengthen controlling the repair welding process.
