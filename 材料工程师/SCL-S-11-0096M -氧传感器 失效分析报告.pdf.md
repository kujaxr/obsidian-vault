Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions:  
 螺纹被烧伤损坏(局部微型焊在安装/拆开扭紧的过程中). The thread has failed through galling (localized heating during 
tightening /loosening).   
 材料化学成分和硬度符合要求 The material composition and hardness meet the drawing requirements. 
 在紫外灯下看不见螺纹处有润滑剂. 要么供应商所使用的润滑剂无法在紫外灯下观察到, 或被焦烧了,要
么螺纹处没有润滑剂. Thread lubricant was not visible using UV light.  Either the lubricant has no UV marker, the temperature has 
burned it, or there was no lubricant present. 
   
建议Suggestions： 
 
，代理商必须注意.零件一定要在室温环境中安装,并且只能使用SAIC 批准的螺纹润滑剂. The servicing 
agent must take care when fitting this component.  The parts must be at room temperature & only SAIC-approved thread lubricants 
may be used. 
 
建议供应商用紫外反应的润滑剂Suggest supplier to use UV marked thread lubricant
