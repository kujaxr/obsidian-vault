Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 1#失效件未做热处理、强度不足导致过载断裂。 
   1# failed parts didn’t heat treatment, result in insufficient strength and fracturing in assembly. 
2. 2#、3#、4#零件未回火或回火不足、硬度偏高引起脆性断裂。 
   2# failed parts didn’t do heat treatment completely or not, result in high hardness and brittle cracking 
3. 抽取同批次和总成件螺栓硬度均偏高、组织异常。    
   Hardness of the same batch parts and assembly is high and microstructure is abnormal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 隔离同批次零件并对已装车零件进行风险评估。 
   Isolate the same batch parts and evaluate the risk. 
 2. 螺栓供应商加强生产过程监控，确保质量。 
Bolt supplier enhance controlling the process to make sure the quality.
