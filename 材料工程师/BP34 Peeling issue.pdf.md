BP34 Peeling issue
1. Issue
   Some of the parts using DNP EIS BUNAKO GRAIN 9825-2235 shows peeling at the edge after 72h at 90C heat exposure
2. Cause of the issue
   The peeling occurred between our top coat layer and printing layer.
   Also the peeling occurred at the edge of the parts, no peeling has observed on the flat surface.
   Adhesion test by tape peeling shows no issue at the flat surface.
   Having said that, we assume that the peeling was caused by residue strength in the film after vac forming.
   This residue strength was relieved by heat and made the sheet shrink. 
   Since the ABS backing is strongly bound to injected resin, the top coat shrank and peeled from printing layer.
   Please refer to our test result, showing this phenomenon.
3. Countermeasures
   Please use higher vac forming temperature. 
   The recommended sheet temperature is 180C.
