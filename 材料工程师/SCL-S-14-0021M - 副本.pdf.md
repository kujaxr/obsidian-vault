Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效是由于原材料表面存在表面裂纹，在后续热处理过程中加剧了裂纹的扩展，最终导致双头螺柱产生大的纵
向裂纹。Failure is due to the presence of surface cracks surface quality raw materials, in a subsequent heat 
treatment process exacerbated by crack propagation, resulting in a large cracks. 
2、失效件的芯部硬度超差（高于技术要求），无全脱碳、无螺牙折叠，金相组织无异常。Failure of parts of the core 
hardness poor tolerance (higher technical requirements), no full decarbonization, no thread folded, 
microstructure is no exception. 
3、未失效批次的零件表面无开裂，芯部硬度超差（高于技术要求），无全脱碳、无螺牙折叠，金相组织无异常。
Uncracked batches of parts of the surface without cracking, poor core hardness tolerance (higher technical 
requirements), no full decarbonization, no thread folded, microstructure is no exception. 
4、失效件和未失效件的化学成分无明显差异，符合技术要求。The chemical composition for failure parts and 
uncracked parts were no significant difference,that met the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、停用、隔离该零件。Disabled and isolate the part. 
2、检查整个生产过程的质量控制（包括原材料）。Quality control checks throughout the production process 
(including raw materials).
