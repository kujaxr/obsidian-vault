1 范围 
本标准规定了热固底色漆-清漆体系试验方
法、试验仪器、试验步骤及验收的要求。 
本标准适用于聚酯中涂上或直接应用于阴
极电泳漆上的热固底色漆-清漆。 
 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the test method of 
the thermosetting basecoat-clearcoat system, 
test instrument, test step and requirements of 
check and acceptance. 
 
This standard applies to the thermosetting 
basecoat-clearcoat paint on polyester primer 
or straight painted on CED.

6 Logistic requirements 
6.1 包装标签要求 
当产品以某种包装形式和容器供货时，每个
包装的顶部和四周必须清楚的标有以下信息： 
—— 供应商名称； 
—— 供应商参考信息； 
—— 生产批号； 
—— 生产日期； 
—— 储存期限； 
—— 参照的标准； 
—— 适当的法定警告标志。 
 
6.1 Marking of deliveries 
When materials are supplied in some 
packaging and containers, the following infor-
mation shall be clearly identified on the top and 
around of the body of each packaging. 
——Supplier’s name; 
——Supplier’s reference; 
——Batch number; 
——Data of manufacture; 
——The limit time of storage; 
——Rover Standard reference; 
——Legal warning labels as appropriate. 
6.2 供货控制要求 
 
6.2 Delivery control requirements 
6.2.1  供应商的责任：在大批量供货前，供应
商必须向相关工厂/质保中心实验室提供1L 样
品并附有一份书面自检报告。报告含有关于供应
商资料，用户信息，批号，制造日期以及参考规
范。 
 
6.2.1 Suppliers responsibility: Prior to making 
bulk deliveries, the supplier must provide the 
relevant works/paint laboratory with a quality 
assurance report. This shall be fully docu-
mented in respect of supplier’s reference, us-
er’s reference, batch number, date of manu-
facture and specification reference. 
 
6.2.2  运输和库存中的危险等级需在供货单上
注明。 
 
6.2.2 The danger grade in delivery and stock 
should be noted on the delivery sheet. 
 
6.2.3  每批产品须附带自检报告、批次板。批
次板需按照质保中心实验室的要求完成常规实
验（色相，附着力，抗石击），并提供给质保中
心实验室。 
 
6.2.3 Self check report、batch panels should 
be attached with every batches of product. The 
batch panels should do the regular test ac-
cording to the requirements of QA-Lab(color, 
adhesion, anti-stone chip ) and provide to the 
QA lab.
