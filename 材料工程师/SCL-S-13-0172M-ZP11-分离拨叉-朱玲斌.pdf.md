Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 分离拨叉的失效是由于铸造缺陷导致的疲劳断裂； The withdrawal fork failure is fatigue fracture due to the 
casting defects;  
（2） R 角出现不应存在的淬硬区，但它不是导致断裂的原因。There is a hardening zone in R angle which should 
not be exist, it is not the reason causing fracture.  
（3） 材料成分、基体硬度合格，组织正常。有效硬化层深度合格。The chemical components and the matrix 
hardness are qualified, the microstructure is normal.the effective hardening depth meet the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 加强铸造工艺及热处理工艺过程控制。To strengthen the process control of casting and heat treatment. 
（2） 总成供应商应作好子零件或分零件的质量控制。Assembly supplier should control the parts quality.
