Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件熔接痕处熔融不良，装配时开裂。 
The failure parts’ weld line was poor melting, cracking during assembly. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  供应商修模，以消除熔融不良问题。 
                    Supplier repairs the mold to eliminate the problem of poor melting.
