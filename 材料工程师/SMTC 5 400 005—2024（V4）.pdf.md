1   范围Scope 
 
  
本标准规定了整车及零部件的耐气候交变
试验。 
本标准适用于通过温湿度的循环交变，对整
车及零部件进行评估。例如，裂纹、变形、合成
材料的分离等。 
 
 
The Standard stipulates the test method and 
procedure, of Vehicle parts testing of resistance 
to environmental cycle test. 
 
 
This standard applies to the behavior of vehicle 
and parts during environmental cycle stressing 
by means of cycling temperature and moisture 
shall 
be 
assessed 
here 
(e.g. 
cracks, 
deformation, 
separation 
of 
the 
composite 
material, etc.). 
2  要求Requirements 
 
   
按相应图纸，SMTC零件材料技术要求和/或
供需双方约定的要求。 
 
According to drawing, SMTC material technical 
requirements and/or requirements agreed by 
supplier and demander side. 
3  测试Test 
3.1 定义Definition 
 
  
通过箱内温湿度变化，检验整车及零部件在
经历各种恒定或交变的极端气候条件下所发生
的变化，评判出零件和材料的耐气候性能。测试
方法分为：方法A 和方法B。方法A 推荐应用于
整车及内外饰零件，方法B 推荐应用于整车及发
动机舱零件。 
 
According to the inside temperature and 
humidity conditions,  test the vehicle and  
parts of the changes in extreme weather 
conditions 
through 
various 
constant 
or 
alternating judge the weather resistance of the 
parts and materials. Test method: method A and 
method B. Method A is recommend for the 
vehicle, interior and exterior compartment. 
Method B is recommended for the vehicle and 
the engine compartment. 
3.2 测试设备Test equipment 
    高低温交变湿热试验箱。 
 
High and low temperature environmental cycle 
test chamber. 
3.2.1 量程范围Range 
  温度-40-150℃，湿度20-98%。 
 
Temperature -40-150℃, humidity 20-98%.

3.2.2 精度Accuracy 
 
 
  温度精度:±2℃；温度波动度：±1℃ 
  温度均匀度：2℃ 
  湿度精度：±5%；湿度均匀度：5% 
  湿度波动度：±3% 
  平均升降温速率：≥1℃/min 
 
Temperature accuracy: ±  2 ℃;Temperature 
fluctuation: ± 1 ℃ 
Temperature uniformity: 2 ℃ 
Humidity accuracy: ± 5%; Humidity uniformity: 
5% 
Humidity fluctuation: ± 3% 
Average rate of temperature rise and fall: ≥ 
1 ℃ / min  
3.3 样品要求Sample request 
 
 
整车、单个零件或按照实际装配状态安装至
夹具、工装中的零部件进行测试。 
试验箱中的样品需按一定规则摆放，以不影
响样品耐候均匀为原则。 
 
The test shall be conducted for the whole 
vehicle, single part or parts installed in fixture 
and tooling according to actual assembly status. 
 
The samples in the test box should be placed 
according to certain rules, and the principle is 
not to affect the weathering conditions uniformity 
of the samples.
