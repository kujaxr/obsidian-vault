Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）1#和2#后扭转梁均是由于疲劳而开裂的；裂纹均起源于内侧表面。局部应力较高是导致其开裂的主要原因。Both 
no. 1 and no. 2 rear torsion beam are cracked due to fatigue; the cracks came from the inside surface. A high stress at the local was the main cause of the failure. 
（2）内侧表面高应力区有多条微小的疲劳裂纹。横梁开裂是这些微裂纹扩展的结果。There are a lot of small crack on the inside surface; 
the beam failure is the result of the small crack expanding.  
（3）2#失效件的起源处有小的碰伤；该碰伤加速了高应力区裂纹的萌生，进一步降低了其疲劳寿命。There is a little damage 
on the origin, which can accelerate crack initiation and further reduce the fatigue life.  
（4）下支撑板与扭转梁的焊缝熔深，厚度以及延伸段的角度和长度均符合要求；2#扭转梁焊缝末端有咬边缺陷，深度
为：0.73mm，但这不是导致失效的原因。The weld penetration and thickness between lower supporting plate and beam, angle and length of the outstretched weld 
meet the requirements. The weld undercutting is at the end of the no. 2 part and the depth is 0.73mm, but this is not the reason causing the failure. 
（5）后扭转梁的成分和强度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and strength meet the requirements; the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）通过粘贴应变片测量实际应力，校核安全系数；若不足，优化设计提高安全系数。Test the real stress by strain gauge and check 
the design safety factor. If not enough, improve the safety factor by design.  
（2）控制焊接工艺，避免咬边缺陷。Control the weld process and avoid the undercutting.
