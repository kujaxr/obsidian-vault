Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）分离指断裂于应力集中的区域，且产生于拉应力较大的表面；从裂纹断口上可以看出明显的裂纹源区，
扩展区和瞬断区，且在断裂区的表面可以观察到多条小裂纹。这一切表明该断裂为正常的疲劳断裂。The release fingers 
were broken in the stress concentration area and were created on the surface with the highest tensile stress; the fracture surfaces clearly show 
the crack source region, propagation region and final fracture region and at the same time, there are several small cracks in the fracture areas. All 
these features show the failure is because of normal fatigue break. 
（2）材料的成分，硬度，组织和夹杂物含量均合格，未见异常。The chemical components, hardness, microstructure and 
inclusion are acceptable. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）从离合器的结构以及装配角度，考察膜片弹簧是否存在承载不均匀的情况；若有，需要改善。According to the 
clutch structure and assembly, check if it is possible to load unevenly; if it is possible, need to improve it.  
（2）从产品设计的角度，降低膜片弹簧的载荷。Based on the product design, reduce the loading.
