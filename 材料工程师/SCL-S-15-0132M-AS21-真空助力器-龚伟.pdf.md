Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件焊接工艺不佳。            
2. 失效件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The weld part was poor. 
2. Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements.  
建议： 1. 供应商改进焊接工艺，变更检测方案。   
Suggestions:  1. Supplier improves the weld part and changes the detection scheme.
