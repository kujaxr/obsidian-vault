Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受对手件反复作用导致失效。 
       2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. Repeated acting on failure part made it fail. 
2. Material performance of failure part conformed to requirements.        
建议： 1. 优化零件结构。2. 装配时注意对零件的保护。 
Suggestions: 1.  Structure of part should be optimized. 
           2. Protection to the part should be improved during assembly.
