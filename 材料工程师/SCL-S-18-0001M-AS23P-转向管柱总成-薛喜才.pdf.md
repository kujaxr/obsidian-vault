Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  固定凸轮属于受到异常外力引起的过载断裂。 
Fixed cam fractured because of abnormal foreign stress. 
2.  硬度、密度满足要求。 
Hardness and density meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查装配和运输等过程。 
Check the assembly、transportion process and so on.
