Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 由于过载引起的断裂失效。失效断裂可能是由于紧固螺栓的夹紧力松掉导致，因没有提供其他相关零件，
这一点无法得到确认。 
The part failed due to overloading.  It’s possible the related bolt lost clamping load, but this cannot be 
confirmed since the parts were not received. 
2、 材料性能符合技术要求。 
The material properties meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 分析失效件使用过程中的受力情况。 
To analysis the conditions during use. 
2、 请提供所有相关零部件进行失效分析。 
Please provide all related parts for failure analysis.
