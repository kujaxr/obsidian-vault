Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 由于连接副的摩擦系数降低引起的过载失效，引起摩擦系数降低的原因可能是转向节机加工表面的防锈
油。Since the connection of the sub reduced coefficient of friction caused by overload failure, causing decrease 
in the coefficient of friction may be due to the knuckle machined surface rust preventive oil. 
2、 螺栓的材料性能符合技术要求。The materials meet the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 控制转向节机加工表面的防锈油用量。Control knuckle machined surface rust oil consumption. 
2、 评估现场实际装配条件对PAM 适用性的影响。Assess the actual site conditions on the PAM applicability assembly.
