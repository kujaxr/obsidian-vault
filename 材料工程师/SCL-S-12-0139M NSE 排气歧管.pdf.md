Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）排气歧管的开裂是由于振动交变载荷和热应力共同作用的结果；其中热应力为主要因素。The crack of the 
exhaust manifold is because of the load from the vibration and the heat stress; the heat stress is the main reason. 
（2）歧管部分尺寸不符合要求，但是不是失效的原因。Some dimensions of the exhaust manifold don’t meet the 
requirement, but this is not the failure reason. 
（3）材料的成分中的Si 含量偏低，但不会导致失效。The Si content is on the low side, but this can’t cause the failure. 
（4）材料的组织以及硬度合格；疏松可以接受。The microstructure and hardness meet the requirement; the porosity 
is acceptable. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检测工作温度是否过高。Check if the working temperature is high. 
（2）供应商应控制成分和尺寸，确保符合要求。Supplier should control the chemical composition and dimension 
well and ensure them meet the requirement.
