Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于载荷较大引起的疲劳磨损。 
Fatigue failure of parts is caused by wear and tear due to overload. 
2、 失效件的表面硬度、有效硬化层深度、密度符合图纸技术要求，金相组织正常。 
The surface hardness, the effective hardening layer depth and the density for the parts failured conform to the 
drawing requirements, the microstructure was normal. 
 
  建议Suggestions： 
1、 校核、优化系统的受力情况。 
To check and optimize the forces of the system.
