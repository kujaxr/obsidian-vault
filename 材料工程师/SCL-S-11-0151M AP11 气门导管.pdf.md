Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 
气门导管制造过程中的损伤导致零件开裂。零件在烧结之前有轻微的损伤，使得粉末不能真正地粘结在
一起。 The valve guide has cracked due to damage during manufacture.  The green part was slightly 
damaged before sintering and therefore the particles didn’t stick together properly in some places. 
2. 
材料的成分，密度, 组织，以及硬度都符合要求。The material composition, density, microstructure and 
hardness meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商在零件的制造过程中必须小心地处理零件，以免造成损伤。Supplier must take care of the 
parts during manufacture.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
 
送检的气门导管在上表面的区域有一个断裂区和几个小的径向裂纹（图1）。其断口已损坏和锈蚀，但SEM 
观察结果显示：断裂面的大多数粒子是光滑的；无颗粒间受力断裂的特征（图2）。为了便于比较，在相同零件上
人为制造一个新的断口；对其进行检测，结果发现断口的许多区域为韧性断裂，表明粒子之间有良好的结合力（图
3）。小裂纹的表面特征类似上表面的断裂区（图4）。由上述检测和分析，我们可以看到，零件在粉末压制后烧结
前遭到了损伤。 
 
材料分析（成分，硬度，密度和金相组织）都符合要求（见表1）。  
 
The returned valve guide was found to have one fractured area at the top surface, and several small radial cracks (figure 1).  The open  
fracture was quite damaged and rusted, but using SEM to view the surface showed mostly smooth particle surfaces, with no particle-particle 
fractures (figure 2).  For comparison, at a forced fracture in the same part there were many areas of ductile fracture, showing good adhesion 
between particles (figure 3).  The surfaces of the small cracks were similar to the upper fracture (figure 4).  From this analysis we can see that 
there was some problem after the powder pressing process, but before sintering. 
 
The material analysis (composition, hardness, density & microstructure) met the requirements of the specification (see table 1)
