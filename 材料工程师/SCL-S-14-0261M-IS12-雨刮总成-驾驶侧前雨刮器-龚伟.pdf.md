Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致脆断。 
       2. 零件材料不符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. External force made part gliding fracture.  
2. Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.   
建议： 1. 责令供应商解释材料问题并整改。 
Suggestions: 1.Let the supplier explain the material problems and rectify.
