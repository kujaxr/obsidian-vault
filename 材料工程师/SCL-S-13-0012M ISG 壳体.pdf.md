Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）壳体的定位孔是由于受到反复装配中异常载荷而断裂。The orientation hole of the shell was broken because of 
abnormal load from the repeating assembly. 
（2）材料存在一定量的疏松，并且变质不良。这些均会降低材料的强度和韧性，但不是失效的根本原因。The 
material is with a certain porosity and modification is not good. These can reduce the strength and toughness of the 
material, but this is not the root cause of failure. 
（3）材料的成分和硬度符合要求。The chemical compositions and hardness meet the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检测定位销以及定位孔的尺寸。Check the dimension of the pin and orientation hole 
（2）重新安装时，按照正确的操作流程。Reassembly according to the right process. 
（3）控制工艺过程，保证铸件质量。Control the process and ensure the quality of the casting.
