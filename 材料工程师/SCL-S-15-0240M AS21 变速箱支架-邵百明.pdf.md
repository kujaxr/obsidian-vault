Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）变速箱安装支架是由于过载而发生断裂。The Transmission bracket was broken due to overload. 
（2）断裂处的孔隙率符合要求；1#断裂处的最大孔隙尺寸为3.6mm，不符合要求（≤1.5mm）；其它断裂处的最
大孔隙尺寸合格。The porosity rates at the rupture meet the requirement; max. hole size at the No. 1 rupture is 3.6mm, 
which doesn’t meet the requirement (≤1.5mm) and the other ruptures’ hole sizes are ok.  
（3）材料的成分和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical compositions and hardness of the material meet the 
requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）调查车辆失效前的使用工况，并评估特定工况下安装支架的安全性。Investigate the use condition of the vehicle 
before failure and at the same time evaluate the bracket safety under a special condition. 
（2）查找局部最大孔隙尺寸超标的原因，并采取措施，加强控制，确保产品的质量。Find the cause which made the 
max. hole size over the requirement and take measures to strengthen the control and ensure the quality of the products.
