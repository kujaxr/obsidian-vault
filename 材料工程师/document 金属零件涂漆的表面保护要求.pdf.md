1 范围Scope 
本标准规定了在金属表面上施涂油漆的表
面保护的要求。 
本标准适用于金属表面施涂黑漆的零件。但
不适用于镀锌金属表面的涂漆（见SMTC 5 100 
012）、装饰性保护清漆（见SMTC 5 100 010）
以及带空腔的底盘部件（见SMTC 5 100 047）。 
 
 
This standard defines requirements for corro-
sion protection of black coatings on metal 
surfaces. 
This standard apply to the parts with black 
coatings on the metal surface, but does not 
apply to paint coatings on zinc-coated metal 
surfaces (see SMTC 5 100 012); to decorative 
protective varnishes (SMTC 5 100 010) or to 
paint coatings on chassis parts that may form 
cavities (see SMTC 5 100 047).

3  基本要求General requirements 
3.1  避免有害物质按SMTC 5 500 001。对于出
口欧盟零件有害物质按SMTC 5 500 002满足
REACH法规标准。进行完全试验大约需8个部件。
所用涂层必须不含Cr
6+或铅化合物。依据零件的
材料特性、结构样式以及安装部位等实际状况，
针对零件的特殊细化要求，体现在《零件开发验
证计划—材料部分》大纲内。 
 
 
3.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 5 500 001. For export to euro 
parts, hazardous substances according to 
SMTC 5 500 002.Eight parts or part sections 
are required for complete testing. The coatings 
used must be free of compounds containing 
chromium (VI) or lead. Based on the actual 
conditions such as the material characteristics, 
structural patte rns and installation positions of 
the parts, the specifically refined requirements 
for the parts are reflected in the outline of 
Component ADV Plan - Material test. 
3.2  零件表面无明显缺陷。 
 
3.2  No obvious flaws on the surface.
