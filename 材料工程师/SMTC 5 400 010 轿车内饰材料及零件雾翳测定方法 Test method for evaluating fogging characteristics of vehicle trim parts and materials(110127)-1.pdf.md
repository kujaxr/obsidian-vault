1 范围 
本标准规定了轿车内饰材料雾翳试验的术
语和定义、试样准备、测试设备、试验方法和结
果计算等。  
本标准适用于所有可能对车内空气质量产
生影响的零件和材料。 
 
1  Scope  
The Standards stipulates the terminology 
and 
definition, 
sample 
preparation, 
test 
equipment, test method, and result calculation 
of test method for evaluating fogging characte-
ristics of vehicle trim materials. 
The Standard applies to all parts and 
materials likely to affect air quality in vehicles. 
2 术语和定义 
雾翳——指在一定条件下，在挡风玻璃及其
它玻璃上形成的一层薄薄的涂层，从而降低可见
度的一种现象。是由塑料装饰材料中的挥发性成
分（如可塑剂、稳定剂、油、润滑油、发泡剂、
脱模剂、粘合剂等）在玻璃表面冷凝引起的。  
 
2  Terminology and Definition 
Fogging—refers to a phenomenon where  
a layer of fog is formed on the windscreen and 
other glass thus reducing the visibility. This is 
caused by the cold condensation of volatile 
components (such as plasticizer, stabilizer, oil, 
lubricant, foaming agent, release agent, 
bonding agent, etc.) from plastic materials 
condensing on the surface of glasses.

3  Sample Preparation 
3.1 取样 
从各种均匀分布的测试材料上采集适用的
圆形试样，直径为（80±1）mm，试样的厚度应
同测试材料的厚度相同，最厚不超过20 mm。厚
度超过20 mm的材料，如泡沫，应将其厚度减至
20 mm。除非另有规定，对于非扁平形的材料（剖
面等），所使用的试样面积为15±1 cm
2。对于
粉状或液状材料，应从样品中取(10±2) g样品
使用。  
 
3.1  Sample  
Applicable round samples are collected 
from various test materials distributed evenly, 
with the diameter being (80±1) mm. Samples 
should be 20 mm at maximum in thick-
ness .Samples with a greater thickness than 
20mm, such as bubble, its thickness should be 
reduced to 20 mm. Unless otherwise stipu-
lated, for non-flat materials (profile etc.), the 
area of samples used is 15±1 cm2. For powder 
or liquid materials, samples of (10±2) g are 
collected for use.

不必为真空。各种材料的干燥时间见表1所示。

Before the test, all samples need to be 
dried at (23±5) ℃. The drier should be contain

4.2  Thermostatic Equipment 
Thermostatic bath: a thermostatic bath 
(see Figure 1) of the temperature between 
60 ℃ and 130 ℃, provided with six flasks, and

equipment to avoid overheating. The quantity 
of liquid used for heat transmission, recycling 
and heating should be able to maintain the 
deviation of bath temperature and required test 
temperature at ±0.5 ℃. After the flasks are 
placed into the bath, the temperature decline 
should not be above 5 ℃, and test temperature 
should be able to reach requirements within 20 
minutes.

5 试验方法  
 
5  Test method 
设备调节后应即刻进行试验。  
方法A 是采用在雾翳试验前后对铝箔称重
的方法来确定铝箔上雾翳冷凝物的重量； 
方法B 是采用反射率测量法来测定有机成
分的挥发性。本标准规定的两个可选的测试方
法，其对雾翳程度的测定方法不同。用不同测试
法获得的测试结果不可直接进行比较。测试方法
必须只能用材料规范、工程图纸等技术文档中编
码所对应的方法。 
 
Test is carried out immediately after the 
adjustment of equipment  
Method A  is to determine the weight of 
fogging condensate on aluminum foil through 
weighing aluminum foil before and after fog-
ging test;  
Method B is to adopt the reflection rate 
measuring method to determine the volatility of 
organic components. The two selectable test 
methods stipulated in the Standard are differ-
ent in the determining of fogging degree. Test 
results obtained through different measuring 
methods can not be compared directly. It is 
only allowed to employ methods correspond-
ing with the codes in technical documents such 
as material criteria and project drawing.  
5.1 方法A 
 
5.1  Method A 
5.1.1  试验中使用的密封圈、金属环、玻璃盘
及烧杯应在80 ℃温度下的实验室用清洗器中清
洗干净。除玻璃盘外，所有物品存放于(23±
2) ℃温度下的无尘环境中，烧杯应口朝下倒放，
在试验开始前方可取出使用。  
 
5.1.1  Seal ring, metal ring, glass tray, and 
flask used in the test should be washed clean 
with the cleaner for lab under the temperature 
of 80 ℃. Except for glass trays, all articles 
should be placed in dustless environment un-
der the temperature of (23±2) ℃. Flasks 
should be placed upside down, and taken out 
for use before the start of the test.  
5.1.2  玻璃盘在用清洗器清洗后，应放入超声
波清洗器中，使用固定夹具将玻璃盘固定住（见
图3），再以清洁剂（见5.4.3）完全浇盖，在设
备不通电且温度为(23±5) ℃的情况下，清洗
(18±2) h，然后将设备通电且温度为(55±
5) ℃下，清洗 （5±1）min。然后把玻璃盘在
去离子水中彻底冲洗，也可用不含可塑剂的海绵
（见5.4.2）进行机械清理，再用蒸馏水漂洗。
在温度为(70±5) ℃的再循环空气柜中干燥玻
璃盘(30±5) min。用于试验的玻璃盘必须冷却
至(23±2) ℃，并且在清洗过程完成后30 min
内使用。  
 
 
5.1.2  After washed with cleaner, glass trays 
should be placed into ultrasonic cleaner, and 
fixed with fixing grip (See Figure 3). And then, 
glass trays are covered with cleaning agent 
(See 5.4.3). When the equipment is not elec-
trified, and the temperature is (23±5) ℃, they 
are cleaned for (18±2) h. Then the equipment 
is electrified and the temperature reaches 
(55±5) ℃, glass trays are cleaned for (5±1) 
minutes. Next, glass trays are thoroughly 
washed in de-ionized water, and cleaned me-
chanically with sponge（See 5.4.2） containing 
no plasticizer, and then rinsed with distilled 
water. Dry the appartus for (30±5) minutes in 
the recycling air cabinet at (70±5) ℃. Glass 
trays used for test should be cooled down to 
(23±2) ℃, and used within 30 minutes after the 
completion of the cleaning.

5.1.4  铝箔盘的制作：为避免铝箔有折痕，必

5.1.4  Production of aluminum foil trays: to 
avoid folding faults of aluminum foil, operators

5.1.5  称重：使用5.5.10要求的天平对铝箔称
重，天平精度为0.01 mg。  
 
5.1.5  Weighing: use the analytical balance  
required in 5.5.10 to weigh aluminum foil.  
5.1.6  装满的烧杯应加盖密封圈及称过重的铝
箔盘（有光泽的一面向下）。为了防止铝箔盘滑
动，应将其小心地同密封圈压在一起，附上边缘
辅助物，然后将干净的玻璃盘放在铝箔盘上。  
 
 
5.1.6  Filled flasks should be covered with 
seal ring and weighed aluminum foil tray (the 
face with gloss downward). To avoid sliding, 
aluminum foil trays should be carefully pressed 
with seal ring and attached with edge auxilia-
ries. Clean glass trays are placed on aluminum 
foil trays.  
5.1.7  通过自动调温器的开口将已准备好的烧
杯放入浴槽，调温器的温度应已设定为规定的测
试温度(100±3) ℃，首先在每个玻璃盘上放一
片过滤纸，然后放冷却板，测试时间为(18±0.5) 
h。  
 
 
5.1.7  Prepared flasks are placed into bath 
through the opening of automatic thermostat. 
The temperature of the thermostat should be 
the stipulated test temperature of (100±3) ℃. 
First of all, operators should place a piece of 
filter paper on each glass tray and then cooling 
plate. The test will last (18±0.5) h. 
5.1.8  在规定测试时间结束后，应小心地取下
放在密封圈上的铝箔,然后放在顶面有灰雾不透
光的干燥器内保持(3.5±0.5) h。只要能保证有
效干燥，干燥器内可以填满硅胶,但放在干燥器
内的铝箔一定不可阳光直射。  
 
 
5.1.8  After the conclusion of the stipulated 
test time, operators should carefully remove 
aluminum foil on seal ring, and then place it in 
light-proof drier with fog on the top surface for 
(3.5±0.5) h. As long as effective drying is 
guaranteed, the drier can be filled with silica 
gel. But aluminum foil in the drier can not be 
under the direct sunlight.
