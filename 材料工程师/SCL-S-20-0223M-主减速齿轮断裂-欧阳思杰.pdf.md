Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1. 主减速齿发生疲劳断裂失效，中间轴从动齿在主减速齿断裂后受到冲击发生过载断裂。The main reduction gear was 
fatigue fracture failure, and the driven gear of intermediate shaft was impacted after the main reduction gear was fractured. 
2. 主减速齿和中间轴齿根圆角存在超差，会导致应力集中，更容易发生疲劳开裂。The main reduction gear and the 
intermediate shaft root fillet exist out of tolerance, which will lead to stress concentration, more prone to fatigue cracking. 
3. 主减速齿的非马深度存在超差，中间轴的非马深度合格；主减速齿和中间轴的表面硬度、芯部硬度、硬化层深度及
金相组织符合要求。The surface hardness, core hardness, hardened layer depth and metallographic structure of the main 
reduction gear and intermediate shaft meet the requirements; the non horse depth of the main reduction gear is out of tolerance, 
and the non horse depth of the intermediate shaft is qualified 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 严格控制零件尺寸。Strictly control the size of parts 
2. 控制齿轮的热处理工艺过程。Control heat treatment process of gear.
