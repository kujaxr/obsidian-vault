Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 泵支架属疲劳断裂, 从很小缺陷开始的。The bracket has failed through fatigue, initiated from a very small surface flaw. 
2. 失效支架的化学成分、孔隙率、金相组织符合技术要求。硬度略高于规范，但是这不太可能有不良影响。
The chemical composition, porosity & microstructure meet the requirements.  The hardness was slightly above specification however 
this is unlikely to have a negative influence.  
3. 滑轮轴承运行的痕迹正常，因此，根本原因并不是过载或皮带驱动系统的错位。The pulley bearing showed normal 
running traces, so an overload or misalignment in the belt-drive system can be excluded as root cause. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查有类似里程的车辆，查找是否有疲劳断裂。 Inspection of vehicles with similar mileages for fatigue fracture. 
2. 对砂铸件小的表面缺陷是不可避免的。工程来确认支架设计在满足安全系数要求的前提下, 小缺陷是否
可以接受。 Small surface flaws are unavoildable in sand cast parts. DRE to confirm whether design can take account of small 
flaws in safety factor.

滑轮轴承  Pulley bearing 
由于接触皮带滑轮的外表面有磨损。磨痕与滑轮边缘平行，并略偏离中心（图7）。  
轴承组件及润滑油处于良好状态。外滚道和内滚道负荷区的痕迹在中央并且正常，已运行一段时间（见图8
和图9）。  
从这些结果，我们可以知道，在运行过程中皮带系统没有明显超载，驱动滑轮或皮带也未发生错位。  
 
The outer surface of the pulley shows light wear due to contact with the belt.  The wear track lies parallel to the edges of the pulley and slightly 
offset from centre (figure 7).  The bearing components and lubricant are in good condition.  Running traces on the outer raceway and in the load 
area of the inner raceway are central and normal, considering the running time (see figures 8 & 9).   
From these results we can see that there was no significant overload in the belt drive system or misalignment of the pulley or belt during 
operation.
