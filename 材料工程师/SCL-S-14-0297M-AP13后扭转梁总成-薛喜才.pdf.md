Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 后扭转梁是由于疲劳而开裂的，局部应力载荷较大是主要原因；Rear torsion beam was broken though fatigue and 
a big stress was the main reason of crack. 
2. 后扭转梁与加强板焊缝熔深、厚度及焊缝延伸段的长度、角度均符合技术要求；Depth of fusion and welding 
height between rear torsion beam and the strength plate met the technical requirement, angle and length of the 
stretched weld met the requirement. 
3. 后扭转梁材质正常；The material of rear torsion beam assembly was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 粘结应变片校核开裂部位应力载荷，并评估其安全系数；Check the active stress though the strain gauge and 
evaluate the safety factor.
