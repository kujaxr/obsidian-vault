Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 换挡轴、拨块皆属于疲劳断裂。 
Shift and block were both cracking by fatigue.  
2. 换挡轴成分、表面硬度符合要求，局部硬化层深度偏深(见图5)，基体硬度及组织正常。 
Chemical components and surface hardness of shift met the technical requirements and partial harden case was high 
(shown in figure5). Base microstructure and hardness were normal. 
3. 拨块硬化层深度、表面硬度符合要求，基体组织正常。 
Harden case and surface hardness of block met the technical requirements. Base microstructure was normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核载荷，评估零件的安全系数。 
Check the load and evaluate the safety coefficient. 
2. 检查台架试验过程。 
Check the bench test process. 
3. 关注换挡轴的热处理工艺。 
  Pay attention to the heat treatment process of shift.

一. 失效零件The failed parts 
1. SC81 变速箱(IS12 major)同步器耐久台架试验，此次试验换挡力有所增加。当试验进行到25%左右时，换挡轴 
断裂；试验继续进行至60%左右时，一二档拨块断裂，同步器结合齿磨损严重。故障件在变速箱内部的情况见图 
1 所示。
