Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效件属疲劳断裂。The part failured belong to fatigue fracture. 
2、失效件的疲劳起始区域发现冲压起皱和裂纹（见图6）；新件在相同部位也发现有起皱和裂纹（见图
14）。Fatigue failure initiation region were found some cracks(see Fig 6).The new part were some cracks in 
the same region(see Fig 14). 
3、零件的材料性能未发现异常。Material properties of the parts were nomal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 评估缺陷的符合性。To assessment compliance of the defect.  
2、 加强零件实物质量控制。Strengthening the physical quality control of parts.
