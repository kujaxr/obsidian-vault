Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 两螺栓属于扭断失效。   
Both bolts are twisting off.  
（2） 两螺栓强度满足技术要求，但2#螺栓螺牙表面粘附金属碎屑，1#螺栓牙底存在折叠。 
Both bolts strength meet the technical requirement. Metal scraping adheres to 2# bolt tooth and folded defect is 
found in1# bolt tooth.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 排查螺栓的成型工艺，确保产品质量。 
Check the bolt deformation process to make sure the quality. 
（2）评估漆层对螺栓摩擦系数的影响，必要时提高螺栓强度。 
Evaluate the influence of coating on the coefficient and improve the bolt strength.
