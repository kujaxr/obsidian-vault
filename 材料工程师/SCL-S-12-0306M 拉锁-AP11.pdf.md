Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件能够满足试验大纲的要求，但在低温情况下受力容易开裂。 
2. 失效件材料与粒料成分有明显差异。  
Conclusions:  1. The performance of part conformed to requirements. But it is easy to cleave at low temperature and 
foreign force. 2. IR spectrum of failure part was not the same as raw material.           
建议： 1.检查材料和工艺情况。2.按要求进行装配。 
Suggestions: 1. The material and process should be checked. 
 
 
   2. The assembly should follow the requirements.
