Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1.1 差锥齿轮由于过载而失效；尺寸不准确进一步恶化受载条件。The differential bevel gear has failed through 
overloading, made worse by dimensional inaccuracies.  
1.2 差锥齿轮材料硬度太高了也金相组织有脆性特征。化学成分合格。The differential gear hardness is too high, and 
microstructure has brittle characteristics. The chemical composition is OK.  
2.1 直齿圆柱齿轮由于局部过载失效；这是由于运转中配合不好或者零件变形造成的。The spur gear has failed 
through local overloading, caused by misalignment or distortion during running.  
2.2 直齿圆柱齿轮硬度符合要求。The spur gear hardness meets the requirements. 
3. 滚子轴承内圈由于润滑不足而失效的，可能与过载有关。The roller bearing inner race has failed through 
inadequate lubrication, which might be related to overloading. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 分析各配合部件的条件作进一步的磨损迹象。Analyse all mating components’ condition for signs of wear. 
2. 分析差速齿轮的承载条件。Analyse loading condition on the differential gear. 
3. 控制差动齿轮硬度在要求的范围内；考虑调整热处理以增加韧性。Maintain differential gear hardness within the 
limits, consider adjusting heat treatment to increase toughness. 
4. 检查直齿圆柱齿轮和匹配的倒档惰轮的齿形轮廓。Check tooth profile of the spur gear & mating reverse idler gear.

断口分析Fracture Analysis 
 
发现断口比较新鲜的，没有明显氧化，显示裂纹不是在淬火中产生的。起源区在失效中被破坏，所以无法对该区
域进行分析。靠近源区的断口处于硬化区间，其特征为沿晶断裂，并带有一些韧窝(图7)。芯部是完全的韧性断
裂（图8）。断口形貌表面齿是由于过载而失效的；未显示疲劳断裂的特征。 
The fracture surface was relatively fresh, with no significant oxidation, indicating cracks were not caused during 
quenching.  The origin area had been damaged during the failure, so could not be analysed.  The fracture surface 
within the hardened layer, near to the origin, showed predominantly intergranular fracture with some ductile areas (figure 
7), the crack had travelled along prior austenite grain boundaries, from the .  The fracture in the core area was 
completely ductile (figure 8).  The fracture morphology indicates the tooth failed through overload.  No indications for 
fatigue fracture were seen.
