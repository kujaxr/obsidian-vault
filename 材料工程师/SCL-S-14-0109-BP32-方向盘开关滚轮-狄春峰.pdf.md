Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件注塑工艺不佳。 
       2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The injection process of failure part was bad. 
            2. The material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 改善注塑工艺。 
       2. 加强检查环节。 
Suggestions: 1. Injection process should be improved. 
           2. Check process should be improved.
