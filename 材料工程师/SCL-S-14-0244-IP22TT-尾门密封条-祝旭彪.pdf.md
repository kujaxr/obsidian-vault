Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论 Conclusion:： 1、失效区域出现的开裂和磨损现象为非正常受力导致 
1、Non normal stress caused cracking and abrasion in failure zone. 
                  2、泰国反馈零件有安装不到位现象； 
2、Thailand feedbacks the sealing has not assembly completely. 
3、零件材料满足要求。 
3、The material of the sponge EPDM meets the requirements. 
 
建议：避免安装不到位而造成受到非正常外力导致的破裂和磨损。 
Suggestion: Avoid the crack and abrasion failures caused by the incomplete installation.
