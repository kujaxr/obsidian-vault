Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 该失效件属于氢脆引起的延迟断裂。 
The failure of parts is delayed fracture caused by hydrogen embrittlement. 
2、失效件的材料性能符合图纸的技术要求。 
The material properties of the failure parts met the technical requirements of the drawing. 
3、抽检的与失效件不同批次的新螺钉表面硬度不合格，（原因是漏工序：表面没有渗碳处理）。 
   New screws surface handness did not meet the requirement of the drawing,due to leakage process: 
surface carburizing treatment. 
4、CKD 件表面无渗碳。The CKD parts did not surface carburizing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查零件生产过程控制（如热处理、电镀、去氢等工序）。 
Check the parts production process control (such as heat treatment, plating, 
dehydroepiandrosterone process) 
2、优化螺钉的设计（如材料、技术要求等）。 
      Optimize screw design (such as materials, technical requirements, etc.).
