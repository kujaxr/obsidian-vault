Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、该蒸发器的白色物质主要来自迎风面铝的腐蚀产物；其腐蚀是由于铜粉和潮湿环境共同作用导致的。The white 
substance of the evaporator mainly comes from the corrosion products of aluminum on the windward side; Its corrosion is 
caused by the combined action of copper powder and humid environment. 
2、扁管和翅片的材料未见异常。No abnormalities of the the flat tube and fins were found. 
建议Suggestions：  
1、监控售后市场的类似失效比例；必要时增加钝化亲水处理工艺，提升蒸发器的耐腐蚀性。Monitor the proportion 
of similar failures in the after-sales market; If necessary, increase the passivation hydrophilic treatment process to improve 
the corrosion resistance of the evaporator.

检零件如图1 所示，其迎风面具有较多的白色物质；背风面的白色物质相对较少。

粉和潮湿环境共同作用导致的。
