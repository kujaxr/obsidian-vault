Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致断裂。    2．失效件注塑中混入异物，注塑工艺不佳。            
3. 失效件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The brittle fracture of the part caused by external force. 
2. The fracture part missed with foreign object during injection molding, with bad injection mold. 
3. Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 责令供应商对注塑问题做出解释并整改。   2．检查装配环节。  
Suggestions:  1. Let supplier explain the injection problem and rectify. 
            2. Check the assembly part.
