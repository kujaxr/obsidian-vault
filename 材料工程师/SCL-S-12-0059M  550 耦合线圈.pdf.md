Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
(1) 铜电箔断裂是异常的，断口显示非常小的塑性变形，是过载引起的。The fracture of the copper electrical foil 
is abnormal.  The fracture surface shows very little plastic deformation compared to other strands which were 
forcibly overloaded. 
(2) 其他六根铜箔线之一也表现出异常断口特征。One of the other six strands also showed abnormal fracture 
characteristics. 
(3) 塑料载体上的飞边妨碍了铜箔带的位置安装。Flash on the plastic carrier prevents good location of the tape. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
(1) 检查缺陷的可能引起失效的过程：如制造和装配过程。Check manufacturing and assembly process for 
possible source of defects.
