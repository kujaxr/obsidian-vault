Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，失效件的材料无异常。 
2. 排气管受外界撞击变形，随后在将其回位过程中发生开裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation, simulation test and material test of the vent, it can be seen that the vent was 
impacted by an external force and departured from the normal position. And then the crack occurred at the 
root of vent after it was moved into place.  
建议：避免油箱总成从料架滑落；避免物流过程中油箱总成受到磕碰；装车前百分百气密性检测。 
Suggestions：The fuel tank fall off rack should be avoided. The fuel tank should avoid being impacted in logistics 
process. Sealing property of the fuel tank should be inspected one hundred percent before installed into 
vehicle.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图2 失效件断口形貌可以看出，排气管部分断裂，裂纹向内凹陷，根据未断部位可以判定导致排气管开
裂的最终受力方向如图箭头所示，排气管表面未发现撞击痕。图4 和图5 分别是图3 断面的上部断面形貌和下部
断面形貌，上部断面布满均匀韧窝；而下部断面可以看到从平整断口向韧窝断裂过度特征，在端口的末端韧窝变
得比较细小。 
From Fig 2, it can be seen that the fuel tank exhaust vent was broken partially and the crack was depressed toward to 
the inner direction. No impact scar is found on the outside of exhaust vent. According to the position of remain region, it 
can be known that the break direction of the vent as shown at the arrow. The dimples appeared on the upper crack surface 
uniformly as shown in Fig 4. The evaluation from the smooth crack to the dimple crack can be observed on the lower 
crack surface (as shown in Fig 5). The dimple sizes become smaller at the end of crack.

Ⅴ. 模拟试验 Simulation testing 
为了模拟油箱排气口不同状态的断裂特征，将油箱整体从1 米高自由落地，同时保证排气口与地面呈45o
最先着地，试验后的排气口根部未发生开裂（图6），排气口与原位发生明显偏离，并出现褶皱、向内凹陷特征；
再将排气口缓慢回位，根部褶皱很容易发生开裂，裂纹处残留轻微的内凹特征（图7），该模拟的裂纹特征与失
效件的裂纹特征类似。 
In order to simulate the fracture mechanism of the vent, a normal fuel tank was dropped from 1 meter height and 
the vent firstly touched ground with 45o. After the test, no crack occurred but wrinkles appeared at the root of the vent 
and the vent departured from the normal position as shown in Fig 6. Then the vent was moved into normal place again, 
a crack easily appeared at former wrinkle region and the depression features remained on crack as shown in Fig 7 which 
is same as that of failure part.
