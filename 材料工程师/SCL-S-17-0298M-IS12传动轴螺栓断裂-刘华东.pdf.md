Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺栓是多源疲劳断裂；这与螺纹根部存在折叠，牙底非圆弧过渡等缺陷有关。The bolt is multi-source fatigue 
fracture, it is related to the defects of thread bottom has fold and non-circular arc transition; 
2、 失效螺栓化学成分、硬度和金相组织均符合要求，螺纹存在缺陷，不符合标准要求，原材料存在缩孔缺陷；
新状态零件检测正常。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure of bolts meet the requirements. The 
thread bottom has defects; they do not meet the standard requirement. And the raw material has shrinkage defect. 
The new situation bolt is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、售后对已装车零件进行跟踪和风险评估。Track and risk evaluate the parts have been assembled on the vehicles.  
2、紧固件供应商选择建议按照上汽推荐清单要求执行。The selection of the fastener supplier is recommended in 
accordance with the SAIC recommendation list.

二、断口分析  Fracture Analysis： 
1、用体视镜观察螺栓断口宏观形貌，如图2 所示。断口较平整，无明显塑性变形，且断口上下两端均有明显

较小，易引起螺栓松动。
