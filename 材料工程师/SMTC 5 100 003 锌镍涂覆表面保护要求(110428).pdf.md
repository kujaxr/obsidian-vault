1 范围 
本标准规定了抗拉强度为Rm≤1000 MPa的
铁基材料和钢制件上的电镀和无六价铬后处理
的锌／镍合金镀层的要求。 
本标准适用于评价钢制件上的电镀和无六
价铬后处理的锌／镍合金镀层。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies requirements for 
electrolytically 
deposited 
and 
Cr(VI)-free 
post-treated zinc/nickel alloy coatings on ferrous 
materials and steel parts with a tensile strength 
Rm ≤ 1,000 MPa. 
The standard is adopted to evaluate the 
electrolytically 
deposited 
and 
Cr(VI)-free 
post-treated zinc/nickel alloy coatings on ferrous 
materials and steel parts.

3.1 保护类型 
3.1  Surface protection types 
3.1.1  这些镀层特别适用于内部传动的连接元
件，以避免附加的施力作用。  
3.1.1
In particular, these coatings are also 
suitable for joining elements with internal drive to 
avoid the clogging of force-application sockets. 
3.1.2  该镀层作为重度防腐（防腐等级为6）的
镀层，特别适用于温度达150 ℃（例如：发动机
室和刹车系统）的零件以及紧固件。也适用于导
电连接（接地线连接）。  
 
3.1.2 
These coatings, used as heavy-duty 
corrosion protection (protection class 6), are 
especially suitable for components and systems 
of threaded joints for which besides an in-
creased corrosion load a temperature load of up 
to 150 °C (engine compartment or brake area) is 
to be expected, as well as for conducting con-
nections (ground connections). 
3.1.3  另外，鉴于其电化相容性，这些镀层尤
其适用于与铝材料的连接，但不适用于与镁材料
的连接。 
3.1.3  Thanks to their electrochemical compa-
tibility, they are also suited for use in contact with 
aluminum materials but not with magnesium 
materials.

允许使用的保护类型,参见表2。

3.2 基本要求 
3.2  General requirements 
3.2.1  抗拉强度Rm > 1000 MPa的高强度钢制件
和表面硬度 > 320 HV的连接元件（例如：螺栓）
不允许使用这种合金镀层。说明：采用封闭层的
表面保护类型，可以影响油漆可涂覆性能。 
3.2.1  High-strength steel parts with a tensile 
strength Rm > 1,000 MPa and joining elements 
(e.g. screws) with a surface hardness > 320 HV 
must not be coated with these alloy coatings. 
3.2.2  由于其微裂性，这些镀层不适合于诸如
空调装置的供气系统的密封元件。 
 
3.2.2  These coatings are also unsuitable for 
sealing elements on gas-carrying systems such 
as the air-conditioning system, because the 
coatings contain microcracks. 
3.2.3  采用这种电镀方法的零件，必须避免后
续的塑性变形（扩孔、挤压、弯曲），这是因为
这种情况会损伤镀层，从而降低零件的耐蚀性。
 
3.2.3  Subsequent plastic deformation (flaring, 
pinching, bending) of components coated in this 
manner must be avoided, as this could damage 
the corrosion protection and thus reduce the 
part’s corrosion resistance. 
3.2.4  避免有害物质按SMTC 90008。  
 
3.2.4
Avoidance of hazardous substances 
according to SMTC 90008. 
3.2.5  进行一次完整的试验需要10个成品件。
 
3.2.5  10 finished parts are required for com-
plete testing. 
3.2.6  除确定的、且在图纸中已表明的表面位
置之外，整个表面上必须具备所要求的、且具有
规定性能的表面保护层。这些镀层必须紧紧地粘
附在基材上。  
 
3.2.6  Unless certain sections of a part that are 
marked in the drawing are excluded from the 
surface coating, the entire surface of the parts 
must comply with the required surface protection 
type and display the prescribed properties. The 
coatings shall have adhere firmly to the base 
material. 
3.2.7  生产过程及其控制不应损坏成品部件的
功能特性。  
 
3.2.7  The production process and its control 
shall not impair the functional characteristics of 
the finished part. 
3.2.8  这些保护性镀层不允许出现气孔、裂纹、
损坏或其它降低耐蚀性或特定外观的缺陷。 
 
3.2.8  The protective coatings must not exhibit 
any pores, cracks, damage, or other flaws that 
impair the corrosion protection or specified ap-
pearance. 
3.2.9  在正确的装配条件下，不能有影响功能
或降低耐蚀性的涂层损坏现象。 
 
3.2.9
Given proper mounting, no coating 
damage shall occur that would lead to impair-
ment of function or decrease of the specified 
corrosion protection. 
3.2.10  零件必须通过去氢处理来避免发生氢
脆。在首件样品的实验报告中，可通过按DIN 50 
969进行张力试验予以认证。 
3.2.10
The surface treatment procedure shall 
be conducted so that damage by delayed hy-
drogen-induced brittle fractures can be ruled 
out. Verification by tension testing according to 
DIN 50969 in the first sample test report.

3.4 锌镍-电镀方法 
3.4  Zinc/nickel coating process 
3.4.1  从碱性电解质系统离析出的镀层，特别
适用于几何形状复杂的部件。这些镀层通过整个
电流密度范围，而获得一种均匀的镍分布。 
  
3.4.1  For components with geometrically 
complicated shapes the coatings deposited from 
alkaline systems are especially suitable. These 
layers display a uniform nickel distribution over 
the entire current-density range. 
3.4.2  假如仅在不利的情况下可以直接采用碱
性电解质进行离析，例如在铸件材料上，那么就
允许通过一种弱酸性锌电解质进行预镀锌离析
来激活表面。然而，要采用这种2层系统以及所
使用的电解质，必须与上海汽车中心实验室进行
磋商。 
3.4.2  If direct deposition from alkaline 
zinc/nickel electrolytes, e.g. on cast materials, is 
possible only under unfavorable conditions, the 
surface may be activated by deposition of a 
pre-zinc coating from a weakly acidic zinc elec-
trolyte. The use of such a 2-layer system as well 
as the electrolytes used must be agreed upon by 
the SAIC MOTOR Central Laboratory. 
3.5 无铬（Cr
6+）钝化层 
3.5  Cr(VI)-free conversion layers 
3.5.1  在电解质离析的锌／镍－镀层中，为了
改善对盐水和冷凝水的腐蚀耐抗性，需采用钝化
液进行后处理。  
 
3.5.1  In order to improve the corrosion resis-
tance of electrolytically deposited zinc/nickel 
coatings to salt water and condensed water, a 
post-treatment in passivation treatment solu-
tions is required. 
3.5.2  所用的钝化溶液不得含有六价铬，以保
证形成的转化层也无铬（Cr
6+）。 
3.5.2  The passivation treatment solution must 
not contain any Cr(VI) compounds in order to 
ensure that the resultant conversion layers are 
likewise Cr(VI)-free. 
3.6 后处理 
3.6  Post-treatments 
3.6.1  尤其对于客户视觉范围内的部件而言，
因为要求强烈的色彩波动（彩虹色）不得影响部
件的外观，所以必须采用一种与保护类型相关的
封闭技术进行涂层后处理。这种后处理同时增强
了腐蚀防护。  
 
3.6.1  As the outer appearance of the parts 
must not be impaired by strong color variations 
(iridescence), especially for parts in areas visible 
to the customer, a sealing post-treatment of the 
conversion layer must be performed depending 
on the surface protection type. This results also 
in an improvement of the corrosion protection.

A coating thickness measurement device 
based on the X-ray fluorescence measuring

allows the measurement of the coating thick-

4.2  可以这样选择测量时间，即重复精密度小

the standard deviation of measuring values un-

equipment, same specimen with same measur-
ing point, short intervals between measure-
ments).
