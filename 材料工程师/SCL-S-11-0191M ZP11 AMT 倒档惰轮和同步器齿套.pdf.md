Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）惰轮和齿套由于受到较大的工作载荷，而出现断齿失效；断裂形式为典型的脆性和剪切型断裂，而非疲
劳。A big working stress led the teeth to break and finally made the idler gear and ring gear fail; the fracture type is classical brittle and shear , not of fatigue。  
（2）材料的成分，组织和硬度正常。The chemical components, microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions: 
（1） DRE 和供应商充分说明齿套和惰轮齿部正常的载荷和接触区。DRE and supplier to show full analysis regarding normal load 
and contact area.
