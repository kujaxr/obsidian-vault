Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 缸盖热疲劳开裂失效，其中2，3 缸裂穿，1，4 缸开裂，开裂主要与热交变应力过大有关。The cylinder cover 
thermal fatigue cracked failure which is mainly relation to the excessive thermal alternation stress. The 2 and 3 
cylinder leaked , the 1 and 4 cylinder cracked. 
（2） 失效缸盖材料成分合格，金相组织和孔隙正常。The components of failed cylinder cover are qualified, the 
hardness、microstructure and porosity are normal 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 试验前确认零件状态，避免因零件异常而造成试验资源浪费。Confirm the status of the parts before the test to 
avoid waste of test resources caused by abnormal parts.



 
图7 的断口区域金相未见明显气孔类缺陷存在； 
 
结合此前类似案例的分析，认为本次失效与之前的案例基本一致，主要是热应力引起的疲劳开裂失效。
