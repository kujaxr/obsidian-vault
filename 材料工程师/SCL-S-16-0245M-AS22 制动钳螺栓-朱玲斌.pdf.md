Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 螺栓过载断裂失效，这可能与装配过程及对手件表面状态有关。The bolt overload fracture, which may be 
related to the assembly process and the surface state of the opponent. 
（2） 失效螺栓材料成分和硬度合格，组织和牙型正常。同批次螺栓摩擦系数测试结果合格。The composition and 
hardness of the failed bolt are qualified, the microstructure and tooth type are normal. The friction coefficient of 
same batch bolt is qualified. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 南京新产线采用与临港完全一致的螺栓装配工艺，包括装配转速要求和增加转角控制。The Nanging new 
production line use the same assembly process with Lingang, including the assembly speed requirements and 
increase angle control.
