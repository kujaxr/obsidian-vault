Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 零件焊接位置虚焊，形成了隐裂纹，导致失效问题发生。 
The welding position was not welded properly, forming hidden cracks, resulting in failure.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商通过优化工装等方式改善焊接面熔合状态，避免失效问题发生。 
                 Supplier improves the fusion state of the welding surface by optimizing tooling and other 
methods to avoid failure.
