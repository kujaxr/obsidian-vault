Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件路试过程中受过大外力后沿熔接痕位置开裂。 
The failed part cracked in the weld line during the road test. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1.供应商优化注塑工艺&零件结构以提高熔接痕位置强度。 
                   Supplier optimizes injection molding process & structure strength to increase weld joint position 
strength. 
2.工程评估此零件结构强度能否满足使用要求。 
                   DRE evaluates the structural strength requirements.
