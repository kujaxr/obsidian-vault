Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：（1）下系杆零件为过载断裂，断裂面未见明显缺陷。The part of lower tie bar is overload fracture，
no obvious defects found on fracture surface 
                （2）下系杆零件硬度、孔隙合格，金相组织正常。The hardness and porosity of lower tie rod parts 
are qualified, and the metallographic structure is normal.
