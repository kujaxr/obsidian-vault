Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 齿圈是由于过载而断裂的；其失效原因是由于尺寸配合紧密，导致装配中出现过载。The gearing ring was 
broken because of overload. The failure is caused by overload during the assembly process because the 
dimension matching is very close. 
（2） 齿圈的材料密度和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The density and hardness meet the requirement and the 
microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 对每批次产品的匹配尺寸进行抽检。Check the key matching dimensions of the samples for every batch. 
（2） 装配完成后，严格执行100%的外观检查，并对不良品进行严格控制。After assembly, make 100% 
visual inspection and control the bad part well. 
（3） 关注售后相同批次的产品。Pay attention to the same batch of parts after sale.
