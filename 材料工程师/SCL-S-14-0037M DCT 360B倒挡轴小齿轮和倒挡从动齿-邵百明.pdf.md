Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）倒档轴小齿轮的3 个齿由于工作载荷较大而先发生疲劳断裂；其断裂后，导致其它齿和倒档从动齿发生过
载断裂。Three teeth of the reverse shaft gear were broken first by fatigue because of a big load, which caused other teeth 
and the reverse idle gear were broken due to overload. 
（2）两齿轮的成分，表面硬度，芯部硬度，淬硬层深，内氧化深度，组织，夹杂物和表面残余应力均符合技术
要求；过渡圆滑。The chemical components, surface hardness, core hardness, hardening depth, internal oxidation depth, 
microstructure, inclusion level and compression stress meet the requirements; the transition is smooth. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核零件的载荷，并通过设计提高其安全系数。Check the load of the part and optimize to improve the safety 
factor.

   利用电镜对19#齿进行观察，其结果如图6~9 所示。可以看出： 





