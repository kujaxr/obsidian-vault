Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承盖毛坯严重缺料，尺寸不符合要求，导致局部凸轮轴匹配面未被加工。The camshaft bearing cap was 
seriously lack of material and size doesn’t meet the requirement, which caused the local matching the surface of the local 
camshaft was not machined. 
（2）基于断口来看，轴承盖材料烧结良好，其开裂是烧结后，在外力作用下过载引起。Basing on the fracture, the 
sintering of the material is well and the camshaft bearing cap was cracked by over load after sintering. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）供应商应查找原因，加强过程控制，避免不合格品流出。Suppliers should find the reasons, strengthen process 
control and avoid the outflow of unqualified products.
