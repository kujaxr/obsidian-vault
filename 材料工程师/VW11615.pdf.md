1 
Scope
The nominal and limit dimensions in Tables 1 to 4 and Figure 1 apply to the final condition phos-
phated or with electrolytic surface protection. For limit dimensions before application of an electro-
lytic surface protection layer, see VW 116 25, before application of a phosphate layer, see
VW 116 14.
Generally, the medium tolerance class according to VW 116 10 is released. The coarse tolerance
class has a smaller pitch depth of engagement and requires special gages. Therefore, it can be
permitted only in exceptional cases, e.g. if the accuracy according to the medium tolerance class
cannot be complied with. It must be specified on the drawing.
2 
Designation
Designation of a metric ISO coarse thread with nominal diameter d = 10 mm and flank lead
P = 1.5 mm, tolerance class 8g for external thread and tolerance class 7H for internal thread:
M10 – 8g (external thread)
M10 – 7H (internal thread).
Designation of a metric ISO fine-pitch thread with nominal diameter d = 10 mm and flank lead
P = 1.25 mm, tolerance class 8g for external thread and tolerance class 7H for internal thread:
M10 x 1.25 – 8g (external thread)
M10 x 1.25 – 7H (internal thread).
The English translation is believed to be accurate. In case
of discrepancies the German version shall govern.
