Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件受外力导致开裂。  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
3. 零件注塑工艺差。 
Conclusions:1. The invalid part was cracked by external forces. 
2. Material performance of the parts conformed to requirements. 3. Injection molding process was bad. 
建议：改善注塑工艺。 
Suggestions: Improve the injection molding process.
