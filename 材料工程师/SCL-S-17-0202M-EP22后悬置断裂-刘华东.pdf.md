Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 后悬置断裂为Y 负方向上的过载断裂。The bracket of lower tie bar fracture is overload fracture. 
2、 后悬置化学成分、硬度和孔隙度符合技术要求，组织存在不均匀，内部有冷隔缺陷。The chemical composition, 
hardness and porosity of the mount meet the requirements; but the microstructure is inhomogeneous, interior has 
cold shut defects. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、校核安装车身改制支架对后悬置受力影响。Check the effect of the subframe rear bracket on the mount. 
2、供应商加强零件质量管控，减少冷隔缺陷，提高铸件质量。Suppliers strengthen quality control, reduce the cold shut 
defects and improve the casting quality.
