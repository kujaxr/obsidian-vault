Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1.失效件为注塑残缺品。2.零件材料符合技术要求。3.零件注塑工艺不佳。 
Conclusions:  1. Failure part was incomplete product. 
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.  
            3. Injection process of part was bad.       
建议： 1.加强检验和管理。2. 改善注塑工艺。 
Suggestions: 1. Checking and management should be improved. 
           2. Injection process should be improved.
