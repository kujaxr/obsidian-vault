Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致脆断。  
2. 零件材料不符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1. The brittle fracture of the part caused by external force.  
2. Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.     
建议： 1.供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
2. 在装配、物流等环节加强对零件的保护。 
Suggestions:  1. Let the supplier explain and rectify the material problems. 
            2. Improve the protection of the part during the assembly and logistics.
