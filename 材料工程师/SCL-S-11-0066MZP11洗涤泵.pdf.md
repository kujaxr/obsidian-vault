Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：经测试，失效件的材料为PA6-GF30，材料无异常，注塑工艺参数较好。水泵出水管的断裂模式是，分
流阀在结构中处于悬空状态，车辆运行时分流阀产生上下震动，导致出水管发生疲劳断裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, it can be seen that the material of failure part is PA6-GF30 which is the design 
requirement and the injection process of the part is good. According to the macroscopic observation and the 
pump structure, it can be seen that the valve is suspended from the motor. The valve will produce a vertical 
vibration during vehicle is running which cause the outlet pipe of the washer pump to fatigue break. 
建议：增大出水管根部圆角；增加水泵减振装置。 
Suggestions：The fillet angle of the outlet pipe should be increased. A bumper should be introduced in the washer 
pump structure.

根据图3 水泵的装配结构可知，电机固定，分流阀通过出水管与电机连接；出水管的断面呈近45o。根据水
泵的装配结构和断面方向，可以判定出水管断裂方向如图中箭头所示。断口周围未发现外界冲击痕迹。 
From the structure of the pump in Fig 3, it can be seen that the motor is fixed and the valve is linked to the motor by 
the outlet pipe of the pump. There is an about 45o crack surface is observed in Fig 3. According to the structure of the 
pump and the direction of the crack surface, it can be known that the fracture direction of the pipe is shown as the arrow. 
And there is no outside impact mark is found near the crack area.

小结：通过水泵的工作状态和断口分析可以判定，水泵出水管的断裂模式是，分流阀在结构中处于悬空状态，
车辆运行时分流阀产生上下震动，导致出水管发生疲劳断裂。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and the pump structure, it can be seen that the valve is 
suspended from the motor. The valve will produce a vertical vibration during vehicle is running which cause 
the outlet pipe of the washer pump to fatigue break.
