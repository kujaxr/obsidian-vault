Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：经测试，失效件管路内的堵塞物与失效件为同一材料——PA66。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the failure part and the blockage in the pipe of it both consist of PA66.  
建议：供应商对生产工艺和流程进行检查。 
Suggestions：Supplier should check up on technology and process of production.
