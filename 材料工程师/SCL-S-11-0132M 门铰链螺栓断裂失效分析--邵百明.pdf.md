Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）螺栓承受了较大的载荷，而发生了过载断裂。The bolt suffered a big load and broke. 
（2）螺栓的成分，组织，硬度，夹杂物以及螺纹的表面状态（脱碳和裂纹）均满足产品要求；The chemical components, 
microstructure, hardness, inclusion and the screw thread surface checking result ( decarburization  and crack) are all ok. 
（3）垫片的成分，组织和硬度满足产品要求。The chemical components, microstructure and hardness meet the requirements of the 
washer. 
 
建议Suggestions: 
（1）建议DRE 和实验工程师从产品和实验角度寻找过载的原因。Suggest that DRE and experiment engineer look for the 
reason of the overload from the part and experiment angles.

III. 测试结果与分析 Test Results and Analysis 
（1） 失效产品 Failure Spring 
     断裂失效的螺栓如图1 所示。其强度级别为10.9 级；规格为M8；表面涂层为锌铝镀层。垫片发生了明显
的变形；螺栓断裂面与轴线呈现45°。与此同时，可以发现与其匹配的零件发生了弯曲变形。The broke bolt is showed 
as fig. 1; the strength level is 10.9; the specification is M8 and the coating is zinc and aluminum. The washer obviously distorted and the broke 
surface is 45°with the axle of the bolt.

①  左后门铰链和垫片发生了明显的弯曲变形，表明在测试中门铰链和螺栓曾受到了较大的载荷，；The 
bending of the left door hinge and washer is visible, which shows that the door hinge and bolt bore a big load. 
②  右后和前门铰链和垫片未见明显变形；The distortion of the right door hinges and washers is unconspicuous.

（2）断口Fracture Surface 
    断口的边部分布有光滑的剪切区；其余断面相对粗糙，并有明显的纹路；未见任何缺陷，如图3（a）所示。 
断口处的螺纹发生了明显的塑性变形，但未见表面裂纹，表明螺栓具有较好的塑性，曾承受了较大的载荷，而 
发生塑性断裂，如图3（b）所示。 A smooth shear region is in the edge of fracture surface; other fracture region is rough and with clear  
texture, but without any defect in the fracture surface, showed as fig. 3 (a). The screws in the broken region were distorted clearly, but there are  
not any cracks on the surface, which showed that the bolt has good ductility and suffered a big load, which caused the break, showed as fig.3 (b).
