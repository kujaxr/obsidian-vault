Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 断口具有疲劳特征。The fracture belongs to fatigue fracture. 
2. 引起疲劳断裂的原因可能是球销未锁紧，在颠簸的路面试车过程中造成失效件疲劳断裂。 
Cause of the fatigue fracture is due to insufficient clamping force leading to fatigue fracture during Pave test driving. 
3. 硬度、金相组织符合技术要求。The hardness and microstructure for failure part and normal part meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 请进一步分析球销的装配、使用情况。Please check the assembly condition and working condition.
