Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）电机不工作是因为ECU针脚向一侧倾斜，与电机端针脚搭接量小，从而引起接触不良。The motor did not work 
because the ECU pins tilted to one side and caused a small overlap with the motor end pins, which resulted in poor 
contact. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化电机设计，增大与ECU 针脚的接触区域，以提升系统鲁棒性。 Optimize the motor design and increase the 
contact area with ECU pins to enhance system robustness. 
（2）对ECU 针脚焊接后的倾斜程度进行监控。Monitor the inclination of ECU pins after welding.
