Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论 Conclusions: 
1. 拉索承受疲劳载荷而断裂；这是由于钩在门把手小孔内旋转不畅造成的。 The cables have fractured through 
fatigue loading, caused by poor rotation of the hook fitting within the door handle eyelet. 
2. 钢丝硬度合格。The wire hardness is acceptable. 
3. 钢丝图纸要求不明确，必须更新，包括零件的强度要求。 The drawing specification of the wire is poor and must 
be updated to include the strength requirement of the part. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 改善钩子在门把手孔内的旋转。Improve the rotation movement between handle and hook. 
2. 依据开发过程中的测试，更新图纸。 Update drawing according to the parts which passed development 
testing.

/ T 9944（2002）的要求，然而无法利用失效件进行相关的测试。  
在报告SCL-S-12-0040M，对一条新的拉索进行拉伸测试，其硬度与失效件的硬度相当，结果如表所示，因此我
们可以得出结论失效件符合GB / T 9944 的拉伸强度要求。 
The drawing does not include any specification for the composition or strength of the stainless steel wire.  According to 
the supplier the wire raw material is 304 grade stainless steel and the finished cable should meet the requirements of 
GB/T 9944 (2002).  However it’s not possible to test to this requirement on the failed part.
