Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1.杯托受力导致失效。 
       2.失效件材料符合质量要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The great stress on the cup stand make it fail. 
            2. Material performance of failure part conformed to requeirements. 
建议： 1.更改杯托位置，使之不易受到驾驶员的撞击。 
Suggestions：1. The position of cup stand should be changed to avoid striking from driver.
