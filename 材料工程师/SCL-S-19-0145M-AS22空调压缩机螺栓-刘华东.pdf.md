Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、螺栓断裂是由于表面硬度高而引起的脆性断裂。The crack of bolt was brittle fracture caused by higher surface 
hardness. 
2、失效螺栓及同批次螺栓表面硬度不符合技术要求，金相组织和心部硬度满足要求。The surface hardness of failure 
bolt and the same batch bolts do not meet the technique requirement. The core hardness and microstructure of bolts meet 
the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离该批次零件并要求供应商整改。Isolate these bolts and require the supplier to rectification. 
2、对该零件进行100%涡流分选，避免不合格品流出。100% eddy current sorting of the bolts to avoid the outflow of 
unqualified products.
