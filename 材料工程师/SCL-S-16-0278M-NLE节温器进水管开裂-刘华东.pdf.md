Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）节温器壳体总成暖风进水管焊接接头泄露是由于焊缝局部无钎料填充而形成贯穿性的孔洞所致。The leakage 
of the welding joint for the temperature sensor was caused by a hole through the welding seam which was because no 
filler filled the area.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）排查生产过程中可能影响钎焊不良的原因，如水管孔口毛刺、钎焊匹配面的尺寸、钎料的填充等；并对关
键环节进行控制。Investigate the factors which may cause the bad welding from the production process，such as pipe 
hole burr, the dimension matching surface, the solder painting and so on; Finally control the key process.
