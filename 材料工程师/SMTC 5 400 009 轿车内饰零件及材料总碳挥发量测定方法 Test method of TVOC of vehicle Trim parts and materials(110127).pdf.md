1 范围 
本标准规定了轿车内饰材料总碳挥发量（总
有机物挥发）测定的试验设备、试验准备、试验
步骤和试验结果的计算。 
 
本标准适用于所有可能对车内空气质量产
生影响的零件和材料。 
 
1  Scope  
The Standards stipulates the experi-
mental equipment, experimental preparation, 
experimental procedure, and calculation of 
experimental results of TVOC (Total Volatile 
organic Compounds) of vehicle trim materials. 
The Standard applies to all parts and 
materials likely to affect air quality in vehicles. 
2 实验设备 
 
2  Experimental Equipment 
2.1 气相色谱仪：带有分流-不分流-进样口和
火焰电离化检测器（FID）。 
 
 
2.1  Gas chromatograph: be provided with 
split stream—non-split stream—injection port 
and flame ionization detector (FID). 
2.2 顶空进样瓶。 
 
 
2.2  Headspace vial. 
2.3 毛细分离柱：内径0.25 mm，膜层 0.25 
μ m，长度30 m， 100%聚乙烯乙二醇分离相。 
 
 
2.3  Capillary separating column: internal 
diameter 0.25mm, film, 0.25μm, length 30m, 
and 100% polyethylene glycol separation 
phase. 
2.4 分析天平：精确度0.1 mg。 
 
 
2.4  Analytical balance: precision 0.1mg. 
2.5  微升注射器：精度5μ l 
 
2.5  Micro-liter syringe: precision 5μl.

3  Experimental Preparation 
3.1 顶空进样器参数 
 
3.1  Parameters of Headspace Injector 
3.1.1 温度：加热炉120 ℃；定量管150 ℃；
转移管180 ℃。 
 
3.1.1  Temperature: heating furnace 120 ℃; 
quantitative tube 150 ℃; and transfer tube 
180 ℃. 
3.1.2 时间：压力升高持续19 s；气体压出16 
s；进样持续5 s。 
 
3.1.2 Time: pressure rise lasts 19 s; gas extru-
sion 16 s; and injection 5 s.  
3.1.3 压力：顶空瓶内部压力23.2 Psi。 
 
 
3.1.3  Vial pressure 23.2 Psi. 
3.2 测试条件 
 
3.2  Test Conditions 
3.2.1 GC-炉温：50 ℃保持3 min 恒温，以12

3.2.1  GC-furnace temperature: maintain the 
constant temperature at 50℃ for 3 minutes,

3.3 样品准备 
 
3.3  Sample Preparation 
3.3.1 预处理：样品应采用涂铝的聚乙烯袋运
输和存储；取样应该在样品进货后直接进行，供
货时间和取样时间应该固定；天然化合物（如棉，
木头，皮革，毛）用氯化钙干燥24 h，其他物
质无需处理，但在称量前应破碎成小块。 
 
3.3.1 Pretreatment ：Samples should be 
transported and stored with aluminum-coated 
polythene bags; sampling should be con-
ducted immediately after the supply of sam-
ples, and sampling time and supply time 
should be fixed; natural compounds (such as 
cotton, wood, leather) should be dried with 
calcium chloride for 24 hours. Other materials 
have no need to be treated, but broken into 
small pieces before weighing. 
3.3.2 采样：应从材料上作有标记并通过整个
试样横截面的位置取样，并把试样破碎成大于
10 mg 小于25 mg 的小块。注意，不能使小块变
热。如果必要，可以在图纸上或供货技术条件中
规定采用另外一种取样准备方法，但必须在取样
结果的说明中标出来。附着于金属零件的有机物
质，如漆、粘结材料等要先用机械方法去除金属
后再进行称量。 
 
3.3.2  Sampling：Sampling should be made at 
the position marked through the whole trans-
verse section, and samples should be broken 
into small pieces weighing above 10mg but 
below 25mg. It should be noted that small 
pieces can not become hot. If necessary, 
another sample preparation method can be 
stipulated in the drawings or supply technical 
conditions. And the method should be marked 
in the description of sampling results. Organic 
substances attached to metal spare parts, 
such as paint, labels, adhesives should be 
weighed after the removal of metal through 
mechanical method.  
3.3.3 样品数量：样品数量是以顶空进样瓶的
大小为准，最小容积是5 ml，每10 ml 玻璃杯
容积称入1.000 g±0.001 g 的试样材料（即称
量误差最大为0.1%），称量前必须去掉金属零
件。把样品零件放入一只顶空进样瓶内测量（每
个样品至少3 瓶），玻璃瓶用聚四氟乙烯密封。 
 
3.3.3  Sample Quantity ：Sample quantity is 
subject to the cubic capacity of headspace 
vials, with the maximum cubic capacity being 
5ml. 1.000g±0.001g sample materials are 
weighed into every glass cup of 10ml cubic 
capacity (namely the maximum weighing error 
being 0.1%). Metal spare parts should be re-
moved before weighing. Sample spare parts 
are placed into a headspace vial for mea-
surement (at least three vials for each sample). 
Glass vials are sealed with polytetrafluoroe-
thylene. 
4 试验步骤 
 
4  Experimental Procedure  
4.1 试验方法 
 
4.1  Experimental Method 
4.1.1 将准备好的样品放入顶空进样瓶内，保
持（120±1） ℃恒温5 h±5 min。将一个针头
通过密封片插入瓶中，氦气通过针头注入瓶中，
这样就提高了瓶子上部空间（顶空）中散发有机
物的气体压力。含有机物的气体经过针头从瓶中
压出，再与载气（氦气）一起经过定量管、转移
管，从进样口进入毛细管分离柱被分离开的。 
 
4.1.1 Prepared samples are placed into 
headspace vials, and the temperature is 
maintained at (120±1)℃ for 5h±5min con-
stantly. A pinhead will pierce into vials through 
seal, and helium gas is injected into vials 
through the pinhead, thus lifting the gas pres-
sure of diffusing organic substances in the 
upper space (headspace) of vials. Gas con-
taining organic substances are extruded from 
vials through the pinhead, and then via quan-
titative tube and transfer tube, enters capillary 
separating column through injection port for 
separation along the column. The carrier gas is 
helium.

4.2  Calibration 
4.2.1 绘制校正曲线，以便对碳的总挥发量以
及各种特殊物质数量进行定量标定。采用丙酮作
为总碳挥发的标定物质。 
 
4.2.1  Draw calibration curve so as to quanti-
tatively calibrate the overall volatilization of 
carbon and quantity of various special sub-
stances. Acetone is taken as the demarcating 
substance of the overall volatilization of car-
bon. 
4.2.2 在安装上新的分离柱后，及当仪器上有
改动后应该用7 种浓度进行基本标定，此外，
至少每4 个星期要用3 种浓度进行控制性标定。 
 
4.2.2  After the installation of new separation 
columns and in the case of changes on in-
strument, 7 different concentration should be 
used for basic calibration. Three concentration 
are used for controlling calibration at least 
every four weeks.  
4.2.3 对丙酮进行基本标定时应在正丁醇中配
制浓度为0.1 g、0.5 g、1 g、5 g、10 g、50 g、
100 g 丙酮/l 正丁醇的溶液作为校正样品。控
制性标定时至少要配制0.5g,5g,50g 三种浓度
的溶液，标定前，必须保证在使用的正丁醇无峰
值和丙酮同时出现。 
 
4.2.3  When making basic calibration on 
acetone, workers should prepare solution of 
acetone/l n-butyl alcohol with concentration 
being 0.1 g, 0.5 g, 1 g, 5 g, 10 g, 50 g, and 100 
g as the correcting sample. In controlling cali-
bration, they should prepare solutions with 
thicknesses being 0.5g, 5g, and 50g at least. 
Before calibration, it should be guaranteed that 
peak-less values of acetone and n-butyl alco-
hol appear at the same time.  
4.2.4 另外，所有标定时使用的物质必须至少
符合每年的质量要求。 
 
4.2.4  In addition, substances used for all ca-
libration should conform to annual quality re-
quirements. 
4.2.5 在标定测量时，用一个5 μ l 的注射器
从10 ml 玻璃容器中取2 μ l 溶液注射进一个
空的、气密的顶空进样瓶中。应注意，注射器中
不能有气泡。 
 
4.2.5  In calibration measure, a 5 μl injector is 
used to obtain 2 μl solution from a 10 ml glass 
vessel, which is then injected into an empty 
and airtight headspace vials. It should be 
noted that there should be no bubble in the 
injector.  
4.2.6 标定样品在120 ℃下经过 1 h 的恒温
处理后，按试验规范进行分析，溶剂稀释后气相
色谱分析仪温度图表中断。对每种标定溶液至少
进行3 次测量。 
 
4.2.6 After going through 1h constant temper-
ature treatment under the temperature of 
120 ℃, calibrated samples are analyzed in 
terms of experimental criteria. After solution is 
diluted, temperature chart of gas chromato-
graphic analyzer is halted. Each kind of cali-
brated solution should be measured at least 
three times.

5.2 计算方法 
 
5.2  Computational method 
5.2.1 碳的总散发值EG（μ gC/g）： 
由分析样品得出的总峰值面积及丙酮标定
的标定系数k(G)得出： 
 
EG=[（总峰值面积-峰值面积空白值）/ k(G)] 
×2×0.6204 
 
式中： 
EG —— 碳的总散发值，单位：μ gC/g； 
k(G) —— 丙酮标定的标定系数 
2 —— 与“g 试样”有关，因此，虽然试样为1 g，
也要将2 μ l 的标定液装入10 ml 玻璃杯中； 
  0.6204 —— 表示丙酮中碳的含量。 
 
5.2.1  Overall emanation value of carbon 
EG(μgC/g): 
Obtained from the overall peak value 
area obtained from analytical samples and 
calibration coefficient k(G) of acetone deduce: 
EG=[(overall peak value area –peak value area 
baseline value)/ k(G)]×2×0.6204 
In the formula:  
EG—Overall emanation value of carbon; 
k(G) —calibration coefficient; 
2—related to ―g sample‖. Therefore, although the 
sample weighs 1 g, 2 μl calibrated solution is injected 
into 10ml glass cup; 
0.6204—indicating the content of carbon in acetone.
