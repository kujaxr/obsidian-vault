Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂，断裂位置与弹簧工作应力最大区域吻合。共有两个疲劳区，其中一个起源于弹簧次
表面的大颗粒非金属夹杂物，另一个起源于弹簧表面。 
Failure for the part belongs to fatigue fracture ，fracture location located maximum stress area during spring 
runing. there are two fatigue zones, one of which originated in the spring of large particles of sub-surface 
non-metallic inclusions, another originated in the spring surface. 
2、 非金属夹杂物尺寸不符合技术要求，其他的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The non-metallic inclusion size did not meet the requirement ,other material properties of parts meet the technical 
requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强原材料质量控制。To strengthen the quality control for raw materials. 
2、 请校核该气门弹簧实际工况中的受力情况。Please check the forces of the valve spring during the actual 
condition .
