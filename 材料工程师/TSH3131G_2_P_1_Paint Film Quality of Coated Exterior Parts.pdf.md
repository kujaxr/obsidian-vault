1. Scope 
This standard covers the quality of paint film to be applied onto automobile exterior parts for 
decorative purpose.  Parts required of rust prevention shall be subject to rust prevention standard(s) 
to be specified separately. 
In addition, the pigment used for coatings to be applied onto automobile exterior parts shall not be 
designed using a micro-titanium oxide without surface treatment.

4. Test Methods 
Prepare the test panels of necessary size by cutting them out from the product prepared in normal 
process, or by using flat panels made of the same material as that of the product and coated in the 
same or equivalent coating process as that of the product. 
 
4.1 Appearance 
Check the appearance with visual observation. 
 
4.2 Tone 
Check the appearance with visual observation. 
 
4.3 Paint Film Thickness 
As specified in ISO 2808 and JIS K 5600-1-7. 
 
4.4 Specular Gloss 
Measure the 60 deg specular gloss as specified in Section 3 of TSH1519G. 
 
4.5 Hardness 
As specified in Section 2.1 of TSH1539G.  Test specimens shall be excluded from evaluation if their 
base materials are broken. 
 
4.6 Impact Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1 of TSH1504G.  Test specimens shall be excluded from evaluation if their 
base materials are cracked. 
 
4.7 Print Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1 of TSH1501G. 
 
4.8 Adhesive Property 
As specified in Section 2 of TSH1503G. 
 
4.9 Water Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1 of TSH1505G. 
 
4.10 Water Discoloration Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1.1 of TSH1509G. 
 
4.11 Alkali Discoloration Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1.2 of TSH1509G. 
 
4.12 Acid Discoloration Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1.3 of TSH1509G.

4.13 Gasoline Resistance 
As specified in Section 2.1.2 of TSH1508G.  However, use No. 1 gasoline as the test fluid. 
 
4.14 Abrasion Resistance 
Drill a hole of about 7 mm diameter through the center of the specimen of 100 
 100 mm (t = 3 mm 
or thicker) size, then fit it to the taber abrasion tester specified in Section 4.6 of TSL2105G, and 
conduct the rotating abrasion test with the load of 4.9 N using a CS#10 abrasion disc (5).  Determine 
the number of test cycles required to cause the exposure of the plastic surface due to abrasion and 
peeling of coating. 
As for the judgment of test result, it shall be deemed to have failed the test if the plastic surface is 
exposed even if it is caused by partial peeling of the coated film (the plastic surface that can be seen 
through the coating is acceptable). 
Prior to using the abrasion disc, use S-11 polishing paper and conduct the 50-time polishing twice with 
the specified measurement load, then conduct the test with the abrasion disc.  The measuring 
abrasion disc shall be polished 50 times at the specified measurement load per 500 test cycles. 
The S-11 polishing paper shall be replaced by a new one per 50 times polishing. 
 
4.15 Light Permeability 
As specified in Section 2.1 of TSH1520G.  The coated film thickness to be measured for light 
permeability shall be the minimum coat film thickness specified for the part subject to the 
measurement. 
 
4.16 Accelerated Weathering Resistance 
Xenon test shall be used as the test method as a rule.  In the case where there is no Xenon 
weathering resistance, SWOM test shall be able to be chosen. 
Color difference shall be measured in accordance with Section 3.3.1 (1) in TSH1518G. 
 
4.16.1 Xenon Method A 
Conduct evaluation in accordance with the Method A specified in Section 3.1 in TSH1585G, using the 
tester specified in Section 2.1 or 2.3 in TSH1585G. 
 
 
 
4.16.2 Xenon Method B 
Conduct evaluation in accordance with the Method B specified in Section 3.2 in TSH1585G, using the 
tester specified in Section 2.1 or 2.3 in TSH1585G.

4.16.3 SWOM Method A 
Conduct evaluation in accordance with the Method A specified in Section 3.1 in TSH1585G, using the

4.16.4 SWOM Method B 
Conduct evaluation in accordance with the Method B specified in Section 3.2 in TSH1585G, using the

4.17 Outdoor Weather Resistance 
As specified in Section 3.1 of TSH1581G.  However, omit the once-in three month waxing, and 
conduct the washing alone. It shall be deemed to have passed the test if the change in gloss retention 
rate, color difference 
E*, chalking, fading, bronzing, smear, etc. are not significant after testing for 
12 months.  Report on blistering, cracking and peeling after testing. 
 
4.18 Salt Spray Test 
Conduct the salt spray test for 240 h as specified in TSH1552G.  After washing, conduct the 
cross-cut test as specified in TSH1503G to check the adhesive property. 
 
4.19 Cyclic Corrosion Resistance 
Apply cross-cuts into the coated film in advance, and conduct the cyclic corrosion accelerated test 
with the Method A (CCT) specified in TSH1555G as follows.  Conduct the tape peeling test over the 
cross-cuts 3 times on both sides per 10 cycles of CCT, and repeat the test up to 60 cycles.  It shall 
be deemed to have passed the test if the peeled portion at each side is less than 3 mm.

4.20 Weathering Adhesion Cycle Resistance 
Use Xenon in the accelerated weathering resistance test as a rule.  In the case where there is no 
Xenon weathering resistance, SWOM test may be able to be chosen. 
 
4.20.1 Xenon Method I 
Conduct a test at 250 MJ/m2 in accordance with the Method A specified in Section 3.1 in TSH1585G, 
using the tester specified in Section 2.1 or 2.3 in TSH1585G.  After conducting a water resistance 
test for 48 h in accordance with Section 2.1 in TSH1505G, conduct a cross cut adhesion test specified 
in Section 2.2 in TSH1503G.  Then perform the additional Xenon test at 250 MJ/m2 and after 
conducting the additional water resistance test for 48 h, conduct the additional cross cut adhesion test 
specified in Section 2.2 in TSH1503G. 
 
 
 
4.20.2 SWOM Method I 
Conduct a test for 400 h in accordance with the Method A specified in Section 3.1 in TSH1582G, 
using the tester specified in Section 2.1 in TSH1582G.  After conducting a water resistance test for 
48 h in accordance with Section 2.1 in TSH1505G, conduct a cross cut adhesion test specified in 
Section 2.2 in TSH1503G.  Then perform the additional SWOM test for 400 h and after conducting 
the additional water resistance test for 48 h, conduct the additional cross cut adhesion test.
