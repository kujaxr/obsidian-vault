Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  面套表面变色是TDA导致的，TDA随光照扩散，污染面套表皮。 
The surface discoloration of the cover is caused by TDA, and TDA diffuses with light and 
pollutes the surface. 
2.  当前已被污染的表皮无法擦拭干净。 
Currently contaminated skin cannot be wiped clean. 
建议Suggestions：1.  国利调查TDA来源。 
                    Let Guoli investigate the source of TDA.
