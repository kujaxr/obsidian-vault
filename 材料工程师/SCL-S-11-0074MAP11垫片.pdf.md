Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 垫片与凸台垂直连接，无圆角过渡，在连接处容易产生应力集中。 
2. 经测试，失效件和新件的材料为PA66+PTFE+30%GF，失效件和新件PTFE 的含量分别为8%和10%，
与要求（15%）相差较大。 
Conclusions: 1. A stress concentration easily appeared at the connection area of the gasket’s boss and the gasket 
because there is no radius at the area.  
2. The material of failure part and new part is PA66+PTFE+30%GF. The PTFE contents of them are 8% and 
10% respectively, which are lower compared with the requirement (15%). 
建议：供应商保证填料含量；增加凸台与垫片连接处圆角过度。 
Suggestions：The filler contents of the part should be guaranteed. A radius should be introduced in the connection 
area of the gasket’s boss and the gasket.

小结：经测试，失效件和新件的材料为PA66+PTFE+30%GF，PTFE 含量与要求相比偏低。由于第一次熔融
过程升温温度较高，造成PA66 分解，所以第二次熔融过程在失效件和新件中未见PA66 熔点和焓值。
失效件和新件中的PTFE 第一次熔融过程和第二次熔融过程中的熔融焓变化很小，说明零件注塑工艺参
数较好。 
Conclusion: After the tests, we can see that the material of failure part and new part is PA66+PTFE+30%GF. The 
PTFE contents of them are lower compared with the requirement. Due to the temperature of the first heating 
circle is so high that the PA66 has been decomposed, so there are no melt points and enthalpy of PA66 are 
observed in the second heating circle. The changes of ΔH of the PTFE in failure part and new part between 
the first circle and the second circle are little, so the injection processes of the parts are good.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图2 左断口形貌和图3 右断口形貌可以看出，垫片的两侧都断在凸台的根部，断口比较粗糙，符合PA
材料断裂形貌特点，在断面上未发现空洞等缺陷。通过图4 的垫片结构可以看出，凸台与垫片垂直连接，无圆角
过渡，造成凸台与垫片连接处容易发生应力集中。 
From Fig 2 and Fig 3, it can be seen that the left and right cracks occur at the corner of the gasket’s boss. The crack 
surfaces are coarse which are the same as the crack character of PA material. There are no voids in the crack areas. 
According to the structure of a new gasket, a stress concentration appeared at the connection area of the gasket’s boss and 
the gasket.

小结：通过离合器的工作状态和断口分析可以判定，垫片和凸台连接处存在应力集中区域，垫片受外力冲击
时，容易导致垫片在凸台根部断裂，随后破碎的垫片与离合器壳体和弹簧发生摩擦。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and the gasket structure, it can be seen that the gasket was 
broken at the stress concentration area by an outside force. And then the broken gasket into the gap of clutch 
shell which cause the friction between the broken gasket and the clutch.
