Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件受112℃以上高温后熔化。 
The failed part is melted after being heated at a temperature above 112 ° C. 
2. 零件材料符合要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1.采用无纺布替代原LDPE座椅保护套。 
                 Replace the original LDPE seat cover with non-woven fabric. 
2. 评估前挡玻璃改为2.1G+0.76PVB+2.1C及以上配置的的可行性。 
                 Evaluate the feasibility of changing the front windshield to 2.1G+0.76PVB+2.1C or above.
