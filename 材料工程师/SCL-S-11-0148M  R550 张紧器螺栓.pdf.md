Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于夹紧力不足，螺栓出现疲劳失效。The bolt has failed through fatigue, due to lack of clamping force. 
2. 螺栓头部不寻常的痕迹表明螺栓装配后，又被拆卸；有可能螺栓返工拧紧不正确。Unusual traces and marks on the bolt 
head, suggest the bolt was untightened after initial assembly, it’s possible that it was not re-tightened correctly.  
3. 失效件芯部硬度、螺牙缺陷、表面全脱碳层深度、基体组织均合格。  The core hardness, microstructure and defect level 
meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 确保后续螺栓返工装配操作正确。Ensure that bolts are retightened correctly following re-work.

III. 测试结果 Test Results  
一、宏观观察： 
 
断裂螺栓宏观形貌如图1 所示；螺栓的头部每个面的两边有不正常的痕迹。这表明螺栓在生产中不容易装配，
或者在最初装配之后，又进行了拆卸。The bolt head shows unusual tool marks on both edges of each face, this 
indicates that either the bolt could not be assembled easily during production, or that it was untightened some 
time after initial assembly.   
 
螺栓头部似乎还有点火花灼烧的痕迹（见图2）。A further mark on the bolt head seems to be from electrical 
sparking (figure 2).   
 
螺杆部位有清晰的磨损痕迹，这是由于夹紧力不足，导致杆部与张紧轮内部接触，发生相对运动所至。The bolt 
shaft shows a wear trace, from contact with the tensioner inner diameter, due to loss of clamping force.  
 
张紧轮的表面有清晰的接触痕迹表明螺栓曾经正确拧紧过。 A clear contact trace on the tensioner surface 
suggests the bolt was tightened correctly initially.
