Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过胶管的宏观分析可以判定，胶管内壁存在空洞缺陷，在压力作用下裂纹以空洞为裂纹源快速向
两侧扩展，当内壁被贯穿后，油液通过编织层渗透到外壁，外壁在局部压力作用下开裂。 
      2．失效件的材料和力学性能满足设计要求。 
Conclusions: 1. According to the macroscopic observation, it can be seen that the crack started from a gas hole in the 
pipe inner layer and it propagated toward the both sides under a oil pressure quickly. Then the exterior layer 
of pipe was broken by the penetrated oil pressure while the interior layer was broken.  
2. The mechanical property and material of the failure part conform to the design requirement.  
 建议：材料供应商对胶管挤压工艺优化，避免产生微观缺陷。 
Suggestions：The extrusion process of the rubber pipe should be optimized for the micro-defect avoidance.

小结：通过胶管的宏观分析可以判定，胶管内壁存在空洞缺陷，在压力作用下裂纹以空洞为裂纹源快速向两
侧扩展，当内壁被贯穿后，油液通过编织层渗透到外壁，外壁在局部压力作用下开裂；高压管开裂后
油压下降，随之裂纹扩展速度减慢，从而产生了裂缝边缘区域的疲劳特征。  
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation of the rubber pipe, a clear hole defect is found in interior 
layer of the pipe. It can be seen that the crack started from the defect and propagated towards the both sides 
under a pressure. Then the exterior layer of the pipe was broken by the inner oil pressure when the interior 
layer was penetrated. After the pipe break, the inner oil pressure decreased, and the crack propagation 
velocity decreased accordingly. So, the clear fatigue trace is observed at the end of crack region.
