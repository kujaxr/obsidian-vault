Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1.  加强板焊缝未100%熔透导致后扭转梁疲劳开裂。    
   Incomplete welding fusion of strength plate result in t he rear torsion beam cracking by fatigue. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1.  调整焊接工艺参数如焊枪角度。 
   Adjust the welding process parameter such as welding angle.

反馈，开裂区域CAE 分析应力不高。
