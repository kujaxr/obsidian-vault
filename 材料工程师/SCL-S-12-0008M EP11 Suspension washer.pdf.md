Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于材料强度不够, 前减振器下垫片断裂。小垫片变形，导致锁紧力降低。随后裂纹从焊缝的应力集中
点产生，随后扩展穿透垫片，导致其失效。The suspension washer has failed due to insufficient strength material.  
Embedment of the fastener lead to a reduction in clamping load. Subsequently cracks formed at stress concentration 
points caused by the welding, and propagated through the washer material. 
2. 制造过程中, 大垫片的厚度从2.2mm 降低到1.5mm。 During the production process the thickness of the big 
washer was reduced from 2.2mm to 1.5mm. 
3. 新件的焊接质量合格, 材料硬度, 金相组织符合要求。On a new part, the welding quality was OK and the 
materials met the requirements of the specifications. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 增加两垫片材料强度。Increase the material strength grade of both washers. 
2. 制造过程中, 注意不要让大垫片的局部厚度过度减薄。Take care not to reduce material thickness during 
production process.

Fracture Analysis 
 
The fracture surfaces of the welds were completely worn, so no fracture features were visible.  However 
the general shape of the fracture surface could be seen (figure 3). 
The fracture surface around the bigger washer was less worn, and features of fatigue initiated at the inner 
surface could be found in one area, (figure 4).  The adjacent areas showed increasing amount of shear 
fracture progressing in both directions, until the final fracture, opposite to the origin. 
From the fracture surfaces it can be concluded that the weldments failed first, then slippage of the small 
washer and subsequent uneven loading caused the circumferential crack of the large washer.

The upper surface of the washer shows abrasion, which was caused by movement between the clamped surfaces (figure 
5a).  The lower surface shows very heavy embedment (deformation), showing that the material is not hard enough to 
bear the clamping load applied during assembly (figure 5b).  This embedment initially led to a reduction in clamping 
load, allowing the cracks to initiate. 
 
 
 
Figure 5  a) Upper surface of the washer shows abrasion.  b) Lower surfce of the washer shows very heavy 
embedment from the fastener 
 
New part 
 
A new part was submitted to check the quality of the welding.  Cross-sections were cut as shown in figure 6.  The 
weldments show slight contamination at the interface between the washers, but there is good fusion between the 
materials, so the weld can be judged as OK.   
Since there is a small gap between the washers, a stress concentration exists here, which could contribute to the 
premature failure. The shape of the heat affected zone is similar to the fracture shape, showing that the cracks propagated 
through the softer raw material (figures 7 & 8). 
Also noticable in cross section is significant thinning of the larger washer.  The original sheet thickness of 2mm is 
reduced to 1.5mm.
