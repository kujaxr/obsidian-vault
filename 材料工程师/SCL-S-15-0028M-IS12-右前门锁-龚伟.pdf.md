Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受异常外力导致脆断。  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1. The brittle fracture of the part caused by abnormal external force.  
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.     
建议： 1. 在装配、物流等环节加强对零件的保护。 
Suggestions:  1. Improve the protection of the part during the assembly and logistics.
