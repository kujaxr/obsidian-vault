Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效断裂的球头在油漆工序之前已存在了原始裂纹。 
There was  an original  crack  before coating  process. 
2、 球头实物质量不理想，但不是根本原因。 
The  quality for the failure part  is not good, microstructure  segregation is too serious. but  that  is not root  reason for failure.
