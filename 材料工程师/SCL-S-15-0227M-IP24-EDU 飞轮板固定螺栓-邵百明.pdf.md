Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）由于轴向夹紧力不足引起螺栓松脱。The lack of axial clamping force caused the bolt loose 
（2）松脱螺栓与未松脱螺栓的硬度均符合技术要求，也未见脱碳和明显螺牙缺陷；组织均正常；两者无差异。
The loose and unloose bolts’ hardness meet the requirement, no decarbonization and screw tooth defects were found and the microstructure is normal. They are same. 
（3）飞轮板装配孔的平行度和粗糙度符合要求；平面度正常；其材料的硬度波动较大，局部点的硬度偏低。The 
parallel degree and roughness of the reaction board’s assembly hole and the plane degree is normal; the hardness vibration is obvious and the local hardness is lower than 
the requirement. 
（4）转子支架的内螺牙未见异常；材料的硬度偏高。The inner screws of the rotor frame are normal and the hardness is higher than the 
requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）评估零件的装配工艺，同时避免出现假扭矩。Evaluate the assembly process and at the same time avoid the false torque.  
（2）加强热处理过程控制，确保产品的质量。Strengthen heat treatment process control to ensure the product quality.
