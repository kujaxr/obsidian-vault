4 程序 
4  Specimen preparation 
4.1 将软管围绕成形器弯曲，用带子或绳索固
定。 
4.1  Bend the hose around the former and 
secure in place by means of a band and cord. 
4.2 将组合件放入所要求温度下的恒温箱内一
段规定的时间。 
4.2  Place the assembly in the oven at the 
required temperature for the specified time. 
4.3 将组合件从恒温箱内取出，使之冷却至室
温，然后将软管从成形器上移去。 
4.3  Remove the assembly from the oven, 
allow to cool to room temperature, and then 
remove the hose from the former. 
4.4 将软管放在平面上矫直，并检验是否有龟
裂、炭化、粘附力降低或其它任何缺陷。 
4.4  Straighten the hose by placing on a flat 
surface and examine for cracking, charring, 
loss of adhesion or any other defect.
