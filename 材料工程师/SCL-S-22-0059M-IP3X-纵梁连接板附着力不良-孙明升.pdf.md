Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）纵梁连接板热冲压件表面存在氧化皮，导致电泳漆附着力不良。 
There was oxide skin on the surface of hot stamping parts of longitudinal beam connecting plate, resulting in poor 
adhesion of electrophoretic paint. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）喷丸后，进行表面外观检查，确保氧化皮100%的去除。 
After shot peening, conduct surface appearance inspection to ensure 100% removal of oxide skin.
