Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 螺栓是由于过载引起的断裂。 
The failure of the parts due to overload.  
2、失效件的芯部硬度低于技术要求。 
The parts meet the technical requirements of the drawing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 本批螺栓停用。Stop this bench parts 
2、 供应商加强控制过程质量。Control supplier process quality 
3、 进一步校核装配过程和工艺的适宜性。Further check of the assembly process and process suitability.
