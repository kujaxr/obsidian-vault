Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  后驱动轴属于过载扭断。 
The crack propagated by fatigue the welding seam and the plate because of big load. 
2.  后驱动轴材料成分、硬度、硬化层深度满足要求，材料组织正常。 
Chemical components、hardness、harden case meet the requirement and microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 结合故障车的失效路况，重新评估零件的安全系数。 
Evaluate again the safety coefficient based on the failure condition.
