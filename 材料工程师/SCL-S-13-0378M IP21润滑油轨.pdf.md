Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 润滑油轨螺纹孔1#~5#加工异常(见图5~图9)，存在双螺纹且螺纹牙高偏低，造成螺纹在承载时发生拉脱；The 
screw thread of oil rail was abnormal (shown in figure 5~ Figure 9). There were double screw thread and the high of 
screw thread was low, which lead to the bearing strength decreasing. At last the bolt was off. 
2. 润滑油轨硬度符合要求，金相正常，孔隙率≤5%；Hardness met the requirement. Microstructure was normal and  
the porosity was less than 5%. 
3. 油轨材质中铁含量稍微偏高，其他元素符合要求； Content of iron was a little high, and other elements were 
normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 抽查库存润滑油轨；Check the oil feed rail assembly in storage. 
2. 供应商提交分析报告并进行整改；Supplier provide the analysis report and rectification.
