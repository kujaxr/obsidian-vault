Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺栓是由于安装未完全贴紧导致拨叉松动，从而引起疲劳断裂。The bolt was broken by fatigue because the 
matching surface was not fit tight fully, which caused the clutch fork loose.  
2、 螺栓硬度符合8.8级技术要求，化学成分和金相组织均正常。The hardness of bolt meet the requirement of grade 
8.8, the chemical composition and microstructure were normal.  
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、排查螺栓未完全贴紧的原因。Investigate the rootcause of the bolt had not fit tight fully.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
一、宏观观察  Macroscopic Observation： 
英国售后市场反馈一台MG3 有两个离合器拨叉螺栓发生断裂。其中2#螺栓磨损严重，零件如图1 所示，
螺栓强度等级8.8 级。Two clutch fork bolts were broken, which from MG3 in UK aftermarket. And the 2# bolt was 
polished serious, the failed parts were shown in figure 1, and the bolts strength was grade 8.8.

二、断口分析  Fracture Analysis： 
1、用体视镜观察1#螺栓断口宏观形貌，螺栓无明显塑性变形且断口较平整，如图2 所示。其中断口右下角有
多处裂纹台阶，可能为裂纹起源位置。The macroscopic morphology of the bolt fracture surface was observed by 
the stereomicroscope. The bolt had no obvious plastic deformation and the fracture surface was flat, as shown in 
figure 2. There were several cracks steps in the lower right corner of the fracture, which might be the origin of the 
crack.

2、用扫描电镜观察断口微观形貌，如图3 所示，断口多处可以观察到明显疲劳辉纹。终断区如图4 图5 所示，
微观形貌为韧窝，且区域很小，依此可以判断螺栓为单向疲劳断裂，且应力不大。The microscopic morphology 
of fracture was observed by scanning electron microscope (SEM). The fatigue striations could be observed in 
many place of fracture surface obviously, as shown in figure 3. The final fracture area was shown in figure 4 and 5, 
the microscopic morphology was dimple and the area was very small. It could be inferred that the bolt was 
unidirectional fatigue fracture and the stress was not large. 
3、其中2#螺栓磨损严重，如图6 所示，扫描电镜观察，可以在局部区域看到疲劳辉纹，如图7 所示。依此可
以判断2#螺栓与1#螺栓类似，为疲劳断裂。The 2# bolt was polished, as shown in figure 6. The fatigue striations 
were observed in local area by SEM, as shown in figure 7. According to this, it inferred the 2# bolt was broken by 
fatigue, which was the same as 1# bolt.
