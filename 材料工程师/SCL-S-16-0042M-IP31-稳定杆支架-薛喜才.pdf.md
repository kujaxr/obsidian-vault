Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1）前稳定杆支架由于应力过大而发生疲劳失效，裂纹从零件的高应力区萌生，并向其他区域扩展。 
Front stabilizer bracket is cracking by fatigue. The crack resource is in the high stress region and propagates to 
other region. 
（2）零件成分、厚度符合要求，组织及硬度未见异常。 
The chemical components and thickness meet the requirements. Microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 优化零件结构，降低应力或提高零件强度。Optimize the parts structure to reduce the stress or improve the 
parts strength.
