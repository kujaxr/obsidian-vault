Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 支承座与连接杆铆接质量正常，属于受到外力作用发生的过载断裂。 
The quality of riveting is normal and bearing is cracking because of overload. 
2、支承座材料成分满足ADC12 要求，硬度正常，组织正常。 
Chemical components meet the requirement, hardness and microstructure are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 排查运输或装配过程。 
Check the transport or assembly process.
