Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于双向交汇应力引起的疲劳断裂。 
Failure of parts is caused due to the two-way intersection stress fatigue fracture 
2、 材料化学成分、减薄率未发现异常。 
The chemical composition, and he thinning rate for the part were not abnormal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请检查该试验中失效部位的实际应力。 
Please check the actual stress during the testing. 
2、 根据实测应力结果优化该部位的设计。 
Optimize the design of the parts of the measured stress results
