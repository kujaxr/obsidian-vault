Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、排气歧管双头螺柱是疲劳断裂，与所受载荷较大、螺柱根部折叠和强度不足有关。The stud-double was fatigue 
fracture; the reason was related to the large load, the root folds and insufficient strength. 
2、失效螺柱硬度不符合11.9 级技术要求，螺纹根部有折叠，这降低了螺柱疲劳寿命。The hardness of failed stud does 
not meet the 11.9 grade requirement and the thread has folds. It reduce the fatigue life of the stud. 
3、失效螺柱金相符合要求，螺母硬度正常。The microstructure of the failed stud meets the requirement; and the nut 
hardness is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、校核失效位置螺柱的实际工作载荷。Check the work load and temperature of the failed position stud. 
2、供应商加强零件质量管控。Supplier should strength control the parts quality.
