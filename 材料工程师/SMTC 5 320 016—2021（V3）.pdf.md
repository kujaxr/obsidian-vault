本标准适用于汽车用硅橡胶密封件。 
 
1  Scope  
This Technical Supply Specification defines 
requirements for the VMQ seals. 
 
This Technical standard applies to the VMQ 
seals using in non-oil immersion system and 
electrical parts.

4 
 Notes on testing 
4.1  硬度测试 
硬度要求在图纸上或双方协议规定,硬度公
差一般为 ± 5。Oring的硬度采用IRHD进行表
征，其余零件硬度采用Shore硬度。如无特殊要
求，Shore硬度测试时间为3s，IRHD硬度测试时
间为30s。 
 
4.1  Hardness test 
The hardness is specified on the drawing or in 
the agreement of the both side. The tolerance 
is preferred to ±5. The hardness of Oring is 
presented by IRHD hardness, and Shore 
hardness is used for other part. The test period 
of shore hardness is 3s, and the IRHD 
hardness is 30s if there is no special 
requirement. 
4.2  热空气老化 
测试样条存放在符合 ISO 188 要求的鼓
风式烘箱内，老化温度可由双方协议或根据零件
使用的极限温度确定，老化后取出样条 30 min 
后进行测试。 
 
4.2  After aging in hot air 
Test specimens are aged in a mechanical 
circulation oven according to ISO 188. The 
temperature of the test is specified in the 
agreement of both sides or according to the 
top temperature of the finished part in use. All 
Tests 
are 
performed 
30 
minutes 
after 
specimen removal. The temperature and time 
is as follows: 
4.3  油品兼容性 
依据ISO 1817，测试试样存放在装有油品
的容器内，需使用零件实际工作中接触的油品
（机油、变速箱油或其他），液体体积至少为试
样体积的80倍。试样在介质中冷却至室温，从介
质中取出样条30分钟后进行测试。 
 
4.3  Oil Resistance 
According to ISO 1817, test specimens are 
aged in a bottle with the actual oil used in the 
car (Engine Oil, Transmission Fluid and other 
oil contact with the part in use) and the volume 
of the oil is at least 80 times of that of the 
specimens. The specimens remained in the oil 
until cooled down to the ambient, and all tests 
shall be performed 30 minutes after removal 
from test liquid. 
4.4 裤型撕裂 
    按ISO 34-1 方法A 制取测试样条，切口深
度t，t≥1mm。 
 
4.4 Tear strength 
The test shall be performed in deviation from 
ISO 34-1, method A concerning the following 
aspects: Notch depth t, t≥1mm. 
4.5  压缩永久变形 
对于平板样件，测试方法按照SMTC 5 400 
239 方法A。对于O型圈等类似零件，测试方法
按照SMTC 5 400 239 方法C。 
 
4.5  Compression set 
The test method is SMTC 5 400 239 method A 
for flat specimens; and the test method is 
SMTC 5 400 239 method C for the specimens 
like O-Ring. 
4.6  低温性能 
零件在-40℃下存放24h 后进行弯曲试验。
试验直接在存放环境中进行，不允许用手直接接
触样品进行试验。 
 
4.6 Behavior in cold air 
The specimen is placed in -40℃ for 24h and 
conduct the bench test immediately. The 
bench test is conducted in the -40℃ too and 
the hand should not touch the specimen 
directly during the test. 
4.7  挥发物含量  
   仅针对接触导电材料的硅橡胶材料，测试混
炼胶成品中挥发性甲基环硅氧烷残留量
（D3-D8）单体的含量，测试方法按照GB/T 
28112 进行。 
 
4.7 Volatile content 
Only for the silicone rubber contacting 
conductive materials. Testing the content of 
D3-D8 in the blended rubber. The test method 
is GB/T 28112.
