3.1  样品和预处理要求 Sampling and conditioning requirements 
3.1.1  样品要求Sampling requirements 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成
品上取样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，其样
品制备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室协商确定。
为完成试验，需要多个成品零件。 
 
The test samples stipulated in the Standard. 
Samples should be made from finished products. 
If samples can’t be made from finished products, 
sample preparation method should be deter-
mined through the negotiation with SMPV SCL. 
Several parts are required for complete testing.

3.1.2  预处理Conditioning 
  
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准气候
条件23℃，50%RH下至少放置24 h。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C, 50%RH according to the ISO 291 at least 
24h before test..

processing defects, such as flow lines, voids, 
cracks or similar faults.

3.2.10  高温性能Elevated-temperature behavior 
若图纸或零件总成认可无其它规定，则采
用100℃。对于前雾灯要求采用110℃。时间：
24h。要求：表面无缺陷，功能无失效。 
 
Temperature unless stated in the drawing or re-
lease is defined as 100°C. Testing temperature is 
110°C for material used for front fog lamps. Du-
ration: 24h. Requirement: no surface defects, no 
function failure. 
3.2.11  低温性能Low temperature behaviour 
若图纸或认可无其它规定，则-40℃。时间：
24h。要求：表面无缺陷，功能无失效。 
 
Temperature unless stated in drawing or release 
is defined as -40°C. Duration:24h.Requirement：
no surface defects, no function failure. 
3.2.12  湿热存放 Humid heat test 
在温度90℃湿度96%RH环境中存放72H。要
求：零件表面无气泡、裂痕等不良外观及功能
缺陷。 
 
The parts tested under the environment of 90℃，
96% RH for 72h.  Requirement：no surface 
defects, no function failure. 
3.2.13  内应力测试 Tension cracks 
在室温下，将测试零件在甲苯：正丙醇为
1:3 的混合溶液中浸泡3min 后取出，冲洗后
静置30min，然后评价。要求：没有应力开裂。 
 
In room temperature, the part immersed in the 
mixture of methylbenzene and n-propyl alcohol 
which ratio is 1 to 3 for 3mins, and evaluated after 
washed and 30min standing. Requirement: no 
tension cracks. 
3.2.14  耐磨性 Scrub Ristance 
仅针对高光内饰零件。试验设备及方法参
照ISO 12947-1和ISO12945-2。要求：参照总
成零件要求。 
 
For high gloss surface interior parts only. The test 
apparatus and the test method according to ISO 
12947-1 and ISO 12945-2. Requirements: Ac-
cording to the Assembly part.
