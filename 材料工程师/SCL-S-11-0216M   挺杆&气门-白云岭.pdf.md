Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
一、 挺杆&排气门Mech-Tappet& Exhaust Valve（广物骏孚） ： 
1、挺杆：编号为E3 的挺杆内凸台磨损严重，形成了杯型凹陷，内凸台尺寸不合格；编号为E3、E4 的挺杆表面硬度和有效硬化层
深度均符合技术要求。The tappet: within a number E3 of the tappet boss badly worn, forming a cup-shaped depression, within the 
boss size failed; number of E3, E4 of the tappet surface hardness and effective hardened layer depth are in line with technical 
requirements. 
2、 排气门：编号为E3、E4 的排气门表面硬度及氮化层深度符合技术要求。The exhaust valve: number of E3, E4 of the exhaust 
valve surface hardness and nitriding depth meet the technical requirements 
 建议suggestions：1、分析进排气系统的运行情况。The analysis of the operation of intake and exhaust system. 
二、挺杆Mech-Tappe（江苏森风）： 
1、 挺杆已严重磨损，挺杆壁已磨穿，挺杆头部已打穿，尺寸、表面硬度和有效硬化层深度无法检测。金相组织不合格，失效件未
经淬火处理。The tappet has serious wear and tear, the wall has been worn tappet, tappet head is punctured, size, surface 
hardness and effective case depth can not be detected. Microstructure failure, the failure part was not quenched and 
tempered. 
建议suggestions：1、加强实物质量过程控制。Improving quality process control.. 
三、 挺杆Mech-Tappe（淮安宏宇）： 
1、 挺杆表面硬度和有效硬化层深度均符合技术要求。The tappet surface hardness and effective hardened layer depth are in line 
with technical requirements. 
建议suggestions：1、分析进排气系统的运行情况。The analysis of the operation of intake and exhaust system.
