Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）12V 电源插座针脚是由于受到异常的外力而发生过载断裂。The 12V power outlet pin is fractured by overload 
due to abnormal external force. 
（2）针脚在折弯处存在初始的折叠裂纹，但不是导致断裂的原因。There is an initial folding crack at the bend, but is 
not the cause of the fracture. 
（3）针脚的材料成分和组织未见异常，硬度符合要求。The material components and microstructure of the pin are not 
abnormal, and the hardness meets the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）供应商应控制好安装、物流和检查环节，避免不合格品的产生和流出。The supplier should control the installation, 
logistics and inspection procedures to avoid the generation and outflow of unqualified products. 
（2）优化针脚的折弯半径和工艺，避免折叠裂纹的产生。The bending radius and process of the pins are optimized to 
avoid the occurrence of folded cracks.
