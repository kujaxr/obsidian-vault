Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 膜片成型时烤盘温度低，导致成型后零件R角处膜片开裂。 
The temperature of the baking pan is low, which causes the diaphragm to crack at the corner R of 
the part after the forming.  
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商更换老化模具并制定烤盘温度点检要求。 
Suppliers replace aging molds and formulate baking temperature inspection requirements.

3） 失效模拟 
依次排查各环节，发现膜片成型过程中烤盘温度异常，设定温度300±20℃，实测温度在
260~300℃内波动。
