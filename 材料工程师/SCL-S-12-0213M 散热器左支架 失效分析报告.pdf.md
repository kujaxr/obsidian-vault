Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions: 
1. 支架是应力腐蚀引起的断裂失效。The bracket has failed through stress-corrosion cracking. 
2. 低碳钢对氢氧化物和硝酸盐化学品敏感。Mild steel is susceptible to attack from Hydroxide and Nitrate 
chemicals. 
3. 在支架表面发现有可能来自于加工过程的化学品残留物。A residue potentially resulting from processing 
chemicals was found on the bracket surface. 
4.硬度和金相组织符合材料要求。The material meets the requirements with regard to hardness and 
microstructure. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 查找外来残留物的来源（检查制造过程，装配过程）。Find source of foreign material (check manufacturing 
process, assembly processes). 
2. 检查其它车辆的支架。Check other vehicles’ bracket.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
实验室收到3 辆车的散热器支架，如图1，2 和3 所示。以往的案例D10UNV041，在SCL-S-12-0155M 有过分析。
裂纹的位置和方向在支架的同一侧。打开了所有断口用宏观和电镜检查的断裂特性。大多数断口面覆盖有严重的
腐蚀。黄色污染物认为是三元乙丙橡胶（EPDM）的粘合剂的残留物，可能是失效后渗入裂纹的。 
在所有的情况下，许多二次裂纹、晶间裂纹起源于表面。这是典型的应力腐蚀开裂。进一步从断裂的起源，一些
疲劳辉纹被发现，表明第一次开始出现的裂缝是由SCC，然后继续由疲劳机制。Radiator brackets from 3 different 
vehicles were received as shown in figures 1, X & X.  A previous case from D10UNV041 was reported in 
SCL-S-12-0155M.  The location and the direction of the fractures was identical in brackets of the same side. All 
fractures were opened to check the fracture characteristics under optical and electron microscopy.  Most of the fracture 
area was covered with heavy corrosion. Yellow colour is thought to be residue from the EPDM adhesive which might 
have washed into the crack after failure. In all cases, many branched, intergranular cracks were seen originating at the 
surface.  These are typical of Stress Corrosion Cracking.  Further from the fracture origins, some fatigue striations 
were found, indicating that the cracks were first started by SCC, then continued by the fatigue mechanism.
