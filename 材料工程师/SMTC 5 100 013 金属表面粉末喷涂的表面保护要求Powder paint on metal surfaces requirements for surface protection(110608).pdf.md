1 范围 
本标准规定了在已预处理的部件上施涂粉
体涂料的要求。 
本标准适用于评价预处理的部件上的粉体
涂料。但不适用于装饰性表面或者铝轮毂。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the requirements for 
powder paints on pretreated components. 
This standard  is suitable to evaluate the 
powder paints on pretreated components. But this 
standard does not apply to decorative surfaces  or 
aluminum wheels.

3.2
General requirements
Avoidance of hazardous substances according 
to SMTC 90008.Ten parts are required for complete 
testing. 
3.3 薄弱区域 
薄弱区域是指由于设计原因，施涂后需进
行机械加工的区域的边缘处（从未涂漆的边缘
开始，最大2.5 mm 宽度的区域）。 
3.3  Weak spots
Weak spots are edges of an area that was me-
chanically worked after coating for design reasons (in 
a zone of max. 2.5 mm width, starting from the edge 
of the unpainted surface). 
3.4 外观 
根据图纸或经核准的样品，部件应在其表
面展现均一的外观。不允许存在对规定外观产
生不利影响的孔隙、粗纹以及其它的缺陷或损
坏。涂层应对基材具有牢固的粘附性。 
3.4  Appearance
The parts shall exhibit a uniform appearance on 
their entire surface, according to drawing and re-
leased sample. Pores, coarse cracks, and other flaws 
or damage that adversely effects the specified ap-
pearance are not permissible. The coating shall have 
firm adherence to the base material.
