Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、平衡阀螺纹咬死是拧入时发生螺纹错扣导致的。The failure of the thread is caused by thread misalignment during 
assembly. 
2、平衡阀化学成分和硬度未见异常。The chemical composition and hardness of failure part meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、改善装配工艺，避免装配时螺纹斜入。Improve assembly process to avoid thread inclination during assembly. 
2、优化螺纹设计，如增加螺纹尾部导向防止螺纹发生错扣。Optimize thread design, such as increasing thread tail 
guidance to prevent thread misalignment.
