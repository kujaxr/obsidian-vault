Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、回火不足引起的延迟脆性断裂。The part failed due to temper embrittlement. 
2、芯部硬度不合格，高于技术要求。The hardness was higher than the requirement of the drawing. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、加强过程质量控制，如热处理。Strengthen the process of quality control, such as heat treatment.
