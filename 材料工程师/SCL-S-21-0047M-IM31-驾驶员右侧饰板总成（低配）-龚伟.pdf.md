Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  INS膜与基材间夹杂有PE薄膜，PE与ABS相容性不佳，导致鼓泡发生。 
There is PE film between INS film and base material, and the compatibility between PE and 
ABS is poor, which leads to blistering. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商排查异物来源并整改。 
                  Supplier checks the source of unknown object and rectifies.
