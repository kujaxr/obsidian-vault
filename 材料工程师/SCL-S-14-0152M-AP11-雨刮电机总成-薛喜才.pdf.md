Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 失效件断口形貌已被破坏，断裂方式已无法确定；Fracture surface was destroy and the failure model wasn’t 
determined.  
2. 电机主轴材料成分符合要求，金相及硬度正常；Chemical components met the requirement. Microstructure and 
hardness were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 关注零件的装配过程；Pay attention to the assembly process.
