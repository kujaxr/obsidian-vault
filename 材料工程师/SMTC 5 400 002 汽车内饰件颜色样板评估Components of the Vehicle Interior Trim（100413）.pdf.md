1  范围 
本标准规定了对色差以及用仪器确定汽车
内饰件色差的测量和分析条件。 
本标准适用于对下列材料进行样板监控和
批产监控。 
a）单色且无亮光的织物； 
——不易起绒且无粒面； 
——不易起绒且带粒面； 
——易起绒且无粒面。 
b）塑料件； 
——采用皮纹表面 （皮革皮纹、精细纹理
等）； 
——采用油漆表面（柔性漆、装饰漆等）；
——搪塑件、泡沫膜和PU 硬泡膜（仪表
板区域、遮阳板）； 
c）油漆过的金属件（与车身同色的零件除
外）； 
d）皮革(真皮和人造革)。 
注：在附录A 中提出用仪器确定原始样板、
标准样板色差的测量和分析条件及其编制、操作
的要求。 
1  Scope 
This standard defines the requirements 
for color deviations and the measurement and 
evaluation requirements for the use of 
technical instruments to determine color 
differences for components of the vehicle 
interior trim and applies to  the sample and 
standard production monitoring of the following 
materials: 
Monochromatic 
and 
non-luminescent 
textiles： 
─ insensitive to stroking with no structure;
─ insensitive to stroking with structure; 
─ sensitive to stroking with no structure. 
Plastic parts: 
─ with grained surfaces (leather grain, 
fine structure, etc.); 
─ with painted surfaces (soft-touch paint, 
decorative paint, etc.); 
─ slush part, foam film, PU hard foam 
(instrument panel area, sun visor); 
Painted metal parts (except: parts painted 
in the body color). 
Leather (real leather and leatherette) 
Note ：Normative Appendix A defines 
requirem- ents for the preparation, handling 
and measu- rement and evaluation conditions 
for the use of technical instruments to 
determine color differ- rences for original and 
sample standards.

3.2.2 上海汽车的样板标准B 
 
油漆过的样板标准用作所有表面可视和比
色的参考。油漆过样板的颜色是由DIN A5 塑料
板组成。皮革的标准样板用于纹理结构和触感的
参考。 
3.2.2 Sample standard B for the SAIC 
MOTOR 
The painted sample standard serves as a 
reference for the visual and colorimetric 
sampling of all surfaces. This painted sample 
standard for color consists of a painted DIN A5 
plastic panel. Sample standards for e.g. 
leather shall only be used as a reference for 
structure and feel. 
3.3  原色调 
所有（最多300）标准样板的绝对颜色配位
的计算均值。 
3.3  Original shade 
Calculated mean value of the absolute 
color coordinates of all (maximum 300) sample 
standards. 
3.4  原始样板 
与计算出来的原色调最接近的标准样板，留
在实验室里，成为原始样板。 
比色测定的基准数据原则上就是在实验室
中测得的原始样板数据。 
3.4  Original standard 
The original standard is the sample 
standard showing the highest compliance with 
the calculated original shade. It serves as a 
colorimetric reference. 
3.5  成品样板 
复杂零件，可能由不同的表面、不同材料组
成（例如方向盘，天窗控制台，门内装饰板）。
3.5  Finished part sample 
Complex part, may be composed of 
different materials with different surfaces (e.g. 
steering wheel, overhead console, door trim 
panel). 
3.6  单一颜色 
单一颜色的表面表现为整体印象在视觉上
是同一颜色。 
3.6  Monochromatic 
Monochromatic surfaces are surfaces 
whose overall visual impression is of a single 
shade. 
3.7  不易起绒 
不易起绒即用手抚摸时织物不会出现视觉
上可见的反衬色差。 
3.7  Insensitive to stroking 
Textiles, which show no visibly perceptible 
contrast differences when stroked by hand, are 
said to be insensitive to stroking. 
3.8  易起绒 
易起绒即用手抚摸时织物会出现视觉上可
见的反衬色差（例如丝绒、裁绒、阿耳康塔腊羊
毛）。 
3.8  Sensitive to stroking 
Textiles, which show visibly perceptible 
contrast differences when stroked by hand 
(e.g., velours, tufted fabrics, Alcantara) are 
said to be sensitive to stroking. 
3.9  纹理结构 
纹理结构即指弯纱和规则的缝迹走向，其导
致有些地方绒头高度不同，因此在视觉上可看出
反衬背景的色差。 
2.9  Structures 
Structures are impressions and regular 
seams, which cause different pile heights in 
places and thus evoke visibly perceptible 
contrast differences.

4.1 一般要求 
在预发展期间，上海汽车定义的目视可接受
测量极限的有效测试，用作为测量公差基准。由
于技术因数，修订版本可在标准生产阶段制作。
供货商要以在线检查统计报告的形式进行
在线检验。 
零件也不例外，应由供货商测量并提供相应
的记录报告。此外，还应对测量值进行外观可信
度检验（即使是测量分析结果合格的也不例外。）
10 次单独测量的色值，波动幅度不得大于
±0.15 CIELAB 单位。(例外：ΔL*)  
在原则上，是在进行外观验收后，再在安装
位置上进行测定。 
在标准中规定的公差标准值，在抽样初始阶
段用作为目标值。其根据材料和颜色方案而各不
相同。在这种情况之下，就要把不同的公差值，
标记在相应的图纸或标准样板上，并通知检验部
门。若是没有约定其他公差值，那就适用现有标
准上的数据规定。 
对于上海汽车，颜色公差应标在标准样板的
背面（见样板附录）。对于主管零件的部门，在
视觉接受的个别特例材料，上海汽车可放宽4.2
中的公差要求。 
4.1  General requirements 
A plausibility test of the measured limits 
for visual acceptance, which is defined by 
SAIC MOTOR AG during pre-development, is 
used as the basis for the measurement 
tolerances. Revisions may be made in the 
standard production phase due to technical 
factors. 
A process test in the form of statistical 
process control is required of the supplier. 
Components shall be measured by the 
supplier without exception and delivered with 
the corresponding logs. In addition, the 
measured values shall be checked for 
plausibility regarding the visual impression 
(even when the measurements indicate an OK 
evaluation). 
The tolerance range of 10 individual 
measurements in the color values shall be no 
greater than ±0,15 CIELAB units (except ΔL*).
On principle, the evaluation is subject to 
visual acceptance in as-installed position. 
The tolerance guidelines established in 
this standard serve as target values at the 
beginning of the sampling phase, which can be 
different depending on the material and color 
variant. If this is the case, the different 
tolerance 
values 
are 
noted 
in 
the 
corresponding technical supply specifications, 
in the drawing or on the sample standard, or 
passed on to the testers. If no other tolerance 
values are agreed upon, the specifications in 
this standard apply. 
For the SAIC MOTOR, the tolerances 
shall always be indicated on the back of the 
sample standards for color (see Appendix for 
examples). 
For 
individual 
materials, 
exceptions can be agreed upon with the 
department representative in charge of the 
component if this is required for the visual 
acceptance. Hence, the tolerances indicated in 
4.2.x are not compulsory for SAIC MOTOR.

4.2.4  根据安装位置和表面的公差 
根据安装位置和测试下的零件表面，发布团
体分配给指定表1 和表7 基本公差一个系数。 
 
 
在以下因素中使用： 
a）在直接目测面积的整合铸模的转变＝基
本公差 x 1； 
b）在直接不可见的非整合铸模的转变＝基
本公差 x 1.5；  
c）对于无组织材料制成的零件，基本公差
x 2.5，适用于参数ΔL*。 
4.2.4 Tolerances depending on the as-installed 
position and the surface 
The releasing party can assign a factor to 
the base tolerances specified in Tables 1 to 7, 
depending on the as-installed position and the 
surface of the component under test. 
The following factors can be applied: 
a） mold-integrated transitions in a directly 
visible area = base tolerance x 1； 
b）non-mold-integrated transitions in an area 
that is not directly visible = base tolerance x 1,5
c） For parts made of amorphous material, the 
base tolerance x 2,5 shall be applied to the 
parameter ΔL*.

4.3  Finished part samples (ASSY parts 
such as steering wheels, overhead cons- 
oles) 
Finished part samples shall always give a 
visually harmonious overall impression. 
In addition, the following shall apply to the 
finished part samples of the Volkswagen 
brand: 
The maximum deviation between surfaces 
made of the same material must not exceed 
the base tolerance. This does not apply to 
areas not directly adjoining each other. A 
maximum deviation of ∣ΔL*∣1,3 of all 
materials from each other must not be 
exceeded. The respective materials shall be 
visually acceptable according to the sample 
standard. 
4.4  叁考规格 
除非另外决定，设计发布的样板将作为上海
汽车的参考。偏离这一标准，也可以使用初始
标准。在标准样板上记录参考细节。集团实验
室应存储各自需要的参考依据。 
4.4  Reference specification 
Unless otherwise agreed upon, the design 
release sample shall serve as the reference for 
the SAIC MOTOR. Deviating from this rule, the 
original standard can also be used. The 
reference details are documented on the 
sample standards. The respective compulsory 
references shall be stored in the group 
laboratory.
