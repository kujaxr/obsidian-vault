Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳开裂。 
Failure pieces belong to fatigue cracking. 
2、 失效件的Mn 含量超差，其他材料性能未见异常。 
The content of Mn did not meet the requirement ,other material properties were normal . 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强原材料控制。 
Strengthen the control of raw materials. 
2、 进一步排查引起疲劳开裂的原因（如与发动机的匹配等）。 
Further investigation lead to fatigue cracking of the reasons (such as the matching engine)
