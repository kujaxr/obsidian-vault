Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件发生了降解，强度低，装配时卡脚断裂。 
The failure parts were degraded, which the strength was low, and the clamp was broken during 
assembly.  
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商解释材料降解问题并整改。 
Supplier explains material degradation problem and rectifies.
