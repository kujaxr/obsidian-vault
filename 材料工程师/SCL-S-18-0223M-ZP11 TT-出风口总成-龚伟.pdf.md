Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件卡脚呈脆断形式，与正常件不符。 
The clip foot of the failure part is brittle fracture, which is not in conformity with the normal part. 
                2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
                    Material performance of part conformed to the requirement.   
建议Suggestions：1.  请供应商查找失效件脆断断裂的原因。 
                    Supplier shall find out the reason why the failure part is brittle fracture. 
2.  失效卡脚根部增加倒角。 
                    Add chamfer at the foot of the failed position. 
                3.  跟踪售后市场表现。 
                    Follow up after-sale performance.
