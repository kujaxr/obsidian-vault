Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 支撑座与连接杆铆接过量、产生原始裂纹，承载即发生断裂。Excessive riveting between bearing and linkage 
assembly result in the crack and fractured on load. 
（2） 化学成分中锌含量偏高，硬度正常，但组织中存在多条冷隔缺陷，失效件可能为冷模件。Zinc content is high 
and hardness is normal. Several cold shuts are observed and the failed parts might be cold mould part. 
（3） 新零件同样存在铆接过量、原始裂纹及冷隔。Excessive riveting、crack and cold shut are observed in new parts. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 库存件全部报废，更换已装车(工程车)零件。Scrap the parts in storage and change the parts in use. 
（2） 优化铆接工艺，确保产品铆接质量。Optimise the riveting process to make the parts quality.  
（3） 排查铸件中产生冷隔的原因并提出相应的整改措施。Check the reason of the cold shut and take measures 
to rectify and reform.
