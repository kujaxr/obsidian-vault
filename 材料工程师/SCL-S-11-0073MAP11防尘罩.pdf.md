Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：失效件的材料和力学性能无异常。防尘罩裂纹是由于内部异物刮擦所致。 
Conclusions: The material and mechanical property of failure part conform to the drawing requirement. According to 
the macroscopic observation and the material test, it can be seen that the dust cap is broken by an inner 
foreign body. 
建议：装配时防止异物进入防尘罩。 
Suggestions：Inner foreign body should be prohibited during assembly of the dust cap.
