Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 后副车架前束支架后片上的裂纹为疲劳裂纹，裂纹起源于鼓包开口根部撕裂区(原始裂纹区)。    
The cracks on the rear plate of the rear sub frame assembly are fatigue cracks, which originated from the root of  
the tearing area( origin crack area).  
2. 材料成分满足要求，硬度及组织正常。 
Chemical components meet the requirements. Hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化鼓包凸起根部开口。 
  Optimize the opening of the root.
