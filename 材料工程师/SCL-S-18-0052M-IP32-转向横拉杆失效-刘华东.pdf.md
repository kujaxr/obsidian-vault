Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 转向横拉杆失效是由于螺母夹紧力不足出现松动引起的。The failure of the steering cross bar may be caused by 
the nut loosening; 
2、 失效螺栓化学成分和硬度均符合要求，金相组织无异常；抽检的螺栓螺母硬度符合要求。The chemical 
composition and hardness of failure bolt meet the requirements; the microstructure is not abnormal. The hardness of 
inspect bolt and nut meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、排查螺母装配过程是否有异常。Investigate if there is any abnormality in the assembly of nuts.  
2、建议工程对螺母松动进行模拟再现试验。Suggest DRE conduct simulated reappearance test on nut loosening.
