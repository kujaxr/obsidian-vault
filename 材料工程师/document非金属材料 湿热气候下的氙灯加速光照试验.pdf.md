exposing specimens to xenon-arc light in the 
presence 
of 
moisture 
to 
simulate 
the 
weathering effects that occur when materials 
are exposed in actual end use environments to 
daylight.
This standard can be applied when assessing 
the performance of resistance to a moist hot 
climate of non-metallic materials.

4　试验方法Test method
4.1  原理 Principle
采用氙弧灯对试样进行照射，并进行循环
增湿。用滤光片对氙孤灯发射的紫外线和可见
光光谱进行调节后对试样进行全辐射。试验设
备参照ISO 4892-2 和 ISO 11341。试验条件相
当于湿热气候地带（如佛罗里达）每年波长为
300-400nm 的紫外线的平均辐射剂量。
Specimens shall be irradiated using Xenon arc 
light and periodically moistened. Filters shall be 
used to adapt the Xenon arc light to the global 
radiation with its spectral distribution of 
ultraviolet and visible radiation. Testing shall be 
performed in devices according to ISO 4892-2 
and ISO 11341.The test conditions each 
describe a year cycle, referred to the average 
dose of UV radiation of (300 to 400) nm in 
moist, hot climate zones, e.g., Florida.

or

with

准），当达到规定的UV 辐射量后, 大气暴露周
期结束。为确保获得均匀的辐射强度。
The end of a weathering cycle is reached for a 
specified intensity of UV irradiation and 
radiation dose after a specific test duration 
(according to each material SMTC standard). 
To ensure even intensity of irradiation. 
4.5.5 辐照度校准 Radiometer calibration

要求的辐照计进行校准。
In order to ensure the uniformity of irradiation

Atlas manufacturer) for regular inspection and 
calibration; Ci series equipment can be 
regularly monitored/calibrated using calibration 
tubes; Q-LAB equipment must be calibrated 
according to the irradiation meter required by 
the manufacturer.

4.6.2
目视评定 Visual evaluation
颜色与光泽上的变化，由几位（至少2 位）
具有经验但普通视力的专家在符合ISO 105-A01
的光源或间接日光下，通过与一个未经光照的
参考试样进行对比，根据ISO 105-A02 采用灰
度等级评定试样的颜色变化。
Changes in color and gloss shall be compared 
against an unexposed reference specimen with 
the gray scale to evaluate the “change in color” 
according to ISO 105-A02 under lighting 
according to ISO 105-A01 or indirect daylight 
by several proficient normal-sighted people (at 
least two).
考虑到消除目视方向对结果的影响，参考
试样和试验后试样必须对齐，以排除方位角影
响。
To eliminate the influence of visual direction on 
the results, the reference specimen and the 
post-test specimen must be aligned to exclude 
the influence of azimuth angle.
任何发生的表面变化（如裂纹、沉积物、
污渍、发粘、分层、颜色变化、腐蚀、玻纤外
露）都必须进行完整记录和拍照并与未光照的
参考样品进行比较。照片中的样品必须进行清
晰标注。
Any surface changes that occur (such as 
cracks, deposits, stains, stickiness, layering, 
colour 
changes, 
corrosion, 
glass 
fiber 
exposure) 
must 
be 
fully 
recorded 
and 
photographed, 
and 
compared 
with 
the 
reference sample that was not illuminated. The 
samples in the photos must be clearly labelled.
注：硬质涂层和阳极氧化层中的微裂纹，
以及其他影响（玻纤外露等），只有在特定角
度的强光（如LED 手电筒）下才能看到。
Note: Microcracks in hard coatings and 
anodized layers, as well as other effects (such 
as exposed glass fibers), can only be seen 
under strong light at specific angles (such as 
LED flashlights).
