3 测试方法 
 
3 
Test Method 
3.1 试验类型 
试验可以采用以下几种形式： 
 
a)  试验 A：在圣安德鲁十字的两条划痕腰 
线上的平直喷射角（见图1），针对没有
自动测试设备的自主喷涂或供应商喷
涂，以及在设备中无法测试形状的零件；
 
 
b)  试验 B：在圣安德鲁十字中央的倾斜喷
射角。方法为DIN 55662 油漆与清漆–
汽车工业塑料饰件涂装– 耐水测试-蒸
汽喷射； 
 
 
 
c)  试验 C：与试验B相仿，更长的试验时间 
以及喷嘴与试样之间更大的距离。 
 
注：应使用哪种试验，必须与各自负责的专业部门商
定。 
 
3.1  Test type 
Test can run follow different types as bellow：
a)  Test A: Flat angle of jet at the two inter-
secting legs of the St. Andrew's cross, for 
locations and suppliers that do not have 
automated test equipment, e.g., made by 
Walter, and for components whose 
shapes cannot be tested in the equip-
ment; 
b)  Test B: Steep angle of jet in the center of 
the St. Andrew's cross, Test Method A of 
DIN 55662 "Paints and Varnishes – 
Coatings for Plastic Mounting Parts for 
Automotive Industry (We do not have 
access to this standard) – Testing of Re-
sistance to Water-Vapour Jetting"; 
c)  Test C: Same as Test B, but with a longer 
test duration and a greater distance be-
tween the nozzle and the specimen. 
Note: The test to be used must be agreed with  
the responsible engineering department.

3.5  喷射图的检验 
     使用具有紧密网状组织和添加阻燃剂的热
挤压成型的聚苯乙烯硬质泡沫来检验喷射图。聚
苯乙烯块被固定后进行操作。对于测试A 过程，
喷嘴距离聚苯乙烯块120 mm 进行试验，测试B/C
过程距离100 mm。喷嘴宽度为8 mm±1 mm。对于
测试A 方法喷射长度不能超过100 mm，对于测试
B和C，喷射长度为70 mm±5 mm。
（相当于DIN 55662
附录A 中的图1） 
 
3.5  Checking the jet pattern 
An extruded polystyrene rigid foam with 
sealed cell structure and flame-retardant additive 
is used to check the jet pattern. The polystyrene 
block is clamped and the test is performed. In this 
process, the distance from the nozzle to the poly-
styrene block is 120 mm for Test A and 100 mm 
for Test B/C. The width of the spray jet is 8 mm±1 
mm. The length of the spray jet must not exceed 
100 mm for Test A and is 70 mm±5 mm for Test 
B/C (corresponds to Figure A1 in Appendix A of 
DIN 55662). 
3.6  过程 
3.6.1  试样上应切割一个安德鲁十字，油漆结构
划至基材（相当于DIN 55662 中8.1 的图2）。 
 
 
 
3.6  Procedure 
3.6.1  A crosscut (St. Andrew's cross) is made in 
the specimen, in which the paint structure is 
scratched or damaged down to the substrate 
(corresponding to Figure 2, Section 8.1 of DIN 
55662).  
3.6.2  试样暴露的压力冲刷条件依据章节3.4。
 
3.6.2  The specimen is exposed to the conditions 
of the pressure washer load indicated in Section 
3.4.
