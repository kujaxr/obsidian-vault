the requirements for surface protection for paint 
finishes on coil springs for the front and rear 
axle suspension, in four types. 
The standard applies to paint finishes on 
coil springs for the front and rear axle suspen-
sion, in four types.

3.1 一般要求 
根据上海汽车标准SMTC 90008，避免有害物
质。完全测试至少要求 4 根螺旋弹簧。涂漆应没
有削弱腐蚀保护的气孔、裂纹或杂质。涂层应紧
紧粘附基体材料。生产流程及其控制过程不应削
弱成品件的功能特性。 
 
 
 
3.1  General requirements 
  Approval of first supply and changes ac-
cording to SMTC 90008. The required minimum 
for complete testing is 4 coil springs. The paint 
coating shall be free of pores, cracks and for-
eign matter inclusions that impair the corrosion 
protection. The coating shall adhere firmly to 
the base material. The production process and 
its control shall not impair the functional cha-
racteristics of the finished part.

3.3 耐腐蚀性 
3.3  Corrosion resistance 
3.3.1  根据 SMTC 5 200 010，在腐蚀周期测试
（3 相测试）中测试三十个周期。不考虑从边缘
距螺旋弹簧末端 2.5 mm 处的腐蚀以及悬架第一圈
的腐蚀。用软管测试带阻尼管的螺旋弹簧。 
 
3.3.1 Thirty cycles are tested in the corrosion 
cycle test (3-phase test) according to SMTC 5 
200 010. Corrosion on the ends of the coil 
springs up to 2.5 mm from the edge and on the 
1st turn of the suspension are not taken into 
consideration. Coil springs that are equipped 
with a damping hose are tested with the hose. 
3.3.2  要求：最大基底金属腐蚀为 Ri 1。不允
许有泡沫形成。根据 DIN EN ISO 7253、第 8.5 节
以及 DIN EN ISO 4628-8，划痕线：粘附损失 D ≤
0.10 英寸。 
3.3.2
Requirements: base metal corrosion 
max. Ri 1. Bubble formation is not permitted. 
Scribing line according to DIN EN ISO 7253, 
section 8.5, and DIN EN ISO 4628-8: adhesion 
loss D ≤ 0.10 in. 
3.4 低温下的反应（仅适用于 C 类和 D 类） 
3.4  Behavior at low temperatures (only for 
types C and D) 
3.4.1  测试：（-40±3）℃下 24 h 老化。完成老
化期后，在低温下根据 DIN ISO 4532（在坚硬的
基板上施加 90 N 的测试力）撞击测试。 
3.4.1
Test: 24 h aging at (-40 ± 3) °C. After 
completion of the aging period, an impact test 
based on DIN ISO 4532 (with 90 N test force on 
a rigid base plate) is performed at low temper-
ature. 
3.4.2  要求：涂漆中无碎屑或裂纹。允许在基体
材料上出现直径不超过 0.12 英寸的区域撞击。 
3.4.2
Requirement: No chipping and no 
cracks in paint coating. Impacts of up to 0.12 in. 
Ø down to the base material are permissible.
