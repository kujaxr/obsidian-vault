Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1）排气歧管双头螺柱由于受到较大的载荷作用引发多源疲劳开裂。Stud-double end is cracking by fatigue because 
of the big load. 
  
（2） 失效件牙底存在折叠，材料成分、硬度、组织及脱碳层满足技术要求。 The failed part is found the fold defect. 
Chemical components、hardness、microstructure  and decarburation meet the technical requirement.  
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）鉴于MCT 试验工况，评估失效部位安全系数。Based on MCT testing situation, evaluate the safety 
coefficient at failed location.  
 
（2）紧固件供应商加强质量监控。Supplier strengths control the quality.
