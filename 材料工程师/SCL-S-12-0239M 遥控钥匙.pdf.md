Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 限位器未按下，硬扳钥匙导致的脆断。 
      2. 零件材料符合要求。 
Conclusions：1. The stopper was not pressed, pull the key and cause the brittle fracture. 
2. Material performance of parts conformed to requirements. 
建议：确保限位器按下的情况下，回扳钥匙。 
Suggestions：Ensure that the stopper is pressed, then pull back the key.
