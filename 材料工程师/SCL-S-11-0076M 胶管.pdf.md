Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：经测试，失效件的材料无异常。橡胶管的断裂模式是装配时胶管受到卡箍刮擦，在胶管表面产生刮擦
缺陷，胶管使用过程受外力作用，导致胶管在缺陷处开始断裂，最终扩展到整个截面。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and the material test, it can be seen that the surface of pipe was scratched by the 
metal loop during assembly. The break of rubber pipe start from the scratch defect area by an external force, 
and then the crack propagate through the whole cross section quickly. 
建议：装配时防止卡箍和工具刮擦橡胶管表面。 
Suggestions：The scratches caused by the metal loop and assemble tool should be prohibited during assembly of the 
rubber pipe.
