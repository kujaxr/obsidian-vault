Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 张紧轮转动轴断裂是由于过载引起的；The rotating shaft was broken, duo to the overload. 
2. 转动轴化学成分、硬度、密度符合要求，微观组织及孔隙率正常；Chemical、hardness、density met the requirement. 
Microstructure and porosity of rotating shaft were normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
检查装配及拆解过程；Check the assembly and disassemble process.
