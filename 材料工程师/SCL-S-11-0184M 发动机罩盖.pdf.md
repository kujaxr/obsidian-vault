Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 发动机罩盖失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
2. 装配时，紧固件与装配孔配合存在偏差。 
Conclusions: 1. The material performance of failure engine hood conforms to requirements. 
           2. Fastener and assemble hole didn’t match well during assembly. 
建议：改进装配工艺。  
Suggestions：The assembly technology should be improved.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic analysis 
 
通过图2 可以看出安装孔底部衬面都是部分破坏，且开裂都是从某一侧开始，并发展延伸。这说明在安装时，
紧固件与安装孔配合存在偏差，使得安装孔局部受力过大，导致安装孔损坏。 
It can be got from fig. 2 that bottom facing of both failure assemble holes were damaged partially. Moreover, the 
crack both began from one side and expanded. The results shows that fastener and assemble hole didn’t match well during 
assembly which made assemble hole local overload and led to the damage.
