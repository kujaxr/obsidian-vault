Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1. 锁轴是由于强度不足引起的剪切断裂。 
   The locking axis shear fractured because of insufficient strength.  
2. 化学成分满足要求，材料组织为变形的铁素体+粒状珠光体，表面无脱碳层。 
Chemical components meet the requirement. The material microstructure is deformed ferrite + granular pearlite and 
there is no decarburization layer on the surface.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  提高锁轴强度如调质处理或更改新材料。 
   Improve the strength of lock axis such as quench tempering and change new material.
