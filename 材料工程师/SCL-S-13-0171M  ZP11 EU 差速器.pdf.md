Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）行星齿轮和半轴齿轮是由于受到较大的冲击载荷而发生断裂的。断裂后的碎片冲击1#卡簧，使其剪切断裂，
随后轴销失去固定，向另一端滑动；最终另一端卡簧，垫片以及壳体受损。The planetary gear and side gear were broken because of 
a big impact. The piece of gear impacted the 1# circlip and made it broken by shear and then the pin moved along the other end because of losing fixture; finally, the 
other circlip, washer and shell were damaged. 
（2）1# 行星齿轮的淬硬层偏厚；其成分，表面硬度和芯部硬度符合要求，组织正常。2#行星齿轮材料符合要求。
The hardening layer depth of the 1# planetary gear is on the thick side; The chemical components, surface and core hardness are ok; the microstructure is normal. The 
material of the 2# planetary gear meets the requirement. 
（3）1#半轴齿轮（失效）的芯部硬度和淬硬层深度偏高；其成分和表面硬度符合要求；组织正常。2#半轴齿轮
的淬硬层偏厚；其成分，表面硬度和芯部硬度符合要求，组织正常。The core hardness and hardening layer thickness of 1# side gear is 
on the high side; the chemical components and surface hardness are ok; the core hardness and thickness of the hardening layer is on the high side; the microstructure is 
normal. The hardening layer thickness of 2# side gear is on the high side; the chemical components, surface and core hardness are ok; the microstructure is normal. 
（4）卡簧的硬度符合要求，组织正常。The hardness of the circlip meets the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核载荷。Check the load. 
（2）控制好工艺参数，确保材料符合产品的技术要求。Control the process and assure the material meets the requirement. 
（3）检查试验过程是否符合要求。Check if the test process meets the requirement.

II. 调查结果Investigation Results  
 
     ZP11 EU 在英国完成离合器试验和牵引起动2 个试验后，进行傻瓜式起步冲击试验；当完成测试的
15%时，车轮无动力输出；检查发现：差速器出现失效。

出现了断裂。类似的失效曾经在63P 中出现过，详情见报告SCL-S-12-0094M。 
      差速器失效后，导致壳体被失效件击碎。
