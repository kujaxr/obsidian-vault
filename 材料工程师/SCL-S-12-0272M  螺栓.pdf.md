Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 造成螺栓松脱的原因是由于装配时该螺栓没有完全拧紧所致。  
The bolt has failed due to the bolt did not fully tighten in the assembly process. 
 
2、 实物质量符合图纸技术要求要求。  
The quality for the part meet to the requirements of the drawing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、检查装配过程质量。 
Check the assembly process quality.
