Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 由于反向弯曲疲劳导致排气管的断裂；The exhaust pipe failed due to reverse bending fatigue. 
（2） 管子处于法兰内表面的裂纹是由于高温非正常热膨胀所致。Additional fractures in the upper pipe 
indicates abnormal thermal expansion due to very high temperatures. 
（3）  材料的厚度，成分，组织，以及硬度正常。The material thickness, chemical components, microstructure 
and hardness are normal. 
（4） 焊接处未见焊接缺陷。No significant defects were found in the weld. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 建议DRE 和实验工程师，对比新旧设计情况下排气管的振动，考察是否由于支架和排气管催化剂的更改
导致排气管的振动加剧而出现过早断裂。Suggest the DRE and test engineer to compare the vibration of 
the new and old design and check if the vibration became more serious. 
（2） 检测排气温度是否过高。Check the exhaust gas temperature is within limits.

（1） 发动机输出动力所需的转速有所降低；
