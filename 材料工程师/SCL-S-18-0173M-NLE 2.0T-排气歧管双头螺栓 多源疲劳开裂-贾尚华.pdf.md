Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 排气歧管螺栓受到较大载荷发生多源疲劳开裂。 
The cylinder head bolt of the engine that through thermal crack test is folded at the bottom, the depth is about 
0.030mm, which does not meet the requirement; 
2. 失效的螺栓有折叠缺陷，但这不是引起螺栓断裂的根本原因。 
The failure bolt has a folding defect, but this is not the root cause of bolt breakage. 
3. 螺栓的金相组织正常，脱碳程度与硬度均满足要求。  
The metallographic structure of the bolt is normal, decarburization degree and core hardness meet the standard 
requirements. 
建议 Suggestions： 
1. 校核螺栓的实际工作载荷。Checking the actual working load of the bolt 
2. 隔离该批次螺栓，加强供应商质量管控，并评估风险。Isolate the batch bolts and strengthen supplier quality control, 
And evaluate the risk.
