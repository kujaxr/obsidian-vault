Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件均属于疲劳断裂，断裂位置均位于橡胶固定套附近。The part failed through fatigue fracture of the wire 
cable, near to the rubber locating grommet. 
2、 没有提供材料技术要求，但未发现明显异常。The material requirements were not supplied, however no obvious 
abnormality was found. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请检查失效件的受力载荷情况。Check the load conditions during use 
2、 改善失效件断裂位置的受力工况。Improve load bearing capability of the part. 
3、 供应商必须确认导线材料是否符合技术要求。Supplier must confirm whether the wire material meets the 
technical requirements.
