Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions  
结论Conclusions： 
1. 零件强度不足导致其发生多源疲劳断裂。 
The failed parts were cracking by fatigue of multiple sources because of insufficient strength.  
2. 故障件仍为原设计状态时的零件。 
   The material of the failed parts was in old designed state. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 建议售后关注稳定杆支架开裂部位。Concern on the cracking region of the bracket
