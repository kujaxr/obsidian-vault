1  Scope  
This standard specifies the procedure for 
determining the force required to pull or slide it 
along the channel. 
This standard applies t the procedure for

2 仪器 
2  Apparatus 
2.1 合适的自动记录张力仪。 
2.1  Suitable autographic tensometer. 
2.2  考虑到与车体一致试验夹具应该有一个保
留导槽。必需确保夹具能够装进张力计中钳分离
速度这样密封材料就可以以技术图纸中（a）指
定的角度在90 °的时候沿着保留导槽纵向铺
展，并且（b）沿着导槽的方向。见图1。 
2.2  Testing jig incorporating a retaining 
channel identical to that of the vehicle con-
cerned. The jig must be capale of being 
mounted in the tensometer such that the seal 
can be pulled (a) at 90 ° to the longitudinal 
direction of the retaining channel and at an 
angle as specified on the Engineering Draw-
ing, and (b) along the direction of the channel. 
See Fig.1.

3 试样准备 
3  Sample preparation 
3.1 试验样品应该从成品零件上截取（包括冰
冻或者可移动覆盖层在内）。 
 
3.1  Test sample shall be taken from the fi-
nished component including any freeze re-
lease or slip coating. 
3.2 用切割工具切割100 mm 长度的试样。 
3.2  Samples of 100 mm length shall be cut 
using the cutting fixture. 
4 过程 
4  Procedure 
4.1 密封保持力 
4.1  Seal retention force 
4.1.1 夹具以水平姿态装夹在张力计上面，将
样品装在保留导槽内。 
 
4.1.1
The jig is attached to the tensometer in 
a horizontal position and the sample is fitted 
into the retaining channel. 
4.1.2 采用一个合适的方法将试验样品的密封
部分夹住，但要注意不要破坏密封材料的基础部
分。然后将夹具安装到张力计上。 
 
4.1.2  The sealing portion of the test sample 
is clamped using a suitable method which 
does not distort the base portion of the seal. 
The clamp is then attached to the crosshead of 
the tensometer. 
4.1.3 张力计以50 mm/min 的速率上升，直到
试样从保留导槽上彻底分离。 
 
4.1.3  The crosshead is raised at a rate of 50 
mm/min until the test sample has been fully 
separated from the retaining channel. 
 
4.1.4 记录由张力计记录下来的最大的力。 
 
4.1.4  The maximum force recorded by the 
tensometer is noted. 
 
4.1.5  拿剩余的2 根样条重复进行4.1.1 至
4.1.4 中的操作。 
4.1.5  Repeat clauses 4.1.1 to 4.1.4.
4.2 密封滑动力  
4. 2  Seal sliding force 
4.2.1 将夹具以垂直的姿态装夹在张力计上
面，将试验样条安装进保留导槽内。 
 
4.2.1  The jig is attaché to the tensometer in a 
vertical position and the sample is fitted into 
the retaining channel. 
4.2.2 采用一个合适的方法将试验样品的密封
部分夹住，但要注意不要破坏密封材料的基础部
分。然后将夹具安装到张力计上面，这样密封材
料就会以一个平行于保留导槽的方向来进行拉
伸。 
4.2.2  The sealing portion of the test sample 
is clamped using a suitable method which 
does not distort the base portion of the seal. 
The clamp is then attached to the crosshead of 
the tensometer such that the seal is pulled in a 
direction parallel to the retaining channel. 
4.2.3 张力计以50 mm/min 的速率上升直到试
样从保留导槽上彻底分离。 
4.2.3  The crosshead is raised at a rate of 50 
mm/min until the test sample has been fully 
separated from the retaining channel. 
4.2.4 记录由张力计记录下来的最大的力。 
4.2.4 The maximum force recorded by the 
tensometer is noted. 
4.2.5 拿剩余的2 根样条重复进行4.2.1 至
4.2.4 中的操作。 
4.2.5  Repeat clauses 4.2.1 to 4.2.4 on a fur-
ther two samples.  
5 报告 
    以牛顿每百毫米（N/100 mm） 为单位报告
平均密封保持力以及平均密封滑动力。 
5  Report 
    Report the mean retaining force and the 
mean sliding force in N/100 mm length.
