Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）齿轮由于受到较大的载荷而发生疲劳断裂失效的。The gear was broken by fatigue because of a big load. 
（2）材料的成分，表面硬度，硬化层深度和芯部硬度均合格；组织正常。The chemical components, surface hardness, 
depth of quenching layer and core hardness meet the requirement; the microstructure is normal. 
（3）齿根平滑过渡，无尖角。The transition at tooth roots is smooth and no taper angle was found. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核齿轮的工作载荷。Check the load of the gear. 
（2）检查试验中系统其它部件是否异常，同时判断该异常是否导致载荷的增加。Check if the other parts of the 
experiment system are abnormal during test, at the same time, estimate if the abnormality increases the load.  
（3）检查与之匹配齿轮的尺寸。Check the dimension of the gear matching with the failed gear.
