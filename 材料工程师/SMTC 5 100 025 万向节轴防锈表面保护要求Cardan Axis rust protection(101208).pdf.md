3.1.4  涂层不得存在妨碍防锈性能的气孔、裂
纹和杂质。涂层必须稳固地黏附在基材上。在
制定相关的生产流程和控制流程时，必须确保

3.1.4  The coating shall be free of pores, 
cracks and foreign matter inclusions that may 
impair the rust protection. The coating shall 
adhere firmly to the base material. Relevant 
production process and control process shall 
not impair the application characteristics of the 
finished parts. 
3.2 涂漆方案 
有以下三种涂漆方案： 
a) 方案A：无需预处理的粉状漆涂装；
 
b) 方案B：先做锌磷化处理，然后涂漆；
 
c) 方案C：先做锌磷化处理，然后粉末
喷涂（电解铝涂敷法的替补方案）。
3.2  Paint Coating types 
The following types are distinguished: 
a) 
Type A: Powder paint coating without 
pretreatment; 
b) 
Type B: Zinc phosphorization first, 
and then paint coating; 
c) 
Type C: Zinc phosphorization first, 
and then powder paint coating 
(alternative 
type 
for 
electrolytic 
aluminum coating method).

3.5 低温特性（仅限于方案C） 
3.5  Behavior at Low Temperature (for Type 
C only) 
3.5.1  试验：试样在-40 ℃±3 ℃环境内停放
24 h，然后对低温试样按类似于DIN EN ISO 4532
（用90N 敲击坚硬基体）规定的方法进行敲击。
3.5.1 Test: Place the specimen in an 
environment of -40 ℃± 3 ℃ for 24 h, and then 
stroke the low-temperature specimen according 
to the method specified in DIN ISO 4532 
(stroke the rigid base plate with 90 N). 
3.5.2  要求：允许出现≤3mm 的裂纹或裂痕，
允许出现直至基材为止的击穿现象。 
3.5.2  Requirements: Flaws or cracks with 
≤3mm are permissible. Punctures down to 
base material are permissible.
