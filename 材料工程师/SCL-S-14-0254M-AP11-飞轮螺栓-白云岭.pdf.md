Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于夹紧力丢失引起连接副松脱和异响。The loose due to the loss of clamping force.  
2. 变扭器凸台的内螺纹螺牙存在严重的双牙尖现象，且有效承载面积过小，塑性变形严重。这均可导致使用过
程中的夹紧力降低。There were serious double cusps in the internally threaded, the effective bearing area is too small 
and severe plastic deformation led to the clamping force is reduced during use. 
3. 螺栓的硬度、金相组织符合技术要求。The hardness and microstructure of bolt met the technical requirements 
4. 对螺栓、飞轮、变扭器凸台的部分尺寸进行测量，实测结果无异常。Bolts, the flywheel, the size of the torque 
converter portion of the projection is measured, the measured results without exception. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 系统排查连接副松动的原因。Deputy loose connection system to troubleshoot the cause.  
2. 校核变扭器凸台内螺纹的适用性及符合性。Check the torque converter station threaded boss applicability and 
compliance.
