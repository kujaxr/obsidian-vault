Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）发电机线缆接头安装孔及附近材料缺失是由于接触不良后放电引起的。The lack of materials in the installation 
hole and nearby region of the generator cable connector is caused by the discharge after poor contact. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）审核线缆安装过程，评估过程是否存在风险。Review the wiring cable installation process and evaluate whether 
there are risks in the process.
