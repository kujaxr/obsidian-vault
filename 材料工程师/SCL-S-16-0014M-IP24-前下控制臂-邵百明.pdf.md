Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）前下控制臂的断裂是由于内部存在初始裂纹，形成了应力缺口，降低了其承载能力，导致裂纹在特定载荷
的作用下沿着初始裂纹快速扩展所致。The interal crack of the front lower control arm formed a notch stress and 
reduced the carrying capacity, which made the crack under specific load expand along the initial crack and finally 
resulted the fracture. 
（2）材料的成分合格；由硬度换算的抗拉强度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components are ok; the conversion 
tensile strength meet the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
 建议Suggestions： 
（1）结合控制臂生产工艺过程，查找初始裂纹形成的原因。Basing on the production process, find the cause for the 
formation of the original crack. 
（2）对现生产的零件进行100%的探伤，同时封存已生产的零件。Inspect and screen the parts by 100% nondestructive 
test and isolate the parts in the production line and warehouse.  
（3）评估已装车零件的风险。Evaluate the risk of the parts assembled to the whole car.
