APPLIED GRAPHICS ON PLASTIC PARTS 
GM7469M 
1 SCOPE. This 
procedure covers the 
durability 
requirements for applied graphics on plastic or painted plastic 
parts. For Qpes I, II, and III, pad printing is the typical process 
used to apply graphics, epoxy ink is the typical material used. 
For Type IV, pad printing or silk screening is the typical process 
used. Some examples of parts which should be covered by this 
specification are first or second surface interior trim and 
underhood components 
with graphics. 
1.1 The graphic shall be designated according to the area of 
application on the vehicle. 
Type I. Covers graphics on the fast surface of interior trim 
parts that are exposed to W light. 
Type II. Covers graphics on underhood parts (fmt surface). 
Type III. Covers graphics on the first surface of parts not ex- 
posed to W light (e.g. interior, trunk). 
Type IV. Covers graphics on the second surface of interior 
trim parts that are exposed to W light.
