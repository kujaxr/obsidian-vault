Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）散热系统泄露是由于局部受到较大的应力发生了多源疲劳开裂所致。主裂纹为纵向，在进水口第一根扁管与主板
连接处的隔离壁与外壁的结合处（朝侧板一侧）。The radiator leaked because of the tube’s longitudinal cracks by multisource fatigue, which was 
caused by a big local stress and at the transition between the first tube's isolation and outer wall next to the side plate and at the tube connection area with the header.  
（2）该散热系统进/排水侧下面第一根扁管以及排水侧上面第一根扁管在与主板连接处的隔离壁也出现了类似裂纹，
具体见图11~13；与其相邻的扁管未见裂纹。The other isolation walls were cracked, which were at connection areas of the upper and lower first tubes 
with the header; see fig. 11~13; no cracks were found at the adjacent tubes. 
（3）引起开裂的应力可能与流体压力或温度产生的热应力有关；与振动的关联性不大。The crack was related with the fluid pressure 
or thermal stress and the vibration has no direct effect on the failure.  
（4）扁管，主板，侧板，散热片的硬度和组织未见异常；其中侧板的Mn 含量偏低，但不是引起失效的原因。The hardness 
and microstructure of metal parts are normal and side plate's Mn content is low, which is not failure reason 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）分析应力状态，优化设计，降低局部应力或提高局部的强度。Analyze stress and optimize design to reduce stress or improve local strength.  
（2）管控好原材料，确保各项指标符合要求。Control the raw materials well to ensure the indicators meet requirements.
