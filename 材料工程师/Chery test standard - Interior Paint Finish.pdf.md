5 技术要求 technical requirement 
5.1 预处理 pre-treatment 
在试验前，要放在正常气候条件下（空气温度为23℃，允许偏差在±1，空气湿度为50％和允许偏
差在±3）24小时，做预处理。 
Before the test, keep samples in room temperature (Temperature 23±1℃, Humidity 50+/-3% ) 
for 24 hours. 
5.2 外观 Appearance 
喷漆件表面均匀，不允许有针孔、裂纹、流挂、条纹、叠层等缺陷。 
The painted surface shall present an uniform appearance. The defects like pin hole, 
crack,sagging,striping,?? and etc are not allowed. 
喷漆表面的颜色和光泽必须与样品或色板一致。色调和光泽按规定应符合由奇瑞公司设计部门发放
的色标或经相关部门同意的标准样品。 
The color and gloss of the painted surface must match to the standard. Color and gloss 
should comply to the color standard released by Chery or standard sample approved by Chery. 
产品在存放之后，不允许颜色改变，不能出现斑点。 
After storage, no color change or speckle should happen on paint surface. 
5.3 气味 odor 
喷漆件不允许分泌出有毒的化合物，也不允许有异味。  
The painted parts should not have hazadous out-gasing or peculiar smell.

6  试验方法 Test Method 
6.1 附着力 Adhesion 
实验方法参照Q/SQR.04.025；Refer to Q/SQR.04.025 for test method. 
6.2 硬度 Hardness 
实验方法参照GB 6739; Refer to GB 6739 for test method. 
6.3 耐水性 Water resistance 
实验方法参照Q/SQR.04.065; Refer to Q/SQR.04.065 for test method. 
6.4 耐冲击性 Impact Resistance 
试验方法参照零部件自身要求；高度和强度参照各零部件要求； 
Refer to Part Requirement for test method; Refer to Part Requirement for height and strength 
requirement. 
6.5 耐摩色牢度 Color Fastness to Rubbing 
参照GB/T 3920-97 对干燥摩擦物进行100 次厘米行程试验，并按GB250 和GB251 进行评级。 
Refer to GB/T 3920-97 to do dry rubbing for 100 cycles with 1mm distance. Evaluate the test result per 
GB250 and GB251. 
6.6 耐清洁剂 Detergent Resistance 
6.6.1 点滴试验 Drop Test 
使用下列清洁剂进行试验 use detergent listed below to do the test： 
洗洁精；abluent 
氨性-乙醇清洗液，不稀释；Ammoniac-alcoholic detergent without dilution 
清洗汽油；Gasoline for cleaning 
1.1.1 三氯乙烷洗涤剂；Trichloride-ethane detergent 
乙烷合成洗涤剂。Ethane compound detergent 
使用滴定管0.1ml 滴在油漆表面上，作用延续10 分钟，然后在60℃的热循环空气中干燥30 分钟。
表面评定工作必须在滴定后24 小时进行。 
Use buret to drop 0.1ml detergent on the film surface. Keep it for 10 minutes then try 
it for 30 minutes in the 60℃ cycling air. The surface evaluation need to be done after 24 
hours. 
6.6.2 摩擦色牢度 Color Fastness to Scratch 
把摩擦物放在清洁剂介质中浸泡至少一分钟然后放在滤纸上吸水，用浸湿了清洁剂的摩擦物，按照
GB/T 3920 进行10 次厘米行程试验。当潮湿擦布不拿掉时，油漆表面和组织结构没有改变，光泽度允
许有微小的变化，摩擦织物上颜色按GB250 和GB251 进行评级。  
Keep the rubbing tool (cloth) in the detergent for at least 1 minute and then absorb liquid 
on the rubbing tool with filter paper. Make 10 cycles of 1mm rubbing per GB/T 3920. Take away 
the cloth and the film surface configuration should not change. Slight gloss level change is 
allowable. Evaluate the level of color on the cloth per GB250 and GB251. 
6.7 耐合成汗液 perspiration Resistance 
试样放在溶液A 中浸泡30 分钟，接着在放在溶液B 中浸泡30 分钟，两种溶液的温度均为35℃； 
Keep specimen in Solution A for 30 minutes then Solution B for 30 minutes. Temperature 
for both solutions should be 35℃. 
A：醋酸溶液含量为0.75%， A: 0.75% acetic acid solution 
B：氨水溶液含0.036%重量，另外加0.5%重量的氯化钠；B: Solution with 0.036% (by weight)

6.7.1 耐摩色牢度 Color Fastness to Rubbing 
把摩擦物放在合成汗液中浸泡至少一分钟然后放在滤纸上吸水，用浸湿了合成汗液的摩擦物，按照

6.7.2  附着力 Adhesion 
参照Q/SQR.04.025 方法进行；Refer to Q/SQR.04.025 for test method. 
 
6.8 耐热性 Heat Resistance 
将试片置于规定的温度下，放置规定的时间（温度和时间参照零部件自身要求）后检查试片表面，
涂膜应没有剥离、破裂、起泡。 
Keep the specimen under required temperature for required time (temperature and time 
requirement refer to Part Requirement). Evaluate the surface of specimen. The film should have 
no flaking or chipping or blistering.  
6.8.1 摩擦色牢度 Color Fastness to Rubbing 
进行10 次行程摩擦后评价，GB250 和GB251 评定试样等级。 
Evaluate after 10 cycles and evaluate the level per GB250 and GB251. 
6.8.2 附着力 Adhesion 
参照Q/SQR.04.025 方法进行；Refer to Q/SQR.04.025 for test method. 
 
6.9 耐寒性Cold Resistance 
将试片置于-40℃恒温中，24 小时后检查试片表面涂膜应没有剥离、破裂、起泡。 
Keep specimen in environment with -40℃ constant temperature. After 24 hours, the film 
should have no flaking or chipping or blistering. 
6.9.1 附着力 Adhesion 
参照Q/SQR.04.025 方法进行；Refer to Q/SQR.04.025 for test method. 
 
6.10 耐气候交变性 Climate Cycle

90℃热循环空气中，放置120 小时；  
Keep specimen under -40℃ for 24 hours, then move to room temperature for 30 minutes, then 
keep in cycling air with 90℃ for 120 hours.

7 产品出厂验收 Product inspection 
7.1  试样的检验分为供方的出厂检验、定期检验、型式检验和本公司的进货检验。Product inspection 
includes Exfactory Inspection by supplier, Periodic Inspection, Type Inspection and Chery 
Acceptance Inspection. 
7.2  出厂检验 Exfactory Inspection 
试样应按批次由供方质量检验部门按本标准的规定出厂检验项目进行检验，全部合格，并出具合格
证后，方可出厂。 
Product from each batch need to be inspected by quality department of supplier basing on 
required items by this standard. The product can be shipped out of manufacturer only if it 
passes all inspection items and have passing certification been presented. 
7.3 定期检验 Periodic Inspection 
7.3.1 供方质检部门每6 个月应进行一次定期检验，并应提供相应的定期检验报告。 
      Quality department from supplier needs to have one inspection every 6 months and provide 
inspection report. 
7.3.2  定期检验项目：外观、附着力、硬度、耐水、耐摩色牢度、耐清洁剂等。 
      Periodic Inspection items: Appearance, Adhesion, Hardness, Water Resistance, Color 
Fastness for Rubbing, Detergent Resistance and etc. 
7.3.3  定期检验应从出厂检验合格的产品中以5 批次为取样基数，随时从2 批次的产品中取样。 
      Periodic Inspection should be taken on samples from 2 batches among 5 batches of products 
that passed Exfactory Inspection. 
7.3.4  定期检验若有一项不合格，则可判定该定期检验不合格。 
The Periodic Inspection can be determined to be fail if one item of Periodic Inspection 
fails. 
7.3.5  定期检验不合格应停止供货、停产整顿，直至检验合格后方可恢复生产和供货。 
      If the Periodic Inspection fails, the delivery and production need to be stopped till 
products can pass the inspection.

7.4  型式检验 Type Inspection 
7.4.1  型式检验每两年进行一次。Type Inspection takes place every two years. 
7.4.2  型式检验时应从本评定同期内生产的并经质检验部门检验合格的产品中取样，随机在3 批次的
产品中取样，取样基数不少于取样数的3 倍。 
      For Type Inspection, sample should be taken from products produced in this period of 
time and have passed Inspection from quality department. Sample should be randomly selected 
from 3 batches. The sample base number should not be less than 3 times of sample number. 
7.4.3  型式检验中，若样品中有一项不合格，则判定型式检验不合格。 
      If one item fails in the Type Inspection, the Type Inspection will be determined as fail. 
7.4.4  型式检验不合格，应停产整顿并停止供货，待检验合格后方可恢复生产和供货。 
      If the Type Inspection fails, the delivery and production need to be stopped till products 
can pass the inspection.  
7.4.5  型式检验应在公认的第三方国家级质量检验机构进行，或在本公司认可的具有检验能力和手段
的检验机构进行（此时应有本公司人员参加监督）。 
      Type Inspection should be held by the certificated national level third-party test 
institutions or those capable test institutions approved by Chery (In this case, people from 
Chery need to be involved for supervisal). 
7.4.6  出现下列情况之一时，必须进行型式检验 Type Inspection need to be held when one of below 
things happens： 
a）停产一年以上恢复生产或转厂生产时；Reproduction after more than one-year without volume 
out-put or change of manufacturing site.  
b）正式生产后，如原料、材料、工艺等有较大改变，可能影响产品性能时；During production, if 
raw material, material or process has big changes which may impact product performance. 
c）出厂检验与定期检验结果有较大差异时；When there’s big difference between result of 
Exfactory Inspection and Periodic Inspection. 
d）出现重大质量问题改进后或本公司提出要求时；Improved after big quality problem happened 
or when Chery require the inspection. 
e）国家质量监督机构提出进行型式检验要求时。When the national quality institution require 
the inspection.
