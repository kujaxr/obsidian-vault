Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 电子真空泵支架由于应力大而在应力集中点产生疲劳断裂。 
The part belongs to fatigue at the stress concentration point due to high stress .. 
2. 电子真空泵支架材料成分，强度和金相组织满足标准要求。 
The composition, the strength and the metallurgical structure meet requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
校核支架的振动载荷，优化加强筋处倒角，降低应力 
Check the vibration load and adjust the guide angle at the stiffener to reduce the stress.
