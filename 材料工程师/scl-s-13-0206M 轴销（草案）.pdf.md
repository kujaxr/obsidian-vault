Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴销是由于载荷较大而发生双向弯曲疲劳断裂的。The shift pin was broken by bending fatigue due to a big load. 
（2）轴销的成分，表面硬度，感应淬火区的位置符合要求；硬化层偏深，但不是轴销失效的原因。The chemical 
components, surface hardness and the quenching position of the shift pin meet the requirement; the depth of the hardened 
layer is a little thinner than the requirement, which is not the failure reason. 
（3）选换档套管成分和硬化层深符合要求；由于淬火层的组织为马氏体+铁素体，使得表面硬度处于要求的下限。
The chemical components and hardened layer thickness of the shift rail meet the requirement. The microstructure of the 
hardened layer is martensite and ferrite, so the surface hardness is on the limit. 
（4）轴销的直径偏大，不符合要求，但不是失效的原因。The dia. of the pin is over the upper limit, which is not the 
failure reason. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核载荷；check the load. 
（2）依据零件强度要求，在图纸中定义轴销芯部的硬度要求。According the strength requirement of the shift 
pin, specify the core hardness requirement in the drawing. 
（3）优化感应淬火的工艺，确保各项指标符合图纸要求。Optimize the induction hardening process, ensure the 
quality meets the requirement of the drawing.
