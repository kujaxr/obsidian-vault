Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 过载引起的断裂。The gears have failed due to the overloading. 
2. 硬度、有效硬化层深度符合图纸的技术要求。The  hardness and effective case depth meet the requirements of the drawing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
工程进行设计校核。Check the design by TC. 
2. 检查同批零件的尺寸。Check dimensions of new parts from the same batch.
