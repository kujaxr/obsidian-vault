samples each applicable to 1 m². The established rate is defined as

为1 平方米。得到的比率即为HC 平方-系数。

3.2.3.1 
Adhesion  
3.2.3.1 
附着力 
 
 As described in the following, the adhesion test is carried out approx. 
one hour after preparing the KURZ TEST PART. 
 如下所述，附着力试验应当在库尔兹测试样件制备出来大约一小时之后
进行。 
 Put KURZ TEST PART on even surface. 
 将库尔兹测试样件放在一平坦表面上。 
 Unroll and cut off a stripe of 13 – 16 cm from the Tesa-roll (Tesafilm 
4104 transparent). 
 从德沙卷（德沙膜 4104 透明）上拉出并截下长度为13~16 厘米的一段
胶带条。 
 Fix the Tesa stripe onto the decorated surface of the KURZ TEST 
PART so that the adhesive overlaps the KURZ TEST PART by approx. 
5 – 7 cm. 
 将德沙箔带固定到库尔兹测试样件的装饰面上，并使库尔兹测试样件的
粘性重叠面的长度保持在大约5-7 厘米之间。 
 Rub the Tesa stripe to the surface 3 to 4 times with thumb. 
 用拇指刮擦德沙胶带条3 至4 次，使其附着到表面。 
 Tear off the Tesa stripe in a single movement at ~45°, forwards 
 以45°角度向前一次性扯掉德沙胶带条 
 
Evaluation: At least 96 % of the Foil must stick on the KURZ TEST PART. 
评价：至少96％的薄膜必须粘附在库尔兹测试样件上。
