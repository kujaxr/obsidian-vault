Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2.调节杆受异常外力导致在根部发生快速断裂。 
Conclusions: After testing, the materials of the adjust levels conform to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the adjust level, it can be seen that the adjust level was broken at 
the bottom region by an excessive outside force.   
建议： 避免对调节杆施加过大外力。 
Suggestions：The excessive outside force on the adjust level should be avoided.
