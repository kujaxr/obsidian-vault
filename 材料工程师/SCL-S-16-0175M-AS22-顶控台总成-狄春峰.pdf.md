Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．零件受异常外力导致开裂，是失效的主要原因。 
2．零件材料不符合技术要求，是失效的次要原因。 
Conclusions: 1. Foreign force made part fracture. 
2.The material performance of part did not conform to requirements. 
建议： 1. 原材料供应商进行整改。 
Suggestions: 1. Raw material supplier should rectify.
