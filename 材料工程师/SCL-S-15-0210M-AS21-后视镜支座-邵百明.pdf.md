Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 后视镜支座是由于过载而发生开裂。The base of rear mirror was cracked because of overload. 
（2） 材料的成分符合要求；开裂处的组织，孔隙率，孔隙大小以及硬度均正常。The chemical components meet 
the requirement; the microstructure, porosity, hole size and the hardness of the crack location are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 关注支座的生产过程，避免可能导致支座损伤的操作，同时加强过程检验。Pay attention the production 
process to avoid the operation which makes the base damaged; at the same time, strength the process inspection.
