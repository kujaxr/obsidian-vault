Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 油标尺管支架是由于疲劳而断裂的，裂纹起源于支架边角(见图4)，裂纹源未发现受损痕迹。The bracket of the 
dipstick tube was broken off though fatigue. The crack originated from the corner and the origin zone was without 
defect. 
2. 支架化学成分符合要求，组织及由硬度转化而来的抗拉强度正常。Chemical components met the requirement. 
Microstructure and tensile strength converted from hardness were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 关注售后车辆失效部位开裂情况；Focus on the failure parts at cracking region of after-sale vehicle  
2. 评估支架的受力。Evaluate the stress at the bracket.

综上所述： 
 
油标尺管支架是由于疲劳而断裂，裂纹起源于支架边角，且裂纹源区无受损。The bracket of the dipstick tube 
was broken off though fatigue. The crack originated from the corner and the origin zone was without defect. 
 
支架材料是正常的，弯曲角度R 也符合要求。Microstructure of the failed parts was normal. Plate thickness and 
angle R met the technical requirement.  
 
支架板材厚度偏下限。调查该供应商在PPAP 阶段检测报告，板材厚度在1.41~1.43mm，失效件与其差别不
大，板厚偏薄应该不是引起其失效的主要原因。Thickness of the bracket was near lower limit. Base on the 
Investigated information, the thickness of the bracket was 1.41~1.43mm in the PPAP stage. Thickness of the failed 
parts was normal, which wasn’t the main failure reason. 


排放标准而经过返修的发动机：发动机部分零件进行了更换其中就包括油标尺管。After investigation, the type
