Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  车门限位器螺母、螺栓咬死是由于咬合不良引起的。 
    Bad matching result in the bolts of ling assembly bolt scuffing. 
2. 失效螺母(见图2)及同批次零件(见图5)牙型异常。 
   The tread of failed nut and the same parts is abnormal. 
3.  螺栓牙型、螺距正常；螺母螺距正常。 
    Thread and pitch of bolt are normal and pitch of nut is normal  
建议Suggestions： 
1.  加强螺母牙型质量监管。 
    Strength nut thread quality supervising. 
2. 排查装配过程。 
    Check the assembly process.
