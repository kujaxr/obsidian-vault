Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件原材料降解，力学性能变差，受外力后断裂。 
The material of failure parts degraded, leading to poor mechanical properties, and fractured after 
forced.  
2. 零件材料不符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 责令供应商进行整改。 
                   Suppliers rectify the problem. 
                 2. 优化安装脚结构。 
                   Optimize the structure of install feet.
