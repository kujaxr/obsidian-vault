1 
Scope 
The nominal and limit dimensions in Figure 1 and Tables 1 to 4 apply to threads with bright final 
condition and to threads with electrolytic surface protection or with surface protection applied using 
the dip/spin coating method.  
Refer to VW 116 24, VW 116 27 and VW 116 10 for the limit dimensions before application of the 
named surface protection.  
2 
Designation 
Designation of a metric ISO coarse thread with nominal thread diameter d = 10 mm, flank lead 
P = 1.5 mm and tolerance class 6g with surface protection layer for the external thread and toler-
ance class 6H with surface protection layer for the internal thread: 
M10 – VW 116 11 
Designation of a metric ISO fine-pitch thread with nominal thread diameter d = 10 mm, flank lead 
P = 1 mm and tolerance class 6g with surface protection layer for the external thread and tolerance 
class 6H with surface protection layer for the internal thread: 
M10 x 1 – VW 116 11 
The English translation is believed to be accurate. 
In case of discrepancies the German version shall govern.
