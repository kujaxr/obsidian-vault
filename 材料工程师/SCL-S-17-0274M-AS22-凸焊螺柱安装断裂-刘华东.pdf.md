Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 基于目前检测的结果，卡死的原因主要有两点：（1）隔热罩的铝屑导致螺柱与螺母卡死；（2）装配不良导致
螺母与螺钉出现啮合异常，从而出现卡死。Based on the current test results, the main reasons are: (1) the heat shield 
cover aluminum chips lead to bolt and nut be stuck; (2) poor assembly led to nut and bolt appear abnormal mesh, and 
then be stuck. 
2、 螺柱的强度符合8.8 级的技术要求。The strength of blot meets the requirements of the 8.8 grade. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 关注焊接螺柱与隔热罩匹配位置，防止零件出现干涉问题。Pay attention to the position of the weld bolt which is 
matched with the heat shield to prevent the interference of parts. 
2、 螺母装配应注意牙纹导入，考虑装配时可以先手动预拧。Pay attention to the thread import during assembly nuts, 
such as considering pre-tighten by manual.

一、宏观观察  Macroscopic Observation： 
临港工厂现场将AS22 后隔热罩螺母紧固到纵梁上焊接螺柱时，发生多例卡死现象，拆卸时发生断裂，失
效零件如图1 所示。
