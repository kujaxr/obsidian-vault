Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件受异常侧向外力后断裂。 
The failure part was fractured after abnormal lateral force. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 注塑环节加强管理，避免提拉杆侧向受力。 
                    Strengthen management of injection molding to avoid lateral force of pulling rod. 
2. 优化注塑工艺。 
                    Optimize injection molding process.
