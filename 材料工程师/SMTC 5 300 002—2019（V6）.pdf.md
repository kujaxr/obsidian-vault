1 范围 Scope 
 
 
本标准规定了对汽车内饰件的涂装要
求。 
本标准适用于安装于汽车内部的油漆、
胶头印刷、热压、模内转印和水转印方法涂
覆的（内部装饰）金属和非金属材料。本标
准将内饰涂装零件分为I类与II类： 
a) I类零件：中级轿车及以上车型的内
饰涂装件（B级车及B级以上车型）与
出口欧洲的车型； 
b) II类零件：中级轿车以下车型的内饰
涂装件（B级车以下车型）。 
 
The standard specifies the paint coating 
requirement on vehicle interior trim. 
 
This Technical Supply Specification applies to 
painted, 
pad-printed, 
heat-sealed, 
in-mold 
decorated (IMD) and cubic-printed metallic and 
non-metallic materials, which are used in the 
vehicle interior. This standard classifies the 
interior trim parts into Type I and Type II. 
a) Type I: interior trim parts in B grade cars 
and above, cars exported to European 
b) Type II: interior trim parts in cars below 
grade B

4  漆层性能 The performance of the coating 
4.1  预处理Conditioning 
    所有有待检验的样品必须进行预置：在
空气温度范围23±5°C内存放7 天或在
60±2℃环境内存放48h。 每个单项检验后零
件必须预置30 min，以便进行目视评定。按
照第4.3 、4.4 条检验必须在23±5℃预置1 
h。纤维增强、滚珠增强或发泡零件必须在23
±5℃下的预置24 h后进行检验。 
 
  
All 
samples 
before 
testing 
must 
be 
pre-conditioned at least 7 days at room 
temperature 23±5°C or 48 h at 60±2 °C. Parts 
must be preset 30 min after each individual test, 
for visual evaluation. Testing acc. to Sections 4.3 
and 4.4 shall be performed after 1 h of 
conditioning at 23±5°C. Fiber-, ball-reinforced 
or foamed parts shall be tested after 24 h of 
conditioning at 23±5°C.

心实验室的同意。

(including
