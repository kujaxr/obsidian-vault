Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过旋钮的材料分析和宏观分析发现，正常工艺制造的旋钮在正常使用状态下不会发生断裂；旋钮
的轴孔弧线部位存在注塑薄弱环节，如果注塑工艺不稳定，容易导致在该薄弱部位开裂。 
2. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the knob, it can be seen that the break 
can’t appeared at normal using conditions and by normal manufacture process. But there is a weak region on 
the hole of knob during injection process. If the injection process unstable, the fracture easily occurs at the 
weak region. 
建议：保持注塑工艺稳定；更改熔合区位置。 
Suggestions：The stable injection process should be provided. The position of weld zone should be changed.

小结：通过旋钮的材料分析和宏观分析发现，旋钮的轴孔弧线部位存在注塑薄弱环节，如果注塑工艺不稳定，
容易导致在该薄弱部位开裂。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the knob, it can be seen that there is a 
weak region on the hole of knob during injection process. If the injection process unstable, the fracture easily 
occurs at the weak region.
