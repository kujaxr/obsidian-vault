Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：零件材料及其衰减程度符合试验大纲要求。 
Conclusions：the material and the change properties after road test conforms to the DV plan. 
由于零件与支架接触点为零件相对较薄区域，且支架为金属支架，故零件材料无法承受疲劳作用力最
终导致零件开裂。 
As the area of the part contact with bracket is thinner relatively and the bracket is made of metal, the 
material can not withstand the fatigue forces eventually led to parts cracking. 
建议：优化结构匹配，降低零件在使用中受到的疲劳作用。 
Suggestions：Optimize the structure to match the lower parts in use by the fatigue effect. 
提高材料的耐疲劳作用。 
Increase the anti-fatigue property of the material.
