Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 失效件属于过载断裂。Bolt fractured because of the overload. 
（2） 失效件螺牙存在折叠，成分、硬度及组织正常。Folding defect exist in the tooth of the failed parts and chemical 
components、hardness and microstructure are normal. 
（3） 同批次及通过试验的零件螺牙存在折叠。There is folding defect in the tooth of the same batch and the tested 
parts. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 排查装配过程质量。Check the assembly process.  
（2） 追踪此批次螺栓，并评估其风险。Trace this batch parts and do the risk assessment. 
（3） 供应商加强控制过程质量。Control the process quality.
