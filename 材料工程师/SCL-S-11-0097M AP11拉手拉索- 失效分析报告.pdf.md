Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、拉线的铸锌接头上有很大飞边(见图6), 阻止其在壳体里（拉手装置）的正常旋转，造成钢丝不均匀
受载，最终导致双向弯曲疲劳断裂(见图3 和4)。 A large burr on the Hook end fitting prevented normal rotation of this part within the 
housing (figure 6).  This caused abnormal bending load on the wire, which then fractured through reverse bending fatigue (figures 3 & 4)  
2、 铸锌接头上有飞边，飞边尺寸不符合图纸要求.。The burr is outside of drawing specification.

应用的因素 
Application considerations 
 
铸锌接头与拉手装置的装配示意图，见图7。 在拉手操作过程中，铸锌接头在拉手装置中需旋转。 图纸因此要
求“压铸毛边允许0.1mm 最大”。铸锌接头的飞边超出要求，会阻碍铸锌接头在拉手装置的旋转，因此导致钢丝
弯曲疲劳。 
 
The end fitting is located in the handle mechanism as shown in figure 7.  During handle operation, the fitting must rotate within the mechanism. The 
drawing therefore requires “die cast flash permitted 0.1max thickness”. 
The large burr is outside specification and would prevent rotation of the part, therefore leading to the bending fatigue of the wire. 
 
 
图7  安装配置 
 
 
新拉锁的拉伸试验： 
进行了3 个新拉锁的拉伸试验，加载速度是5mm/分钟，结果3 个零件均符合要求。 
Tensile testing of new parts 
3 new parts were loaded in tension.  The loading rate was 5mm/minute.  All parts met the requirements of the drawing.
