Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）增压器进水管受到较大的振动载荷而发生疲劳开裂。The water inlet pipe of supercharger suffered from fatigue 
cracking due to large vibration load. 
（2）材料硬度、基体组织和壁厚正常，表面脱碳层深度约40μm。The hardness , microstructure and thickness of the 
material are normal, and the depth of decarburization layer is about 40μm. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）校核载荷，排查系统的振动。Check the load and the vibration of the system.
