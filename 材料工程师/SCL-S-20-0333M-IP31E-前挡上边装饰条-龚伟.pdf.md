Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions: 1.  失效件受锐物反复划伤导致破损。 
Failure part was damaged by repeated scratches of sharp objects. 
2.  失效件材料不符合技术要求，但不是失效的主要原因。 
Performance of parts’ material didn’t conform to requirement, but it's not the main reason of the 
failure. 
建议Suggestions: 1.  排查物流过程异常。 
Check logistics process.  
2.  供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
Suppliers explain and rectify the material problem.
