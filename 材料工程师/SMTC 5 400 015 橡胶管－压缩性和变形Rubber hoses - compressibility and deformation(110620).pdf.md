1 范围 
    本标准规定了轻载加强型真空制动胶管压
缩性和变形的测试方法。 
本标准适用于轻载加强型真空制动胶管。 
                                       
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the test of com-
pressibility and deformation on light duty rein-
forced vacuum brake hoses.  
This standard applies to the light duty 
reinforced vacuum brake hoses.  
 
2 设备 
2  Apparatus 
2.1 压缩装置，具有测量压扁样品需要的施加
载荷的手段（设备）。 
2.1  A compression device having a means of 
measuring the applied load required to col-
lapse the specimen. 
2.2 塞尺，用于测量压扁的胶管内壁间的距离。
2.2  Feeler gauges for measuring the free 
distance between the inner faces of the col-
lapsed hose.
