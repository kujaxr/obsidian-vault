Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件发生了疲劳开裂。 
The failure parts were fatigue cracking. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 失效位置增加倒角以提高强度。 
                 Add chamfer at the failure position to improve the strength. 
2. 进行疲劳耐久试验，评估高温对失效的影响。 
                 Take fatigue endurance test to evaluate the effect of high temperature. 
3. 供应商调查失效批次生产变化点。 
                 The supplier investigates the production change points of invalid batch.
