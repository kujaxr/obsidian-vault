Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 前副车架左前、左后安装支架沿焊缝热影响区疲劳开裂。Mounting plate in the front frame was cracking along the 
welding heated zone. 
2. 零件成分符合要求，金相及由硬度转化而来的抗拉强度正常。Chemical components met the requirement. 
Microstructure and converted tensile strength were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核载荷。Check the load.

该区为焊缝接头薄弱区域。
