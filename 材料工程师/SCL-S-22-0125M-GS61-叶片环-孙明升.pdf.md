Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）叶片环受到异常载荷而导致过载断裂。 
The vane ring occurred overload fracture because of abnormal load. 
（2）材料表面硬度符合要求，金相正常。 
The surface hardness of the material met the requirements and the metallography was normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
排查系统异常载荷的来源。Check the source of abnormal load of system.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results

3 段，实验室受委托对叶片环断裂进行失效分析，失效件形貌如图1 所示，断裂位置处存在明显的磕碰变形。
