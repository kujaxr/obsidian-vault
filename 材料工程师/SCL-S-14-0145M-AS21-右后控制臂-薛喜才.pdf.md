Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 后下控制臂是由于疲劳而开裂的；ARM ASSY-RR SUSPENSION was cracking though fatigue propagation. 
2. 从检测结果来看，开裂部位并未按照技术要求进行”包边”处理，降低了疲劳强度，造成过早发生疲劳失效；
Base the testing result, the welding seam wasn’t covering the edge at the cracking region, which reduced the fatigue 
strength, resulting in fatigue failure early. 
3. 材料成分、厚度符合要求；硬度及组织正常； Chemical components and thickness met the requirement. 
Microstructure and harness were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商应按照焊接技术要求进行焊接，保证焊接质量；Supplier should weld according the technical requirement to 
make sure the welding quality. 
2. 优化零件结构设计，降低关键部件应力载荷(参照大众PQ35)；Accord the PQ35 structure, optimize the design to 
reduce the load at main location.
