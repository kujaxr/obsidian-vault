1  范围 
本标准规定了舒适型聚氨酯泡沫的一般要
求、材料要求及测试方法。 
本标准适用于火焰复合的聚氨酯泡沫。 
1  Scope  
This standard covers the general per-
formance requirements and test methods for 
comfort PUR foam. 
This Standard is mainly used as foam for 
flame-bonded laminates.

3.5  材料代号 
每种材料应当符合SMTC 90007中的标识。
3.5  Types 
All materials should meet SMTC 90007. 
3.6  散发特性 
必须满足SMTC 5 300 004 的要求。 
3.6  Emission behavior 
 Emission behavior should meet SMTC 5 
300 004. 
3.7  燃烧性能 
按照SMTC 5 400 008执行。 
3.7  Flammability 
Flammability should meet SMTC 5 400 
008. 
3.8  取样要求 
在制造48 h之后才能进行取样。为进行完
整实验需要面积为2 m
2的样品。 
3.8  Specimens 
Sampling shall begin no sooner than 48 h 
after the manufacture of the PUR flexible 
foam. For complete testing, at least 2 m2 is 
required. 
3.9  状态调节 
试验前，样品必须在ISO291 23/50的标准
气候下至少放置24 h。 
3.9  Conditioning 
Prior to testing, the specimens must be 
conditioned for at least 24 h in the ISO291 
23/50 standard climate.
