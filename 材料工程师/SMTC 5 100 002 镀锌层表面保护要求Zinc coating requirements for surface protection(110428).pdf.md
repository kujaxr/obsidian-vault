1 范围 
本标准规定了钢制件上电解析出的镀锌层
以及热镀锌半成品制成零件的更改要求，但不是
针对与镁接触的零件。 
本标准适用于金属零件镀锌层的评价。不适
合使用于温度持续增加到大于100℃的镀锌层。
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the requirements 
for electrolytically deposited zinc coatings on 
steel parts as well as for parts made from 
hot-dip zinc-coated semi-finished products, but 
not for contact with magnesium. The coatings 
are not suitable for lasting conditions of ele-
vated temperatures > 100 ℃. 
The standard is adopted to evaluate the 
zinc coatings on steel parts.

3.2.3  假如在图纸中未明确标出零件的某些地

3.2.3  Unless certain sections of parts that are

3.2.11  在设计和控制制造工艺时必须注意不
让成品件的使用性能受到影响。特别是必须通过
对预处理和热处理的恰当选择和组合，确保不发
生氢脆。 
3.2.11  The layout and control of the produc-
tion process shall not impair the functional 
characteristics of the finished part. In particu-
lar, it shall be ensured, through the appropriate 
selection and combination of pretreatment and 
heat treatment that no hydrogen-induced brittle 
fractures occur. 
3.2.12  为避免氢脆，必须迅速在电镀后，至少
在4小时之内和在镀层进行后处理前进行所有的
热处理（详见DIN EN ISO 4042）。 
 
3.2.12  All heat treatments to avoid hydro-
gen-induced embrittling must be performed as 
quickly as possible after depositing, however 
within 4 hours at least and before any further 
treatment of the coatings (see DIN EN ISO 
4042). 
3.2.13  由于氢脆的风险，高强度钢制件（Rm > 
1000 N/mm
2）的表面保护不允许使用电镀锌层。
3.2.13  Electrolytically deposited zinc is not 
permitted on parts in excess of 1000MPa as 
the risk of hydrogen-induced brittle fractures. 
3.3 用于油漆的镀锌层 
3.3  Zinc coatings for paintwork 
3.3.1  镀锌后没有直接进行表面处理的半成品
的电镀镀锌层（例如从镀锌车间运输到油漆车
间），按照SP-c310不允许进行钝化处理和磷化
处理。 
 
3.3.1  Electroplated zinc coatings on semi 
finished products, the surface treatment of 
which is not completed immediately after the 
zinc coating process (e.g. dispatch from the 
zinc-coating plant to the painting plant) may 
not, according to SP-c310, display any passi-
vation or phosphating. 
3.3.2  对镀锌件的必要运输和仓储保护，例如
用油，必须与油漆车间进行协商。  
3.3.2  Any required transport or storage pro-
tection of zinc coated parts, e.g. by oiling, shall 
be agreed upon with the painting plant. 
3.3.3  在上海汽车的工厂油漆时，运输保护和
仓储保护必须符合质量要求。 
3.3.3  Where painting is performed in a SAIC
plant the transport or storage protection shall 
correspond to quality requirement. 
3.3.4  此外，镀锌层厚度较厚时，诸如在边缘
区域，镀锌层也必须具有良好的附着强度。 
3.3.4  In addition, the zinc coatings must dis-
play impeccable adhesive strength even at 
greater layer thicknesses, e.g. in the edge 
area. 
3.4 不含Cr
6+的钝化层 
3.4  Cr 6+ free passivation treatment layers 
3.4.1  在电镀锌层中，相对于盐水和冷凝水，
在技术上一般以钝化溶解液进行后处理，以改善
耐蚀性。 
 
3.4.1  In order to improve the corrosion re-
sistance of electrolytically deposited zinc 
coatings to salt water and condensed water, a 
post-treatment in passivation solutions is 
technically standard. 
 
3.4.2  如果已使用的钝化溶解液不含Cr
6+化合
物，那么产生的转化涂层也不含Cr
6+。 
 
3.4.2  The passivation treatment solution 
must not contain any Cr (VI) compounds, and 
the resultant conversion layers are likewise  
Cr 6+ free. 
 
3.4.3  涂层厚度约0.1μm的透明和蓝色钝化
层，亦称谓薄层钝化层，允许优先使用于乘客车
厢或行李箱内的零件，只要外观需要银色的话。
若需要，还可以另外涂封闭层。 
 
3.4.3  Colorless and blue passivation treat-
ments, so called thin film passivation treat-
ments, with a layer thickness of approx. 0.1 µm 
are preferred for parts in the passenger and/or 
trunk compartment area, if a silver appearance 
is required, they may also be sealed.

3.5 封闭层 
3.5  Coatings with sealing 
3.5.1  罩一层封闭层，把有机和/或无机材料涂
到事先形成的涂层上，并且部分贮存。 
 
  
3.5.1
In the sealing process, organic and/or 
inorganic materials are applied to the pre-
viously formed conversion layers and partially 
included. 
3.5.2  增加封闭层，涂层结构的厚度可提高0.5
μm至2.0μm。 
 
3.5.2  The additional sealing can increase the 
thickness of the layer structure by 0.5 to 2.0 
µm. 
 
3.5.3  与没有封闭层的涂层相比有下列优点：
  
a） 提高耐蚀性（在镀锌层厚度未改变的情
况下，延缓锌腐蚀和金属母体腐蚀）；
 
b） 改变了滑移性能（降低连接元件上的磨
擦系数和其差异范围）； 
 
c） 色泽（明显降低厚镀层钝化的虹彩，由
此可改善视觉效果）。 
3.5.3  This offers the following advantages 
over unsealed coatings: 
a)  Increased corrosion resistance (de-
layed zinc and base metal corrosion 
with an unchanged zinc coating 
thickness); 
b)  Change in the sliding properties (re-
duction of the friction coefficients and 
their variation ranges in joining ele-
ments); 
c)  Color coding (the iridescence of the 
thick layer passivation treatments is 
significantly reduced, thus improving 
the appearance).
