Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 上片裂纹为疲劳开裂，源于上下片弯曲配合位置焊缝中断和再次起弧处背面，上片焊趾处，此处为高应力区；
upper shell crack belongs to fatigue failure, crack initiated at weld toe of upper shell in bend match area with high stress. 
2. 裂源附近上片熔深不良，且有未熔合缺陷；Weld penetration defect in crack original area. 
3. 上片材料性能正常，符合技术标准要求；Upper shell conform to material standard requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 关注台架试验过程，并评估设计载荷；Pay attention to bench test process and design load. 
2. 确保上下片间焊缝在弯角位置高应力区熔合良好，避免因焊缝中断、补焊等导致熔合不良；Weldseam between 
upper and lower shell should not interrupt either rework in high stress area to ensure good penetration.
