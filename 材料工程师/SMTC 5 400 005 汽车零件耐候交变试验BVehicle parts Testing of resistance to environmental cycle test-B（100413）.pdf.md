1 范围 
本标准为试验规范，适用于对汽车零件，特
别是批量零件的质量连续监控。进行循环气候交
变试验的样品，可以是新零件，也可以是经过特
殊预老化后的零件。 
此标准可以评判零件在承受气候交变负荷
时的性能（例如：裂纹敏感性，变形，合成材料
的分离等）。 
A方案：零件 
B方案：整车，借助红外射线 
1  Scope  
This test specification is used in sample and 
series monitoring tests for quality testing of 
vehicle parts, in particular Group parts, in the 
environmental cycle test both in unused 
condition as well as after component-specific 
pre-aging. 
The behavior of the parts during environmental 
cycle stressing shall be assessed here (e.g. 
susceptibility to cracks, deformation, 
separation of the composite material, etc.). 
Variant A single parts. 
Variant B complete vehicle, with IR radiator 
support.

4 试验程序 
4  Test Procedure 
4.1 试验的实施 
待试验零件（作为单个零件或总成）或整车
放入准备好的模拟气候室内并在要求的循环后
进行评定。 
4.1 Testing 
The parts to be tested (as a single part or an 
ASSY) or the vehicle are placed in the 
prepared climatic chamber and assessed after 
the required number of cycles. 
4.1.1 老化 
按照技术标准要求或图纸要求（优先考虑图
纸要求）。 
4.1.1 Aging 
According to technical supply specification or 
drawing (drawing entries take precedence). 
4.1.2 模拟气候条件下存放 
循环次数按照技术标准要求或图纸要求（优
先考虑图纸要求）。 
必须始终遵守保持时间，降温和升温阶段可
以根据所使用模拟气候室的工作能力有所变化，
即使因此而比此试验方案所规定的循环持续时
间延长了不超过1/3也是允许的。偏差情况应写
入试验记录中。在制冷阶段（温度<0℃）不必
调节湿度，当然它的相对湿度不应超过30％。 
模拟气候室装料要保持在23℃和相对湿度
30％的环境下进行。 
4.1.2 Climatic aging 
Number of cycles according to technical supply 
specification or drawing (drawing entries take 
precedence). 
The holding times must always be maintained. 
The cooling-off and heating-up phases can be 
varied according to the performance capability 
of the climatic chambers used, even if this 
extends the cycle duration specified for both 
variants by max. 1/3. Deviations shall be 
specified in the test report. 
The humidity must not be controlled in the cold 
phases (temperature < 0℃), however it should 
not exceed 30 % rel. humidity. 
The chambers are loaded in the holding phase 
at 23℃ and 30 % rel. humidity.

1 cycle (see also Diagram 1) takes 12 hours 
and includes the following temperature and 
climatic conditions:

rel. humidity 
- 90 minutes Cooling-off phase from 23 to -35
℃ at 30 % rel. humidity

30 % rel. humidity 
- 80 minutes Heating-up phase to 50℃ and 80 
% rel. humidity 
- 120 minutes Holding time at 50℃ and 80 % 
rel. humidity 
- 30 minutes Heating-up phase to 80℃ and 30 
% rel. humidity 
- 240 minutes Holding time at 80℃ and 30 % 
rel. humidity 
- 60 minutes Cooling-off phase to 23℃ and 30 
% rel. humidity 
The actual water content of the air must not 
exceed 95 g/m3 during the phase of heating up 
to 80℃, 30 % rel. humidity. At no time during 
testing of parts from the vehicle interior may 
the specimen parts exhibit signs of 
condensation.
