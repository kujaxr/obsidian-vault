Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）柴油滤清支架是通过疲劳而开裂的。其原因有可能是：焊接螺母的紧固力丧失导致振动加剧，从而出现失
效。The diesel filter bracket fractured through fatigue. It’s likely that a loss of clamping force at the weld nut was the 
reason for excessive vibration, leading to failure. 
（2）材料组织，强度以及焊接符合要求。The material microstructure, strength and key weld dimensions meet the 
requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）确认安装工艺满足要求。Confirm mounting process meets requirements. 
（2）在试验前检测零件的尺寸。Test the dimension before experiment.

III. 测试结果与分析 Test Results and Analysis  
（1）失效产品 Failed Parts 
      失效产品如图1 所示；支架的5 个区域已完全断开。值得注意的是一个焊接螺母的焊点已断开。装
配的螺母和螺栓未提供用于分析，但是焊接螺母的周围有一些非正常损伤，这表明螺母在测试中发生了转
动。 The failed part is shown in figure 1. The bracket was fractured in 5 positions. It was noticed that weld of the 
weld nuts was broken. This nut and the mounting bolts were not submitted for analysis, however, abnormal 
damage around the weld nut mounting area indicates the nut was able to rotate during the test.

（2）断口分析Fracture Analysis 
     各开裂处断口形貌分别如图2-图6 所示。图中虚线箭头显示裂纹扩展方向；实线箭头显示疲劳台阶。 
     从断口来看，支架的开裂是一种多源的疲劳开裂；断口上未见有预先存在的损伤和缺陷。 
The fracture morphology and features of each crack are shown in Figures 2 to 6. The dashed arrows show 
the direction of crack propagation; solid line arrows show ratchet marks, characteristic of fatigue. 
From examination of the fracture surfaces, the bracket failed through multi-origin fatigue cracking. No 
pre-existing damage or defects were found.
