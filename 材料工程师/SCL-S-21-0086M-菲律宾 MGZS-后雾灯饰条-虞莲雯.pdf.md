Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论：失效件同时存在两种失效模式，镀层鼓泡及轻微腐蚀。失效件的铜层和镍层均远低于额定值。 
建议：供应商应加强批量质量的稳定控制。 
    Conclusion: there are two failure modes, blistering and slight corrosion. The copper layer and nickel layer 
of the failure parts are far lower than the rated value. 
Suggestion: Suppliers should strengthen the stable control of batch quality.
