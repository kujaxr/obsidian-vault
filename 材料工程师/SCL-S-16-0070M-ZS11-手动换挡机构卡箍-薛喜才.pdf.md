Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1）卡箍属于脆性沿晶断裂。The clam was brittle fractured.  
（2）失效件及同批次零件硬度均高于技术要求，材料成分、厚度符合要求。 
Hardness of the failed parts and the same batch is high. Chemical components and thickness meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 隔离同批次零件，对已装车零件进行追踪。Isolate the parts with the same batch and track the assembly parts.  
（2） 加强热处理工艺控制，确保硬度符合技术要求。Control the heat treatment to make sure the hardness meet the 
requirements.
