Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：失效件和抽检零件的材料和力学性能无异常。吊耳存在缺口和分层缺陷，受到异常外力时，在缺陷处
开始断裂，最终扩展到整个截面。 
Conclusions: The material and mechanical property of failure part conform to the drawing requirement. According to 
the macroscopic observation and the material test, it can be seen that the break of lifting lug start from the 
void defect area by an excessive force, and then the crack propagate through the whole cross section quickly. 
建议：优化吊耳制造工艺，避免产生缺口和分层等缺陷；避免吊耳装配时产生异常外力。 
Suggestions：The manufacture process of the lifting lug should be optimized for the quality improvement. The 
excessive force during assembly should be prohibited.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图2 的断口形貌可以看出，在左、右断面上存在明显的光亮区和毛边区，同时在断面中间位置存在断面
台阶，说明断裂经历了两个过程。 
From the crack surface of Fig 2, it can be seen that there are clear sooth area, coarse area and fracture step on the left 
and right crack surface.

图3 分别是失效件和新件的外观，从失效件外观可以看出在裂纹端点存在一缺口缺陷，在缺口的同一水平面
上存在一明显分层面；在新件上也可看到明显的分层面，该缺陷主要是由于制造工艺不好导致。 
Left picture and right picture in Fig 3 are the failure part and new part respectively. A void defect is appeared near the 
end of the side crack. And at the same horizontal plane of the void defect, a clear delamination defect is observed. Also, 
the same delamination defect as that of failure part is found at the new part. The delamination defect is mainly caused by a 
bad manufacture process.

小结：通过吊耳的材料分析和断口分析可以判定，吊耳的断裂模式是，吊耳存在缺口和分层缺陷，在受到异
常外力时，在缺陷处开始断裂，最终扩展到整个截面。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the lifting lug, it can be seen that the 
crack of lifting lug start from the void defect area by an excessive force, and then the crack propagate through 
the whole cross section quickly.
