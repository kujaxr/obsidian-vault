Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该高压EGR进气管在高应力区发生疲劳开裂，断裂面无明显缺陷。 
The high-pressure EGR inlet pipe experienced fatigue cracks in the high stress zone, with no obvious defects on the 
fracture surface. 
（2）材料化学成分合格，硬度正常。 
Chemical composition of material met the requirement, hardness was normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）建议优化结构，降低该处振动和受力。 
Optimize the structure, reduce the vibration and stress.
