Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）张紧器壳体是由于局部应力过大而发生疲劳开裂的。The shell of the tensioner was broken by fatigue because of 
a big stress at the local. 
（2）材料的成分符合要求；硬度，组织和孔隙正常。The chemical components meet the requirement; the hardness，
microstructure and porosity are normal. 
  
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核壳体的载荷；优化A 面的凸台分布，同时增加面积，以降低局部应力。Check the load; optimize the 
distribution of the boss and increase the area for reducing the local stress.

The macrofracture surface was showed as fig. 4. A large of area close to the surface B was worn and the area 
circled by dotted line was seriously worn; the main crack originated from the junction region between the boss and 
the large surface (see fig. 5) and there were several ratchet marks; Fig. 6 showed there were beach marks at the 
local. The characters showed it was broken by multisource fatigue.
