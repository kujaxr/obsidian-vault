Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）变形箱悬置的裂纹是由于粘模引起的。The crack in the transmission mount bracket was caused by mold sticking. 
（2）材料的成分和硬度符合要求，组织正常。The composition and hardness of the material meet the requirements, and 
the microstructure is normal. 
（3）同批次未粘模零件的孔隙率符合要求。The porosity of the same batch of non-stick parts meets the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）采取措施防止粘模（如增加脱模剂的喷涂量），同时对生产的零件进行100%探伤和外观检查。Measures should 
be taken to prevent die sticking (such as increasing the amount of spraying agent), and 100% flaw detection and 
appearance inspection should be carried out on the parts produced. 
（2）对已装车零件进行100%检查。100% checking should be carried out for the parts assembled in the whole cars.
