Foreword:
Conversion to chromium (IV)-free surface protection types requires greater coating thickness val-
ues than is the case for surface protection containing chromium (IV). In order to comply with the
thread limit dimensions according to VW 116 11, the external thread shall be manufactured in tol-
erance zone position “e” according to this standard.
1 
Scope
The thread limit dimensions in Tables 1 and 2 according to this standard apply to bright threads
prior to application of a chromium (IV)-free surface coating, e.g. zinc flake coatings according to TL
233/TL 245, duplex coatings according to TL 196, zinc-nickel coatings according to TL 244 or zinc
coatings according to TL 217.
See VW 116 11 for limit dimensions after coating.
Tolerance class 6G according to VW 116 24 shall be used for internal threads.
2 
Designation, nominal dimensions and limit dimensions
Designation of an M 10 external thread according to this standard: M10 - 6e
See Figure 1 for nominal dimensions.
