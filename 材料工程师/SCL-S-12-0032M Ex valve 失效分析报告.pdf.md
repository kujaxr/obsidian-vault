Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1.气门盘锥因冲蚀而出现失效。 无法敲定基本根源。The valve has failed through erosion of valve seat material.  The 
root cause cannot be found. 
2. 大量的钙沉积在气门头和盘锥；其起源不清楚。 Significant calcium deposit was found on the valve head and seat, 
the origin of this is not known.  
3. 气门杆上有一个划痕；由于处于较高的部位，在运行过程中不会干涉气门的密封。划痕起源不明确。A scratch 
on the valve stem is too high to interfere with the valve seal during running. The origin of this is not known.  
4. 气门头及盘锥材料合格。The valve and seat materials met the requirements. 
 建议Suggestions： 
1. 为了进一步调查，受影响的缸盖应返回到实验室，连同油样。In order to investigate further, the affected cylinder 
head should be returned to the laboratory, together with an oil sample.   
2. 请不要对提交到实验室故障组件进行清理，因为这有可能消除重要的证据；只是去除任何水是必要的。 
Please do not clean failed components before submission to laboratory, as this can remove important evidence. Only 
removal of any water is necessary.

用EDX 来分析黄色沉积物，发现含有大量的钙和氧，并含有一定量的硫，磷，锌（图3 和4）。这些元素在气门
材料中不是必要的元素。 钙盐的沉积物是含有大量添加剂的石油燃烧产生的结果。  
EDX analysis of the yellow deposit, showed a substance high in Calcium and Oxygen, with significant amounts of 
Sulphur, Phosphorous and Zinc (figures 3 & 4). None of these elements are prevalent in the valve alloy material. The 
calcium salt deposit was generated from combustion of oil containing high quantities of oil additives.

气门杆有一个显着的划痕（图6）。根据工程部门提供得尺寸,最大的升程，划痕和气门的油封之间仍然应该有>5
毫米的。然而，有可能在装配时，要么划伤损坏的印章，或外力损坏气门和密封在同一时间。  
气门油封的磨损可能导致原油泄漏，并造成气门上的碳积聚。  
The valve stem has a significant scratch (figure 6).  According to the dimensions provided by engineering, at maximum 
lift, there should still be >5mm between the scratch and the valve seal.  However, it’s possible that either the scratch 
damaged the seal during initial assembly, or that an external force damaged both the valve and the seal at the same time.  
Valve seal wear could lead to oil leakage, and cause carbon build-up on the valve.
