Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 该前缓冲梁为返工件：失效部位先点焊、后经过弧焊补焊、再打磨处理；且上、下板匹配间隙较大。  
The front buffer beam is an abnormal part: First spot welding, then welding repair and polishing in the failure location 
and the matching gap is big between the above and low plate. 
 
2. 正常焊点中存在密集型气孔，长度约1.5mm，超过标准要求值。 
The dense pores are observed in normal welding and the length is about 1.5mm, more than the standard requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 调查该零件的来源。Investigate the resource of the part.  
2. 加强焊接质量监控，避免气孔。Strengthen the welding quality kontrolling to avoid the porosity.
