Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件外力导致断裂。 
The failure part fractured by external force. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   The part’s material conformed to the requirements. 
3. 零件断裂位置存在注塑孔洞，但不是失效的主要原因。 
   There is injection hole in the fracture position, but it was not the main reason of failure.   
建议Suggestions：1. 失效位置根部添加倒角以提高结构强度。 
                   Add R-angle in failure position to improve the strength of structure. 
                2. 供应商优化结构设计及调整注塑工艺以消除注塑孔洞。 
                   Supplier optimizes the structure design and improves the injection molding process to eliminate 
the holes.
