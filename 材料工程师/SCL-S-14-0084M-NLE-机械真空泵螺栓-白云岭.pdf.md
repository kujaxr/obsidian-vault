Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属过载失效。Failure for the parts due to overload. 
2、 现场装配扭矩大于PAM 要求。Site assembly torque greater than the PAM requirements. 
3、 零件的材料性能符合图纸技术要求。Material properties meet the technical requirements of the drawings. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查装配过程质量。Check the quality of the assembly process. 
2、校核装配工艺的适宜性。Check the suitability of the assembly process.
