4 步骤 
4  Procedure 
4.1 测量的胶管应为25 mm 长，最大间隔为
0.025 mm。 
4.1  Measure the outside diameter of the hose 
to the nearest 0.025 mm at intervals of 25 mm 
along the length. 
4.2 测试设备应配备测试温度能提升到（95±
2）℃和能向胶管或是管件传送200 kN/m2 压力
的装置。并维持以上这些条件30 min。 
 
4.2  Raise the temperature of the rig to (95± 
2) 
 and introduce a pressure of 200 kN/m
℃
2 to 
the hose or tube. Maintain these conditions for 
30 min. 
4.3 在测试的完成后并仍然在有压力条件的时
间重新测量胶管的外径。 
4.3  On completion of the test re-measure the 
outside diameter while still under pressure. 
5 结果的计算 
阅读值的差异性应使用百分比的数据来表
示。 
5  Calculation of results 
The difference in reading shall be ex-
pressed in terms of percentage.
