结论与建议 Conclusions  
 传动轴是过载断裂；没有疲劳迹象，损伤或严重的材料缺陷；材料性能符合要求。 The driveshafts have failed 
through overload.  There were no signs of fatigue, damage or significant material defects.  The material properties met the requirements. 
 裂纹起始位置与装配中固定螺母的敲击相一致；装配螺母所产生的过载应力以及敲击所产生的冲击应力导致了
产品的失效。The Fracture origins coincide with the impact made to fix the driveshaft nut.  It is therefore believed that the over-stressing of 
the shaft during torque tightening, and additional stressing due to the impact is the cause of the failures. 
 在装配过程中所产生的夹紧力超过了一些传动轴的材料屈服强度； 然而工程部门并不清楚夹紧力要求值和实
际值。During assembly, the yield strength of some driveshafts is passed.  However neither the required nor actual clamping load values are 
known by Engineering department. 
 对比ZP11 和IP21，差异主要体现在：传动轴原材料，装配的工厂和润滑类型（来自不同供应商的油）。Comparison 
with ZP11 and IP21 highlighted differences in driveshaft material, assembly location and lubrication type (oils from different suppliers).  
建议 Suggestions 
 请轴承供应商提供夹紧力要求。Request required clamping load from bearing supplier  
 做试验确定实际的夹紧力。Perform torque testing to establish the actual clamping load 
 跟IP21/22 及ZP11，对比材料。Compare materials of IP21/22, ZP11 with AP11. 
 在装配过程中控制润滑条件。Control lubrication conditions during assembly

传动轴断裂于轮毂末端花键区（图1）；其位置与过去的失效案例相同。在该区域，感应淬火的区域越来越大，因
此传动轴的延展性也在相应地降低(见图2)。 
The driveshafts had fractured through the splined area of the hub end (figure 1).  The same position as in previous cases.  At this point the 
induction hardening region is becoming larger and the ductility of the shaft is therefore reduced (figure 2). 
 
通过断面的人字形花样，可以确定裂纹起始位置；断裂区域没有变形。这两点特征表明该断裂是一种过载的脆性
断裂。断裂的起源与装配操作中产生的敲击相一致；该操作防止螺母松脱（见图3）。 
The fracture origins were located by observing chevron marks on the fracture surface, and lack of deformation at the fracture origin.  These are 
both very typical signs of brittle overload fracture. In all cases, the fracture origin coincided with the impact made during vehicle assembly, to 
prevent the nut loosening (figure 3)..

装配调查  Assembly Investigation 
南京工厂对AP11 传动轴和轮毂进行组装。 IP21/22 和ZP11 的设计虽然是相似，但是在这些车辆上没有发现故
障。 IP21/22 和ZP11 组装在临港工厂的相同生产线上进行。 
The AP11 driveshafts are assembled into the hub in Nanjing Plant.  Although the designs for IP21/22 and ZP11 are similar, no failures have been 
found on these vehicles.  IP21/22 and ZP11 are assembled in Lingang plant (on the same production line). 
 
设计和装配对比Design and assembly comparison - AP11, ZP11 and IP21/22  
表2 显示了3 种车型传动轴组件和装配的主要特点。红色标识了装配的差异。对比分析如下： 
Table 2 shows the main features of the components and assembly of three vehicles’ driveshaft. Differences 
between the assemblies are given in red.  The following important comparisons can be made. 
1. 材料  AP11 驱动轴的原料是55 号（GB/T699）。在正火状态下，材料最小的拉伸强度是645MPa；ZP11 和
IP21 传动轴原材料是UC1, 拉伸强度是GKN 的秘密。 
Material.  The raw material of the AP11 driveshaft is #55 to GB/T699.  In normalized condition, the minimum tensile 
strength is 645MPa .  ZP11 and IP21 driveshafts are made from UC1, tensile strength is GKN secret. 
硬度  失效件（AP11）的芯部硬度测定值是214 至236 HB。需要与IP21 和ZP11 的硬度进行对比。  
Hardness. Core hardness of failed parts (AP11) were measured between 214 & 236 HB, samples from ZP11 and IP21 must be compared. 
2. IP21 传动轴的颈部直径比AP11 和ZP11 稍大。The neck diameter of IP21 shaft is slightly larger than AP11 and ZP11. 
3. AP11 在花键区的最小直径比IP21 的约小3 毫米，比ZP11 的约小0.5mm。The AP11 minimum diameter in the spline 
area is nearly 3mm smaller than IP21, and 0.5mm smaller than ZP11. 
4. 螺母与AP11 法兰接触区域的内径小于ZP11 和IP21。这意味着，螺母和法兰的接触面积更大，因此比ZP11
＆IP21 摩擦应力更高，所取得夹紧载荷更低。 The nut face diameter on AP11 flange is smaller than ZP11 and IP21.  This 
means that the contact area between nut and hub is bigger, therefore friction should be higher, and acheived clamping load lower than ZP11 
& IP21. 
内径的影响效果很小，但是这与AP11 传动轴失效现象相反。 Although the effect is small at the inner diameter, this is 
contrary to the experience of failures on AP11. 
5. 在某些情况下，螺母的装配速度会影响摩擦，从而影响夹紧力，但是这两个机器之间的速度差异并不显著。In 
some cases, the turning speed can influence the friction and therefore the achieved clamping load, however the difference in speed between 
these two machines is not significant.

在紧固过程中，应用扭矩为352Nm。当装配扭矩在150Nm 和352Nm 之间时，对其角度进行监控。 从9 月份的
一些监控结果（见图8）来看，当扭矩在150 至352Nm 之间，螺母正常的旋转角度只有40-60°，有时高达100 °。
偶尔，角度可以达到150 °，甚至200 °。这会导致材料发生屈服。 
During tightening, the torque is applied up to 352Nm.  Between 150Nm and 352, the angle is monitored.  Some results from september are 
shown in figure 8.  From this we can see that between 150 and 352Nm, the nut can normally be turned only 40-60°, sometimes up to 100°.  
Occasionally, a nut can be turned through 150° or even 200° as seen below.  This is a clear sign of material yielding.
