Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
 
1. 发动机悬置首先在底座处发生疲劳开裂失效，然后受力异常，引起罩盖过载断裂；裂纹源处未见明显铸造
缺陷和材料损伤。At first, the base of the engine mount cracked by fatigue, which made the force abnormal and 
the cover broken by overload; no obvious casting defects and material damage were found at the crack tip. 
2. 底座的成分、硬度符合要求；金相组织及孔隙率正常；Chemical components and hardness met the technical 
requirements. Microstructure and porosity were normal. 
3. 撞块的材料符合技术要求。The material of the block meets the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 校核和验证优化后车身和悬置的结构强度。Check and validate the body and mount structure after optimizing.  
2. 请工程评估已装车零件（50 辆）的风险。Please evaluate the risks of the vehicles (number: 50).
