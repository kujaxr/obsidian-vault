Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件原已受力损伤，装入总成后受力失效。 
The part has been damaged by external force, and fractured after loading into assembly.

The performance of the parts material conformed to requirement.

Supplier checks the assembly & logistics process.
