Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  横梁焊趾上裂纹为多源疲劳裂纹，裂纹起源于焊缝起弧区，且位于零件的高应力区。 
The welding side crack belongs to several source fatigue crack and the crack source was in the welding arc with high 
stress area. 
2.  横梁成分满足要求，硬度转换而来的强度正常。 
Chemical components meet the requirement. Strength converted from hardness was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化焊缝布置或者提高横梁强度。 
    Optimise the welding disrobution or improve strength of the crossbeam.
