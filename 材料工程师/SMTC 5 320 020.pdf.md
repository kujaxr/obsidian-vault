drive ancillary equipment.

4.4 取样要求Requirement of the specimen 
试验样品从零件上直接制取进行试验，其中
皮带宽度方向为纵向（即纤维方向），皮带长度
方向为横向（即垂直纤维方向）。拉伸试样推荐
使用ISO 37 3型和4型样件。只有在成品尺寸无
法满足取样要求的情况下才使用标准试片进行
测试，试片厚度为2mm。 
    
 
The standard specimen is cut from the finished 
part, in which the belt width direction is the 
longitudinal (fiber direction), the belt length 
direction is horizontal (perpendicular to fiber 
direction). The type 3 and type 4 specimens of 
ISO 37 is recommended for the tensile test. 
The test sheet of 2mm thick is only used when 
the dimension of the part can not meet the 
specimen preparation. 
 
4.5 状态调节Condition 
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291 23 ℃，
50 %RH的标准气候下至少放置24h。 
   
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 ℃，50 %RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 24h before test.

5.1 硬度测试Hardness Test 
Shore A 硬度测试时间为3s。 
 
   
The test period of shore hardness is 3s, if there 
is no special requirement.

式烘箱内，老化后取出样条在23℃下存放30min
后进行测试。 
 
  
 
The specimens are aged in a mechanical 
circulation oven according to ISO 188. 
Measurements 30 min after removal.

4.5  低温性能Behavior in cold air 
    试样长度为220 mm 在低温中存放规定时间
后立即在Ф= 50 mm 的圆轴上进行弯曲。弯曲需
以背胶向内和背胶向外两种方式进行弯曲。 
 
 
 
The specimen is 220 mm long and aged in 
cold air. Then bend the belt over a mandrel Ф
= 50 mm with the back side inward and 
outward respectively. 
4.6  耐臭氧老化Ozone resistance 
 
 
    臭氧测试条件为：臭氧浓度：2ppm，测试温
度：25℃，相对空气湿度：60%。测试时间为46h。
将皮带带齿向内绕在直径为50 mm 的圆柱上。 
 
 
The test condition is as follow: the O3 
concentration is 2ppm, the test temperature is 
25℃ and the humidity is 60%. The part is 
placed around a Φ50 mm cylinder with the 
teeth facing inward.
