Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1 失效件材料性能满足标准对材料供货态要求。 
      2 失效雨刮端部异常受损，受作用力导致向内撕裂。 
Conclusions: 1. the material properties of the failure part can meet the requirements of the as-received condition. 
2. the end of the wiper blades got impaired abnormally, and was torn to the interior by force. 
 
建议： 1 确保零件不受异常外力损伤。        
Suggestions:  1.make sure the wiper don’t be damaged by abnormally external force.
