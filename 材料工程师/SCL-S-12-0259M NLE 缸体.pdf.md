Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）发动机是由于疲劳而开裂的；裂纹源产生于形状变化应力集中的区域。The engine is cracked through fatigue; 
the crack sources are at the region with concentrated stress because of changing shape.  
（2）应力过大是导致裂纹产生的主要原因；局部有严重的疏松，对疲劳不利，但不是导致失效的直接原因。The 
big stress is the main reason causing the crack generation; the local heavy porosity brought negative effect on the fatigue, 
but this is not the direct reason. 
（3）材料的成分和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical compositions and hardness meet the requirement; the  
Microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化设计，降低开裂处的应力。Optimize the design and reduce the stress at the crack region. 
（2）在图纸中，添加开裂处的疏松要求。Add the porosity requirement to the drawing. 
（3）优化工艺，降低局部疏松，并在检验中，关注此部位的铸造质量。Optimize the casting process, reduce the local 
porosity and during check, pay attention to the casting quality of the crack region.

断口分析 Fracture Analysis 
     断口1 和断口2 的宏观形貌如图2 和图3 所示。从断口上可以看出反应裂纹扩展方向（箭头所示）的贝壳

疲劳开裂的能力。
