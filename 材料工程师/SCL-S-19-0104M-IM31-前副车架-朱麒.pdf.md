Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1．裂纹属于疲劳开裂。The crack is belong to fatigue crack. 
2．失效区域材料减薄率为21.78%（要求≤18%），偏高。The disabled area’s material thickness decrease is 21.78% (standard≤18%)，too high. 
3．失效区域焊缝熔深为0.180mm，1.119mm（要求≥0.276mm），其一侧熔深偏低；焊高为2.728mm（要求≥1.61mm），
符合要求。The welding depth is 0.180mm，1.119mm (standard≥0.276mm), the depth is too small, the welding height is 2.728mm(standard≥1.61mm), it meet the 
standard. 
4．材料化学成分符合要求，硬度转化抗拉强度符合要求。 
The chemic component and the pull intensity transform with rigidity of material are meet the standard. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
结合失效区域实际材料厚度进行CAE 应力分析。Please analysis CAE of the part’s area of crack with the actual thickness. 
2. 
改进焊接工艺，保证焊接质量。Advance the welding technique, ensure the welding quality.
