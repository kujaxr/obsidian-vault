Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）装配螺栓的松动导致涡杆承受较大的载荷而出现过载断裂。The bolt was loose, which caused the whorl shaft to 
break because of overload. 
（2）涡杆的组织和硬度正常。The microstructure and hardness of the whorl shaft are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）供应商查找螺栓松动的原因，并采取相应的避免措施。The supplier should find out the loosing reason and take 
the preventive means.
