Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，失效件的材料无异常，硬度偏低，尺寸偏大。 
2. 装配过程中O 型圈受到挤压，导致O 型圈被撕裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement, but the hardness of 
failure part is lower and the diameter of failure part is bigger compared with that of the used part. According 
to the macroscopic observation and the material test, it can be seen that the O ring was impressed during 
assembly and finally the O ring was broken. 
建议：检查O 型圈批量尺寸的稳定性；装配时O 型圈预安装到位；保证O 型圈硬度。 
Suggestions：The dimensional stability of O ring should be inspected. The O ring should be pre-installed in place 
during assembly. The hardness of O ring should be guaranteed.

小结：经测试，失效件和使用后零件的材料为EPDM，材料符合设计要求。失效件硬度与使用后零件相比偏
低，失效件和使用后零件的直径偏大。

hardness of the failure part is lower compared with that of the used part. The diameters of the failure part and 
used part are little bigger than the requirement.
