1 范围Scope 
 
 
本标准规定了用于添加不同玻纤含量的阻燃
级PA6制成的汽车成品件的材料要求。 
    本标准适用于阻燃级PA6制成的汽车成品件的
材料。 
 
This standard defines the material requirements 
for finished parts for automobiles that are made 
from PA6 with different levels of glass fiber 
content and flame retardant. 
This standard applies to the material require-
ments for finished parts for automobiles that are 
made from PA6.

要求 Requirements 
3.1 样品与预处理要求Sampling and Conditioning requirements 
3.1.1 样品要求Sampling requirements 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成品上
取样，并记录取样位置。若遇到无法从成品上取样
时，其样品制备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室工程
师协商确定。 
 
The test specimens stipulated in this Standard 
should be sampled from finished parts, and 
the sampling method/area must be recorded 
and report. When the finished part does not 
meet the sampling requirement, the specimen 
preparation method should be determined 
through the negotiation with SMPV engineers.  
3.1.2 预处理Conditioning 
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准气候条
件23℃,50 %RH下至少放置48 h。断裂应力，冲击
韧性，缺口冲击需用干态（105℃直至恒重）进行
试验。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 48h before test. Breaking stress, Impact 
resistance, Notched impact strength need test 
in dry(105℃ until a constant weight) state.

3.2  特性Feature 
3.2.1  在加强筋、增强区域和管接头处不允许存在
凹痕，填充玻纤的材料，其表面在触觉或视觉上都
不应出现瑕疵。 
 
3.2.1 Sink marks at the base of ribs, rein-
forcements and pipe unions are not permitted. 
Materials containing glass fibers must exhibit a 
surface that is free of imperfections both to the 
eye and the touch. 
3.2.2  成品件中的填充物分布应均匀，在成品件的
不同部位所取的三个试样中的灰分含量之差不应
超过1.0 %。 
 
3.2.2 The distribution of additives throughout 
the component must be sufficiently uniform so 
that three specimens taken from different 
points on a finished part do not differ in ash 
content by more than 1.0 %. 
3.3 要求 Requirements 
3.3.1  外观Appearance 
    除非有明确定义并得到上汽乘用车工程师的
认可，成品件表面与内部不允许存在加工缺陷，如
熔接痕、缩孔、裂纹及类似缺陷。 
 
Unless defined and approved by the SMPV 
engineers, the surface and interior of the fin-
ished parts shall be free of flaws and pro-
cessing defects, such as flow lines, voids, 
cracks or similar faults. 
3.3.2 颜色Color 
    按图纸，并符合 SMTC 5 400 002 的要求。 
 
According to SMTC 5 400 002 and drawing. 
3.3.3 禁用/限用材料、避免使用的危险品 Avoidance of hazardous substances 
必须符合 SMTC 5 500 001 及SMTC 5 500 
002 的要求。 
 
Must comply to SMTC 5 500 001 and SMTC 5 
500 002. 
3.3.4 材料鉴定 Material identification 
 
 
零件材料应符合图纸要求，使用红外光谱法
（GB/T 6040）与DSC法（ISO 11357-3）测定。 
如无其他特殊要求，成品件材料熔点要求不低
于200℃。 
 
The part material shall conform to the drawing, 
and test using Infrared spectrometric （GB/T 
6040） and DSC method （ISO 11357-3）. 
The melting point of the finished parts, unless 
otherwise defined, shall be no less than 200℃. 
3.3.5 密度 Density 
    依据图纸要求或按实际产品批量生产前二十
件的密度平均值确定，公差范围±0.02 g/cm3，测
试方法依据ISO 1183-1。 
 
According to drawing or the density should be 
determined by the average of the first twenty 
PPAP finished products, Tolerance range is 
±0.02 g/cm3. The test method is according to 
ISO 1183-1.   
3.3.6 灼烧灰分 Ash content 
依据图纸要求或按实际产品批量生产前二十
件的灼烧灰分平均值确定，公差范围±3%，测试方
法依据 ISO 3451-1。 
 
According to drawing, glass fiber content 
should be determined by the average of the 
first twenty PPAP finished products, Tolerance 
range is ±3%. The test method is according to 
ISO 3451-1.

3.3.7 阻燃性能 Flame-retardant behaviour 
依据图纸要求或试验大纲，材料应符合UL94
规定的垂直燃烧 V-0 等级，且阻燃厚度不大于应
用零件的最小实际厚度。所使用的阻燃剂体系应对
零件的实际应用没有不利影响，并获得上汽乘用车
工程师的认可。 
 
According to drawing or ADV plan, the material 
shall meet the vertical burning performance V-0 
grade according to UL 94, and the flame rating 
thickness shall not be thicker than the minimal 
thickness of the actual part. The flame retardant 
system used in the material should not affect the 
use of the finished part, and must be approved 
by the SMPV engineer.

3.3.9 体积电阻率 Volume resistance  
 
 
以标准试片进行测试，需用稳定的直流电压
源，测试电压为1000V，公差范围±10%，测试方
法依据 GB/T 1410。 
 
To test with standard specimens, a stable DC 
voltage source is needed. The test voltage is 
1000V and the tolerance range is ±10%. The 
test method is according to GB/T 1410. 
3.3.10 介电强度 Electrical Strength 
 
 
以标准试片进行测试，需用稳定的直流电压
源，测试电压为1000V，公差范围±10%，测试方
法依据 GB/T 1408.1。 
 
To test with standard specimens, a stable DC 
voltage source is needed. The test voltage is 
1000V and the tolerance range is ±10%. The 
test method is according to GB/T 1408.1. 
3.3.11 耐老化特性 Aging resistance 
 
 
依据图纸要求，使用成品件在通风式烘箱中进
行实验，推荐实验温度为150℃，试验周期依据不
同的零件推荐值如表2： 
1）实验完成冷却至23℃后，零件不允许有脆化、
粉化、开裂等现象； 
2）老化试验后，材料拉伸强度保持率 ≥ 50%。 
 
According to drawing, finished parts shall be 
aged at 150°C in an air-circulated oven, and the 
test period of different parts is recommended 
according to Table 5: 
 
1）After the test parts are not allowed embrit-
tlement to cool down to 23°C;  
2）The tensile strength property of material after 
aging must above 50% of the initial tensile 
strength value.

3.3.12  耐候性能 Weathering performance 
依据SMTC 5 400 005，使用成品件进行试验。
试验周期依据图纸或试验大纲确定。在试验中及试
验后冷却至室温时，零件不允许有变形、开裂等影
响功能性的变化。 
 
The finished products are tested according to 
SMTC 5 400 005. The number of test period is 
determined by drawing or ADV Plan. No detri-
mental effects such as deformation or crack are 
allowed on the part both during the test and af-
ter the test. 
3.3.13  高温性能 Elevated-temperature behavior 
依据图纸或试验大纲要求，使用成品在通风式
烘箱中进行测试。推荐试验条件为90℃，240h，或
依据实际工况协商确定。在试验中及试验后冷却至
室温时，零件不允许有变形、开裂等影响功能性的
变化，不允许有阻燃剂析出。 
 
According to drawing or ADV plan, the finished 
part shall be tested in an air-circulated oven. 
The recommended test condition is 90°C, 240h, 
or determined on agreement. No detrimental 
effects such as deformation, crack and the 
separation out of the flame retardant, are al-
lowed on the part both during the test and after 
the test. 
3.3.14 低温性能 Low temperature behaviour 
依据图纸或试验大纲要求，使用成品在低温箱
中进行测试。试验温度若无明确规定，则使用
-40℃。试验周期：24h。试验过程中及结束后，成
品件不允许有变形、开裂等影响功能性的变化，不
允许有阻燃剂析出。 
 
According to drawing or ADV plan, the finished 
part shall be tested in a cold chamber. The 
recommended test condition is -40°C, 24h, un-
less otherwise defined. No detrimental effects 
such as deformation, crack and the separation 
out of the flame retardant, are allowed on the 
part both during the test and after the test.
