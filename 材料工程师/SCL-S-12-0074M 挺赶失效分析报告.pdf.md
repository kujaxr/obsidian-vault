Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1）这两个挺杆失败，由于污染粒子，它堵塞了液压挺杆功能。这些粒子必须避免在发动机供油系统。Both tappets 
failed due to contamination by particles, which blocked the hydraulic function of the tappet.  Such particles must be 
avoided in the engine oil system. 
2）挺杆＃2 发现的大粒子是纯粹的低碳钢，而不是类似挺杆自己的材料。The large particle found in tappet #2 was 
unalloyed low carbon steel, not similar to the tappet’s own material. 
3）封锁挺杆＃1 的颗粒没有被发现。有可能的发动机装配时引入了这些被腐蚀的钢或铁颗粒跟另一个组件。The 
particles which blocked tappet #1 were not found.  It’s possible these were corroded steel or iron particles which were 
introduced with another component during engine manufacture. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1) 
控制发动机部件的清性，以防止这种失败。Control cleanliness of engine components to prevent this failure.  
2) 
不要把锈蚀部件组装成发动机Do not assemble any corroded components into the engine.

挺杆＃1 Tappet #1 
挺杆＃1 不能轻易拆开。拆开之后，壳内表面上看到了一些严重划痕；这是硬质颗粒卡在漏隙处造
成的（图5）。它阻碍了活塞的正常移动，导致失效。附近也有一些来自颗粒的锈斑，而不是来自
挺杆材料。由于机油保护发动机零部件在工作过程中不受腐蚀，生锈的粒子可能来自另一个发动机
部件，在装配前进入发动机的。 
Tappet #1 could not be easily dismantled.  After opening, some heavy scratches were seen on the inside 
surface of the housing, which were caused by hard particles becoming stuck in the leakage gap (figure 5). 
This prevented the piston from moving properly, leading to the failure.  There is also some rust nearby, 
which is from the particles (not from the tappet material).  Since the engine oil protects the engine parts 
from corrosion during running, the rusty particle probably came from another engine component, before it 
was assembled into the engine.

挺杆＃2  Tappet #2  
一个大型粒子（图6）卡在液压挺杆＃2 球阀处。根据EDX 检测结果结果，其化学成分是一个低
碳低合金钢材料。显微组织是铁素体与碳化物，硬度为208HV0.3（拉伸强度〜640MPa），看图7
和图8。The hydraulic ball valve of Tappet #2 was blocked by a large particle (figure 6). The chemical 
composition was found by EDX to be a low carbon, low alloy steel material.  The microstructure was 
ferrite with carbides, and hardness was measured at 208HV0.3 (tensile strength ~ 640MPa).  See figures 
7 & 8.
