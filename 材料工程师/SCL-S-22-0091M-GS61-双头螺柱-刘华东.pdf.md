Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效螺柱是疲劳断裂，疲劳的原因与试验过程中异常振动有关。The failure stud was fatigue fracture, and the reason 
was related to abnormal vibration during the test.  
2、失效螺柱化学成分、硬度和金相符合技术要求。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure of failure 
stud meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、排查试验过程中异常振动的原因。Check the reason of abnormal vibration during the test.
