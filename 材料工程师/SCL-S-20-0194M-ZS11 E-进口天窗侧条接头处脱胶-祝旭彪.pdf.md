Ⅰ.任务描述 Task description 
售后质量分析科反馈ZS11 E 印度KD 车身的天窗密封条在安装接头处有9 例脱胶案例，总共装车500 件，在
国内由郑州工厂安装到达印度工厂，且脱胶均发生在接头位置，失效零件具体信息如下： 
The after-sales SQE reported that there were 9 cases of degumming at the installation joint of Sealer outer of ZS11E KD,a total of 500 
pieces ,which were installed from Zhengzhou factory to India factory in China, and the degumming occurred at the joint position. The specific 
information of the failed parts is as follows page: 
Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 抽检的德国美德天窗外密封条胶带剥离力满足要求，使用活化剂后的粘结强度有明显增强； 
1．The results show that the peeling force of the Sealer outer meets the requirements, and the adhesive strength is obviously enhanced after 
using activator. 
2、模拟辊轮对密封条施加拉伸、挤压力安装情况，经环境交变后端部出现不同程度的脱胶现象；而无拉伸挤压
力安装的零件贴合状态良好； 
2．Simulation of the installation of the roller to the Sealer outer with tension and extrusion force shows that there are different degrees of 
degumming phenomenon in the joint area after environmental alternation aging, while the parts installed without tension and extrusion force 
fit well. 
3、油漆面使用活化剂后，模拟辊轮对密封条施加拉伸力安装，经环境交变老化后端部同样出现部分脱胶现象； 
3．After the activator was used on the paint surface, the tension force was applied to the sealing strip of the simulated roller, and the joint was 
partially degummed after the environmental alternating aging. 
4、模拟辊轮对密封条施加拉伸力安装，通过压缩工装模拟天窗安装固定，同等老化条件下端头无脱胶现象，验
证试验继续进行中。 
4. Simulation of the installation of the roller to the Sealer outer with tension force, Through the compression tooling to simulate the 
installation and fixation of the skylight .There is no degumming phenomenon in the joint area under the same aging conditions, and the 
verification test is continuing 
建议Conclusions： 
1、 基于目前郑州工厂安装密封条后，在无固定情况下较长时间经历高温高湿和海上运输，存在脱胶风险，建
议密封条移到印度安装，在较短时间内完成天窗安装和固定，防止脱胶。 
1．Based on the fact that after the sealing strip is installed in Zhengzhou factory, there is a risk of degumming due to the long-term experience 
of high temperature, high humidity and sea transportation. It is suggested that the sealing strip should be moved to India for installation, and 
the skylight installation and fixation should be completed in a short time to prevent degumming.
