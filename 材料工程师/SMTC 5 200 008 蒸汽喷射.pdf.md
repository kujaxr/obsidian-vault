1 范围 
本标准规定了使用蒸汽喷射测定油漆涂层附着力
的方法。 
本标准适用于测定金属和非金属材料表面油漆涂
层的附着力。 
 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies a method to determine 
paint adhesion with steam jet. 
 
This standard is adopted to test paint adhesion on 
metallic and non-metallic materials.

3 试验方法 
 
3 
Test Method 
3.1  试验设备 
 
3.1  Devices and auxiliary equipment 
设备与附件如下： 
a） 压力冲水设备(如Karcher与Walter产品)； 
b） 喷嘴，型号EG 2505； 
c） 刀具； 
d） 硬质聚苯乙烯泡沫。 
 
Devices and auxiliary equipment are described as 
follows: 
a)  Pressure washer (e.g., products made by 
Kärcher or Walter);  
b)  Nozzle, EG 2505; 
d)  Cutter knife; 
e)  Polystyrene rigid foam. 
 
3.2  样品预处理 
 
3.2  Conditioning of specimens 
3.2.1  对塑料基材涂装零件：（18～28）°C 室温
中预置168h 或在60°C 中存放48 h。蒸汽喷射
试验前样品在（18～28）°C 室温下冷却2h；对
于环境气候存放（例如，根据IS0 6270-2CH 的耐
冷凝水气候试验）后的蒸汽喷射试验，样品需在
（18～28）°C 室温下进行24h 气候适应性处理。 
 
3.2.1  For painted plastic parts: specimens shall 
be pre-conditioned for 168h at room temperature 
with narrow air temperature range (18 °C to 28 °C) 
or 48 h at 60 °C. The specimens should be cooled 
down at room temperature (18 °C to 28 °C) for 2h 
before test. As for environment resistant aging 
(eg, constant-humidity condensation atmosphere 
test according to ISO 6270-2CH), 24h of acclima-
tization at room temperature (18 °C to 28 °C)  
before test must be ensured. 
 
3.2.2  对金属基材涂装零件：（18～28）°C室温
中预置24h。对于环境气候存放（例如，根据IS0 
6270-2CH的耐冷凝水气候试验）后的蒸汽喷射试
验，样品需在（18～28）°C室温下进行2h气候适
应性处理。 
 
3.2.2  For painted metallic parts: specimens shall 
be pre-conditioned for 24h at room temperature 
with narrow air temperature range (18 °C to 
28 °C). As for environment resistant aging (eg, 
constant-humidity condensation atmosphere test 
according to ISO 6270-2CH), 2h of acclimatization 
at room temperature (18 °C to 28 °C)  before test 
must be ensured.

3.3  试验参数 
 
3.3  Test parameters 
试验参数如下： 
a）划线类型：十字划线（两划线相互垂直）； 
b) 冲击部位：划线处； 
c) 喷射角度（喷出扇面与划线所在平面所呈角
度）：45°； 
d）喷嘴至样件距离：150mm； 
e）水温：70℃（在容器中测量） 
f）压力：9 MPa ±0.5 MPa（流速10.5 L/min） 
g）冲击时间：每条划线20s。 
 
Test parameters are described as follows: 
a)  Type of scribe mark: cross cut (2 scribes 
are vertical from each other); 
b)  Jet location: Scribe marks 
c)  Jet angle (angle of the jet fan and scribe 
surface): 45°; 
d)  Distance from nozzle to specimen: 
150mm; 
e)  Temperature: 70℃ (measured in vessel) 
f)  Pressure: 9 MPa ±0.5 MPa (flow rate 10.5 
L/min) 
g）Test duration: 20 s per scribe mark 
 
3.4  喷射效果的验证 
使用热挤压成型的具有紧密网状组织并添加阻燃
剂的聚苯乙烯硬质泡沫来验证喷射效果。固定泡
沫块后进行操作。喷嘴距离泡沫块120mm 进行检
验，喷射痕长度不能超过100mm。 
 
3.4  Verification of the jet effect 
An extruded polystyrene rigid foam with sealed 
cell structure and flame-retardant additive is used 
to check the je effect. The foam block is  fixed 
and the verification is performed. In this process, 
the distance from the nozzle to the polystyrene 
block is 120 mm. The length of the spray jet could 
not be over 100 mm. 
 
3.5  试验过程 
 
3.5  Test procedure 
3.5.1  根据ISO 7253 第8.5 节的要求进行划线，
切割时须确保切口划入基材，两条划线呈垂直十
字。 
 
3.5.1  Scribe marks is made according to ISO 
7253, Section 8.5, make sure that the cuts do 
enter the substrate. Two scribes cut are vertical 
from each other. 
 
3.5.2  将样品固定，根据3.4参数要求进行蒸汽
喷射试验 
 
3.5.2  The specimen is fixed and test according 
to section 3.4.
