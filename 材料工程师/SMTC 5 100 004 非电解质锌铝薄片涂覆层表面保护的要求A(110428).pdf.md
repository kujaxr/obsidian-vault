Cr
6+面漆组成的。

Cr(VI)-free surface protection types made of zinc 
and aluminum flakes non-electrolytically applied 
to ferrous materials.

3.2  基本要求 
3.2
General requirements
3.2.1  避免有害物质按照SMTC 90008。 
3.2.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 90008. 
3.2.2  为了进行完整的试验，需要10 个成品
件。在诸如螺栓连接原件等的小型零件中，为
了初次样件的认可，必须进行一次大于50 kg
的试验性生产。 
3.2.2  10 finished parts are required for complete 
testing. For first-sample release of small parts, 
e.g., threaded fastening elements, a test produc-
tion of > 50 kg shall be performed. 
3.2.3  施涂的涂层和涂层材料必须不含有任
何Cr
6+化合物。 
3.2.3  The coating materials must not contain 
any Cr(VI)- compounds in order to ensure that 
the resultant coatings are likewise Cr(VI)-free. 
3.2.4  除非部件的某些部位在图纸中标注无
需表面保护涂层，否则整个零件的表面都应有
相应的涂覆层且具有该涂覆层应有的性能。在
基材和涂覆层之间应有牢固的附着力（见
3.10）；在弹性形变情况下，涂覆层不应脱落或
开裂。 
3.2.4  Unless certain sections of a part that are 
marked in the drawing are excluded from the 
surface coating, the entire surface of the parts 
must comply with the required surface protection 
type and display the prescribed properties. There 
shall be firm adhesion between coatings and 
base material (see Section 3.10); there shall be 
no flaking of the coating or cracks under elastic 
deformation. 
3.2.5  涂覆层不应出现任何气孔、开裂或其它
缺陷，导致防腐层被破坏。 
3.2.5  The protective coatings must not exhibit 
any pores, cracks, damage, or other flaws that 
impair the corrosion protection and/or function. 
3.2.6  整个生产控制过程不应降低成品件的
功能性要求。例如，安装弹簧或弹簧垫圈时，
不应造成涂覆层的剥落或开裂，以致于影响零
件性能降低。 
3.2.6  The production process and its control 
shall not impair the functional characteristics of 
the finished part. Thus for example, the coating 
must not flake or crack when the springs or spring 
washers are mounted or used as intended. 
3.2.7  在正确的装配条件下，不能有损害零件
功能性和/或降低零件防腐性能的涂层损害出
现。 
3.2.7  With proper mounting, the coating shall 
not be damaged in a way that would result in 
impairment of function and/or decrease in the 
specified corrosion protection. 
3.3 带有米制ISO-螺纹的螺纹件 
3.3  Threaded parts with metric ISO standard 
threads 
3.3.1  涂覆过程中不允许影响连接元件规定
的机械和物理性能。因此，制造上必须确认，
由涂覆材料供应商提供的烘烤环境是否适合将
被涂覆的零件。 
3.3.1  The coating process shall not impair the 
mechanical and physical properties specified for 
joining elements. Therefore, the manufacturer 
has to check whether the baking conditions spe-
cified by the coating company are suitable for the 
parts to be coated. 
3.3.2  带有米制ISO-螺纹的螺纹件的基本性
能就是满足所规定摩擦参数，为了满足摩擦系
数的要求，除了底层的锌铝涂覆层，含有集成
润滑添加剂的表面层也被用于Sp-t647 的表面
保护类型中。 
3.3.2  An essential functional property of 
threaded parts with metric ISO standard threads 
is their compliance with specified coefficients of 
friction. In order to ensure that the coefficients of 
friction, a topcoat with integrated lubricant addi-
tive is prescribed for surface protection type 
Sp-t647, in addition to the base flake coating. 
3.3.3  对于螺钉和螺栓，下列检测要求仅适用
于头部和扳手面；对于螺母，仅适用于除螺纹
面之外的部分。对于螺纹件或与其形状相似的
零件，如双头螺柱，则下列检测标准也只针对
于除螺纹之外的表面。 
3.3.3  In the case of screws and bolts, the test 
requirements listed below only apply to the head 
and/or the wrench bearing surfaces, in the case 
of nuts, they apply only to the nut body with the 
exception of the thread. For threaded and similar 
shaped parts, such as studs, the test require-
ments only apply to the face surfaces.

3.4 底层涂覆层 
3.4
Base coating
3.4.1  在涂覆过程中，多次涂覆有利于生成均
匀且不透明的涂覆层，并且对于滚涂来说，多
次涂覆是必须的。 
3.4.1  Repeated treatment would be useful to 
produce an even and opaque coating. It is com-
pulsory for barrel coating. 
3.4.2  通过多次涂覆，涂覆层的耐腐蚀性能得
以提高。如果除了规定的涂覆层厚度，涂覆过
程的类型也对该涂覆层的性能有重大影响，那
么该涂覆类型和编号应在图纸中标明。 
3.4.2  The coating system’s corrosion protection 
performance is enhanced by repeated coating. 
Type and number of the coating processes for 
flake coating should be specified on the drawing 
if this is important for the quality of the basic layer 
in addition to the specified layer thickness. 
3.5 面涂/润滑添加剂 
3.5  Topcoats/lubricant additives 
3.5.1  为了满足防腐要求以及保证规定的摩
擦系数，则可能需增加含有集成润滑添加剂的
特殊表面层。表面层必须配合底层涂覆层，不
允许产生任何影响性能的后果。 
3.5.1  In order to observe the anti-corrosion re-
quirements and to guarantee the defined coeffi-
cients of friction, additional system-specific top-
coats, optionally with integrated lubricant addi-
tive, may be used. The topcoats must be coordi-
nated to suit the base coating. They must not 
cause any functional impairment. 
3.5.2  如果除了防腐以外还要求了诸如：可涂
覆性、介质相容性、摩擦特性、螺栓连接特性、
可硫化性、温度特性等的其他的功能性表面性
能，那么就必须按零件规定进行试验或者进行

3.10 Adhesion
Procedure: Adhesive tape with an adhesive 
strength of (7 ± 1) N per 25 mm width is used for 
the adhesion test acc. to DIN EN ISO 10683. It is 
firmly pressed onto the specimen surface and 
then removed with a jerk-like motion perpendi-
cular to the surface. There must be no large-area 
removal of the coating. Small particles of the 
coating that adhere to the tape are permissible.
