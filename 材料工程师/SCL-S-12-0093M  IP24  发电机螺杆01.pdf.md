Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于机加工不良、圆角过小和高载荷产生的应力集中导致的多源疲劳断裂The part failed 
through multiple origin fatigue.  The crack was initiated due to high stress concentration, because of poor 
machining, small radius and high applied load.  
2、 芯部组织和金相组织不合格，但这不是导致失效的主要原因。 
The core hardness and microstructure are out of technical requirements, but this is not the main reason for 
failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 减少在退刀槽处的应力集中。 
Reduce the stress concentration factors at the radius. 
2、 改进生产过程控制，如热处理工序。To strengthen the production process control, such as heat treatment 
processes. 
3、 请提供所有相关失效件进行失效分析。 
Please supply all part of the failure for analysis.
