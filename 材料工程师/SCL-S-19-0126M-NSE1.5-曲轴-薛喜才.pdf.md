Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 曲轴属于过载断裂，很可能喷丸过程中或之前已经产生初始裂纹。Crankshaft fractured because of overload and the 
initial cracks have occurred during or before shot peening process.  
2. 曲轴基体材料硬度、金相组织满足要求。Hardness and microstructure of the crankshaft meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查生产过程，特别是喷丸处理。Check the crankshaft process specially the shot peening process.
