Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于受到较大的循环载荷导致的疲劳断裂。 The failure are fatigue fracture. 
2、 发现失效螺柱与排气歧管腰型安装孔有干涉现象。Stud and exhaust manifold mounting hole interference. 
3、 失效螺柱和新螺柱的螺牙中径以上有折叠，这不是引起此次失效的根本原因。There are folds in thread pitch 
diameter above for failure studs and new stud. 
4、 其他材料性能合格。Other material properties meet the technical requirements. 
  建议Suggestions： 
1、 请进一步评估尺寸干涉与排气歧管热变形对疲劳断裂的影响。Please further assess the size of the interference 
with the exhaust manifold heat distortion effects on fatigue fracture.  
2、 供应商加强过程质量管控。To strengthen the process quality control.
