Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 冲压生产过程中产生的原始裂纹导致铰链断裂。 
The trunklid hinge has failed due to a p re-existing crack caused during the stamping process. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
优化产品设计和冲压工艺。 
Optimizing production design and stamping process.
