Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件焊接时间不足，焊接位置强度差，导致装配时受力断裂。 
Insufficient parts have insufficient welding time, and the welding position has poor strength, 
resulting in force fracture during assembly. 
2.  失效件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the failure part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  供应商排查工艺异常原因并整改。 
                 Suppliers investigate the causes of process abnormalities and rectify.
