Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：前排出风口吹出的白色粉末系硅油。失效模拟进行中。 
Conclusions: The white powder blown out from front air outlet was silicon oil. Failure state simulation was under 
way. 
建议：对硅油的性能和使用情况进行检查。  
Suggestions： The performance and service conditions of silicon oil should be checked.
