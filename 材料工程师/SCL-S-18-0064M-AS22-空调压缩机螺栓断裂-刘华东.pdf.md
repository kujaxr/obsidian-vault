Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺栓是由于硬度较高而发生的脆性断裂。The bolt is brittle fracture due to the high hardness; 
2、 失效螺栓化学成分符合要求，芯部硬度，金相组织均不符合要求，但同批次零件符合标准要求。The chemical 
composition of failure bolt meets the requirements; the core hardness and microstructure do not meet the 
requirements. The same batch parts meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、供应商加强生产过程中管理，并对零件进行100%涡流筛选。The supplier strengthens the production process and 
carries out 100% swirl screening for the parts. 
2、更换表面保护方式，避免引入氢的可能性。Change the surface protection process to avoid the possibility of the 
hydrogen introduction.
