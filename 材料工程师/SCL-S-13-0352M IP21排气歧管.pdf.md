Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 1#， 2#排气歧管开裂都是由于热应力和振动共同作用的结果；The exhaust manifold was broken, due to the 
multi-source fatigue cracking. The cracking is the multi-action of thermal stress and mechanical stress. 
2. 排气歧管部分壁厚偏薄，但偏薄处并未开裂；Partial of wall thickness was lower, but the crack wasn’t observed.  
3. 材料的微观组织及硬度符合要求，疏松正常；Microstructure and hardness met the requirement. The porosity was 
normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核开裂处实际热应力；Check active thermal stress at cracking region. 
2. 在不影响装配的前提下，在开裂部位(2 缸和3 缸，3 缸和4 缸)增加加强筋，增加该处的强度与散热面积；
In the premise of not affecting the assembly, strengthening rib is added at the crack position, to increase the 
strength and the heat radiating area.
