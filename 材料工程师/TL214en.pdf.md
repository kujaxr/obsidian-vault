1 
Scope 
This Technical Supply Specification (TL standard) defines the requirements for zinc-dust paint on 
ferrous materials with the following degrees of protection: 
Ofl-y600 
Base coating, e.g. for leaf springs, 
Ofl-y650 
Heat-resistant zinc-dust paint, e.g. for elements of brake drums and brake discs that  
 
 
are at risk of corrosion. 
2 
Designation 
See Volkswagen standard VW 137 50, Section 2. 
3 
Requirements 
3.1 
General requirements 
Approval of first supply and changes according to VW 011 55. 
Avoidance of hazardous substances according to VW 911 01. 
For complete testing, three finished parts and, where required, six test panels that have been 
coated under standard production conditions are required. 
The coating shall exhibit the required surface protection, with the specified properties, over the 
entire surface. The coatings shall be free of pores, cracks, foreign matter inclusions, damage and 
other flaws that impair the corrosion protection and/or specified appearance. The layers must 
adhere firmly to the base material when the parts are used according to the specifications. 
The production process and its control shall not impair the functional characteristics of the finished 
part. If the coated parts are to be subsequently painted, the zinc-dust paint must be dried 
sufficiently beforehand. 
Given proper mounting, damage must not occur that would lead to an impairment of function and/or 
a reduction in the specified corrosion protection. 
The parts shall be conditioned for 144 h at room temperature before the tests.

3.2 
Color 
Gray. Other colors can be agreed. 
3.3 
Base material 
Steel according to drawing. 
3.4 
Pretreatment 
Degrease or sandblast. 
Phosphating is specified in the new specification of Ofl-y600. This does not apply to Ofl-y650 or 
Ofl-y600, which were defined before the publication of this standard. 
3.5 
Coating thickness 
Testing according to DIN EN ISO 2178 or DIN EN ISO 1463. 
Ofl-y600: 60 µm to 110 µm (in the area of roughness peaks > 30 µm), 
Ofl-y650: 30 µm to 50 µm (in the area of roughness peaks > 30 µm). 
3.6 
Composition 
Ofl-y600: ≥ 60% zinc dust in the dry paint film, 
Ofl-y650: ≥ 60% zinc dust in the dry paint film, whereby up to 7% of that can be replaced with Al. 
3.7 
Adhesion and brittleness 
Crosscut test according to DIN EN ISO 2409.
