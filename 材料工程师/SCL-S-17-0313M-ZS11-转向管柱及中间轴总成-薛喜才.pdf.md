Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  电机法兰盘受到异常外力发生过载断裂。 
    Motor flange plate fractured because of abnormal foreign stress.  
2.  材料成分、硬度、孔隙及组织无异常。 
Chemical、hardness、porosity and microstructure are normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查零件运输、装配过程。 
    Check the transportation and assembly process.
