Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 下系杆安装支架裂纹源存在冷隔缺陷，且承受应力载荷较大，最先发生断裂失效。支架断裂后造成4#螺栓部
位(图1)载荷突然变大，进而在大载荷下将离合器壳体过载拉断。Bracket of lower tie bar was broken because of 
the over loading. Cold shut defect was observed at the main crack origin. The bracket cracking caused the stress 
increasing at the 4# bolt positions (shown in figure 1), and then the clutch housing overload broken off. 
2. 离合器壳体成分及硬度符合技术要求；组织、孔隙率正常。Chemical components and hardness met the technical 
requirement. Microstructure and porosity were normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核零件的使用工况及载荷。Check the working condition of the failed parts and the load. 
2. (下系杆安装支架)供应商提交分析、整改报告。Suppliers provide the analysis, corrective report.
