Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、该螺栓断裂原因是由于夹紧力不足引起的.主要原因是支架和变速箱之间的尺寸存在偏差，造成螺栓和支架之
间的干涉而导致连接螺栓的夹紧力不足。这种干涉影响到拧紧时扭矩夹紧力的关系，因此所得的夹紧负载明显
低于设计要求。 The bolt failed through fatigue, which was caused by insufficient clamp load.  The reason is 
dimension inaccuracies between the bracket and gearbox, leading to interference between bolt and bracket during 
clamping.  This interference affected the torque-clamp load relationship, and therefore the clamp load was much 
lower than the design requirement. 
2、由于螺栓的断裂，该支架未能承受由此产生的过载，从而失效。The bracket failed through overload, due to the bolt 
fracture. 
3、螺栓硬度和金相组织，支架金相组织和孔隙度达到要求。虽然支架存在少量的孔隙率，但不是失效的因素。
The bolt hardness and microstructure, bracket microstructure and porosity met the requirements.  Small amounts of 
porosity in the bracket did not contribute to the failure. 
建议Suggestions: 
1、对支架和变速箱螺栓孔的尺寸位置进行研究。Carry out dimension study on bracket & gearbox bolt hole positions. 
2、在装配过程中，如果发现尺寸问题，应停止装配、查找原因。Reject parts if dimension problems found during 
assembly. 
3、请提供失效系统所有零件，如螺栓。Please submit all part of the failure system, eg bolts.

委托的安装支架和断裂的螺栓的一部分，如图1 所示。螺栓和支架分开地检查。 The mounting bracket and part of 
the failed bolt were received as shown in figure 1.  The Bolt and Bracket were examined separately.

螺栓Bolt 
 
断裂发生在该螺栓的螺纹根部处，断裂部位处在支架和变速箱之间的结合面。通过视觉检查，发现螺栓螺杆部分
一个区域有磨损（图2a），这个摩擦痕迹与螺栓孔的内壁相一致。螺纹有转动特性的痕迹（图2b），显示磨损痕
迹，但不可能的，这些螺纹能够接触变速箱的内螺纹。 
The bolt had fractured at the thread root, at the interface between the bracket and the adjoining component.  Through 
visual examination, damage to the bolt shaft was seen on one area (figure 2a) this is consistent with fretting against the 
inside wall of the bolt hole.  The lower threads of the bolt show wear features with rotational characteristic (figure 2b), 
suggesting engagement, however it is impossible these threads could meet the internal thread of the gearbox.

支架Bracket 
 
支架是断裂在两地的，标在图5a。在这两种情况下断裂为过载，进一步分析请见图8 和图9。相应的断裂螺栓的
安装点的出现明显运动的迹象，严重的嵌入，相比断口#2 旁边的安装点是正常的磨合痕迹（图5B，C）。而且很
明显的，失效螺栓没有和安装孔对中，磨合痕迹显示偏心（图5b）。 
螺纹孔的内表面上还示出因螺栓螺纹的接触而造成的损坏（图6），SEM 分析表明，这是由于在安装过程中（图
7），支架和变速箱的配合尺寸不正确。断口#1 旁边的安装点也显示偏心和孔内表面损坏（图8）。关键的尺寸控
制如图9 所示。不幸的是，由于零件失效而导致损坏，失效件的尺寸无法测量。 
The bracket was fractured in two places, as marked in figure 5a.  Fracture in both cases was overload, further details are 
given in figures 8 and 10.  The mounting point corresponding to the fractured bolt showed clear signs of movement, the 
embeddment is severe, compared to the normal bedding pattern at the mounting point near fracture 2 (figure 5b,c).  It 
was also clear that the failed bolt was not centrally located in the hole as the bedding pattern shows misalingment (figure 
5b).  The inner surface of the bolt hole also shows damage from contact with the bolt (figure 6), SEM analysis reveals 
that this was caused during assembly (figure 7), suggesting a dimensional problem with either the bracket or the gearbox.  
The mounting mount near fracture 1, also showed sign of misalignment and thread damage in the hole (figure 8).  The 
key dimensions are shown in figure 9.  Unfortunately, due to the damage caused during the failure it was not possible to 
measure the failed part.
