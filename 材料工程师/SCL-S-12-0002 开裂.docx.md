结论Conclusions：

由于材料疲劳老化造成强度下降，导致零件使用过程中开裂。

   The failure of the part is caused by the material of the part which is fatigue aged.

    建议 Suggestions： 

1增加橡胶连接面的区域

The area of the rubber connection should be enlarged.

    2 扩大缓冲区域的面积，且加大缓冲角度。

       Enlarge the buffer area and increase the buffer angle.

3 结构分析 construction analyze：

   从结构来看，零件橡胶连接面比例仅为30%，同时缓冲区域的角度为50度，并且整个结构成圆弧型，没有有效的缓冲区域。

   The rubber connection is only 30% to the whole working area. The construction of the buffer area present the arc shape and the angle of the arc is only 58 degree. So there is no sufficient section for buffer.

小结 conclusion：

1 TGA的测试结果说明失效件材料和抽样件材料是一致的，即可推断失效件材料初始状态满足要求且满足70度静态老化要求，且零件破坏力力和动静刚度满足图纸要求。

  The material of failure part is as the same as the sampling part from the result of TGA, we can deduce the as received performance of the failure part material conforms to the standard and meets the 70 centi-degree aging requirement. The functional tests also conform to the drawing requirement.

2 失效件断口分析表明在缓冲角区域呈现出材料老化的开裂裂纹，同时垂直于作用负荷方向出现材料分层现象。由于失效件材料强度仅为9MPa，结合零件失效公里数，可认为橡胶在长期疲劳作用下发生材料老化，导致开裂。

The fracture in the buffer area is caused by the material aged based on the section analyze. The direction of the fracture is parallel to that of the force. As the tensile strength of the failure part material is only 9MPa, we can get the conclusion that the rubber fracture took place mainly because the long time fatigue.

3 结构分析表明，橡胶连接面积很小，导致整个零件承受力偏低，同时零件缓冲角的缓冲区域很小呈圆弧型且角度较小，在零件的实际使用中作用力基本在此区域集中，加速材料疲劳老化，导致开裂。

From the construction analyze, the rubber connection is narrow which leads to the low force endurance of the part. And the buffer area is small which present the arc-shape, and the angle of the arc is small, which will lead the force focused on this area and accelerating the material fatigue aging.
