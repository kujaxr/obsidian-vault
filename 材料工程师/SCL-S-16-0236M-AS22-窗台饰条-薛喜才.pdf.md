Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1）饰条表面因沾染了含有Cl、S 的腐蚀性介质而发生点状腐蚀。 
Chrome waist rails pitted rusty because of contacting with the corrosive medium (chlorine、sulphur). 
（2）材料成分满足技术要求；金相组织正常。Chemical components and hardness meet the requirements. Microstructure 
is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）关注车辆使用环境。Pay attention to the useful situation.
