Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：主簧内侧受损，导致零件在使用中开裂，并形成漏油。 
  Conclusion: the rubber was damaged inside which leads the part broke during the usage.  
建议：在零件总成总装过程中，确保主簧不受外界损伤。 
  Suggestion：assure the rubber free of damage during the assemble
