Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 回水管在高应力区发生疲劳开裂，主要与应力过大有关。The backwater pipe fatigue cracked in the high stress 
area which is mainly relation to the large stress. 
. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 对失效区域结构进行优化以降低应力。Optimize the structure of the failure to reduce the stress.
