Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承是由于接触疲劳而发生失效的。The bearing was failed because of contact fatigue. 
（2）轴承的成分和硬度符合要求；夹杂物，金相组织和碳化物正常。The chemical components and hardness meet 
the requirements; the inclusion, microstructure and carbide are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）对轴承的油脂进行检查。Examine the grease of the bearing. 
（2）校核轴承的安全系数。Check the safety factor of the bearing.
