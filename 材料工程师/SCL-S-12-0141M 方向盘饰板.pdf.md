Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件受外力导致脆断。 
      2. 失效件注塑工艺不佳。 
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made failure part brittle failure. 
            2. Injection process of failure part was bad. 
建议： 1. 检查装配扭矩是否过大。 
       2. 责令供应商改进注塑工艺。 
Suggestions：1. Torque moment during assembly should be checked. 
            2. Injection process should be improved.
