Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件零件材料熔指异常偏高，是性能变弱，使用时断裂的主要原因。 
The melting index of the material of the failed part was abnormally high, which was the main 
reason for the weakened performance and fractured during use. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商对工艺问题做出解释并整改。 
                 Supplier explains craft problem and rectifies.
