Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  冷却管路的开裂是挤压管熔合不良导致的。 
   The cracking of the cooling pipe is caused by the bad fusion of the extruding tube. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  查找熔合不良的原因，并对其进行管控。 
Find out the cause of poor fusion, and control it. 
2.  严格执行生产过程的气密性检查和不合格品的管控。 
    Strictly enforce the air tightness inspection and control of unqualified products
