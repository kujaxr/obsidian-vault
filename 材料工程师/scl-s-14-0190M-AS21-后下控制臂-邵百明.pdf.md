Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 后下控制臂是由于局部应力较大而在热影响区发生疲劳开裂。The rear control arm was broken by fatigue 
because of a big local stress. 
（2） 焊缝熔合和厚度均符合技术要求；“包边”质量正常。The weld penetration and thickness meet the 
requirements and the edge covered by the end weld is normal.  
（3） 材料的成分，强度和板厚均满足材料的技术要求；开裂处的组织正常。The chemical components, strength 
and thickness meet the requirements and the microstructure at the crack location is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 优化设计（参照大众PQ35 控制臂），降低局部的应力，充分考虑热影响区对材料性能降低的影响。Optimize 
the design (refer to PQ35 control arm) to reduce the local stress and at the same time take into account the effect 
of the weld heat on reducing material properties.
