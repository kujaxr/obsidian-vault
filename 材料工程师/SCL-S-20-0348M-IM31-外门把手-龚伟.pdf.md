Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件上壳体&下壳体间装配应力偏大，使用过程中开裂。 
The assembly stress of the parts upper shell & lower shell is too large, and cracks during use. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  优化零件结构以改善零件装配应力状态。 
                 Optimize part structure to improve assembly stress state.
