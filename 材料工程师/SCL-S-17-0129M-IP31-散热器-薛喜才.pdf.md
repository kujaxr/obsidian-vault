Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  散热器扁管与边板焊接不良、存在贯穿性孔洞，导致散热器发生泄漏。 
    Bad welding in tube and back plate results in radiator assembly leak.   
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 加强焊接工艺控制，确保焊接质量。 
   Strength the welding process to make sure the quality.
