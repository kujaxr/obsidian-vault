Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 汽车行驶过程中的振动使零件与支架间产生摩擦，导致零件磨损；The friction between the part and 
bracket while the car running result in the attrition of the part. 
2、 POM 材料的耐温性能不能满足发动机仓使用要求；The heat resistance of POM can’t meet the 
requirement of engine compartment. 
3、 失效件材料符合设计要求；Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 优化结构，消除零件与支架间的摩擦；Optimize the structure in order to remove the friction between the 
part and bracket. 
2、 选用耐温性能更优的材料。Select the materials that have better heat resistance.
