Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1）前副车架撑杆发生严重的变形、磕碰，引起零件受力异常，发生疲劳开裂。Front-sub frame bracket happened 
deforming、knocking against, which resulting in the parts abnormal stress and cracking at last. 
 
（2）零件成分、板厚满足要求，组织及硬度正常。Chemical components and thickness meet the requirement. Hardness 
and microstructure were normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 检查试验过程。Check the testing process.
