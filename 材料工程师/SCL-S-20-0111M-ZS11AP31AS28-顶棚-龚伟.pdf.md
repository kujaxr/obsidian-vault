Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 顶棚黄斑为异物沾染所致。 
The maculae on the ceiling were caused by unknown objects contamination. 
 
建议Suggestions：1.排查各生产环节，检查可能的异物来源。 
                   Troubleshoot all production processes and check the source of possible unknown objects.
