Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 螺栓是由于过载而发生断裂。这可能是由于螺栓重复拧紧所致。The bolt was broken by overload, maybe 
which was because of reworking. 
（2） 螺栓的成分，硬度以及组织均符合技术要求；未见螺牙缺陷。The chemical components, hardness and 
microstructure meet the requirements and the screw thread defects were not found. 
（3） 垫片的硬度符合技术要求。The hardness of the washer meets the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 检查装配过程，避免螺栓重复拧紧。Check the assembly process and avoid repeating tightening.
