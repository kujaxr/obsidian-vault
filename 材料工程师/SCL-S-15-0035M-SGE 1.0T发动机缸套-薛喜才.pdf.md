Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 缸体属于疲劳开裂，裂纹源存在铸造缺陷。Cylinder was broken off though the fatigue and the casting defect was 
found at the crack source. 
2. 铝合金缸体成分符合要求；硬度正常，组织变质无异常。Chemical components of the cylinder met the requirement. 
Microstructure and hardness were normal. 
3. 铸铁缸套组织及硬度均正常。Microstructure and hardness of the cylinder liner were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商关注浇注工艺，提高铸件质量。Supplier focus on the casting process to improve the quality. 
2. 校核零件的工作载荷。Check the active load. 
3. 检查试验过程质量。Check the testing process.
