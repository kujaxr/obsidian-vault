1 范围 
本标准规定了轿车车厢（含行李箱）和与车
厢（含行李箱）相关联的部件气味性试验的术语
和定义、标示、测试方法和步骤及评判标准。 
本标准适用于汽车车厢（含行李箱）和与车
厢（含行李箱）相关联的部件中所有可能对车内
气味产生影响的零件和材料。 
 
 
1  Scope  
The Standard stipulates the terminology
 and definition, sign, test method and proce
dure, and judgment standard of odour test 
of parts related to passenger car compartm
-ent (including baggage compartment). 
The Standard applies to parts and 
materials among those related to passenger 
car 
compartment 
(including 
baggage 
compartment) likely to affect the odour in 
vehicles.  
2 术语和定义 
气味性——在一定温度和气候下时效处理
后散发出带有可辨别气味的挥发性成分的倾向
理解为气味性。 
 
气味测试——将放在密封容器内的试样在
一定温度下存放一定时间，按照一个标准用鼻子
进行感官评价等级称为气味测试。 
 
2  Terminology and Definition 
Odour property: the potential of a material 
to 
emit 
volatile 
components 
with 
distinguishable odour after the aging treatment 
under the given temperature and climate is 
understood as odour property. 
Odour test: samples are placed in sealed 
vessels under the given temperature for a 
given time, and making sensory evaluation 
with nose according to one standard is called 
odour test.

4  Test 
4.1 测试设备 
 
4.1  Test Equipment 
4.1.1  恒温设备：具有空气循环功能的干燥器
或干燥室，精度等级为2（±2 ℃）。 
 
4.1.1  Thermostatic equipment: drier or drying 
room with air recycling function, with precision 
grade being 2 (±2 ℃). 
4.1.2  测试容器：带有无气味的封口和盖子，
且容量为1 L 的玻璃容器。如为多次测量，建议
采用3 L 玻璃容器。测试容器在每一次测试步骤
前必须清洗（如在实验室玻璃器皿清洗机中清
洗），容器必须清洁、无气味。清洗容器的步骤
如下： 
 
a)  用无气味的液体清洁剂清洗容器； 
b)  用水彻底漂洗容器，然后擦掉容器上的  
水； 
c)  将容器留放进80 ℃温度的一个热风循
环干燥机（内部无异味）内使容器干燥
30 min； 
d)  按照上述相同的步骤，清洗容器的盖子。 
 
 
4.1.2  Test vessel: 1 L glass vessel with 
odourless seal and cap. If the measurement is 
made for several times, 3 L glass vessel is 
suggested. The vessel should be cleaned be-
fore each procedure of test (cleaned in glass 
ware cleaner of the lab). Vessel should be 
clean and odourless. The procedures for 
cleaning vessel are as follows: 
a)  Use odourless liquid cleaning agent to 
clean vessel. 
b)  Rinse vessels with water thoroughly,   
and sweep water on vessel. 
c)  Place vessel in a hot air recycling 
drier (without odour internally) at the 
temperature of 80 ℃ for drying 30 
minutes. 
d)  Clean vessel cap acc to the above 
procedures as well.

4.3.2  如C 类材料的厚度小于3 mm，应采用1 L
气味瓶测试，需使用(200±5) cm²的试样，用3 
L 气味瓶测量需使用(600±10) cm²的试样。 
 
 
4.3.2  If the thickness of Category C material 
is below 3 mm, then a (200±5) cm² sample 
should be used. Measurement with 3 L odour 
bottle requires sample of (600±10) cm². 
4.3.3  如均质材料厚度大于20 mm，应将试样
的厚度准确切割成20 mm 厚。如是夹层结构，应
全幅粘合作为整体进行测试。 
 
 
4.3.3  If the thickness of homogeneous ma-
terial is above 20mm, samples should be cut 
into pieces of 20mm thick accurately. In the 
case of interlayer structure, samples should be 
bonded as an integrated body for test. 
4.3.4  如A 类零件小于表2 中要求的重量或体
积，则将整个零件放入气味瓶中测试。但如果单
车中该零件使用较多大于测试重量或体积，则应
放入一定数量的该零件以达到所要求的测试重
量或体积。 
 
4.3.4  Weight or area of Category A parts are 
smaller than those required in Table 2, the 
whole parts is placed into odour bottle for test. 
If the parts are used in a single car in large 
quantity, which is above test weight or area, a 
given amount of the parts should be placed 
into bottle. 
4.3.5  表1 中的油漆和胶水根据所使用该油漆
或胶水的零件大小来确定试样种类，并根据试样
种类来按照发泡材料的取样面积取样。 
 
4.3.5  Paint and adhesive in Table 1 deter-
mine sample category according to the size of 
parts using the paint or adhesive, and samples 
are made according to the sampling area of 
foaming materials in terms of sample category. 
 
4.3.6  利用工具将样件裁取要求的大小，测试
前需除去裁取过程中产生的毛刺和飞边。 
 
 
4.3.6  Samples are cut into the required sizes 
through tools. Before the test, operators should 
remove burrs and flash edges generating in 
the process of cutting.  
4.3.7  对于总成零件中的每种材料需单独测试
气味，如仪表板中的上体、下体、饰条和风道等
需要分别测试气味。但对于复合在一起的材料不
必要分开测试，如仪表板搪塑上体（PU、PVC 和
骨架）、门板中嵌板及座椅面套等。对于小的总
成零件不必要分材料测试可以整体测试，如换挡
球头。 
 
 
4.3.7  Each kind of material in assembly parts 
should go through odour test separately, such 
as upper body, lower body, weather strip, and 
vent of instrument panel. Materials com-
pounded together may not be tested sepa-
rately, such as composite structure upper body 
(PU, PVC, and framework) of instrument pan-
el, panelling in panel seat, chair surface cover. 
Small assembly parts can be tested as a 
whole, such as gear shift.  
4.3.8  如无特殊要求及零件适宜测试气味一般
采用1 L 气味瓶。 
 
 
4.3.8  If no special requirements a 1 L odour 
bottle is normally used.

4.4  Procedure 
 
4.4.1  常温气味评价(常态)：测试人员在室温
（装配车间或实验室环境内）内将鼻子靠近待测
零件表面对样件常温气味进行评价，鼻子与待测
零件表面距离为1-2 cm。 
 
 
4.4.1  Normal-temperature odour evaluation 
(Normal): testing personnel hold the sample at 
a distance of 1-2 cm away from their nose and  
evaluate the odour under the normal room 
temperature conditions.

4.4.3  试验说明 
 
4.4.3  Test explanation: 
 
a) 对于方案1 和方案2，1 L 测试容器应
加入50 ml 的去离子水，3 L 测试容器
应加入150 ml 的去离子水。方案3 中
的测试容器不用加水； 
b) 放置在试验容器中的试体，应避免直接
与水接触； 
c) 测试容器应密闭，置于预加热干燥器
中； 
d) 方案1 和方案2 是将测试容器从干燥器
中取出后，立即进行测定； 
e) 方案3 是将测试容器从干燥器中取出
后，冷却至测试实验室温度(60±5) ℃
后 ，再进行测定。由三个检验员测定
后，容器必须在(80±2) ℃的干燥器中
放置30 min，再由其他检验员测定； 
f) 测定至少需要3 个检测员。如各个测定
人员评分结果与其他人差距在两个等
级以上，应至少有5 个检测员重新进行
该测量。 
 
 
 
 
 
 
a)  For Scheme 1 and 2, 1 L test vessel 
should be used and 50 ml de-ionized 
water added, a 3 L test vessel has 
150 ml de-ionized water added. For 
Scheme 3, the test vessel has no  
water added; 
b)  Samples placed in test vessel should 
not be in direct contact with water; 
c) Test vessel should be sealed, and 
placed in preheated drier; 
d)  As for Scheme 1 and 2, test vessel is 
tested immediately after being taken 
out from the drier; 
e)  For Scheme 3, test vessel is cooled to 
the temperature of (60±5) °C after 
removal from the drier, and then 
tested. After the test of three check-
ers, vessels should be placed in the 
drier at (80±2) °C for 30 minutes, and 
then tested by other checkers; 
f)  Test should be carried out at least by 
three checkers. If the evaluation re-
sults of a tester are two grades dif-
ferent from those of the other two, at 
least five checkers should make the 
test again.

4.4.5  评判标准 
 
4.4.5  Judging standard 
4.4.5.1  所有方案的气味评判标准分为1 至6
级（见表4），对所有材料均可采用半级测定。 
 
 
4.4.5.1 Odour judging standards of all 
schemes are divided into Grade 1 to 6 (See 
Table 4), all materials can be tested with 
semi-grade.  
4.4.5.2  参考测试法及所采用的方案说明，采
用各评分结果的算术平均值说明气味特性。评定
的等级如介于两者之间时，以最接近的半级的四
舍五入的平均值对气味性加以说明。 
 
 
4.4.5.2  Refer to test method and scheme 
explanation, and explain odour features with 
the arithmetic average value of evaluation re-
sults. If the evaluation grade is between two 
grades, the average value or semi-grades is 
allowed to explain the odour. 
4.4.5.3  如无特殊说明样件气味测试至少需评
价常温气味等级和方案3 气味等级。 
 
 
4.4.5.3  If no special requirements, testers 
should evaluate normal temperature odour 
grade based on Scheme 3.
