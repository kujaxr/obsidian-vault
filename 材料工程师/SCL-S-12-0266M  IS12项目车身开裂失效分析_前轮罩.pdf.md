Ⅰ.任务描述 Task description 
      
实验室受委托对IS12 项目G11/PAVE 试验车车身开裂进行失效原因分析，失效零件及部位见下页附表， 
 
失效车情况：共有3 辆试验车：①号G11 车，②号PAVE 车，③号G11 车，共发现25 处车身开裂，主要集中在 
 
前舱，开裂类型有焊点边缘开裂和钣金开裂。开裂零件材料均为镀锌低碳软钢板，选取开裂部位最多的轮罩进行 
 
分析，其与前端模块连接区域既有焊点边缘开裂又有钣金开裂。 
During G11 and PAVE testing of IS12, the vehicle bodies suffered cracking failures, see schedule on next page.  In three 
test cars: 1) G11 car 2) PAVE car 3)G11 car, 25 body cracks were found, mainly in the front cabin.  Both weld edge 
cracking and sheet metal cracks were found. Cracked panel material is galvanized low carbon mild steel plate. The wheel 
arch to front end module area was selected for analysis due to it’s numerous cracks, both at welds and in the panel edge. 
 
 
Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是在一定载荷下局部产生沿晶开裂，然后疲劳扩展。The part has failed through fatigue, however 
areas of abnormal intergranular fracture were also found. 
2、 失效件的材料化学成分符合技术要求。失效件的强度、金相组织未见异常。The chemical composition, 
tensile strength and microstructure meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 对原材料进行二次加工脆性测试。Test raw material for secondary work embrittlement. 
2、 校核该区域的工作应力。Please check the working stress of the region. 
3、 改善失效焊点钣金匹配。Reduce panel gap at welding points.
