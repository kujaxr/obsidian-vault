Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 压盘与摩擦片出现剧烈滑动摩擦导致压盘表面过热，且出现密集小裂纹，最终发生瞬间过载断裂；压盘断
裂后，传送带受力异常随后出现过载断裂。The severe sliding friction between the pressure plate and disc 
happened, which caused the cover surface overheated and with dense small cracking and finally made the disc 
broken by instantaneous overload; after the disc was broken, the strap was broken by abnormal big load. 
（2） 压盘芯部的石墨大小和形态符合要求；边部以D 型和E 型石墨为主。The graphite size and shape (mainly A 
type) of the core met the requirements and the edge was mainly D and E type. 
（3） 压盘的硬度和珠光体的含量满足要求。The hardness and the content of the pearlite met the requirements. 
（4） 传送带的硬度和组织正常；厚度符合要求。The hardness and microstructure of the strape are normal and the 
thickness meets the requirement.  
建议Suggestions： 
 
（1） 查找压盘与摩擦片剧烈滑动的原因。Find the cause of the severe sliding friction between the pressure plate and 
the disc.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
    AS22 进行CRT 上海道路试验，行驶28000km 后，离合器失效，如图1 和2 所示。 
由图1 可见，压盘和传送带出现断裂。压盘表面受到了剧烈的摩擦磨损，原始的机加工纹路很少可见，同时
剧烈摩擦形成了高温，导致压盘背面发蓝，工作表面形成许多的径向裂纹。
