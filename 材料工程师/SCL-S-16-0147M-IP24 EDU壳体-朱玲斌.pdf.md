Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） EDU 壳体吊耳受到较大载荷发生过载断裂，这与吊装时吊耳的实际承载面积过小有关。The EDU housing 
lug overload fracture which is related to the small actual bearing area during lifting. 
（2） 2#吊耳材料Mg 元素含量有超差，硬度和金相组织正常。The Mg element content is poor, the hardness and 
microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 优化吊装方式或吊具结构。Optimize the hoisting method or spreader structure.  
（2） 加强对供应商零件的质量控制。Strengthen the quality control of supplier parts.
