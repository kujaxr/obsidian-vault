1  范围 Scope 
  
本标准规定了由热塑性塑料
（PP/EPDM/(PE)+10~22％滑石粉的聚合物混合
物）制成的保险杠蒙皮及其相关零件的材料性能
要求和试验方法。 
本标准适用于上汽乘用车公司的各款车型保
险杠蒙皮及其相关零件。 
 
This Standard covers the general performance 
requirements and test method for thermoplastic 
bumpers cover (e.g. PP/EPDM/ (PE) polymer 
blend with 10~ 22% talcum). 
 
This standard applies to the bumpers cover and 
correlative parts for SMPV.

24h before test.

3.2.8 耐化学性 Resistance to agents 
该试验仅适用皮纹外观零件，不适用涂装零
件。样品大小为70 mm×25 mm，在常温下将样品
的一半分别浸入到 5% 清洗剂溶液、工业除尘剂、
漆面清洗剂、油漆防腐剂、汽油和柴油中，保持
30 min（每种试剂1 个试样）。接着在室温下存放 
24 h。然后用一块浸过水或工业除尘剂或清洗剂
溶液或汽油的布将试样擦拭干净后进行评价。 要
求：不得出现溶解、膨胀、变色和裂纹。 
 
The test applies only to the striae appearance 
part, not to coating parts. 70mm x 25mm part 
sections are immersed halfway in the 5 % 
detergent solution, industrial grade dust 
remover, paint cleaner, paint preservative, petrol 
and diesel for 30 min at homoeothermy.. This 
treatment is to be followed by a 24 h period of 
aging in air at homoeothermy. Afterwards, the 
specimen strips must be rubbed clean with a 
cloth soaked with water (for industrial grade dust 
remover and detergent solution) or with 
petroleum ether. The specimens are then 
evaluated. Requirement：No signs of separation, 
swelling, discoloration, and cracking. 
3.2.9 防脱腐试验 Transit coating removal test 
常温下用一块在当地使用脱防腐剂浸过的棉
布，擦拭零件（每个方向擦拭 10 次，压力约为 7 
N）。在冷却24 h 后，对零件进行评判。要求：不
得出现溶解、膨胀、变色和裂纹 
 
The location transit coating remover is applied to 
the finished part using a saturated cotton rag 
(ten times in each direction with a pressure load 
of approx. 7N). The finished part is visually 
evaluated after 24h. Requirement：No signs of 
separation, swelling, discoloration, and cracking.
