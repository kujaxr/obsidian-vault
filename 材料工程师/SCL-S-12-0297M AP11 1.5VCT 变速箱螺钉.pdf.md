Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）螺栓是由于受到较大的载荷而疲劳断裂的。The screw was broken because of a big load. 
（2）螺栓组织正常，无脱碳和表面缺陷；硬度符合要求。The microstructure is normal; decarburization and surface 
defect are not found; the hardness meets the requirement. 
（3）头部孔的尺寸符合要求。The hole dimension on the head meet the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 检测挡板的平面度是否正常。Check the flatness of the baffle. 
（2） 检测变速箱其它零件是否损坏。Check if other components of the gearbox are damaged. 
（3） 校核螺栓的载荷。Check the load of the screw. 
（4） 提供所有的与失效件匹配的零件，并保存完好。拆解总成之前，通知失效分析人员到场。Supply all the parts 
contacting the failed part and pay attention to keep them well. Before disassembling the assembly, inform the engineer in 
charge of failure analysis to be on the scene.
