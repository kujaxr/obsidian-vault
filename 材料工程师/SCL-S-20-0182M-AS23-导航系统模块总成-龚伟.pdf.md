Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 屏幕保护膜胶为PU胶，保护膜售后未撕除，光照后PU胶粘附在屏幕表面。 
The screen protective film glue is PU glue, the protective film is not removed after sale, and the 
PU glue adheres to the screen surface after light influence。 
建议Suggestions：1.短期措施：屏幕残胶可用清水擦除。 
                   Short-term measures: The residual glue on the screen can be wiped off with water. 
2.长期措施：验证硅胶膜有效性并用硅胶膜替代。 
                   Long-term measures: Verify the effectiveness of silicone membranes and replace it.
