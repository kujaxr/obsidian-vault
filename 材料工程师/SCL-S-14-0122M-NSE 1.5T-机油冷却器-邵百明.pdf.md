Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 机油冷却器是由于受到了较大的载荷而发生多源疲劳开裂。The oil cooler was broken by multi-source 
fatigue due to a big load. 
（2） 芯片的成分，组织和硬度正常；板厚和折弯半径符合要求。The chemical components, microstructure and 
hardness of the core plate are normal; the thickness and radius meet the requirements. 
（3） 翅片的组织和硬度正常；板厚符合要求。The microstructure and hardness of the wing plate are normal and the 
thickness meets the requirement. 
（4） 安装板的成分和厚度符合要求；组织和硬度正常。The chemical components and thickness of the assembly 
plate meet the requirements; the microstructure and hardness are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核载荷；如果安全系数不足，提高零件的承载能力。Check the load; if inadequate safety factor, improve the 
bearing capacity of parts.
