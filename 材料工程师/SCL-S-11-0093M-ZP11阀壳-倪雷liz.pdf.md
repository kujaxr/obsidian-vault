Ⅱ
结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1) 
从失效模式可以确定，该螺纹拉脱是因装配时扭矩过大造成的。可能导致这种情况的因素如下：
扭矩过大，内螺纹尺寸不合格，或者是装配过程中螺纹上存在润滑油。The failure mode was 
determined, as the excessive clamping load caused the thread stripping during assembly. 
Factors which may have led to this include: too-high torque, internal thread dimensions not OK, 
and/or lubrication on thread during assembly. 
2) 
材料的成分、硬度、金相和孔隙度等符合要求。The material composition, hardness, 
Microstructure and Porosity meet the requirements.  
3) 
阀壳螺纹孔的有效深度符合零件图纸要求。The valve housing threaded hole’s effective depth 
line with the part drawing. 
建议Suggestions： 
负责 
时间 
检查螺栓的装配和紧固过程。提供8D 报告。 
Check assembly and tightening process for the bolt. Provide 8D report.
