Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）摩擦片碎裂是由于外伤导致的。The brake pad fragmentation should be caused by exoteric damage. 
（2）失效的摩擦片和好的摩擦片均有打磨倒角的现象；这不同于国内的摩擦片。The edges of the failed and good 
pad were rounded by polishing, which is different from Chinese part. 
（3）材料的密度符合技术要求。The material density meets the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）排查导致外伤的原因。Find out the reason causing the exoteric damage.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
     
    泰国售后MG6 的前右制动钳的摩擦片局部出现碎裂。实验室收到的零件如图1 所示。其中图1（a）
所示的为碎裂的摩擦片及碎裂位置；另一片完好。The front right brake pad from Thailand customer's MG6 was 
broken at the local. Laboratory received the two brake pad, as shown in figure 1. The figure 1 (a) shows a brake 
pad was failed and broken at the circled by dotted line; another part is ok. 
正常摩擦片的工作面与圆周面是直接相交的，呈直角；但从外观（图2（a））可以看出：摩擦片的边
部经过了人为的打磨处理，且该打磨操作发生在碎裂之前，因为碎块表面也呈现打磨的痕迹。另一片的表
面同样留下了打磨的痕迹，见图3。The working surface and the circumferential surface of the normal brake pad 
directly intersects, but from appearance (Fig. 2 (a)) , it can be seen the edge of brake pad was rounded artificially, 
and the operation happened before the failure, because the fragments' surface was with the round edge due to 
polishing. The surface of another part was with the same round edge (see fig. 3).  
在碎裂处U 型区域的表面可以看见几条异常的痕迹。图4（a）和（b）显示在靠近碎裂的区域有硬物
刺伤的凹坑，并留下明显线性的痕迹；图4（c）显示局部具有轻的擦伤。On the surface of the U type area at 
the fragmentation, it can been seen several abnormal marks. Figure 4 (a) and (b) show a pit near the fractured 
region which was caused by a hard object, and left a clear linear traces; Figure 4(c) shows a light abrasions.
