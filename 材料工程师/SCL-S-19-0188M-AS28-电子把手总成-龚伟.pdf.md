Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件上下壳体过盈配合，装配应力过大，使用过程中开裂。 
The upper and lower parts of the parts are interference fit, the assembly stress is too large, and 
crack during use. 
              2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   The material of the parts conforms to the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 调整零件上下壳体的加强筋尺寸，经验证，优化后零件应力测试未见异常。 
                    Adjust the size of the upper and lower parts of the part. After verification, the stress test of the 
 parts after optimization is OK.
