Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 活塞环表面多条裂纹是由疲劳引起的，但其完全断裂可能是在活塞环拆解过程中所致；Several surface cracks of 
piston ring were made by fatigue, it completely fracturing could be caused by the disassembly.  
2. 活塞环材质正常；The material of piston ring was normal.  
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.对活塞、缸套等进行检查，确认引起活塞环表面裂纹的因素如活塞机加工误差或其他缺陷；Check the piston ring 
and cylinder. Make sure the possible reason of resulting in the piston ring cracking such as a machining error or other 
defect.
