Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件系因热板焊过度，导致壶体内侧支撑筋熔化过多，流至外焊接面，形成气泡状缺陷。 
The inner support ribs inside of tank in failure part melt too much because of excessive hot plate 
weld, flew to the outer welding surface, forming bubble-like defects.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions：  1. 供应商排查工艺异常原因并整改。 
                    The suppliers investigate the cause of abnormal process and rectify.
