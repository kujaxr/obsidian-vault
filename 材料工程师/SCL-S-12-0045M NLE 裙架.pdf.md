Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
(1) 由于断裂面严重损伤，因此不可能对所有的区域进行分析；但未损伤的区域显示处放射状的脆性断裂特征， 
表明失效是由于过载所致。Due to heavy damage on the fracture surface, it’s not possible to completely analyse all 
areas.  However, an undamaged area shows radial marks and brittle fracture, indicating overload as the cause of the 
failure.  
(2) 其它失效件的断口（见图6（a））可以看出类似疲劳类型的扩展纹路；这有可能是测试中小的过载导致裂纹 
阶段性的脆性扩展。From observation of other failed parts, fatigue-style beachmarks were seen.  It’s possible that a 
small overload caused a brittle crack to progess in stages during the test. 
(3) 材料的成分，组织和硬度符合要求。The chemical composition, microstructure and hardness of the material meet  
the specification. 
建议Suggestions： 
(1)对系统的振动和热膨胀影响进行分析；Analyse the effects of vibration & thermal expansion on the system. 
(2) 采用长的螺栓进行连接，以增强零件的抗弯应力。The joint would benefit from a longer bolt, to strengthen against 
bending stresses.  
(3)确保实验中下管固定相同。Ensure the fixture of the down pipe is same during every experiment. 
(4) 测试中有规律的对零件进行检查，以防止零件失效后，断口过度损伤。Please check parts regularly during engine 
testing, to prevent excessive damage after fracture.
