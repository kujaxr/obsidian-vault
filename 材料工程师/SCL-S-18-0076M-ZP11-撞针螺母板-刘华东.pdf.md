Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺母板是由于硬度较高而发生的脆性断裂。The nut plate striker is brittle fracture due to the high hardness; 
2、 失效件化学成分符合要求，硬度不符合要求，金相组织为回火马氏体。The chemical composition of failure part 
meets the requirements; the hardness does not meet the requirements, the microstructure is tempered martensite.  
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、隔离该批次零件，并对已装车零件进行风险评估。Isolate the batch of parts and carry out a risk assessment of the 
loaded parts.  
2、对同批次零件及现场零件进行抽样检查。Inspect the same batch parts and factory’s parts.
