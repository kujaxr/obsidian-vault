Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）缸体泄露是由于局部存在密集性的气孔和疏松引起的。The leakage of cylinder block is because there are dense 
pores on the local. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）改善局部补缩，并优化排气系统。Improve the local shrinkage and optimize the exhaust system. 
（2）控制铸型砂的发气量。Control the gas quantity of the cast sand. 
（3）控制炉前处理剂的使用量，并充分烘干。Control the usage amount of pretreatment agent and dry them before 
using.
