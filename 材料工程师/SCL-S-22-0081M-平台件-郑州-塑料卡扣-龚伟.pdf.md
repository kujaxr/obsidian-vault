Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件为冷模件，性能差，装配时断裂。 
The failure parts were cold module with poor performance and broke during assembly. 
2.  零件材料不符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material didn’t conform to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  责令供应商对私自更换材料问题做出解释并整改。 
Let the supplier explain the material problem and rectify.
