Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  链条B 端断口1(见图2)由于接触含硫介质先发生应力腐蚀断裂，其后断口2 发生疲劳断裂，A 端被最后拉断。 
    Timing chain fractured though stress corrosion, then fractured by fatigue and tension fracture at last. 
2.  链片硬度，金相组织无异常。 
    Hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查腐蚀介质的来源。 
Track the source of corrosion.

   综合以上分析结果可知：
