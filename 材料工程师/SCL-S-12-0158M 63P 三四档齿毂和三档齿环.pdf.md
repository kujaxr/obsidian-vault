Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）由于定位不好，齿毂和齿环在装配中被压坏。Because of poor position, the tooth hub and tooth ring were pressed 
and then damaged during assembly. 
（2）齿毂的材料密度，组织和硬度符合要求。The density, microstructure and hardness of the tooth hub’s material meet 
the requirement. 
（3）齿环的材料组织，硬度符合要求。The microstructure and hardness of the tooth ring meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）改善装配工艺。Improve the assembly process.
