Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属疲劳断裂，是在运行过程中受力不均造成的。Failure of the part was  fatigue fracture，
that due to uneven force during operation. 
2、 材料性能未见异常。Material properties were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1、 请检查失效件在实际工况中受力不均的原因（如装配等）。Please check the uneven stress causes in 
the actual failure conditions.
