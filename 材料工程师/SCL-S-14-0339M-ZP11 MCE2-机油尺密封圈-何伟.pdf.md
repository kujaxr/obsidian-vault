Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1 失效件密封圈受到异常扭转力导致撕裂。  
2 失效件材料硬度和材料定性满足标准对材料供货态要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The sealing ring was torn by an abnormal torsion force. 
2. The material hardness and identification results of the failure part meet the requirements of the 
as-received condition. 
 
建议： 1 确保零件装卸、使用不受异常外力。 
       2 确保密封圈表面平整光滑,无损伤。        
Suggestions:  1. Make sure the sealing ring is free from abnormal external force. 
             2. Make sure the surface of the sealing ring is smooth, and has no damage.

The surfaces of both sides of the failure part have scratch marks as shown in figure 2. 
图2.划伤痕迹 
Figure 2. The scratch marks 
机油尺密封圈的外侧划伤可能是装卸机油尺过程中造成。 
The scratch marks in the outer of the ‘O’ ring maybe be caused during the dip stick handling.   
 
3 断口分析 Fracture Analysis 
观察密封圈的断口，如图3。 
Observe the fracture surface of the sealing ring in the figure 3. 
 
 
图3.整体断口 
Figure 3. The fractures of the failed part 
两个断口的表面均不平整，且断面最高点在同一侧，说明受力方向一致。 
The surfaces of the fractures are not flat, and the highest points on both fracture srufaces are on the same 
side of the failed sealing ring, which indicates the sealing ring was pressed in the same direction.  
根据HET 路试发现机油尺漏油时拍摄的密封圈失效照片，图1 中左侧图片，可知断面最高点处于密封圈安
装状态的上侧。 
The photograph of the dipstick in the figure 1 that taken during the HET test in Spain shows that the highest 
points on the fracture surfaces are on the upper side of the sealing ring while in installation status.
