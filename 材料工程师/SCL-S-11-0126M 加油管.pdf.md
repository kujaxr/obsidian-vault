Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 由于加油管成型后要经气密性检查，并且加油管是以加油管和加油管护套总成方式供货，可以确定
加油管在气密检查后到加油管总成装配好之间的过程，被外界钝器刺穿。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of the fuel pipe conforms to the drawing requirement. Due to the sealing 
property of fuel pipe will be inspected after molding, and the fuel pipe with the pipe protector fuel will be 
sent to upper level suppler as a pipe assembly, so the fuel pipe was broken by an outside blunt tool between 
the sealing inspection and assembly process.    
建议：避免加油管受外界刮擦；装车前百分百检测油箱气密性。 
Suggestions：The scratches from outside should be prevented. Sealing property of the fuel tank should be inspected 
one hundred percent before installed into vehicle.
