Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）排气歧管开裂失效是热疲劳开裂。The Exhaust Manifold cracking failure is thermal fatigue cracking. 
（2）管部裂纹始于排气歧管中隔板以及中隔板与管壁连接过渡处的应力集中处。The crack on the tube began in the 
Exhaust Manifold partition and the stress concentration of the partition connection at the transition. 
（3）歧管尺寸符合要求。The dimensions of the exhaust manifold meet the requirement. 
（4）失效零件的材料成分、组织以及石墨形态和大小均符合技术要求，硬度稍高于技术要求（要求130~170HB，
实测181~183HB）。The chemical compositions, microstructure, graphite shape and size of the failure parts all 
conformed to the technical requirement, the hardness slightly higher than the technical requirement（Requirements 
130~170HB, Measured 181~ 183HB）. 
（5）通过MCT 试验的零件材料组织、石墨形态和大小均符合技术要求，硬度也稍高于技术要求（实测
173~175HB）；失效零件硬度比通过MCT 试验零件的高8HB。The microstructure, graphite shape and size of the parts 
through MCT parts conformed to the technical requirement, the hardness slightly higher than the technical requirement 
too（Measured 173~175HB）;the hardness of failure parts is 8HB higher than the one of the part through MCT. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）过高的热应力与振动交变载荷共同作用，加速开裂。建议检测发动机实际工况，优化零件结构，降低热应
力。High thermal stress and vibration alternating loads, accelerated cracking. Recommended to detect the actual 
operating conditions of the engine, optimize the parts structure, and reduce heat stress. 
（2）控制工艺过程，确保硬度符合技术要求。Control the process and ensure the hardness meets the requirement.
