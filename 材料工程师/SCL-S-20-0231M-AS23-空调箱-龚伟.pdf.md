Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 安装孔因扭矩过大导致滑牙。 
Mounting hole slipped due to excessive torque.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  按PDM推荐值设定安装扭矩。 
                    Set installation torque according to PDM recommended value.
