Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1．前下摆臂受到异常外力作用而发生弯曲变形，零件在非正常状态下工作，最终发生了疲劳失效。The front 
lower-arm ever occurred bending deformation due to abnormal external force, which caused the parts working under the 
abnormal condition, and finally failed by fatigue. 
2． 零件材料成分符合技术要求，硬度和金相组织正常。Chemical composition met the technical requirement. Hardness 
and microstructure were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1．谨慎驾驶。Drive carefully.
