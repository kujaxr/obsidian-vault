Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）轴销上存在异常划痕导致衬套使用后严重磨损，匹配间隙增大，从而产生异响。The abnormal scratches on the 
shaft pin led to serious wear of the bushing and increased the matching clearance after use, which finally caused abnormal 
sound; 
（2）1#，2#，3#失效轴销表面硬度符合要求。The surface hardness of 1#,2#and 3# shaft pin is qualified.  
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 对轴销的生产及初次装配环节进行排查，查找划痕产生的原因。Check the production and initial assembly of 
the shaft pin to find out the cause of scratches.
