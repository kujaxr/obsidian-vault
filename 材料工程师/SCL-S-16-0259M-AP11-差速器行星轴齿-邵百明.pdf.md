Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）行星齿轮轴是由于存在初始的感应淬火裂纹而发生疲劳断裂。The differential planetary shaft was broken by 
fatigue because of an original crack caused by abnormal induction quenching. 
（2）失效行星齿轮轴的成分和硬化层深符合要求；表面硬度偏低。The chemical components and hardening layer 
depth meet the requirements and the surface hardness is lower than the requirement of the hardness. 
（3）抽检新件的表面硬度也偏低；市场返回件的表面硬度符合要求。The surface hardness of the new part is lower 
than the requirement of the hardness and the returning part from the market. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）供应商应调查引起初始裂纹的感应淬火原因，并提交整改报告。The supplier should investigate the reason for the 
induction quenching causing the crack and offer the rectification report. 
（2）供应商应加强过程质量控制，确保表面硬度符合要求。The supplier should strengthen the process quality control 
to ensure the surface hardness meets the requirements.
