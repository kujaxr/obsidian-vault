Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 驱动半轴是由于受到较大的扭转载荷而发生扭转剪切断裂。The drive shaft was broke by torsion shear due 
to a large torsional load. 
（2） 失效驱动半轴的成分、表面硬度、芯部硬度、硬化层深和组织均符合技术要求；夹杂物水平正常。对比
同批次通过相同路试驱动半轴的材料检测结果，未见明显差异。The chemical components, surface hardness, 
core hardness, quenching depth and microstructure meet the requirements; the inclusion level is normal. There 
are not any difference in the test results between the failed part and the same batch of the part passing the test. 
（3） 失效驱动半轴断裂部位过渡圆滑，其过渡半径和直径与同批次通过相同路试的无差异。The transition at the 
fracture location is smooth and the transition radius and diameter of the failed part and the same batch of the part 
pasting the test are without difference. 
（4） 38B3 是一种普遍应用于驱动半轴的材料，其韧性显著优于UC2。38B3 is a kind of material widely applied to 
drive shaft and its toughness is significantly greater than UC2.  
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 请工程分析驱动半轴各工况下的受力，并调查路试中扭转载荷的输出是否正常。Please DRE analyze the 
load under any kinds of conditions and at the same time, investigate if the torsion output is normal.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results

35km/h；当制动到0 时，发动机自动熄火，再次启动发动机，挂入D 档，车辆无法前进，断电无效。车辆拉入车
间检查，发现左侧半轴球笼处断裂，如图1 所示。

以及与失效件断裂部位相同的区域进行检查，也未见异常。
