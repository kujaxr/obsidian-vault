Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1，前处理工序（酒精擦拭表面灰尘、油脂）不充分使局部附着力性能变差，导致脱漆； 
2，按小糸内部标准进行漆层附着力测试（十字划格）抽检合格，国内售后没有发生过客户抱怨，风险低。 
建议：1，该零件小糸划格测试需与上汽标准统一，按照SMTC 5 300 001，划百格，胶带保持5min 后再迅速撕掉； 
      2，加强前处理工序，喷涂黑漆前样件套袋，并保证酒精充分擦拭表面。 
Conclusions: 1. The insufficient of pretreatment caused the paint stripping. 
2. The batch test of cross cut test conform the requirement of Koito standard. And there is no after-sale 
complain about this painting, therefore the risk is low. 
Suggestions: 1, Cross cut test of Koito should be the same as required in SMTC 5 300 001, 100 grid should be cut and 5 
min should be maintained before the tape being stripped.\  
           2, It is suggested that the pretreatment of painting should be promote to ensure all surface is clean.
