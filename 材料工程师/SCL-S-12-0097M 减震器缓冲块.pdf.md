Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件受高温导致失效（温度高于170℃）。 
      2. 失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The failure was caused by high temperature (T>170℃). 
 
  2. The material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 降低该零件的环境温度。 
Suggestions：1. Service temperature of this part should be reduced.
