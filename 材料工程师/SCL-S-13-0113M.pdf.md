Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂，断口的疲劳区面积较小，终断区面积较大，说明断裂的弹簧所受的载荷很大。 
The failure is fatigue fracture. fatigue fracture area is smaller and finally crack area is bigger, which indicating a 
breaking spring suffered great load. 
2、 零件的抗拉强度低于标准技术要求。The tensile strength for the parts is lower than the standard technical 
requirements. 
3、 化学成分、表面脱碳符合技术要求。失效件与新零件的金相组织和非金属夹杂物无差异，未发现异常。Chemical 
composition, surface decarburization meet the technical requirements.Microstructure and non-metallic inclusions for 
failure parts and new parts do not found abnormalities. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请检查台架试验的实际工况和该零件的实际载荷情况。 
Please check the actual working conditions of the bench and the actual load for the part. 
2、 根据弹簧实际工作情况优化产品设计，如使用合金弹簧钢丝等。According to the actual work of the spring 
optimization product design , such as the use alloy spring steel wire.
