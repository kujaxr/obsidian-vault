Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1. 空调管发生疲劳而失效。The air conditioning pipe is fatigued and fails. 
2. 空调管的尺寸和焊接质量合格。The dimension and welding quality of air conditioning pipe are qualified. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 检查拆装过程是否变形。Check whether the disassembly process is deformed. 
2. 校核断裂位置的应力和安全系数。Check the stress and safety factor of fracture position.
