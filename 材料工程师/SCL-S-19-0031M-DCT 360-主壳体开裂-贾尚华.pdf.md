Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 主壳体发生的是过载断裂。 
Fatigue fracture occurs in the cooling tubing of the motor. 
2. 主壳体的材料成分、硬度、孔隙度满足要求，金相组织正常。 
The material composition, hardness and porosity of the main shell meet the requirements, and the metallographic 
structure is normal.

1. 排查异常外力的来源。 
Investigate the sources of abnormal external forces.
