Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：经测试，失效件的材料无异常。使用过中橡胶管受到外界异物刮擦导致开裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and the material test, it can be seen that the pipe was broken by an external scratch. 
建议：使用时防止卡箍和工具刮擦橡胶管表面。 
Suggestions：The scratches caused by the metal loop and assemble tool should be prohibited during use of the rubber 
pipe.

layer rubber is lower compared with that of requirement. But the different values are small, so the mechanical 
property of exterior layer rubber also conforms to the design requirement when we take into account the part 
has been used for a period of time.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图2 的胶管外壁形貌可以看出，胶管在卡箍边缘处出现裂纹；将卡箍卸掉后，在卡箍区域未发现划痕。
通过图3 的放大形貌可以看出，裂纹向内凹陷并伴有碎胶现象。外壁裂纹对应的内壁出现贯穿裂纹和划痕（图4）。 
From the outside pipe surface of Fig 2, it can be seen that there are several scratches near the edge of metal loop. 
After the metal loop removed from the pipe, no scratch is found on the loop section as shown in Fig 3 and the crack 
surface with shredded rubber was depressed toward to the inner direction. In Fig 4, several penetrated cracks are found on 
the inside pipe surface which are the corresponding positions of outside cracks. And several scratches are found at the 
same region.

小结：通过胶管的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，胶管使用过中受到外界异物刮擦，导致胶管开裂。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the rubber pipe, it can be seen that the 
pipe was broken by an external force.
