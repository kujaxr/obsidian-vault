Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：实效轮胎材料符合要求，鼓包是由于安装不当产生的内层划痕导致的。 
Conclusions: The material of failure part is conform to the requirement. The bubble summoned up on the tire is 
caused by the nick in the inner side rubber because of the improper installment. 
建议：轮胎安装时确保安装角度和轮滑液的覆盖。 
Suggestions：Assure the proper fitting angle and the cover of the lubricating fluid in the tire installment.

综上所述，分析所得的重要因素如下： 
1 轮胎失效点胶料性能符合要求； 
2 在失效件上发现的内层裂纹长度同鼓包的长度相同； 
3 通过模拟，我们发现安装不当产生的裂纹同失效件上的裂纹相似。 
所以可以得出结论：由于安装不当造成轮胎内侧划伤，充气后，气体向裂纹进行渗透，对胎面（外层胶和增
强层）进行推压，形成胎面的鼓包。 
As mentioned above, the important information acquired after analyze is as follow:  
1 the rubber property is conform to the requirement; 
2 the length of the nick found on the failure tire is almost as the same as that of the bubble;  
3 we found the sphere of nick scratched in the installment simulation is very like that of the failure part.  
The conclusion is as follow: the inner side of the tire is scratched because of the improperly installment, and the air 
was penetrating through the nick in the tire after gas filled. Then the surface of the tire (the outside rubber and the 
reinforcement) is pushed by the penetrated air and created the bubble on the sidewall of the tire.
