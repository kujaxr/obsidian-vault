Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 倒档惰轮和一二档同步器齿套在非工作面上受到冲击过载而失效；没有疲劳的特征。Reverse idler gear and 1st/2nd 
speed synchronizer ring gear have failed due to impact overloading.  There was no indication for fatigue.  
（2） 由于获得的失效样品不完整（供应商先对其进行了取样分析），同时，与其配合的其它零部件没有提交， 
因此不可能找到过载的原因。Since the parts were already cut by the supplier, and other parts of the assembly were not submitted, it is not possible to 
find the reason for overload. 
（3）材料的成分，芯部硬度以及渗碳层均满足产品的要求；材料的组织正常；没有发现任何缺陷。The chemical 
components, core hardness and carburized layer depth meet the requirement of the parts; the microstructure is normal. No defects were found.  
建议Suggestions: 
（1） 建议SQE 和DRE 进一步查明什么工况可以导致过载。Suggest the SQE and DRE find out what circumstances can create the  
overload.

III. 测试结果与分析 Test Results and Analysis 
（1） 失效产品 Failure Parts 
失效的产品如图1 所示。由图可见：（1）齿套有13 个齿（齿1~13）从根部完全断裂，并有3 齿（齿B，齿
C，齿D）出现部分断裂，2 个齿(齿A 和齿B)出现损伤；（2）惰轮有3 个齿（齿1~3）完全断裂，并有2 个齿（齿
A 和齿B）出现部分断裂，2 个齿(齿4 和齿5)出现严重损伤。零件中其余缺少区域是由于供应商取样所至。 
The failed parts are shown as following. With reference to the photos, it was found: (1) Of the ring gear, 13 teeth (tooth 1 ~13) are broken completely 
from the tooth roots; 3 teeth (tooth B , tooth C, tooth D) are broken partly and 2 teeth (tooth A and B) are damaged. (2) Of the Idler gear, 3 teeth ( tooth A , tooth 
B) are broken completely from the tooth roots; 2 teeth (tooth 4 and tooth 5) are broke partly and the 2 teeth are damaged seriously. Note: some parts of the gears 
were removed by the supplier.

（2） 工作原理 Work Principle 
新的齿套与倒档惰轮在工作时候的匹配关系如图2 所示。倒档时，惰轮带动齿套转动；如果车子还未完全停
止向前运动，此时进行倒档操作，惰轮与齿轮接触瞬间，在工作面上会产生冲击力。该车为自动挡，当前进的行
驶速度小于3Km/h 时，可以进行倒档操作。 
当车子在后退过程中，如果齿圈的速度大于惰轮的速度，齿圈与惰轮的相互作用面为反车面。 
The matching of new ring gear and reverse idler gear are shown by fig. 2 during working. When reverse gear is selected, the reverse idler gear will move to mesh with 
and drive the ring gear. If the car runs along and the driver makes a reverse gear, the working surface of the reverse idler gear will receive an impact from the ring gear 
during the contact. The failure car is automatic transmission, when the speed is less than 3 Km, the operation of reverse gear can be made. 
If the car is running backward and the speed of the ring gear is more than the one of the reverse idler gear, there is a load on the reverse working surface.
