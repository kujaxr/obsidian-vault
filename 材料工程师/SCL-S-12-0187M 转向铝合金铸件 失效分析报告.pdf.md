Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、由于异常的紧固条件使铸件过载导致开裂。The casting has failed through overload, due to abnormal tightening 
conditions. 
2、 螺纹尺寸，硬度和微观结构符合图纸要求，并未发现缺陷。The thread dimensions, hardness and microstructure 
met the drawing requirements, and no defects were found. 
 
建议意见： 
1、检查相关匹配零件的尺寸。Check the dimensions of the mating part 
2、根据扭矩规范紧固零件。Tighten according to torque specification

断口分析Fracture analysis 
 
如图2a 所示：断口平坦、无特色的，典型的拉伸超载特征。电镜分析结果显示为完全脆性断口，这断口特征对
这种类型的材料很正常（图2b）。未发现缺陷，如孔隙或夹杂物。 
The fracture surface is shown in figure 2a.  It is flat and featureless, typical of tensile overload.  SEM analysis revealed 
completely brittle fracture surface, normal for this type of material (figure 2b).  There were no defects such as porosity 
or inclusions.
