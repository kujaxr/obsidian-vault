Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 纵梁与车架安装支架焊缝热影响区发生疲劳开裂，裂纹起源于零件的高应力区，且裂纹可能从B 侧手工焊接
与机器人焊接交汇区域 (见图5)最先萌生。The frame was cracking by fatigue at the heat affected zone and the 
crack origin was in the high stress area. The crack may be from the B side between manual welding and automatic 
welding area (show in figure 5). 
2. 手工焊接区域焊缝质量正常；机器人焊接区域(A 侧)存在熔深不足、未熔合缺陷(见图10)。The weld quality of 
the manual welding was normal. Insufficient welding and no fusion defect were found in the automatic welding area 
(show in figure 10).  
3. 纵梁材料性能正常。The material of the girder was normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化焊缝布置，避免在高应力区起弧、收弧或补焊，同时改善焊缝质量。Optimise the welding distribution to avoit 
patch weld or arcing and improve the welding quality.  
2. 降低零件高应力区的应力。Reduce the stress at the high stress region.
