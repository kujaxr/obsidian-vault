Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 滑动片Ⅰ失效件结构干涉导致形变；滑动片Ⅱ失效件受外力导致脆裂。 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1.Structural interference caused the failure part of sliding piece 1 failed; external force made the failure 
part of sliding piece 2 fractured. 
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.    
建议： 1. 检查零件的尺寸及结构匹配情况。 
Suggestions:  1. Check the size and structural adaption of the part.
