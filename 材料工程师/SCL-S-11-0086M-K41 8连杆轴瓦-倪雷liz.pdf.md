Ⅱ
结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 一个大的硬颗粒被发现嵌在轴瓦材料中。我们认为它是来自发动机中某一低碳金属零件
上的毛刺。A large hard particle was found embedded in the bearing material. We 
believe it is a burr from a Low-carbon metal part in the engine.  
 
建议Suggestions：这些颗粒能导致发动机严重损坏。对零部件的清洁度控制应加以严格要求。These 
particles can cause serious damage to the engine. The cleanliness of the parts 
should be strictly controlled.

在扫描电镜下对连杆轴瓦合金层内嵌异物进行EDX 测试，见图2。 
 
结论：1）铁、氧、碳和铝的相对值较高。而Al 可能来自于轴瓦的合金层。The relative amounts of Iron, Oxygen, 
Carbon and Aluminum are higher, the Aluminum may come from the bearing alloy layer. 
 
      2）硫和锰最有可能是来自合金中的微量元素。The Sulfur and Manganese come from trace elements 
in the alloy are most likely. 
 
3）微量的钙不容易得到，最有可能是由于污染。 Small amounts of Calcium cannot be easily 
accounted for, although contamination is most likely.
