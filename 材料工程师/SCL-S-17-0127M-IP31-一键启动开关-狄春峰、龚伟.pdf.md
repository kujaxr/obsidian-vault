Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  因装配不当，导致卡脚未完全安装到位，受挤压后变形，按键倾斜。 
Because of bad assembly process, leading to pin not fully installed, deformed after extrusion, and 
button leaned.  
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  供应商调整装配方式，确保类似问题不再发生。 
Suppliers adjust their assembly process to avoid similar problems.
