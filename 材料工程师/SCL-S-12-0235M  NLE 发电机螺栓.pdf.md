Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂。The failure for the part belong to fatigue fracture . 
2、 零件材料性能符合技术要求。The parts material properties met the technical requirements.
