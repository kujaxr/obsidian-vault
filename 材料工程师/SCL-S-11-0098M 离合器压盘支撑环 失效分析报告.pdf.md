Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions: 
 
 
两个离合器支撑环都因为超载而断裂，这是从在缺陷（见第1 及2）发起的。这些缺陷是由于焊接不良和整
理的过程。 
 
另一种失败是在早些时候报道SCL- S -11 -0059M。 
 
对焊缝形状不符合设计要求（见第3 节）。   
 
Both clutch support rings have failed through overload, which was initiated from defects in the parts (see sections 1 & 2).  These defects 
were caused by poor welding and finishing processes. 
 
Another failure was reported earlier in SCL-S-11-0059M.  
 
The shape of the welded part does not meet the design requirement (see section 3). 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
改善焊接工艺 
 
Improve welding process

3  零部件应用   Component application  
 
离合器压盘支撑环作为压盘的支点，圆形截面是必要的。 断裂发生在焊缝附近,此处支撑环的截面为椭圆形，可
能是是压盘施加了强大的作用力导致支撑环承受高负载。一个强大的应用压盘可能会导致上环高负荷  
The support ring acts as a fulcrum for the pressure plate, therefore a round cross section is necessary. 
The failed parts’ welded position is not round.  Therefore it’s possible that a strong application of the pressure plate can cause a 
high load on the ring.
