1 范围 
本标准规定了钢铁材料上富锌漆涂层的下
列保护种类要求： 
a) Sp-y600 基本涂层，例如，用于板簧；
b) Sp-y650 耐热的锌粉，例如，用于鼓式
制动器和盘式制动器上有腐蚀风险的
零件。 
  本标准适用于评估钢铁材料上的富锌漆涂
层。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the requirements 
for zinc-dust paint on ferrous materials with the 
following degrees of protection: 
a) Sp-y600 Base coating, e.g. for leaf 
springs; 
b) Sp-y650 
Heat-resistant 
zinc-dust 
paint, e.g. for elements of brake drums 
and brake discs that are at risk of 
corrosion. 
This standard is adopted to evaluate the 
zinc-dust paint on ferrous materials.

3 要求 
3  Requirements 
3.1 基本要求 
3.1  General requirements 
3.1.1  避免有害物质按SMTC 90008。 
3.1.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances 
acc. to SMTC 90008. 
3.1.2  为了进行完整测试，需要3 件成品，而
必要时，需要在正常生产条件下生产的6 块测试
样板。 
 
3.1.2  For complete testing, three finished 
parts and, where required, six test panels that 
have been coated under standard production 
conditions are required. 
3.1.3  在全部面积上，镀层必须符合规定的表
面保护并展示出要求的特性。镀层不得存在气
孔，裂纹，外界杂质，损坏和会损害腐蚀防护和
/或规定外观的其余缺陷。在零件使用中镀层必
须牢固粘附于基材上。 
 
3.1.3  The coating shall exhibit the required 
surface protection, with the specified proper-
ties, over the entire surface. The coatings shall 
be free of pores, cracks, foreign matter inclu-
sions, damage and other flaws that impair the 
corrosion protection and/or specified appear-
ance. The layers must adhere firmly to the 
base material when the parts are used ac-
cording to the specifications. 
3.1.4  产品加工及控制过程，不允许损害成品
的使用性能。倘若规定随后要对镀层的构件涂
漆，那么，事先务必让锌粉充分干燥。 
 
3.1.4  The production process and its control 
shall not impair the functional characteristics of 
the finished part. If the coated parts are to be 
subsequently painted, the zinc-dust paint must 
be dried sufficiently beforehand. 
3.1.5  在合理装配情况下，不允许出现会导致
功能受损和/或降低规定腐蚀防护性能的损坏。
 
3.1.5  Given proper mounting, damage must 
not occur that would lead to an impairment of 
function and/or a reduction in the specified 
corrosion protection. 
3.1.6  测试之前，要把零件放在室温下进行144 
h的预处理。 
3.1.6  The parts shall be conditioned for 144 h 
at room temperature before the tests.

3.5 镀层厚度 
3.5  Coating thickness 
按照 DIN EN ISO 2178 或者DIN EN ISO 1463 
进行测试: 
a) Sp--y600：60 μm到110 μm (在粗糙
度峰值>30 μm的范围内)； 
b) Sp--y650：30 μm到50 μm (在粗糙度
峰值>30 μm的范围内)。 
Testing according to DIN EN ISO 2178 or 
DIN EN ISO 1463: 
a) Sp-y600: 60 μm to 110 μm (in the area 
of roughness peaks > 30 μm); 
b) Sp-y650: 30 μm to 50 μm (in the area 
of roughness peaks > 30 μm). 
3.6 成分 
3.6  Composition 
a） Sp--y600：干漆膜中锌粉含量≥60%；
b)  Sp--y650：干漆膜中锌粉含量≥60%，
其中可用Al替代≤7%。 
a) Sp-y600: ≥ 60% zinc dust in the dry 
paint film; 
b) Sp-y650: ≥ 60% zinc dust in the dry 
paint film, whereby up to 7% of that 
can be replaced with Al. 
3.7 附着性 
3.7  Adhesion 
按照 DIN EN ISO 2409，采用划格法进行测
试，特征值不大于1。 
  Crosscut test according to DIN EN ISO 
2409. And the characteristic is no more than 
value 1. 
3.8 耐腐蚀性 
3.8  Corrossion resistance 
按照 DIN EN ISO 7253， 8.5章节的方法划
线，依据ISO 9227-NSS进行盐雾测试。 
Salt spray fog test according to ISO 
9227-NSS with scribing line according to DIN 
EN ISO 7253, Section 8.5. 
3.8.1  Sp-y600 
测试时间：240 h。镀层表面上没有基体金
属腐蚀现象。按照 DIN EN ISO 4628-3的锈蚀程
度：Rio。单边扩散D≤1.5 mm。 
3.8.1  Sp-y600 
Test duration: 240 h. No base metal cor-
rosion on the coated surfaces. corrosion grade 
according to DIN EN ISO 4628-3: Ri0. adhe-
sion loss D ≤ 1.5 mm. 
3.8.2  Sp-y650 
测试时间：120 h。要求参见3.8.1。 
3.8.2  Sp-y650 
Test duration:120 h. For requirements see 
Section 3.8.1.
