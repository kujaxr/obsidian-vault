Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  装配扭矩过大，安装孔被压溃。 
The assembly torque was too large and the mounting hole was crushed. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议Suggestions：1. 调低PAM中的装配扭矩。 
                    Lower the assembly torque in the PAM.
