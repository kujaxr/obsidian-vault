Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  冷凝器边框有多余的焊料堆积，经过打磨处理后管材变薄，充入冷却剂后承受压力而开裂。 
Excess welding material is found in the condenser frame. After grinding, the tube becomes thin and cracks under 
pressure when filled with coolant. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  供应商排查多余焊料堆积边框的原因。 
Supply checks the reason of excess welding material.
