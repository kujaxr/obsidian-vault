Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件受与装配方向不一致的外力导致断裂。 
The fracture of part caused by external force which is different from assembly direction. 
              2. 零件材料不符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商排查失效零件流入下一环节原因并整改。 
                   Supplier identifies the cause of failure parts getting to next link and rectifies. 
                 2．供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
                 Supplier explains the material problem and rectifies.
