Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 该失效件属于氢脆引起的脆性断裂。The failure  part belonging to the brittle fracture caused by hydrogen 
embrittlement 
2、 失效件的化学化成分、硬度符合该零件的技术特性 The chemical composition and hardness with the parts meet the 
technical characteristics. 
建议suggestions 
1、 增加去氢工序。Implement hydrogen removed processes. 
2、 增加去氢检测项目。Implement test items
