Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 缸垫属于多源疲劳开裂，裂纹从缸体向缸盖方向扩展；缸垫上疲劳裂纹的萌生可能与发动机长时间大负荷
下形成的冲击载荷有关。Cylinder gasket was crack by a multi-source fatigue and the crack propagated from the 
cylinder to cylinder head. Crack initiation in cylinder gasket may be related to the impulse load when the engine 
worked under the heavy load for a long time. 
2. 缸垫材料成分中镍含量略微偏高，其他符合要求；硬度符合要求，组织正常。Content of nickel was beyond the 
requirement, others met the requirement. Hardness met the requirement and microstructure was normal. 
3. 缸垫厚度符合要求。Thickness met the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 评估目前试验工况下垫片承受载荷。Evaluate the load of cylinder gasket under current test condition. 
2. 加强开裂部位垫片强度。Strengthen the strength of the gasket at the crack region.
