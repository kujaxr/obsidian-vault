3 试验方法Test method 
 
 
3.1 预处理 
 
3.1  Conditioning  
所有供试验用的样品须经预置处理：23±
5°C室温中放置168h或在60±2°C 烘箱中存放
48 h；进行每个单项试验前须将零件在23±5°C 
中至少放置1h。 
 
All 
specimens 
before 
testing 
shall 
be 
pre-conditioned for 168h at 23 ± 5°C or 48 h at 
60 ± 2°C. The tests are carried out after cooling 
down at 23 ± 5°C for at least 1 hour. 
3.2  试验介质与辅件 
 
3.2  Test media and auxiliary equipment 
试验介质与辅件如下： 
a） 手面霜采用THIERRY PV3964手面霜； 
b） 纯棉脱脂纱布（20支）； 
c） 刀具； 
d） 德莎 4657 型胶带 
e）硬度笔：例如，Erichsen-318 硬度硬
度笔, 探针直径 0.75 mm 
 
Test media and auxiliary equipment are 
described as follows: 
a) Hand and face cream: THIERRY 
PV3964; 
b)  Cotton gauze (20-count); 
c)  Cutting knife; 
d)  TESA 4657 tape; 
e)  Scraper, e.g. ERICHSEN Model 318 
Hardness Test Pencil –with 0.75mm test tip.

3.3  试验过程 
 
3.3  Test procedure 
3.3.1  把纱布带覆盖在样品上，手霜与面霜涂覆
在不同的样品上进行试验。将乳霜涂抹在样品表
面的纱布上，压紧纱布使乳霜均匀的充满纱布中
间的网格空隙，刮除多余的乳霜。 
 
3.3.1 Cover the specimen with gauze. Separate 
specimens are used for hand cream and face 
cream. Apply the cream onto the gauze, press 
the cream against gauze until the cream is evenly 
and fully fills the gaps in the gauze. 
3.3.2  涂抹完乳霜后，将带有纱布的样品放入80 
±2℃循环空气烘箱内存放24 h。存放完成后，揭
掉纱布并擦去表面乳霜。 
 
3.3.2  After cream applying, the specimens 
with gauze are put into an air circulation oven at 
80±2 °C for 24 hours. After aging, the gauze 
need to be removed and the surface of 
specimens with cream need to be cleaned. 
3.4  试验结果评价 
 
3.4  Test evaluation 
3.4.1  气候适应性处理 
 
3.4.1  Acclimatization 
在评价之前，样品须在23±5℃进行 4 h 气
候适应性处理。 
 
A 4h of acclimatization at 23 ± 5°C need to be 
performed before evaluation. 
3.4.2  外观与触感 
 
3.4.2  Vision and haptic characteristics 
允许增加光泽度, 但不允许颜色或手感的变
化 
 
An increase in glossiness is permitted, changes 
of color or haptic characteristics are not 
permitted. 
3.4.3  附着力 
 
3.4.3  Adheision 
按照ISO 2409 进行百格试验；胶带牌号：德
莎4657。要求：特征值≤1。如果受到零件表面结
构或大小的限制无法进行该试验，可以用十字切
割试验替代，要求：无油漆颗粒黏附在胶带上。 
 
Test according to ISO 2409, tape: TESA 4657. 
Requirements: characteristic value ≤1. If limited 
by the specimen structure or size, crosscut test 
can 
be 
used 
to 
replace 
with 
ISO 
2409.Requirement: No paint particles shall 
remain on the adhesive tape after the tape has 
been removed. 
3.4.4  耐刮性能 
 
3.4.4  Scratch resistance  
    用Erichsen-318 硬度笔，10N 负荷，探针直
径为0.75 mm，按照SMTC 5 400 014 进行试验。
要求：涂装层无油漆的开裂现象，但是由载荷造
成的压痕是允许的。 
 
Test e.g. with the Erichsen hardness tester, type 
318, with a load of 10 N and a test needle (point: 
0,75 mm) according to SMTC 5 400 014. 
Requirement: No cracking (material removal) of 
the coating. An indentation mark in the 
substrate material resulting from the load is 
allowed.
