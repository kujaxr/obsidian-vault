Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）润滑不良导致裙部出现严重磨损。The poor lubrication caused serious wear of the skirt. 
（2）积碳可能是导致润滑不良的原因，但是基于来样，还无法确定。Accumulation of carbon is a possible reason for 
the bad lubrication, but based on the sample, it is unable to be certain. 
（3）材料的成分，组织，硬度合格。The chemical composition, microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查喷油是否正常；check if the fuel spray is normal. 
（2）建议用户在可靠的加油站加油，确保油品，减少积碳；Suggest the customer goes to the reliable gas station, ensure 
the gas quality and reduce the carbon. 
（3）建议用户定期做保养，或者更换机油。Suggerst the customer makes maitain regularly, and change the oil.
