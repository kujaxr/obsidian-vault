Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 
1. 
基材性能和电镀层厚度满足标准要求；Δ Tg 为2.9℃，其值比较小，可推断出基材聚合较完全，注塑工
艺没问题。 
2. 
由于设计问题，灯泡腔体为密闭腔，散热性差，导致ABS 基材在高温下变形、膨胀，引起基材开裂和表
面镀铬起皮、起泡。 
Conclusions: 
1. 
The performance of basic material and the thickness of plating layers meet the standard requirement; Delt Tg is 2.8 ℃, 
its value is relatively small. It can be inferred that the polymerization is completed, and injection molding technology is 
no problem. 
2. 
Due to the design problem, the bulb cavity is a airtight cavity, the heat radiation performance is poor, which causes 
ABS substrate at elevated temperature deformation and expansion, finally lead to substrate cracking and chrome 
surface peeling, blistering.

2 . 分析 Analysis 
 
图片2 撕开起皮的镀层后的基材照片 
Fig.2 The picture of basic material after stripping peeling layer 
 
撕开起皮的镀层后，在基材上发现较多的垂直方向的裂纹。一般来说，镀层引起的内应力不足以引起基材开裂。
唯一的解释是基材的应力过大而造成了断裂。镀层起皮起泡的位置巧好为安装后牌照灯的位置(见图1)。我们注意
到此零件属于09 年12 月底整改之前的零件。由于设计问题，灯泡腔体为密闭腔，散热性差，导致ABS 基材在
高温下变形、膨胀，引起基材开裂和表面镀铬起皮、起泡。 
Stripping the peeling plating layer, it is can be found some perpendicular cracks on the substrate. Generally speaking, the 
inner-stress caused by electroplating layers is not sufficient to cause substrate cracking. The only explanation is that the 
excessive stress of substrate leads to failure. The coating peeling, bubbling area is just installation position for license 
plate lamp (see Figure 1). We noticed that this part belongs to the parts before rectification at the end of Dec. 09 year. Due 
to the design problem, the bulb cavity is a airtight cavity, the heat radiation performance is poor, which causes ABS 
substrate at elevated temperature deformation and expansion, finally lead to substrate cracking and chrome surface 
peeling, blistering.
