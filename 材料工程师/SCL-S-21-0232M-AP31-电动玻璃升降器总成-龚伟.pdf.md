Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 零件安装孔处存在注塑孔洞且熔融不良，零件强度低，是导致失效的主要原因。 
The main reason is that there are poor melting and injection holes at the mounting hole.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商优化注塑工艺，消除注塑孔洞&熔融不良问题。 
                  The supplier improves the injection molding process. 
2. 为降低装配故障率，建议进行接头试验。 
                  Make joint test to reduce the assembly failure rate.
