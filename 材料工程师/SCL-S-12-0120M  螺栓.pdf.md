Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 螺栓夹紧力松造成的疲劳断裂。 The bolt has failed due to fatigue fracture due to insufficient or loss of clamping 
force.  
2、 实物质量符合图纸技术要求要求。 The quality for the part meets the requirements of the drawing. 
3、 离合器的滑动轴承组件没有收到。The clutch sliding bearing assembly was not received. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、检查装配、维修过程质量。Check the assembly process & any maintenance conditions  
2、请提供失效件相关所有零件。 Please submit all parts of a failed joint for analysis
