9
Test of Magni/试验
Introduction of  resistance to stress corrosion cracking (bleach) test/
抗应力腐蚀断裂(漂白)试验介绍
Norm/标准: GMW14083
Test/试验: Resistance to stress corrosion cracking in 5.0% sodium hypochlorite (Bleach)/在
5%的次氯酸钠(漂白剂)中浸泡抵抗应力腐蚀断裂的能力
Result/效果: There shall be no evidence of cracks or fracturing of clamps after 28 hrs 
immersion in 5.0% sodium hypochlorite solution/浸泡28小时不产生断裂
Parameter/参数: 
• 4.9-5.1% sodium hypochlorite solution/次氯酸钠溶液浓度4.9-5.1%
• 20-26°C in a vented plastic container/20-26°C的塑料容器中
• 1950-2050ml solution/溶液量
• Clamp should be assembled on the mandrel, the diameter of the mandrel should be 0.3mm 
smaller than the full opened diameter of the clamp/卡箍装配在塑料纤维圆棒上，圆棒的直径
比卡箍最大张开直径小0.3mm
