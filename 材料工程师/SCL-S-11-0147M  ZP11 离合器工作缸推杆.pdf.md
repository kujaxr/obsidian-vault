Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 断口具有疲劳特征。The fracture belongs to fatigue fracture. 
2、推杆杆端严重磨损表明其工作工况不正确，如受力角度不正确。  Heavy wear on the end of the shaft indicates that the working 
condition may be incorrect,such as misalignment. 
3、硬度符合技术要求。The hardness accords with the requriement. 
4、 金相组织不合格。The microstructure doesn’t accord with the requirement 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请进一步分析使用情况。Please check working condition including condition of matching part and installation condition. 
2、 请供应商必须按图纸要求生产零件。Supplier must produce parts according to drawings.
