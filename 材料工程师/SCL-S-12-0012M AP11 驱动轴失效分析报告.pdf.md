Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于扭转过载，驱动轴断裂失效；过载来自于车轮或变速箱。  
2. 材料的成分，硬度和微观组织符合要求。 
3. 变速箱轴承被卡住；保持架由于过载断裂，轴承里没有反常的运行迹象。无法说明哪个先发生。 
1. The driveshaft has failed through torsional overload, from excessive loading at either the wheel or transmission. 
2. The material composition, hardness and microstructure met the requirements of the drawing. 
3. The gearbox-end bearing was stuck, and the cage had fractured through overload, there were no signs of 
abnormal running in the bearing. It’s not possible to say which failure occurred first. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查变速箱运行不正常的迹象Examine the gearbox for signs of abnormal operation.

Bearing 
变速箱边的轴承被拆开。由于这是一个左边传动轴，轴承位置显示：在失效时，变速箱进行前进挡的操作（见图
3）。油脂情况正常的，无污染物。拆开后发现保持架断裂（图4）。断裂发生在钢球被卡住的压痕处。这表明，
保持架断裂为扭转过载的后果，而不一个原因。 
The gearbox side bearing was disassembled.  Since this is a left hand side driveshaft, the bearing position shows that 
the gearbox was moving in a forward gear at the time of the failure (see figure 3).  The grease was in normal condition, 
with no contamination.  After disassembly the cage was found broken (figure 4).  Fractures occured at impressions 
made by the ball bearings during the seizure, and showed overload characteristics only.  This shows that cage fracture 
was a consequence of the torsional overload, not a cause.
