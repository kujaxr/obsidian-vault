3.1.1  外观要求Appearance requirements 
装饰件的表面和内部不得有裂纹和加工缺
陷，如：流痕、空隙、龟裂或类似故障。（只要不
对部件的功能或外观产生不利影响，筋底部和加
强件上允许出现缩痕。） 
 
The surface and interior of the finished parts 
shall be free of flaws and processing defects, 
such as flow lines, voids, cracks or similar 
faults. (Sink marks at the base of ribs and 
reinforcements are acceptable only if they do 
not affect the part’s function or appearance).

3.1.7 光照性能  Color fastness performance 
本要求仅针对于材料应用于内饰产品。试验
按照SMTC 5 400 003，光照周期按照下列规定： 
A 在非阳光直接照射区域中的构件为三个光
照周期； 
B 在阳光直接照射区域中的构件为五个光照
周期（例如：车门胸区）； 
C 阳光强烈照射区域中的构件为十个光照周
期（例如：后搁板） 
 
Color fastness test only for the materials 
used in the interiors products. The test is 
according to SMTC 5 400 003. And the test 
periodicity  defined as  
A 3 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to indirect sun 
radiation; 
B 5 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to direct sun 
radiation (e.g., door upper trim); 
C

of 
exposure 
for 
components in areas subject to the 
highest sun radiation (e.g., rear 
shelf).
