Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件过载导致脆断。2. 失效件注塑工艺不佳。 
      3. 失效件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made part brittle failure. 2. Injection process of part was bad. 
            3. Material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
建议：  1. 工程评估输出端受力要求。 
        2. 改善注塑工艺。 
Suggestions：1.  Mechanical requirements of output side should be evaluated. 
            2.  Injection process should be improved.
