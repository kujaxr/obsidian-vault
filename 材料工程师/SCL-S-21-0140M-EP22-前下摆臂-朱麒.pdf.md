Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 套管开裂由于内壁材料折叠造成。 
The tube cracking occurs because of the inner surface overlap. 
（2） 材料化学成分符合要求，硬度转化抗拉强度符合要求。 
The composition、pull intensity transform which is changed from core hardness are meet the material standard. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 查找折叠原因，并加强零件制造过程管控。 
Find the cause of tube cracking, and enhance the manufacturing quality management.
