Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 尾气中含有较多的Cl、S 导致尾管内侧锈蚀，这主要与使用的油品有关。There are more Cl、S in the tail gas 
which lead to corrosion on the inside of the tailpipe, which is mainly related to the used oil. 
（2） 尾管材料成分合格，金相组织正常。The chemical composition of failed tailpipes are qualified, the 
microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 排查失效车辆使用的油品。Investigation the oil quality used in failed vehicle.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results 
AS23 TT 项目售后报11 例排气管尾管锈蚀；送检其中一例至实验室分析锈蚀原因，失效车辆里程7182km。 
There are 11 corrosion cases of exhaust pipe Tailpipe found in AS23 TT project. One failure part were sent to the 
laboratory to analyze the cause of corrosion, and the mileage of the failed vehicle was 7182 km. 
 
图1 为送检失效件外观，可以知道： 
 
左右尾管锈蚀特征类似（图1a），锈蚀主要发生在尾管内侧、排水孔周围，近焊缝搭接区域也存在腐蚀
特征；但尾管近排气出口的两侧区域锈蚀明显较轻； 
 
尾管外侧（除排水孔及焊缝）基本无锈蚀（图1b）； 
 
因此可以认为尾管锈蚀是自内而外发生的，其腐蚀与外界环境因素无关，而是由发动机工作排出的尾气
所致。 
 
The failure parts were sent to the laboratory as shown in Figure 1, we can found: 
 
The corrosion characteristics of the left and right tailpipe are similar(Fig 1a), the corrosion mainly occurs on the 
inside of tailpipe, around the drainage hole ,and the weld area; but corrosion on both sides of the tailpipe near 
the exhaust outlet is obviously light; 
 
There is almost no corrosion found in the outsides of tailpipe (except drainage holes and welds)（Fig 1b）; 
 
Therefore, it can be considered that the corrosion of the tailpipe occurs from inside to outside, and the corrosion 
caused by external environmental factors can be eliminated .

综合分析   Comprehensive analysis 
由于尾管材料符合要求，同时AS23 项目的整车腐蚀、尾管零件腐蚀试验，都未发现尾管明显锈蚀的情况，
因此可以认为失效与使用材料无关。 
结合失效尾管自内而外的锈蚀特点，认为失效过程如下：尾气排出后，尾气中较多的有害物质Cl、S 在尾管
内侧积聚从而发生腐蚀，尾气主要与油品有关。 
AS23 在国内售后市场从未发现过类似案例，同时泰国AS23 项目长里程试验车的尾管也没有发现类似腐蚀。
因此，建议对失效车辆的使用进行排查，重点关注油品及常去的加油站。

The tailpipe material meets the requirement, at the same time, there is no obvious corrosion found in the whole 
vehicle corrosion of the AS23 and the tailpipe parts corrosion test, so it can be considered that the corrosion is not related 
to the use of materials. 
Combined with the corrosion characteristics of the tailpipe( from inside to outside), it is considered the failure 
process as follows: after the exhaust gas is discharged, more harmful substances(Cl、S) in the tail gas accumulate in the 
inner side of the tailpipe and corrosion occurs. The tail gas is related to the oil quality. 
AS23 have never found a similar case in the domestic after-sales market, and the tailpipe of Thailand AS23 project 
has not found similar corrosion after vehicle long mileage test. Therefore, it is recommended to check the use of failure 
vehicle, focusing on oil quality and the gas stations.
