4  Technical Requirements 
4.1 基本要求 
 
4.1  General requirements 
4.1.1  由各设计开发部门，按照汽车零部件在不
同部位的耐腐蚀载荷要求，进行级别划分；开发
部门和中心实验室根据零件的实际使用情况选用
合适的表面防护工艺；由中心实验室提供技术支
持并承担零件的实验认可工作。 
 
4.1.1 Design and Development Departments 
shall grades according to the anticorrosion 
behaviors 
of 
automobile 
parts 
and 
components at different positions; The 
development 
departments 
and 
center 
laboratory shall select appropriate surface 
protection technology according to the use of 
components; The center laboratory shall 
provide technical support and undertake the 
tests and ratification of the components. 
4.1.2  防腐等级划分： 
轻度防护（L级）：其腐蚀防护只针对于零部
件的运输过程，且装配不外露的零件。 
中度防护（M级）：主要用于安装位置具有防
护要求的汽车内饰零部件或外饰不可见零件。 
重度防护（H级）：其主要用于暴露在车外，
直接受到大气腐蚀的零部件（例如，底盘、发动
机仓靠近热源和底部的零件）；在装配状态下，
（从车的下部）观察，可见的零部件。

接或与其他金属零件焊接，所以要检验它们的可
焊接性。在底盘结构中规定CM40为标准的表面防

The 
parts 
having 
undergone 
surface 
protection 
treatment 
sometimes 
need 
soldering with each other or with other metal 
parts, so their solder ability shall be checked. 
For chassis structure, it is stipulated that 
CM40 shall be the standard form of surface 
protection (for example, soldering screw nut).

4.4  Friction coefficient  
表面处理工艺对紧固件摩擦系数有显著的影
响，应采用SMTC 7 100 013标准进行紧固件摩擦

求。 
 
The surface treatment process has a 
significant impact on the friction coefficient of 
fasteners. SMTC 7 100 013 standard should 
be used to test the friction coefficient of 
fasteners to ensure that the friction coefficient 
of fasteners meets the technical requirements 
of parts.

4.7 锌镀层和锌合金镀层 
 
4.7  Zinc coating and zinc alloy coating 
4.7.1  对于抗拉强度 大于1000MPa 的高强度
钢件和紧固件、硬度大于等于HRC 32 不应使用
电镀的加工工艺，应使用锌薄片涂层工艺。 
 
 
 
4.7.1 For high-strength steel parts and 
fasteners with tensile strength greater than 
1000MPa and hardness greater than or equal 
to HRC 32, electroplating process shall not 
be used, and zinc flake coating process shall 
be used. 
4.7.2  为了改善耐腐蚀特性，电解镀锌和锌合金
涂层的零件可以使用钝化作为后续处理。但是则
不应使用含Cr(VI)的铬酸盐钝化。 
 
 
 
4.7.2 In order to improve anticorrosion 
characteristics, deactivation can be used as 
post treatment for electrolytic zincing and zinc 
alloy coated parts. But containing the Cr (VI) 
not permitted.
