Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  主壳体属于过载断裂，载荷较大+严重的疏松孔是主要原因。Main shell fractured because of overload and main 
failure reason is big load and serious porosity. 
2.  材料成分、硬度满足要求。Chemical components、hardness meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商提高铸件质量。Supply improves the casting quality.
