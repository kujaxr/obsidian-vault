Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions：经X 射线成像可见在失效零件的泄露部位存在铸造缺陷（缩松3.82mm）。By X-ray 
Imaging, we found the failed parts have casting defect（shrinkage porosity 
3.82mm）in the leakage space. 
建议Suggestions：供应商应加强铸造工艺质量控制。The supplier shall strengthen the casting technology’s 
quality control.
