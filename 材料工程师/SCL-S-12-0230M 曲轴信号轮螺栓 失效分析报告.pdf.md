Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 三件螺栓之一由于丢失夹紧力导致发生反向弯曲疲劳断裂。随后其他两件螺栓发生过载断裂，引起曲轴信号
轮失效。One of the returned bolts failed through fatigue due to loss of clamping load. Overload fracture of the remaining 
two bolts then occurred, leading to failure of the trigger wheel.  
2. 疲劳失效螺栓的硬度和金相组织合格。The bolt hardness and microstructure met the requirements. 
3.夹紧力可能会由于曲轴信号轮和曲轴之间的微动磨损而丢失。装配错误的根本原因可以排除。The clamping load 
was potentially lost due to fretting wear between the trigger wheel and crankshaft.  An assembly error as root cause can 
be excluded. 
4. 提交了新的曲轴信号轮片的安装位置的是质量较差。表面凸起和表面腐蚀颗粒可能导致失效件间产生微动磨
损。此外，沉孔处的粗加工，会增加摩擦并降低初始的夹紧力。The mounting points of the submitted new crankshaft 
trigger wheel are poor quality.  Surface protrusions and corrosion could lead to the fretting wear seen in the failure. 
Additionally the rough machining of the countersunk hole would increase friction & reduce initial clamping load. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 改进曲轴信号轮沉孔处的粗加工。Improve quality of trigger wheel countersink machining 
2. 在腐蚀上保护曲轴信号轮。Protect trigger wheel from corrosion

applied load.  This bolt was the first to fail.

3. 曲轴信号轮 – 新件  Trigger wheel – new part 
失效曲轴信号轮没有送回进行分析；在失效期间，遭受完全销毁（图6a）。提交一个新的样品，并表示出安装在
曲轴上如图6b 所示。 
目视检查，发现所钻的孔质量差。在信号轮反侧（接触曲轴）有可见的凸起区域，可能是沉孔钻孔过程中受压的
结果。这些地区是导致产生轻微磨损的直接原因。其次，有表面腐蚀。硬质粒子的氧化铁作为磨料在微动磨损中
增加磨损。第三，沉孔倒角表面的加工质量差。粗糙的表面增加摩擦扭矩拧紧过程，因此所取得夹紧负载低于预
期。 
The failed trigger whell was not returned for analysis, having suffered complete destruction during the failure (figure 6a).  
A new sample was submitted, and is shown as mounted to the crankshaft in figure 6b.  
On visual inspection, the drilled holes were found to be poor quality.  On the reverse side of the wheel (contact to 
crankshaft), there were visible raised areas, presumably a result of pressure during the countersink drilling operation.  
These areas prevent proper seating onto the crankshaft and are the direct cause of the fretting wear.  Secondly, there was 
corrosion present.  Hard particles of iron oxide act as an abrasive during fretting and increase wear.  Thirdly, the 
machining quality of the chamfered surface of the countersink was poor.  The rough surface increases friction during 
torque tightening, and therefore the achieved clamping load is lower than expected.
