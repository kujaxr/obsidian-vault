1 范围 
本标准规定了在金属表面上施涂黑色漆的表
面保护的要求. 
本标准适用于金属表面施涂黑漆的车身部件。
但不适用于镀锌金属表面的涂漆（见SMTC 5 100 
012）、装饰性保护清漆（见SMTC5 100 010）或者
在可能形成空腔的底盘部件（见SMTC 5 100 037）。
1  Scope  
This standard defines requirements for cor-
rosion protection of black paint coatings on metal 
surfaces. 
This standard apply to the body parts with 
black coatings on the metal surface, but does not 
apply to paint coatings on zinc-coated metal sur-
faces (see SMTC 21512); to decorative protective 
varnishes (see SMTC 21511) or to paint coatings 
on chassis parts that may form cavities (see 
SMTC 21547).

3.2  General requirements
Avoidance of hazardous substances according 
to SMTC90008.Eight parts or part sections are re-
quired for complete testing.The coatings used must 
be free of compounds containing chromium (VI) or 
lead.

3.5  预处理 
如果使用自干涂层系统和/或无高温（＞
60 ℃）烘干过程的涂层体系，则零部件应在试
验前于60 ℃烘烤24 h，然后试样根据标准DIN 
50014 冷却至室温。 
3.5  Conditioning 
If air-drying coating systems are used and/or no 
paint system baking process at elevated temperature 
(> 60 °C) will be carried out, the components shall be 
conditioned for 24 h at 60 °C before the tests. The 
Specimens are then cooled to room temperature (18 
to 28) °C according to DIN 50014.
