Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）减振器活塞杆是通过疲劳而断裂于退刀槽的应力集中处。由于所收样品的裂纹源已破坏，因此无法对起源
处进行检测分析。The piston rod of the damper was broken at the undercuts with stress concentration by fatigue. 
Because of the crack source was damaged, it is impossible to check it. 
（2）材料的成分和硬度符合要求，组织正常。The chemical components and hardness meet the requirement; the 
microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核载荷，优化设计，降低应力集中，或提高承载能力。Check the load and optimize the design to reduce the 
stress concentration or improve the load ability. 
（2）用于分析的零件应该是完整的。The failed part for the analysis should be whole.
