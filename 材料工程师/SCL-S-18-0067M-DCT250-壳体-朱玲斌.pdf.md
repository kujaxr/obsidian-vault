Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 壳体多源疲劳开裂失效，疲劳源无明显缺陷。The clutch housing multi-source fatigue fractured, there is no 
defect found in the fatigue source. 
（2） 失效壳体材料成分合格，硬度、金相组织和孔隙正常。The chemical composition of failed clutch housing is 
qualified, the hardness、microstructure and porosity are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核TCU 策略变更对壳体工作应力的影响。Check the effect of TCU policy change on the working stress of 
the clutch housing. 
（2） 排查装配及台架情况。Inspection of assembly and bench conditions.
