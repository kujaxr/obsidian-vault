3 抗大气暴露性Resistance to Open Air Weathering 
3.1 抗大气暴露性的定义。如果在历时一年的
湿热环境试验和一年的干热环境试验过程中未
出现本标准3.2 中描述的缺陷，就可以认为整
车、总成和零部件具有抗大气暴露性。但图样上
或技术规范允许的偏差不属于失效的范畴。 
 
 
3.1 The definitions of resistance to open air 
weathering. Vehicles, assemblies (ASSY) and 
components 
are 
considered 
open-air 
weathering resistant if no detrimental effects 
that refers to section 3.2 are detected during 
testing, after a test duration of one year in humid 
heat and after a test duration of one year in dry 
heat. However, Deviations specified in drawings 
or technical supply specifications are not 
grounds for failure. 
 
3.2 实验中及实验后不允许出现以下缺陷 
a) 材料变化，例如材料开裂,脆化,软化,
粉化,发粘等改变。 
b) 零件轮廓、结构的扭曲,变形。 
c) 安全性缺陷； 
d) 复合材料层与层之间分层,脱开； 
e) 使用常规手段(例如清水冲洗擦拭)无
法消除的小分子物质析出； 
f) 色调变化。目视可见的颜色变化。必
要时可在图纸或供货技术要求中规定
变化公差； 
g) 按照DIN EN 20105-A02 评价灰色牢度
＜4.0 或按照SMTC 5 400 002 评价，
dE≥3.0； 
h) 光泽度变化（经过容许的1 年的大气
暴露后光泽度相对初始光泽度偏差＜
10%）。 
 
3.2  The following change are forbidden during 
and after test: 
 
a) 
Material changes such as cracks, 
brittleness, softening,  pulverization, 
tackiness. 
b) 
changes such as distortion of the total 
structure, warping on contours,.； 
c) 
Defect 
that 
affects 
the 
secure 
performance； 
d) 
Delamination； 
e) 
Exudation that can’t be removed with 
ordinary methods (e.g., car wash).； 
f) 
Hue changing. Color changing that 
can be detected by naked eye. 
Tolerance zone could be specified on 
the drawing or the specification if 
necessary ； 
g) 
The grey Scale ≤4.0 according to 
DIN EN 20105-A02 or dE ≥3.0 
according to SMTC 5 400 002； 
h) 
Changes in degree of glossiness 
(deviation in degree of glossiness <
