This standard specifies the steel grades 、
features 
and 
technology 
required 
for 
hot-formed steel sheet. 
This standard is primarily applicable to 
parts such as door intrusion.

4.2   交货状态 
   钢板及钢带以热轧状态交货。 
 
 
4.2  Consignment state 
    Pre-pressed product is in the form of hot 
rolled sheets. 
4.3   表面处理 
   钢板的表面处理方式可采用酸洗表面和非
酸洗表面两种方式。酸洗表面的钢板及钢带，
可以涂油或不涂油交货；非酸洗表面的钢板采
取机械涂油。 
 
4.3  Surface Treatment  
    Cleaning of the surface, the use of 
non-acidic and acidic surface cleaner is 
permitted. For acidic surface cleaner, the 
product can be lubricated or not lubricated. 
Non-acidic surface cleaner must adopt a 
mechanical lubrication.

5   钢板质量 
 
5  Steel Quality 
5.1   夹杂物 
    采用ASTM E45 Method D，Plate Ⅲ,100X
分析方法，其中夹杂物不能超过等级2.0。 
 
5.1  Inclusions.  
ASTM E45 Method D, Plate III, by 
analysis @ 100X inclusion content shall not 
exceed a rating of 2.0 for thin and 2.0 for 
heavy. 
 
5.2   脱碳层 
表面脱碳层不能超过0.05 mm。 
 
5.2  Surface Decarburization.  
Total (complete) surface decarburization 
exceeding 0.05mm is prohibited . 
 
5.3   表面质量 
 
5.3  Surface Quality 
5.3.1   钢板表面不得有裂纹、结疤、压痕、
气泡和夹杂等对使用有害的缺陷，钢板及钢带
不得有分层。对酸洗表面的钢板不得有停车斑。
 
5.3.1  It is not allowed for the existence of 
surface defects which will be deleterious for 
using such as crack, scar, impression, air 
bubble, inclusions and delamination. For the 
acidic cleansed sheet, speckles are not 
allowed.  
5.3.2   钢板按表面质量分为二级，如表4所
示。 
 
 
5.3.2  Sheet is divided into two grades 
according to surface quality.
