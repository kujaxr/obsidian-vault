Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  变速箱悬置是由于受到冲击发生过载断裂。 
    Transmission suspension fractured because of seriously shock load.   
2. 材料成分、硬度、组织无异常，局部孔隙率偏高。 
Chemical、hardness、porosity and microstructure are normal.

1.  建议售后关注此结构悬置的失效情况。 
    Pay attention to this structure parts failed situation.
