范围 Scope
本标准规定了轿车车厢（含行李箱）和与
车厢（含行李箱）相关联的部件气味性试验的
术语和定义、标示、测试方法和评判标准。
本标准适用于汽车车厢（含行李箱）和与
车厢（含行李箱）相关联的部件中所有可能对
车内气味产生影响的零件和材料。
The Standard stipulates the terminology and 
definition, sign, test method and procedure, 
and judgment standard of odour test of parts 
related 
to 
passenger 
car 
compartm-ent 
(including baggage compartment).
The Standard applies to parts and materials 
among those related to passenger car 
compartment 
(including 
baggage 
compartment) likely to affect the odour in 
vehicles.

术语和定义 Terminology and Definition
气味性——在一定温度和气候下时效处理
后散发出带有可辨别气味的挥发性成分的倾向
理解为气味性。
气味测试——将放在密封容器内的试样在
一定温度下存放一定时间，按照一个标准用鼻
子进行感官评价等级称为气味测试。
Odour property: the potential of a material to 
emit volatile components with distinguishable 
odour after the aging treatment under the 
given temperature and climate is understood 
as odour property.
Odour test: samples are placed in sealed 
vessels under the given temperature for a 
given time, and making sensory evaluation 
with nose according to one standard is called 
odour test.

4.1 测试设备Test Equipment
4.1.1  恒温设备：具有空气循环功能的干燥器
或干燥室，精度等级为2（±2 ℃）。
4.1.1  Thermostatic equipment: drier or drying 
room with air recycling function, with precision 
grade being 2 (±2 ℃).
4.1.2  测试容器：带有无气味的封口和盖子，
且容量为1 L 或3L 的玻璃容器。测试容器在每
一次测试步骤前必须清洗（如在实验室玻璃器
皿超声波清洗机中清洗），容器必须清洁、无气
味。清洗容器的步骤如下：
a)  用无气味的液体清洁剂溶液清洗容器；
b)  用蒸馏水彻底漂洗容器；
c)  将容器放进80 ℃温度的恒温设备（内
部无异味）内干燥30 min；
d)  按照上述相同的步骤清洗容器的盖子。
4.1.2  Test vessel: 1 L or 3L glass vessel with 
odourless seal and cap. The vessel should be 
cleaned before each procedure of test 
(cleaned in glass ware cleaner of the lab). 
Vessel should be clean and odourless. The 
procedures for cleaning vessel are as follows:
a)
Use odourless liquid cleaning agent 
to clean vessel.
b)
Rinse vessels with water thoroughly,  
and sweep water on vessel.
c)
Place vessel in a hot air recycling 
drier (without odour internally) at the 
temperature of 80 ℃ for drying 30 
minutes.
d)
Clean vessel cap acc to the above 
procedures as well.

4.3.7  零件取样位置尽量取零件在车内的暴露

4.3.7  As far as possible, choice the sampling 
position of the parts in the exposed surface 
area of the parts in the car, and the non-
exposed surface area, the back mandrel, card 
holder, screw column and the rib structure 
should be taken less.
4.3.8  对于总成零件中的每种材料需单独测试
气味，如仪表板中的上体、下体、饰条和风道
等需要分别测试气味。但对于复合在一起的材
料不必要分开测试，如仪表板搪塑上体（发泡、
PVC 和PP 骨架）、门板中嵌板及座椅面套等。
对于小的总成零件不必要分材料测试可以整体
测试，如换挡球头。
4.3.8  Each kind of material in assembly parts 
should go through odour test separately, such 
as upper body, lower body, weather strip, and 
vent 
of 
instrument 
panel. 
Materials 
compounded together may not be tested 
separately, such as composite structure upper 
body (PU foam, PVC, and PPframework) of 
instrument panel, panelling in panel seat, chair 
surface cover. Small assembly parts can be 
tested as a whole, such as gear shift. 
4.3.9  若样件切面明显影响零件气味，需将边
缘用铝箔包覆密封。如软包覆件，PVC 搪塑件，
方向盘，密封条等。
4.3.9  If the cut surface of the sample affects 
the smell of the part obviously, the edge needs 
to be sealed with aluminum foil. Such as soft 
coated parts, PVC slush plastic parts, steering 
wheel, sealing strips, etc.
4.3.10  如无特殊要求，测试气味一般采用1 
L 气味瓶。
4.3.10  If no special requirements a 1 L odour 
bottle is normally used.

4.4.3  试验说明 Test explanation
a)
测试容器盖内侧包覆铝箔，以避免瓶盖内
侧的涂层和硅胶影响气味结果
b)
对于方案1 和方案2，1 L 测试容器加入50 
ml 的去离子水，3 L 测试容器加入150 ml
的去离子水。方案3 中的测试容器不加水；
c)
放置在试验容器中的试样，应避免直接与
水接触；
d)
恒温设备加热到规定温度后，将测试容器
密闭并置于恒温设备中；
e)
方案1 和方案2 是将测试容器从恒温设备
中取出后，立即进行测定；
f)
方案3 是将测试容器从恒温设备中取出后，
冷却至测试温度(60±5) ℃后 ，再进行测
定。
g)
测定至少需要3 个检测员。如测定人员评
分结果之间的最大差异在1.5 级及以上，
应至少有5 个检测员重新进行测试。
a)  The inside of the test vessel cap should be 
covered with aluminum foil to prevent the 
coating and silicone on the inside of the bottle 
cap from affecting the odor result;
b)  
For Scheme 1 and 2, 1 L test vessel 
should be used and 50 ml de-ionized water 
added, a 3 L test vessel has 150 ml de-ionized 
water added. For Scheme 3, the test vessel 
has no  water added;
c)  Samples placed in test vessel should not 
be in direct contact with water;
d)  Test vessel should be sealed, and placed 
in preheated drier;
e)  
As for Scheme 1 and 2, test vessel is 
tested immediately after being taken out from 
the drier;
f)  For Scheme 3, test vessel is cooled to the 
temperature of (60±5) °C after removal from 
the drier, and then tested. 
g)  Test should be carried out at least by three 
checkers. If the evaluation results of a tester 
are 1.5 grades different from those of the other 
two, at least five checkers should make the 
test again.
4.4.4  嗅闻Smelling
4.4.4.1  将气味瓶盖一边掀开3 cm-4 cm，测
试人员一个一个地嗅闻。然后，分别将评价等
级填写在原始记录上。每次测试人员的数量应
该为3 个或者3 个以上。当测试人员达5 人以
上时，每个试验应该需要两个或以上的容器和
试件。
4.4.4.1  Slide vessel cap 3 cm - 4 cm to open 
testers smell the odour one by one, and then 
fill the evaluation grade on the original record. 
The number of testers should be three or 
above every time. When there are five testers, 
each test needs two or more vessels 
containing the samples.
4.4.4.2  在嗅闻气味的强烈的样件后应到室外
呼吸一下新鲜空气，然后再嗅闻后续的样件。
4.4.4.2  After smelling samples with strong 
odour, testers should breathe fresh air outdoor, 
and then return to smell subsequent samples.
4.4.4.3  嗅闻气味时，不要将气体吸入肺中，
因为不清楚测试材料中是否含有有害物质。
4.4.4.3  Do not breathe gas into lungs, since it 
is not clear whether test materials contain 
harmful substances.

袋子体积：100L和2000L；
6)

1)

5.3 测试步骤 Procedure
5.3.1 将零件总成置于事先清洗干净的气味袋
中并密封采样袋。一整车量份零件选用2000L
气味袋，单个零件选用100L 气味袋。
5.3.1  Place the assembly part in a cleaned 
odor bag, then seal the bag. A 2000L odor bag 
is used for a complete vehicle's parts, and a 
100L odor bag is used for individual parts.
5.3.2 用真空泵通过控制阀将袋内的空气抽出
再向气味袋中充入50%±10%的洁净空气。
5.3.2  Empty air of the bag through the control 
valve using a vacuum pump. Then fill the bag 
with clean air, the air volume accounts for 
50%±10% of the bag volume.
5.3.3 将采样袋放入烘箱内，开始加热待温度
升至并稳定在65℃后开始计时，2 小时后停止
加热，使烘箱内温度降至40±5℃。
5.3.3  
Place the sampling bag in the oven, 
start heating until the temperature reaches 
65℃, and then start timing. Stop heating after 
2 hours to reduce the temperature inside the 
oven to 40±5℃.
5.3.4 测试员轻拍气味袋使内部气体均匀，然
后打开袋口约3~4cm, 
测试人员一个一个地嗅闻。
然后，分别将评价等级填写在原始记录上。每
次测试人员数量应该为3 个或者3 个以上。
5.3.4  The tester lightly taps the odor bag to 
evenly distribute the internal gas. Slide bag 
mouth 3 cm-4 cm to open testers smell the 
odour one by one, and then fill the evaluation 
grade on the original record. The number of 
testers should be three or above every time.
5.3.5 嗅闻气味时，不要将气体吸入肺中，因
为不清楚测试材料中是否含有有害物质。
5.3.5  Do not breathe gas into lungs, since it 
is not clear whether test materials contain 
harmful substances.
5.3.6 测试完成后取出样件，翻转气味袋，对
气味袋高温老化至无明显气味。
5.3.6  After the test is completed, take out the 
sample and deodorize the odor bag for high 
temperature aging until there is no obvious 
odor.

6  评判标准 Judging standard
6.1  
所有方案的气味评判标准分为1 至6 级
（见表4），对所有材料均可采用半级测定。
如：3.5 级、5.5 级等。
6.1 Odour judging standards of all schemes 
are divided into Grade 1 to 6 (See Table 4), all 
materials can be tested with semi-grade. Such 
as:3.5grade,5.5grade etc.
6.2  参考测试法及所采用的方案说明，采用各
评分结果的算术平均值说明气味特性。评定的
等级如介于两者之间时，以最接近的半级的四
舍五入的平均值对气味性加以说明。
6.2  
Refer to test method and scheme 
explanation, and explain odour features with 
the arithmetic average value of evaluation 
results. If the evaluation grade is between two 
grades, the average value or semi-grades is 
allowed to explain the odour.
6.3  如无特殊说明，样件气味测试时需测试所
有条件下的气味等级，包括常温气味和时效处
理（包含方案1、2 和3）气味等级。
6.3  If no special requirements, testers should 
evaluate odour under all conditions including  
normal temperature and all three of 1, 2 and 3 
aging treatment schemes .
