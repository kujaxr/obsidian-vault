Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件受力超过材料的负荷，导致失效。 
         2. 失效件的材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. Force exceeds the material load，which led tothe failure .             
2. The material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
建议：1. 建议对零件的结构和工作环境进行排查。 
   Suggestions:  1. Propose to check the part structure and work environment.
