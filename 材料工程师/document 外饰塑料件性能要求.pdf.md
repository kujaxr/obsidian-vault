3  要求Requirement
3.1 样品要求Sampling requirements
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成品
上取样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，其样品制
备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室协商确定。为完
成试验，需要多个成品零件。
The test samples stipulated in the Standard. 
Samples should be made from finished 
products. If samples can’t be made from 
finished 
products, 
sample 
preparation 
method should be determined through the 
negotiation with SMPV SCL. Several parts 
are required for complete testing.
3.2 预处理 Conditioning
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准气候
条件23℃,50 %RH下至少放置24 h。
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 24h before test.
3.3 外观要求 Appearance requirements
成品件表面与内部不允许存在加工缺陷，如
熔接痕、缩孔、裂纹及类似缺陷。组件的尺寸及
公差应符合相关工程图纸说明。
The surface and interior of the finished parts 
shall be free of flaws and processing defects, 
such as flow lines, voids, cracks or similar 
faults. The dimensions and tolerances of the 
component shall be as specified on the 
relevant Engineering drawing.
3.4  装配适应性Resistance to Assembly Conditions
零件应能耐运输、储存及后续装配操作等，
如特殊的包装材料、储存环境及装配要求等。
The part shall be capable of resisting 
conditions during transport, storage and 
assembly, such as special packing material, 
storage 
environment 
and 
assembly
