Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件焊接状态不佳导致其强度偏弱，使用中脱开。 
The poor welding state of the failed part leads to weak strength and disconnection in use.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions： 1.  供应商解释工艺异常原因并整改。 
                    Let the supplier explain the causes of process abnormalities and rectify.
