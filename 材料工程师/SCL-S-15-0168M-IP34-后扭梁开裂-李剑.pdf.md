Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 后扭梁属于焊接熔深不足导致早期开裂。This torsion beam was broken off because of the less welding. 
 
建议Suggestion 
1. 供应商应加强焊接工艺控制。Supplier should strengthen the controlling of the welding process.
