Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 散热器支架受到较大的振动载荷发生疲劳断裂。Brake was broken off by fatigue because of a big vibrating load. 
 
2. 材料成分、板厚符合要求；微观组织、硬度正常。Chemical components and plate thickness met the requirement. 
Hardness and microstructure were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.优化结构设计或提高材料强度。Optimize the structure designing or improve the plate strength. 
2.检查试验车其他零件如底盘件是否有失效，其可能会导致振动增加。Make sure other parts such as chassis 
assembly were normal, which improved the vibrating load.
