Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1）下系杆支架由于受到较大的冲击载荷发生过载断裂。 
Bracket fractured because of the impact load. 
 
（2）材料成分和硬度符合要求；孔隙率、组织正常。 
Chemical components and hardness meet the requirements. Microstructure and porosity are normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）排查试验过程，评估零件实际承受载荷。 
Evaluate the actual load in test process.
