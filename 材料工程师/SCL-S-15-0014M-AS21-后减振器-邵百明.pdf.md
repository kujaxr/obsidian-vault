Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）异物卡在油封密封唇处，引起漏油； The foreign matters locked at the oil seal lip, which caused the oil leaking. 
（2）该异物为聚氨酯，是生产过程中混入的。 The foreign maters were PU, which were from the production process. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）加强过程控制，确保系统清洁度。Strengthen the process control and make the cleanliness well.
