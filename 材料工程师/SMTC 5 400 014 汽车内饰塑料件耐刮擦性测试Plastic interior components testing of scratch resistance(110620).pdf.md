1 范围 
    本标准规定了汽车内饰塑料件耐刮擦性试
验测试方法。 
本标准适用于测试汽车内部非涂装塑料零
件（指聚丙烯及其改性材料的着色带皮纹的外
观内饰零件）的耐刮擦性。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies a procedure for testing 
the scratch resistance.  
This test standard serves to determine the 
scratch resistance of unpainted plastic interior com-
ponents-Unpainted plastic interior components are 
defined as: PP and modified PP material, with color 
and with grain, apparent.

3 定义 
把零件耐外力机械作用（例如，锐利边缘
或倒棱物件的刮伤）的阻力定义为塑料件耐刮
擦性。 
3  Definition  
The scratch resistance of plastics is defined as 
the resistance offered by the material to a mechanical 
stress, in this case the scratching motion of a sharp 
edge.

测试方法要求与误差，按照SMPV 和/或认可

5.3  样品要求 
5.3  Sample request 
5.3.1  用于提交认可的样品必须从被测试的
零件中加工获取，表面必须均质，清洁而无油
腻。并且不允许有受污染的痕迹和被触摸。 
5.3.1  The submitted samples must be selected from 
the tested parts with clean homogeneous surface, 
which should not show the imprint of being polluted or 
touched.   
5.3.2  样品大小要确保有足够的面积进行色
差仪测量和可靠的放置在仪器支撑面上刮划。
建议的尺寸至少是80 mm×50 mm，样品厚度最
大不超过20 mm，如果测试部位面积非常小，允
许在不影响测试表面和试验的情况进行镶嵌等
方式，来达到样品的尺寸要求。 
5.3.2  The sample should be big enough to be 
measured by color-difference meter and has proper 
place for scratch during the measurement. The ad-
vised size is bigger than 80 mm×50 mm, and the 
thickness of the sample should be less than 20 mm. If 
the area for measurement is quite small, inlay could 
be used to satisfy the size request, however the sur-
face of the sample and the test results could not be 
influenced.  
5.3.3  取样位置。根据客户或供需双方的约定，
如果没有这些特殊约定，遵循平整和手能够触及
部位的原则。可以是若干零件的相同部位或同一
零件的不同部位。用照片在记录中体现取样部
位，并用文字说明取样部位的颜色、皮纹和可能
影响结果的其它外观情况。 
5.3.3  Measurement position. The measurement
position should be identified according to customer’s 
request or the agreement of supplying and requisi-
tioning parties. If not, the smooth area and easy 
touched area should be selected. The measurement 
positions could be the same position on different 
samples or different positions on the same sample. 
The measurement position should be recorded in 
photos with the notes including the color, surface 
texture and other related surface morphology of the 
measured position.   
5.3.4  对于形状特殊等原因，使获得的样品可
能会影响检测结果的，必须给予记录。 
5.3.4  If the shape of the sample is unusual, this 
might have an effect on the measurement results 
and, it should be recorded on the test report. 
5.3.5   数量。要求每次测试至少取3 个平行样
件，记录每一个样件的测量数值。 
5.3.5 Sample number. The test should be taken on 
at least 3 parallel samples, and each of the mea-
surement results should be recorded.  
5.4   预处理 
试样要在符合ISO 291-23/50-1 要求的标
准气候中存放至少24 h。 
5.4  Conditioning 
    The specimens must be conditioned for a mini-
mum of 24h in a standard climate according to 
ISO291-23/50-1.

5.5.1   读取颜色值（初始） 
在色差仪上读取样品的初始光亮度-L 值，
注意标记测量部位。测量方法按照DIN 5033-4，
色差按照DIN 6174，光种类：D65 / 10°，测
量区域直径：≥7 mm。 
5.5.1 Obtain the initial color value(before scratch)
    Obtain the initial lightness - L value from the 
color-difference meter, and record the measurement 
position. Measurement method is according to DIN 
5033-4. Color difference is according to DIN 6174. 
Light type: D65/10°.Diameter of the measurement 
area: ≥7 mm. 
5.5.2  刮擦 
在刮擦仪上制作至少40 mm×40 mm 的交叉
网格刮痕。参见图3。测试参数： 
a) 负载：F=(10±0.5) N（作用于测试表
面的作用力）； 
b) 刮划速率：V=(1000±50 ) mm/min； 
c) 刮痕线距：2 mm。 
5.5.2
Scratch
The 40 mm x 40 mm cross gril imprint should be 
fabricated using scratch meter as shown in figure 3. 
Measurement parameters: 
a) Force: F=(10±0.5) N (The force acted on the 
measurement surface) ; 
b) Scratch speed: V=(1000±50) mm/min; 
c) Scratch line distance: 2 mm.  
5.5.3  刮擦时的注意事项  
a) 刮擦前必须检查负载是否调整至规定的
要求； 
b) 刮擦前必须检查刮擦头及样品的固定是
否可靠，刮擦头是否在有效使用要求内，
样件固定后是否存在由于受压而使刮擦
面有明显凹或凸的情况，如果有这样的
情况必须给予调整和避免； 
c) 样品的摆放应尽可能的使得刮擦线与皮
纹方向一致，并保证颜色值读取位置在
刮擦范围内，否则必须重新测试； 
d) 刮擦过程中不得随意移动样品位置及刮
擦仪砝码，否则必须重新测试； 
e) 每做完一次刮擦试验需在相应记录中记
录测试数量和刮擦头的编号。 
5.5.3  Notes  
a)  The force should be set as requested before 
scratch; 
b)  Make sure both the scratch tip and sample 
are fixed properly before scratch. The 
scratch tip should follow the application 
standard. If there are obvious sags and 
crests on the measurement surface after the 
sample was fixed, it should be modified; 
c)  The sample should be placed to make sure 
the scratch line and surface texture in the 
same direction, and the color value should 
be obtained from the scratched area. Oth-
erwise, the measurement should be taken 
again; 
d)  In the course of the scratch test, do not 
movie the sample or change load; 
e)  The number of measurement times and I 
dentification number of scratch tip should be 
recorded after each scratch. 
5.5.4  读取颜色值（刮擦后） 
在色差仪上读取刮擦后样件的光亮度L 值，
记录刮擦前后的L 值变化（ΔL 值），需保证读
取颜色终点值测量位置与起始值测量位置一
致。 
5.5.4  Obtain the color value (after scratch) 
The lightness value L for the scratched sample 
should be obtained from the color-difference meter. 
The difference of L (ΔL value) before and after 
scratch should be recorded. Make sure the final and 
initial lightness values are obtained in the same posi-
tion.  
5.6  评估 
至少取3 个单独测量值的平均值作为最终结
果。 
5.6  Calculation 
The results should be the average of at least 3 
isolated measurement values.
