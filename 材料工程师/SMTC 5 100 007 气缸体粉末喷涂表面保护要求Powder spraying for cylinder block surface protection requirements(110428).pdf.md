3.1 基本要求 
按照 SMTC 90008 避免有害物料。 
3.1  General requirements 
Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 90008. 
3.1.1  进行一次完整的检测需要10个成品件。 
3.1.1 A total of 10 finished parts are required 
for complete testing. 
3.1.2  涂层应在整个表面上提供规定的表面保
护，具有固定的特性。涂层不得有孔隙、裂缝、
含有异物、损坏以及其他破坏防腐蚀保护功能和/
或指定外观的缺陷。涂层应牢固地黏着在基底材
料上。 
3.1.2
The Coating shall exhibit the specified 
surface protection, with the specified properties, 
over its entire surface. The coatings shall be free 
of pores, cracks, foreign matter inclusions, 
damage and other flaws that impair the corrosion 
protection and/or specified appearance. The 
layers shall have firm adherence to the base 
material. 
3.1.3  必须设计和控制生产流程，以使制成零件
的功能特性不受损害。 
3.1.3  The production process must be de-
signed and controlled such that functional cha-
racteristics of the finished part are not impaired. 
3.1.4  如果封装正确，涂层不应受到破坏；否则
会损坏功能和/或降低规定的防腐蚀保护功能。 
3.1.4 Given proper mounting, the coating shall 
not be damaged if this would lead to impairment 
of function and/or decrease of the specified 
corrosion protection.
