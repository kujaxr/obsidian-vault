Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 从外观上看，摩擦片与飞轮、压盘长时间摩擦，产生高温，造成摩擦片高温烧伤。In the work process, clutch disc 
assembly rubbed against the flywheel and the platen, resulted in the friction plate high-temperature erosion. 
2. 采用TGA 分析证实了摩擦片材料在使用过程发生了聚合物分子大量热分解。整个摩擦片长期承受超过437℃
温度，表面甚至达到600℃以上。Using the TGA analysis confirmed the friction plate the large number of thermal 
decomposition of polymer molecules. The friction plate was withstand more than 437 ℃ and the surface temperature 
even reach more than 600℃. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查故障车摩擦片长时间异常摩擦的原因。Check the reason that the clutch disc assembly was abnormal friction.
