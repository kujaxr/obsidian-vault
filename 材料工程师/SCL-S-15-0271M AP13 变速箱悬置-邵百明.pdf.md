Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）铆钉是由于受到异常载荷而发生过载断裂。The rivet was broken due to abnormal overload. 
（2）材料的成分和孔隙符合技术要求；组织和硬度正常。The chemical components and porosity meet the 
requirements; the microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）关注生产和物流运输环节，避免承受异常的载荷或磕碰。Pay attention to the production and logistics and avoid 
the abnormal load and bump.
