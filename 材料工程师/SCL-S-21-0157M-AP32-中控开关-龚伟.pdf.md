Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件装配后应力偏大。 
The stress of parts is too large after assembly. 
2. 零件材料受香水等有机溶液腐蚀后开裂。 
    Part materials were cracked after corrosion by organic solvents such as perfume.   
3. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议Suggestions：1. 优化零件结构&生产工艺，降低零件应力，避免零件被香水等溶液腐蚀开裂。 
                   Optimize parts structure & production process, reduce parts stress, avoid parts being corroded and 
cracked by perfumes or other solutions.
