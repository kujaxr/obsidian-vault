Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 半轴齿过载断裂失效，掉落的断齿冲击变速箱壳体，导致壳体破裂。The differential gear was broken by 
overload, and the falling off gear impacted the transmission housing, which caused the housing to crack.  
（2） 失效半轴齿成分、硬度和硬化层深度合格，组织正常。The chemical compositions 、hardness and the hardened 
layer depth of differential gear are qualified, the microstructure is normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 调查客户车辆的使用过程。To investigate the use of the customer vehicles.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
泰国客户在驾驶MG3 去加油站过程中，突然听到车辆有异响，停车后检查发现变速箱漏油（车辆总里程：
10146km）。送至经销商处拆解发现变速箱壳体局部位置开裂，半轴齿轮有一个齿断裂。 
 
An abnormal sound was heard when the Thailand customers was driving MG3 to go to the gas station. It was 
found that the transmission housing was leaking oil(total mileage of the vehicle: 10146km). The dealer dismantled the 
transmission and found that local position of the transmission housing fractured and a tooth of the differential gear 
fractured. 
 
送至实验室失效零件如图1a 所示，变速箱壳体断裂形貌如图1b~1d，由图可知： 
 
壳体内部有两处可见的断裂特征，局部位置还有明显的磕碰痕迹（图1b）； 
 
断口1 和断口2 的断裂方向如图1c，1d 中黄色箭头所示，都是由壳体自内而外； 
 
结合后续半轴齿的断裂分析，认为半轴齿失效后，掉落的断齿与壳体发生撞击，导致壳体发生自内而
外的断裂； 
 
壳体外侧还存在一处断裂，据了解为拆解时破坏，故不做分析。 
   
The failed part sent to the QA lab is shown in Figure 1a, the transmission crack morphology is shown in Figure 
1b~1d, from the Figure it can be known: 
 
There are two visible fracture characteristics and some bump signs exist inside the housing(Figure 1b); 
 
The fracture direction of the fracture 1 and fracture 2 are shown by yellow arrow in Figure 1c and 1d, which 
shows that the housing was broken from inside to outside; 
 
Combined with subsequent analysis of the differential gear, it can be thought that the falling broken tooth 
impacted the housing after the differential gear failure, which caused the housing fracture from inside to 
outside; 
 
There is another fracture in the housing which was broken during dismantling, so it doesn’t need to be 
analyzed.

断口分析   Fracture Analysis 
 
对半轴齿断口进行扫描电镜分析，如图3，由图可知： 
 
半轴齿过载断裂失效。断裂起始于齿表面，断裂方向如图3a 中黄色箭头所示； 
 
位置1 断裂特征为沿晶+少量韧窝，位置2 断裂特征为韧窝； 
 
断裂起始一侧未见明显缺陷； 
 
此前AP11，AP14 该零件均进行极限工况试验时出现过类似断裂。 
 
The failed tooth was analyzed by SEM as shown in Figure 3, from which it can be known: 
 
The differential gear was broken by overload. The fracture direction is shown in yellow arrow in Figure 3a; 
 
The fracture characteristics of the position 1 is intergranular and little dimple and the fracture characteristics of 
the position 2 is dimple; 
 
No obvious defects were found in the fracture initiation side; 
 
A similar failures hanppened in AP11 and AP14 Item druing the limiting condition test.
