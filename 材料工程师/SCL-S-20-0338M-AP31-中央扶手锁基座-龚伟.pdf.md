Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件材料不符合技术要求，性能弱，扶手盖开启过程中，锁基座卡脚断裂。 
The performance of failure part material didn’t conform to requirement, with weak performance, 
resulting in the fracture of the lock base clamp during the opening of the armrest cover. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  责令供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
Order the supplier to explain and rectify the material problem.
