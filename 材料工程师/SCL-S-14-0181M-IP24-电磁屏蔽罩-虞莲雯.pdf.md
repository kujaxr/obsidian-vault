Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：失效零件不符合图纸要求，表面没有镀层；此外，后送的批次零件表面虽然有镀层，但是镀层厚度不
符合图纸要求（图纸要求为镀Zi-Ni 合金，三价铬钝化，镀层厚度为8±2μm.） 
建议：该供应商的零件需要加强管理，避免存在零件漏镀或镀层不合格的情况。 
Conclusion: The failure part had no e-plated layer on the surface. In addtion, the casual inspection part 
provivded by SQE also was unqualified to the drawing requirement which saying Zi-Ni plating,Cr3+passivation, 
layer thickness 8±2μm. 
Suggestion: The supplier need to be inspected tightly in order to avoid parts from missing e-plating or layer 
unqualified to the drawing.

Ⅴ. 分析  Analysis 
 
 
图2 失效件背面 测试问题

也没有电镀锌层。该零件在整个生产过程中缺失了电镀该工序。
