Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 簧片是由于在外力作用下发生过度弯曲变形而引起韧性断裂。  
The leaf spring was broken by ductile fracture because it was bended excessively under external force, 
（2） 失效簧片和同批次簧片的硬度相当，均符合技术要求；其组织相同。 
The hardness of the failed part is same to the same batch of new part and meets the requirement; their 
microstructures are same. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 关注整个零件的生产过程和装配过程，避免簧片过度变形而引起断裂。 
Pay attention to the whole production process and assembly process; avoid the excessive deformation.
