3.1 样品和预处理要求 Sampling and conditioning requirements 
3.1.1 样品要求 Sampling requirements 
 
 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成品
上取样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，其样品制
备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室协商确定。为完成
试验，需要多个成品零件。 
 
The test samples stipulated in the Standard. 
Samples 
should 
be 
made 
from 
finished 
products. If samples can’t be made from finished 
products, sample preparation method should be 
determined through the negotiation with SMPV 
SCL. Several parts are required for complete 
testing. 
3.1.2 预处理 Conditioning 
  
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准气候条
件23℃,50 %RH下至少放置24 h。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at least 
24h before test.

3.2.4 耐温度交变性能 Weathering performance 
零件在工装状态下，按照SMTC 5 400 005-A
执行，若图纸或认可无其它规定，50周期。要求：
表面无缺陷，不发粘，功能无失效。 
 
The test is conducted and fulfilled on the 
finished part which assembles in tooling. The 
weathering cycle is 50 unless stated in the 
drawing or other release according to SMTC 5 
400 005-A. Requirement：no surface defects, no 
stick, function without failure. 
3.2.5 高温性能 Elevated-temperature behavior 
   零件高温性能：零件在工装状态下，若图纸或
认可无其它规定，则采用90℃。时间：500h。要
求：表面无缺陷，不发粘，功能无失效。 
 
Parts：The test is conducted and fulfilled on the 
finished part which assembles in tooling. 
Temperature unless stated in the drawing or 
release is 90°C. Duration: 500h. Requirement：
no surface defects, no stick, function without 
failure. 
3.2.6 低温性能Low-temperature behavior 
零件低温性能：零件在工装状态下，若图纸
或认可无其它规定，则采用-40℃。时间：24h。
要求：表面无缺陷，功能无失效。 
 
Parts：The test is conducted and fulfilled on the 
finished part which assembles in tooling. 
Temperature unless stated in the drawing or 
release is -40°C. Duration: 24h. Requirement：
no surface defects, function without failure.
