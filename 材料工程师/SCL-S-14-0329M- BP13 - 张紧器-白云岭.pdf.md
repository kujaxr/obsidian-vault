Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于夹紧力丢失引起的疲劳断裂。 
The failure is fatigue fracture ,due to the clamping force was loss. 
2、失效件的材料性能未见异常。 
The material properties of the part were nomal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 建议系统排查螺栓夹紧力丢失的具体原因，如维修、装配等。 
Recommended System bolt clamping force investigation and the specific reasons for the missing, such as 
maintenance and assembly.
