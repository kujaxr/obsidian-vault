Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 由于感应淬火工艺不当，造成淬火进行组织不合格，导致齿圈断裂失效。 
          The induction hardening process was incorrect, resulting in poor microstructure after quenching, leading to ring fracture failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请供应商自查热处理工艺及过程控制。 
Supplier must check heat treatment Technology and process control.
