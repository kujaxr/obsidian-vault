Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  供应商生产过程中添加回料，导致材料不符合技术要求，材料降解严重，现场装配失效。 
Suppliers add returning materials during production process, leading to performance of material 
didn’t conform to requirement, with serious material degradation, resulting in assembly failure. 
建议Suggestions：1.  禁用回料。 
                    Disable returning material. 
2.   停机后螺杆中残料不可再用。 
                    Residual material in the screw can’t be reused after shutdown. 
3.   改用小螺杆注塑机，避免材料降解。 
                    Switch to a small screw injection molding machine to avoid material degradation. 
               4.   注塑件增加时间钟，方便排查失效批次。 
                    Increase the time clock in injection molded parts for checking failed batches.
