Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于存在原始裂纹而造成的断裂，原始裂纹产生于电泳漆之前。 
The failure is due to the presence of cracks caused by the original, the original cracks before electrophoretic paint. 
2、失效件的材料性能未见异常。 
The material properties of the part were nomal.  
建议Suggestions： 
1、 停用、隔离此批零件。 
Isolated and stop this batch parts. 
2、 建议加强过程质量控制(包括原材料)。 
Strengthen control process quality (including raw materials).
