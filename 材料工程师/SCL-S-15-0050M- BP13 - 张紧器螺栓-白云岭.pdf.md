Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于夹紧力不足引起的疲劳断裂。Failure is fatigue fracture due to insufficient clamping force. 
2、 螺栓性能符合图纸技术要求。The material properties meet to the drawing requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请进一步分析螺栓夹紧力不足的原因。 
Please further analysis of bolt clamping force is insufficient.
