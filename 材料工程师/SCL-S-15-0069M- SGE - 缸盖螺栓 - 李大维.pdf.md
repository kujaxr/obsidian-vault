Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 缸盖螺栓是由于摩擦系数低造成预装配时过载断裂。这可能与其表面含有过多的油有关。The cylinder 
head bolt was broken by overload during assembly due to low friction coefficient, maybe which is 
related with a great deal of oil on the surface。 
2、 螺栓的材料符合技术要求；摩擦系数低于标准要求。The Material properties of the bolt meet the 
technical requirements. The friction coefficient is lower than the standard requirement。 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离此批零件。Isolate the batch of the parts.  
2、请SQE 控制供应商实物质量。Please SQE supplier control the quality. 
3、校核装配工艺的适用性。Check the applicability of assembly technique.
