Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  故障零件被有机溶剂严重腐蚀。 
The faulty part was seriously corroded by organic solvents. 
建议Suggestions：1.  排查各环节使用有机溶剂的风险，避免失效再次发生。 
Check the risk of using organic solvent in each process to avoid failure again.
