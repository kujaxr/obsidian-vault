Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1）双头螺柱属于氢脆断裂。Double-screw bolt fractured though hydrogen brittleness. 
（2）零件硬度高于要求，组织不符合要求，成分符合40Cr 要求。Hardness is high and microstructure doesn’t meet the 
requirements. Chemical components meet the requirements. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 调查热处理工艺，确保样件质量。Check the heat treatment process to make sure the quality.
