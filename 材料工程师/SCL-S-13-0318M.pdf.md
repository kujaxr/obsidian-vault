Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属疲劳断裂。Failure of the parts are fatigue fracture. 
2、 失效件的板材厚度不符合技术要求，实测结果为0.91mm(技术要求：t=1.0±0.06 mm)  The plate thickness of 
failure parts do not meet the technical requirements, the measured result is 0.91mm (Technical requirements: t = 
1.0 ± mm). 
3、 材料性能（硬度、金相组织）未见异常。Material properties (hardness and microstructure)were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核失效件在实际工况中的受力情况。 
Please check the force for the failure parts during the actual failure conditions. 
2、 供应商使用符合技术要求板厚的原材料。 
Supplier use the raw materials that meet the technical requirements of thickness
