Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 两个失效件是由于受到过载冲击导致断裂。 Failure of both parts was through overload. 
2、 裂缝和变形的方向是与车轮碰撞方向相符的。The direction of the fractures and deformation is consistent with 
a wheel strike from the front. 
3、 硬度、金相组织未发现明显异常。No obvious abnormality was found for the hardness and microstructure.
