Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 局部应力高引发尾灯安装支架疲劳失效。 
Taillight’s mounting bracket was cracking by fatigue because of big local stress.  
2. 涂胶部位固化前已被拉开，这可能会导致局部应力发生变化。 
   Glue has been pulled apart before curing, which may change the local stress.  
3. 材料成分、板厚及减薄率满足要求，强度及组织均正常。 
Chemical、thickness and thinning rate meet the requirements. Strength and microstructure are normal.  
建议Suggestions： 
1.  评估涂胶部位被拉开对失效部位应力的影响。 
    Evaluate the effect of the glue being pulled apart at the failed location. 
2.  排查涂胶固化前被拉开的影响因素，并优化涂胶工艺。 
Check the factors affecting the painting before curing and optimize the coating process.
