1  Scope  
This standard specifies requirements for 
Cr(VI)-free passivated zinc-iron coatings on 
ferrous materials and steel parts with a tensile 
strength of Rm≤1,000 MPa. 
This standard is adopted to evaluate the 
Cr(VI)-free passivated zinc-iron coatings on 
ferrous materials and steel parts.

3.1.1  这些低合金的锌铁镀层应更适合用于短
暂时期中等防腐荷载且工作温度＞120 ℃的钢
铁部件。它们不适合用于连接组件。连接组件上
的电镀镀层应使用SMTC 5 100 003的表面保护类
型。 
 
3.1.1  These low-alloy zinc-iron coatings shall 
preferably be used for steel parts that are 
subject to medium corrosive loads and thermal 
loads>120 °C for brief periods only. They shall 
not be used for joining elements. The surface 
protection types according to SMTC 5 100 003 
shall be used for electroplated coatings on 
joining elements. 
3.1.2  拉伸强度Rm＞1 000 MPa或表面硬度＞
320 HV 的高强度钢铁部件不允许采用锌铁合金
镀层。 
 
3.1.2  High-strength steel parts with a tensile 
strength of Rm>1,000 MPa or a surface hard-
ness of >320 HV must not be coated with 
these alloy coatings.

3.2.6
The protective coatings must not exhi-
bit any pores, cracks, damage, or other flaws 
that impair the corrosion protection or specified 
appearance. The coatings shall firmly adhere 
to the base material; there shall be no flaking 
or cracks of the coating under elastic deforma-
tion. 
3.2.7  在正确的装配条件下，不能有影响功能
或降低耐蚀性的涂层损坏现象。 
3.2.7  Given proper mounting, no coating 
damage shall occur that would lead to im-
pairment of function or decrease of the speci-
fied corrosion protection.

be conducted so that damage by delayed hy-

DIN 50969 in the first sample test report.
