Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 左前基座滑阀是由于锁芯装配不到位，导致螺栓拧紧时，受力异常而发生过载断裂。 
The front left foundation bed slide valve is the overload fracture caused by improper assembly. 
2. 2#件左断裂面处的孔隙率偏大，为9.6%，但不是引起失效的原因；失效件其余断裂面处的孔隙率均在
1.1%~3.0%间；最大单孔尺寸小于1mm。The porosity of the left fracture surface of 2# is relatively large, which is 
9.6%, but not the cause of the failure; the porosity of the rest fracture surfaces of the failed pieces is between 1.1% 
and 3.0%; the maximum single hole size is less than 1mm. 
3. 所有失效件的硬度正常。The hardness of all failed parts is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
关注锁芯的安装过程，保证装配到位。Pay attention to the installation process of the lock cylinder to ensure the 
assembly in place. 
2． 定义滑阀的孔隙率要求，以促进供应商的过程管控。Define porosity requirements for spool valves to 
facilitate supplier process control.

所示。在整个过程中，基座滑阀受到螺栓和阀芯相反方向的推力。
