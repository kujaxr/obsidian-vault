Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  涡轮增压器蜗壳在震动载荷和热应力共同作用下于应力较大的区域发生疲劳断裂。 
   Fatigue Fracture of Turbocharger Spiral Case under Vibration Load and Thermal Stress in Higher Stress Zone. 
2.  材料的成分和硬度符合要求；孔隙和组织正常。 
   The composition and hardness of the materials meet the requirements, and the pores and tissues are normal. 
 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化局部结构，降低应力。Optimize local structure and reduce stress. 
2.  关注高应力区的铸造质量。Focus on casting quality in high stress zone
