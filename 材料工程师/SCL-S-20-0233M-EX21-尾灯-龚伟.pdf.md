Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 此失效件灯罩注塑工艺异常，产生分层现象。 
The lampshade injection process of this failure part was abnormal, causing delamination.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  供应商对工艺问题做出解释并整改。 
                    Let the supplier explain the craft problem and rectify.
