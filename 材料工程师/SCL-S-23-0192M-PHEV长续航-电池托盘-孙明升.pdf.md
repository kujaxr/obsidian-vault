Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该电池托盘开裂是由于局部铸造缺陷-夹渣导致的疲劳开裂。 
The battery tray occurred fatigue crack due to local initial casting defects-slag.  
 
建议Suggestions：  
 
（1）加强铸造工艺管控。 
Strengthen the control of casting processes.
