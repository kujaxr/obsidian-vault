Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致断裂。2. 失效件存在虚焊，部分焊接部位熔深不足，但这不是失效的主要原因。  
3. 零件材料符合技术要求 
Conclusions: 1. The failure part fractured by external force. 
2. There is false welding in the failure part, and part of the welding position penetration is shallow, but it’s 
not the main reason.               3. Material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 检查工况异常。                 2. 供应商检查焊接工艺并整改。 
Suggestions: 1. Check the abnormal conditions in operating mode. 
2. Let the supplier check the welding process and rectify.
