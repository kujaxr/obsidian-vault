Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions

结论：防尘罩材料符合要求。

      The material of the cover is ok. 

      防尘罩受到石子等杂质刺穿后导致破裂。

      The broken dust cover is caused by the impact of the rock or other rigid stuff 

建议：零件在使用过程中避免受到杂质冲击。

Suggestions：the dust cover should avoid the impurity impact during the use.

III. 测试结果 Test Results 

1材料性能测试（Material Test）：

      从测试结果来看，防尘罩CR的材料性能可满足要求。

      Based on the test result, the value is acceptable.

2 断口分析（Analyze）：

裂纹外侧：在裂纹上端有一个孔洞，在裂纹中间区域有孔洞，在孔洞周围发现镶嵌石子等杂物。

Outside of the crack: we found the holes at the upper and middle area of the crack. There are many impurities like rock around the hole in the middle.

裂纹内侧：发现有刺穿孔洞。

The inside of the crack: we found a hole in the middle.

孔洞裂纹断口分析

Analyze of the hole

由于孔洞外侧呈现处明显的凹陷，而内侧没有，可以认为孔洞的形成由外向内刺穿。

Because the outside of the hole shows significant depression and the inside of the hole does not present such phenomenon, we conclude that the rapture hole is caused from outside to the inside.

小结：

  防尘罩材料符合要求。

  The material of the cover is ok.

 从孔洞的断口分析，孔洞是由外向内刺穿形成，结合在孔洞的周围发现的杂质，可推断为防尘罩受到石子等杂质冲击。

Based on the result of the analyze, the rapture hole is caused from outside to inside. We also found the impurity around the hole. So we can conclude that the rapture hole is caused by the impact of the rock or similar rigid impurities.
