Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力超负荷导致断裂。 
       2. 失效件材料含有异常成分。 
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made failure part fracture. 
2. There was abnormal component in the failure part.        
建议： 1. 责令供应商对材料问题解释并整改。 
       2. 对零件失效位置的强度进行评估。 
Suggestions: 1. Let supplier explain and rectify the problem. 
           2. The mechanical of the failure position should be evaluated.
