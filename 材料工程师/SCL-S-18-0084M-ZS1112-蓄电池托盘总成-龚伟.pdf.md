Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件材料不符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.   
2．零件模压工艺不佳，熔接痕位置强度较低，导致零件开裂。 
Parts were cracked because of poor molding process, weak strength of weld line. 
建议Suggestions：1. 责令供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
                   Let the suppliers explain the material problem and rectify. 
                2．优化模压工艺。 
                   Optimize molding process.
