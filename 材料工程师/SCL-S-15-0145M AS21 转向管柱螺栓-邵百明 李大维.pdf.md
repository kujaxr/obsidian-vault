Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）螺栓是由于受到较大的载荷而发生缩颈和韧性断裂；螺栓断裂面未见材料缺陷。The bolts were necking and 
broken because of a big loader. No defect was found on the fracture surface. 
（2）失效螺栓的成分，硬度，组织符合要求；螺牙表面无脱碳和缺陷。The components, hardness and microstructure 
meet the requirements and no decarburization and defects were on the thread surface. 
（3）同批次螺栓的摩擦系数和强度符合要求。The friction coefficient and the strength of the same batch bolts meet the 
requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查转向中间轴螺栓孔的表面状态。Check the bolt surface state of intermediate steering shaft 
（2）评估装配工艺。Evaluate the matching process.
