Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
后悬置橡胶主簧Body Rubber： 
1. 后悬置橡胶开裂区域由于受力撞击导致开裂，并向四周扩展。Body rubber was impact and cracked at the cracking region 
then the crack propagated to the surrounding zone.  
2. 后悬置应变集中区域由于受力已产生细微疲劳裂纹。Tiny fatigue cracks were observed at the strain concentration zone. 
3. 失效件Shore A 硬度较新件大6 度，拉伸强度及断裂伸长率较新件有所降低，材料发生老化。Shore A hardness of 
the failed parts was 6 degree more than the new one. Tensile strength and elongation at tear was low. The material was aging. 
后悬置金属支架Mount Rerar Bracket： 
1. 后悬置支架属于过载断裂。The bracket was broken off because of the overload. 
2. 支架成分、硬度符合要求，金相变质正常。Chemical components and hardness met the requirement. Microstructure was normal. 
3. 支架孔隙率、最大孔尺寸符合要求。Porosity and max gas dimension met the requirement. 
综上所述： 
变速箱后悬置是由于受到异常的外力载荷作用导致其发生失效。Mount asm-trans rr was failed because of abnormal load. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核零件使用工况及载荷。Check the working condition of the failed parts and the load.
