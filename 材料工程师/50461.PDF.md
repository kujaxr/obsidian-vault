PRINCIPLE
Method consists of checking detachment of painted coating after being cross hatched or scriber.
2.1
PROCEDURE
2.1.1
Carry out test at 23 ± 2 ˚C.
2.1.2
Hatch the covered surface in a significant area (flat and visible) of the specimen to be tested or painted
sheet, acting a continuous pressure on cross hatcher (ref. § A---1) up to the incisions reaches the plastic
or metallic support. For covers very hard, use a scriber (ref. § A---2).
NOTE: Adhesion determining of coating on plastic materials, test shall be carried out at 23 ± 2 ˚C at least
96 h after complete polymerization, unless otherwise specified.
2.1.3
Apply adhesive tape on etched surface (ref. § A---3).
2.1.4
Rapidly detach by hand the tape from hatched surface: operation shall be performed applying a force
from bottom to top.

INTERPRETATION OF RESULTS
Assess adhesion checking the compliance with P.S of specific product or coating under test.
In addition to the % adhesion loss (in general, a 10% max. adhesion loss after ageing is allowed) the
ratings shown in the following table is used (to be use for comparison among more samples/specimens
coated with alternative cycles).
