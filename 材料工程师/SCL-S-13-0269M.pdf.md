Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于原材料存在表面裂纹而后弯曲成形时产生了横向裂纹，在装配现场装配时引起了断裂。 
Failure piece surface cracks due to the presence of raw materials and then bending transverse cracks generated 
when the assembly site assembly caused the fracture. 
2、 零件的材料化学成分不符合技术要求，但不是引起此次失效的原因。 
The material chemical composition for the part does not meet the technical requirements, but not the cause of 
this failure causes. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 停用此批次零件，无损探伤合格后方可使用。 
       Stop use this batch parts，NDT qualified before use 
     2、检查原材料质量：如冷拔改制的过程质量，原材料的化学成分控制。 
Inspection of the raw materials: such as cold to the restructuring, the chemical composition.
