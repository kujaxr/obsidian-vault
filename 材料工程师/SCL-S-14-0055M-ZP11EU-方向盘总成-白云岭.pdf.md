Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效件是由于外力过载引起的断裂，可能是试验后的零件混入所致。Failure for the part due to overload by an external force,that may be 
caused by mixing parts after the test 
2、失效件零件的化学成分符合技术要求，硬度未见明显异常。The chemical composition of parts met the technical requirements and hardness no 
obvious abnormalities. 
3、 英国寄回件的其中一件内部缺陷不符合铸件供应商的企业标准，但上海汽车和延锋对此技术要求不明确。Internal 
defects sampling casting parts supplier does not comply with corporate standards, but the technical requirements of SAIC and YAN FENG are not clear. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强、完善试验后零件管理。Itrengthen and improve management after the test parts. 
2、明确零件内部缺陷的技术要求及检测方法。 To clearly define the technical requirements and test methods of internal defects.
