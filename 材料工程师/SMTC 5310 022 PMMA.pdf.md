1  范围Scope 
本标准规定了PMMA（有机玻璃）成品件材料
要求，如制动车、转向灯、尾灯、停车灯和备用
灯的配光镜、组合仪表外罩等的材料要求。 
本标准适用于PMMA 材料的成品件要求。 
 
This 
standard 
specifies 
the 
material 
requirements 
for 
PMMA(Polymethyl 
methacrylate), e.g. for the lens of stop lamps, 
turn signal lights, taillights, parking lamps  
and back-up lights, combination instrument, etc. 
This 
standard 
applies 
to 
the 
material 
requirements for PMMA.

3  要求Requirements 
3.1 样品和预处理要求Sampling and conditioning requirements 
3.1.1 样品要求Sampling requirements 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成品
上取样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，其样品制
备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室协商确定。为完成
试验，需要多个成品零件。 
 
The test samples stipulated in the Standard. 
Samples should be made from finished 
products. If samples can’t be made from 
finished products, sample preparation method 
should be determined through the negotiation 
with SMPV SCL. Several parts are required for 
complete testing. 
3.1.2 预处理Conditioning 
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准气候条
件23℃，50%RH下至少放置48 h。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C, 50%RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 48h before test.. 
3.2 基本要求General requirements 
3.2.1 外观Appearance 
    成品件不允许存在加工缺陷，如熔接痕、缩
孔、裂纹及类似缺陷。 
 
The finished parts shall be free of flaws and 
processing defects, such as flow lines, voids, 
cracks or similar faults.

3.2.11.1 对于车灯配光镜，在室温下，将测试零
件在90%的乙醇溶液中浸泡15min后取出，冲洗后
静置30min，然后评价。要求：没有应力开裂。 
 
3.2.11.1 For lights’ Lens, the part be immersed 
in 90% ethanol for 15mins, and evaluated after 
washed 
and 
30min 
standing 
in 
room 
temperature. Requirement: no tension cracks. 
3.2.11.2 对于组合仪表类零件，在室温下，将测
试零件在50%的乙醇溶液中浸泡15min后取出，冲
洗后静置30min，然后评价。要求：没有应力开裂。 
 
3.2.11.2 For Instrument Cluster, the part be 
immersed in 50% ethanol for 15mins, and 
evaluated after washed and 30min standing in 
room temperature. Requirement: no tension 
cracks.
