Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 放油塞为过载断裂。The bolt was overload fracture. 
2、 失效件化学成分和硬度符合图纸要求。The chemical composition and hardness of the failed bolt meet the drawing. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、排查装配过程是否有异常。Check if there are any abnormalities during the assembly process. 
2、优化成型工艺，适当调整R角处变形。Optimize the forming process and appropriately adjust deformation 
at the R corner.
