前言 
本标准规定了汽车玻璃用单组份粘结密封
胶的产品标准。 
本标准是对SMTC 9 610 002—2013（V1）《用
于无边框玻璃单组份胶的密封系统》的修订,自
本标准实施之日起废除SMTC 9 610 002—2013
（V1）。 
本标准与SMTC 9 610 002—2013（V1）相
比主要变化如下： 
——更新了标准的中文名称，由《用于无边
框玻璃单组份胶的密封系统》更改为《汽车玻璃
用单组份粘结密封胶》 
——更新了规范性引用文件，删除了标准
SMTC 5 400 004 
——更新了表1 材料要求，密封胶的流动性
试验方法由附录E 改为SMTC 6 210 011，密度
的试验方法由附录F 改为SMTC 6 330 044。 
——更新了表2 性能要求，明确了50 个环
境循环的要求，删除了SMTC 5 400 004 的试验
方法要求。 
——附录A、附录B、附录C 和附录D 为规
范性附录，删除了附录E 和附录F。 
请注意本标准的某些内容可能涉及专利，上
汽集团不承担识别这些专利的责任。

1   范围 
 
1  Scope  
本标准规定了对单组份粘结密封胶系统的
性能要求，该系统适用于对汽车玻璃的固定和
防水密封。此外，本标准还规定了单组份密封
胶和相关表面底涂的供货要求。 
本标准适用于汽车用单组份粘结密封系
统。 
 
This Standard defines performance require-
ments for single component adhesive-sealer 
systems, as used to produce a strong and 
water tight seal in the direct glazing of auto-
motive vehicles. It covers conditions for the 
supply of both the single component sealant 
and, where applicable, the surface primers 
required for the sealants application. 
This Standard apply to single component ad-
hesive-sealer systems of automotive vehicles.

3  一般要求 
 
3  General requirements 
3.1  样件 
 
3.1  Samples  
要求提供2 L桶装的全新的密封胶样件，并
且同时提供试验所需的辅助材料。所提供的样件
状态必须与供货状态保持一致。根据4.1要求，
进行线上密封胶试验时，应该采用1升桶装的密
封胶样件，同时提供试验所需的辅助材料，以便
与最初的样件相比较。 
 
An initial two-liter sample of sealant in car-
tridge form shall be submitted, together with 
any associated materials, for testing to this 
standard. These shall, in all respects, be rep-
resentative of the materials to be supplied for 
production.When sealant is to be supplied for a 
line trial for compliance with clause 4.1, a rep-
resentative one liter sample in cartridge form 
shall, together with any associated materials, 
be submitted for comparison with the initial 
sample. 
3.2  健康与安全 
 
3.2  Health and safety 
所有材料均不得含有令人反感的气味，必须
符合有关工厂的健康与安全要求。 
 
All materials shall be free from offensive odour, 
and meet the Health and Safety requirements 
applying at the relevant plant. 
3.3  颜色 
 
3.3  Color 
粘结胶和相关底涂应为黑色，除非工程图纸
有专门规定。 
 
The sealant and the relevant primers shall be 
black unless otherwise specified on the Engi-
neering drawing. 
3.4  应用 
 
3.4  Application 
粘结胶应为均质成份，在被挤压变形时，可
以牢固地附着在产品表面。 
 
The sealant shall be homogeneous and when 
extruded form a regular, well adhered bead on 
the surfaces used in production.

permit rolling road tests and transport of the 
vehicle to holding areas. 
3.6  性能 
 
3.6  Performance 
粘结胶系统应始终牢固附着在产品表面，在
车辆使用中可能遇到的各种条件下都保持性能
稳定。 
 
The adhesive sealer system shall remain firmly 
adhered to the surfaces as used in production, 
and perform satisfactorily under all conditions 
likely to be encountered in service. 
3.7  储存 
 
3.7  Storage 
在保存于原装未开启容器中时，粘结胶和底
涂应始终具备符合本标准的生产使用条件，时间
为距交付期至少6个月。容器应密封保存在5 ℃ 
至30 ℃ 的温度条件下，远离阳光照射或其它热
源。 
注意在使用前，应在车间温度下对材料作稳
定处理。 
 
When stored in the original unopened con-
tainers, sealants and primers shall both remain 
suitable for production use, and meet the re-
quirements specified herein for a period of up 
to six months from the date of delivery. Con-
tainers shall be stored under cover at temper-
atures of 5 ℃ to 30 ℃, and away from direct 
sunlight or any localized heat source. 
Notice that materials shall be allowed to stabi-
lise at shop temperature prior to use. 
3.8  标识 
 
3.8  Identification 
所有容器都应含有如下信息：供货商名称、
供货商说明书、生产批号、生产日期、BL 标准
说明书、工厂说明书（如有）、有关法定警告（如：
燃点）。 
 
All containers shall bear the following informa-
tion: suppliers’ name, suppliers’ reference, 
batch number, date of manufacture, SAIC 
standard reference, plant reference if applica-
ble, legal warnings as appropriate (e.g. flash 
point).

5.1  试验板 
 
5.1  Test panels

 The test panels shall be of body steel meet to 
SMTC 5 110 001 and SMTC 5 110 003 and 
windscreen glass as used in production. Each 
panel shall be processed in order to simulate 
the relevant production operations up to the 
stage where the sealant is applied. Test panel 
sizes shall be as follows:

钢板:150 mm×25 mm×1 mm 
玻璃板:80 mm×30 mm×4 mm。 
 5.1.1  Flexibility, paint staining and paint ad-
hesion tests: 
Steel - 150 mm x 100 mm x 1 mm. 
5.1.2  Cold slam test 
Steel – 250 mm x 250 mm x 1 mm 
Glass – 80 mm x 30 mm x 4 mm;  
5.1.3  Shear strength adhesion test

5.2  材料的应用 
 5.2  Materials application 
应使用批量生产工艺将密封胶材料涂到试
验板上，根据供应商的建议，湿尺寸为25 mm×8 
mm×5 mm的胶型用于剪切试验，而湿尺寸为150 
mm×8 mm×5 mm的胶型则用于所有其它试验。 
 A bead of material shall be applied to the test 
panel by the production technique, and in ac-
cordance with suppliers’ recommendations, to 
give wet dimensions of 25 mm x 8 mm x 5 mm 
for shear testing, and 150 mm x 8 mm x 5 mm 
for all other tests.

 5.3  Conditioning of test specimens Test

前，都应在23 ℃±2 ℃ 和相对湿度65 %±5 %
的环境下放置168h。 
 Specimens shall, unless otherwise stated, be 
conditioned at 23 ℃ ± 2 ℃ and 65 %± 5 % 
RH for 168 hours before being subjected to the 
procedures detailed in Table 2.
