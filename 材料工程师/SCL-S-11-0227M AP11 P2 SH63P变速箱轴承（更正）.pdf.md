Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）输入轴后轴承的损坏导致输出轴前圆锥滚子轴承承受异常高的载荷而出现损伤。在后期运转中，轴承的
损伤区域在工作载荷下逐步扩大；异物的出现也恶化了这种损伤过程。The rear bearing failure of the input shaft caused the front 
bearing of the output shaft to bear high stress and become damaged. With the time going on, the damaged area was enlarged and foreign material going in the 
bearing exacerbated the damage process.  
（2）异物的来源不明确，有可能来自损坏的输入轴前轴承或者输出轴后轴承，也有可能来自于现场更换输入
轴的过程中。It is not clear where the foreign material is from. It is possibly from the damaged front bearing or rear bearing, or from the operation changing 
the input shaft in the test location. 
（3）轴承材料合格。The materials of the bearing are acceptable. 
 
建议suggestions： 
（1）在测试中，如果发现异常，立即停止试验，查找原因。During the test, if find abnormality,stop the experiment and find out the reason.  
（2）制定变速器等重要部件的修复操作规程。Set up the operation instruction about the repair of the key components.
