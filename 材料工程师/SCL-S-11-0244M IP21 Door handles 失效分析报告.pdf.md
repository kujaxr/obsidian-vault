Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 三件开门扣手的弹簧为疲劳断裂，起源于钢丝外表面。The three door handle springs have failed by fatigue, 
originating at the outer surface of the wire.   
2. 沿着弹簧钢丝发现有其它的疲劳裂纹，表明首要原因是载荷过大。这可能是由于门闩系统磨损或腐蚀了，增
加拉手上的载荷。Additional fatigue cracks were found along the wire, indicating the primary cause is excessive load.  This 
may be due to wear or corrosion in the door latch system, increasing the load on the handle. 
3. 起源位置无缺陷或腐蚀。No defects or corrosion were found near to the origins. 
4. 弹簧材料化学成分不符合要求。硬度正常，但是无法判断机械性能是否符合要求的。The material composition does 
not meet the requirements of the drawing.  The hardness is normal, but this test alone cannot qualify if the mechanical 
strength is ok or not. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 调查开门装置载荷过大的原因。Investigate reason for increased load in the door latch system. 
2. 供应商提供拉伸结果，之后将测试后的样品提供给实验室。Supplier to provide tensile testing results and 
submit tested samples to laboratory.

断口分析 Fracture Analysis 
 
三个弹簧断裂在相同的位置，在弹簧的外表面，见图2。 都为疲劳断裂，起源附近没有损坏或缺陷（见图2＆3）。
疲劳裂纹扩展深度约0.3mm，然后过载断裂。没有可以导致疲劳失效的表面腐蚀或损坏。 
The three springs had fractured at the same location at the outer surface of the spring (figure 2).  All had failed through 
fatigue, with no sign of damage or defect near to the origin (see figures 2 & 3). The fatigue crack propagated for ~0.3mm 
before final overload failure occurred.  There was no surface corrosion or damage which might have contributed to fatigue.

微观分析Microstructural analysis 
一个通过＃C 弹簧断裂的截面显示：疲劳裂纹区具有典型的疲劳特征：平坦，其深度约〜0.3mm，其余断裂的区
域是由于过载所致（图4）。在裂纹起源的附件，无缺陷，脱碳或其它的表面特征；金相组织正常的。靠近主裂
纹的附近，有两个的疲劳裂纹，说明疲劳是由于过载，不是应力集中。 
A cross-section through spring #C fracture revealed a typical flat fatigue crack to a depth of ~0.3mm, the remaining fracture 
was through overload (figure 4).  No defects, decarburisation or other surface features were found near to the crack origin.  
The microstructure is normal.  Two further fatigue cracks were found near to the main crack, indicating that the fatigue was 
due to a general overload of the material, not stress concentration.

使用情况Operational considerations 
 
对枢轴销进行了检查，发现表明有严重的磨损（图6），这阻碍了弹簧的运动，并促使了过早失效。枢轴销的材
料是0CrNi18 - 10 钢，不是特别硬或也不耐磨。更合适的材料将是镀铬的硬钢材。 
The pivot pins were also examined, and a high degree of wear was found (figure 6)  This has prevented easy movement of the 
spring and has contributed to the premature failure.  The material is specified as 0CrNi18-10 steel, which is not especially 
hard or wear resistant.  A more suitable material would be a hardened steel with a chromium coating.  
 
进一步考虑操作过程中的负载。如果门闩出现磨损或腐蚀，这会增加了把手的载荷，那么将促使枢轴销的磨损和
弹簧过早失效。 
A further consideration would be the load applied during operation.  If wear or corrosion in the door latch mechanism has 
increased the load at the handle, then this would also contribute to the pin wear and the springs’ premature failure.
