Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）垫片和滚针由于受到较大的载荷而出现疲劳剥离失效。Overload caused fatigue spalling of washer and needles. 
（2）材料的组织，硬度和夹杂物正常。The microstructure, hardness and inclusion level of the material are normal. 
（3）关键尺寸符合图纸要求。The key dimensions meet the requirement of the drawing. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）DRE检查轴承的选型是否满足实际载荷的要求。Check if the bearing is suitable for the actual load.  
（2）检查由于轴的刚性导致偏载是否在合适的范围内。Check if any axle stiffness issue leading to abnormal load is 
within appropriate limits.
