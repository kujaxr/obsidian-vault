Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 由于内应力较大导致凸桃产生原始裂纹，使用过程中疲劳开裂。 
   Big internal stress results in the cam lobe initial crack producing and crack though fatigue in use. 
2. 凸桃淬硬层深度、表面硬度及金相组织未发现异常。    
   Hardness case、surface hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 供应商加强对凸桃热处理、磨削等工艺的监控。 
Enhance controlling the heat trentment、grinding process and so on.
