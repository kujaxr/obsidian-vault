Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 排气弯管是由于疲劳而开裂的；裂纹起源于焊趾，由表向内扩展。Exhaust pipe is broken by fatigue, which 
started from the weld toe region and spread from the outside to the inside . 
（2） 排气弯管与消音器内管匹配不良，局部间隙较大，管子的熔深不符合要求; 消音器的内板大部分区域未
100%熔透或熔合。The matching between the exhaust pipe and the inner pipe of the muffler is bad, the local gap 
is big and the weld penetration of the exhaust pipe doesn’t meet the requirement; most weld region of the 
muffler’s inner plate is without full penetration or fusion. 
（3） 排气弯管的化学成分合格；组织和硬度正常。The chemical composition of the exhaust pipe met the 
requirement; the microstructure and hardness are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 检查排气管及焊接用工装夹具的尺寸，确保排气管和消音器内管能良好匹配。Check the exhaust pipe 
assembly parts and welding jigs size and ensure the matching between exhaust pipe and inner pipe of the 
muffler is good. 
（2） 加强焊接的过程控制，确保焊接质量及稳定。Strengthen the control of welding process and ensure the 
welding quality and stability.

BP11 埃及项目的排气管总成由埃及供应商组装焊接而成；其中消音器由国内原供应商提供；与之匹配的管
子由埃及供应商制造。总成运回国内进行焊缝耐久测试；试验要求5 万次振动试验完成后焊缝处无裂纹。1 号，

suppliers and matching exhaust pipe was made by Egyptian suppliers. The assembly weld durability test was done in 
china. Test requirements is no crack after 50000 times vibration. Cracks of 1# and 3# muffle assembly were found after 
50000 times test; 2# muffle assembly after 30000 times test was cracked.

焊缝对比分析   Comparative analysis of the weld 
     依据图2 所示的取样位置，对2#和3#件的焊缝进行剖切取样；同时抽检了国内产品的焊缝。相关的
检测结果如表1 所示。由检测结果可以看出： 
According to figure 2, samples of the 2# and 3# weld were got by cutting on the positions; the Chinese parts 
weld was also tested. The test results are shown in table 1. According to the test results, it can be seen: 
 
排气管焊缝大部分区域无熔深，不符要求；同时发现，消音器内层板焊接不良（见图10 和11 中
箭头所示部位）；Most region of the pipe weld is without weld penetration, which does not meet the 
requirement; at the same time, the weld of the muffler’s inner plate is bad, which are shown by the 
arrows in fig. 10 and 11.  
 
由于排气弯管与消音器内管的轴心存在明显的夹角，导致局部匹配间隙过大；这会对焊缝局部的
熔深产生不利的影响。产生匹配不良的原因可能与管子的尺寸或者焊接夹具有关；An angle was 
formed between the two axes of the exhaust pipe and the muffle inner pipe, which made that local 
matching gap is big. This will affect the local weld penetration. Pipe dimensions or welding fixture 
maybe lead to the bad matching. 
 
国产件匹配良好，焊缝合格。Chinese part’s matching is well and the weld is qualified.

材料测试 Material Test 
三个失效件为同一批零件，因此仅取其中一件（2#管）用于材料检测分析。材料检测结果如表2 所示。 
The three failed parts are of the same batch, so only one of them (2# failed part) was chose for material 
analysis. The results were showed in table 2: 
 
排气弯管的成分合格；The components of exhaust pipe are ok; 
 
基体及焊缝区域硬度见表2，排气弯管靠近焊缝区域硬度明显要比基体硬度低，也低于焊缝硬度；
依据经验和相关的文献，该硬度是正常的；其抗拉强度可以超过板材抗拉强度的下限要求。The 
hardness of matrix and weld is shown in table 2. The hardness of exhaust pipe near weld is obvious 
lower than that of the matrix and weld; basing on experience and some relevant literatures, the hardness 
is normal; its performance is over the lower limit of the plate’s tensile strength requirement. 
 
排气管基体组织为细小的铁素体，焊缝组织正常。The microstructure of exhausted pipe is fine ferrite and 
weld microstructure is normal.
