Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
小结：1、失效件和新零件的材料符合要求，硬度符合标准，两者配方基本相同。2、两者零件表面未发现破损、
气孔、杂质、异常压痕等缺陷。3、尺寸：失效件和新零件的小O 型圈尺寸符合要求；失效件和新零件的大O 型
圈厚度符合要求，内径偏大，偏差分别为0.05mm 和（0.06mm，0.02mm，0.04mm）。经DRE 确认，新零件没
有出现泄露情况。又由于失效件的O 型圈都经过装配，有可能产生变形，故失效的大O 型圈0.05mm 偏差造成
泄露的可能性不大。 
综上所述，零件材料符合要求。大O 型圈在装配过程中可能产生了变形，内径偏大0.05mm，零件表面无发
现破损、气孔、杂质、异常压痕等缺陷，故导致泄漏的风险不大。建议从其他方面进行分析，查找根本原因。 
Conclusion: 1. The material performance and hardness of the failure and new part conform to the 
requirement. The formulation is basiclly the same. 2. There are no defects such as pores, impurities, 
damage and abnormal indentation on the surface of the failure and new part. 3. Size: the size of the failure 
and new small O ring conforms to the requirement. The thickness of the failure and new big O ring 
conforms to the requirement. But the inner diameter is larger than the requirement and the deviation is 
0.05mm and (0.06mm，0.02mm，0.04mm). After confirmed by DRE, the new part does not appear leak. 
What’s more, the failure part has assembled to the parts which may cause deformation, the leak is unlikely 
caused by the 0.05 deviation of the failure big O ring. 
In summary, the parts conform to the material requuiments. The big O ring may had a deformation 
during the assembly process, which cause the 0.05 deviation. As there are no defects such as pores, 
impurities, damage and abnormal indentation on the surface of the O ring, the risk of the leakage is low. It 
is suggest to analysis the root cause from the other aspects. 
建议：从零件装配等方面着手，分析零件失效原因。 
Suggestions：It is suggest to analysis from the assembly and the other aspects.
