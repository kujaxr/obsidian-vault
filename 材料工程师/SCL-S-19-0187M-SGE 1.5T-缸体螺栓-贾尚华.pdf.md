Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  缸体螺栓松脱是由于缸体螺纹进行了二次加工。 
The loosening of cylinder block bolts is due to the secondary processing of cylinder block threads. 
 
 
. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商加强质量监控，避免二次加工。 
To find out the causes of sticking die such as insufficient spraying amount of demoulding agent or not to repair 
dies in time after long-term use of dies.
