Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件焊接工艺不佳，焊接位置结合不良。 
The welding process of failure part was poor, the combination of welding position was bad. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 优化焊接工艺。 
                    Improve welding craft.
