Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
 
1. 该失效是由于装配错误导致的。传动轴螺栓拧得过紧，导致裙架内螺纹断裂。The failure has occurred due 
to assembly error. Overtightening of one of the driveshaft bolts stripped the internal thread from the 
bearing ladder. 
2. 相邻的螺栓由于载荷增加而出现疲劳失效，最后剩下的螺栓因过载而失效。The adjacent bolt then failed 
through fatigue due to the increased loading, finally the remaining bolt failed due to overload. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 控制螺栓拧紧和装配过程中的检查程序。Control tightening and overcheck procedures during assembly.

Joint A 
▪ The internal thread has been stripped from the aluminium bore (figure 4b).  The darkened colour is oxidation 
and indicates that this occurred some time before final failure, ie during initial assembly (by overtightening). 
▪ The loosened bolt worked it’s way from the bore, and was loaded abnormally, which caused both the thread 
markings on the upper portion of the bore (figure 5b), and the overload fracture of the material opposite (see 
figure 6). 
▪ The driveshaft bracket was therefore not held against the bearing ladder, and the subsequent movement 
generated the fretting wear seen in figure 5c.   
▪ The fracture surface of the aluminium did not show any defects or porosity.

Joint B  
▪ The aluminium boss has been crushed, and fractured in shear (figure 7a, b).  The surface shows a clear imprint 
of the bracket’s machining marks and a raised edge is also prominent. 
▪ These features are due to overtightening of the joint, which caused the aluminium to collapse.  The joint was 
therfore unstable and clamping load was lost, leading to fatigue failure of the fastener (figure 8). 
▪ Continual movement during service, led to the abrasions on the top of the boss (figure 7c) 
▪ In this case the internal thread did not fail, either because the applied torque was less than with Joint A, or more 
likely, due to the increased thread engagement in this joint (~12.5mm, compared to ~10mm in joint A). 
▪ The bolt met the hardness specification of ISO898-1 Grade 8.8 (table 1)
