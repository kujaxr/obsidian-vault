Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  变速箱悬置是由于受到冲击发生过载断裂。 
    Transmission suspension fractured because of seriously shock load.   
2.  裂纹源C（见图3）附近存在有轻微的冷隔，对铸件整体强度影响不大。 
    Cold shot is observed near crack source C (shown in fig 3), but it affect slightly casting quality. 
3. 材料成分、硬度、孔隙率及组织无异常； 
Chemical、hardness、porosity and microstructure are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  重新评估此结构零件的安全系数。 
   Evaluagte the safety coefficient again. 
2.  优化工艺参数，提高铸件质量、避免冷隔。 
    Optimize the process to improve the casting quality and avoid the cold shot.
