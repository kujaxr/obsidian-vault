Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 失效件属于过载断裂，螺牙上有油渍。 
The part failed because of overload and oil was observed in bolt tooth.  
（2） 失效件性能无异常，螺母牙型正常。 
Property of the failed part is normal and nut tooth is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）排查装配过程质量。 
Check the assembly process. 
（2）追查油渍的来源，如包装、装配过程。 
Trace the oil source such as packing、assembly process.
