Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 减震器安装加强板是由于疲劳而开裂的；REINF-W/HSE RR INR UPR-LH was broken by fatigue. 
2. 板厚符合图纸要求；The thickness met the technical requirement. 
3. 失效件化学成分不符合常见的SAPH440 材质：碳含量偏高，锰含量明显偏低；微观组织中珠光体也多于正常
的SAPH440；Chemical composition of the failure didn’t comply with the common material SAPH440: the content of 
carbon was high and content of Mn was significantly lower. The content of pearlite was also high. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 评估右后Top mount 失效对开裂部位的影响；Evaluate the failled Top mount effect on the crack region. 
2. 校核开裂处应力载荷；Check the active stress at crack region.  
3. 供应商提供原材料质检报告；Supplier provides the quality testing report.
