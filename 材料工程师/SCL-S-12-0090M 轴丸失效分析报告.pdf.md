Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 客户的发动机Customer Engine 轴瓦由于润滑不良失效，是由于油流中断造成的。供油系统堵塞，机油缺乏，或低
温发动机启动后高加速度等因素强烈影响发动机的润滑条件。The bearings have failed due to poor lubrication, resulting 
from interrupted oil flow.  Factors such as blockage in the oil feed, lack of oil in the system, or low temperature engine start 
followed by high acceleration can strongly affect the engine lubrication conditions.  
2. 检查引擎Audit Engine  2.1）曲轴轴瓦显示疲劳特征。 2.2）连杆轴承显示粘着磨损开始的特征，是润滑差造成的。 
2.1) The crankshaft bearing shows signs of fatigue.  2.2) The con-rod bearing shows beginnings of adhesive wear, caused by 
poor lubrication conditions. 
3.没有发现的任何外来粒子。依据所能测试的项目，零件符合图纸的要求。 No foreign particles were found in any parts.  
The parts met the requirements of the drawing, as far as could be tested. 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 客户的发动机Customer Engine 1.1) 在这种情况下，机油供应被彻底检查。 1.2）在寒冷的条件下，加速之前，客
户先应让发动机预热。1.1) In such cases, the oil supply much be checked thoroughly.  1.2) In cold conditions customers 
should allow the engine to warm up before accelerating strongly. 
2. 检查引擎Audit Engine 2.1) 应检查发动机的工作条件。 2.2）为了失效分析，请提交进一步的信息和有关零件 
2.1) The operating conditions of the engine should be checked.  2.2) Please submit further details and components for such 
failure analysis investigations

1. Failure Engine A01-53513 
提交的连杆轴瓦如图1 所示。很明确，3 缸轴瓦严重过热和损坏；无法进行分析。 4 缸轴瓦开始显示粘着磨损的
迹象（见图2 至4）。对图3 中看到暗区进行能谱分析，确认无异物存在（见图5）。黑暗物质是氧化的油。 
The connecting rod bearings were submitted as shown in figure 1.  It’s clear that the bearings for cylinder 3 were 
severely overheated and damaged.  These could not be analysed.  The bearings for cylinder 4 also started to show 
signs of adhesive wear (see figures 2 to 4).  The dark area seen in figure 3 was analysed using EDX, which confirmed 
no foreign material was present (see figure 5).  The dark substance is oxidised oil.

engine was provided. The connecting rod bearing shows slightly increased wear in the centre and the edge, with 
traces of adhesive wear, which indicates a poor lubrication condition.  No foreign particles were found, or 
indications for manufacturing or assembly errors.
