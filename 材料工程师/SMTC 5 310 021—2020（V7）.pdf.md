3 要求 Requirements 
   
3.1 样品和预处理要求 Sampling and conditioning requirements 
3.1.1  样品要求 Sampling requirements 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成品
上取样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，其样品制
备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室协商确定。为完成
试验，需要多个成品零件。 
 
 
The test samples stipulated in the Standard. 
Samples should be made from finished prod-
ucts. If the samples can’t be made from fin-
ished products, sample preparation method 
should be determined through the negotiation 
with SMPV SCL. Several parts are required for 
complete testing. 
3.1.2 预处理 Conditioning 
试验前，样品必须在ISO 291 23/50的标准气
候下至少放置24 h。 
 
 
Before the test, samples should be placed for 
at least 24 hours in the standard climate of 
ISO 291 23/50. 
 
3.2  基本要求 General Requirements 
  
3.2.1  外观 Appearance 
成品件表面与内部不允许存在加工缺陷，如
熔接痕、缩孔、裂纹及类似缺陷。 
 
 
The surface and interior of the finished parts 
shall be free of flaws and processing defects, 
such as flow lines, voids, cracks or similar 
faults. 
3.2.2 颜色 Color 
按图纸，并符合 SMTC 5 400 002 的要求。 
 
 
According to SMTC5 400 002 and drawing. 
3.2.3 禁用/限用材料、避免使用的危险品 Avoidance of hazardous substances  
    必须符合 SMTC 5 500 001 的要求。 
 
According to SMTC 5 500 001.

必须符合 SMTC 5 400 008 的要求。 
 
 
According to SMTC 5 400 008 
3.2.5 散发特性 Emission behavior 
必须符合 SMTC 5 300 004 的要求(只针对
内饰零件)。 
 
 
According to SMTC 5 300 004（apply only to

3.4  内应力测试 Internal stresses test 
    推荐测试方法：将待测零件完全浸入23±
1℃的冰醋酸（分析纯）中15~60 秒。取出后立
即清洗后晾干，检查表面。要求：没有应力开裂。 
 
 
 
Recommended test method：The sample 
should be completely immersed into acetic 
acid (AR) for 15~60 seconds at room tem-
perature. Wash the sample and dry it imme-
diately after taking out from the mixture, and 
evaluate the surface of the sample. Re-
quirement: No stresses crack.

The required numerical values apply to each 
individual measurement.
