Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承的失效是由于组件的疲劳失效造成的。The bearing has failed due to fatigue of the component parts. 
（2）保持架，钢球和内圈都出现了疲劳失效。导致其疲劳失效的原因有两点：①润滑不良；②保持架过渡处
质量不好。The cage ball and inside race failed because of fatigue. The fatigue reasons are poor lubrication and the bad quality of the 
cage in the transition area. 
（3）由于来样损坏严重，无法辨别最先失效的部件是保持架，还是钢球或内圈。Due to the serious damage to the failed 
part, it can’t be determined which part (cage, ball and inside race) failed first. 
（4）材料满足图纸的要求。The materials meet the requirements of the drawing. 
（5）前轴承正常。Analysis of the front bearing on the shaft revealed no problems. 
（6）抽检润滑油脂的滴点和工作锥入度正常。The dropping point & consistency (worked penetration) of grease samples are 
normal. 
建议suggestions： 
（1）检查轴承的润滑是否充分。Investigate whether the lubrication condition is adequate. 
（2）改善保持架的质量。Improve the quality of the cage. 
（3）对变速器轴承的进一步路试结果进行跟踪。Pay attention to the further road test result of the transmission bearing.
