Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  活塞环岸断裂，可能与异常燃烧有关。 
    Abnormal combustion results in the piston abnormal stress and fractured at last.   
2.  活塞材料正常。 
    Porosity、hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查发动机的燃烧系统，查找可能引起发动机异常燃烧的原因。 
   Check the burn system and investigate the abnormal combustion.
