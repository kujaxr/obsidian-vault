Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  导风网装配不到位，关闭天窗后骨架被压断。 
The air guide net was not assembled in place, and the frame was broken after sunroof closed. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  供应商增加点检措施，避免装配不到位问题再次发生。 
The supplier adds spot inspection measures to avoid the recurrence of inadequate assembly.
