5  测试方法 Test Method 
1：至少需要3 个零件，分别代表挂具低、
中、高电流密度区域。见图1。 
2：选取每个零件电流密度最薄弱的区域，
取1ml 俄罗斯泥，均匀涂抹在20mmx20mm 区域内。
如被测试零件面积无法满足20mmx20mm，则被测
试区域至少满足长度20mm，不可使用样板进行
替代。 
3：放入高温试验箱，60℃±2℃x96h。 
 
1: For each test a minimum of 3 parts 
representing low, middle and high current 
density rack locations is required. see figure 1. 
2: Select the area with the weakest current 
density for each component, take 1ml of 
Russian mud, and evenly apply it to the 
40mmx40mm area. If the area of the tested 
part cannot meet the requirement of 20mm x 
20mm, the tested area must have a minimum 
length of 20mm and cannot be replaced with a 
template. 
 
3: Put it into a high-temperature test chamber, 
60℃±2℃x96h.
