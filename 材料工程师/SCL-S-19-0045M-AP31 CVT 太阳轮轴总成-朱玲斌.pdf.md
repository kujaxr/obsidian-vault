Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 离合器毂双向疲劳断裂失效，主疲劳源位于油孔一侧的组织变形位置。The clutch hub biaxial fatigue 
fractured, the main fatigue source is located in the microstructure deformation position near the oil hole .   
（2） 离合器毂存在明显的折叠缺陷，这不是导致疲劳的主要原因。There is obvious folding defect in clutch hub, 
which is not the main cause of fatigue. 
（3） 离合器毂材料成分合格，硬度正常。The composition of the clutch hub is qualified, the hardness is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核开裂区域的工作应力。Check the work stress of the crack area . 
（2） 对成品失效区域的残余应力进行检测。Test the residual stress in the failure position of finished product. 
（3） 关注可能导致太阳轮轴总成工作状态异常的因素，比如动平衡。Pay attention to factors that may lead to 
abnormal working conditions of the solar shaft assembly , such as dynamic balance. 
（4） 关注该车型的后续市场表现。Pay attention to the follow –up market performance of the model.
