Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  局部应力高引发内加强板与横梁焊趾疲劳开裂，并扩展至母材。 
Fatigue crack initiates along the welding seam and propagate to base material. 
2.  加强板与横梁内焊缝(见图5)熔深、焊高满足要求；其他区域存在补焊、焊缝轨迹异常，但这不是引起
零件失效的原因。 
Fusion and welding thickness (shown in fig5)meet the requirement. There are repair welding and abnormal 
weld track in other areas, but this is not the failure cause. 
3.  横梁成分满足S500MC 要求，硬度及组织正常。 
    Chemical components meet the requirement of S500MC. Hardness and microstructure are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化零件结构设计或调整焊缝轨迹，以降低应力。 
    Optimise the structure or adjust the welding to reduce the stress.  
2.  按照图纸或技术要求，进一步改善焊接质量。 
    Further improve the welding quality based on the drawing or technical requirement.
