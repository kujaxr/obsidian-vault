Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）增压器进油管快接头泄露是由于密封圈内圈飞边切除不干净，残余量高低不均匀所致。The leakage of the 
turbocharger feed pipe quick connector was becaue the inner ring flash of the seeling ring was not cut completedly and the 
residual amout is uneven. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）排查生产过程，查找切除不干净的原因，关注模具的维护保养或优化分模线的位置。Check the process and find 
the reasons of unclean removal and pay attention to the mold maintenance or optimize the position of parting line
