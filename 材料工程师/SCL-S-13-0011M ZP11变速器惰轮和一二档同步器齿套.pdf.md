Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）倒档惰轮和一二档同步器齿套在工作面上相互冲击而过载失效。Reverse idler gear and 1st/2nd speed 
synchronizer ring gear have failed due to impact overloading on the working surface.   
（2）所有零件的材料成分，芯部硬度以及渗碳层均满足产品的要求；组织和齿根正常。The chemical compositions, 
core hardness and carburized layer depth meet the requirement; the microstructures and the tooth roots are normal. 
（3）1#失效件惰轮的表面硬度符合要求；2#和3#失效件惰轮的表面硬度偏高，但不是导致产品失效的原因。The 
surface hardness of 1# failed idler gear meets the requirement; the ones of 2# and 3# idler gears are higher than the 
requirement, but this is not the reason of failure. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）查找失效时的车辆使用信息。Investigate how to drive the car while the car was failed. 
（2）检查程序是否正常，可靠。Check if the program is normal and reliable. 
（3）控制热处理工艺过程，保证表面硬度符合要求。Control the heat treatment and ensure the surface hardness 
meets the requirement.
