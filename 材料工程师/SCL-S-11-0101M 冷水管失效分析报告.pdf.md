Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 出水弯管腐蚀发生在管道出口端的〜10mm 处。在出水弯管的其他部位未发现腐蚀。The pipe has failed 
by corrosion of ~10mm from the outlet end of the pipe. Corrosion was not found in other areas of the 
pipe.  
2. 这种类型腐蚀是杂散电流腐蚀的特点，小电流会导致金属的氧化。This type of corrosion is characteristic 
of stray current corrosion, where a small electrical current causes oxidation of the metal. 
3. 材料成分符合要求。微观结构不是最佳的，但是，这并未导致其失效。The material composition met the 
requirements of the drawing.  The microstructure was not optimal, however, this has not influenced 
the failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 由电气工程专家检查受影响的汽车，来找到电流源头. Examination of the affected car by electrical 
engineering expert, to find the source of the current.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
这是车辆（LSJW26G399S015796）第二次出水弯管腐蚀掉.  
第一次：2010 年10 月份，车辆里程数: 28711km （QAL 报告SCL-S-10-0062M）。 第二次：2011 年6 月份，
车辆里程数： 40079km。  
图1 显示弯管的安装和条件。 腐蚀仅限于管子的末端和近末端的管道内表面。没有外面或附近的其他孔径内的腐
蚀。除底面的区域由于冷却液泄漏，其它腐蚀均匀分布在圆周。 
This is the second failure of the coolant outlet pipe for VIN LSJW26G399S015796.   
1st occurrence: October 2010, mileage: 28711km (QAL report no SCL-S-10-0062M).  2nd occurrence: June 2011, mileage: 40079km.  
Figure 1 shows the installation and condition of the failed pipe.  The corrosion is limited only to the end and inner surface of the pipe near to the 
end.  There is no corrosion of the outside or the inside near to the other apertures. The corrosion is evenly distributed around the circumference, 
except for an area on the underside due to coolant leakage.
