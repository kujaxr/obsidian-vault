Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该变速箱壳体端盖发生过载断裂，起源于高应力区，断裂面未见明显缺陷；该断裂与局部应力过大有关。
Overload fracture occurred on the end transmission end cover, which originated from the high stress area, no obvious 
defects were found on the fracture surface；The fracture was related to local excessive stress. 
（2）材料化学成分符合要求，硬度、孔隙率正常。 
 The chemical composition of the material met the requirements, and the hardness and porosity were normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）建议优化结构，降低局部应力。 
It is suggested to optimize the structure and reduce local stress.
