Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）蜗杆花键齿齿型不良（见图5（a）），降低了花键齿以及与联轴器紧密配合的强度，导致蜗杆与联轴器在工
作中逐渐出现松动，最终完全失效。The teeth shape of the worm shaft (see fig. 5(a)) was bad, which reduced the strength of the teeth 
and the mate and made the loosing gradually happened under the working and finally failed completely. 
（2）蜗杆的材料成分和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and hardness of the worm shaft meet the 
requirements and the microstructure is normal. 
（3）联轴器的成分符合要求；硬度和组织无异常。The chemical components of the shaft adaptor meet the requirements and the 
hardness and microstructure are normal.  
建议Suggestions： 
（1）追溯该批次装车零件，并评估其风险。Investigate the same batch of the parts and evaluate the risk. 
（2）供应商应加强过程控制，确保产品质量。The suplier should strengthen the process control to ensure the product quality.
