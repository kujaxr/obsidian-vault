Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  尾灯受除胶剂等类似的溶剂腐蚀后贯穿开裂。 
The tail lamp is cracked after being corroded by similar solvents such as stickers remove. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of the part conformed to requirements. 
建议Suggestions：1. 避免使用除胶剂等腐蚀性较强的溶剂。 
                    Avoid to use strong corrosive solvents such as stickers remove. 
              2. 更换现有的灯罩保护膜，避免残胶问题。 
                    Replace the existing protective film to avoid the problem of residual glue.
