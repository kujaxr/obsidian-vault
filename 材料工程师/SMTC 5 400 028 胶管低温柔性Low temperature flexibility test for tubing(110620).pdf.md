胶管低温柔性 
Low temperature flexibility 
test for tubing 
1 范围 
    本标准规定了所有塑料和橡胶软管的测试
方法。 
本标准适用于所有塑料和橡胶软管测试。 
                                       
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the test method of 
all plastic and rubber tubing. 
This standard applies to test method of all 
plastic and rubber tubing. 
2 设备 
2  Apparatus 
2.1 试验用冷却设备，应能维持所要求温度。
2.1  Laboratory freezer, capable of maintain-
ing the specified temperature. 
2.2 测试时，芯轴直径为（50±2）倍软管壁厚。
2.2  Mandrel, with diameter 50 times ± 2 
times the radial wall thickness of the tubing 
under test. 
3 试样准备 
3 
Specimen preparation 
测试时，切割样件，样件长度充分满足 5.2 
条所要求。 
Cut representative samples of tubing un-
der test. The length of each sample shall be 
sufficient to meet requirements of clause 5.2. 
4 步骤 
4  Procedure 
4.1 将样件和芯轴放置在冷却设备里，保持要
求温度至少 4 h。 
4.1  Place the specimens and the mandrel in 
the freezer maintained at the specified tem-
perature for a minimum of 4 h. 
4.2 样件从冷却设备里拿出的（10±2）s 内，
将软管绕芯轴缠绕至少一圈半。 
4.2  Within 10 s ± 2 s of removal from the 
freezer, wind the tubing around the mandrel for 
a minimum of one-and-a-half turns. 
4.3 试验重复三次。 
4.3  Carry out the test in triplicate. 
5 评估 
5  Assessment 
5.1 软管有任何裂缝，裂纹，表面龟裂或其他
缺陷，都被认为是不合要求的。 
5.1  The tubing shall be considered unsatis-
factory if there is any evidence of cracking, 
splitting, surface crazing or any other defect. 
5.2 软管若不能满足接下来的任何标准，也被
认为是不合要求的。 
5.2  The tubing shall be considered unsatis-
factory if it fails to meet any further requirement 
imposed by the specification.
