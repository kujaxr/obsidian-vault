Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 油管由于应力过大引起的疲劳开裂。 
The fatigue cracking for the part due to large stress.  
2、失效件的拉伸强度偏低。 
The tensile strength for the part failured is low . 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核此零件的受力情况，减小振动。 
Please check the forces for the part and reduce thevibration a . 
2、 请检查试验过程中的拆装情况。 
Please check the disassembly during the test.
