Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．失效件受外力断裂失效。2.零件材料不符合技术要求。3.注塑工艺不良。 
Conclusions: 1. The brittle fracture of the parts caused by external forces. 
2. Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements. 
 
3. The injection molding process was poor. 
建议： 1.责令供应商解释材料及工艺问题并整改。 
Suggestions: 1. Force the supplier to explain the materials and injection molding process problems. Solve the problems.
