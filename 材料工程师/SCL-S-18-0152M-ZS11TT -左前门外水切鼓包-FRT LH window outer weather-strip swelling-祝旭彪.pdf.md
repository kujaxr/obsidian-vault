Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
 1、鼓包区与未鼓包区材料定性结果一致，符合定义要求。排除其他材料引起鼓包。It shows that the Bulging and 
the Unbulging area are the same material. It meet the requirements. It exclude other materials to cause the bulging problem. 
2、鼓包区红外图谱未见降解氧化峰，材料焓值未见异常，显示鼓包区材料非高温降解所致。No degradation peaks 
were observed in the IR spectra and no abnormality in the material enthalpy. It indicate that there was no obvious degradation cause the 
bulging.  
3、剖切后TPV 胶层内部为气孔，未见异物。分析认为TPV 原材料未充分干燥，挤出过程 水汽引起鼓包。 
After cutting, the pores in the TPV layer are stomata, no foreign bodies are found. The analysis shows that TPV raw materials are not fully 
dried, and water vapor in the extrusion process causes bulging. 
建议Conclusions： 
1、要求供应商加强质量监控，挤出前对TPV 进行充分的干燥.  
Suppliers are required to strengthen quality control and fully dry TPV before extrusion.
