Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 是由于在冷镦过程中产生的原始裂纹导致了螺栓的断裂。  
The bolt has failed due to the original cracks during the cold forging process. 
2、 实物金相组织、硬度符合图纸技术要求要求。  
Failed parts microstructure, hardness comply with the the drawing technical requirements 
建议Suggestions： 
1、检查冷镦过程质量。Check the cold forging process quality. 
   2、请供应商提供原材料质保书和该材料冷镦实验报告。
