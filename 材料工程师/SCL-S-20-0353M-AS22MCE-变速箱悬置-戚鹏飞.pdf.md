Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：（1）外壳零件为过载断裂，为匹配间隙过大所致。The housing part is an overload fracture. This is 
due to the match gap is too large. 
                （2）外壳零件成分、硬度及孔隙度符合材料要求，组织正常。The chemical composition, hardness 
and porosity of the housing parts meet the material requirements, microstructure is normal.         
建议Suggestions：
（1）排查零件尺寸是否正常，关注匹配后的零件间隙。Check if the part size is correct and focus on 
the matching part gap.
