Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 胶管材料为ECO，符合要求。 
胶管未发现欠硫现象；接头内侧受到划伤，导致内侧形成裂纹，最终导致胶管开裂。 
Conclusion: the material of the tube conforms to the standard. The damage in the inner of the tube cause the crack of the tube. 
  建议： 保证安装及使用中胶管内侧不受损伤。 
Suggestion: .Assure the tube free of damage during the install and usage.
