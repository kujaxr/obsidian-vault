Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件弹簧较长，使得组装后都异常外力导致断裂。 
       2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. Spring of failure was too long, which led to abnormal foreign force after assembly and the crack of part.  
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议： 1. 供应商明确弹簧尺寸要求，并制作检具工装保证零件尺寸合格。 
Suggestions: 1. Supplier should define dimension requirements of spring clearly and made test tool to assure the key 
dimensions of part.
