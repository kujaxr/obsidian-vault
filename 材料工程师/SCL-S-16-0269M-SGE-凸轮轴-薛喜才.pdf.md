Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1）凸轮轴头端部压偏，工作过程中头端部与钢管晃动、产生异响。 
     Pressure deviation makes the camshaft shaking, noise in work. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 进一步排查压偏的原因。Further investigate the pressure deviation.
