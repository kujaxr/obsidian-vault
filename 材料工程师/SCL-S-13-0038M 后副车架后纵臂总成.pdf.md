Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 
后副车架总成与后纵臂总成失效的多处断裂位置中，后副车架总成上25RH 焊缝首先断裂，焊缝中存在集群
状气孔和夹渣等焊接缺陷，焊缝剖切显示搭接加强板没有100%熔透。 
The rear subframe assembly and the rear longitudinal arm assembly failed due to early fracture on 25RH weld； 
There are pores and slag inclusions in the weld，and it is not 100% penetrated at the section. 
2. 
后副车架及后纵臂总成上的37RH 焊缝，39RH 焊缝旁的窄连接板和连接头的断裂，是在25RH 焊缝断裂后
载荷异常所致的断裂。 
The 37RH weld, plate near 39RH weld and plunger failed due to the abnormal stress after the fracture of 25RH 
weld.  
3. 
36LH 焊缝根部出现脆性裂纹，表明该处承受应力较大。 
Crack on 36LH weld shows that the stress on subframe is large.  
4. 
后副车架总成的板材及后纵臂总成连接耳的材料正常。 
The material of the subframe and the plunger is OK. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
供应商加强控制工艺过程，保证焊接质量。 
Supplier should control the manufacturing process and assure the stable welding quality. 
2. 
工程校核实际应力，优化结构强度。 
Designer check the real stress, optimize the subframe if necessary.
