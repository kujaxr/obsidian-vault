Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  中间轴的断口大部分区域磨损严重，保留区域的形貌特征为沿晶+韧窝。 
The fracture surface of the intermediate axis is worn seriously in most areas, and the morphology of the retained area 
is intergranular + dimple. 
2.  中间轴电子束焊接部分的焊宽、熔深满足要求。 
The welding width and penetration of the electron beam welding part of the intermediate shaft meet the requirements. 
3.  失效中间轴齿轮的材料成分，硬度符合要求，金相组织正常。 
    The material composition, hardness and metallographic structure of the failed intermediate shaft gear meet the 
requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 鉴于失效原因不明，且售后仅出现了一例，因此建议关注售后类似失效。In view of the unknown cause of 
failure and only one case of after-sale failure, it is suggested to pay attention to similar after-sale failure.
