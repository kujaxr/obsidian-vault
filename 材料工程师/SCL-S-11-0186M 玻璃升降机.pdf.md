Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 钢丝绳卡扣未正常装配，与安装孔倾斜，因而在钢丝绳运动时受到超负荷的力，导致断裂。 
 2. 钢丝卡扣材料符合质量要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The block button of steel wire was not assembled correctly, which made it slope and suffer overload 
force, resulting in cracking.  
2. The material performance of block button of steel wire conformed to requirements. 
建议：改进装配工艺，装配后增加检查工序。  
Suggestions：The assembly technology should be improved. A checking process should be increased after assembly.
