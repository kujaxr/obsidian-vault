Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）金属异物为带螺纹的杆状物体，来自于制造过程。 The metal thing is a threaded shaft, which was embedded 
during pad manufacture. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）查找金属物混入磨成片的原因；Find out how the metal part entered the brake pad. 
（2）采取措施，避免类似事情的再次发生。Take measures to avoid the similar things happen again.
