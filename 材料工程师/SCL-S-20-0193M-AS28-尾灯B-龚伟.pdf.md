Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件工艺不佳，灯罩透黑&明白位置分层开裂。 
The failure parts were in poor craft, lampshade black & transparent position stratified & crack.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.供应商排查工艺问题并整改。 
                 Let the supplier troubleshoot process problems and rectify.
