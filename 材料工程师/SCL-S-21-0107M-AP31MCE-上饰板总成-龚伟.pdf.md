Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 发白系防晒霜导致，涂抹防晒霜零件经80℃/16h高温试验后，水&酒精无法擦拭。 
Whitening is caused by sunscreen. After 80 ℃ / 16h high temperature test, the parts coated with 
sunscreen can’t be wiped by water or alcohol. 
 
建议Suggestions：1. 评估防晒霜等化妆品对高光件的影响并制定标准。 
                    Evaluate the effect of sunscreen and other cosmetics on high gloss parts and formulate standards.
