Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1． 飞轮连接板与轮毂焊接处萌生裂纹，导致其发生多源疲劳断裂。The crack origin in the welding seam between  
the plate and the hub, resulting in the fatigue fracture . 
2． 焊缝（连接板与轮毂）存在焊接缺陷如焊接凹陷、焊接内裂纹。The welding defects such as the crack and the  
shrinkage groove were observed in the weld seam 
3． 焊缝宽度和1/2 焊深处焊缝宽度小于焊接工艺要求。The welding width was less than the requirement. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 调整焊接工艺参数，以保证焊接质量符合工艺要求。Adjusting the welding process parameter and improving the 
welding quality, to meet the requirement.
