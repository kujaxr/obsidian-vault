 Resistance to contaminants 
- spot test method 
1  范围 
本标准规定了抗污染物的现场测试方法。 
本标准适用于元件和组件要求防止液体和
其他污染的情况。仅用于浸渍方法不适用的情
况。 
注1：对于涉及固体污染的试验，应采用标准SMTC 5 
400 198。 
注2：有可能需要进一步测试加压破裂效果，此测试
使用的样件最好是完整件，并在尽量接近实际工况的物
理条件下进行试验。 
 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the spot test method of 
resistance to contaminants. 
 
This standard applies to the test method Which 
is 
applicable 
to 
components 
and 
sub-assemblies where resistance to fluids and 
other contaminants are required. It should 
only be used where immersion methods 
are not applicable. 
 
Note 1: For tests involving solid contaminants SMTC 5 
400 198 is applicable. 
 
Note 2: Further testing may be necessary to check for 
stress cracking effects using test pieces, or preferably 
complete components, under physical restraints as 
near as possible to actual service conditions.

4  程序/测试 
 
4  Procedure / Test 
4.1 确保测试表面清洁干燥。 
 
4.1  Ensure that the surface to be tested is 
clean and dry. 
 
4.2 在水平的表面上标出测试区域，并确定其与
测试材料的关系。必要时，为了固定测试材料，
可选用环氧粘土或短玻璃管或短聚乙烯管将测
试材料的一端与测试面进行密封，根据测试材料
和测试温度来选择。 
 
4.2  Mark the periphery of the test area(s), 
which must be on a horizontal surface, and 
identify relative to the test material. Apply 
means for confining the test material if 
necessary with epoxy plasticine or a short 
length of glass or polythene tubing sealed at 
one end to the test surface, the choice will 
depend on the test material and temperature. 
 
4.3 在准备好的测试区域放置选定材料，使用载
玻片或相似品盖住测试液来尽可能减少蒸发。如
无另行说明，测试在室温下进行24  h。 
 
4.3  Apply the selected materials to the 
prepared test area, cover the test fluid with a 
glass slide or similar to minimise evaporation. 
Unless otherwise stated, test for 24 hours at 
ambient temperature. 
 
4.4 测试后要清理残余材料，使用无棉绒软布轻
轻擦拭测试区域或适当吹干并检查是否有降解 
 
 
4.4  After test remove residual material, 
gently wipe the test area with a soft lint-free 
cloth or blow dry as appropriate and inspect for 
any degradation. 
 
4.5 根据要求，在对第5 节中列出的有害影响
进行初步检验后，要根据SMTC 5 400 003 将测
试区域暴露在光线下，具体条件由产品标准确
定。 
 
4.5 
Where 
required, 
after 
preliminary 
inspection for any detrimental effects as listed 
in Section 5, expose the test area to light in 
accordance with SMTC 5 400 003 the 
conditions to be specified in the Product 
Standard.
