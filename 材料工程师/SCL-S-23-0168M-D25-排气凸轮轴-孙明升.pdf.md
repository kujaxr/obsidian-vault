Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该排气凸轮轴由于残余应力过大导致疲劳剥落。The exhaust camshaft experienced fatigue peeling off due to 
excessive residual stress. 
（2）材料表面硬度、硬化层深度符合要求，金相组织正常。 
The surface hardness and depth of the hardened layer of the material met the requirements, and the metallographic 
structure was normal. There were grinding burn marks on the surface. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）查找残余应力超标的工艺原因，并加强过程管控。 
To find the reason to cause excessive residual stress and strength process control.
