Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 断口具有疲劳特征。The fracture belongs to fatigue fracture. 
2. 硬度：试验车件Ⅲ超硬度稍微出上限，但不会造成失效，其他零件符合技术要求。The hardness for part III from test car 
was a little high, but this did not cause the failure.  The hardness for the other parts meet the requirement. 
3. 金相组织： 
（1）、失效件不合格。The microstructure for failure part doesn’t meet the requirement. 
（2）、试验车件：Ⅰ和Ⅱ不合格；Ⅲ合格。The microstructure for Ⅰand Ⅱ part in testing car  doesn’t meet the 
requirement. The microstructure for Ⅲ part  accords with the requirement. 
（3）、量产件合格。The microstructure for production part accords with the requirement. 
建议Suggestions 
1. 
请进一步分析失效件的工作情况，包括零部件之间的匹配和安装情况。Please check working condition including condition 
of matching part and installation condition. 
2. 
加大圆角和提高机加工质量以提高疲劳寿命。Increase radius, and improve machining quality to improve fatigue life. 
3. 
金相组织的技术要求加入图纸的技术要求中。Add microstructure requirement to the drawing.
