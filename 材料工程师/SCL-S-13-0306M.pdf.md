Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属过载断裂。Failure of the parts due to overload.. 
2、 有效硬化层深度不合格，其他材料性能（硬度、金相组织）未见异常。Effective hardened layer depth did not 
meet the requirementt ,other material properties (hardness and microstructure)were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核失效件在实际工况中的受力情况。 
Please check the force for the failure parts during the actual failure conditions. 
    2、建议法兰轴考虑使用中碳合金钢材料（如40Cr），整体调质处理+表面中频感应淬火处理。 
Recommended flange shaft consider using carbon steel materials (such as 40Cr), the overall quenching + surface 
induction hardened.
