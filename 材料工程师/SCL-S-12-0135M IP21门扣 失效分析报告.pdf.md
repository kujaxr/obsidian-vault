Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 门锁锁扣是由于杆和底板之间产生微动磨损而失效的。The door catches have failed through fretting wear between 
the pin and base plate. 
2. 杆和板之间的接触面积太小。孔直径太大定位不好，产生不均匀接触。 The load contact area between the pin and 
plate is too small.  The large hole diameter prevents good location and creates uneven contact area. 
3. 底板硬度稍高，杆硬度偏低的，但不会影响失效。The plate hardness is slightly high, and the pin hardness is on the 
low side, however this did not influence the failure. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 增加杆和底板间的接触面积；应该考虑使用带锥度的杆，保证与底板良好的接触，见AP11 的设计。Increase the 
load contact area between the pin and plate. Use of the flanged pin, as seen in AP11 design, should be considered.  
2. 增加杆和板材的硬度。. Increase the hardness of the pin and plate together. 
3. 评估驾驶中的载荷。Evaluate the load during driving 
4. 与其它车的设计进行对比。Benchmark other vehicle’s design

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
外观检查Visual Inspection 
 
共收到四件门锁扣。选择三件进行分析，如图1 所示；它们标记为A，B，C。 对A 和B 件的关键尺寸进行测
量，其结果符合图纸要求（见附录1），所以支架没有发生变形。  
拆开后，发现了板和杆间有严重的磨损（图2 至图4）。这种微动磨损是由于驾驶中的负载及振动导致的。 
 
Four door catches were received, three were selected for analysis as shown in figure 1, they were labelled A and B as 
shown.  Key dimensions of both parts were measured, and found to be within specification (see appendix 1), so 
deformation of the bracket did not occur.  
After opening, significant wear was found at the plate, and at the pin (figures 2 to 4).  This fretting wear is due to load 
& vibration during driving.

设计方面Design considerations 
 
如图6 显示了安装的情形。在驾驶过程中，杆板结合处承受着来自门，气压以及振动的沉重负荷。图6b 来自于
装配图，显示孔比杆的直径大。这种设计将阻止杆和底板的良好接触，从而减少接触面积；该区域承受着载荷。
由于材料的硬度低，微动磨损会发生。  
AP11 门锁扣的杆部有一定锥度（图7b 红色箭头所示）。这可以改善接触，以确保与板紧密配合，增加接触面积。 
The installation condition is shown in figure 6.  During driving, the pin-plate joint has to bear heavy loading from the 
door, air pressure and vibrations.  Figure 6b is from the assembly drawing, and shows that the hole is designed to be 
bigger than the pin.  This design prevents good location of the pin and reduces the contact area which has to bear the 
load. Considering the low hardness of the materials, fretting wear has occurred. 
The AP11 door catch pin is flared (figure 7b).  This can improve the situation by ensuring a tight fit in the plate, and 
increasing the contact area.

beneficial effect since the pin is harder than the plate, and is therefore not the cause of the failure.
