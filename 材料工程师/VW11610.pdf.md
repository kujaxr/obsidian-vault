1 
Scope 
The nominal and limit dimensions in Tables 1 to 4 and Figure 1 apply to threads of tolerance 
classes 6g/6H with bright final condition. 
Refer to VW 116 11 for limit dimensions for phosphated threads. 
2 
Designation 
Designation of a metric ISO coarse thread with nominal thread diameter d = 10 mm, flank lead 
P = 1.5 mm and tolerance class 6g for the external thread and tolerance class 6H for the internal 
thread: 
M10 
Designation of a metric ISO fine-pitch thread with nominal thread diameter d = 10 mm, flank lead 
P = 1 mm and tolerance class 6g for the external thread and tolerance class 6H for the internal 
thread: 
M10 x 1 
The English translation is believed to be accurate. 
In case of discrepancies the German version shall govern.
