Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）由于内部表面没有全部凃胶，橡胶与金属件存在间隙，因此在腐蚀测试中，金属表面出现严重的锈蚀，从
而导致橡胶件出现松动。The glue coating is not on all the suface and there is a clearance between the rubber and metal 
parts, so during corrosion test, the inside suface of the metal part was seriously rusted,which led the rubber part to get 
loose. 
（2）中间金属的表面镀层为Zn，不是图纸要求的Zn/Ni涂层。这会影响中间金属件暴漏在外的表面的耐腐蚀性，
但这不是失效的根本原因。The coating of the middle part is Zn, not the required Zn/Ti coating, which will affect the 
corrosion resistance of the middle metal part’s exposed surface. But this is not the root cause of the failure. 
（3）中间金属件的材料组织正常。The microstructure of the middle metal part is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）要求所有的表面均匀涂胶，以防止金属件表面腐蚀和橡胶的松脱。Require all the surface with even glue coating 
and prevent the metal surface to corrode and the rubber part loose . 
（2）采取措施避免混料。Avoid mixing other parts with Zn coating by some means. 
（3）应对接头供应商的生产过程进行审核，以保证过程的稳定性。Audit the produce process of the supplier and 
assure the process is stable and reliable.

工艺及技术要求 Process and Technology requirement 
根据技术要求，外部金属件和中间金属件的表面均采用Zn/Ni 镀层。Zn/Ni 镀层相对镀Zn 层具有更好的耐腐蚀
性。这对于暴漏在外的金属表面是非常重要的。 
According to the technology, the coating of the outside and middle metal parts was Zn/Ni., which was more anticorrosive 
than the Zn coating. This is very important for the exposed surface of metal parts. 
中间金属件完成Zn/Ni 镀层处理后，对内表面进行喷砂处理，然后进行涂胶和硫化操作。涂胶可以保证接头具有
更大的拉脱力；但是对于该产品来说：由于拉脱力要求（≥800N）不高，因此若不涂胶，其拉脱力同样满足要
求。 
After plating Zn/Ni, make a sand blasting and glue coating for the inside surface of the middle metal part and then 
vulcanize. The glue coating can let the part with bigger pulling-out force. But the requirement about the pulling-out force 
is more than 800N, which is not very high, so if not making glue coating, the pulling-force still can meet the 
requirement. 
但是，良好的涂胶可以让橡胶与金属间紧密结合，助碍腐蚀液接触金属表面，防止金属表面锈蚀。但是如果不凃
胶或凃胶不良，橡胶与金属件间会形成缝隙，腐蚀液体进入这些缝隙会恶化腐蚀的发生。 
But good glue coating can let the rubber combine with the metal part more tightly and keep the corrosive solution from 
contact with the metal surface. But if no glue coating and bad glue, there is a clearance between the parts and the 
solution will enter the clearance and accelerate the corrosion.

由于未完全凃胶，中间金属件的内表面未能得到保护。这导致了锈蚀的发生，从而出现了松动。如果完全涂胶，

the corrosion would not happen and the rubber part would be separated easily from the middle metal.

Zn coating don’t meet the coating requirement, but this only reduced the corrosion resistance of the metal which is open.
