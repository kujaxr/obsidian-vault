Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.零件存在两种失效模式，一种为卡扣&螺母间过盈配合导致，一种为异常受力所致。 
There are two failure modes for parts, one is caused by interference fit between buckle & nut, and 
the other is caused by abnormal force. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商优化零件尺寸，修改零件匹配为间隙配合。 
                 The supplier optimized the part size and modified the part matching to a clearance fit. 
2. 优化包装方案，避免物流过程中过大的撞击力。 
  Optimize the packaging scheme and reduce the impact force during logistics.
