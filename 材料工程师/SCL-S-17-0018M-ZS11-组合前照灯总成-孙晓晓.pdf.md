Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．失效件受外力导致脆断。 2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The brittle fracture of the parts caused by external forces. 
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.  
建议： 1.改善零件结构，提高卡脚强度。2.建议更换排水口位置 
Suggestions: 1. Improve the structure of the parts, increase card foot strength.  2. Replace outlets location.
