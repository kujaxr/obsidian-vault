Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 送检断口属于韧性过载断裂。The part belongs to toughness fracture.. 
2、 送检断口和全新支架的硬度和板材厚度符合技术要求，其他材料性能（金相组织、非金属夹杂物、表面脱
碳）未见异常。 
Hardness and sheet thickness meet the technical requirements, other material properties were normal. 
3、 送检断口和全新支架的R 角处存在成形（折弯）工序产生的原始裂纹。 
original cracks in R corner were formed during the forming (bending) process. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 为了确定断裂的根本原因，请提供失效件。 
In order to determine the root cause of breakage, please provide fail pieces. 
2、 消除R 角处的裂纹。Eliminate R corner cracks.
