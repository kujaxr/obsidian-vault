Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 手动拉索支架材料不符合图纸要求，强度不足导致其发生疲劳开裂；The material of BRACKET-PARK BRK FRT 
didn’t meet the drawing requirement. The material strength was low, resulting in the fatigue crack. 
2. 抽检生产上的现有零件，零件材质为DC01，仍为原设计时的状态；The material of the new parts was DC01, which 
was the old stage. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 隔离现有零件并进行风险评估；Separate the parts and evaluate the risk. 
2. 在裂纹起源位置增加一个圆弧角，以降低应力载荷；Add the circular arc at the crack source to reduce the stress.
