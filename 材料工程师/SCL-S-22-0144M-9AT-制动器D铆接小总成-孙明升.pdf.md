Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 制动器D铆接小总成断裂为多源疲劳断裂，起源于高应力处。 
The fracture of brake D riveted small assembly was fatigue fracture, which originated from high stress place. 
（2） 齿圈支架、轴承座化学成分符合要求，硬度、金相正常。 
Chemical composition met the requirements, hardness and metallography structure were normal.
