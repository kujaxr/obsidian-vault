Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂。The bolt has failed due to fatigue fracture. 
2、 失效件存在螺牙缺陷；全新件也存在螺牙缺陷。. The fail part has the thread defects .New parts have the thread 
defects too. 
3、 螺栓其他性能符合技术要求。The material properties meet to the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 隔离/停用此批螺栓。Isolation and stop use this batch of bolts. 
2、 供应商立即整改螺牙缺陷。Suppliers immediate rectification thread defects 
3、 检查螺栓的装配过程。To check the conditions for the use and the assembly 
4、 校核螺栓的受力情况。Checking bolts force during using.
