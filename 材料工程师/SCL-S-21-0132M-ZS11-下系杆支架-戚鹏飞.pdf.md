Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：（1）下系杆支架零件为过载断裂。The part of lower tie bracket is overload fracture.  
                （2）下系杆支架的成分、硬度、孔隙度符合要求，金相组织正常。The chemical composition、
hardness and porosity of lower tie bracket parts are qualified, and the metallographic structure 
is normal.  
建议Suggestions：（1）排查制动试验及路试工况情况。Check the braking test and road test conditions.
