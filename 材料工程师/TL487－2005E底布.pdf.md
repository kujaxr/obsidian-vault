1 
Scope 
This technical supply specification specifies the material requirements for PA-knit and circular-
knitted fabric, used for example as base knit fabrics for compound padding. 
2 
Description 
Description example for a base knit fabric for compound padding with 14.0 mm foam lamination. 
PA-knit according to TL 487-A 
3 
Requirements 
3.1 
General requirements 
Approval of first supply and changes according to Volkswagen standard VW 011 55. 
Avoidance of hazardous substances according to VW 911 01. 
Emission behavior according to VW 501 80. 
The required minimum for complete testing is 1.5 m2. 
3.2 
Special requirements 
With constant processing conditions, the laminated types must allow proper processing. The 
material may not contain substances which could adversely affect high-frequency weldability and 
the bond stability, like textile finishes and/or foreign matter. 
The laminated knit fabrics and circular-knitted fabrics may not tear before the face fabric during 
testing of tear strength in the total structure. 
The knit fabrics and circular-knitted fabrics shall be distortion-free.

5 
Notes on testing 
5.1 
Weight per unit area 
A minimum of five round specimens of 100 cm2 each shall be taken on the diagonal of the total 
width of the fabric. No individual specimen may be below the required minimum value. 
5.2 
Tear strength 
Determination of the tear strength in the strip tensile test acc. to DIN EN ISO 13934-1 at a traverse 
speed of 100 mm/min and a free clamping length (gage length) of 100 mm. The material is 
clamped between the leatherette-lined clamping jaws of the tensile test machine. Specimens torn 
in the area of the clamping jaws shall not be evaluated if the results are below the lowest individual 
value for an intact specimen. No individual value may fall below the minimum requirement.
