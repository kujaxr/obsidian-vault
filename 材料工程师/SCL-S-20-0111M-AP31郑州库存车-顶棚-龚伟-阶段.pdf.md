结论Conclusions：1. 顶棚黄斑为异物沾染所致，目前分析出的异物均为聚氨酯胶。 
The macula is contaminated by unknown object, which is polyurethane glue analyzed so far.

                   Investigate the source of contaminated polyurethane glue.
