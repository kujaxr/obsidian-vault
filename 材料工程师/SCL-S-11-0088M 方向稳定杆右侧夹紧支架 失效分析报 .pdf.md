Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
  该支架因疲劳而失效, 来自最高压力的区的多源位置. The bracket has failed through fatigue, originating at several points in the 
area of highest loading.  
  支架材料没有缺陷, 成分和金相组织符合要求. 屈服强度无法测试，但硬度测试表明，抗拉强度是在正确的
范围. There were no material defects, the chemical composition and microstructure met the specification.  Yield strength could not be tested, 
but hardness testing indicates the tensile strength is in the correct range.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
  应对来自悬架系统的外加负载进行检查，尤其是减震器和球关节。 The applied load from the suspension should be 
checked, especially the function of the damper and ball joints.  
  考虑提高材料的强度或厚度，以防止疲劳失效。Consider to increase the material strength or thickness to prevent fatigue failure.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
使用情况Application situation 
失效的支架夹紧前防倾杆到前副车架。外加荷载来自悬挂减震器，通过环节和球关节 (见图1). 球关节和悬挂减震器也
应进行检查，以确保它们正常工作。 
The failed bracket holds the front anti-roll bar to the front sub-frame.  Applied load is from the suspension damper, via the drop link and ball joints (see 
figure 1).  The ball joints and suspension damper should also be checked, to ensure they are functioning correctly.
