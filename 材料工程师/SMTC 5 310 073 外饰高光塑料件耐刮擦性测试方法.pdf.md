1　范围
    
本标准规定了汽车外饰高光塑料件耐刮擦
性试验测试方法。
本标准适用于测试汽车外部非涂装高光塑
料零件的耐刮擦性。除非在技术标准中另有特
殊规定。
1  Scope 
This standard specifies a procedure for testing 
the scratch resistance. 
This test standard serves to determine the 
scratch resistance of unpainted high gloss 
plastic exterior components. Unless otherwise 
specified in the technical standard.

3　术语
耐刮擦是指零件受外力机械作用（例如，
锐利边缘或倒棱物刮伤）的阻力。
3  Term 
The scratch resistance of plastics is defined as 
the resistance offered by the material to a 
mechanical stress, in this case the scratching 
motion of a sharp edge.

5　测试
5
Test
5.1  定义
5.1  Definition
刮擦头以(1000±50)mm/min 的速率在塑料
件表面刮出40 mm 的刮痕。接着对照未刮划的
表面，观察刮痕形态。
A scratching gouge scratches the sample a 
distance 
of 
40mm 
by 
a 
speed 
of 
1000±50mm/minute. 
Then 
compared 
the 
scratching line to the unscratched surface.
5.2  测试设备
5.2  Test equipment
Erichsen-硬度试棒，型号318，最大20N
负荷，刮擦头直径为0.75 mm 硬质合金顶尖。
Test with the Erichsen hardness tester, type 
318, with max 20 N load and a hard metal tip 
φ=0.75mm.
5.3  样品要求
5.3  Sample request
5.3.1  用于提交认可的样品必须从被测试的零
件中加工获取，表面必须均质，清洁而无油腻。
并且不允许有受污染的痕迹和被触摸。
5.3.1  
The submitted samples must be 
selected from the tested parts with clean 
homogeneous surface, which should not show 
the imprint of being polluted or touched.  
5.3.2  样品大小要确保有足够的面积进行测试。
建议的尺寸至少是80 mm×50 mm，如果测试部
位面积非常小，允许在不影响测试表面和试验
的情况进行镶嵌等方式，来达到样品的尺寸要
求。
5.3.2  The sample should be big enough to 
be measured. The advised size is bigger than 
80 mm×50 mm. If the area for measurement is 
quite small, inlay could be used to satisfy the 
size request, however the surface of the 
sample and the test results could not be 
influenced. 
5.3.3  取样位置根据客户或供需双方的约定，
如果没有这些特殊约定，遵循平整和手能够触
及部位的原则。可以是若干零件的相同部位或
同一零件的不同部位。用照片在记录中体现取
样部位，并用文字说明取样部位的颜色、外观
和可能影响结果的其它外观情况。
5.3.3  
Measurement 
position. 
The 
measurement position should be identified 
according to customer’s request or the 
agreement of supplying and requisitioning 
parties. If not, the smooth area and easy 
touched area should be selected. The 
measurement positions could be the same 
position on different samples or different 
positions 
on 
the 
same 
sample. 
The 
measurement position should be recorded in 
photos with the notes including the color, 
surface and other related surface morphology 
of the measured position.  
5.3.4  对于形状特殊等原因，使获得的样品可
能会影响检测结果的，必须给予记录。
5.3.4 If the shape of the sample is unusual, 
this might have an effect on the measurement 
results and, it should be recorded on the test 
report.
5.3.5   
数量：要求每次测试至少取3 个平行
样件，记录每一个样件的测量数值。
5.3.5 Sample number: the test should be taken 
on at least 3 parallel samples, and each of the 
measurement results should be recorded. 
5.4   预处理
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291 标准气
候条件23 ℃、50 %RH 下至少放置24 h。
5.4  Conditioning
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 24h before test.

5.5  刮擦
用硬度笔，在试样上划至少40 mm 的刮痕。
测试参数：
a) 负载：F=(最大20) N（作用于测试表
面的作用力）；
b) 刮划速率：V=(1000±50 ) mm/min；
c) 刮头：φ=0.75mm
5.5  Scratch
Using scratch tester imprint a 40 mm line on 
the 
sample 
surface. 
Measurement 
parameters:
a)
Force: F=(Max 20) N (The force acted 
on the measurement surface) ;
b)
Scratch speed: V=(1000±50) mm/min;
c)
Tip: φ=0.75mm
5.5.1
刮擦时的注意事项
在实验过程中应注意以下内容：
a) 需在(23±5) ℃环境下进行测试。
b) 刮擦前必须检查负载是否调整至规定的
要求；
c) 刮擦前必须检查刮擦头及样品的固定是
否可靠，刮擦头是否在有效使用要求
内，样件固定后是否存在由于受压而使
刮擦面有明显凹或凸的情况，如果有这
样的情况必须给予调整和避免；
5.5.1  Notes 
During the experiment, the following must be 
focused on:
a) The test should be taken in (23±5) ℃
b) The force should be set as requested 
before scratch;
c)  Make sure both the scratch tip and 
sample are fixed properly before 
scratch. The scratch tip should follow 
the application standard. If there are 
obvious sags and crests on the 
measurement 
surface 
after 
the 
sample was fixed, it should be 
modified;
5.6 结果评价（刮擦后）
要求： 基材无明显的刮伤，但是由负荷造
成的基材的压痕是允许的。与刮面垂直角度观
察划痕无明显发白。
5.6  
The 
results 
requirement 
(after 
scratch)
Requirement: The material should be no 
scuffing.  An indentation mark in the substrate 
caused by the load is allowed. No white 
scratching line in the vertical observation.
