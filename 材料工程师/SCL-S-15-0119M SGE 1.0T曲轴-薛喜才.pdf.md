Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 曲轴是由于受异常载荷引起的低周疲劳断裂。Crankshaft was broken off by fatigue because of the abnormal load. 
 
2. 失效件成分、硬度及连杆颈滚压圆角符合技术要求，硬化层深度略高于要求，零件微观组织正常。Chemical 
components, hardness and the fillet radius met the technical requirement. Depth of harden case was beyond the 
requirement. Microstructure was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查试验台架。Check the testing bench. 
 
2. 校核实际载荷。Check the load.
