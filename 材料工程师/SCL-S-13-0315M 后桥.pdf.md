Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 扭转梁开裂后，断口两侧金属由于相互挤压、碰撞及腐蚀作用，其形貌已无法辨别；但其失效形式与之前案
例很相似，为疲劳开裂；The torsion beam cracking, both sides of fracture surface sides of metal due to the mutual 
extrusion and corrosion, the morphology cannot be observe. But the failure forms was similar with the former case, it 
was broken by fatigue. 
2. 在开裂区域附近，未发现任何材料缺陷及表面损伤；No material defects or damage were observed near the crack 
region. 
3. 扭转梁材质符合要求；The material met the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.关注扭转梁开裂处实际应力载荷；Pay attention to the active stress at crack region.
