Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）发动机缸体开裂是疲劳开裂，起源于表面较小缺陷。  
The crack of engine cylinder block was fatigue crack, which originated from surface small defect. 
（2）材料化学成分符合要求，硬度、孔隙率、金相组织正常。 
The chemical composition of the material met the requirements, and the hardness, porosity and metallographic structure 
were normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）校核新状态缸体该处所受载荷状况。 
Check the load condition of this place in the new state cylinder block.
