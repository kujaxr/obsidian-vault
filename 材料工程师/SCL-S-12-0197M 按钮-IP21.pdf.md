Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件内应力较高，在高温环境或装配、使用环节受外力容易发生开裂。 
Conclusions:  1. Internal stress of part was on the high side. High temperature and external force may make part split . 
建议： 1. 优化生产工艺，降低零件内应力。 
Suggestions: 1. Process should be improved to reduce internal stress.
