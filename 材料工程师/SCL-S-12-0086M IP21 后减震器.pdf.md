Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）减振器活塞杆是由于过载而断裂的。The piston bar of the damper failed because of overload. 
（2）材料的成分和组织符合要求。The chemical composition and microstructure meet the requirements. 
（3）镀铬层硬度和表面硬度符合技术要求。The Cr layer hardness and surface hardness meet the requirements. 
（4）材料的基体硬度偏高一点，但不会导致零件失效。The hardness of the matrix is a little higher than the 
requirement, but this can’t cause the part failure. 
（5）淬硬层深度不符合要求，但这不会导致产品失效。The hardening depth does not meet the requirement, but this 
can’t lead the failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）售后服务的工程师和客户联系，了解其使用情况。Request the engineer for after service connect to the customer 
and get the use condition. 
（2）控制工艺，保证淬硬层深度满足技术要求。Control the process and assure the hardening depth meets the 
requirement.
