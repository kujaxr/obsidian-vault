Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 通过失效件的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，装配时橡胶垫未装配到位，连接两处破损压痕的长条压痕以外的橡胶
垫未压入间隙内，造成曲轴减震器滑移扭矩降低，工作时导致曲轴减振器齿环脱落。 
Conclusions: After testing, the material of the rubber cushion conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the macroscopic 
observation and material test of the TV damper, it can be seen that the rubber cushion wasn’t impressed in place which caused 
the slip torque to decrease. During it was working, the ring gear dropped out from the TV damper by a side force. 
建议：对成品橡胶圈进行长期厚度和硬度抽查和记录；保证橡胶圈装配前硬度满足图纸要求；保证橡胶圈装配到位。 
Suggestions：The thickness and hardness of the rubber cushion should be random inspected and reported for long term. The hardness of 
the rubber cushion should conform to the drawing requirement. The rubber cushion should be assembled in place.
