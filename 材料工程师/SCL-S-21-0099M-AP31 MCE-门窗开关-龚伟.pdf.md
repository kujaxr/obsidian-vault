Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件共2种形式，第一种为装配干涉导致应力开裂，第二种为受外力后开裂。 
There were two types of failure parts, the first was stress cracking caused by assembly 
interference, and the second was cracked by external force. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 第一种失效：供应商优化装配工装，消除装配干涉问题。 
                    The first failure mode: Suppliers optimize assembly tooling to eliminate assembly interference. 
2. 第二种失效：排查异常受力环节。 
                    The second failure mode: Check abnormal forced processes.
