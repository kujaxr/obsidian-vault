1  范围Scope 
本标准规定了乘客舱内聚丙烯的材料要求。 
本标准适用于乘客舱内各部件上聚丙烯材
料，如仪表板、门板、立柱、门槛饰板、尾门饰
板等。 
 
This 
standard 
specifies 
the 
material 
requirements for polypropylene in passenger 
compartment trims. 
This standard applies to the polypropylene 
materials used in components of passenger 
compartment trims, e.g. dashboard, door trim 
panels, pillar trim panels, door trays, rear door 
trim panel and so on.

3.2 特性Feature 
3.2.1  在加强筋、增强区域和管接头处不允许
存在凹痕，填充玻纤的材料，其表面在触觉或视
觉上都不应出现瑕疵。 
 
3.2.1 Sink marks at the base of ribs, 
reinforcements and pipe unions are not 
permitted. Materials containing glass fibers 
must exhibit a surface that is free of 
imperfections both to the eye and the touch. 
3.2.2  成品件中的玻纤分布应均匀，在成品件
的不同部位所取的三个试样中的玻纤含量之差
不应超过1.0%。 
 
3.2.2 
The 
distribution 
of 
glass 
fibers 
throughout the component must be sufficiently 
uniform so that three specimens taken from 
different points on a finished part do not differ 
in glass fiber content by more than 1.0 %.

3.7  实验备注 Notes on testing 
 
 
3.7.1  弯曲弹性模量 Modulus of elasticity 
在ISO 178所规定的最大力条件下测定弯曲
强度，但是试样：长度（501）mm，宽度（100.6）
mm，厚度按照产品厚度（最大为4 mm）；跨距
40 mm；试验速度（141）mm/min，支承半径
1.0 mm到1.2 mm。如果是从一面带有皮纹或油
漆的成品件上取下的试样，在将其放置到试验机
支架上时，应使未带皮纹或未油漆的一面被拉伸。 
 
Determination of flexural stress at maximum 
load acc. to ISO 178; specimen: length (50±1) 
mm, width (10±0.6) mm, thickness acc. to the 
products thickness (max. 4 mm); support 
separation 40 mm; test rate (14 ± 1) mm/min, 
support radius 1.0 to 1.2 mm. Specimens taken 
from finished parts grained on one side shall be 
positioned on the support of the test equipment 
so that the non-grained side is subjected to 
tensile stress. 
3.7.2  冲击韧性 Impact resistance 
试验符合ISO179-1；偏差试样长度：(50±1)

Impact resistance test (broadside impact)
