Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 传动轴是由于预先存在内部裂纹而失效的。 The driveshaft has failed due to a pre-existing internal crack.  
2、 内部裂纹是感应淬火过程之前产生的，有可能是锻造过程中出现的。该裂纹类似单向拉伸过载的裂纹。
这些载荷可以在锻造中产生，但是，这应当由供应商调查确认。 The internal crack was created before the 
induction hardening process, possibly during forging.  The crack’s morphology is similar to uniaxial tension 
overload.  Such forces could be generated during forging, but this should be confirmed by the supplier’s 
investigation. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 停止使用这一批次的转动轴。Stop using this batch of parts 
2、 对新的部件进行裂纹检测，方法如超声波检测。Implement crack detection method such as ultrasonic test on 
new parts 
3、 供应商必须研究开裂的原因Supplier must investigate the reason for fracture

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
收到的传动轴如图1 所示。花健，螺纹和其它外部表面没有损害或异常迹象。初步观察断口具有拉伸过载
的外观特征（图1）。感应淬火区域似乎清晰的处于外圈，但是从横截面金相试样来看，感应淬火区比断口
所显示的深度更深。 
The driveshaft was received as shown in figure 1.  There were no abnormal marks or signs of damage to the 
spline, threaded part or other external surfaces.  On initial observation, the fracture has signs of tensile overload 
(figure 1).  The characteristic induction hardnened region is seemingly distiguishable around the outer 
circumference, however, in cross section, it can be seen that the IH region is considerably deeper than the fracture 
morphology suggests.

断口分析Fracture analysis 
 
图2 是断口横截面腐蚀后的照片。感应淬火区域，可以清晰地分辨出，硬层深度如图所示（硬度基准550 HV1）。
断裂的方向是从内到外，与拉伸超载一致。然而，因为裂纹进入硬化区域的断口形貌不改变，开裂区“A”的组
织不均有连续性；这说明断口是感应淬火前产生的。图3，图4 和图5 是电镜下图2 所示的位置的照片；由图可
见，位置＃3 和＃5 的断口具有早期熔融的特征，表明该区域预先有裂纹存在。 
Figure 2 is a cross section through the fracture after etching.  The induction hardened region can be clearly 
distinguished, and the hard layer depth (to 550 HV1) is indicated.  The fracture direction is from inside to outside, 
consistent with tensile overload. However the fracture morphology as it enters the hardened region does not change, and 
the microstructure of the fractured layer in area “A” does not match with the material below, indicating the fracture 
occurred prior to the hardening process.  Figures 3, 4 and 5 are SEM and microstructure images from the locations 
indicated in figure 2, and show incipient surface melting of the fracture at positions #3 & #5.

材料分析 Material Analysis 
 
化学成分分析表明，该材料满足供应商的规范要求。断口附近测量硬化层深度，合格。  
芯部的金相组织正常的，但是，断口附近的奥氏体晶粒尺寸非常大的。有可能，是由于感应淬火过程中有裂纹的
局部过热. 新样品马氏体晶粒小很多（图8）。  
Chemical composition analysis showed that the material met the requirements of the supplier’s specification.  The 
hardening depth was measured near to the fracture, and was also acceptable.  
The core microstructure was normal, however, the former austenite grain size near to the fracture was very large.  It’s 
likely that this is due to local overheating during hardening as a result of the existing fracture.  The new sample grain 
size was much smaller (figure 8).
