Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 拉手注塑工艺较差，造成拉手熔合区结合不充分，同时拉手与拉手壳体存在尺寸干涉，容易导致在
拉手中心位置（熔合区）开裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of the plastic handle conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the plastic handle, it can be seen that there is an incomplete 
weld appeared at the weld zone due to the bad injection process. And, size interference exists between the 
handle and the handle shell. 
建议：优化拉手注塑工艺；检查并消除拉手尺寸干涉；更改熔合区位置。 
Suggestions：The injection process should be optimized. The size interference should be avoided. The position of 
weld zone should be changed.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
图2 是失效件表面形貌，断口比较平直，位于拉手的中心位置。图3 是新件形貌，从表面可以清楚地看到熔
接痕和浇口，可以判定失效件的断口位于熔接痕区域。通过图4 和图5 可以看出拉手两侧的断面异常平整光滑，
在断面上分布少量粗糙连接区。初步判定注塑工艺不好，造成拉手熔合区结合不充分，导致该区域的强度降低。 
From Fig 2, it can be seen that the crack is straight and locates at the center region of the handle. The weld line and 
runner can be observed in Fig 3 clearly and the weld line locates at the center region of the handle. The crack surface very 
smooth as shown in Fig 4 and Fig 5 which indicates the injection process is bad.
