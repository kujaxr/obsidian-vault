1 
Scope 
The nominal and limit dimensions in Tables 1 to 4 and Figure 1 apply to the bright final condition. 
See VW 116 15 for limit dimensions for phosphated or electrolytically treated threads. 
Tolerance class "medium" according to VW 116 10 is generally released. Tolerance class "coarse" 
has a smaller thread overlap and requires special gages. It may therefore only be approved in spe-
cial cases, e.g. for plastic parts, if the accuracy of the tolerance class "medium" cannot be complied 
with. It must be specified in the drawing. 
 
2 
Designation 
Designation of a metric ISO standard thread with nominal thread diameter d = 10 mm and flank 
lead P = 1.5 mm, tolerance class 8g for external thread and 7H for internal thread: 
M10 - 8g (external thread) 
 
 
 
M10 – 7H (internal thread) 
Designation of a metric ISO fine-pitch thread with nominal thread diameter d = 10 mm and flank 
lead P = 1.25 mm, tolerance class 8g for external thread and 7H for internal thread: 
M 10 x 1.25 – 8 g (external thread)  
 
M 10 x 1.25 – 7H (internal thread) 
The English translation is believed to be accurate. 
In case of discrepancies the German version shall govern.
