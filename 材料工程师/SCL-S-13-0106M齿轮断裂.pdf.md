Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1． 该断齿是由过载引起的。The gear fractured, resulting from the over load. 
2． 金相正常，表面硬度和芯部硬度未见异常，渗碳层深度符合要求。Microstructure was normal, the surface and  
core hardness was normal, but the harden case met the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.工程试验时应采用符合要求的零件。Make sure the parts meet the requirement when it was experimented .
