Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件系冷模件流出，装配时开裂。 
The failure part flowed out of the cold module and cracked during assembly. 
2.  失效件件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the failure part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  供应商排查冷模件流出原因并整改。 
                 Supplier investigates the cause of cold module outflow and rectifies. 
2.  评估将现有生产工艺改为嵌件注塑可行性。 
                 Evaluate the feasibility of changing the existing production process to insert injection molding. 
3.  熔接痕处增加溢料井。 
                 Add overflow well at the weld line.
