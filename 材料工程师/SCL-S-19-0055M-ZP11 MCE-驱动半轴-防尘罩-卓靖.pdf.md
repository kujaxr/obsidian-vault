Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：防尘罩裂口为外物损伤所致，撕裂方向由外及内。 
Conclusion: The crack of the boot was caused by foreign body injury, and the tear direction was from outside to inside.   
 
建议：核查零件使用工况，确保零件不受外物异常损伤。 
Suggestion: Check the working condition of the part, ensure that the part is not affected by any abnormal foreigner matter.
