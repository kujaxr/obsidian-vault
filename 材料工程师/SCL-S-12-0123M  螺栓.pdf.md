Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于未经充分调质热处理导致强度不足引起的断裂。The tensile strength of the fractured bolts was 
insufficient, due to inadequate heat treatment.  This led to failure of the bolt during assembly. 
2、 失效件的芯部硬度和基体金相组织不能符合技术要求。Hardness & microstructure do not meet requirements. 
3、 检测分选后的不合格螺栓（共11 件）硬度主要集中在147—268HV 范围内，有1 件硬度是310HV；金相
组织均不符合技术要求。On testing suspect parts, 11 were found Not OK. For 10 failed parts, hardness was 147- 
268 HV10, one part’s hardness was 310 HV10.  These 11 parts’ microstructure did not meet requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强过程控制。Control the manufacturing process. 
2、 与失效件同批次的螺栓停止使用并隔离。Stop using bolts from this batch.
