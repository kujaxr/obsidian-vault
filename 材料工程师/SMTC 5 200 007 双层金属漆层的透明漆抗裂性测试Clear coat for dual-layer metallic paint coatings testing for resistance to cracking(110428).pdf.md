本标准适用于测试双金属漆层中的透明漆
的抗裂性。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies a procedure for testing 
the resistance to cracking of the clear coats used in 
dual-layer paint coatings.  
The standard is adopted to test the resistance to 
cracking of the clear coats used in dual-layer paint 
coatings.

3.2  试样制备 
除非另有约定，必须使用大小适合相关测
试设备、厚度为 0.8 mm 的钢板（车身板件）作
为涂漆基底。为确保测试结果不受腐蚀影响，
必须采用底漆保护钢板。为确保风化结果尽量
与现实接近，必须使用原漆结构；根据第3.3 节
保持涂层厚度尤其重要。 
 
 
3.2  Specimen preparation 
Unless otherwise agreed, 0,8 mm thick steel 
panels (body panels) of a size suitable to the relevant 
test equipment must be used as the paint substrate. 
To ensure that the test results are not corrupted by 
the influence of corrosion, the steel panels must be 
protected by a primer coat. In order to ensure that the 
weathering result is as close to reality aspossible, the 
original paint structure must be used; adherence to 
the layer thickness acc. to Section 3.3 is particularly 
important.

3.3  测试标准 
3.3  Test standards 
3.3.1  为监控风化装置的连续操作，必须使用
开裂性和时间均已明了的透明漆。风化装置在
测试过程中始终保持这些测试标准，并在开始
开裂时即更换。它们按以下标准制作： 
a) 涂漆基底：0.8 mm 厚的车身板件金属
薄片； 
b) 预处理：锌磷化； 
c) 腐蚀保护：电泳； 
d) 结构： 
1)  装填物； 
2)  底漆：金属银（无抗光剂），约 15 
μm 厚的涂层； 
3)  透明漆： BASF L+M 、Münster 
Hiltrup 的 FF 68-0102，约 40 
μm 至 50 μm 厚的涂层。 
3.3.1  In order to monitor the continuous operation of 
the weathering device, clear paints for which the 
cracking behavior and times are known must be 
used. These test standards remain in the weathering
device constantly during testing and are replaced 
when they start to crack. They are produced as fol-
lows: 
a) Paint substrate: 0,8-mm-thick body panel 
sheet metal; 
b) Pretreatment: zinc-phosphated; 
c) Corrosion protection: cathodic dip paint; 
d) Structure: 
1)  Filler; 
2)  Base coat: silver metallic (without light 
stabilizers), approx. 15 μm layer thick-
ness; 
3)  Clear coat: FF 68-0102 from BASF 
L+M, Münster Hiltrup, approx. 40 μm to 
50 μm layer thickness. 
3.3.2  晾干10 min，然后在 +130 °C 的机械
循环烘箱中烤 20 min。 
3.3.2  10 min flash-off time, then baked for 20 min at 
+130 °C in the mechanical circulation oven. 
 
3.3.3  室温下液态漆的老化期最长为 12 个
月。室温下测试标准的老化期最长为 24 个月。
 
3.3.3  The aging period of the liquid paints is max. 
12 months at room temperature.The aging period of 
the test standards is max. 24 months at room tem-
perature. 
3.4  步骤 
最好至少在各个涂漆系统的两块试样面板
上执行测试。装置条件如表1 所述。 测试过程
中，确保无水滴溅落到试样上非常重要。每次
模拟下雨阶段必须用更新的水进行喷淋，且必
须无反应生产物、研磨材料和二氧化硅。 
 
3.4  Procedure 
The tests are preferably conducted on at least 
two specimen panels per paint system. The device 
conditions are described in Table 1.During testing it is 
important to ensure that no spray water stains form 
on the specimens. The spray water must be renewed 
in every rain simulation phase and must be free of 
reaction products, abraded materials, and SiO2.

3.5  评估 
每 500 h（按标准是 250 h 后进行检查，
然后是每隔 24 h 检查一次）使用放大镜（8 倍
放大）检查一次试样面板上的裂纹形成情况。
如有疑问，必须使用立式显微镜（放大 20 倍）。
开裂时间是指在油漆表面上至少有 10 条明显
裂痕后的测试期。必须按 DIN ISO 4628-4 描
述裂纹类型和数量。必须按 DIN 67530 在风化
开始前和结束后评估光泽变化。 
 
3.5  Evaluation 
The specimen panels are examined for the forma-
tion of cracks every 500 h (standards after 250 h, 
then every 24 h thereafter) using a magnifying glass 
(8× magnification). In cases of doubt, a stereo mi-
croscope (20× magnification) must be used. The start 
of cracking is the test period after which at least 10 
cracks are evident on the paint surface. The type and 
number of cracks must be described according to 
DIN ISO 4628-4. Changes in gloss must be eva-
luated before and after weathering in accordance 
with DIN 67530. 
3.6  测试报告 
在测试报告中提供以下信息： 
a)  漆结构的类型和名称； 
b) 透明漆层的厚度； 
c) 出现裂纹的时间；  
d) 裂纹类型和数量； 
e)  泡疤上和异物杂质上的非油漆开裂时
间； 
f) 光泽变化； 
g) 油漆涂层的其他变化，例如变黄和起
泡； 
h) 测试日期。 
 
3.6  Test report 
The following information must be provided in 
the test report: 
a) 
Type and designation of the paint structure; 
b) 
Layer thickness of the clear coat; 
c) 
Start of cracking according to device hours; 
d) 
Type and number of cracks; 
e) 
Start of non-paint-specific cracking on blis- 
ters and foreign matter inclusions; 
f) 
Change in gloss; 
g) 
Other changes in the paint coating, such as 
yellowing or blistering; 
h) 
Test date.
