Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 右后门外板在油漆工艺之前发生严重的缩颈和微裂纹。 
Panel-rr s/d otr lh happened necking and cracking before the oil paint.  
2. 零件成分符合要求，硬度及组织正常。 
Chemical components met the requirement. Hardness and microstructure were normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查零件的成型工艺。 
Check the deformation process.
