Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 摩擦环在R 角位置发生疲劳断裂失效，这可能与工作应力较大有关。The balk ring fatigue cracked at R angle. 
Which may be relation to the actual working stress. 
（2） 失效摩擦环硬度和金相组织正常。The hardness and microstructure of failed balk ring are normal. 
 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核开裂区域的实际工作应力。Check the actual working stress of the cracked area.
