3.2 特性 
3.2  Appearance
3.2.1  缺陷部位，如缩孔和裂纹，不得影响成
品件的功能、可安装性和使用寿命。 
3.2.1  Flaws such as voids or cracks shall not 
impair function, assembly or service life of the 
finished parts. 
3.2.2  表面不得带有明显的缺陷。不得出现诸
如装饰材料分离、装饰材料错位或装饰材料翘
曲、光斑和缩孔、粘胶剂渗出（表皮粘接而成的
立柱内饰）、渗料（织物表皮注射复合的立柱内
饰）、折皱等外观缺陷。 
3.2.2  The surface appearance shall be free 
of visible defects. Flaws in appearance, such 
as detached decorative material,  is matched 
or distorted decorative material, shiny areas 
and sink marks, adhesive bleed-through (with 
laminated pillar trim panels), visible plastic on 
fabric surface(with back-molded pillar trim pa-
nels) or wrinkling are not permissible. 
3.2.3  各边必须光洁无毛刺（可采用将边封住
的修边工艺或包边）。粘接包边时应使用聚酰胺
基的熔料。 
3.2.3  The edges shall be properly finished 
without fraying, etc. (cutting methods that seal 
the edges or fold-overs may be used). A po-
lyamide-based hot-melt adhesive shall be 
used for bonded fold-overs. The edges shall 
be properly finished  
3.2.4  表面覆盖料在被加压后不得出现残余变
形（即压痕）。当表面覆盖料为织物时，在使用
过程不能够从布料中抽出单独的一根线。 
3.2.4  After pressure loading the trims must 
not exhibit any permanent deformation (pres-
sure marks).In the case of textile trims, the 
design of the fabric must be such that individ-
ual threads cannot be pulled out during use. 
3.2.5  在老化（大气曝晒试验、气候温度存放、
老化试验）后，表面覆盖料不得出现明显可见的、
超出允许公差范围的变化。 
3.2.5  After aging (open-air weathering, envi-
ronmental aging, aging test) the trims must not 
exhibit any visual changes outside the re-
leased tolerance. 
3.3 对人的亲和性 
3.3 Physiological compatibility
饰件总成使用的材料不得损害人的身体健
康，不允许出现诸如刺激皮肤或气味污染（由甲
醛、氨、苯乙烯或酚引起的）的现象。散发性部
件所散发出的物质必须无毒性。 
The materials used for the trim panel 
ASSY shall be physiologically safe and shall, 
for example, not induce skin irritation or objec-
tionable odors (e.g., formaldehyde, ammonia, 
styrene or phenol).Emitting constituents shall 
be toxicologically safe.

4  Component requirements
4.1 颜色和皮纹 
用比色法将零件与经过批准的原始样件进
行比较，颜色一致性满足SMTC 5 400 002。通过
目测将皮纹或表面结构与经过批准的样品进行
比较。 
4.1  Color and grain 
The color is compared colorimetrically to 
the released original sample (requires an 
original sample or working sample for the pil-
lar).Color constancy according to SMTC 5 400 
002.The grain or the surface structure is 
compared visually to the released sample. 
4.2  大气暴晒 
原则上1年大气暴晒试验，无抱怨。（该项
试验由上汽乘用车进行） 
4.2  Open-air weathering 
Basic quality criterion is open-air wea-
thering for one year without complaint. (This 
test is performed exclusively by the SAIC 
Group.) 
4.3  气候交变试验 
零件按SMTC 5 400 005进行20周期的存放试
验，试验尽可能在安装状态下进行，试验后零件
形状和表面不得出现变化。对于注射复合或粘合
表皮的部件，试验后不得出现装饰材料局部脱
落、气泡、翘曲等不良缺陷。 
4.3  Environmental cycle test 
20 cycles according to SMTC 5 400 005. 
Testing shall be performed in as-installed po-
sition if possible. There shall be no changes of 
shape or surface and no structural changes of 
the surface. In the case of back-molded or la-
minated components, partial separation of 
decorative material or blistering is not permit-
ted.
