Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于剧烈震动，载荷偏大引起的疲劳断裂。 
The failure is fatigue fracture, due to severe vibration, caused by too large a load. 
2、 失效件的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The material properties of the part failured met the technical requirements. 
  建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查零件实际的受力情况，降低振动，并校核其安全系数。 
Check the part of the actual stress situation ,reduce vibration, and check the safety factor.
