Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）离合器壳体是由于过载而断裂的。The clutch housing was broken because of overload. 
（2）材料合格。Material meets the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查实验过程是否有异常情况发生。Check if the experiment process is normal. 
（2）利用CAE 对载荷进行校核。Check the load by CAE.
