Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于表面腐蚀引起的疲劳断裂。 
The springs failed through fatigue, which was initiated from heavy corrosion pitting at the spring surface.  
2、 失效件的材料性能未见异常。 
The material properties of the part is normal. 
 
  建议Suggestions： 
1、 优化周边结构，以降低对零件的腐蚀强度。 
Optimize the surrounding protection structure to reduce the environment’s corrosion intensity. 
2、请校核表面保护的能力。 
To check the ability of the surface protection.
