Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）散热器的侧板和扁管均是由于疲劳而开裂；裂纹产生于应力较大的区域。The side plate and tube of the radiator 
were cracked by fatigue, which happened at the area with a big stress. 
（2）散热器的侧板成分和板厚符合要求；硬度和组织正常。The chemical components and thickness of the side plate 
meet the requirements and the hardness and microstructure are normal. 
（3）扁管的板厚符合要求；硬度和组织正常。The thickness of the tube meets the requirement and the hardness and 
microstructure are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）在侧板上开应力槽，提高侧板释放热应力的能力，降低断裂处的应力。Make stress groove on the side plate to 
improve the ability of releasing thermal stress and reduce the stress at the fracture location. 
（2）在开裂扁管处，增加加强片，提高局部强度。Add the strengthening piece in the cracked tube to improve the local 
strength.
