Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
 
（1）前/后稳定杆均属于多源疲劳断裂。 Front/ rear stabilizer bar were both cracking by several source fatigue. 
 
（2） 前、后稳定杆皆为返工件，挡圈处(断裂处)组织异常、强度低，这会降低零件疲劳寿命。Front/rear stabilizer 
bar are rework. Microstructure at the cracking region is abnormal and the property is low, which reduces the fatigue 
life.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）加强试验样件质量监管。Strengthen the quality supervision of the testing sample.
