Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件受外力导致断裂。 
      2. 失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
      3．失效件注塑工艺不佳。 
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made the failure part fracture. 
 
    2. The material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
            3. The injection process of the failure part was bad. 
建议： 1. 改进注塑工艺。 
Suggestions：1. The injection process should be improved.
