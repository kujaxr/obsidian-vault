Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 1/7 档齿套属疲劳断裂，疲劳源起始于齿根部的R 角附近。1/7 speed gear sets belongs to fatigue fracture , 
fatigue source starting at the root of the tooth near the corner of R. 
2、 1/7 档拨叉属过载断裂，由于齿套断裂引起。The failure for1/7 fork was overlong, the fracture caused due sets of 
teeth  
3、 齿套的表面硬度、硬化层深度、芯部硬度、金相组织和拨叉的硬度、孔隙率未见异常。The surface hardness, 
case depth, core hardness、microstructure for the Sets of teeth and the hardness, porosity showed no abnormalities. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校齿套R 角处的实际载荷。Check the actual load for the gear sets R corner. 
2、 请检查各零部件之间的配合情况和试验工况。Check with the situation between the two parts and test 
conditions..
