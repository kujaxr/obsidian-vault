Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 因为弧线弹簧的疲劳断裂而导致双质量飞轮失效。The Dual Mass Flywheel has failed due to numerous fatigue 
fractures of the arc springs. 
2. 大型和小型弹簧的疲劳裂纹，主要来自腐蚀坑，但磨损也可能造成一些裂纹。Fatigue cracks were initiated in both 
large and small springs, predominantly from corrosion pits, although wear might also have caused some cracks. 
3. 腐蚀是喷丸后，但安装到汽车之前发生的。The corrosion occurred after shot peening, but before installation to the 
car. 
4. 弹簧磨损已去除或削弱喷丸强化层的作用。Wear of the springs has removed or weakened the shot peened layer. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商必须消除在弧弹簧的腐蚀， 及实施表面缺陷的限制。The supplier must eliminate corrosion in the arc 
springs and set limits for surface defects 
2. . 检查双质量飞轮的设计是否能够承受应用加载（包括弹簧负载和弹簧磨损）。 Check if DMF design is capable 
of handling the applied loading (including load on springs and wear of springs).

第一个关键的观察是内外弹簧磨损量。 “外”部是弹簧与磨损壳的接触点。 “内”部是大与小弹簧的接
触点。图5 显示了所观察到的一些磨损例子。虽然弹簧表面经过了喷丸处理，但磨损已去除了喷丸层。磨

The first key observation is the amount of wear both outside and inside the springs. The “outside” position is where 
the spring contacts with the Wear Shells. “Inside” refers to where the large spring contacts with the small spring. 
Figure 5 shows some examples of the wear seen.  Although the springs have been shot-peened, the wear has 
removed the shot peened layer.  The depth of wear can be seen generally in figure 6.

断裂分析 Fracture analysis 
失效弹簧装配后的情况如图7 所示（注意：上下部分的弹簧可能颠倒）。一些弹簧由于严重损坏，而不能装

were not too damaged clearly showed fatigue characteristics, with the crack origin at the inner surface.  Many 
more cracks were found in the springs (fig. 7c).

原产于腐蚀的疲劳裂纹  Fatigue fractures originating at Corrosion 
一些疲劳裂纹是起源于内表面小范围腐蚀（图8-10 就是例子）。在运行中时，总成表面涂有油脂，所以腐蚀
可能在弹簧制造过程中发生的。裂纹源头的一个横截面如图8c 所示；靠近裂纹的区域呈现出深度为43µm
腐蚀坑。  
Several of the fatigue fractures had originated at small areas of corrosion on the inner surface (figures 8 to 10 are 
examples).  The parts were coated with grease during running, so the corrosion is likely to have occurred during 
spring manufacture.  A cross section through a crack origin is shown in figure 8c, showing a corrosion pit depth of 
43µm adjacent to the crack.

疲劳裂纹的显得源于磨损 Fatigue fractures appearing to originate from wear 
几个裂纹显示起源于严重耐磨的地方。在高倍的照片中（图11C 12B），表面变形清晰可见。涂抹和变形层，
可以造成不利的材料金相组织和应力集中（参见图17）。但是，横截面检测显示疲劳裂纹起源于腐蚀坑 （图
13D）有可能其他的磨损有关的裂纹也是从腐蚀坑开始。  
Several of the fractures appear to have originated from areas of high wear.  At high magnification (fig. 11c & 12b) 
deformation of the surface is clearly visible.  The smeared and deformed layers can create unfavourable material 
phases and stress concentrations (see figure 17).  However, one cross section shows that such a fatigue crack 
actually originated from a corrosion pit (figure 13d), this may also be the case for other wear-related cracks.
