Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴套是由于受到较大的轴向弯曲载荷而发生疲劳断裂；主裂纹起源于应力集中的退刀槽处。The shaft sleeve was 
broken by fatigue due to the larger axial bending load, and the main crack originated from the stress concentration of the retractable groove. 
（2）失效轴套的材料检测结果与通过测试零件的结果接近，其成分，表面硬度，芯部硬度，硬化层深均符合技术
要求；夹杂物以及微观组织正常。The test result of the failure shaft sleeve is close to the one of the part passing the test. Its 
compositions, surface hardness, core hardness and hardened layer meet the technical requirements; inclusions and microstructures are normal. 
（3）失效件退刀槽加工质量（过渡半径和表面起伏）与通过测试零件的加工质量接近。The retreating groove processing 
quality (transition radius and roughness) of the failure parts is close to that of the parts passing the test. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核轴承载荷。Check the bearing load. 
（2）排查变速箱的装配以及实验过程是否存在异常。Check the assembly of the gearbox and whether the test process is abnormal.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
DCT360 MY18 进行变速箱R3 电机耐久试验，运行到55%时，5 档轴承轴套出现断裂，如图1 所示。
表面有正常的接触压痕和磨损痕迹，与通过测试的零件相比，未见明显异常。 
在工作中，挡边承受轴和齿轮施加的弯曲载荷；断裂的沟槽是应力集中点。
