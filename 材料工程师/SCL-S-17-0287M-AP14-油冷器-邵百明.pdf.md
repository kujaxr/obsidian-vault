Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）油冷器是由于过载而开裂。The oil cooler was cracked by overload 
（2）2#失效件出现了明显的熔蚀，组织为α-Al+网状分布的第二相；这会降低材料的抗腐蚀性、增加材料的脆性，
促使腐蚀失效和开裂发生的概率增加。The no. 2 failed part suffered from melting corrosion with the microstructure of 
the alpha -Al+ mesh distribution second phase, which will reduce the corrosion resistance and increase the brittleness to 
increase the possibility of corrosion failure and cracking. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）排查安装过程。Check the installation. 
（2）控制钎焊工艺，避免熔蚀的发生。Control brazing process to avoid the occurrence of melting corrosion. 
（3）关注售后同批次零件的表现。Pay attention to the performance of the same batch parts after sale.
