Ⅱ
结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1) 
腐蚀仅发生在发动机中的最后一个导电点的位置，可能表明杂散电流腐蚀来自于发动机内冷却系
统的电源。The location of the corrosion only at the last conductive point in the engine, could 
indicate stray current corrosion from an electrical source within the engine coolant system.  
2) 
没有电偶腐蚀的迹象或冷却液中的腐蚀性物质。There was no indication for galvanic corrosion, or 
aggressive substances in the coolant. 
3) 
材料的成分和硬度符合要求。The material composition and hardness meet the requirements.  
建议Suggestions： 
1) 
检查车辆问题，确认在冷却管道内是否有电势存在。Examine the problem vehicle, confirm if any 
electrical potential at this coolant pipe. 
2) 
做一个试验以复原失效机理。Make a test to replicate the failure mechanism.

1、失效零件宏观观察Macroscopic Observation of the Failure Parts 
1）送作分析的失效出水弯管与橡胶管连接的出水端部位有严重腐蚀，见图2； 
2）该腐蚀形貌呈片状剥落，见图2。 
 
结论：该铝管的腐蚀只在一端，腐蚀发生在管末端的内外侧。The corrosion of the aluminium pipe was only 
at one end.  Corrosion occurred both inside, and at the end of the pipe.

对失效出水弯管腐蚀部位的腐蚀物在扫描电镜下进行EDX 测试，见图3。 
 
结论：1）氧、铝、硅和铜的相对值如预期。高量的碳可能是从乙二醇中吸收到多孔氧化物中。The relative

probably from absorption of the Ethylene Glycol into the porous oxide.

Sulfur and Calcium cannot be easily accounted for, although contamination from fingerprints is most likely.
