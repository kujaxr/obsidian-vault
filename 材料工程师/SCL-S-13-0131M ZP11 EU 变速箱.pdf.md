Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）挡圈孔处早期断裂一块；后续长时间使用后，另一端出现疲劳断裂（断裂1）；最后由于过载产生断裂2。
断裂1 产生后，挡圈松脱，导致齿毂轴向移动过大，并与4 档齿接触，从而发生严重的磨损。The hole side of the 
clip was early broken; after a long time, the other end was broken by fatigue (fracture 1), and finally the fracture 2 
happened by overload. After fracture 1, the clip got loose, which caused the gears hub to move axially and touch with the 
4th gears. Finally, the gears hub and 4th gears were seriously worn. 
（2）失效挡圈的硬度合格；组织和夹杂物正常；厚度偏小。The hardness of the failed clip meets the requirement; the 
microstructure and inclusion are normal. The thickness is on the low. 
（3）失效齿毂硬度合格；厚度低于下限，且波动较大；内孔齿尖倒角过大，不符合要求。The hardness of failed gear 
hub meets the requirement; the thickness is below the lower limit and the fluctuation is remarkable; the chamfer of the 
tooth tip is bigger than the requirement. 
（4）4 档齿的硬度合格。The hardness of the 4th gears meets the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）抽检新的齿毂，关注尺寸的稳定性。Test the new hub and pay attention to the dimension stability. 
（2）改善装配工艺，避免装配过程对挡圈造成损伤。Improve the assembly process to avoid damaging the clip. 
（3）关注同批次的变速箱。 Pay attention to the transimission of the same batch. 
（4）校核动静载荷。Check the static load and dynamic load.

尺寸检测 Dimension Test 
对一些关键尺寸进行测量（见图12），其结果如表1 所示。由表可以看出： 
（1）齿毂厚度不符合要求，且波动较大；测量工具为游标卡尺；测量点为无磨损和损伤的表面。为了
进一步确认厚度的波动，采用三坐标对厚度进行测量，其结果如图13 所示；与表1 所测得的结果相一致。
对新的产品以及进行过路试的63Z 齿毂进行检测，其厚度均符合要求；最大厚度和最小厚度的差值小于
0.03mm。厚度的波动过大，会影响卡箍的接触和受力，还可能对装配产生不好的影响。 
（2）齿毂的倒角为0.48mm×46.7°，不符合要求0.2mm(±0.1)×45°(+1°)。 
（3）失效档圈的厚度偏小0.005mm。 
（4）3 档齿的运动间隙为0.22-0.28mm，处于控制要求（0.10-0.27mm）的上限。由于厚度波动的影响，
齿毂的轴向运动间隙为0.06-0.12mm，大于控制的要求（≤0.06mm）。 
Some key dimensions were checked （see fig. 12） and the results were showed as table 1. It can be seen 
that : 
(1) The thickness of the hub didn’t meet the requirement and fluctuated obviously. The test tool is vernier 
calipers;the test point is on the region without wear and damage. In order to affirm the thickness fluctuation, the 
thickness was tested by the three coordinate measuring instrument and the results are shown in fig. 13, which is 
consistent with the table 1. At the same time, the new hub and the hub of 63Z were tested. The thicknesses meet the 
requirement and The difference between the maximum and minimum thickness  is below 0.03mm.The big fluctation 
will affect the touch and stress born by the clip and the assembly process.  
（2）the chamfer of the gear hub is 0.48mm×46.7°which does not meet the requirement (0.2mm(±0.1)×45°
(+1°)). 
（3） the thickness of the failed clip is 0.005mm lower than the requirement. 
（4）the movement gap of 3rd gear is 0.22-0.28mm, which is on the upper limit. Because of the thickness 
fluctation, the axle movement gap of the hub is 0.06-0.12mm, which is higher than the requirement(≤
0.06mm).     .
