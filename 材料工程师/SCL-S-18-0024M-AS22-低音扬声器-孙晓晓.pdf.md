Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 螺钉安装孔位置熔融不好，存在熔接痕，导致强度降低，装配时开裂。 
2．零件材料不符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The melt of the screw hole is not good, with weld mark, lead to lower strength, and cracked during 
assembly. 2.Material performance of parts did not conform to the requirements. 
建议： 1.优化熔接痕 2.责令供应商解释材料问题并整改。 
Suggestions: 1.Optimize weld lines. 2.Force the supplier to explain the materials and solve the problem.
