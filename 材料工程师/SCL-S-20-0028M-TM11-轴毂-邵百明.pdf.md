Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 轴毂由于受到较大的载荷而在焊缝处发生疲劳断裂；裂纹起源于应力集中的焊根部位。The fatigue fracture 
of the hub occurred at the weld due to the large load, and the crack originated from the weld root with 
stress concentration. 
（2） 失效件以及同批新件焊接深度符合要求。The welding depth of failed parts and new parts of the same 
batch meets the requirements. 
（3） 轴和毂的基体硬度均符合技术要求。The matrix hardness of axle and hub meets the technical 
requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 
排查系统以及实验过程是否存在异常。Check the system and experiment whether there is abnormally  
（2） 
校核载荷，并优化设计（如增大环形焊缝的半径），降低焊缝的应力。Check the load and optimize the 
design （such as increasing the radius of the circular weld）to reduce the stress of the weld.
