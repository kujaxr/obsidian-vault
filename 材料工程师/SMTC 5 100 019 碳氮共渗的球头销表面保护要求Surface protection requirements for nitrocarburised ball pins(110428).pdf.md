1 范围 
本标准规定了碳氮共渗的球头销表面保护要求。
包括球头销在盐浴（A 类）中碳氮共渗和氧化，在气
体（B 类）中碳氮共渗和氧化，或者在离子（C 类）
中碳氮共渗和氧化。 
本标准适用于底盘区域球接头碳氮共渗处理的
表面保护要求。 
1  Scope  
The standard specifies surface protection 
requirements for nitrocarburised ball pins of 
which are nitrocarburised and oxidized in salt 
bath (type A), nitrocarburised and oxidized in gas 
(type B), or nitrocarburised and oxidized in 
plasma (type C). 
The standard applies to the surface protec-
tion requirements for nitrocarburised ball pins in 
the chassis area.

3 概述  
对于带螺纹连接的球头销，建议对球头销部分碳
氮共渗，即螺纹和周边区域不进行碳氮共渗。只有 B 
类和 C 类允许部分碳氮共渗球头销；仅 C 类可部分
涂装。 
3 
General 
For ball pins with threaded connections a 
partial nitrocarburisation of the ball pin is rec-
ommended, i.e. the thread and possibly sur-
rounding areas are not nitrocarburised. With par-
tially nitrocarburised ball pins only types B and C 
are permitted; a partial thread coating is only 
possible with type C.

roughness depth of Rz < 2 μm prior to treatment. 
If the ball pins are partially nitrocarburised and 
oxidized (possible with types B and C), the areas 
must be specified in the drawing.

7 测试 
7  Tests 
7.1 球头销耐腐蚀性测试 
根据 DIN EN ISO 9227，在盐雾中对组装后的球
头销测试（在技术工程发布/首样测试框架范围内）。
在耐腐蚀测试之前，要求清除待测试部件的油脂并进
行清洗。在耐腐蚀测试过程中，测试零件水平排列，
绝不能彼此接触。要避免接触其他金属零件。仅评估
直接接触盐雾的部件表面。 
 
非机械加工区，如球头销中心和六角插孔，应从
耐腐蚀评估中排除，因此，建议在测试前将这些区域
覆盖。部件的其他偏离区，要求不同的测试条件，将
在部件绘图中单独定义。 
 
7.1  Ball pin corrosion test 
Testing (within the framework of technical en-
gineering release/first-sample testing) is to be 
performed in the salt spray fog acc. to DIN EN 
ISO 9227 on ball pins which went through the 
assembly process. Prior to the corrosion test de-
greasing and washing of the test parts is re-
quired. During the corrosion test the test parts are 
horizontally arranged and must not come in con-
tact with each other. Contact with metallic, not 
corrosion-protected parts (brackets etc.) is to be 
avoided. Only the component surface directly 
subjected to the salt spray fog is evaluated. 
Non-mechanically machined areas, such as 
ball pin centers and hexagon sockets are to be 
excluded from the corrosion evaluation. There-
fore, it is recommended to cover these areas 
prior to the test run. Further deviating areas of a 
component, which require different test condi-
tions, are to be defined in the component drawing 
individually. 
7.1.1 
球头销区向上至密封螺纹管的连接 
A、B、C 类：经过 240 h 测试后，最多允许出现 
5 个红锈。未达到耐腐蚀时间的原因必须鉴定，进行
切片核查。在任何部件表面都不允许腐蚀点（红锈）
的累积。 
7.1.1  Ball pin area up to the sealing bellows 
connection 
Types A, B, C: Max. 5 individual spots of red 
corrosion are permitted after a test duration of 
240 h. The cause of failure to reach the required 
corrosion times must be verified by means of 
micro-section in arbitration cases. An accumula-
tion of corrosion spots (red surface corrosion) is 
not permitted on any component area. 
7.2 螺纹 
A 类和 C 类的氮化处理区在 96 h 后不能出现任
何红锈。根据 DIN EN 26157-3 允许的螺纹缺陷除外。
仅进行氧化处理的区域（C 类）因不直接与外界环境
接触，按ISO 554 在湿气凝结交替环境下进行 24 小
时测试后，红锈个数不超过3％。 
 
7.2  Thread 
The nitrated thread area according to types A 
and C must not show any red corrosion after 96 
h; permitted thread defects acc. to DIN EN 
26157-3 are excluded. Only oxidized component 
areas (type C), which are not directly subjected to 
environmental influences, have to withstand a 
24-h test in the moisture condensate alternating 
atmosphere acc. to ISO 554 with ≤ 3 % red cor-
rosion.

7.3 球中心 
除非已加工，否则耐腐蚀评估中将排除球头销中
心。建议在测试开始前覆盖它。部件的其他偏离区，
需要不同的测试条件，将在部件绘图中单独定义。 
7.3  Centre on the ball
Unless machined, the ball pin centering is to 
be excluded from the corrosion evaluation. It is 
recommended to cover it prior to the test run. 
Further deviating areas of a component, which 
require different test conditions, are to be defined 
in the component drawing individually.
