effect of galvanic corrosion on unit Housings made 
from Magnesium Alloys. 
This standard is suitable to test the effect of 
galvanic corrosion on unit Housings made from

6 和图7 中的样本0、1、2）。 
3.4.1  Slight surface corrosion (minor pitting) over 
the entire surface of the Mg test section is nor-mal 
when Mg is aged in NaCI solution and is therefore 
acceptable. (sample 0 to 2 in figure 6 and 7).
