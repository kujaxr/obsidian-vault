1 范围 
本标准规定了对汽车用铝电镀层、铝/镁合
金镀层表面保护要求。 
本标准适用于具有任何强度、无六价铬处
理后的铁材料和钢制件，特别适用于以提高腐
蚀-和温度负载为基础的螺栓连接元件和小件。
 
1  Scope  
The standard specifies deposited Al coatings and 
Al/Mg alloy coatings requirement electrolytically on 
vehicle interior trim. 
The standard applies to ferrous materials and 
steel parts with any tensile strength，are especially 
suitable for threaded fastening elements and small 
parts that are subject to an increased corrosion and 
temperature load.

3.2  基本要求 
3.2  General requirements 
3.2.1  按照 SMTC 90008 避免有害物料。 
3.2.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances accord-
ing to SMTC 90008.  
3.2.2  对于完整的测试，10只成品是必需的。
除非在图纸中标注部件的某些部分无需表面镀
层，否则部件的整个表面必须符合规定的表面
保护并展现出要求的特性。镀层对基底材料应
有牢固的粘附性。 
3.2.2  A total of 10 finished parts are required for 
complete testing. Unless certain areas of parts (which 
must be identified on the drawing) have been ex-
cepted from surface coating, the parts must exhibit 
the specified surface protection, with the specified 
properties, over the entire surface. The coatings must 
adhere firmly to the base material. 
3.2.3  在涂层之前，螺纹部分必须具有与涂层
厚度相适应的公差位置。在涂层之后，在外螺
纹时，不允许超过h-位置，在内螺纹时，不允
许低于H-位置。保护镀层不允许显示气孔，裂
纹，损坏和会损害腐蚀防护和/或规定外观的其
余缺陷。 
3.2.3  Before coating, threaded parts must exhibit a 
tolerance zone position corresponding to the coating 
thickness. After the coating process, the h-position 
must not be exceeded or fallen below. The protective 
coatings must not exhibit any pores, cracks, damage, 
or other flaws that impair the corrosion protection or 
specified appearance.  
3.2.4  在合理装配情况下，不允许出现会导致
功能受损和/或降低规定腐蚀防护性能的损坏。
基于利用非水状电解液电解沉积，所以，不存
在对镀层零件拉伸强度上限的限制。 
3.2.4
Given proper mounting, the coating must not 
be damaged in any way that would lead to impair-
ment of function or of the specified corrosion protec-
tion. There is no upper limit of the tensile strength for 
the parts to be coated due to the electrolytic deposi-
tion from non-aqueous electrolytes. 
3.3  连接元件 
在螺栓和快速紧固元件情况下， 3.10条所
述的测试要求仅适用于头部和/或扳手面，而对
螺母，仅适用于端面和/或扳手面。在螺纹件和
类似于螺纹的成形件(如像双头螺钉)情况下，
此测试要求仅适用于端面。对于连接元件处受
过程限制的镀层薄弱环节 (如颈部和螺纹)而
言，要依照3.10.1 条，降低应用要求。对于螺
纹型面中电镀层最大允许厚度，要考虑DIN EN 
ISO 4042 中的说明。为了调节恒定的摩擦系数，
必须用润滑剂处理带有公制螺纹的连接元件。
按照SMTC 7 100 013，进行摩擦系数测试。 
3.3 Joining elements
For screws and quick fastening elements, the 
test specifications listed in Section 3.10 only apply to
the head and the wrench bearing surfaces; for nuts, 
they apply only to the face surfaces and wrench 
bearing surfaces. For threaded and thread-like 
formed parts, such as tap end studs, the test re-
quirements only apply to the face surfaces. The re-
duced requirements according to Section 3.10.1 ap-
ply to process-related weak spots of the coating on 
joining elements, such as the shank and the thread. 
The specifications in DIN EN ISO 4042 concerning 
the maximum permissible thickness of the electrop-
lated coating in the thread profile must be observed. 
Joining elements with metric threads must be treated 
with sliding agent in order to ensure constant coeffi-
cients of friction. The coefficients of friction are tested 
according to SMTC7 100 013.

3.4  铝电镀层 
在使用由最纯铝制成的可溶性阳极情况
下，只允许由非水状铝有机复合电解液，电解
沉积成铝镀层。在无六价铬电镀结束时，可直
接钝化此镀层。 
3.4 Galvanic deposition of aluminum layers
The aluminum layer must only be deposited 
electrolytically from non-aqueous aluminum-organic 
complex electrolytes, using soluble pure aluminum 
anodes. The layers must be passivated immediately 
after the electrolytic deposition using Cr(VI)-free pas-
sivation treatments. 
3.5 铝/镁合金电镀层 
 
在使用由铝和镁或者由铝/镁合金制成的
可溶性阳极情况下，只允许由非水状铝有机复
合电解液，电解沉积成铝/镁合金镀层。在无六
价铬电镀结束时，可直接钝化此镀层。 
3.5 
Galvanic deposition of aluminum/ magne-
sium alloy layers  
    The aluminum/magnesium alloy layers must only 
be deposited electrolytically from non-aqueous alu-
minum-organic complex electrolytes, using soluble 
aluminum 
and 
magnesium 
anodes 
or 
alumi-
num/magnesium alloy anodes. The layers must be 
passivated immediately after the electrolytic deposi-
tion using Cr(VI)-free passivation treatments. 
3.6  无六价铬的腐蚀防护 
 原则上允许把无色-和蓝色钝化，即层厚
约0.1 μm 的所谓薄层钝化，作为钝化后处理。
借助于所谓的厚层钝化(带有约0.5 μm 层
厚)，达到改善腐蚀防护效果。也允许在较高温
度和较高铬含量情况下，在电解液中进行无电
流分离的这种钝化用作钝化处理。这种厚层钝
化的续后硅酸盐封闭剂不允许用于连接螺栓，
因为此举会降低为保证必需摩擦系数而进行后
续润滑剂处理的质量。 
3.6 Cr(VI)-free conversion layers
Colorless and blue passivation treatments, so 
called thin film passivation treatments, with a layer 
thickness of approx. 0,1 μm are generally permitted 
as passivation post-treatment. The corrosion protec-
tion is further improved using so-called thick layer 
passivation treatments (with a layer thickness of ap-
prox. 0,5 μm). These types of passivation treatments, 
which result from currentless deposition at high 
temperatures and elevated chromium contents, may 
also be used for passivating. A subsequent silicate 
sealing of this thick layer passivation treatment is not 
permitted for threaded fastening elements; it would 
impair the quality of the subsequent sliding agent 
treatment, which is necessary to ensure the required 
coefficients of friction. 
3.7  粘附性 
   为了测试粘附性，要应用粘附强度(7±1)N/ 
25 mm 宽度的粘结带，用手把它紧压在表面上，
接着垂直于表面抽动拉紧。此时，镀层不允许
大面积松脱或者损坏。允许少量钝化膜附着于 
粘结带处。 
3.7  Adhesion  
Adhesive tape with an adhesive strength of (7 ± 
1) N per 25 mm of width is used for the adhesion test. 
It is firmly pressed by hand onto the surface and then 
removed with a sudden motion perpendicular to the 
surface. There must not be any large-surface-area 
separation or damage of the coating. Small amounts 
of the passivation film adhering to the tape are per-
missible. The deposition of a nickel or copper flash to 
optimize adhesion is permissible. 
3.8  预处理     
为了对作为基体材料的钢件进行预处理，
原则上只允许采用非水状媒质。这同样也适用
于对氢脆敏感的其它基体材料。 
3.8
Pretreatment
Only non-aqueous media are permissible for 
pretreatment of parts made of steel as base material.
The same applies to other base materials that may be 
subject to hydrogen embrittlement.
