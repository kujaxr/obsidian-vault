Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  由于保养不当造成正时链条锈蚀而发生应力腐蚀断裂。 
    Timing chain fractured though stress corrosion because of bad maintenance. 
2.  链片硬度，金相组织无异常。 
    Hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 建议严格按照车辆的维护规范执行。 
Advised strictly follow the vehicle maintenance specifications.
