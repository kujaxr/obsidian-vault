Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 离合器壳体是由于疲劳而开裂的，主裂纹起源于壳体结构变形处(见图2)，局部载荷过大是导致开裂的根本原
因；Clutch housing was broken by fatigue and the main crack source was at the deformation of structure (shown in Fig2). 
A big stress at the location was the root cause of fatigue crack. 
2. 离合器壳体孔隙尺寸偏大，但不是失效的主要原因；The pore size was too large which wasn’t the main cause of 
failure. 
3. 壳体微观组织无异常，硬度、化学成分符合要求；Microstructure was normal, hardness and chemical components 
met the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 分析失效件其他异常部位(下系杆支架、悬置等)，评估其是否会导致开裂处应力载荷加剧；Checked other 
abnormal parts and assessed whether it would increase the stress at crack region. 
2. 优化设计，降低开裂处应力；Optimized the structure design, to reduce the load at crack reqion. 
3. 改进铸造工艺，降低孔隙尺寸；Improved the casting process, to reduce the pore size.
