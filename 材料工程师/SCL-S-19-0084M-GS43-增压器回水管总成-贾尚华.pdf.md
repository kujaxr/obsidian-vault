Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  增压器回水管因疲劳开裂而失效，这与试验过程中振动较大有关。 
The return pipe of supercharger fails due to fatigue cracking, which is related to the greater vibration during the test.. 
2.  增压器回水管的材料成分满足要求，金相组织和硬度正常，钎焊部分填充良好。 
The composition of the return pipe of the supercharger meets the requirements, and the metallographic structure and 
hardness are normal, the brazing part is well filled. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查振动，校核油管的局部应力。Investigate the vibration and check the local stress of the tubing.
