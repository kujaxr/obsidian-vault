Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 防撞梁由于受到焊接热的影响，导致管材强度遭到弱化，在撞击力的作用下发生过载断裂。 
Due to the influence of welding heat, the impact beams was weakened and cracked under the impact force. 
2. 材料成分、抗拉强度符合要求，硬度及组织正常。 
Chemical components and tensile strength met the requirement. Hardness and microstructure were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化焊缝布置或更改防撞梁与支架的连接方式。 
Optimise the welding distribution or change the connected mode of the impact beams and the bracket.
