Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）暖风芯体是由于扁管与主板的局部匹配间隙未被焊料填充而出现孔洞，最终引起泄露。 
The warm air core body leaked because the local matching gap between the tube and main plate wasn’t filled by 
solder. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）查找钎焊不良的原因，重点关注主板与扁管的匹配间隙，并加强过程控制。Find the causes for the bad braze , 
pay attention to the matching gap and strengthen the process control.
