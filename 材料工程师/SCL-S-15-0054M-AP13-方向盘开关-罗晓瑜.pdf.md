Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 堵盖与外壳装配后软筋处有过盈挤压，挂接后挂扣搭接面存在持续挤压作用，导致挂扣疲劳断裂； 
The continuous extrusion of the contact surface after assembly result in the fatigue cracking of the clip.  
2、失效件材料符合技术要求；Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements 
3、零件内应力水平较低；The internal stress of the part is small. 
4、2 状态失效件存在注塑缺陷，但这不是失效的根本原因。There’s an injection flaw on the status 2 failure part, 
but this is not the root cause of failure.  
建议Suggestions： 
1、 优化结构，消除搭接面的持续挤压；Optimize the structure in order to remove the continuous extrusion of the 
contact surface. 
2、优化注塑工艺。Improve the injection mold.
