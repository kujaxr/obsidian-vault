Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过失效件的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，油封内存在硬性杂质，减震器运行中导致油封唇边和
轴表面磨损，降低了油封的密封作用。 
2. 经测试，零件材料为NBR，符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the oil seal, it can be seen that there were 
some hard inclusions in the oil seal. During the damper was working, the lip of oil seal and the surface of 
shaft were scratched by the hard inclusions and the seal performance of the oil seal was decreased. After 
testing, the material of the oil seal is NBR which conforms to the drawing requirement. 
建议：保证减震器装配时清洁度。 
Suggestions：The higher cleanness should be guaranteed during the damper assembly.
