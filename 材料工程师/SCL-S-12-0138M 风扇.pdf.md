Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1.风扇受外力导致其结构最薄弱处断裂。 
       2.失效件材料(或工艺)异常。 
Conclusions: 1.Foreign force made the weakness of fan fractured. 
           2.The material performance or process of failure part was abnormal. 
建议： 1.责令供应商对失效件的异常情况进行解释和整改。 
Suggestions: 1. Let supplier explain to the abnormality of failure part and rectify them.
