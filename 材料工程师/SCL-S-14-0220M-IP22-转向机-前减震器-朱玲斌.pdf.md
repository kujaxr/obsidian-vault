Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）前减震器柱和转向机外接球头是由于过载而断裂的；这是由于车辆失控撞击路边物体所致。The front shock 
absorber and steering OBJ was broken due to overload which was because the car was out of control and impacted the 
objects at the road side.  
（2）失效件（减震器柱和转向机外接球头）成分合格，硬度和组织正常。The chemical compositions of failure parts 
（shock absorber column and steering OBJ） are qualified, the microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）调查车辆驾驶情况。To investigation of vehicle driving.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
IP22/23 在泰国进行路试，运行至3245 号公路时，车辆发生事故；转向机外接球销、前减震器柱断裂，
摆臂变形，球销被拉脱。当时车辆里程为27540km，车速90km/h；天气为雨天。失效前，车辆可能的行进路线
如图1 所示，由图可知，车辆转弯后，开始向右侧行驶并撞击到路边的物体。 
The IP22/23 accident was on road No.3245 in Thailand. At that time, the car was on road test and it was rainy. 
The steering OBJ、front shock absorber column were broken, the swing arm deformation, ball stub was pulled out . The 
vehicle mileage was 27540km and the speed was 90km/h. Maybe the vehicle travel routes was shown in Figure 1. It can 
be seen that from the figure the vehicle turned right and hit the object at the road side.

The failure parts were sent to the laboratory as shown in Figure 2. The fracture locations of front shock absorber 
column、steering ball stub and ball stub position of swing arm  were obviously rusty and deformed (see the area whit 
yellow dotted line in Figure 2 ).

材料测试  Material Test Results 
对前减震器柱和球销材料进行检测，其结果如下： 
（1） 前减震器柱材料化学成分合格，硬度正常，微观组织为铁素体和珠光体，正常； 
（2） 球销材料化学成分合格，硬度正常，微观组织为回火索氏体，正常。 
     To test the material of the front shock absorber column and ball stub, the result are as follows: 
(1)  The chemical component of the front shock absorber column are qualified, the hardness is normal, the 
microstructure is ferrite and pearlite, which is normal. 
(2) The chemical component of the ball stub are qualified, the hardness is normal, the microstructure is tempered 
sorbite,  which is normal.
