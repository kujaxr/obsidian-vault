Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 防转销是过载断裂。The part is overload fracture. 
2、 防转销化学成分和硬度未见异常。The chemical composition and hardness of the failed part were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、排查装配过程是否有异常。Check if there are any abnormalities during the assembly process. 
2、校核装配工艺。Reconfirm assembly process.
