Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
小结：失效件的尾门密封条的压缩负荷过大(要求：6±2N/100mm；实测：17.2 N/100mm，17.1 N/100mm，
17.5 N/100mm，17.7 N/100mm)，导致密封条严重磨损。抽样新件压缩负荷符合要求(要求：6±2N/100mm；
实测：7.4 N/100mm)。 
Conclusion: The compression load of the failed seal is larger than the requirement which caused the 
severe wear of the seal(requirement: 6±2N/100mm; measured: 17.2 N/100mm,17.1 N/100mm,17.5 
N/100mm,17.7 N/100mm). The compression load of the new seal conforms to the requirement 
(requirement: 6±2N/100mm; measured: 7.2 N/100mm).  
 
建议：供应商加强产品生产控制。  
Suggestions：the supplier should be request to strengthen the control of production.
