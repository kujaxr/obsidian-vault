Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）螺纹部位材料硬度偏低，不能承受规定安装扭矩（90-110）Nm 是导致滑牙的主要原因；Material hardness of 
screw area is low, which can not endure defined Torque requirement thus led to the failure. 
（2）临港滑牙零件、质保PPV 及PP 状态车上拆解下的零件与底盘实验室取样的零件原材料状态不一致，临港
滑牙件、PPV及PP状态拆解零件为退火态，底盘实验室取样零件为热轧态；material condition is different between PPV 
& PP status part and chassis laboratory sampled part, PPV & PP status parts were annealed, chassis laboratory sampled part 
was hot rolled. 
（3）质保PP 状态车上拆解下的零件，由于螺纹部位强度偏低，已经产生缩颈；Due to low strength in screw area, 
necking happened in QA PP status vehicle dismounted part. 
（4）牙纹质量不良，但这不是导致滑牙的主要因素；bad screw dimension quality is not the main reason of failure. 
（5）原材料化学成份符合技术协议要求；Raw material chemical composition conform to technical specification. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）工程进行CAE 分析和螺纹接头试验，确定材料强度的要求；Engineering should conduct CAE analysis and organize 
joint connection test to give material strength requirement. 
（2）工程对现场装配工艺及防锈油影响进行调查评估；Engineering should give assessment to the tighten assemble 
process and impact of anti-rust oil. 
（3）供应商要确保原材料状态改进螺纹质量；Supplier should guarantee raw material condition and improve screw quality.

（1）临港滑牙零件和质保PPV/PP 状态车拆解零件的螺纹位置硬度值分布，其性能级别低于8.8 级螺栓
（250-320）HV；

也是因为螺纹位置强度不足； 
（3）原材料组织状态（退火态和热轧态）的差异导致零件有不同的硬度，退火态硬度低于热轧态；
