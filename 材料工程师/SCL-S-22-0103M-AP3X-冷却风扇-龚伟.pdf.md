Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  风扇叶轮受力后断裂，运行过程中一侧磨损，导致故障问题发生。 
The impeller broke after being stressed, and one side was worn during working, resulting in 
failure. 
2.  失效件材料不符合技术要求。结合材料的缺口冲击强度&特性粘数，零件性能无显著降低， 
因此非失效的主要原因。 
The performance of the failure parts material didn’t conform to requirement. Combined with the 
notch impact strength characteristic viscosity of the material, the performance of the part does 
not decrease significantly, so it is not the main cause of failure. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  责令供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
Let r the supplier explain the material problems and rectify.
