Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 由于夹紧力不足引起的螺栓松脱，根本原因有待进一步的分析。Insufficient bolt clamping force caused 
loose fundamental reason for further analysis. 
2、 螺栓的硬度、镀层厚度、化学成分、金相组织、表面脱碳、螺牙缺陷均符合技术要求。Hardness, coating 
thickness, chemical composition, microstructure, surface removal, thread defective bolts of th bolt met the 
technical requirements 
3、 皮带轮的电泳漆膜厚、转换强度、板厚均符合技术要求，金相组织无异常。Pulley electrophoresis coating 
thickness, the conversion strength, thickness meet the technical requirements, no abnormal microstructure. 
4、 惰轮的硬度、密度符合技术要求，金相组织无异常。Hardness and density of the idler met the technical 
requirements, no abnormal microstructure. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 系统排查不同阶段零件状态和装配的差异。Differences between the different stages of the investigation system 
status and assembly parts. 
2、 评估螺栓表面电泳漆对松弛的影响。Assess the impact of the bolt on the loose surface electrophoretic paint.
