Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1. 失效件正常工作时受到张应力作用，由于接触到含S 的腐蚀物质，导致出现应力腐蚀而断裂。When the failure 
parts work normally, they are subjected to tensile stress. Because of contact with corrosive substance containing S, stress 
corrosion occurs and fracture occurs. 
2. 失效件和库存件的硬度超过技术要求，金相组织正常。The hardness of the failed parts and the stock parts exceeds 
the technical requirements, and the metallographic structure is nomal. 
建议Suggestions:  
1. 避免接触到腐蚀物。Avoid contact with corrosion.  
2. 控制卡箍的硬度。Control the hardness of the clamp.

断口分析Fracture Analysis 
失效件的宏观断口如图2 所示，裂纹从一侧向另一侧扩展。The macroscopic fracture of the failed part is shown in 
Figure 2. The crack expands from the one side to the other side of the clamp. 
对失效件断口进行SEM 分析，断口微观形貌如图3 所示，可知失效件的断口形貌均为：沿晶+少量的韧窝。
SEM analysis was performed on the fracture of the failed part. The micro morphology of the fracture is shown in Figure 3. 
It can be seen that the fracture morphology of the failed part is: intergranular + a small amount of dimples. 
为进一步确认失效件是否为过载断裂，对失效件进行模拟外力使其发生过载断裂，并观察其断口形貌，如图
4 所示。模拟件的断口形貌为：韧窝。In order to further confirm whether the failed part is an overload fracture, a 
simulated external force is performed on the failed part to cause an overload fracture, and the fracture morphology is 
observed, as shown in Figure 4. The fracture morphology of the simulated part is: dimple. 
通过对比失效件和模拟件断口形貌可知，失效件的断裂不是由于过载产生的。By comparing the fracture 
morphology of the failed part and the simulated part, it can be known that the fracture of the failed part is not caused by 
overload.
