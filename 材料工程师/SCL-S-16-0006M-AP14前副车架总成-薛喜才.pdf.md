Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 安装支架由于受到较大载荷发生疲劳开裂，裂纹萌生于焊缝热影响区并向母材扩展。  
The mounting bracket was crack by fatigue because of a big load. The crack initiation was in the weld heat affected 
zone and extending to the base. 
2. 安装支架焊缝100%熔透，焊缝间隙正常。 
  Mounting bracket was 100% weld penetration and the gap was normal. 
3. 材料成分、板厚满足技术要求，硬度及金相组织正常。 
Chemical components and thickness met the requirement. Microstructure and hardness were normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 基于目前试验工况，评估副车架、下摆臂实际受力情况。 
Evaluate the actual load on the front sub frame and the low control arm based on the testing situation. 
2. 图纸上应定义焊接方向。 
Define the welding direction in the drawing.

是否是造成本零件开裂的根本原因，尚不能确定。
