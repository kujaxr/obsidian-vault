Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论 Conclusions： 
1、前副车架上2#裂纹属疲劳开裂，裂源部位有毛刺导致的应力集中； No.2 crack belongs to fatigue failure, stress 
concentration due to burrs found at the crack initial area;  
2、前副车架上3#裂纹裂纹源附近区域材料厚度异常减薄；Material thickness abnormally reduced near No.3 fracture 
area;  
3、前副车架上片材料符合图纸技术要求；Upper shell material of front subframe conforms to drawing technical 
requirement. 
4、前副车架上管状支架附近位置1#裂纹属疲劳开裂，由异常载荷所致。No.1 crack belongs to fatigue failure due to 
abnormal load. 
建议 Suggestions： 
1、监控板材冲压厚度减薄和毛刺缺陷等问题；Monitor and control steel thickness reduction and stamping burrs;  
2、优化焊接参数和过程。Optimize welding parameter and process.
