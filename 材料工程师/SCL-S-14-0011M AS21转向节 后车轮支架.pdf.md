Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 转向节和后悬车轮支架皆是由于过载而断裂；KNUCKLE ASM-STRG and CARRIER ASM-RR SUSP WHL were 
broken due to the big load. 
2. 转向节材质、硬度及微观组织符合技术要求；Chemical components、hardness and microstructure of KNUCKLE 
ASM-STRG met the technical requirement. 
3. 后车轮支架材料符合图纸要求；The material of CARRIER ASM-RR SUSP WHL met the drawing requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 调查事故车辆翻车原因；Investigated the reason of the turning over car
