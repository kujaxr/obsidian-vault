Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：经测试，失效件的材料无异常，失效件的力学性能与额定值有轻微差异。衬套开裂主要是由于衬套受
外界异物冲击所致。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part confirm to the drawing requirement. There is a small 
different value of mechanical property between the failure part and requirement. According to the macroscopic 
observation and the material analysis, it can be seen that the rubber sealing boot is broken by the excessive 
impression of outside force. 
建议：避免外界异物冲击。 
Suggestions：The excessive force on the rubber sealing boot should be forbidden.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图2 的衬套外壁形貌可以看出，裂纹表面存在明显的压痕，压痕处材料向内凹陷，裂纹没有继续扩展迹
象。 
From Fig 2, it can be seen that there are several impressions on the outside surface of the rubber sealing boot, the 
crack edges are bended toward the inside of the sealing boot, and no crack propagation is observed on the outside surface.
