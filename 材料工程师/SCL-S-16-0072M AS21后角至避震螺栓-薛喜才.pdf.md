Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1）螺栓属于过载断裂。Bolt was cracking because of the overload.   
（2）螺栓材料符合技术要求。The material properties of stud and bolts meet the technical requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 检查装配过程。Check the assembly process.
