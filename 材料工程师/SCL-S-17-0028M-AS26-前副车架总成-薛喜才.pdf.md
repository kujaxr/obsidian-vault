Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 纵臂与套管焊缝沿着焊趾发生疲劳裂纹，裂纹起源区位于此焊缝的高应力区。The crack propagated along the 
welding seam between front sub frame and pipe and the crack source region was in the high stress region. 
2. 失效部位未发现起、收弧及补焊现象，焊缝熔深满足技术要求，焊缝热影响区组织、硬度正常。Arc and repair 
welding weren’t observed and fusion met the requirement. Microstructure and hardness were normal. 
3. 纵臂与套管另两条焊缝存在未熔合、熔深不足的现象，焊缝组织、硬度无异常。Insufficient penetration and 
incomplete fusion were observed in another two welding seams, but weld microstructure and hardness were normal. 
4. 纵臂上片材料性能正常。The material property of the upper plate is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商加强焊缝质量管控，避免未熔合、熔深不足。Control the welding quality to avoid insufficient 
penetration and incomplete fusion. 
2. 重新评估套管部位的安全系数。Evaluate again the safety coefficient.
