Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  1#-4#逻辑阀弹簧由于疲劳而发生断裂失效；疲劳的发生与弹簧表面缺陷有关。 
The 1#-4# logic spring valve fails due to fatigue cracking, which is related to the surface defect of the spring. 
2.  1#-4#逻辑阀弹簧的硬度、非金属夹杂物及金相组织满足要求。 
The hardness, non-metallic inclusions and metallographic structure of 1#-4# logic valve spring meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商关注弹簧原材料以及成型过程的表面质量。 
Suppliers should pay attention to the surface quality of spring raw materials and forming process.
