Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）装配中，螺栓头部的位置偏离中心，同时材料被严重去除，从而导致侧板失效。During the assembly, the head 
of the bolt is offset the center of the hole and the material is removed, which caused the failure. 
（2）材料的组织和性能符合要求。The material microstructure and mechanical properties meet the requirement.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）对同批次的零件尺寸进行检测。Check the part dimension of the same lot. 
（2）检查螺栓与钢板匹配面的应力是否合适。Check underhead bearing stress is suitable for the material. 
（3）增加螺栓头部的尺寸。 Increase the dimension of the bolt head.
