Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件表面腐蚀蚀和橡胶套的保护不当引起的疲劳断裂。The spring failed through fatigue, due to heavy 
corrosion and loss of the protective rubber sleeve. 
2、 失效件材料性能符合技术要求。The material performance for the failed part meet the technical requirements. 
3、 考虑到车辆的行使里程，弹簧的腐蚀是严重的。应引起关注的是，在IP21 和IP22 整车腐蚀试验中，第
6 和第9 个腐蚀年的前弹簧发生过类似的失效。在国内是没有考虑如此严重的腐蚀环境的，因为道路通
畅不使用盐的。The corrosion of the spring assembly is severe, considering the mileage of the car. It should be 
noted that during whole vehicle corrosion testing of IP21 and IP22, the front springs failed in a similar manner 
after 6 and 9 “very severe”corrosion years respectively.  Such a severe corrosive environment is not 
anticipated in China,since road salt is not generally used. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查该车辆的使用工况：如在珠海相似车辆该弹簧此位置的情况，并记录信息（包括车辆的使用年限和
行使里程）。Check the use conditions for the car, including checking similar cars (age, km) in this location 
(Zhuhai). 
2、 考虑改善橡胶套对涂层的保护。Consider to improve the protection of rubber sleeve on the coating.
