Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）水管的开裂是由于铝管存在原始的裂纹，产生于制管过程，而非弯管阶段。The water tube’s crack is due to the 
original crack of the aluminum tube, which was formed in the tube manufacturing and not in the bending process. 
（2）管子的成分符合要求；硬度和金相正常。The chemical components of the tube meet the requirement; the hardness 
and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 查找原始裂纹产生的原因，并对制管工艺过程进行控制。Find out the cause for the original crack and control the 
process.
