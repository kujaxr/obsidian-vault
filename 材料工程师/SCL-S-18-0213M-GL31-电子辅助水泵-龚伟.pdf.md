Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件金属嵌件位置不正，导致失效位置壁厚实测值与设计值不一致。 
The position of the metal insert is incorrect, resulting in the measured value of the thickness of the 
failed position is inconsistent with the designed value. 
2. 零件失效位置壁厚设计过薄，注塑工艺调节窗口较小，导致熔接痕问题。 
The thickness of the failure position is too thin, with small adjustment for injection molding 
process, resulting in weld mark problem. 
              3. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商优化结构&调整金属嵌件固定方式。 
                   Supplier optimizes structure & adjusts the metal insert fixing method.

效，提供两例失效委托实验室分析。
