Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 零件生产工艺存在异常导致开裂。 
The abnormal production process of parts led to cracking.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商排查注塑过程异常并整改。 
Supplier checks the abnormal injection molding process and rectifies.
