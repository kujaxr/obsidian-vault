Scope 
This TL standard specifies the material requirements for brake pressure hoses with EPDM 
inside layer. 
2 
Designation 
Brake hose according to TL 523 79 
3 
Requirements 
3.1 
General requirements 
Approval of first supply and changes according to Volkswagen standard VW 011 55. 
Avoidance of hazardous substances according to VW 911 01. 
20 finished parts and 2 test panels (2 mm thick) are required for complete testing. 
3.1.1 
Appearance 
The braided inserts are embedded in elastomer. The elastomer covering shall not have any sulfur 
deposits. The inner elastomer hose consists of material that does not form any deposits.  
The individual layers shall exhibit proper bonding. 
3.2 
Design 
According to drawing; deviating requirements called out in drawings take precedence over this 
TL standard. 
3.3 
Identification 
Permanent marking, according to drawing.

4 
Notes on testing 
4.1 
Outside layer and finished parts 
For testing, the hose outside layer is separated from the finished part by means of a belt stripping 
machine. 
4.2 
Microhardness 
Specimens (thickness  0.9 mm) of approx. 30 mm in length and approx. 7 mm in width shall be 
prepared from the elastomer layer. The measurements are performed on the inside surface. In the 
event of impurities, the specimens shall be cleaned with a dry cloth. 
The mean value from three individual values, each measured on a different specimen, shall be 
indicated in the test report. 
The change in microhardness after aging at elevated temperature is determined on the same 
specimen as in the as-received condition (see Table 1 No. 3.1). 
4.3 
Aging at elevated temperature of the outside layer and finished parts 
S3A standard specimens according to DIN 53504 shall be aged for 94 h at +110 °C in a natural 
convection oven according to DIN 53508. Measurements are taken 30 min after removal. 
4.4 
Aging in engine oil 
S3A standard specimens are aged 46 h at +70 °C in Reference Engine Oil C.20 SAE 5W-40 
according to TL 521 85. Measurements are taken 30 min after removal. 
4.5 
Mandrel bending test at low temperature 
The hose shall be kept straight and, together with a mandrel that is 76 mm in diameter, shall be 
aged for 72 h at -54 °C to -57 °C in the cold chamber. Next the hose is wound at least 180° around 
the mandrel at this temperature within (3 to 5) seconds. 
The test apparatus according to VDA 675 254 is used for this. 
4.6 
Low temperature resistance after aging at elevated temperature  
Finished parts shall be aged for 8 hours at +120 °C in a natural convection oven according to  
DIN 53508. The hoses are then acclimatized for 6 h at room temperature (23 ± 2) °C. The finished 
parts are then aged for 48 h at -46 °C to -48 °C in the cold chamber. 
4.7 
Aging at elevated temperature of the hose core 
166 h aging at +120 °C in a mechanical circulation oven according to DIN 53508. Measurements 
are performed 30 min after removal.

4.8 
Aging in brake fluid 
Maximum four specimen parts, up to 5.7 ml total volume, are aged 70 h at +120 °C in 75 ml of a 
standard production brake fluid released by Volkswagen. To do this, tightly closable glass 
containers with approx. 250 ml volume and 50 mm diameter shall be used according to 
DIN ISO 4928. After the test is over, the elastomer parts are conditioned in the container at 
(23 ± 5) °C for 60 min to 90 min. Subsequently, the specimens shall be removed from the liquid, 
rinsed briefly (max. 10 s) in isopropyl alcohol or ethyl alcohol and dried with a clean, fiber-free 
cloth. The measurements are performed within 60 min after the specimens have been rinsed 
in alcohol. 
The test duration for the long-term test is 1006 h. 
4.9 
Hardness measurement at low temperature 
The measurements are carried out in cold air. 
4.10 
Permanent deformation after aging at elevated temperature 
22 h aging at +120 °C in a natural convection oven according to DIN 53508. 
4.11 
Extractable ingredients content 
For each determination, one test part section is extracted for 8 h with n-hexane, then left for 16 h at 
room temperature in the system (extractor without solvent) and then again extracted for 
a further 8 h. 
After 30 to 60 min airing at room temperature, the specimens shall be dried for 48 h at +80 °C in 
a natural convection oven according to DIN 53508. 
4.12 
Ozone resistance 
The brake hoses shall be wound 360° around a cylinder; the cylinder diameter must be 8 times the 
nominal outer diameter of the brake hose. The assembled specimens shall be aged for 24 h 
at room temperature. Then testing is carried out in the ozone climate chamber.
