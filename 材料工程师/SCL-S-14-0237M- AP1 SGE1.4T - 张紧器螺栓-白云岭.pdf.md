Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效螺栓是由于夹紧力丢失造成的疲劳断裂。 
The failure is fatigue fracture ,due to the clamping force was loss. 
2、失效件的材料性能符合图纸技术要求。 
The material properties of the part meet the technical requirements of the drawing.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 建议系统排查螺栓装配及试验过程。 
Recommended System investigation bolt assembly and testing process.
