Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）挡圈是由于受到弯曲和扭转载荷而发生疲劳断裂。The circlip was broken by fatigue because of bending and 
torsion load. 
（2）材料的硬度符合要求；组织正常。The material hardness meets the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）检查挡圈的装配过程中，关注挡圈厚度选择是否合适、装配是否到位、是否存在变形可能性以及是否是二次
装配。Check the assembly process of the circlip and pay attention to the thickness choice of the circlip, the assembly 
position correctness, deformation probability and distinguish if the assembly is secondary. 
（2）请泰国的工程师对其它相关失效件作进一步检查，以综合判断和明确失效的原因。Please engineers in Thailand 
check other related failed parts to synthetically judge and determine the cause for the failure.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
    泰国售后一辆MG3 的变速箱出现故障；拆解发现：换挡拨叉和5 档挡圈断裂；滚针轴承开裂。实验室仅收
到失效5 档挡圈的一部分，如图1 和图2 所示。 
In Thailand after sale market, a MG3’s transmission was wrong; after disassembly, it was found: the 5th gear shift 
fork and circlip were broken and the needle bearing cracked. SCL only received a part of the failed 5th circlip, which was 
shown as fig.1 and fig.2. 
由图1 可见：裂纹起源的一侧表面在靠近外侧的部分区域比较光滑，为磨损的区域，且磨损是不均匀的；这
反应接触和受力是不均匀的。 
According to figure 1, it can be seen that the local area on the crack origin side surface and close to the outer side is 
more smooth because of wear, and the wear is not uniform; This shows the contact and stress are not uniform.  
图2 显示挡圈表面的弧形接触痕迹距离内侧表面的距离不均匀：端部的距离较大；断裂截面的距离较小。不
均匀接触会导致受力出现异常，可能引起断裂。 
Figure 2 shows the distance between the arc contact traces on the surface of the circlip and the inside edge is uneven: 
the distance at the end is bigger than the one at the fracture section. Uneven touch can make an abnormal stress, which 
may cause fracture.  
鉴于上述结果，需对装配过程进行检查，关注挡圈厚度选择、装配是否到位、是否变形以及是否是二次装配。 
Basing on the above observational results, it is necessary to check the assembly process and pay attention to the 
thickness choice, the assembly position correctness, deformation probability and distinguish if the assembly is secondary.

断口分析  Fracture Surface Analysis 
断口宏观形貌如图3 所示。表面可见明显的海滩纹，显示出疲劳断裂的特征；依据纹路收敛的方向，不难判
断裂纹起源于图3 的上侧表面，偏向挡圈外侧的区域，然后向其它区域扩展。从裂纹的起源点和扩展方向来看，
挡圈断裂是受到了弯曲和扭转载荷所致。 
The macro fracture is shown in fig.3. The surface is obviously with beach mark, which shows the characteristics of 
fatigue. According to the direction of the convergence, it is easy to judge that the crack originated form the upper surface 
in fig. 3 and close to the outside of the circlip and then extended to other area. Based on the origin point and the 
expanding direction of the crack, the fracture was caused by the bending and torsion load. 
利用扫描电镜对微观的断口形貌进行检查，结果如图4 和图5 所示。由图可见：裂纹起源于表面，无材料缺
陷；扩展区可见细小的疲劳辉纹，同时呈一定方向分布的二次裂纹。该微观检测同样表明挡圈的断裂为疲劳断裂。 
The fracture surface micro morphology was examined by scanning electron microscopy. The result is shown in figure 
4 and figure 5. It can be found that the crack originated from the surface and there was not material defect on the origin 
point. Visible tiny fatigue striations and a certain direction of secondary cracks were on expanding area. The microscopic 
test results also show that fracture is by fatigue, which is the same to the macro fracture.
