1 范围 
本标准规定了采用感应耦合等离子体
（ICP）法测定锌铁合金中锌铁含量的测试流
程、试剂配制、试样制备及测量计算的方法。 
本标准适用于用ICP法对锌铁合金镀层中
铁和锌含量的测定。 
 
1  Scope  
The standard specifies the method of inductively 
coupled plasma(ICP) to measure the content of iron 
and zinc in the zinc-iron alloy coatings including test 
process 、reagents and specimen preparation 、
measurement. 
This standard is used to measure the content of 
iron and zinc in the zinc-iron alloy coatings by ICP.

2.1  流程简述 
将含锌和铁的合金镀层加热后溶解于盐酸
和硝酸的混合液中，然后用 ICP 发射光谱法进
行分析，以测定其含量。 
2.1 Description of process in short form  
The alloy coating which contains zinc and iron is 
dissolved under heat in a mixture of hydrochloric acid 
and nitric acid and then analyzed by ICP emission 
spectroscopy to determine the quantity.

2.3  试样制备 
将试样放入装有 45 ml 盐酸 (1+1) 和 1 
ml 硝酸 (1+1) 混合液的150 ml 玻璃烧杯中并
加热，待镀层完全溶解后，将试样放入去离子
水中清洗，然后将溶液转移到 100 ml 量瓶中。
待溶液冷却后，加满量瓶并用光谱分析法测量。
  
 
2.3  Preparation of specimens 
   The specimen is placed into 150ml glass beaker 
containing a mixture of 45ml hydrochloric acid and 
1ml nitric acid and heated. After the coating has 
completely dissolved, the specimen is rinsed in 
de-ionized water and solution is transferred to a 
100ml volumetric flask. After the solution has cooled, 
the flask is made up to the mark with de-ionized wa-
ter and measured by spectral analysis.  
2.4  测量 
采用 ICP 发射光谱法测定溶液中的锌/铁
浓度。在以下波长下执行测量： 
a) 锌：330259 nm； 
b）铁：240488 nm。 
 
2.4  Measurement 
   The zinc-iron concentration of the solution is de-
termined by ICP emission spectroscopy. Measure-
ment is conducted at the following wave lengths: 
   a)  Zn : 330259 nm 
   b)  Fe : 240488 nm
