范围Scope  
本标准规定了抗液性能-浸泡/干燥方法的
测试步骤。 
本标准适用于内/外饰件塑料件材料，部分
或偶然浸入液体。其他材料和部件不适用。 
 
This standard specifies a procedure for testing 
the resistance to fluids – dip/dry method. 
 
This standard applies to interior/exterior trim 
materials such as integrally colored plastic 
which partially or occasionally immersed in the 
liquids. Other materials and parts are not ap-
plied.

样品Specimens 
4.1 样品要求 Sampling requirements 
 
 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从成品上取
样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，其样品制备方
法应当与SMPV 中心实验室协商确定。每种试验
零件准备三个样品。 
 
Unless otherwise specified in the relevant 
standard, Samples should be cut from finished 
products. If samples can’t be made from fin-
ished products, sample preparation method 
should be determined through the negotiation 
with SMPV SCL.Three specimens for each test 
fluid. 
4.2 样品预处理 Conditioning 
 
 
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291 标准气候条件
23℃,50 %RH 下至少放置24 h。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 24h before test. 
4.3 样品尺寸 Sample Size 
样条尺寸最小 80*20mm，厚度按照零件厚度。 
 
Sample Size at least 80*20mm, the thickness 
according to the parts.

5 液体 Fluids 
液体规定见附录 A。除非另有说明，否则所
有试验在室温（23 ℃±2 ℃）下进行，液体体
积需确保样品有1/2浸没在其中。 
 
Fluids are specified in Appendix A. 
All testing to this method shall be carried out at 
room temperature (23 °C ± 2 °C) unless oth-
erwise specified. The volume of the fluid shall 
be at least twenty times the combined volume 
of the specimens and sufficient to ensure 
complete immersion. 
6 过程/试验 Procedure / Test 
6.1 将样品浸入试验液体 2 s，取出按照车上工
况，在23 ℃±2 ℃，湿度(65 ± 5) %的条件下
存放1 h。 
 
Immerse the specimen in the specified test 
fluid for two seconds remove and leave in the 
on-vehicle attitude for one hour at 23 °C± 2°C 
and (65 ± 5) % RH. 
6.2 重复 6.1 条 8 次（8次浸润构成一个循
环）。 
 
Repeat 6.1 eight times (eight immersions 
comprising one cycle). 
6.3 如果循环要求不止一次，则循环间隙将样
品在23 ℃ ± 2 ℃，湿度（65 ± 5） %的条件
下存放保养16 h。存放保养结束后，按6.2 条操
作，每个循环都须更换新的试验液体。 
 
If more than one cycle is called for, age the 
specimen for a period of sixteen hours at 
23 °C± 2°C and （65 ± 5）% RH be-
tween cycles. At the end of each ageing peri-
od, prior to repeating 6.2, discard the used test 
fluid and replace with fresh fluid. 
6.4 结束试验后观察样品，要求无外观变化：如
光泽、收缩、褪色、褶皱、鼓泡、扭曲变形、开
裂等不良变化。 
 
On completion of the required number of cy-
cles, visually examine the specimens then 
determine the relevant property as specified in 
the material standard within the time specified 
after completion of the test cycle or cycles.

A  试验液体  Test fluids 
以下是试验液体指导清单，在试验中如果需要用
到其它试验液体、或者有不适用液体试验零件，
需在相关标准或实验要求中特别规定。 
 The following are a list of fluids, this is a guide 
and any further fluids required or not adapted to 
the part be tested will be stated on the relevant 
standard or test requirements. 
 
A．1  内饰零件  Interior components 
 
 
内部零件所需测试液体如下： 
 
The test fluids for interior components are as 
followings:  
a) 汗液，详见SMTC 5 400 180； 
 
a) Perspiration fluids (as stated in SMTC 5 400 
180); 
b) 饮料（包括咖啡，可乐、橙汁、番茄酱）；  
b) Beverages (to include coffee, coke，orange 
juice and tomato ketchup); 
c) 内饰清洗剂（5%白猫洗洁精）； 
 
c)Cleaning agent-Interior（5% cleaning agent 
of Bai mao）; 
A．2  外部零件  Exterior components 
 
 
外部零件所需测试液体如下： 
 
The test fluids for exterior components are as 
followings: 
a) 汽车用汽油，GB 17930； 
 
a) Automotive gasoline to GB 17930; 
b) 汽车用柴油，GB19147； 
 
b) Automotive Diesel Fuel to GB19147; 
c) 原厂风窗玻璃清洗液（Qx-35） 
 
c) Factory Fill Windshield wash Qx-35 
d) 洗车液； 
e) 15%甲醇汽油(用于材料开发阶段)； 
f) 10%乙醇汽油(用于材料开发阶段)； 
g) 消毒液，如84，浓度按需配比(用于材料
开发阶段)； 
 
d) Car Shampoo; 
e) 15% Methanol Gasoline (applied into mate-
rial development experiment); 
f) 10% Ethanol Gasoline (applied into material 
development experiment); 
g) Sanitiser, e.g. 84, concentration based on 
demand (applied into material development 
experiment);
