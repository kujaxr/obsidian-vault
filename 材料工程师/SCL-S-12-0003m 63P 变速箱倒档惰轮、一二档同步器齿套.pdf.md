Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）齿套和惰轮的损坏是由于两齿不完全接触，应力过大所致。The damage of the idle and ring gear is because of 
the uncompleted contact causing high stress. 
（2）材料的成分，组织，芯部硬度和硬化层深度满足要求；表面硬度偏高，但是这不会导致失效。The chemical 
components, microstructure, core hardness and hardened layer depth meet the requirement; the surface hardness is a little 
higher than the requirement, but this did not contribute to the failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）鉴于倒档惰轮和齿套出现了几次失效（见报告：SCL-S-11-0139M，SCL-S-11-0191M），工程部门应该考虑
变速箱的耐用性，以满足用户的使用。Considering this is not the first failure of this type (see 
reports :SCL-S-11-0139M，SCL-S-11-0191M), Engineering dept should consider if the gearbox is robust enough to 
withstand customers’ driving.

III. 测试结果与分析 Test Results and Analysis 
（1）失效产品 Failed Parts 
图1 显示了惰轮和齿套的配合以及旋转方向；红色的箭头标识的是出现明显断裂的齿。 
图2 和图3 分别显示了失效齿套和惰轮的情况。由图可见：①断裂位置集中于齿尖（图2 和3（a））；②所
有的齿尖均有明显的磨损痕迹（图2 和3（b））；③齿面曾出现过不充分接触（图2 和3（c）），齿面的前端磨损
比较厉害，该过程应该持续了一定的时间；④齿面前部分的接触导致齿的局部承受着较大的载荷，而出现弯曲和
开裂（图2 和3（d））。 
由上述观察可以看出：齿套和惰轮的失效是在两齿轮工作中未完全咬合；局部接触导致齿承受着较大的载荷
所致。 
Fig.1 shows the contact of the ring gear and idle gear and rotating direction during reverse gear. It can be seen: ① 
the position of break is at the front of the tooth (fig.2 and fig.3); ② all the teeth tines were worn; ③the surface of the 
teeth shows incomplete contact （fig. 2 and fig.3 (a)）, the serious wear status of the front of the working surface 
indicates a long time of incomplete contact.; ④ the contact on the front of the teeth made the teeth bear a big local load, 
which led the teeth to break (fig. 2 and fig. 3(d)). 
According to the above checking results, it can be found that the failure of ring gear and idle gear is because of the 
incompleted contact which led the teeth to bear a big load and become damaged.

（2）微观检测Microstructure Check 
     齿套和惰轮的微观形貌如图4 和图5 所示。表面渗碳层主要组织为细小的回火板条状马氏体；齿根基体的

     The microstructures of the ring gear and reverse idler gear are showed by fig. 4and 5. It is found: (1) the main microstructure on the region close to the su rface is 
a fine temper batten martensite; (2)  the one on the matrix is a bigger temper batten martensite. The microstructure is normal.
