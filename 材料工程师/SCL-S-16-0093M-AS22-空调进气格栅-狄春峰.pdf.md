Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．零件由于面差较大形成较大的装配应力，且本身结构不佳，导致失效。2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. Assembly stress caused by surface flushness difference and weak structure led to the failure.  
2.The material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1.供应商改善零件装配面差至符合工程要求。2. 在失效位置增加倒角，添加加强筋。3.泡棉剪切工艺
由“C”型改为“O”型。 
Suggestions: 1. Surface flushness difference should be improved to meet requirement.2. Chamfer and reinforcing bar 
should be added. 3. Foam shearing process should be ‘O’ instead of ‘C’.
