Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺栓安装断裂属于过载断裂，原因与螺栓摩擦系数偏低有关。The bolt fracture in assembly is overload fracture, 
the reason is related to low friction coefficient of bolt. 
2、 螺栓化学成分、硬度和金像组织均符合要求；摩擦系数低于要求值。The chemical composition, hardness and 
microstructure of bolt meet the requirements, but the friction coefficient does not meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 调整螺栓摩擦系数至合格范围内，并控制其稳定性。Adjust the friction coefficient of bolt to qualified range and 
control its stability.
