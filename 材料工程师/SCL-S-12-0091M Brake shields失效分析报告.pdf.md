Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1）制动盘护板是由于多源双向弯曲疲劳开裂。The brake disc shields have failed through multi-origin reverse 
bending fatigue. 
2）疲劳裂纹处在夹紧位置，表明该处材料的震动太大。共振也会有影响。The fatigue origin lies directly at a 
clamped position, suggesting that there is too much vibration of the material surrounding this position.  The 
effects of resonance should also be checked.  
3）材料性能符合该材料的技术要求。The material meets the specificaton. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1）减少该处的震动载荷（包括共振）Reduce the vibrational loading (including resonance) at this position. 
2）为了加强刚度，增加钢板厚度或加强筋。Increase the stiffness by increasing material thickness or forming ribs 
in the part.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
失效制动盘护板如图1 所示。两件都断裂于螺栓装配处。螺栓产生的痕迹正常，表明夹紧力一直合格（见
图2）。其它螺栓孔附近都无裂纹，但是中间螺栓孔的材料在深冲过程中出现撕破 （见图3）。 
The brake disc shields are shown in figure 1.  Both had fractured around the upper bolt hole.  The bedding 
pattern of the bolt is normal and indicates there was no lack of clamping load (figure 2).  There are no cracks at 
the other bolt holes, although the deep drawing of the central bolt hole has torn the metal sheet (figure 3).
