Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 增压器减压阀机构铆钉由于双向多源疲劳失效。The turbocharger waste gate mechanism rivet has failed 
through multi-origin fatigue.  
2、 铆钉表面有制造过程中产生的锻造折叠，诱发了裂纹。The cracking was initiated at folds in the rivet’s 
surface, which were created during the manufacturing process.  
3、 金相组织和硬度与未失效的增压器相似。由于没有提供材料信息，所以不能对材料进行评价。The 
microstructure and hardness were similar to a non-failure turbocharger, however, no materials information was 
provided, so materials evaluation could not be given. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 消除锻造缺陷。Eliminate forging defects. 
2、 检查这台发动机的排气系统压力。Check the gas pressures created by the exhaust system in this engine. 
3、 检查空气阀工作是否正常。Check if the waste gate regulator operation is normal. 
4、 考虑增加半径。 Consider increasing the radius.

断口分析Fracture analysis 
 
图2 显示了断裂面。橙色部分，已弯曲氧化，这清楚地显示：已经断裂了一段时间。橙色表示温度超过260℃。  
断口是典型的疲劳，许多疲劳台阶和凹形裂纹前端表明边部应力集中。主要断裂传播的方向很清楚，但相
对面也产生了小得多的裂纹。没有明显的最后断裂区，表明负载相比材料的强度是非常小的。用电镜对断
口进行了检查，图3 和4 显示了其细节。  
Figure 2 shows the fracture surface.  From the orange colour, it’s clear this has been fractured for some time and 
the surface is completely oxidised.  The orange colour indicates a temerature of over 260°C has been reached. 
The fracture is typical of fatigue, the many ratchet marks and concave crack front indicate a relativly high stress 
concentration effect.  The main fracture direction is clear, however cracking was also initiated on the opposite side, 
although this was much smaller.  There is no obvious final-fracture zone, indicating the load is very small 
compared to the strength of the material.  The fracture was examined with SEM, details are shown in figures 3 & 
4.

金相分析Microstructure analysis 
 
断口的横截面，如图6 所示。制造工艺和表面层结构不知，所以不能给出一个完整的评价。表层由2 个不
同的层组成，均匀地涂覆在铆钉及支架上。发现涂层中有缺陷，这将产生显著地应力集中（图7 至9）。最
外层的涂层出现了氧化；目前尚不清楚这是否对失效有影响，（图10）。 
A cross section was taken through the fracture as shown in figure 6.  The manufacturing process and coating 
structure are not known, so a complete conclusion cannot be given.  The coating structure consists of 2 distinct 
layers, evenly coated over both the rivet & bracket.  Defects in the coating were seen, which would create 
significant stress concentration (figures 7 to 9).  Corrosion of the outermost coating was also seen, although 
whether this is related to the failure is not clear (figure 10).
