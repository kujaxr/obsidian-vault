Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1.零件装配时受过大外力导致断裂。 
2.零件材料符合技术要求。  
Conclusions: 1. The brittle fracture of the part caused by excessive external force during the assembling progress. 
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.  
建议： 1. 检查装配扭矩。 
2. 跟踪工厂装配表现。  
Suggestions: 1. Check the assembly moment.  
2. Follow the assembly performance.
