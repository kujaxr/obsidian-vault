Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）后弹簧盘开裂为双向多源疲劳开裂。 
The cracks of the rear spring holder were bidirectional multi-source fatigue crack. 
（2）材料成分、减薄率符合要求，硬度、金相组织正常 
The material composition and thickness reduction rate met the requirements, hardness and metallographic were normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）校核该处工作载荷，优化设计，增加材料厚度或强度，以提高结构强度。 
Check the working load, improve the design, or increase the thickness or strength to improve the structure strength.
