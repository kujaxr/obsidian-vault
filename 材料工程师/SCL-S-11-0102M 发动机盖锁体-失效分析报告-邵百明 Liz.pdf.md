Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1.断口形貌分析显示零件的断裂为弯曲疲劳断裂； Analysis of the fracture surface indicates bending fatigue failure. 
2. 从受力分析来看，零件断裂区承受较大拉应力；Through analysis of the applied forces, the failure region bears 
high tensile stress during working. 
3. 根据成分，机械性能以及组织的检查结果，材料完全正常；The results of chemical composition, mechanical 
properties and microstructure are acceptable. 
从上面的三点来看，该零件的失效为正常的疲劳断裂失效。Based on the above points, the failures are normal 
fatigue breaks. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 工程部对测试参数和实际加载进行检查。Engineering department must check the test parameters and applied 
load. 
2. 工程部和供应商对现有弹簧的设计进行检验。若确实存在承载能力不能满足产品的要求，需更改设计，
以提高其疲劳寿命。Engineering department, together with supplier must check the spring design. If the 
spring’s load-bearing ability doesn’t meet the requirement of the application, it will be required to redesign the 
part in order to improve the fatigue life.

（2）受力分析 Force Analysis 
在工作中，外部的拉力在弹簧的直线段上产生拉应力，同时产生扭转力矩；为了平衡该力矩，弹簧会发生变
形，并在直线段靠近弹簧轴心的一半截面上产生拉应力；另一半产生压应力，其结果直线段靠近轴心的一边拉应
力增加；另一边处于压应力状态。最大的拉应力出现在靠近弹簧轴线最近的表面上。 
The applied force creates a tensile stress on the linear area of the spring and at the same time, applies a torque. In order to balance the 
torque, the spring distorts. A tensile stress will form on the half section close to the spring axis and a compressive stress on the outer half section. 
Consequently, the total tensile stress on the half section close to the spring axis is increased and that on the other section half is decreased. The 
max.tensile stress is on the surface which is the closest to the spring axis. 
不考虑弹簧在最大伸长量的情况下自身变形的影响和一些小因素的影响，对钩环处的最大拉应力以及弹簧弯
曲部分最大切应力进行求解如表1 所示。 
Without considering the spring elongation during operation and some other small factors, the max tensile stress in the hook area and the 
max. shear stress in the curved area is calculated and shown in table 1.

（3）断口Fracture Surface 
AP11 和AP12 断口的照片如图3 和图4 所示；可以看出明显的裂纹源区，疲劳扩展辉纹，表明该断裂为疲
劳断裂。疲劳扩展辉纹的间距较大，约2µm；在AP12 弹簧的裂纹扩展区，发现有二次裂纹。这一切都表明弹簧
所受到的拉伸载荷较大。瞬断区具有明显的韧窝，说明材料具有一定的韧性。在裂纹源处，未发现引起疲劳源产
生的缺陷。 
The fracture surfaces of AP11 and AP12 springs are shown in figures 3 and 4. Crack origin and fatigue striations were found on the 
fracture surfaces, which show the fractures are because of fatigue. The fatigue striation spacing is nearly 2µm, which is very big; at the same time, 
there are secondary cracks in the AP12 crack propagation area. All these prove the tensile load is too high.  There are a lot of dimple fracture 
patterns on the final rupture regions, which show the material is tough. No material flaws were found at the crack origins.
