Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 增压器壳体是由于热疲劳而开裂的。The turbocharger shell was cracked by thermal fatigue. 
（2） 材料的成分和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and hardness meet the requirements and 
the microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 优化材料和结构，提高零件的抗热疲劳性能。Optimize the material and structure to increase the thermal 
fatigue resistance of the part.
