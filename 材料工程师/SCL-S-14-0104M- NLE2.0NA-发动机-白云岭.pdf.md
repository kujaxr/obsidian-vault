Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、由于排气门热疲劳断裂，气门头部脱落导致第三缸连杆、活塞机构发生缸内撞击。 Due to thermal fatigue fracture 
exhaust valves, valve cylinder head off leads to the third rod, cylinder piston mechanism collision occurred. 
2、与断裂失效气门匹配的摇臂有异常磨损的圆深坑，位置与气门杆头部相吻合，摇臂和气门材料性能符合技术要
求，怀疑排气机构运行异常，因未收到与之匹配其他零件，原因有待分析。 There is a abnormal wear round pit in 
the matching rocker, that position coincide the valve stem, valve and rocker material properties meet the technical 
requirements. Suspect exhaust mechanism abnormal operation. Because not received matching other parts, the reason 
to be analyzed. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 排查排气机构零件部件和匹配情况及该发动机的运行情况。To investigation the parts and components and the 
operation of the system of the engine.
