Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件为低周期疲劳断裂，因为考虑载荷，夹紧力不够。The failure was low cycle fatigue fracture, caused by 
the clamping force is insufficient for the applied load. 
2、 材料性能符合技术要求。 
The requirements of material meet to the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核螺纹连接的实际情况。Check of the actual situation of threaded connections. 
2、 检查装配工序的质量。Check the assembly process quality. 
3、 请校核试验工况。Please check of the test conditions. 
4、 请检查零部件的相关尺寸。Please check the parts related to size.
