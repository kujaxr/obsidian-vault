Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 螺钉属于过载断裂。 
   Bolt was cracking by overload. 
2. 失效螺钉硬度、硬化层深度及根部R 角满足要求。 
Hardness、harden case and R angle of the failed parts meet the requirements.  
3. 同批次螺钉破坏扭矩满足要求，十字槽深度不符合要求。 
   The destroying torsion of the same batch part meets the requirements. Depth of the cross-recess screw didn’t meet the 
requirement.  
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查现场装配过程、螺钉对手件表面质量。 
Check the assembly process、surface quality of the opponent parts. 
2.  供应商控制十字槽深度。 
    Supply controls the depth of the cross-recess screw.
