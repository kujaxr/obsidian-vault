Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）主减速从动齿轮由于受到了较大载荷而发生疲劳断裂。The ring gear tooth was broken by fatigue due to a large 
load. 
（2）主减速从动齿轮齿面接触异常；这可能会导致齿轮载荷增大。The contact of the ring gear tooth surface is 
abnormal, which may cause the working load to increase. 
（3）主减速从动齿轮的成分，表面硬度，芯部硬度以及硬化层深均符合技术要求；金相组织和齿根过渡均无异常。
The composition, surface hardness, core hardness and hardened depth of the ring gear meet the technical requirements. 
The microstructure and root transition are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）排查异常接触的可能原因，如装配和尺寸。Find out the possible causes of abnormal contact, such as assembly and size. 
（2）检查试验过程是否存在异常。Check whether the test process is abnormal.

宽短，表现出接触不均匀的特征。
