Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 发盖内板焊点开裂是疲劳开裂，疲劳起源于内板表面应力集中处。The weld cracking of the inner plate is fatigue 
cracking, which originates from the stress concentration on the surface of the inner plate. 
2. 发盖内板材料成分和抗拉强度合格，焊点的熔核直径和压入深度符合要求。The composition and tensile strength 
of the inner plate are qualified, and the nugget diameter and pressed depth of the welding spot meet the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 评估区域安全系数，校核焊点处应力，优化焊点设计，降低焊点位置应力。Evaluate the safety factor of the area, 
check the stress at the welding spot, optimize the design of the welding spot, and reduce the stress at the welding spot.
