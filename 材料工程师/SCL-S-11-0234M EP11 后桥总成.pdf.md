Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 焊缝是疲劳开裂的；由于加强管未100%焊透，连接处存在应力集中，导致裂纹过早的萌生。加强管的硬
度偏低，其机械性能可能不满足材料的要求；如果偏低，焊缝承受的应力增加，对疲劳产生不利的影响。The weld 
break is due to fatigue; because the strengthening tube didn’t penetrated fully, stress concentration happened at the weld 
and caused the crack germination early. The hardness of the strengthening tube is on the low side and it is possible that 
the mechanical properties don’t meet the requirement, which will make the weld bear a bigger stress and have bad effect 
on the fatigue. 
（2）材料的成分满足技术要求，组织正常。The chemical meet the requirement and microstructure is normal. 
（3）横梁管的硬度正常，加强管的硬度偏低。The hardness of the beam tube is normal and the one of the strengthening 
tube is on the low side. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）增加熔深以减少焊缝处的应力集中。Increase the weld penetration to reduce the stress concentration. 
（2） SQE 考察供应商，检查其原材料，并取样品用于上汽实验室的材料测试。SQE visit suppler to check the raw 
material and collect sample for test at SAIC laboratory.

（2）断口检查 Fracture Surface Checking 
对整个断裂面进行检查，发现断裂为多源的疲劳断裂；裂纹源分布于焊缝的内侧，产生后向外表面扩展；当
裂纹扩展到一定程度，焊缝无法承受弯曲和扭转的载荷，最后出现拉伸和剪切断裂，如图2 所示。图中红色箭头
所标识的疲劳台阶；黄色线条所标识的是最后瞬断区。瞬断区含有拉伸断口和剪切的断口，这是由于后桥在工作
中不仅承受着弯曲载荷，还承受着一定的扭转载荷。 
Checking all the fracture surface, it is found that the break is led by multi-origin fatigue. The crack sources are at 
the inside of the weld and expanding to the outside. When the weld can’t bear the load of bending and torsion, the break 
happened due to tension and shear stress. All is showed by fig.2. The red arrow shows ratchet marks of fatigue; the 
yellow lines show the final rupture. The final fracture surface includes tension fracture surface and shear fracture 
surface，which is because the axle bore bending and torsion.

（3）焊缝检测 weld checking 
     按照如图3 所示的取样方法对开裂的焊缝进行取样。According to the fig.3, the samples for weld checking is 
cut. 
 
 
 
图3 焊缝取样

     各截面的焊缝形貌以及检测结果如图4所示。加强管的熔深满足焊接的要求，但是横梁管的熔深偏低，未
100%的熔透。依据标准：SMTC 5 110 015，搭接焊的要求为：加强管熔深≥0.12*4=0.48mm；横梁：100%熔透；
堆高≥0.7*4=2.8mm ）。The appearance of the weld and test results are showed by fig. 4. The weld penetration of the 
strengthening tube meet the weld requirement, but the one of the beam tube is on the low side and didn’t penetrate the 
wall completely and meet the requirement of the weld. According to the standard SMTC 5 110 015, the Overlapping 
fillet requirement is : strengthening tube ≥0.12*4=0.48mm; beam tube :100% penetrated; Welded seam thickness: ≥
0.7*4=2.8mm. 
     由于横梁未焊透，在横梁和加强管端面的焊缝连接处形成一个应力集中点；疲劳裂纹容易从这些区域产生。
如果焊透(见图5 所表示)，可以减少此处的应力集中，对提高疲劳强度有利。Because of weld penetration less than 
the wall of the beam, a point with high stress comes into being on the weld connecting the beam and strength tube. The 
fatigue crack brought from the regions easily. If the weld penetration is more than the wall of the beam, the stress will 
decrease, which is good for the fatigue life.

（4）材料检测结果 material test results 
材料的相关检测结果如表1 所示。 由表可见：（1）材料的成分合格，但是供应材料检测报告的结果与机动
车检测结果有一定的差异；（2）加强管的硬度低于强度要求换算的硬度，材料的抗拉强度有可能低于要求值；（3）
管材的组织正常；（4）焊缝的硬度和组织正常。The material test results are showed by table 1. It can be shown: (1) the 
material chemical components are ok, but the results from the supplier test report and SMVIC are different;(2) the 
hardness of the strengthening tube is less the hardness converted from the tensile strength requirement and the material 
strength is possible less than the requirement;(3) the microstructures of the tubes are normal;(4) the hardness and 
microstructure of the weld are normal. 
图纸规定管材采用的原材料为热轧板材QStE420TM(Q/BQB310-2009)，但是实际检测发现管材采用的材料为
无缝钢管,因此实际采用的材料与图纸的规定的材料不同。The material required by the drawing is a hot rolled sheet 
(QStE420TM(Q/BQB310-2009)), but in fact, the tube is seamless steel tube，so the material used in the parts is different 
with the required material.
