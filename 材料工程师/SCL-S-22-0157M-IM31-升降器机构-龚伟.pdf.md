Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  攻入自攻钉时，螺钉柱发生了滑牙，继续攻入，导致开裂问题发生。 
When tapping the tapping screw, the screw column slipped, continuing tapped, crack happened. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  将自攻钉尺寸调整至技术要求范围内。 
Adjust the size of tapping screws to the technical requirements. 
2.  跟踪装配表现，需失效问题继续新增，建议进行接头试验。 
Track the assembly performance, if failure problems can’t stop, we suggest to conduct joint test.
