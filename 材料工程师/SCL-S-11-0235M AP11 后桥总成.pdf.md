Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）横梁开裂是由于疲劳所致；过早开裂的原因与局部载荷过大有关。The crack on the beam happened because of fatigue, which 
is due to the big stress on the local area.  
（2）在开裂的区域附近，未发现任何材料缺陷和损伤。No material defect or other damage was found near to the fracture origin. 
（3）材料符合要求。The material meets the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions: 
（1）从设计上减少横梁开裂处的应力。Reduce the stress of the local area through design improvement.
