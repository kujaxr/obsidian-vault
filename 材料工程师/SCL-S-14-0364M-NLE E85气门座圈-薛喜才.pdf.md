Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 排气门座圈与气门之间磨损严重，磨损特征主要以磨粒磨损为主，但具体磨损原因还需进一步系统排查。
Abnormal abrasion happened between exhaust valve seat and the valve. The wear characteristics were mainly the 
abrasive wear, but the specific cause of wearing needed further investigation systemically. 
2. 排气门导管靠近燃烧室侧颜色发黑，其内表面呈现纵向均匀磨痕。Exhaust valve guide near the combustion 
chamber was observed the black traces and the inner surface appeared even wear. 
3. 排气门座圈密度、硬度符合技术要求，金相组织正常。Density and hardness of exhaust valve seat met the technical 
requirement. Microstructure was normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查气门座圈工作载荷及润滑性。Check the working load and the lubrication of exhaust valve seat. 
2. 排查气门与气门座圈的匹配性如形状、尺寸、表面质量等。Check the matching between exhaust valve seat and the 
valve such as shape、dimension、surface quality.  
3. 评估现有材质气门座圈适宜性。Evaluate the suitability of the valve seat.

质。 
 
4#导管内表面粘附物质EDS 分析，见图12 所示。黑色粘附物与导管口白色附着物类似，应该是同一物质。

此会导致气门与导管磨损加剧。
