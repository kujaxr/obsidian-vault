After the desiccant of the failed part absorbs moisture, the solution seeps out and drips onto the 
driver board, resulting in a short circuit and the daytime running lamp does not work.

2） 失效分析 
故障灯返回后，供应商进行了拆解，发现日行灯驱动板上存在不明液体，干燥剂包装上有浸润
痕迹，推断为上方CaCl2干燥剂包中液体渗漏，导致驱动板故障。
