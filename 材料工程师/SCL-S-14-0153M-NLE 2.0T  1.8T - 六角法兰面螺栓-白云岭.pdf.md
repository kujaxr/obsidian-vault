Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属多源疲劳断裂，断裂位于最后一个螺牙，牙底形状不良可能是引起失效的主要原因。 
The failure for the part belong to multi-source fatigue fracture, fracture located in the last thread,  
bad teeth at the end shape is probably the main cause of failure. 
2、 螺栓的牙底形状不合格，螺牙缺陷不合格（最后一牙合格）。 
The shape of the teeth at the end of the bolt failure, defect thread unqualified (last tooth qualified). 
3、 其他材料性能符合技术要求。 
Other material properties meet the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 停用此批螺栓。 
To stop the bolt. 
2、 供应商加强过程质量控制。 
Suppliers to strengthen quality control process.
