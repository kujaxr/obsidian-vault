Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件卡脚因装配过程中敲击到黑色面板导致断裂。 
The failure part was fractured because of hitting black panel during assembly process.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions:  1. 明确装配环节只能拍打面板本体。 
                   Only the panel body can be patted during assembly process.
