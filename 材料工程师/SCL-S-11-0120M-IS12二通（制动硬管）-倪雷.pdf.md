Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1）铸件的材料成分不满足材料的要求，导致其金相组织不符合材料要求；The 
chemical composition doesn’t meet the material requirements, the cast 
microstructure doesn’t meet the requirements. 
2）较大的晶粒尺寸和较多的晶间夹杂物，导致材料强度和韧性降低，受力后发生
晶间开裂。 The large grain size and intergranular inclusions lead to reduced 
material strength & toughness, so intergranular crack occured during assembly. 
 
建议Suggestions：1）隔离该批零件，报废处理；Isolate batch of the parts, scrap the part; 
             2）采用模锻来替代铸造工艺。Use forging process to replace the casting process.

III. 测试结果 Test Results 
1、失效零件宏观观察Macroscopic Observation of the Failure Parts 
（1）两件断裂的二通管如图1所示；断裂在相同的位置。The failure parts have broken at the same position , 
shown in fig. 1. 
（2）其中一件断口表面有明显的颜色差异；有可能在断裂前出现了开裂，而氧化。Different colours are seen on 
the fracture surface; it is possible that there was a crack before final fracture.  
（3）断裂面未发现任何塑性变形的痕迹，非常脆。Material deformation is not seen; brittle fracture only. 
结论 result：脆性断裂。Brittle fracture.

1）成分/Composition：失效铸件成分不合格，见表1；Unqualified, see the table 1 
                    锻件的Fe 元素含量超标，其余元素合格； Iron is higher than the requirement

                      锻件：正常，见图7、8；Ok，see fig. 7、8. 
 
结论：经检测，铸造材料的成分与标准材料要求严重不符，导致金相组织不符合要求，同时晶间有较多夹杂
物。The casting chemical composition doesn’t meet the material requirement, resulting microstructure doesn’t meet 
the requirements, and many intergranular inclusions.
