Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 在卷簧工序产生的原始裂纹导致了零件断裂失效。 
The original crack in the spring coiling process led to the failure of the parts.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、检查卷簧工序过程质量。 
Check the spring coiling process quality. 
2、 增大R 角半径，利于工艺成型。 
Increasing the radius of the R angle, which will help the molding process.
