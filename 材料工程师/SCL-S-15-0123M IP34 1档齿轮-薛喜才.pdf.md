Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 一档齿轮由于承受较大载荷先后发生疲劳断裂。Gear-input 1ST was broken off by fatigue because of the over load. 
2. 失效齿轮的成分、表面硬度、芯部硬度及硬化层深度符合技术要求。失效件表面渗透组织及芯部组织无异常，
零件夹杂物等级正常。齿轮齿根圆弧过渡光滑，未见异常。Chemical components, surface hardness, core hardness 
and harden case met the technical requirement. Microstructure at surface region and core region were normal. 
No-metal inclusion was normal and the gear tooth arc was smooth. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核设计载荷。Check the design load. 
2. 检查试验过程。Check the testing process.
