Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
▪ 由于过载导致门锁锁扣失效；断裂特征为剪切断裂；未发现材料缺陷。The door catches have failed due to overloading.  
Failure characteristics show shear fracture, with no material defects 
▪ 
立柱上有凹痕，表明曾受到高负荷的作用，但是过载的原因不清楚。The pins were dented showing a very high load was 
applied.  The reason for the load cannot be found. 
▪ 供应商对新零件进行了测试，结果超过14kN，但测试过程是一种缓慢的加载过程，对测试结果有重要的影响，
不同于冲击载荷。New parts tested by the supplier have a failure load over 14kN, however the rate of strain is much lower in this test, 
which greatly influences the load to failure.   
建议Suggestions： 
▪ 
供应商必须确保所有产品无加工和脱碳缺陷。Supplier must ensure all parts are supplied without machining defects or 
decarburization. 
▪ 推荐DRE 从设计的角度考虑消除应力集中，并补充图纸中的相关尺寸。Recommend DRE to consider the design of the 
part, removing the stress-concentration.  Also add relevant dimensions to the drawing. 
▪ 为了便于失效分析工作的进行，完整的装配总成必须提交给实验室。For Failure Analysis problems, the whole assembly 
must be submitted to the laboratory.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
对两个失效的侧门锁锁扣总成进行了分析；其失效模式相同（见图1 中失效件S1 和S2）。支架完全变形，立柱
断裂，胶护套管失踪。 在失效之后，失效件S2 遭到损坏，有可能在委托之前对其进行了硬度测试；委托者还提
供了4 个新的部件用于比较。为了说明本报告中断口，图1a 对立柱的里面和外面进行定义；图2 显示了安装位
置和门锁机制，以供参考。 
Two door striker pins were submitted for analysis, the failure characteristics were identical and are shown in figure 1, identified S1 & S2. The 
bracket was completely deformed, the pin fractured and the plastic pin sheathing was missing. S2 had been damaged after failure (possibly for 
hardness test).  Four new parts were also supplied for comparison. For explanation of features in this report, the positions “inside” and “outside” 
are used, as shown in figure 1a.  The installation location and door lock mechanism are shown in figure 2, for reference.

外观检查Visual Examination 
两个立柱断裂位置都是在立柱穿过底板，直径变小处。图3, 4 和5 清楚地表明，该断裂是一种由“内”向“外”
的剪切断裂。断口上的一些区域在失效过程中，受到摩擦而出现了损坏；从断口上，未发现原材料的缺陷。 
S1 立柱有一个加工的凹口（图6a），但开裂不是从这里开始，所以凹口不是导致失效的原因。两个立柱的内表面
上都有明显凹痕（图6b）；而在新零件的表面没有发现凹痕，该凹痕是由于锁闩造成；由于分析来样不含有塑料
套管，因此不能作进一步的分析。这表明，从一开始就缺失塑料套管，或套管在失效过程中破裂而丢失。 除了底
板的弯曲，锁扣总成其它位置，包括铆接，底板，螺栓装配处等没有表现出任何不寻常的特征（图7）。 
Both pins failed at the reduction in diameter as the pin enters the plate. The fracture surfaces clearly indicate shear fracture from the “inside” of 
the catch to “outside” (figures 3 ,4 & 5).  Some areas of the fractures have been damaged by abrasion during the failure, however no indication 
of defects in the raw material were found.   
The pin of S1 has a machined notch (figure 6a), however the fracture did not originate from this point, so it did not contribute to the failure.  Both 
pins have a significant dent on the inside surface (figure 6b) which was not found on the new part.  This was caused by the lock latch, however 
since the plastic sleeve was missing, further analysis cannot be made.  Apart from the bending of the plate, the other areas of the assembly, 
including riveting, base, bolt mounting seat etc did not show any unusual features (figure 7).

金相组织Microstructure Analysis  
 
选择门锁锁扣总成S1 的一个截面作为分析对象，对微观组织和断裂特征进行了检查。图8 显示了一个典型的剪
切断裂特点，材料在相反的方向流动。值得注意的是，立柱大直径表面脱碳非常严重，深度>0.5mm。严重的脱
碳不符合材料要求。 
A cross-section through Striker pin assembly S1 was taken and the microstructure and fracture characteristics checked.  The key features are 
shown in figure 8 and indicate a typical shear fracture, with material flow in opposite directions.  It is notable that a very high level of 
decarburization (>0.5mm) can be seen on the pin larger diameter.  .
