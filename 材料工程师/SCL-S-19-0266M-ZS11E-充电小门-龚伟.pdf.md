Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件壳体内进水，LPP电机锈蚀，导致小门无法开启。 
The water in the component housing, the LPP motor was rusted, and door cannot be opened. 
                 2. 模拟正常使用&雨淋线工况，未能再现壳体进水状态，零件符合技术要求。 
Simulating normal use conditions & rain line, failed to reproduce the water inlet state of the case, 
parts confirmed to technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions：1. 请工程评估LPP 零件防水等级要求。 
                   Please evaluate the LPP parts waterproof rating requirements.
