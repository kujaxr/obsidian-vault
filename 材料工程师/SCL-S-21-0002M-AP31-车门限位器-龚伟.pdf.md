Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 包塑件因限位臂金属骨架变形开裂。 
The plastic package cracked due to the deformation of the metal framework of the limit arm.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions：1. 检查装配环节风险。 
Check the risk of assembly process.
