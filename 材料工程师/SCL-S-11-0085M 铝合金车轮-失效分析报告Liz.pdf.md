Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
 轮缘大规模的损坏是右前轮失效的结果，是由早先某一时间高速驾驶事故中侧面冲击负载造成的。The right 
front wheel failed as a consequence of massive damage to the rim, caused by a side impact load some time prior to the 
highway driving incident.  
 由于这种类型的轮毂损坏不可能发生在高速公路行驶中，可以认为该严重破环与之前事故中第四根轮辐预先
断裂有关系。这种损伤大大减少了车轮的强度，并有漏气的可能。一旦轮胎的压力下降，在行驶中因更大程
度的冲击或紧急制动可能致使轮辐的断裂扩展，导致这一事故Since this type of rim damage cannot occur during 
highway driving, it is believed that the heavy damage and pre-cracking of spoke 4 occurred shortly before the accident.  
This damage significantly reduced the strength of the wheel, and allowed air to escape from the tyre.  Once the tyre 
pressure was reduced, further impacts or sharp braking propagated the spoke fracture during driving, leading to the crash. 
 
 
建议Suggestions：没有None

Right Front Wheel - Spoke fractures 
 
轮辐的断裂面如图3 所示。5#轮辐中的部分已失踪，故其有两个不同的断面。4#辐条有平坦的断裂面并且辐
条表面的油漆有开裂现象，这表明在该部位有一个高的应变力。这个辐条是第一根发生断裂的，断裂是从外
侧向内侧扩展，这表明了在轮辐正面受到过重大冲击，与其表面有严重磨损痕迹相一致。在4#辐条与轮缘结
合的根部处有一个二次裂纹，在整个侧面边缘这也是一个高应力区域（图2）。 
 
1#、2#、3#和5b#辐条随即从内向外发生断裂。5#（5#a）辐条从外到内发生第二处断裂，这可能发生在辐
条高速撞击地面时。 
 
失效后导致了辐条断裂端面的磨损，与车轮内圈接触然后再与路面接触。 
 
The spoke fracture surfaces are shown in figure 3.  One section of spoke #5 was missing, so the two faces are from two 
different fractures. Spoke #4 has the flattest fracture surface and shows paint crazing on the front surface of the spoke, 
which indicates a high level of strain at this point.  This spoke was the first spoke to fail, and fractured from outside to inside, 
suggesting a heavy force acting on the base of the spoke, consistent with the heavy abrasion there. There is a second 
fracture at the rear of spoke #4, where it meets the wheel, which would also be an area of high stress during a lateral rim 
impact (figure 2).  
 
Spokes1#, 2#, 3# and 5#b fractured immediately afterwards from inside to outside. The second fracture to spoke 5# (5#a) 
was from outside to inside, and probably occurred when the spoke hit the ground at high speed. 
 
Abrasive damage on the fractured ends of the spokes was caused after failure, from contact with the inner rim of the wheel 
and then the road.

在3#和4#辐条之间的轮缘部位有严重的破坏。在轮缘断裂点处是完全剪切，表明该处是一最高强度点，在







entirely shear, indicating this as a point of highest stress, which also initiated the brittle fast fracture in both directions around 
the rim (figure 7).  Abrasions to the rim and base of spokes #3, #4 & #5 show that the wheel was turned backward and 
forward from this point against the force, causing shear fractures where the rim section was already cracked, and further 
propagating the brittle crack around the rim.  
 
It must be clarified that rim damage of this level could only occur while the wheel is still properly attached to the vehicle (ie 
before the spoke fractures). 
 
Most of the outer surface of the rim was severely damaged, with heavy abrasions. However, the section of the wheel 
between spokes #2 and #1 is undamaged, suggesting not a gradual build up of abuse, but one single damage occurrence 
(figure 8).
