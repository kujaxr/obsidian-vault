Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions

The impeller has failed before using, during working, due to the centrifugal force, one side of the 
impeller was seriously worn, leading factory complaining the abnormal noise. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement.
