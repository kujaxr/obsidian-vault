Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 吊装过程异常导致壳体过载开裂。The clutch housing overload fractured caused by the abnormal hoisting 
process. 
（2） 失效壳体材料成分合格，硬度、金相组织和孔隙正常。The chemical composition of failed clutch housing is 
qualified, the hardness、microstructure and porosity are normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 关注吊装过程。Pay attention to the hoisting process.
