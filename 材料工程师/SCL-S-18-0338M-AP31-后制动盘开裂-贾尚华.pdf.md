Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  后制动盘是受到外力导致的过载断裂。 
   The rear brake disc is overload fracture caused by external force. 
2.  材料的硬度符合要求；金相组织正常。 
   The hardness of the materials meets the requirements, and the metallographic structure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
关注物流与制造过程，避免发生磕碰损伤。 
Focus on logistics and manufacturing processes to avoid collision damage.
