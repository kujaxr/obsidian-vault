Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、由于1#螺钉丢失夹紧力，信号轮发生轻微松动，导致该处信号轮发生双向疲劳开裂。裂源位于齿根的冲压翻
边处，并逐步扩展至沉孔根部，同时由于1#处先发生开裂，致使2#螺钉处的信号轮发生类似的双向多源疲劳开
裂。As the No.1 screw is lost the clamping load, trigger wheel slightly loose，resulting trigger wheel occurs 
bidirectional fatigue cracking. The crack source located at the flange of the tooth root, and gradually extended to 
the root of the counter bore, as No.1 cracking result the similar two-way multi-source fatigue cracking at the 
trigger wheel of No.2 screw. 
2、疲劳失效信号轮的硬度和金相组织合格。The trigger wheel hardness and microstructure conform to the 
requirements.  
3、1#螺钉夹紧力可能是由于螺钉与信号轮或信号轮和曲轴之间的微动磨损而丢失。The clamping load was 
potentially lost due to fretting wear between the screw and trigger wheel or the trigger wheel and crankshaft.  
建议Suggestions：  
1、改进曲轴信号轮沉孔处的粗加工。Improve quality of trigger wheel countersink machining。 
2、寻找夹紧力丢失的根本原因。Find the fundamental reason for the clamping load.
