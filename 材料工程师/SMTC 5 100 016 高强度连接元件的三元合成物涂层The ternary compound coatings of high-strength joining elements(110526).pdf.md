1 范围 
本标准规定了高强度连接元件的三元合成物
涂层的要求、涂层结构和特性。 
本标准适用于采用机器涂敷在硬度高且在连
接加工过程中容易变形的钢质连接件（例如铝制车
身或者车身附件上的冲裁铆钉和铆钉栓等）表面。
该涂层既可用于保护连接件本身不发生腐蚀，也可
用于防止铝连接件发生接触腐蚀。 
 
1  Scope  
The standard specifies requirements, coating 
structure and properties for the ternary compound 
coatings of high-strength joining elements. 
These coatings are applied to high-hardness 
steel joining elements and are prone to deforma-
tion during connecting processes (such as the 
surface of blanking rivet and rivet shank generally 
used in aluminum body or body accessories). The 
coatings are used in protecting the parts from 
corrosion and are also used in protecting the 
joining aluminum parts from contact corrosion.

3 要求 
采用滚筒油漆法涂制层叠的至少两层防护层。
需采用合适方法将涂敷过程中（包括必要的预处理
和后处理）涂层内的氢去除，以避免铆钉出现氢脆。
3  Requirements 
Applications of no less than two direct-
ly-contacting protection layers are carried out by a 
paint roller. Proper methods shall be used to re-
move hydrogen from within the coatings during 
application (including required pre-treatment and 
after-treatment) to avoid hydrogen-inducted brit-
tleness of the rivets. 
4 涂层结构 
4  Coating structures  
4.1 预处理 
在开始涂敷之前，需将涂敷工件清洁（例如喷
丸）。 
4.1  Pretreatment  
Parts to be coated shall be cleaned (e.g. shot 
peening) before the application.

4.2 增附剂 
在工件母材与4.3中所述的防护层中间，允许
采用机械涂敷法以及渗碳硬化法来涂制一层中间
黏附层（Flash）。 
4.2 Binding agent
It is permissible to apply an intermediate ad-
hesion layer (flash) using mechanical coating 
method or carburizing hardening method between 
the substrate and the protection layers according 
to section 4.3.  
4.3 保护层 
保护层的材质主要为锌、锡和铝，并且由两层
构成。整个涂层最佳层厚为14 µm，允许层厚范围
为10 µm至20 µm。在盲孔和通孔内允许超过下限值。
两分层内含有的颗粒直径均应小于7 µm，此外覆盖
层表面周围不得含锌。 
4.3  Protection layers 
The two protection layers are mainly com-
prised of zinc, tin, and aluminum materials. The 
nominal value of the total coating thickness is 14 
µm, permissible tolerance value is between 10 µm 
to 20 µm. It is permissible to exceed the lower limit 
value in areas around the blind holes and through 
holes. No particle with a diameter equal to or over 
7µm is acceptable between the two layers, and 
the coating surface shall not contain zinc. 
4.3.1  底漆和面漆 
底漆：由锌颗粒与锡层铝粒组成的最佳层厚为
8µ m，允许层厚范围为5µm 至12µm。面漆由锡层
铝粒组成，最佳层厚为6 µm，允许层厚范围为5 µm
至8 µm。面漆涂层各成分含量：  
 
 
a)  铝成分：(10±5)% ；  
b)  锡成分：(20±5)% ；  
c)  锌成分：(70±5)% 。 
4.3.1
Primer and top coating
Primer coating: comprised of zinc particles 
and tin layer of aluminum particles and with a 
structure of an 8µm total nominal coating thick-
ness which allows tolerance of 5 to 12µm. Top 
coating:  comprised of tin layer of aluminum par-
ticles and with a structure of a 6µm total nominal 
coating thickness which allows tolerance of 5 to 
8µm. Percentage of total coating compositions:  
a)  Aluminum composition: (10 ± 5) %; 
b)  Tin composition: (20 ± 5) %; 
c)  Zinc composition: (70 ± 5) %. 
4.3.2  底漆 
底漆由锌颗粒与锡层铝粒组成，最佳层厚为8 
µm，允许层厚范围为5 µm 至12 µm。涂层各成分
含量： 
a)  铝成分：(12±3)% ； 
b)  锡成分：(85±5)% ； 
c)  锌成分：(3±3)% 。 
4.3.2  Top coating  
Comprised of thin layer of aluminum particles 
and with a structure of an 8µm total nominal 
coating thickness which allows tolerance of 5 to 
12µm. 
a)  Aluminum composition: (12 ± 3) % ; 
b)  Tin composition:  (85 ± 5) % ; 
c)  Zinc composition:  (3 ± 3) % . 
4.4 后处理 
在上述工艺过程完成之后，需对已涂敷的工件
实行4 h、200 ℃的高温时效处理，以祛除涂敷作
业时工件内所吸收的氢。 
4.4
After-treatment
After the stated processes, parts shall un-
dergo an aging treatment at 200 °C for 4 h to re-
move the hydrogen generated during the applica-
tion of paint.  
5 特性 
    防护层的特性检验项目包括供货状态、接合和
时效处理后的状态以及整套接合件的涂层状态。 
 
5  Properties
The testing of protection layer properties in-
volves as-received condition, the condition after 
joining, hot aging treatment, and the condition of 
the coating of assembled binders.

5.2.1  试样准备 
为对工作状态下的连接件防护层特性进行检
验，应制作试样。试样材料的物质成分、硬度和韧
性，必须与连接件材料完全相符。为满足5.2和5.3
中的检验所需的条件，总共需准备10件试样，且应
按以下方法来制备： 
 
 
a)  用可连接超过2个部件的连接件（例如冲
裁铆钉），将两块面积各为30 mm x 200 mm
且完整重合的截面拼合成一个试样。 
 
b） 如果连接件用于连接装配件（铆钉栓或螺
母），则应置入扩展度相同的单面截面内。
各连接件之间的间距为30 mm，即每个试
样需用5个连接件。 
 
 
 
c） 试样厚度或者用于制作试样的带材的厚
度，要以需检连接件的夹紧长度为基准。
应按整件复合厚度与各连接件总料厚最
小允值相等的原则来选取试样厚度。 
 
 
 
 
d） 在制作试样时，需注意遵守各连接件的批
量生产工具（凸模，凹模，压板）规定以
及相应的过程参数。接合之后，需对试样
实行40 min (210±5) ℃的时效处理。  
 
5.2.1
Specimen preparation
Specimens shall be prepared to test the pro-
tection layers of joining elements under connect-
ing condition. The material compositions, hard-
ness, and roughness must conform to the cor-
responding joining elements. 10 specimens are 
required for the determination purpose according 
to section 5.2 and 5.3, and shall be manufactured 
through the following process:  
a)  The joining elements which can join more 
than two blanks are permissible (e.g. 
blanking rivet), combine two perfectly 
matching sections with a surface area of 
30mm x 200mm into one testing speci-
men; 
b） If the joining elements used in blanks are 
also used in joining assembly parts (such 
as rivet shanks or nuts), they must be 
inserted into single sections with the 
same extensibility. Distance between two 
joining elements is 3.0mm, which means 
that each specimen requires 5 joining 
elements; 
c） Specimen thickness or the thickness of 
the belt material used in making speci-
mens shall be based on the clamping 
length of the joining elements to be 
tested. The selection of specimens shall 
be in compliance with the principle that 
the thickness of complete part equals to 
the minimum acceptable value of the to-
tal material thickness of each joining 
elements; 
d） The manufacturing of specimens shall be 
in compliance with the specifications of 
the mass production tools (male mould, 
female die, clamp plate) and the cor-
responding procedure parameters. Spe-
cimens shall undergo an aging treatment 
for 40 minutes in 210±5°C after con-
necting.

5.2.2  防护层粘附性试验和脆裂性试验 
     
应按以下步骤进行： 
a)  外观检查（所有试样）：对连接完成
之后可见的连接件表面的防护层进
行外观检查，不得出现肉眼可见的损
伤（裂痕，划痕）； 
 
b)  带试验：仅限于与接合件表面相接触
的连接件表面，例如铆钉头，仅需测

c)  显微照片（1个试样）：对接合之后
与接合件紧贴结合的连接件表面，需
采用显微图片来证明其中的防护层。 
 
5.2.2 Adhesion and brittleness tests of pro-
tection layers 
    Click these steps: 
a)  Appearance inspection (all specimens): 
Inspecting the appearance of protection 
layers of joining elements after connect-
ing, no visible damage (crack, scratch) is 
permissible; 
b)  Adhesive tape test: (Only suitable for the 
fitting surfaces that are in contact with 
the binders, e.g. rivet heads, only one of 
the specimens is required). After the 
adhesive tape is jerked away from the 
surface, it must not contain any of the 
protection coating parts in the adhesive 
surface. Slight color change or individual 
particle inclusion is allowed to appear on 
the adhesive tape surface, but no base 
drop-off is allowed; 
c)  Microscopic photos (one specimen): Mi-
croscopic photos of the contacting sur-
face between binders and joining ele-
ments are required to verify the integra-
tion between protection layers. 
5.2.3  熔融特性（所有试样） 
    已接合的连接件可视表面，在经过高温时
效处理之后不得出现肉眼可见的铜质或半铜
质熔融组织（类似于焊珠）。 
5.2.3
Melting properties (all specimens)
Suitable to all specimens, No visible copper 
or semi-copper melded structures (like weld 
beads) are permissible on the exposed surfaces 
of joining elements. 
5.2.4  防锈性（2 个试样） 
按照DIN EN ISO 9227实行盐雾喷射试验

specimens) 
Suitable to two specimens, Salt spray fog 
test according to DIN EN ISO 9227-SS (only apply 
to aluminum binders), the goal is to detect no 
surface corrosion on substrate after 480 h.  Indi-
vidual red spots are permissible, but no pitting 
corrosion is permissible.
