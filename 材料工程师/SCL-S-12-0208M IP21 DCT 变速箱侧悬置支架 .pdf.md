Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）支架疏松严重且集中，强度被削弱，从而导致配合面出现相对运动，并伴随着铝的磨损，最终螺栓出现严
重的松动。The porosity of the bracket is serious and concentrated and the strength is reduced, which allowed relative 
movement between the matching faces, wear of the aluminum and finally the bolt was lose. 
（2）硬度和组织正常。The hardness and microstructure were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）通过工艺控制或模具的设计，改善疏松，并采用X 射线对铸件进行100%的检测。Improve the porosity by 
controling the process and casting die design and at the same time, check every part by X-ray. 
（2）采取措施消除与螺栓底部配合处的裂纹。Eliminate the crack at the match face with the bottom of the bolt.
