Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 缸盖因热疲劳开裂而失效，这与失效位置材料高温时的性能衰减及受到的综合应力有关。The cylinder head 
cracked due to hot fatigue, which relations to the property degradation of the material at high temperature and the 
integrated stress.  
（2） 失效缸盖排气侧火花塞区域有明显的硬度衰减，这与排气侧工作温度较高有关。The failed cylinder head has 
a obviously hardness degree in the spark plug side of the bridge which is relation to the work temperature. 
（3） 缸盖材料成分合格，组织正常。The chemical compositions of cylinder head is qualified, the microstructure is 
normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 优化开裂区域的水套结构从而改良开裂区域的工作条件。Optimize the water jacket structure to improve the 
working conditions of the cracking region. 
（2） 优化缸盖火力面结构，尽可能避免出现有明显应力集中的区域。Optimize the cylinder head structure and 
avoid obvious stress concentration region as far as possible.
