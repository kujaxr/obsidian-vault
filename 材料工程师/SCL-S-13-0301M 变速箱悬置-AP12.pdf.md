Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件受外力导致断裂。 
2. 零件注塑工艺不佳。3.失效件灰分含量略高。  
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made part fracture. 
2. Injection process of the part was bad.  3.  The ash content of the failure part was slightly higher.    
建议：改善注塑工艺。 
Suggestions: Injection process should be improved.

有银纹，说明是韧断。
