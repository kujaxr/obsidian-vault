Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions

The parts are out of size, resulting in failure to fit in place and failure during part removal.

                    Material performance of part conformed to the requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  优化零件尺寸，避免装配过程中无谓的拆卸。 
                    Optimize part size to avoid unnecessary disassembly during assembly.
