Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1 胶管外层受到外力损伤，导致胶管开裂。 
Conclusion: The crack was damaged by the external injury, leading the hose crack. 
 2 开裂区域位于卡箍区域。且卡箍有移动现象。 
   The crack section is located at the clip area, and the clip was moved. 
 3 胶管材料性能满足要求，且内外层厚度未发现有异常偏薄的现象。 
   The material of hose conforms to the standard, and there is no too thin section of tube 
and cover. 
建议：  避免胶管受到外力损伤。 
 
Suggestion:  Free of the external damage of the hose.

小结： 
1 
胶管开裂由外侧表面引发，断裂纹路为撕裂纹路，起始区域呈现出异常尖角型外力痕迹。说明零件受到外力
损伤后导致开裂。 
The crack start from the surface of the cover, the start section was caused by the abnormal triangle 
mark and tear apart the material. So the crack was caused by the external injury. 
2 
胶管管身具有两个卡箍印记，说明卡箍有移动过的迹象，且开裂点位于卡箍印记内。 
We found the two marks of the clip on the hose, which means the clip was moved, and the crack 
was in the area of the mark. 
3 
新件上卡箍印记说明卡箍边缘会对胶管表面会形成一定的挤压痕迹。 
There is also found the compression at the edge of the clip on the new part. 
4 
胶管材料性能满足要求，且内外层材料无过薄区域。 
The material of the hose conforms to the standard, and there is no too thin section in the tube and 
cover.
