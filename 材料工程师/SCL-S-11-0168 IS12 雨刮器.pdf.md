Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）导条尺寸与图纸不符；Dimensions of guide strip didn’t conform to drawing. 
（2）连接器材料正常；The material performance of the linker conformed to the requirements of standard. 
（3）胶条材料正常。The material performance of the rubber strip conformed to the requirements of standard. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）审核导条供应商生产工艺与工序，责令严格按照图纸尺寸生产。The production process and technology should be audited. In 
production procedure, the dimensions of products must be in accordance with drawing strictly.
