Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）中冷器是由于疲劳而开裂；主裂纹源产生于内表面。其失效原因是由于未喷助焊剂导致扁管与紊流板大面
积未熔合，降低了扁管抵抗内压的能力所致。The CAC was cracked through fatigue; the main crack sources are 
generated in the inside surface. The failure is because no flux caused bad weld between the flow board and flat tube , 
which reduced the load ability. 
（2）扁管的基材成分符合要求；硬度和组织正常。The matrix chemical composition of flat tube meets the requirement; 
the hardness and microstructure are normal. 
（3）散热片和紊流板的组织正常。The microstructures of the thermal fin and flow board are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）隔离同批次的产品。Isolate the parts of the same lot. 
（2）控制钎焊工艺过程，做好巡检，确保产品熔合良好。Control the weld process, carry out the rule check and 
assure the part with good weld.

II. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
该产品沿用IP22 K4 1.8T 的零件，其工作压力有所提高。但是基于前期开发实验的结果，该产品未出
现过失效，表明该产品是可以承受其工作负荷的。 
The part is the same to IP22, but the work pressure is higher. The part passed all the development experiment 
without failure, which showed it can bear the work load. 
收到的失效件如图1 和图2 所示。由图可见： 
（1）扁管有一条长的和一条短的裂纹，长度分别约为：142mm 和20mm，处于中间的区域。 
（2）开裂处内部紊流板冲出了扁管。 
（3）由于膨胀变形，中间5 根管子的截面呈鼓形;与其连接的散热片出现开裂。 
The failed part is showed as Fig. 1 and 2. It can be found:  
(1)There are two cracks in the middle of the fate tube; the lengths are separately 142mm and 20mm; 
(2) The flow board is out of the flat tube at the broken area. 
(3)Due to distortion, the section of the middle 5 tubes is like drum; the thermal fins connected with the flat tube were 
cracked.

   By SEM, the fracture surface was checked, which were showed by Fig. 27 and 28. The results are the same to the 
ones from the macro morphology. The main crack source generated from the inside, which is because the inside surface 
suffered the tension stress. Good weld between the flow board and the flat tube can increase the load ability which makes 
the tube is not easy to distort and at the same time raise the fatigue property.
