Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）同步环是由于过载而断裂的。The tooth ting was broken because of overload. 
（2）同步环的直径和厚度符合要求。The dimension about the dia. and thickness meet the requirement. 
（3）材料的成分，硬度和组织符合要求。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）该零件出现过多次失效，需要对比其它变速箱的同步环，从设计和选材的角度考虑，以解决该问题。The part 
failed several times and compare with other tooth ring from other transmission cases and resolve the problem form the 
design and material.
