1  Scope  
This standard defines the requirements on 
uniform organic coating of a mixture of epoxy 
resins and fluorocarbon resins on electrolytically 
zinc-coated substrate.  
This standard adopts to uniform organic 
coating of a mixture of epoxy resins and 
fluorocarbon resins on electrolytically zinc-coated 
substrate.

3.1  必须设置并控制制造流程，使其不会削弱

and controlled in such a way that the functional 
properties of the finished part are not impaired.

occur that leads to impairment of function and/or 
reduction of the specified corrosion protection.

with metric threads, the friction value properties 
must be determined on a case by case basis; in 
addition, the total thickness of coating system
