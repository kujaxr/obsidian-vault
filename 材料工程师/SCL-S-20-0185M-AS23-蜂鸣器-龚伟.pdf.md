Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 装配时安装孔受挤压开裂。 
The mounting hole cracked during assembly.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商优化注塑工艺以消除注塑孔洞并提高熔接痕位置强度。 
                 The supplier optimizes the injection molding process to eliminate injection holes and increase the 
strength of the weld line. 
2. 安装环节&零件尺寸设计影响分析中。 
                 Installation link & part size design influence is analyzing.
