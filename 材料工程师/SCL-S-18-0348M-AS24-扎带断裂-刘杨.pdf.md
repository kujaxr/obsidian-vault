Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.失效件受外力断裂。 
The failure part was fractured by external force.  
                2. 失效件（亮面扎带）与未失效件（磨砂面扎带）的物性一致，且零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the failure part (glossy ribbon) is the same with the unfailure part(frosted 
surface ribbon), and the performance of the parts material conformed to requirement.

Check abnormal situation in assembly process.
