Scope 
This Technical Supply Specification defines the material requirements on PVC film (e. g. for use on 
sun visors) in the vehicle interior. 
The PVC films shall comply with the legal requirements on flammability according to TL 1010. 
1 
General requirements 
Approval of first supply and changes according to VW 01155. 
Avoidance of hazardous substances according to VW 91101. 
Rounding of figures according to DIN 1333 
At least 2 m² of material are required for complete testing. 
Colorimetric evaluation according to VW 50190 and Application directive 3212 from Laboratory IN 
2 
Scope 
PVC film for trim parts and sun visors. 
3 
Types 
Color, grain and width according to order and basic sample. 
4 
Testing 
The samples shall be conditioned for at least 24 h in standard atmosphere acc. to 
DIN 50 014-23/50-2. 
The required numerical values apply for each individual measurement. 
The English translation is believed to be accurate.In case of discrepancies the German version shall govern. 
Numerical notation according to ISO practice (see VW 01000).
