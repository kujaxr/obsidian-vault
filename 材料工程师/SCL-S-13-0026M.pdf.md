Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属疲劳断裂，A 断面为双向疲劳，B 断面为单向疲劳。 
The failure for the part is a fatigue fracture. 
2、 失效件杆部有严重偏磨现象。The shank for the part was eccentric wear. 
3、 零件的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The parts met the technical requirements of the drawing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查该处连接的实际工况。Check where it will connect with the actual working conditions. 
2、 校核该处的实际使用应力工况。To check actual use stress conditions.
