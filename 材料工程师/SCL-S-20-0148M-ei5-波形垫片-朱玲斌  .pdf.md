Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 波形垫片发生脆性断裂，这与垫片硬度偏高有关。The corrugated gasket brittle fractured, which is related to 
the high hardness of the corrugated gaske. 
（2） 失效垫片的厚度和金相组织正常。The thickness and microstructure of the gaskets failure are normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 关注失效件同批次零件的售后市场表现。Pay attention to the aftermarket performance of the same batch of the 
failed parts.
