Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 下骨架存在冲压裂纹导致使用时发生断裂。The existence of cracks in the LWR washer lead to fracture when 
used.  
（2） 失效件材料成分合格，组织正常。The chemical compositions of the failed part is qualified, the hardness and 
microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 排查下骨架的冲压过程，关注调模件的管控。Investigate of the stamping process of the LWR washer; focus on 
the control of adjusting module parts. 
（2） 关注同批次零件的市场运行情况。Focus on the market operation of the same batch parts.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
在英国售后市场，出现1 个MG3 前减震器下骨架断裂的案例，车辆里程789km。 
The front shock absorber‘s washer of MG3 was failed in the UK aftermarket, the failure vehicle mileage is 789km. 
 
送至实验室失效件如图1 所示，由图可知： 
 
下骨架在顶盖位置发生开裂（图1a）；该位置属于冲压变形较大位置； 
 
宏观断口局部位置没有明显的断口特征，颜色与顶盖内侧镀锌层颜色相近（图1c，1d）；后续的断口和
能谱检测证实断口完全被镀锌层覆盖； 
 
据了解，下骨架的生产工序为：落料拉伸-整形-切边-冲孔-翻孔-镀锌，每批零件生产前，都有调模过程，
模具调整到位后，锁死，然后进行批量生产； 
 
失效件为2015 年9 月份的批次，目前零件已经全部装车，仅出现这一例失效。 
 
The failure was sent the laboratory as shown in Figure 1; the graph shows: 
 
The washer was fractured in the top hat position(Figure 1a) where the stamping deformation is larger; 
 
No obvious fracture characteristics exists in the local fracture where the color is similar to the Zinc 
coating(Figure 1c,1d); subsequent fracture and energy spectrum test confirmed that the position was completely 
covered by Zinc coating. 
 
The washer production processes: falling material tensile-shaping ,cutting edge and punching turning hole 
–galvanized. Before the production of each batch of the parts, the mold should be adjusted to the best, then was 
locked and make mass product. 
 
The failed part was made in September 2015, all the same batch parts have been used; only this part was failed.

断口分析   Fracture Analysis 
 
对图1c 的断口进行扫描电镜和能谱分析，如图2，由图可知： 
 
断口局部区域（位置1）表面有明显的覆盖物，如图2b，2c，能谱结果显示（图2e），整个断口表面有
明显的镀锌特征；位置2 局部区域有镀锌特征（图2f）； 
 
正常断裂形貌如图2d，断口为韧窝； 
 
结合失效件的生产过程，认为失效件在镀锌工艺前已经存在原始裂纹，局部位置已经完全断开，这些裂
纹应该是在冲压成型过程中产生的；裂纹的存在导致失效件在使用过程中过载断裂。 
 
The fracture of the figure 1c was analyzed by SEM and EDX. The result was shown in Figure 2. We can find： 
 
There are obvious covering in position 1(Figure 2b,2c), the EDX result shows: the covering is Zinc 
coating(Figure 2e); the Zinc coating is also found in position 2(Figure 2f) ； 
 
The normal fracture morphology is dimple as shown in Figure 2d； 
 
Combined with the failure parts production process, we can know: the original cracks exists before galvanized, 
these cracks should be produced during the stamping; the cracks leaded to failure when the washer was 
working.
