Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件疲劳开裂，开裂顺序是9、10、11、12 焊点附近先开裂，而后其他位置开裂。Failure part is fatigue crack, 
cracking from9,10,11,12 point ,and then other locations cracking. 
2、 除1 号与2 号焊点的间距不合格外，未发现其他焊接异常。In addition to the 1st and the 2nd pitch solder joints 
failed, the other found no abnormalities. 
3、 失效件的材料性能符合技术要求。The material properties meet to the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强焊接过程质量控制。Strengthen the welding process quality control. 
2、 请校核失效件在路试中的实际受力情况。Check the actual force during the road test
