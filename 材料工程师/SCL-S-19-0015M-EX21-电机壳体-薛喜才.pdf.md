Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 电机壳体搅拌摩擦焊接区域焊缝横向偏移严重，致使两结合面未焊透、结合强度降低。 
   Serious lateral offset in friction srir welding region result in incomplete penetration and strength reducing. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查焊缝出现大幅度偏移的主要原因。 
    Check the reason of serious lateral offset in friction srir welding region. 
2.   加强焊接生产过程控制，制定合适的质量监控计划。 
Strengthen controlling the welding process and formulate appropriate quality plan.
