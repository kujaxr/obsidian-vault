Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 机油尺套管由于受到较大载荷而失效，断口附近无明显缺陷。The Pipe-Dipstick assy failure due to overload, 
no obvious defects was found near fracture. 
（2） 失效件硬度不符合要求，但这不是导致失效的根本原因。抽检4 个机油尺套管新件，硬度全部合格。The 
hardness of failure part does not meet the requirements, but it is not the root cause of failure. 4 new 
Pipe-Dipstick assy tested, the hardness are qualified. 
（3） 失效的成分合格，金相组织正常。The chemical compositions of failure part meets the requirement, the 
microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 按规范进行装配。According to the specification of assembly. 
（2） 供应商应提供合格的零件。The supplier shall provide qualified parts.
