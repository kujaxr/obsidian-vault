Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  开裂位于注塑件分型线处，分型线上有毛刺/尖角。 
The crack was located at the parting line, and there were burrs on it. 
2.  电镀铜层&镍层厚度偏低，不符合技术要求。 
The thickness of copper & nickel layer is low, which does not conform to requirements. 
3.  素材件材料不符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements. 
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商对材料和工艺问题做出解释并整改。 
                    Let the supplier explain and rectify the material and craft problems.
