Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 局部应力较大引发横梁加强板焊趾疲劳开裂。 
   Weld at the cross beam reinforce plate was cracking though fatigue because of big local stress. 
2. 横梁材料成分满足要求，组织正常。 
   Chemical components meet the requirement and microstructure was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化横梁加强板结构或焊缝布置以降低应力。 
   Optismize the cross beam reinforce plate structure or welding distribution to reduce the stress.
