Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 转向机壳体由于过载而在结构变形位置发生开裂，断裂处无明显铸造缺陷和异常损伤。The steering gear 
housing cracked in the structure deformation position, no obvious casting defect and abnormal damage found 
in the fracture. 
（2） 据反馈，与转向机匹配的副车架平面度不符合要求，这会增加装配应力，促进装配开裂的几率。According 
to the feedback, the flatness of the subframe matching to steering gear does not meet the requirement, which 
will increase the chances of the assembly stress, promote the assembly of cracking. 
（3） 失效件成分合格，硬度和组织正常。The component of failure part are qualified, the hardness and 
microstructure are normal.  
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 调查同批次副车架的平面度情况。Investigation the flatness of the same batch of the subframe.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
IP22 在泰国进行装配时，转向机壳体发生开裂。此前临港工厂装配该零件时，也出现过类似案例，见报
告SCL-S-13-0089M，是由于匹配不良导致的。据泰国方面反馈，对失效转向机和副车架进行装配平面度的测量，
测量结果显示，失效件平面度无异常，而副车架的平面度不符合要求。 
The steering gear housing cracked during IP22 assembly in Thailand. A similar case happened in Lingang 
assembly shop which was due to bad matching, see report SCL-S-13-0089M. According to the Thailand feedback on the 
failure part and subframe flatness measurement, the flatness of failure is normal and the flatness of subframe is NG. 
如图1a 为转向机的装配顺序图，图1b 为失效件宏观形貌，1c 和1d 为开裂位置的正面和侧面形貌，由图
可知： 
 转向机壳体连接面一侧的装配压痕明显不均匀，且偏差较大，这会使装配面出现受力异常； 
 转向机壳体外表面无异常痕迹。 
Figure 1a is the steering gear assembly sequence diagram, the Figure 1b is the macro morphology of the failure 
part, the Figure 1c and 1d are the front and side appearance of the cracking position. It can be seen from the figure: 
 The assembly mark of the steering gear connecting surface is uneven, the deviations is large which will cause 
the assembly surface abnormal force. 
 No abnormal force is in the steering casting surface.

材料测试  Material Test Results 
对失效的转向机壳体材料进行检测，其结果如下： 
（1） 失效件材料化学成分合格，硬度正常； 
（2） 失效件组织为a固溶体上分布着棒状、针状及少量块状共晶硅，正常； 
（3） 断口平面未见明显的孔洞和疏松缺陷，如图3。 
     Testing the material of steering gear failure, the results are as shown: 
（1）  the chemical composition of the failure part are qualified, the hardness is normal; 
     （2）  the microstructure of the failure part is a solid solution distributed rod、needle and small amount of lump 
eutectic silicon, normal. 
     （3）  there is no obvious pore and porosity defect, as shown in figure 3.
