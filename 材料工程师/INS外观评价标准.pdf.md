Specification of Imperfection
瑕疵规格说明
Pits (and dot like
effects)
凹点
• A disruption of the applique surface caused by contamination within the injection mold,
thermoforming mold or decorated film. 由于注塑模具，吸塑模具或者装饰薄膜污染造成的零件表面的
缺陷；                                                                                                         • Pits have a circular or
oval shape. 圆形或椭圆形的凹点；
• Pits larger than 0.6mm (0.3mm2) are not allowed. 凹点直径大于0.6mm(0.3mm2) 是不允许的；
• Pits larger than 0.3mm (0.07mm2) up to 0.6mm (0.3mm2) are acceptable. 大于0.3mm
(0.07mm2) 小于等于0.6mm (0.3mm2)的凹点是可以接受的；
• Pits 0.3mm (0.07mm2) in diameter and smaller are allowed and not to be considered. 直径小于
等于0.3mm的凹点不视为缺陷；
Cluster
凹点群
• A grouping of two or more pits within 50 mm of each other. 2个或2个以上凹点且每两个凹点之间的
距离在50mm之内；
• The diameter of any pit in a cluster must be 0.30mm (0.07mm2) or greater. 在凹点群中的凹点直
径必须大于等于0.30mm
• Two clusters are allowed per part. 每个零件允许有两个凹点群；
Dimples
球面凹痕
• A type of soft top coat depression. These blemishes have been proven to mold out. 这是一种由于
保护层较软下陷形成的，这些表面缺陷是被证明可以在注塑以后消失的；
• Dimples up to 2.5mm diameter are acceptable. 直径在小于等于2.5mm的球面凹痕是可以接受
的；
Film Scratch
薄膜刮擦
• A disruption of the applique clear coat caused by abrasion on the surface. 一种由于表面磨损导致
的零件透明保护层的破坏；
• The scratch cannot disrupt the color of the film (No color content). 这种刮擦不能破坏薄膜的颜色
（无颜色变化）；
• One scratch is allowed given its length is less than 5mm. 允许有一条长度小于5mm的擦痕；
Fiber / Hair
纤维 / 头发
• A disruption of the applique surface casued by contamination within the injection mold,
thermoforming mold or decorated film. 从注塑模具，吸塑模具或者装饰薄膜的污染而导致的注塑零件
表面的破坏；                                                                                                 • Fibers have a long
and narrow shape; 长而窄形状的纤维；
• Fibres / Hairs of max. 1.5mm are acceptable pending they are greater than 200mm apart from
each other; 距离200mm以上且最大1.5mm长度的纤维/头发是被接受的；
Scuff
磨损
• Small grouping of scratches usually due to drag in the injection mold, small draft angles on the
part or mishandling of the product.  这是零件在脱模时由于脱模角太小或者由于误操作而引起的刮擦
• Not allowed. 不允许；
Irregular color
spots
不规则色斑
• A disruption of the applique surface caused by printing defects, pin hole effects, unevenness of
carrier film surface. 由于印刷缺陷，针眼效果，载体薄膜的不平整而引起的零件表面的破坏；
• Color spots have a circular or oval shape. 色斑为圆形或者椭圆形的形状；
• Color spots larger than 0.6mm (0.3mm2) are not allowed. 直径大于0.6mm的色斑是不允许的；
• Color spots larger than 0.3mm (0.07mm2) up to 0.6mm (0.3mm2) are acceptable. 色斑直径大于
0.3mm小于等于0.6mm是可接受的；
• Color spots 0.3mm (0.07mm2) in diameter and smaller are allowed and not bo be considered.
直径小于等于0.3mm的色斑不视为缺陷；
• In case of high-contrast color spots boundary samples to be eslablished even if spot sizes
comply with a.m. specification.  如果色斑为高对比度的，需要建立边界样品作为参考，无论这些
色斑是否符合以上的界定；
• For color spots of lower contrast (e.g. matt dots, white spots) which are not following effect
sizes, boundary samples to be established. 对于色斑为低对比度的 （如哑光点，白点），不能遵
照以上界定，需要建立边界样品作为参考。
Color / Pattern
• Color and pattern must match signed master swatch. 颜色及图案必须符合签字的标准样板；
• Woodgrains: Δ E = Boundary samples to be established;  木纹：Δ E＝所建边界样品；
• Metalized patterns: Δ E = up to 2.8; 真金属薄膜：Δ E＝最大为2.8；
• Metallics and Unicolor: Δ E=up to 1.7; 金属油墨或者单色薄膜： Δ E ＝ 最大为1.7；
• Must match within tolerances; 必须在公差范围之内；
• In case of non compliance of boundary samples presented by Kurz with the OEM requirements
the customer need to establish part assenbly out of same foil batch respectively foil batches with
colour harmony. 若无OEM认可的边界样板的情况下，客户需要根据不同的薄膜的批号进行零件的
组装以使颜色匹配；

Inspection Technique  检查技术
Parts are to be inspected at arms length (50cm to 70cm) in car position. Slight rotation is acceptable, but no "gun sighting".  零件须放置于类似车中位置，大约一臂远距离 （50cm~70cm) 进行检查；
轻微的转动是可以接受的，但不允许瞄准式检查；
3 to 6 senconds maximum.  3至最多6秒钟；
Lighting should be: Light source D65, illumination of 1000 Lux ± 200 Lux. 灯光应该为： D65, 光照强度为1000 Lux ± 200 Lux.


• Kurz推荐针对吸塑工艺客户须使用有以下功能的先进的吸塑设备：
a) 可以对整个加热表面进行均匀加热以防止局部区域过冷或者过热；                                                                                                                              b)
需要有真空和正压（最高6bar)进行成型,特别是针对于真金属的薄膜；                                                                                                                             •
吸塑设备基本配置：压力箱；独立控制的真空和正压供给装置；可分别独立设置的陶瓷加热装置；上加热和下加热可以独立控制；有温度控制功能的吸塑模具；
• Kurz推荐在吸塑工艺中薄膜装载处和吸塑设备的顶部使用片流箱（10000无尘室级别），并在INS薄膜输送至吸塑设备的加热通道时有清洁装置；
• Kurz推荐处理薄膜和零件，例如刮擦拉丝和高光设计，因为比较容易刮擦，需要在整个生产过程中给予特别关注；对于零件转运托盘的运用，操作人员和检查人员手套的应用，特别批准的保护薄膜的应用，堆栈设备
的应用必须避免零件A面之间的碰触；
• 为了避免薄膜极度变薄（以及图案扭曲），吸塑模具型腔的R角最小为0.5mm；
• 为了避免薄膜破裂，例如冷拉伸，注塑模具型腔最小R角为1.0mm；嵌片的几何形状必须和注塑型腔尽量完美匹配；
• 吸塑模具不同型腔之间需要有足够的距离以防止薄膜和装饰层过度拉伸以及薄膜和装饰层破裂；
• 由于不均匀的加热装置的设置，缺少辅助正压或者由于模具设计/型腔几何形状而引起的过度拉伸，会造成真金属薄膜嵌件表面发白现象；
• 基材中的凝胶点是由于材料的特性或者材料供应商生产工艺过程中产生的，是不可避免的。由于凝胶点而产生的最高2％的ABS基材废品率和最高20％的TPO基材废品率是须被接受，投诉无法成立；

Applique (Insert) Production Aspects
INS 产品外观
• Kurz recommend for thermoforming process to use a modern thermoforming machine which should allow the following main conditions:
a) homogenous distribution of temperature over full heating surface to avoid cold and/or hot spots.
b) Forming should be performed by vacuum and pressure support (up to 6 bar respectively 87 psi) especially for metalized films.
• Minum configuration of thermoforming machine: pressure box; independent control of vacuum and pressure supply; ceramic heaters with single settings; independent control of top heater and bottom heater;
temperature control for thermoforming tool;
•Kurz recommends for thermoforming process to use a laminar flow box (10000 clean room class) at the film load station and on top of the thermoforming machine and a cleaning device for the insert foil running into
the heating channel of thermoforming machine.
• Kurz recommends to handle films and appliques, e.g. with mechanical brushed and glossy designs, with special care during all production steps due to higher disposition of scratches. The use of transit trays for
appliques, gloves for the operators and inspection personnel, specially approved protective tape, stacking lifters to avoid touch on A-surface between appliques have been proven for part production.
• To avoid extreme film thinning (and pattern distortion) minimum radii of the forming cavity of min R0.5mm should be adopted.
• To avoid cracking effects, e.g. cold stretch, in the applique along the radii of the part minimum radii of the injection mold cavity of min R1.0mm (A-surface radii) should be adopted. Perfect fit of applique geometry
into injection mold cavity is strongly required.
• Sufficient distance between cavities of the thermoforming tool to be considered to avoid film or decor overstretch and film or decor cracking.
• Whitening effects by using chrome-foils can occur on the applique surface caused by unbalanced heater setting, forming without pressure support and/or by high stretch conditions of mold design and/or cavity
geometry.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                •
Substrate gels in the matrix of the backing foil are the result of the material characteristics and/or production process at the Extrusion manufacturer and can not be avoided. A scrap rate of up to 2% for ABS backing
films and up to 20% for TPO backing films due to substrate gels must be considered by the applique manufacturer and is not subject to claim.
