Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  滑门限位块发生了疲劳开裂。 
    Fatigue cracking of sliding threshold limiting block 
2.  滑门限位块的材料硬度、金相不满足图纸规定的SCR340LA 的要求，疑似DC01。 
The material composition and hardness of the front door inner panel meet the requirements. 
. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 加强供应商管控，按照图纸要求的原材料进行后续试验。 
Reduce the local stress of the front door inner panel, such as adding gaskets at the installation point Strengthen 
supplier control and follow-up test of raw materials according to drawings.
