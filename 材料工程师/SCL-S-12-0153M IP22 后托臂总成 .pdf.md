Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 后托臂通过双向疲劳而断裂。The rear trailing arm fractured through reverse bending fatigue. 
（2） 材料满足厚度，硬度和微观组织要求。The material meets the requirements for thickness, hardness and 
microstructure. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核开裂处的强度。Check the applied load at the broken region.
