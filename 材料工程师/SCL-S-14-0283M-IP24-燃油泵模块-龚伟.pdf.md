Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受异常外力断裂。  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1. The fracture of the part caused by abnormal external force.   
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.     
建议： 1.加强装配物流环节对零件的保护。 
       2.断裂位置添加R 角过渡。 
Suggestions:  1. Improve the protection of the parts during the assembly and logistics.  
            2. Add a R-angle in the fracture position.
