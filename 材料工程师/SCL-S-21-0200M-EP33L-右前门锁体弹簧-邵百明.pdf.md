Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）左前门锁体复位弹簧由于工作应力过大而发生疲劳断裂。The return spring of the left front door lock was 
fractured by fatigue due to excessive working stress. 
（2）弹簧的线径、外径、硬度以及组织均正常。The wire diameter, outer diameter, hardness and structure of the spring 
are normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化设计，降低弹簧的工作应力。Optimize the design and reduce the working stress of the spring.
