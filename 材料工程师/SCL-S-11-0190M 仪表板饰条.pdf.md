Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：注塑工艺不佳，导致该部位负荷降低，容易开裂。 
Conclusions: The bad injection process make this position bear less load and crack more easily. 
建议：优化注塑工艺。  
Suggestions：The injection process should be optimized.
