Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1．通过宏观分析和材料分析可以判定，衬套受内部异物刮擦，导致开裂。 
2.  失效件的材料符合设计要求。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the rubber pipe, the main reason for the 
rubber dust shield is the inside surface of the dust shield was scratched by foreign body. After tests, the 
material of the dust shield conforms to the drawing requirement. 
建议：避免防尘罩内存在异物。 
Suggestions：The foreign body in the rubber dust shield should be avoided.
