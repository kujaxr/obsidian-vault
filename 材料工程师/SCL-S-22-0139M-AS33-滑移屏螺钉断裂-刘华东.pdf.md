Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效螺钉是延迟断裂。The failure screw was delayed fracture. 
2、失效螺钉硬度、渗碳层深度符合标准要求，金相组织未见异常。The hardness and carburizing layer depth of failure 
screw meet the standard requirements. And the microstructure of core was normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离该批次零件并对已装车零件进行更换。Isolate these bolts and replace the installed bolts of the same batch. 
2、排查电镀后去氢工序是否有异常。Check whether the hydrogen removal process after electroplating is abnormal  
3、评估该使用位置是否可以使用8.8 级米制螺栓，从而避免延迟断裂的发生。Evaluate whether grade 8.8 metric bolts 
can be used at this use position to avoid delayed fracture.
