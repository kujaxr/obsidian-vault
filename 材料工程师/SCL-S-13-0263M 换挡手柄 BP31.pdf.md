Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致开裂。2. 零件材料符合技术要求。3. 零件结构不一致。 
Conclusions: 1. The failure part was cracked by external forces. 
       2. Material performance of the parts conformed to requirements. 
       3. The structure of the part was different. 
建议： 1. 工程对该结构的可靠性进行评估。2. 供应商对结构不一致做出解释。 
Suggestions: 1. Please the engineer department assess the reliability of the structure. 
           2. Let the supplier explain the difference of the structure.
