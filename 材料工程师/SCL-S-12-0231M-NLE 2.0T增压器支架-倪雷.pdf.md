Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、疲劳断裂始于线切割后形成的凹坑处，该部位为应力集中区。The site has a pit after wire cutting. The site 
is stress concentration area, fatigue fracture from here. 
2、化学成分，硬度和金相组织均符合要求（见表1）。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure 
all conforms to the requirements（see table 1）. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1）建议供应商可采用冲切工艺进行成型。Suggest suppliers use punching process for molding. 
    2）建议优化零件结构（如翻边），并进行校核。Optimization of the the parts structure（such as flanging）, 
and checked.
