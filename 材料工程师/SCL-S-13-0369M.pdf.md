Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属疲劳断裂。The fracture belong to fatigue crack. 
2、失效件的C 含量超差，由硬度转换的失效件拉伸强度偏低，其他材料性能未见异常。C content for the failure 
parts did not meet the requirement and the tensile strength is low ,other material properties of the parts 
were normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 校核失效件的实际受力情况，并优化设计结构和选材等。 
Check he actual failure of parts of the forces, and optimize the design of the structure and material 
selection.
