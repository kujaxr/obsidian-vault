Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  开裂位于注塑件分型线处，分型线上有尖角。 
The crack was located at the parting line, and there were burrs on it. 
2.  电镀镍层厚度偏低，但不是失效的主要原因。 
The thickness of nickel layer is low, but it’s not the main reason of failure. 
3.  素材件材料符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商对工艺问题做出整改。 
                    Let the supplier rectify the craft problems.
