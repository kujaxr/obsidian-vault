Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．零件由于强度低，装配时发生断裂，导致失效。2．零件材料不符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The strength of part was low and fracture caused by assembly force which led to the failure.  
2. The material performance of part not conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1.责令供应商解释材料问题。2.在失效位置增加倒角。 
Suggestions: 1. The material of part should be explained by supplier.2. Chamfer should be added.
