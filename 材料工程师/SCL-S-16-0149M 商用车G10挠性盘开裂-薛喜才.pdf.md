Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 失效件属于疲劳开裂。The part failed though fatigue fracture. 
 
（2） 材料成分、厚度符合技术要求；硬度、组织正常。Chemical components、thickness meet the technical requirement 
and hardness、microstructure are normal.  
（3） 尺寸符合图纸要求。Dimensions meet the drawing requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）进一步排查引起挠性盘疲劳开裂的原因(如与发动机或变速箱的匹配等)。Further investigate the reason 
that lead to fatigue cracking such as the matching situation.
