Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 缸体在铸造过程中于高温下出现了开裂。The engine block was cracked under high temperature during the 
casting. 
（2） 材料硬度，组织和石墨形态均符合技术要求；未见孔隙。The hardness, microstructure and graphite shape meet 
the requirement; no porosity was found. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 控制铸造生产过程降低局部开裂的可能性，同时加强对开裂部位的外观检查。Control the casting process to 
reduce the crack probability and at the same time strengthen the appearance check for the crack location.
