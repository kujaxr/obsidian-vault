Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）线束是通过疲劳断裂的。这是由于振动剧烈所致。The wire was broken by fatigue, which was caused by serious 
vibration. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化设计，降低振动。Optimize the design and reduce the vibration.
