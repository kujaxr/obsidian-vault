Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1）大灯喷嘴的材料符合图纸要求。 
2）通过宏观分析可以判定，大灯喷嘴安装时受过大外力，导致裂纹。 
Conclusions:1）The material of headlight nozzle conforms to the drawing requirements. 
             2）According to the macroscopic observation，it can be seen that  the headlight nozzle 
was broken by the excessive force during  assembly. 
建议： 避免安装时承受异常外力 。 
Suggestions：The  excessive force should be avoided during  assembly.
