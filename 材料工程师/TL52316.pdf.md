1 
Scope 
This TL standard specifies the material requirements for EPDM seals for radiators, heat 
exchangers and seals/sealing washers in the cooling system. 
Temperature range: -35 °C to +150 °C 
2 
Designation 
Sample designation for type A: 
EPDM seal according to TL 523 16-A 
3 
Requirements 
3.1 
General requirements 
Approval of first supply and changes according to Volkswagen standard VW 011 55. 
Avoidance of hazardous substances according to VW 911 01. 
A total of 15 finished parts and 2 test plates, DIN A5 format, 2 mm thick, are required for complete 
testing. 
3.2 
Types 
According to drawing; deviating requirements called out in drawings take precedence over this 
TL standard. 
3.3 
Color 
Black.

5 
Notes on testing 
Due to the different shapes of the radiator and heat exchanger seals, S3A standard specimens are 
taken from radiator seals and S3 standard specimens from the heat exchanger seals, each 
according to DIN 53504. 
5.1 
Aging at elevated temperature 
Standard specimens are aged in a mechanical circulation oven according to DIN 53508. 
Measurements are taken 15 min after removal. 
5.2 
Coolant aging 
Standard specimens are aged in an autoclave in a coolant mixture (50 volume percent de-ionized 
water and 50 volume percent coolant additive according to TL 774, type F) at +150 °C. The fluid 
volume must be 150 to 180 times the specimen volume. 
The specimens are subsequently cooled to 23 ± 2 °C in the test fluid and measured. 
5.3 
Oil aging 
Test part sections are aged in reference engine oil C.20 (Cecilia 20, by the company Mobil) 
according to TL 521 85. The fluid volume must be 80 ± 5 times the specimen volume. 
Test containers and specimen holders according to PV 3323. Measurements taken 30 min after 
removal from the test medium. 
5.4 
Permanent deformation 
Due to the shape of the component, the values for heat exchanger seals must be determined on 
test plates (2 mm thick) according to PV 3307 with 50% compression. For sealing washers or 
similar components, the permanent deformation is carried out according to PV 3330 with 40% 
compression. 
5.5 
Behavior in air at low temperature 
Test plate sections 15 mm wide and approx. 100 mm long are aged in cold air.
