Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 缸套黑斑是由于腐蚀产生的；The black spots on the cylinder liner are caused by corrosion. 
（2） 能谱检测结果显示：黑斑区域未见明显的异常腐蚀性元素。The energy spectrum results show that there are no 
obvious abnormmal corrosive elements in the black spots. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 结合产品的生产，装配和实验过程，排查腐蚀液体的来源。Check the source of corrosive liquid in combination 
with the production process, assembly and experiment porcess of the the product.
