Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于强度不足引起的过载断裂，造成强度不足的原因是该失效件未经调质热处理。 
The crack due to overload by the lower strength. The reason of lower strength was the bolt without quenching 
and tempering. 
2、 失效件的化学成分符合技术要求，硬度不合格。 
The chemical composition met the technical requirements, the hardness did not meet he technical requirement. 
 
  建议Suggestions： 
1、 停用此批零件。Stop useing this batch of parts. 
2、 加强供应商过程质量管控。To strengthen the process quality control .
