Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）增压器回油管是由于振动载荷过大，而产生多源疲劳开裂的。The supercharger return pipe was broken through 
multi-origin fatigue because of overload which was caused by vibration. 
（2）材料的成分符合要求；组织和硬度正常；无焊接缺陷。The chemical compositions of the material meet the 
requirement; the microstructure and hardness are normal; no weld defect was found. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）建议优化结构，降低局部应力。Optimize the structure and reduce the local stress.
