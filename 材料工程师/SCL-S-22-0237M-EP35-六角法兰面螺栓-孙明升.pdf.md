Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 该六角法兰面螺栓受到侧面冲击载荷而发生过载剪切断裂。The hexagon flange bolt was subjected to side 
impact load and overload shear fracture occurred. 
（2） 螺栓材料表面硬度、芯部硬度符合要求，金相组织正常。The surface hardness and core hardness of bolt 
materials met the requirements, and the metallographic structure was normal.
