Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  喷丸工艺前，转向机壳体已存在原始裂纹，使用过程中发生断裂。 
Crack existed in housing before shot peening and fractured in using at last. 
2.  壳体材料成分、硬度及组织无异常。 
    Chemical components、hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 进一步排查可能导致壳体开裂的工序，并采取预防措施。 
   Futher check the possible procedure and prevent happening again.
