Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 螺栓端部光杆高度过高，不符合要求。 
The length of polish rod in thread is too long, didn’t confirm to the requirement. 
                2. 零件材料符合技术要求。

                2．供应商优化注塑工艺。 
                   Suppliers improve the injection process.
