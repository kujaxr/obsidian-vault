Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）变形的原因是工作应力超过了材料的屈服强度。这可能与铸件的原始组织，内应力，试验过程或者选材有
关，但是具体原因不明确。The cause of the deformation is working stress over the yield stress of the material. This is possibly related 
with original microstructure, casting stresses, test process or material choice, but the root cause is not clear. 
（2）材料的硬度，组织符合要求。The hardness and microstructure meet the requirements. 
（3）成分中Si 含量偏低，但不会导致产品变形失效。 The Si content is on the low side, but this did not cause the deformation. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检测台架试验，看是否有异常。Check the bench test and make sure if it is abnormal. 
（2）选择同批次的3 件歧管，在最大的工作温度（约815℃）下保温5h，进行退火处理，检测热处理前后，尺
寸和组织。注意缓慢升温和缓慢冷却。 Make an anneal treatment（about 815℃/5h）for 3 exhaust manifolds of the same batch and 
check the dimensions and microstructure before and after the experiment. Please pay attention to heating and cooling slowly. 
（3）检查设计，确认材料是否合适。Check the design and make sure if the material is proper. 
（4）为了便于失效分析，请提供全部的零部件。For failure investigations, please submit the whole part.

III. 测试结果与分析 Test Results and Analysis  
（1）失效产品 Failed Parts 
     据委托者介绍，一件排气歧管进行150.5h 的测试后，发生变形，导致拆卸困难。试验室接收的样品为左半
部分（见图1）。 
According to the introduction from the submitter, a exhausted manifold deformed after 150.5 h test, which led to 
difficulty while disassembling the part. Only the left half part （see fig.1）was received from the submitter.  
      
 
 
图1 排气歧管变形 
Fig. deformation of the exhaust manifold 
 
导致排气歧管出现变形的根本原因为工作应力超过了材料的屈服强度。其可能原因有如下几种： 
 材料的原始组织中珠光体含量比较高，在高温下发生相变，导致内部应力增加，从而发生尺寸变化。 
 选材不对，无法承受高温下的载荷。 
 排气管存在较大的内应力，导致后续测试中，发生变形。 
 测试中，某种异常情况发生导致排气歧管承受了较大的载荷。 
 已排除合金成分不对的可能原因。（Mo 含量不足，会降低材料在高温下的机械性能，请看(2)材料测试） 
The root cause of the deformation is working stress over the yield stress of the material. The possible reason is as 
following: 
 The pearlite content was high and transformed under the high temperature, which led to internal stress 
increasing and the dimension changing.  
 The material choice is not suitable to the high temperature & stress condition. 
 There was high stress from the casting process in the new manifold, which led to deformation during the test.  
 Some abnormal circumstance, which led to high stress and deformation. 
 A reason relating to the chemical composition was excluded.  (Insufficient Mo can reduce the mechanical 
properties under high temperature;（see (2) material test）
