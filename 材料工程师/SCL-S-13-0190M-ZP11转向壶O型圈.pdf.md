Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论：1、O 型圈受外力挤压磨损导致表面处多处异常变形，致使使用中转向储液罐密封不良。 
建议：1、确保O 型圈在安装及使用过程中不产生异常变形。 
Conclusion: 1. The O ring was extruded by the external force in actual use and its surface have some abnormal 
deformation, resulting in the poor sealing performance for steering fluid reservoir. 
Suggestions: 1. Ensure the O ring do not appear abnormal deformation in the process of installation and and use .
