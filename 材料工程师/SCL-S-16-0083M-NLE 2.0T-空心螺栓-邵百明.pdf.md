Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）空心螺栓是由于受到较大的扭转载荷而发生断裂。The hollow bolt is broken due to the large torsion load. 
（2）失效螺栓的牙形与同批次零件略有不同，但这不是导致螺栓断裂的原因。The tooth shape of the failed bolt is 
slightly different from that of the same batch, but this is not the reason for failure of the bolt. 
（3）失效螺栓的成分符合要求；其硬度和组织正常，与同批次零件的相比无明显差异。The components of the failure 
bolt meet the requirements; the hardness and microstructure are normal and there is no significant difference compared 
with the parts of the same batch. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）排查装配过程。Investigate the assembly process. 
（2）请供应商关注牙形尺寸及稳定。Please pay attention to the size and stability of the tooth.
