Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1. 悬置内嵌件折弯处疲劳开裂，开裂处无R 角。Sub-Inserts crack by fatigue at bending region and there isn’t R 
angle at the crack. 
2. 内嵌件材料成分满足要求，强度、组织以及断裂处减薄率正常。Chemical composition meets the requirements. 
Microstructure、strength and thinning rate at fracture region are normal. 
3. 新件断裂处也无R 角，且经过Z+X 向疲劳试验后也发生开裂。There isn’t R angle at the crack region of the 
new one and it also cracks after Z + X direction fatigue test. 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 开裂处增加R 角。Increase the R angle at the crack region. 
2. 对后续的路试件进一步检查，确认失效部位是否存在同样的裂纹。Further check the subsequent testing parts to  
confirm whether the same cracks exist at the failure position
