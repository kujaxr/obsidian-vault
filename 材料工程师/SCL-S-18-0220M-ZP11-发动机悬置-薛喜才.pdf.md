Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 铸件内存在分层、热态裂纹、疏松等微观缺陷，造成零件强度变弱，受到多次冲击后断裂失效。 
   There are casting defects such as delamination, hot crack and porosity, which result in the strength reducing and 
fracturing under repeatedly impact. 
2.  铸件材料成分、孔隙率、孔隙大小满足要求，硬度及组织正常。 
Chemical components、porosity and dimension of casting hole meet the requirements. Hardness and microstructure 
are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  铸件供应商加强过程监控，确保零件质量。 
Casting supplier strengthens monitoring the process to make sure the parts quality.  
2.  关注售后市场类似的失效零件。 
    Pay attention to the similar failure parts after sale market.

图2  断口宏观形貌 
检测失效件与正常件的断口，结果如图3~图5 所示。对比发现：（1）正常件有明显的疏松和气孔，但其
破断力远远大于设计要求。（2）失效件断口与正常件存在一定的不同，其表面存在大量的分层、热态裂纹、
疏松等铸造缺陷，这会导致零件强度降低，容易断裂失效；此外，铸件局部区域有疲劳+过载混合特征，推
断零件受到了多次冲击。 
The fracture surface of failure parts and normal parts is shown in figure 3~ fugure5. It is found that: (1) obvious 
porosity and porosity is observed in the normal parts, but the broken force is much larger than the design 
requirements. (2) the fracture surface of failure parts is different from that normal. There are lots of casting defects 
such as porosity, delamination and hot cracks on the fracture surface of the failure parts, which will lead to the 
strength reduction and fracturing failure. Besides, mixed characteristics of fatigue and overload were observed in the 
local, which were subjected to repeatedly impact.
