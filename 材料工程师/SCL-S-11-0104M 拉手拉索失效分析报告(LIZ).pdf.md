Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 
钢丝断口为双向弯曲疲劳断裂； 断裂位置位于钢丝与铸锌接头连接处。The wire has failed through fatigue at the 
point where it enters the cast end fitting. 
2. 钢丝无缺点，材料硬度及金相组织都合格。There were no defects in the wire, and the material hardness and microstructure 
were acceptable. 
3. 严重磨损是在铸锌接头表面上，表明铸锌接头不容易旋转。 这导致钢丝承受非正常工作载荷，导致最终
疲劳断裂。 Heavy abrasions were found on the zinc end fitting, which suggests that the fitting could not rotate easily in the door 
handle housing.  This can explain the abnormal loading which caused the fatigue failure of the wire. 
4. 故障发生在热循环测试中。锌接头的热膨胀有可能对失效产生了影响。The failure occurred during hot cycle testing.  
It’s possible that the thermal expansion of the zinc fitting has influenced the failure. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查系统中的其它组件，找到铸锌接头不能旋转的原因。Check other components in the assembly, to find the reason 
why the fitting cannot rotate. 
2. 检查相关组件的热性能 Check the thermal properties of the relevant components.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
失效拉手拉索的断口清楚地表明，钢丝是由于发生双向弯曲疲劳断裂而失效的（图1 及2）。铸锌接头表面
有旋转的痕迹，这是由于接头转动不顺畅所致。转动不顺畅使得钢丝承受异常高负荷，导致最终疲劳失效。  
The failed door cable hook end fitting clearly shows failure through reverse bending fatigue of the wires (figures 1 & 2). There are also 
heavy scratches on the surface of the fitting, suggesting that it was rubbing against the interior diameter of the handle, and therefore was 
not rotating properly. This caused an abnormal high load on the wire, causing fatigue failure.
