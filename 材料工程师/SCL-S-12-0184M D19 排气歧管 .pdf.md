Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）粘着导致排气歧管表面氧化层的剥离。The adhesion led to removal of the oxidation layer from the surface of the 
manifold. 
（2）硬度和组织符合技术要求，但是石墨球有聚集的倾向。The hardness and microstructure meet the requirement, 
but graphite spheres tend to join together.   
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）控制好铸造工艺过程，确保获得好的石墨形态。Control the casting process well and ensure to get good 
graphite shape. 
（2）请提供所有的失效件用于分析。Please supply all parts of failure for analysis
