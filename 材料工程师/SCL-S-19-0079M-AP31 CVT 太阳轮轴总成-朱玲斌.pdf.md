Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 离合器毂存在冲压裂纹，以裂纹为中心，分别向两侧疲劳扩展直至断裂失效。There is a stamping crack in the 
clutch hub, which is centered on the crack and propagates to both sides of the clutch until the fracture failure. 
（2） 离合器毂材料成分合格，硬度正常。The composition of the clutch hub is qualified, the hardness is normal.   
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 对比开发阶段通过试验的零件，排查冲压裂纹形成的原因。Comparing the tested parts in the development 
stage to find our the causes of forming stamping cracks. 
（2） 评估现有零件的风险。Risk assessment of existing parts.
