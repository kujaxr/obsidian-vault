Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于弹簧次表面的大颗粒非金属夹杂物引起的疲劳断裂。 
Failure for the part belongs to fatigue fracture, due to the large non-metallic inclusions in second surface. 
2、 零件的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The material properties of parts meet the technical requirements. 
3、该气门弹簧在2013 年8 月份发生过相同原因的断裂失效，参见中心实验室的报告SCL-S-13-0243M。 
The valve spring occurred in August 2013 over the same reason the fracture failure, see the central laboratory 
reports SCL-S-13-0243M. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强原材料质量控制。To strengthen the quality control for raw materials. 
2、 请校核该气门弹簧实际工况中的受力情况。Please check the forces of the valve spring during the actual 
condition .
