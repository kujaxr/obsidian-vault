Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 下系杆支架由于受到较大的冲击载荷而发生过载断裂。 
Bracket of lower tie bar was broken off because of the big impact load. 
2. 零件成分和硬度符合要求，组织及孔隙率未见异常。 
Chemical components and hardness met the requirement. Microstructure and porosity were normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 校核零件的实际工作载荷。 
Check the active load of the parts. 
2. 评估发动机或变速箱抬升对下系杆支架的影响。 
Evaluate the influence on the bracket of lower tie bar when the engine or transmission is uplift.
