Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  扭簧属于正常的疲劳失效。 
Spring belongs to normally fatigue failure. 
2.  扭簧线材成分、硬度及组织正常；扭簧中径、间距正常。 
Chemical components、hardness and microstructure are normal. Middle diameter、spacing are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化扭簧设计如改变中径、圈数，进一步提高疲劳寿命。 
    Optimise the spring design such as meddle diameter and circle nurmber to improve the fatigue life.
