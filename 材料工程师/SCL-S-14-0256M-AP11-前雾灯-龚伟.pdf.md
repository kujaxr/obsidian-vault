Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件反光镜烧蚀熔化。  
2. 零件材料不符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1. Reflector of front fog lamp ablated. 
2. Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements.     
建议： 1. 检查高温起火原因，调查失效件来源。 
           2．供应商就材料问题进行分析整改。 
Suggestions:  1. Check the reason of high temp, find the source of failure part. 
           2. Analysis the material problem with suppliers.
