1 
Scope 
This standard applies to threads without special requirements or is used if the medium tolerance 
class cannot be complied with.  
 
The nominal and limit dimensions in Tables 1 to 4 and Figure 1 apply to bright threads in tolerance 
classes 8f (external thread) and 7G (internal thread) before electrolytic surface treatment. 
 
Only tolerance class “medium” according to VW 116 10 is generally released. The coarse tolerance 
class has a smaller pitch depth of engagement and requires special gages. It is therefore permissi-
ble only in exceptional cases and must be specified on the drawing. 
 
Refer to VW 116 15 for limit dimensions after application of the surface protection.
