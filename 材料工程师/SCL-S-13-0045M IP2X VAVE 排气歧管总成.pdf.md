Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）排气歧管总成焊缝开裂是由于疲劳所致。其原因是由于振动载荷超过了该结构的承受能力。The weld of the 
exhaust manifold is cracked by fatigue which is because of the vibration load over the bearing capacity of the structure. 
（2）标准SMTC 5 111 002 中无对应的焊接结构要求，因此不能对该焊接质量进行准确地评价。The standard SMTC 
5 111 002 doesn’t have the weld requirement for the weld structure, so the weld quality is unable to be evaluated exactly. 
（3）材料的成分和厚度符合要求；硬度和组织正常。The chemical compositions and thickness meet the requirement ; 
the hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化结构，提高其承载能力或降低振动载荷。Optimize the structure and increase the bearing capacity or reduce 
the load because of vibration.
