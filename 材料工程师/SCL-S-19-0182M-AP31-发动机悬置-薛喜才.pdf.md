Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  铸件内存在热态裂纹，在受到冲击后发生过载断裂。Hot cracks are observed in castings and fractured though 
overload after impact.  
2.  材料成分、硬度满足要求。Chemical components、hardness meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查铸件生产过程中热态裂纹产生的原因并采取有效控制措施。Investigate the reason of hot cracks in the 
casting process and take effective control measures.  
2.  评估已装车零件的风险。Evaluate the risk of load parts.
