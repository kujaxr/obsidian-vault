Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 支架是通过应力腐蚀断裂失效的。The bracket has failed through stress-corrosion cracking. 
2. 低碳钢对氢氧化物和硝酸盐化学品敏感。Mild steel is susceptible to attack from Hydroxide and Nitrate chemicals. 
3. 在支架表面发现有可能来自于加工过程的化学品残留物。A residue potentially resulting from processing 
chemicals was found on the bracket surface. 
4. 材料符合硬度和金相组织要求。The material meets the requirements with regard to hardness and microstructure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 找外来残留物的本源（检查制造过程，装配过程）。Find source of foreign material (check manufacturing process, 
assembly processes). 
2. 检查其它车辆的支架。Check other vehicles’ bracket.

EDX 分析 
对裂纹＃2 附近的黄色粉末进行了EDX 分析（图8）。从检测的元素，锌，碳，铁，磷，可以被解释为正常
的支架和涂层材料。氧，硫和硅经常在路上的灰尘被发现。但是金属的铬，钠，钙不容易出现。有一可能
性：该元素来自于工业加工化学品铬酸钙和氢氧化钠的残留，但也应该考虑车辆生命历程中使用的产品。 
The yellow powder near to fracture #2 was analysed by EDX (figure 8).  From the elements detected, Zinc, 
Carbon, Iron and Phosphorous may be explained by the normal bracket and coating material.  Oxygen, Sulphur 
and Silicon are often found in road dust.  The metals Chromium, Sodium and Calcium cannot easily be accounted 
for.  One possibility is residue from industrial processing chemicals Calcium Chromate & Sodium Hydroxide, 
although products used in the life history of the vehicle should also be considered.
