Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 顶棚黄斑为异物沾染所致，异物为聚氨酯胶。 
The macula is contaminated by unknown object, which is polyurethane glue. 
 
建议Suggestions：1.排查沾染的聚氨酯胶来源。 
                   Investigate the source of contaminated polyurethane glue.
