Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
(1)  由于缺乏夹紧力以及球铰轴和装配孔的变形，球铰轴因过载而出现断裂失效。The ball joint has failed through overload, 
due to a lack of clamping load and heavy deformation of the taper and bore. 
(2)  早期变形发生之后，车子在行驶中，应该会产生振动和噪音。这种变形可能是由于车轮转向受到阻碍所致；
当然，也不能排除螺母和装配的问题（无法获得装配的螺母）。The deformation had occurred much earlier, and should have 
produced vibrations and noise while driving. It’s possible that the driver had caused damage to the steering by turning the wheel into a kerb, 
although an nut or assembly fault cannot be ruled out (the nut was not recovered). 
(3)  球铰轴材料的合金成分不满足要求，对产品的失效有一定的影响，但是这不是导致产品失效的直接原因； 硬
度和组织正常。The chemical composition of the ball joint doesn’t meet the requirement, which didn’t cause the part failure directly, 
although might have contributed. The hardness and microstructure are OK  
(4)  转向节的石墨球化率不满足产品要求，但是对失效没有影响。 The graphite spherodization of the knuckle was slightly 
below requirement (no influence on failure). 
建议Suggestions： 
(1) 让供应商更改材料，并提供风险评估报告。Supplier to correct the material immediately and provide risk analysis report. 
(2) 该车开了三万多公里，没有保养记录。推荐用户到上汽指定的服务中心做维修和保养。The car had 
driven >30000km without maintenance check record. Recommend to customer to do maintenance check at SAIC Service Center.

II. 测试结果与分析 Test Results and Analysis 
（1） 失效产品 Failed components 
球铰轴从螺纹段根部断裂，并发生了弯曲；转向节装配处，出现了明显的塑性变形和开裂。这一切均表明
拉杆和转向节间产生了较大的载荷。腐蚀和磨损的痕迹显示变形在断裂之前已发生了很长时间。The ball joint is broken 
at the end of the screw and severely deformed. The hole of the steering knuckle is distorted and cracked.  Such damage can result from heavy 
force between the steering knuckle and ball joint. The corrosion and wear shows that it happened a long time before the final failure.
