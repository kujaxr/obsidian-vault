Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 有外力过载引起的断裂。The failed due to overloading. 
2. 抗拉强度和硬度图纸无要求；但失效件实测硬度值相当于《GBT 8738-2006 铸造用锌合金锭》中规定的该牌号的原材料的硬度
要求。The tensile strength and hardness were not required in the drawing. However hardness result corresponds to 
requirement of GBT 8738-2006 (Zinc alloy ingots for casting). 
3. 
建议Suggestions： 
1.进一步分析使用情况。Please check working condition. 
2.考虑提高设计强度（材料牌号、尺寸等）。 Consider improving the design strength (material type, dimensions etc).  
3.在图纸中加入操纵杆成品硬度的技术要求 Add finished part hardness requirement to drawing.
