Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）后制动卡钳是由于螺栓夹紧力不足而发生松脱的。Rear brake caliper was loose because of insufficient clamping force 
of the bolt. 
（2）车轮支架，卡钳的平面度和粗糙度符合要求；螺栓的螺纹尺寸符合要求。The flatness and roughness of the wheel 
bracket and caliper meet the requirements; the dimension of the bolt is ok. 
（3）车轮支架和卡钳的硬度符合要求；螺栓的材料符合要求。The hardness of the wheel bracket and caliper meet the 
requirements; the material of the bolt is ok. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）对所需的夹紧力和装配扭矩进行评估。Check if the clamping force and torque meet the requirements of the different 
conditions.   
（2）增加涂胶、减小螺牙间距、采用带齿的防松法兰面或增大装配扭矩。Coat antiloosing glue, reduce the screw pitch, let 
the flange with tooth for antiloosing, or increasing the assembling tourque.
