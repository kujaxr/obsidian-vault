Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 零件受交变载荷作用产生的多源疲劳导致排气管支架断裂。 The bracket has failed due to fatigue, 
beginning from multiple origins.   
2. 鉴于支架承受动态，热和振动载荷，相比其他可用材料,409L 的强度等级相对较低。 The material grade 
409L is a relatively low strength grade, compared to other available materials and considering the application is 
to withstand dynamic, thermal and vibration loading. 
3. 材料的成分及硬度满足技术要求；组织正常。 断口无明显缺陷。The bracket chemical composition & 
hardness met the requirements. & no significant defects were found. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 工程部门分析材料的强度是否合适。Engineering department to analyse whether the material strength is 
suitable. 
2. 考虑如何可以避免共振，减少动态载荷。Consider how to avoid resonance, and how vibration and dynamic 
loading on the bracket could be reduced.
