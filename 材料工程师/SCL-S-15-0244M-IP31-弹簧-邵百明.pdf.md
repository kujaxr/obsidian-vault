Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 发动机罩盖主锁总成的棘爪弹簧是由于疲劳而断裂；裂纹起源于应力较大的区域，其表面有轻微的损伤。
The return spring of the latch ASM-Hood for the pawl was broken by fatigue and the crack originated in the 
region with high stress and the surface is with little damage. 
（2） 弹簧材料成分，硬度，组织和线径无异常。The material is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核载荷。Check the load of the spring.   
（2） 弹簧成型中避免应力集中部位出现损伤。During the production of the spring, avoid the damage in the local.
