1 范围 
本标准规定了夹子插入及拔出面板所需要
的力，或将其从缝隙密封材料中拔出所需要的力
的测试方法。 
本标准适用于夹子插入及拔出力的测试。 
 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the procedure for 
the determining the force required to insert or 
pull out of a panel, and to pull an aperture seal 
from the retaining clip. 
This standard applies to determine the 
force required to insert or pull out of a panel。

2  Apparatus

2.1  Suitable autographic tensometer.

板上的洞要与安装于车体上的一致）。

2.3
Suitable jig for holding panel in a hori-
zontal position and attaching it to the lower 
platen of the tensometer crosshead. 
2.4 一个直径比夹子大的平底压头，将这个连
接到张力计的滑架上面。 
2.4
Flat bottomed indentor with a diameter 
larger than that of the clip, to be attached to the 
tensometer crosshead.

2.5
Metal wire with end loops.
2.6 合适的能够将密封材料从夹子中拔出的夹
具。 
2.6  Suitable jig for pulling the seal off the clip.

4.1.1
Place the painted panel into the panel

4.1.2 将底部平板架到张力计的滑架上面。 
4.1.2
Attach flat bottomed indentor to ten-
someter crosshead. 
4.1.3 直接将夹子排列在压头的下面。 
4.1.3  Align the clip directly under the inden-
tor. 
4.1.4 以20 mm/min 的速度下降压头，直到彻
底将夹子压进洞里面。 
4.1.4  Lower indentor at 20 mm/min until clip 
is completely inserted into the hole. 
4.1.5 记录下最大的压力。 
4.1.5
Record the maximum insertion force.

4.1.6  Repeat clauses 4.1.1 to 4.1.5 on the 
further two samples.

4.3 密封材料中夹子保持力 
4.3 Clip retention force in seal section
4.3.1 将夹子装进密封材料并且将其推进面板
中。 
4.3.1  Fit the clip to the seal section and push 
clip into the panel. 
4.3.2 将面板装进夹具中。 
4.3.2  Place the panel into the panel retaining 
jig. 
4.3.3 如果夹具不会对密封部分造成重大破坏
的话，用拉伸夹具直接夹住密封材料，或者可以
用一个圆条穿过密封部分，用皮带将圆条系到夹
具上面。 
4.3.3  Attach the pulling jig to the seal either 
by clamping onto the seal section if it does not 
significantly distort the section or by passing a 
round bar through the section and attaching to 
the jig with a strap. 
4.3.4 将夹具装到张力计的滑架上面。 
4.3.4  Attach the jig to the tensometer cross-
head. 
4.3.5 以50 mm/min 的速度上升滑架直到彻底
将夹子拔出。 
4.3.5  Raise the crosshead at 50 mm/min until 
the clip is pulled fully out. 
4.3.6 记录最大的拔出力。 
4.3.6  Record the maximum pull out force. 
4.3.7 用剩下的两个样条根据条款4.3.1 至
条款4.3.6 中的操作重复进行试验。 
4.3.7  Repeat clauses 4.3.1 to 4.3.6 on a fur-
ther two samples. 
5 报告 
5 
Report 
报告应包含以下内容： 
a) 夹子的平均插入力； 
b) 从盘子中平均的拔出力； 
c) 从密封材料中平均拔出力。 
The contents should be included in the
reports as below:    
a) The mean insertion force for the clip; 
b) The mean pull out force from the 
plate; 
c) The mean pull out force from the seal.
