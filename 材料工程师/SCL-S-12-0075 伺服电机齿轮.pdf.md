Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1）经测试，零件材料与原宝理粒料焓值有较大偏差。 
       2）检查失效件，齿轮的剖面有很多小孔洞，注塑工艺不佳。 
Conclusions：After test，there is large deviation of  the enthalpy between the failure and the Polyplastics pellet. There are 
a lot of holes on the fracture surface, poor injection molding process, resulting in part failure. 
建议：责令供应商解释材料及生产工艺。 
Suggestions：Ordered to suppliers to explain the materials and production processes
