Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 螺钉安装孔位置熔融不好，存在熔接痕，导致强度降低，装配时开裂。 
The melt of the screw hole is not good, with weld mark, lead to lower strength, and cracked during 
assembly. 
              2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商改进工艺以改善此处熔接情况。 
                   Supplier should improve the craftwork to improve the weld problem. 
                2. 选用较小扭矩安装螺钉。 
                   Select smaller torque to mount screws.

件的强度，对开裂问题有直接的帮助，供应商需改进工艺以解决熔接痕问题，如增加溢料井等。
