Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 双质量飞轮是由于弹簧在大的载荷下出现疲劳断裂而失效的。The Dual Mass Flywheel was failed due to 
fatigue fractures of the springs caused by big load. 
（2）垫片的严重磨损不是导致弹簧断裂的原因。The serious wear of the washer is not the reason for springs’ break.  
（3） 弹簧的成分，组织，夹杂物以及硬度符合相应的技术要求。The chemical components, microstructure, inclusion 
and hardness meet the requirement. 
（4）垫片的材料正常，为PA46+PTEF。The material of the friction washer is PA46, which is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）采集路试中的数据，分析并校核弹簧承受的载荷。Analyze and check the load of the springs by collecting data 
during road test. 
（2）基于采集的数据，对比国内路试和英国路试过程中的差异。Basing on the collection data, compare the 
difference of the experiment process in UK and China. 
（3）拆解分析在英国完成相同路试的车子。Disassemble and analyze the flywheel which passed the test in UK.

断口分析 Fracture Analysis 
对所有大弹簧和小弹簧的断口（除个别已严重损伤的断口之外）进行检查，可以发现如下特点： 
 
弹簧为疲劳断裂，裂纹源起源于弹簧的内表面（图6,7,8 显示的是代表性断口）。 
 
除图6（b）所示断口外，所有起源区的内表面均有磨损。相对其它内表面，通常磨损更严重。在正常工作
中，大弹簧和小弹簧间会出现一些正常的接触和磨损；当弹簧断裂后，这些损伤很可能会加剧（图9 显示的
一些起源区的表面情况）。 
 
弹簧的表面还存在一些疲劳裂纹（见图10，11 和12）。图13 是从图6（b）所示断口处的取样获得的，表面
无磨损，但有很多细小的疲劳裂纹，表明其承受较大的载荷。 
 
未发现锈蚀诱发疲劳裂纹。 
Check all the fractures of the large springs and small springs, except several seriously damaged fractures. They show the 
same characters as following: 
 
The springs were broken through fatigue and the fatigue fractures had originated on the inner surface. (figures 6 to 
8are examples) 
 
All of the inner surfaces at the fractures were worn except one fracture （see fig. 6 (b)）; the wear was usually more 
seriously than other inner surface. In the normal work, the large springs and small springs can contact and wear 
together. After break, the injury is likely to intensify.( fig. 9 is examples) 
 
There are some fatigue crack on the surface of the springs（see fig. 10 ~12）. Fig. 12 is got by cutting from the 
sample showed by fig. 6 (b). There is no wear on the surface, but there are several fatigue cracks，which shows it 
bore a big load. 
 
No cracks were led by corrosion.

材料检测 Material Test  
1. 弹簧 spring 
弹簧基体组织为正常的屈氏体。表面组织和芯部组织相同，未见脱碳。 
局部有一些金属流变并带有一些裂纹。这是来自摩擦或喷丸。这些裂纹是沿着圆周面，且深度很浅。在新件的检
测中，也发现类似的喷丸引起的裂纹，且主要出现在弹簧的外表面。对于喷丸工艺来说，这是无法避免的；此裂
纹不会对疲劳裂纹的萌生产生显著的影响。 
第一组大弹簧具有一定量非常细小的C 类夹杂物。根据标准GB/T 10561，夹杂物评级中宽度小于＜2μm 的C 类
夹杂物不计算在内，因此第一组大弹簧的夹杂物级别为C0.5；第二组大弹簧和小弹簧的夹杂物级别为D0.5。它
们均符合技术要求。 
弹簧的硬度和成分也均符合LUK 的材料要求。 
The microstructure is normal Troostite. The ones on the surface and the core are same; no decarburization. 
The metal on the local surface was deformed with some cracks, which are caused by wear or shot peening. The direction of 
the crack is along the surface and the depth is very small. The same cracks caused by shot peening are also found in the 
surface of the new part and mainly on the outside surface of the spring. This can’t be avoided for the shot peening. This 
kind of the crack don’t have remarkable effect on the fatigue crack germination. 
The first large spring has a certain quantity of very fine inclusions---type C. According GB/T 10561, if the width is less than 
2μm, the inclusions are not took as inclusion for the evaluation, so the inclusions level of the first large spring is C0.5; the one 
of the second large and small springs is D0.5. They meet the requirement. 
The hardness and chemical components of the spring meet the requirement of LUK.
