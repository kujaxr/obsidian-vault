3.1  零件外观要求 
零件表面无气泡、裂纹、划痕、毛刺及其他
影响功能的缺陷。 
 
3.1  Requirements  
No bubble, crack scratch, burr and other  
defects affecting the function of the part 
surface. 
3.2  结构要求 
结构要求按照图纸。 
 
3.2  Design 
Design According to drawing.

试样推荐使用ISO 37 3 型和4 型样件。只有在
成品尺寸无法满足取样要求的情况下才使用标

The standard specimen is cut from the finished 
part. The type 3 and type 4 specimens of ISO 
37 is recommended for the tensile test. The 
test sheet of 2mm thick is only used when the 
dimension of the part can not meet the 
specimen preparation.

50 % RH 的标准气候下至少放置24 h。 
 
3.11  Condition 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50 %RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 24 h before test.

4、试验要求Notes on testing 
4.1 硬度测试 
硬度要求在图纸上或双方协议规定，采用
Shore A硬度，如无特殊要求，Shore A硬度测试
时间为5s。 
 
4.1  Hardness Test 
The hardness is specified on the drawing or in 
the agreement of the both side. The test period 
of shore A is 5s if there is no special 
requirement. 
4.2 热空气老化 
测试样条存放在符合 ISO 188 要求的鼓风
式烘箱内，老化后取出样条 30 min 后进行测 
试。 
 
4.2  Aging at elevated temperature 
Test specimens are aged in a mechanical 
circulation oven acc to ISO188. Measurements 
are performed 30 minutes after removal.  
4.3 压缩永久变形 
   压缩永久变形参考SMTC 5 400 239 方法A。
当样品硬度≤80 Shore A 时，压缩量为50% 
当样品硬度＞80 Shore A 时，压缩量为25% 
 
4.3  Compression Set 
The test of compression set should refer to the 
SMTC 5 400 239 method A.  
Hardness ≤80 Shore A: 50% deformation  
Hardness ＞80 Shore A: 25% deformation 
 
4.4  低温性能 
零件在-40℃下存放24h 后进行弯曲试验。试
验直接在存放环境中进行，不允许用手直接接触
样品进行试验。 
 
4.4  Behavior in cold air 
The specimen is placed in -40℃ for 24h and 
conduct the bench test immediately. The 
bench test is conducted in the -40℃ too and 
the hand should not touch the specimen 
directly during the test. 
4.5 臭氧老化 
试样为150 mm 长的成品，将成品在固定架
上固定并夹紧。在试样中心段作100 mm 的标距。
拉伸试样，使标距具有 20 % 的伸长，测试前在
室温下调节46 h。臭氧老化条件为200 pphm 、 
40 ℃保持72 h。 
 
4.5  Ozone resistance 
150-mm long finished part sections are 
fastened in the clamping device on the test 
frame and stretched until a 20% elongation is 
reached between the markings, which are 
spaced 100 mm apart. Prior to aging in the 
ozone climate chamber, the test frame, with 
the finished part sections clamped in, is 
acclimatised at room temperature for 46 h. The 
test condition is 200pphm 、 40 ℃ keep 72 h. 
 
4.6 铜腐蚀性能 
试样尺寸为20×10mm，铜片尺寸确保覆盖样
品尺寸。将试样置于铜片中央，覆盖玻璃片，并
在玻璃片上施加450g±10g 的重量，确保试样紧
贴于铜片并放入100℃的鼓风烘箱中，保持24h。 
 
4.6  Copper corrosion test 
The sample size is 20×10mm，and sure the 
copper size can embrace the sample. Put 
sample in the middle of the copper and 
covered with the glass. The load on the glass 
is 450g±10g to ensure the sample touch the 
copper close. Then put assembly into the 
machanical air circulate oven for 24h at 100℃. 
4.7 水存放后的变白测试 
试样为150 mm 长的成品，将试样长度的一
半浸泡在去离子水中，存放在90℃环境中22h
后取出样件进行评价。 
 
4.7  White discoloration after aging in 
water 
The sample is a 150 mm long finished product. 
Half of the sample length is immersed in 
deionized water and stored at 90 ℃ for 22 h. 
then the sample is taken out for evaluation.
