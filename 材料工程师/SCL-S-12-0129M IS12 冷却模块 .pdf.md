Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）侧板疲劳断裂后，导致冷却管开裂和泄露。The frame was broken because of fatigue, which led to the cooling 
tube fracture and leaking. 
（2）侧板断裂是由于两种因素（漏焊和差的材料）综合作用的结果。The frame failure is because of the integrated 
effect of two factors: no welding and bad material. 
（3）侧板所用材料为1 系列的变形铝，不是要求的3003+0.5%Cu，性能较低。The material of the frame is 1 series 
wrought aluminum, not the required 3003+0.5%Cu and so the strength is lower. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）追溯出现混料的产品。Track the products with wrong material 
（2）让供应商查找漏焊的原因，并进行改善。Supplier must find the reason for the missing welding and rectify.
