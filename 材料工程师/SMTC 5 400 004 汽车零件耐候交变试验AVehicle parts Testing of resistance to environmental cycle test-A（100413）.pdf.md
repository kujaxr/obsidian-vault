1 范围 
本标准为试验规范，适用于对汽车试验单
元，例如发动机舱零部件，进行循环气候交变试
验（高低温循环）。 
 
通过温度和湿度的循环交变，可以评估零部
件在此环境中的表现（例如，裂纹敏感性、变形、
合成材料的分离等）。 
 
本标准的目的（例如－40℃）是在短期时
间内加速强化效应，发现零部件的缺陷，而不是
在定义连续操作下对零部件的一般要求。 
1  Scope  
This 
Test 
Specification 
describes 
an 
environmental 
cycle 
test 
(elevated 
temperature/low temperature cycle) for testing 
units, e.g. vehicle parts in the engine 
compartment.  
The behavior of the units and/or parts during 
environmental cycle stressing by means of 
cycling temperature and moisture shall be 
assessed here (e.g. susceptibility to cracks, 
deformation, separation of the composite 
material, etc.).  
The purpose of the test specifications (e.g. 
temperature -40℃) is to uncover component 
weaknesses 
in 
a 
short-term 
test 
with 
accelerated time effect, not to define general 
component 
requirements 
for 
continuous 
operation.

4 试验方法 
4  Test method 
4.1 试验程序 
温度公差应控制在±2℃以内，相对空气湿
度公差（以下简称相对湿度）应控制在±5%以
内。 
 
在试验样品放入气候室之前，气候室应保持
在室温（23℃），相对湿度30％。 
试验样品放入气候室1小时后开始试验。 
保持阶段必须被保留。加热和冷却阶段根据
气候室的实际能力允许改变，但改变要在试验报
告中标出。 
4.1 Procedure 
The temperature shall be regulated with a 
tolerance of ± 2 ℃ and the relative air 
humidity (rel. humidity in the following) with a 
tolerance of ±5%.  
The climatic chamber shall be set to room 
temperature (23 ℃) and 30% rel. humidity 
before the test specimen is inserted.  
The samples shall be kept in the climatic 
chamber for 1 hour before the test. 
The holding times must always be maintained. 
The heating and cooling phases can be varied 
according to the performance capability of the 
climatic chambers used. Deviations shall be 
specified in the test report.
