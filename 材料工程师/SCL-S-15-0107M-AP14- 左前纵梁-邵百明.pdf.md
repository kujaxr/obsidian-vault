Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 左前纵梁由于局部承受较大的应力而发生双向多源疲劳开裂。The left front longitudinal beam was cracked 
by bending multi-source fatigue of because of the big local stress.  
（2） 材料的成分和板厚符合要求；材料的换算强度，组织和焊缝无异常。The chemical components and thickness 
meet the requirements; the conversion strength, microstructure and weld are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核载荷；若应力较大，优化设计，降低局部的应力。Check the load. If the stress is big, reduce the stress by 
improving the design.
