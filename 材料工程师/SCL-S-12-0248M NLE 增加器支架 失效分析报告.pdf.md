Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 支架通过疲劳失效，起始于一个正常的边缘磨痕。可能是与之连接的另一零件（磨损）导致的失效。The bracket 
has failed through fatigue, initiated at a normal grinding mark on the edge.  Contact with another part (resulting in 
abrasion) may have contributed to this fracture. 
2. 第二个的疲劳裂纹，位于增加器的安装点、支架和加强板之间的焊接熔核区的边缘处。焊缝熔核的位置是不
符合设计的要求。 A second fatigue crack, at the turbo mounting point, was initiated between the bracket and 
reinforcement plate, at the edge of the weld fusion zone.  The weld fusion position was not in accordance with the 
design intent. 
3. 该材料的化学成分和硬度是可以接受的。The material chemical composition and hardness were acceptable. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 进行负载分析，以确定过载的源。Carry out load analysis to determine the source of the overload. 
2. 考虑改变焊缝设计，以消除应力集中。焊接在受弯曲载荷处之外，或改成为角焊焊缝。Consider changing the 
weld design to remove the stress concentration.  Either weld outside of the bending-load area, or change to fillet 
welding.  
3. 调整造成磨损的冲突条件。Rectify the clash condition which caused the abrasion.

主裂缝已开始于支架边缘并通过疲劳扩展至整个支架（图2a）。在裂纹源处（裂纹源的进一步分析见图3）未发

The main fracture had initiated at the sample edge and progressed through the whole bracket by fatigue (figure 2a) .  No 
defects or damage was found at the crack origin (further analysis of the origin is shown in figure 3).  Typical fatigue 
characteristics such as beachmarks and lack of deformation were observed at the fracture face (fgure 2d).  The final 
fracture area was damaged, but the area was certainly less than 0.5mm2.  Given a material tensile strength of ~628 MPa 
(Nmm-2) this shows that a load of less than 314 N caused the final failure.  Note: The crack-initiating load might be 
different from the final load, due to the structural changes resulting from the fracture.

焊接注意事项Welding Considerations 
 
设计要求进行储能焊接，预期的焊接熔合区如图10 所示。然而，在焊接过程中已形成的融合区在孔的附近，大
体上如半圆形图案所示。这将导致在一个小区域的边缘处的融核区是具有的最高的应力。如果熔核区依据设计的
要求，应力将将分布于一条线而不是一个点（假设施加的载荷是垂直于焊接缝）。 
The design calls for persussive welding, with the intended weld fusion zone as shown in figure 10.  However during the 
weld process, the fusion zone has formed nearer to the hole, in a roughly semi-circular pattern.  This causes the highest 
stress to be bourne at one small area at the edge of the fusion zone.  Should the fusion zone lie according to the design, 
the stress would be bourne over a line rather than a point (assuming applied load is perpendicular to the weld line). 
 
 
另外一个考虑是焊接的类型。储能焊核与两个连接板的边缘存在有距离，这使得焊核与两板材之间的区域形成了
一个天然的裂纹形态，这会造成应力集中（图11a）。相比角焊缝会形成平稳过渡，应力集中将不存在（图11b）
的。 An additional consideration is the type of weld.  The percussive weld nugget lies some distance into the joint, 
forming a natural crack morphology, which would create a stress concentration. (figure 11a).  In contrast, a fillet weld 
would form a a smooth transition, and the stress concentration would not exist (figure 11b).
