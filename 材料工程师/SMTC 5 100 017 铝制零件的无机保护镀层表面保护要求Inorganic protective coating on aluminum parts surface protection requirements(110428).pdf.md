1 范围 
本标准规定了纯铝和纯铝合金制成零件上阳
极氧化层和无机覆盖镀层的要求。 
本标准适用于要求纯铝和纯铝合金制成零件
上阳极氧化层和无机覆盖镀层。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies requirements for 
anodic oxide coatings and inorganic top coating 
on parts made of pure aluminum and pure alu-
minum alloys. 
This standard is adopted to raise  re-
quirements for anodic oxide coatings and inor-
ganic top coating on parts made of pure alumi-
num and pure aluminum alloys.

Requirements 
If the following requirements for specific 
components or parts of components do not ap-

3.1 表面保护种类（装饰性） 
3.1  Surface protection type（decorative） 
3.1.1  适用Sp-b140 表面保护方式。 
3.1.1
Surface protection type Sp-b140 applies.
3.1.2  该表面保护是一种包含无机非金属镀层
的阳极氧化层（铝阳极氧化处理及部分密封处
理）。对于具有装饰功能的零件，其需要提高耐
候性或者耐蚀性时，使用这种表面保护。 
3.1.2  Surface protection is an anodic oxide 
coating（anodizing and partial sealing）with an 
additionally applied inorganic non-metallic. 
This surface protection is used for decorative 
add-on parts with increased requirements for 
weather resistance or chemical resistance. 
3.1.3  包含无机非金属镀层的附加镀层，必须在
系统认可范围内，与买方的材料工程技术部门协
商一致。 
3.1.3
The additional coating with an inorganic 
non-metallic coating must be released in coor-
dination with the purchaser’s Materials Depart-
ment as part of a system release. 
3.2 基本要求 
按照 SMTC 90008 避免有害物。 
3.2  General requirements 
Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 90008. 
3.1.1  进行一次完整的检测需要10个成品件。 
3.1.1   A total of 10 finished parts are required 
for complete testing. 
3.1.2 镀层应覆盖整个零件表面，使其达到特定
的表面保护性能。镀层不应有孔隙、裂缝、含有
异物、损坏以及其他破坏防腐蚀保护功能和/或指
定外观的缺陷。镀层应牢固地粘附在基底材料上。
3.1.2   The Coating shall exhibit the specified 
surface protection, with the specified properties, 
over its entire surface. The coatings shall be 
free of pores, cracks, foreign matter inclusions, 
damage and other flaws that impair the corro-
sion protection and/or specified appearance. 
The layers shall have firm adherence to the 
base material. 
3.1.3  必须设计和控制生产流程，以使制成零件
的功能特性不受损害。 
3.1.3  The production process must be de-
signed and controlled such that functional cha-
racteristics of the finished part are not impaired. 
3.1.4  在正确的安装程序下，镀层不应受到会导
致其功能损坏和/或降低规定的耐蚀性的损害。 
3.1.4   Given proper mounting, the coating 
shall not be damaged if this would lead to im-
pairment of function and/or decrease of the 
specified corrosion protection. 
3.3 外观 
3.3  Appearance 
3.3.1  要在裸视和自然光下进行外观评估。 
3.3.1  The specimens are evaluated with the 
naked eye in natural light.

3.3.3  带有附加保护涂层的构件，不允许有肥

3.3.3  Components with additional protective 
coating must not exhibit any flaws, such as edge

3.3.4  成品不得有抛光缺陷、擦伤、损坏或者影

3.3.4  Part finish must be free of polishing 
flaws, scratches, damage, or similar flaws that 
impair the appearance of the part. The surface 
of the component must not exhibit any loss of 
glossiness, cloudiness, or shadowy areas. 
3.4 镀层厚度 
按照DIN EN ISO 1463确定镀层厚度。阳极氧
化层的镀层厚度必须为（5~10）μm，而附加有机
涂层的厚度必须为（0.5~5）μm.。 
3.4  Coating thickness 
The coating thickness is defined according 
to DIN EN ISO 1463.The coating thickness of 
the anodic oxide coating must be 5~10μm and 
the coating thickness of the organic additional 
coating must be 0.5~5μm..

3.5 Resistance to acids, heat, and alkali
（SMA- Corrosion） 
3.5.1  依照列出的顺序，在同一构件上依次进行
以下测试。 
3.5.1
The following tests are performed con-
secutively on the same component in the order 
specified. 
3.5.1.1  顺序（在23~35 ℃温度范围内进行测
试）： 
a) 在PH=1的溶液中浸渍10 min； 
b) 在H20（水）中洗涤和干燥； 
c) 在40 ℃时热储藏1 h（不需室温冷却直
接继续试验）； 
d) 在PH=13.5的溶液中浸渍10 min； 
e) 在H20（水）中洗涤和干燥。 
3.5.1.1  Sequence （the tests must be per-
formed within a temperature range of 23~35℃）
a)  10 min immersion in pH 1 solution; 
b)  Rinse in H2O and dry; 
c)  Age at elevated temperature at 40
