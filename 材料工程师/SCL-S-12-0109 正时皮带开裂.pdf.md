Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：  皮带开裂为瞬间承受过大外力导致。 
Conclusion: The timing belt was broken because of sudden over-loaded. 
            
建议：  确定动力系统是否存在瞬间过载现象导致皮带开裂。 
Suggestion: .Assure the engine system free of the sudden over-load.
