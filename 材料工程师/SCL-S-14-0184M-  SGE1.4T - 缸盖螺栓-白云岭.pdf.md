Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 三件失效件均为过载断裂，可能与装配工艺和二次装配有关。 
Three failure is overload fracture, may be related to assembly process. 
2、 螺栓的材料性能符合技术要求，垫片材料无异常。The Material properties of the bolt met the technical 
requirements. The Material properties of the gasket were nomal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、请检查装配过程质量。 
Please check the assembly process quality.   
  2、校核装配工艺的适用性。 
To check the applicability of assembly technique.
