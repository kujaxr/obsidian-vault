Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）前制动盘是受到异常外力导致的过载断裂。 
The fracture of front brake disc was overload fracture due to abnormal load. 
（2）材料硬度符合要求，金相正常。 
The hardness of the material met the requirements and the metallography was normal.
