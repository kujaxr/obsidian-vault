1.  异常燃烧导致四缸活塞发生断裂失效，异常燃烧的原因与油品质量有关。

related to oil quality.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
ZS11 TT/NSE major1.5 四缸活塞环岸开裂，车辆行驶里程285 公里，从现场拆解照片来看(见图1)： 
 
气门、火花塞、活塞顶部及活塞环、裙部均无异常，仅四缸环岸断裂。 
 
发动机燃烧室、气门盘部、活塞顶部皆呈现黑色，而车辆刚刚行驶285 公里，燃烧室内颜色不正常。根
据以往分析的经验来看，此异常颜色可能与燃烧不正常有关。 
 
ZS11 TT / NSE major1.5, four pistons (one engine) happen cracking and the vehicle mileage is 285km, shown 
in figure 1. According to the disassembly photos:  
 
The valve, spark plug, piston top, piston ring and skirt are all normal, only the four cylinder ring shores are broken.  
 
The combustion chamber, valve disc part and piston top of the engine are all black, while the vehicle has just driven 
285km, and the color of the combustion chamber is abnormal. According to the experience of previous analysis, this 
abnormal color may be related to abnormal combustion.

结论： 
Conclusion: 
排除活塞材料以及其他零件的影响因素，活塞环岸断裂通过与异常燃烧产生的冲击有关；从活塞、气门
及火花塞颜色来看，发动机异常燃烧与油品质量有较大的关联。 
   Exclude the influencing factors of piston materials and other parts and the fracture of piston ring bank is related 
to the impact caused by abnormal combustion. According to the color of piston, valve and spark plug, the abnormal 
combustion of engine is closely related to the quality of oil.
