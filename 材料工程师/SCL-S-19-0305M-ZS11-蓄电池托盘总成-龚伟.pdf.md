Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件模压工艺不佳，熔接痕位置强度较低，导致零件开裂。 
Parts were cracked because of poor molding process, weak strength of weld line. 
 2．零件材料不符合技术要求，具体原因分析中。 
   Material performance of part didn’t conform to requirements, and specific cause was analyzing. 
建议Suggestions：1．优化模压工艺。 
                   Optimize molding process. 
2. 验证失效螺栓孔安装扭矩是否合适。 
                   Verify proper installation torque for failed bolt holes.
