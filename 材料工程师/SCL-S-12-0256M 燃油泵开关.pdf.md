Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件材料符合要求。 
      2. 失效件注塑工艺差，内部有孔洞，但不是导致失效的主要原因。 
Conclusions：1.The material performance of parts conformed to requirements. 
2. Injection molding process was bad, there were a lot of holes inside of the failure part. But it was 
not root cause of the failure. 
建议：1.检查装配环节和对手件的情况。 
      2.改善零件注塑工艺。 
Suggestions：1. Check the situation of the assembly and the opponent .  
            2.Improve the injection molding process.
