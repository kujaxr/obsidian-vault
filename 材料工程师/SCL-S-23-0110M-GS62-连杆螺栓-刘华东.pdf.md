Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、1#螺栓装配时被屈服拉长，引起连接松弛，从而导致1#螺栓发生过载断裂、2#螺栓发生单向疲劳断裂。1 # bolt 
was yielding and elongated during assemble; causing the connection to relax, and then resulting in overload fracture of 1# 
bolt and unidirectional fatigue fracture of 2 # bolt.  
2、失效螺栓化学成分、硬度、金相和摩擦系数均符合技术要求。The chemical composition, hardness, microstructure 
and frictional coefficient of failure bolts meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、排查螺栓屈服拉长的原因。Investigate the cause of bolt yield and elongation.
