Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：. 失效件材料硬度略偏低，但总体性能符合要求。 
从断口分析可推断，胶管内层收到异常损伤，形成割口，导致胶管在时候破裂。 
Conclusions：the hardness of material is little lower than the requirement, but the whole performance of the 
material conforms to the standard . 
The analyze shows the inner side of the hose is cut by the hard article accidentally, which cause the 
hose brake during the use. 
建议 ：确保胶管在不受到意外损伤。 
Suggestions：assure the hose free of damage by accident.
