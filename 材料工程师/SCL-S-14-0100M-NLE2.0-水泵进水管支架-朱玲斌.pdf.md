Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 水管支架由于受到较大载荷而发生疲劳断裂，疲劳源位置见图2a。The pipe bracket fatigue fracture because 
of overload, fatigue source position is shown in figure 2a. 
（2） 失效件材料成分合格，硬度和组织正常。The chemical component of the failure part is qualified, the hardness 
and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核支架的结构强度。Checking the structural strength of bracket. 
（2） 调查台架的震动情况。Investigation the vibration of bench.
