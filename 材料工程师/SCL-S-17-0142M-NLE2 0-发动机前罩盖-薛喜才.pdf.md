Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  发动机前罩盖内螺纹孔1#、3#(见图1)与螺栓匹配异常，导致内螺纹部分牙型被挤掉、螺栓滑牙。 
    Bad matching between front cover and bolts results in partial screw thread edging out. 
 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  排查装配过程。 
    Check the assembly process.
