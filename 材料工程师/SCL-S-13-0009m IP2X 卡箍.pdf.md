Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 卡箍断裂属于腐蚀疲劳断裂，腐蚀疲劳的发生与两钢带缝隙间存在含有Cl 元素的介质有关。 
The exhaust clamp failed due to corrosion fatigue，which is related to the medium with the element Cl in the gap 
between the two belts. 
2. 卡箍材质符合409 的技术要求；焊接熔合区大小符合供应商标准。 
The material meets the technical requirement; the weld length meets the technical requirement from supplier. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 请调查含有Cl 元素介质的来源。 
Please investigate the source of the medium with Cl.
