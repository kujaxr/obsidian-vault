Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 尖角处的应力集中容易造成衬套在尖角处的薄弱区域开裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the materials of the bushes conform to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the failed bushes, it can be seen that the stress concentration 
easily occurs at the angle of square notch. So, the stress concentrations easily cause the angle of square notch 
regions to break. 
建议：优化衬套注塑工艺；将方形缺口改为圆形缺口。 
Suggestions：The injection process should be optimized. The square notch should be changed into a circular notch.

小结：经测试，失效件的材料为PA66，材料符合设计要求。失效件第一次熔融过程和第二次熔融过程中的熔

Conclusion: The material of the failure part is PA66 which conforms to the design requirement. The change of ΔH 
between the first circle and the second circle of the two failure parts are small, so the injection process of 
them are good.
