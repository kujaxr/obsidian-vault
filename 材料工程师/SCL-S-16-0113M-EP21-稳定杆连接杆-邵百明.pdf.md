Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）后稳定杆连杆是由于疲劳而断裂。The link of the rear stable bar was broken by fatigue 
（2） 连杆和衬套钢套的成分，组织，硬度以及焊接未见异常。The chemical components, microstructure, hardness and 
weld are without abnormity. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核局部载荷，提高安全系数。Check the local load and increase safety factor. 
（2）评估连杆与衬套钢套的连接方式由电阻焊改为弧焊是否可行。Evaluate if the arc welding between the outer tube 
and bushing is feasible.
