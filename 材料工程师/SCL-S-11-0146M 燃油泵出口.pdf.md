Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过支架的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，支架断口附近内部组织无缺陷，钢珠与内壁为过盈配合，管壁表面受到较
大张力，使用过程中受到压力和温度交变影响，容易导致与钢珠表面接触管壁的外壁发生银文和开裂。 
2. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the bracket, it can be seen that the external surface of outlet 
pipe existed a bigger tensile strength due to the interference fit between the pipe and the steel ball. During the use, the tensile 
strength and the influence from the alternating pressure and temperature easily cause the external surface of outlet pipe to 
produce craze and crack. After testing, the material of the bracket conforms to the drawing requirement. 
建议：装配前百分百检查钢珠尺寸；保证支架注塑工艺的稳定性；装配钢珠时避免冲击速度过快。 
Suggestions：One hundred percent inspect the dimension of the steel ball. The stabilization of the injection process of the bracket should 
be ensured. During the steel ball assembly, the quickly putting the steel ball in place should be prevented.
