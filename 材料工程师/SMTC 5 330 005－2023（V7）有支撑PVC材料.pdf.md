1  范围Scope
本标准规定了有支撑PVC 革的一般要求、
材料要求及测试方法。
本标准适用于座椅及门饰板包覆。不适用
于非PVC 包装材料及非底布支撑的PVC。
 
This Standard covers the general performance 
requirements and test methods for supported 
PVC. 
This Standard are mainly used in the seat and 
door casing, but cannot be used in Non-PVC 
wrapper and PVC unsupported by backing 
fabric.

3  要求Requirements
3.1 样品和预处理要求Sampling and conditioning requirements
3.1.1 样品要求Sampling requirements
样品的宽度必须为标准卷的完全宽度，为
进行完整实验需要面积为 2 m2的样品。
For complete testing, at least 2 m2 roll stock is 
required.
3.1.2 预处理Conditioning
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准气候
条件23℃,50 %RH下至少放置24 h。
The test specimens should be conditioned at 23 
°C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at least 
24h before test.
