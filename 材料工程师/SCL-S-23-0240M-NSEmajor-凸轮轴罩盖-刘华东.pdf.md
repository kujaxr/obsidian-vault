Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 嵌件螺母是受载过大产生开裂。The failure part was cracked due to excessive load. 
2、 失效件金相组织和硬度未见异常。The microstructure and hardness of the failure part were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
 
1、排查对手件以及装配过程是否有异常。Check if there are any abnormalities in the components and assembly process.
