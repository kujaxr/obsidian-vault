Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 前减震器安装板是由于疲劳而开裂的，载荷较大是导致开裂的主要原因；FNL-FRT SHOCK ABSORBER 
MTG-LH was broken by fatigue and the main reason was a big stress load. 
2. 零件成分、硬度及金相分析表明，失效件材质不是SCG220Y+Z50/50；Base on the chemical components、hardness 
and microstructure，the material of the failed parts wasn’t SCG220Y+Z50/50. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
校核开裂处应力载荷；Check the stress load at crack region.
