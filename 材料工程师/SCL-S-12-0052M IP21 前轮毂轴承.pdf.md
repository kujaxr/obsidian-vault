Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承的疲劳失效是由于载荷过大所致。The fatigue failure of the bearing is because of overload. 
（2）润滑正常。The lubrication is normal. 
（3）材料的硬度，组织和夹杂物含量正常。The hardness, microstructure and the inclusion content of the material are 
normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）提高轴承的承载能力。Improve the loading ability.
