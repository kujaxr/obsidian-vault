Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是旋转弯曲疲劳断裂。The failure is rotating bending fatigue fracture 
2、 表面与芯部硬度差超差。The difference between surface and core hardness does not meet the technical 
requirements 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 控制螺栓实物质量。Control bolt quality 
2、 请检查载荷情况。Check the loading conditions.
