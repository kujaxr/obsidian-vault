Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 通过O 型圈的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，装配时O 型圈受到刮擦，造成O 型圈发生扭曲、褶
皱，使用过程中O 型圈在折痕处发生开裂，进而导致O 型圈密封不严。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of the O ring conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the O ring, it can be seen that the O ring was scratched during 
assembly. Then the O ring was distorted and folded. During the using process, the split occurred on the fold 
which cause the O ring can’t keep the original sealing performance. 
建议：装配时避免O 型圈受到刮擦；回油管喇叭口端面应倒圆角。 
Suggestions：The scratch on the O ring during assembly should be prevented. The edge of the flanged bell mouse of 
the pipe should be changed into fillet shape.

Ⅳ. 尺寸与宏观分析 Dimention and Macroscopic analysis 
 
图2 的O 型圈表面存在一条连续的不规则折痕，从O 型圈中心部位向侧面扩展，可以判定O 型圈在装配位
置内发生扭曲。图3 是放大的O 型圈形貌，在O 型圈表面可以看到一块明显的刮痕。图4 是折痕放大图片，发现
折痕部位已经发生开裂。 
A continuous irregular fold is observed on the O ring as shown in Fig 2 which indicates the O ring distorted during 
assembly. A piece of abrasion is found in the magnified picture of the O ring (Fig 3). Fig 4 is the magnified picture of the 
fold, it can be seen that the split appeared on the fold.
