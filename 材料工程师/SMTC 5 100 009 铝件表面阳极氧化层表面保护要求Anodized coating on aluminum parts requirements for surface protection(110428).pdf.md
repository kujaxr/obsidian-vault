1 范围 
本标准规定了纯铝件和铝锻件上的阳极氧
化层的表面保护要求。 
本标准适用于装饰性的汽车用内外饰零件，
也可用于有表面功能性要求的汽车内外饰零件。
1  Scope  
This standard specifies requirements for 
anodic oxide coatings on pure aluminum and 
aluminum wrought alloy parts.  
The standard is suitable for decorative inte-
rior and exterior automobile components; how-
ever, they can also be applied for achieving func-
tional surface properties.

3.2 基本要求 
有害物质禁用按照SMTC 90008。对于完整的
检验则需要10个成品。表面保护层不允许出现任
何影响防腐性能和/或其他规定性能的小孔、裂
纹、损伤或其他缺陷。 
    
3.2  General requirements 
Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 90008. 10 finished parts are 
required for complete testing. The protective 
coatings shall not exhibit any pores, cracks, 
damages or other flaws that impair corrosion 
protection and/or the specified appearance.

Part finish shall be free of polishing flaws,
scratches, damages or similar flaws that impair 
the appearance of the part. The whole surface of 
the component shall not display patchiness, 
shades or loss of glossiness.

3.6  Sealing quality 
3.6.1  吸收性能的损失 
试验：根据DIN EN 12 373-4进行试验。
要求：吸收性能的损失 最大值特征值为1。 
 
3.6.1  Loss of absorptive power
Test: acc. to DIN EN 12 373-4; Require-
ment: max. value for loss of absorptive power: 1 
3.6.2  耐碱性（只用于装饰性车外饰件） 
 
a) 假如零件被油、油脂或者其他类似的
物质弄脏时，该零件表面应用合适的
试剂清洁，并干燥。 
 
b) 试验：把零件浸入18-20 ℃，由2 g/l 
Na3PO4 200 mg/l CI-；pH = 12.5±2
组成的无铝混合试验液中（PH 值用
NaOH 和H3PO4调整）。 把零件约10cm
的阳极氧化表面浸入到试验液中，浸
置约10 min，然后用蒸馏水洗净，并
自然吹干。其后，把对该零件进行DIN 
50 018 KFW 2.0 SO2测试，5 个周期，
然后进行评价。 
 
 
 
 
 
c) 要求：用商用抛光剂，或蘸有氧化镁
溶液的抛光棉进行处理后，表面无变
化。无明显浸入的边界痕迹。 
 
 
3.6.2  Alkali resistance (only for decorative 
parts applied on the vehicle exterior) 
a)  If the specimen is soiled with oil, grease 
or similar substances, the surface shall 
be cleaned with a suitable solvent and 
dried. 
b)  Test: The specimen shall be immersed 
in an aluminum-free test solution com-
prising 2 g/l Na3PO4, 200mg/l Cl-, pH = 
12.5±0.2 (achieved through NaOH and 
H3PO4) at a temperature of 18 °C to 
20 °C.A length of approx. 10 cm of the 
anodically oxidized surface of the spe-
cimen shall be immersed for ten mi-
nutes in the test solvent. The surface is 
then rinsed with distilled (or fully deio-
nised) water and dried in air. After the 
immersion process the specimen shall 
be subjected to moisture condensate 
alternating atmosphere DIN 50 018 
KFW 2.0 SO2 for 5 cycles and subse-
quently be evaluated. 
c)  Requirements: No visual change after 
treatment of the surface with a com-
mercial polishing agent or with ana-
queous suspension of magnesium 
oxide and polishing cotton. No visibly 
perceptible immersion line.
