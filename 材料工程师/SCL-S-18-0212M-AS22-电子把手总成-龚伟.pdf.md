Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 第一例失效件为正面受外力断裂，同时饰条未正确装配；第二例失效为背面受外力断裂。 
In the first case, the front part was broken by the external force and the trimming was not properly 
assembled; the second case was the failure of the back side by external force. 
              2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   The material of the parts conforms to the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 排查装配物流环节异常。 
                    Check the abnormal situation in assembly and logistics process. 
                 2. 跟踪售后抱怨情况。 
                    Track after-sales complaints.

回零件，IP32 车型606km。
