Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件受外力导致断裂。2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
      3．零件注塑工艺不佳，但这不是导致失效的主要原因。 
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made failure part fracture. 
            2. Material performance of the part conformed to requirements. 
            3. Injection process of the part was bad. But it was not root cause of the failure. 
建议： 1. 加强对零件的保护。2．改进注塑工艺。 
Suggestions: 1. Protection of parts should be improved. 
           2. Injection process should be improved.
