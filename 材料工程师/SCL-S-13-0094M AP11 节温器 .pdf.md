Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）来自缸盖的大颗粒机加工铝屑卡住节温器，导致其无法工作。A big aluminum scraps from machining cylinder 
head locked the thermostat which could not work. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化并控制好清洗过程，确保清洁度符合要求。Optimize and control the cleaning process well and ensure the 
cleanness meets the requirement.
