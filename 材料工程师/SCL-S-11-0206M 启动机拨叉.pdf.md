Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1.启动机拨叉的材料符合质量要求。 
      2.失效件系受瞬时外力超负荷造成脆断。 
Conclusions: 1. The material performance of starter shifter conforms to requirements. 
           2. The brittle fracture was cause by overloaded instantaneous external force. 
建议：起动机机构进行检查。 
Suggestions：The mechanism of starter should be checked.
