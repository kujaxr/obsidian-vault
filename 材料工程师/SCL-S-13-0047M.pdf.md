Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂，失效件表面磨损严重。 
The failure of parts belong to fatigue fracture，surface was wear and tear seriously. 
2、失效件的材料强度低。 
the material strength of failure part is low. 
3、 失效件的实测板厚为技术要求（t=3.2±0.22mm）的下限，接近于公称厚度为3.0mm 的板厚。 
The thinckness of failure part is the lower limit of the technical requirements of the (t = 3.2 ± 0.22mm), 
close to the nominal thickness of 3.0mm thickness. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 校核和检查连接付的连接情况。 
To check connections. 
2、 供应商加强过程质量控制。 
   Suppliers to strengthen the process of quality control.
