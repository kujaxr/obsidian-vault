1 范围Scope 
 
 
本标准规定了抗拉强度为Rm≤1000 MPa的
铁基材料和钢制件上的电镀锌／镍合金镀层的
要求。 
本标准适用于评价钢制件上的电镀锌／镍
合金镀层。 
 
This standard specifies requirements for elec-
trolytically deposited zinc/nickel alloy coatings 
on ferrous materials and steel parts with a ten-
sile strength Rm ≤ 1,000 MPa. 
 
The standard is adopted to evaluate the elec-
trolytically deposited zinc/nickel alloy coatings 
on ferrous materials and steel parts.

3 要求Requirements 
 
 
3.1  避免有害物质按SMTC 5 500 001。完成完
整的试验需要10 个零件。对于出口零件有害物
质按SMTC 5 500 002满足REACH法规标准。零件
表面不允许有可见的外观缺陷。加工及装配过
程，不允许损害零件的防腐性能。 
 
3.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 5 500 001. Ten finished parts 
are required for a complete test. No visible de-
fects are permitted on the surface of the parts. 
No damage is allowed in production and 
mounting process that would lead to an impair-
ment of corrosion function.

3.2  除非在图纸上指出零件的某些部分不需要
表面保护，否则图纸上标注的类型适用于整个零
件表面。 
 
3.2  If one certain surface protection type is 
defined on the drawing ,that means the whole 
part should treated by this type, unless some 
areas is defined as an exception. 
 
3.3  对于紧固件，适用于端部、头部等非螺纹
部分。对于螺纹部分的考核适当降低要求。 
 
3.3 As to the fasten parts, only the head, the 
wrench bearing surface is evaluated according 
to one certain surface protection type which is 
defined on the drawing. The threaded areas 
usually relatively decrease the requirement. 
 
3.4  该镀层作为重度防腐的镀层，特别适用于
温度达150 ℃的零件以及紧固件（例如：发动
机室和刹车系统）。 
 
3.4  These coatings as heavy-duty corrosion 
protection, are especially suitable for compo-
nents and systems of threaded joints working in 
a temperature load of up to 150 °C (engine 
compartment or brake area).  
 
3.5  该镀层适用于导电连接（接地线连接）。
鉴于电化学相容性，该镀层尤其适用于与铝材料
的连接，但不适用于与镁材料的连接。该镀层同
样适用于内部传动的连接元件，以避免附加的施
力作用。 
 
3.5  These coatings are suitable for conducting 
connections (ground connections). Due to the 
electrochemical compatibility, these coatings are 
suitable for contact parts with aluminum materi-
als, but not with magnesium materials. These 
coatings are also suitable for joining elements 
with internal drive to avoid the clogging of 
force-application sockets. 
 
3.6 抗拉强度Rm > 1000 MPa的高强度钢制件和
表面硬度 > 320 HV的连接元件（例如：螺栓）
不允许使用这种合金镀层。 
 
3.6  High-strength steel parts with a tensile 
strength Rm > 1,000 MPa and joining elements 
(e.g. screws) with a surface hardness > 320 HV 
must not be coated with these alloy coatings 
 
3.7  在生产过程中，必须进行去氢处理来避免
零件发生氢脆。 
 
3.7 The surface treatment procedure shall be 
conducted so that damage by delayed hydro-
gen-induced brittle fractures can be ruled out.

5  镀层性能Coating Performance 
 
 
5.1  电镀层厚度Coating thicknesses 
 
 
镀层厚度要求：  
a)  8μm至25μm（用于一般类型的部件）  
b)  8μm至15μm（用于螺纹件，测量点按
照ISO 4042）。 
 
Requirement for coating thickness: 
a)  8 μm up to 25 μm (for components of a 
general nature); 
b)  8 μm up to 15 μm (for threaded parts, 
measuring point according to ISO 
4042). 
5.2  镍含量Nickel content 
 
 
按照ISO 3497，用X射线荧光法进行试验。
镍含量要求： 
a） 对Sp- RH42，Sp- RH43，Sp- RH47， 
Sp- RH72，Sp- RH73和Sp- RH77：
12％至16%； 
b）对Sp- RH49： 10％至18％； 
c）在特殊情况下，如铸件而言（例：
制动钳）。如果镀层采用碱性电解
质离析的话，镍含量允许为12％至
17％。 
 
X-ray fluorescence test method according to 
ISO 3497. Requirement for nickel content: 
a)  For Sp-RH42, Sp-RH43, Sp-RH47, 
Sp-RH72,Sp-RH73 and Sp-RH77: 12% 
to 16%; 
b)  For Sp-RH49: 10% to 18%; 
c)  In exceptional cases, exclusively for 
castings (e.g. brake caliper), a nickel 
content of 12% to 17% is permissible 
for deposits from an alkaline electrolyte 
system. 
 
5.3  附着力Adhesive strength 
 
 
热冲击试验按照ISO 2819 。试件在
（300±10）℃下存放30 分钟，紧接着浸入室温
水中。要求：镀层没有气泡状或大面积的剥落。 
 
Thermal shock test based on ISO 2819.The 
specimen part is aged for 30 minutes at (300 ± 
10) °C and then dipped in water of room tem-
perature. Requirement: no large-scale or bub-
ble-shaped stripping of the coating.
