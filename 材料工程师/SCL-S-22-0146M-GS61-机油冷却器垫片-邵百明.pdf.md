Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）垫片凸起物是螺栓拧紧时表面涂胶的胶囊被挤破，内部的丙烯酸酯挤入密封面，在垫片表面形成的。The gasket 
protrusion is formed on the gasket surface when the bolt is tightened, the capsule coated with glue on the surface is 
crushed, and the acrylate inside is squeezed into the sealing surface. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）评估是否可以去掉表面涂胶或选择合适的涂胶类型。Evaluate whether the surface coating can be removed or 
select the appropriate coating type.
