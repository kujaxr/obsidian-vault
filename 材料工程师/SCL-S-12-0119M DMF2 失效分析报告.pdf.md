Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
1. DMF 弧弹簧由于喷丸过程中引入的表面缺陷引发疲劳失败。The DMF Arc spring failed through fatigue, initiated 
from surface defects which were introduced during shot peening.  
2. 缺陷的深度约25μm，比以前失效件（SCL-12-0101M）约45μm 腐蚀坑更浅，因此寿命延长了。这件案例，没
有发现腐蚀。The depth of the defects was ~25µm, which, compared to the ~45µm corrosion pit found in the previous 
failure (SCL-S-12-0101M), can explain the extended lifetime.  Corrosion was not seen in this case.   
3. 尼龙的摩擦垫圈遭到严重破坏，可能是由于离合器片过热造成。The nylon friction washer was severely damaged, 
possibly due to overheating of the clutch plate. 
4. 弹簧表面磨损严重已去除或削弱喷丸强化层的作用。Severe wear of the springs has removed or weakened the shot 
peened layer. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商必须控制喷丸过程中，既要满足覆盖位置和内应力要求，又要避免产生缺陷。。The supplier must control 
the shot peening process, to achieve coverage and required internal stress, without the introduction of defects. 
2. 确认双质量飞轮的设计是否能够承受应用加载（包括弹簧负载和弹簧磨损）。 Confirm that DMF design is 
capable of handling the applied loading (including load on springs and wear of springs).

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
目视检查 Visual inspection 
 
双质量飞轮收到的情况为1 和2 图所示。打开后，发现一条弧形弹簧断裂。主飞轮铸件接触法兰铆钉处严重地磨
损（图1b），摩擦垫片和轴衬套非常严重磨损（图1C 和3）。由于高温导致磨擦垫片软化并在随后的压缩载荷下
发生变形，最终造成磨擦垫片的磨损。 
The Dual Mass flywheel was received as shown in figures 1 & 2.  On opening, one of the Arc springs was found to 
have broken.  The Primary flywheel casting was heavily worn from contact with the flange rivets (figure 1b)， and the 
Friction washer and Axial bushing were very heavily worn (figure 1c and 3).  The wear of the Friction washer is due to 
high temperature which caused softening and subsequent deformation under the compressive loading.

Additional cracks  
 
The springs were checked by Magnetic Particle Inspection and optical microscope.  Many additional cracks were found.  
The crack positions lay along one side of the spring (nearest to the Primary flywheel), with the crack origin at the inner 
surface of the wire coil (fig. 7b).  One crack was opened for SEM analysis and another was prepared in cross-section.
