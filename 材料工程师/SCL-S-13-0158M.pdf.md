Ⅰ.任务描述 Task description 
实验室受委托对分离拨叉固定螺栓进行失效原因分析，78Z 变速箱分离拨叉断裂迄今售后市场共抱怨8 例，
问题表现为车辆离合器无法分离，检查发现分离拨叉固定螺栓断裂，且从照片看断裂位置基本一致。失效零件具
体信息见下一页，实验室收到3 例失效件（序号为6、7、8）： 
As requested, QA lab has analyzed the failure of Bolt. 78Z gearbox fork isolated fracture of the aftermarket complain 
so far eight cases, the problem can not be separated the performance of the vehicle clutch, examination revealed fracture 
separating fork bolts, and from the photo look basically the same fault location. Failure parts specific information see next

Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 螺栓是由于夹紧力不足引起的疲劳断裂。 
The failure of the parts is fatigue fracture, due to insufficient clamping force.  
2、 零件的材料性能符合技术要求。 
Hardness is less than about technical requirements 10HV, but this is not the root cause of breakage caused. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 校核螺栓的实际应力载荷。 
Check bolt actual stress loading. 
2、 校核装配工艺的适用性和检查装配过程质量。 
Check the suitability of the PAM and the assembly process quality.
