Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效螺栓是由于回火不充分导致硬度过高而发生延迟断裂。The failure bolts were delayed fracture due to the high 
hardness caused by insufficient tempering. 
2、螺栓化学成分符合要求，但硬度超差，螺纹有折叠缺陷，表面有磷聚集层，均不符合要求。The chemical 
composition of bolt meets the requirements. But the hardness is out of tolerance, the thread has fold defects, the surface 
has phosphorus accumulation layers, all of these do not meet the technique requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离该批次零件并要求供应商整改。Isolate these bolts and require the supplier to rectification. 
2、更换同批次已装车螺栓。Replace the installed bolts of the same batch.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
一、宏观观察  Macroscopic Observation： 
泰国工厂质保部门发现MG3 车型有4 例燃油箱钢带螺栓断裂，失效零件如图1 所示，螺栓强度等级10.9 级，
表面达克罗处理。 
The Thailand factory QA department found 4 cases of bolts fractured on the MG3 vechile. The failure bolts are 
shown in figure 1. The bolts strength is grade 10.9 and the surface is Zn-Al coating. 
 
图1 失效零件图片 
Fig 1 Failure parts 
二、断口分析  Fracture Analysis： 
1、使用VHX-2000 型体视镜观察螺栓断口形貌，螺栓无明显塑性变形，断口平齐，表面呈现白亮色小刻面，如
图2 图3 所示，这些均为脆性断裂特征。其他失效螺栓与其相似。 
1. The macroscopic morphology of the bolt fracture surface was observed by the stereomicroscope. The bolt had no 
obvious plastic deformation，the fracture was flat and the surface appeared white small facets, as shown in figure 2 and 3. 
These are brittle fracture characteristics. The other failure bolts are same with it. 
 
 
图2  断口宏观形貌                               图3 螺栓侧面形貌 
Fig.2 Macroscopic morphology of fracture                    Fig.3 Side profile of bolt                     
2、采用EVO MA25 型扫描电镜观察失效螺栓断口微观形貌。断口微观形貌主要为冰糖状沿晶特征加少量韧窝，
如图4 图5 所示。依此可以判断螺栓发生延迟断裂。
