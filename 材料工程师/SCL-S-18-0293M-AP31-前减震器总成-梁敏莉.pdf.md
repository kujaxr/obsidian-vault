Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件活塞杆头部由于过扭，而发生扭转剪切断裂。 
Failure piston rod head is torsional shear fracture due to excessive torsion. 
2、失效件活塞杆头部材料无异常。 
Material of failure piston rod head is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 排查装配过程，如扭矩。 
Check the assembly process, such as torque.
