Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、螺母脱落主要与焊接不良有关。The nut falling off is mainly related to poor welding. 
2、同侧焊接螺母破坏扭矩符合要求。The destruction torque of welding nut on the same side meets the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、排查螺母焊接工艺与质量稳定性。Check the nut welding process and quality stability.
