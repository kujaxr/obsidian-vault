Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  主减速从动齿轮属于过载断裂，可能是试验前零件已经损坏。 
    Main deceleration driven gear fractured though fatigue. It may be that the parts have been damaged before the test. 
2.  主减速从动齿轮材料成分、硬度均满足要求，金相组织正常。 
    Main deceleration driven gear content and hardness are meet the requirement, metallographic structure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  检查同批次零件是否有损坏，并对生产过程进行排查。 
    Check if parts of the same batch are damaged,and check the production process.
