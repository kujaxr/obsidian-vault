Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 冷却系统异常导致发动机高温、燃气吹蚀缸盖而泄漏。 
   Abnormal cooling system result in engine high temperature and the gas blows cylinder cover. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查发动机冷却系统如冷却液泄漏、散热系统故障等。 
   Investigate cooling system such as cooling liquid leakage and bad heat dissipation.
