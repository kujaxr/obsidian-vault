Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  上导轨支架由于承受较大的载荷，在断口1 位置(见图6)先疲劳断裂，然后再造成断口2 位置疲劳断裂、上导
轨异常磨损、断裂。 
    Top guide bracket fractured by fatigue at fracture surface 1(shown in figure1)because of big load, which result in 
other area cracking and top guide abnormal wear、cracking. 
2.  支架材料成分、强度满足要求，金相组织正常。 
    Chemical components and strength of the bracket meet the requirement. Microstructure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 对凸轮轴及相应系统进行排查，查找可能引起正时链条负载增加的因素。 
Check camshaft and its system to find the load increasing factor of timing chain.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results

发现正时链条上导轨位置歪斜、被磨损，磨损的塑料进入油底壳，造成油道堵塞，凸轮轴等磨损失效。
