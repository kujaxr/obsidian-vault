Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：铜层远低于额定值，导致附着力不足，镀层和基材分离。 
建议：调整工艺，确保批次零件的每层镀层厚度均达到额定值（失效件镍层也低于额定值）。 
    Conclusion: the copper layer is far below the rated value, resulting in insufficient adhesion and separation 
of coating and substrate. 
Suggestion: adjust the process to ensure that the thickness of each coating layer of parts in batch reaches 
the rated value (the nickel layer of failure parts is also lower than the rated value).
