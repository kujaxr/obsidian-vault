Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该缸盖喷油器漏油是因该处存在一贯穿性孔洞，该孔洞内壁光滑，为铸造过程中气体不能充分排出，包裹在
铝液中形成的。The reason for the oil leakage of the cylinder head injector was that there was a consistent through hole at 
this place. The inner wall of the hole was smooth, which was formed when the gas cannot be fully discharged in the 
casting process and was wrapped in the aluminum liquid. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化模具设计，加强排气。Optimize mold design and strengthen exhaust. 
（2）加强气密性检测和内部铸造缺陷的检测。Strengthen the management of air tightness inspection and inner casting 
defecting inspection
