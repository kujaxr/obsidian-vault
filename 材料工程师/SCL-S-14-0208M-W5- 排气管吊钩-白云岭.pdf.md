Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于原始开裂引起的过载断裂，原始开裂是由于原材料化学成分不合格致使硬度较高在弯折工序
产生的。 The failure is due to overload fracture by original crack.The original cracking was made from the 
bending process ,due to the chemical composition of raw material unqualified cause high hardness reduction. 
2、 永红库存抽检件的化学成分合格，硬度正常。 
The chemical composition for yong hong inventoryparts met the technical requirements, the hardness is normal. 
  建议Suggestions： 
1、 停用此批零件。Stop useing this batch of parts. 
2、 加强供应商原材料及过程质量管控。To strengthen the quality control the supplier of raw materials and process.

（1）测试位置宏观观察：
