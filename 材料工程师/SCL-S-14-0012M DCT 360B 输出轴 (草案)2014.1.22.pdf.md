Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）输出轴齿轮是由于疲劳而失效的；这是由于其承受的载荷较大所致。The gear of the output shaft was broken by fatigue 
because the load ability is not enough. 
（2）失效件10μm 深处的表面压应力符合要求；30μm 深处的压应力低于下限，但不会对弯曲疲劳产生显著的影
响。The compression stress of the failed part at the 10μm depth location meet the requirement; the one of the 30μm depth location is below 
the lower limit, but it will not has a significant effect on the bending fatigue. 
（3）齿根过渡圆滑，其半径符合要求。The transition of the teeth root is smooth and the radius meets the requirements. 
（3）材料的成分，表面硬度，芯部硬度以及淬硬层深度符合要求；组织和夹杂物水平正常。The chemical components, 
surface hardness, core hardness and the hardening depth of the material meet the requirement; the microstructure and inclusion are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核输出轴齿轮的工作载荷，并通过设计提高其安全系数。Check the load of the output shaft gear and optimize to 
improve the safety factor. 
（2）评估万向节失效对输出轴齿载荷的影响。Evaluate the effect of the cardan failure on the load of the shaft gear. 
（3）请供应商提供新件的表面压应力检测报告。Supplier should offer the test report about the compression stress of new parts.
