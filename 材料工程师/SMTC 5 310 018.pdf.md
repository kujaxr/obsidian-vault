1 范围Scope 
本标准规定了应用于仪表板、门槛、保险杠
蒙皮、门内饰板、轮罩等区域的弹性改性、滑石
改性及矿物改性 的 PP 成品件。 
本标准适用以上应用区域的弹性改性、滑石
改性及矿物改性 PP 成品件。 
 
This standard specifies the material require-
ments for elastomer, talc and mineral-modified 
PP for finished parts of dashboard, sill plank-
ing, extended sill, bumper cover, door cover, 
wheel housing and so on. 
This standard applies to the material require-
ments for elastomer, talc and mineral-modified 
PP for finished parts for the following areas of 
application.

3.2 特性Feature 
3.2.1  在加强筋、增强区域和管接头处不允许存
在凹痕，填充玻纤的材料，其表面在触觉或视觉
上都不应出现瑕疵。 
 
3.2.1 Sink marks at the base of ribs, reinforce-
ments and pipe unions are not permitted. Mate-
rials containing glass fibers must exhibit a sur-
face that is free of imperfections both to the eye 
and the touch. 
3.2.2  成品件中的玻纤分布应均匀，在成品件的
不同部位所取的三个试样中的玻纤含量之差不应
超过1.0 %。 
 
3.2.2 The distribution of glass fibers throughout 
the component must be sufficiently uniform so 
that three specimens taken from different points 
on a finished part do not differ in glass fiber 
content by more than 1.0 %.

3.3.9 耐老化特性 Aging resistance 
 
 
A.全球车型:成品件在150℃干燥箱中（内外饰
≥400h；底盘≥700h；机舱近车身≥700h；机舱
发动机上≥1000h）,冷却至23℃ 后零件不允许有
脆化现象。 
 
A. Global type: Finished parts shall be aged at 
150°C in a desiccator（Interior and exterior ≥ 
400h; chassis ≥ 700h; cabin near the body ≥ 
700h; cabin engine ≥ 1000h）. After the test 
parts are not allowed embrittlement to cool 
down to 23°C. 
B.国内车型:成品件在150℃干燥箱中（内外饰
≥168h；底盘≥336h；机舱近车身≥336h；机舱
发动机上≥700h）,冷却至23℃ 后零件不允许有
脆化现象。 
 
B. China type: Finished parts shall be aged at 
150°C in a desiccator（Interior and exterior ≥ 
168h; chassis ≥ 336h; cabin near the body ≥ 
336h; cabin engine ≥ 700h）. After the test parts 
are not allowed embrittlement to cool down to 
23°C.

3.3.10 高温性能 Elevated-temperature behavior 
若图纸或认可无其它规定，则采用90℃。对
于接受更强阳光照射的内饰件，如：仪表板，门
板上装饰板则采用110℃。时间：24h。要求：表
面无缺陷，功能无失效。 
 
Temperature unless otherwise stated in the 
drawing or release is 90°C. This temperature is 
110°C for those vehicle passenger compartment 
parts that are exposed to a higher level of sun 
radiation, such as on the dashboard or upper 
edge moldings of the door panel. Dura-
tion:24h.Requirement：no surface defects, 
function without failure. 
3.3.11 低温性能 Low temperature behaviour  
 
若图纸或认可无其它规定，则采用-40℃。时
间：24h。按SMTC 5 400 030，落球试验应至少3
个平整试样，油漆或皮纹面向上；球重880g；落
球高度650mm。要求：不断，不裂。 
 
Temperature unless stated in the drawing or 
release is -40°C. Duration: 24h. Ball drop test 
according to SMTC 5 400 030 with at least three 
plane part, painted or grained top surface. Ball 
weight is 880g; ball drop height is 
650mm. Requirement：No fracture, no cracking.

3.7 实验备注 Notes on testing 
 
 
3.7.1 屈服应力、拉伸强度、断裂伸长率 Yield stress, tensile strength and elongation at tear 
根据ISO 527-2，样品型号为5A；试验速度 
50mm/min。 
 
Test according to ISO 527-2, specimen type 5A, 
test rate 50 mm/min. 
3.7.2 缺口冲击 Notched impact strength 
 
 
试验符合ISO179-1；偏差试样长度：(50±1)
毫米；宽度：(6±0.2)毫米；厚度符合成品零件厚度
（最大达到4毫米）；支撑分离：40毫米；试验装置：
摆锤式冲击试验机 4J。试样宽边有一个0.8毫米宽
的U形切口。切口深度应较试样厚度的1/3。与切口
底部相接的边，其曲率半径必须小于0.1毫米。如果
是从一侧有颗粒或油漆的模塑零件上取的试样，应
在试样的颗粒或油漆侧开口，并且在放置到支架上
时，切口中央应对准冲击面的中央，从而使切口不
在冲击方向上。 
 
Impact resistance test (broadside impact) ac-
cording to ISO 179-1; deviating specimen length: 
(50 ±1) mm; width: (6 ± 0.2) mm; thickness ac-
cording to finished part thickness (up to max.4 
mm); support separation: 40 mm; test apparatus: 
pendulum impact tester 4J. The specimens are 
given a 0.8 mm wide U-shaped notch on the 
broad side. The notch depth shall be 1/3 the 
specimen thickness. The edges adjoining the 
base of the notch must have a curvature radius 
of < 0.1 mm. Specimens taken from molded 
parts with one-sided graining or paint shall be 
notched on the grained or painted side and 
placed on the support so that the center of the 
notch is exactly in the center of the striking plane 
and the notch is turned away from the direction 
of impact. 
3.7.3 弯曲强度 Flexural strength 
 
 
在ISO 178所规定的最大力条件下测定弯曲
强度，但是试样：长度（501）mm，宽度（100.6）
mm，厚度按照产品厚度（最大为4 mm）；跨距
40 mm；试验速度（141）mm/min，支承半径
1.0 mm到1.2 mm。如果是从一面带有皮纹或油漆
的成品件上取下的试样，在将其放置到试验机支
架上时，应使未带皮纹或未油漆的一面被拉伸。
在不同温度下试验部件强度时，应将试样在测量
设备（例如：带有弯曲试验吊架的拉伸试验机）
的恒温室中放置30分钟。接着将恒温室暂时打开，
并将第一批试样放置在支架上。在再次调温15分
钟之后进行测量。每次试验必须使用5个试样。 
 
Determination of flexural stress at maximum load 
acc. to ISO 178; specimen: length (50±1) mm, 
width (10±0.6) mm, thickness acc. to the prod-
ucts thickness (max. 4 mm); support separation 
40 mm; test rate (14 ± 1) mm/min, support radius 
1.0 to 1.2 mm. Specimens taken from finished 
parts grained on one side shall be positioned on 
the support of the test equipment so that the 
non-grained side is subjected to tensile stress. 
In order to test the component strength at dif-
ferent temperatures the specimens shall be 
placed in the tempering chamber of the mea-
suring device for 30 min. (e.g. tensile test ma-
chine with bending hanger). Subsequently, the 
chamber shall be opened shortly to position the 
first specimen on the support. The measurement 
is taken after a further tempering period of at 
least 15 min. Five samples shall be tested each 
time.
