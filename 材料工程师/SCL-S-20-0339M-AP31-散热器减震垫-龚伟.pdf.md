Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions:  1. 低温&低湿时，尼龙件受冲击易断裂。 
nylon parts are easy to fracture under impact at low temperature and low humidity. 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions:  1. 关注零件的调湿工艺。 
                 Pay attention to the humidity control process of parts. 
2. 装配现场低温时增加零件的预热工序。 
                 Increase preheating process of parts at low temperature during assembly.
