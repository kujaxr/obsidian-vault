Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 驱动轴由于过载出现裂纹, 然后在后续使用中, 通过疲劳扩展而断裂。The driveshaft has fractured due to an 
initial overload crack, and subsequent propagation by fatigue during vehicle running. 
2. 过载原因是由于制造过程中受到冲击力。The overload is most likely due to an impact force during handling, 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 在轴的制造和装配过程中注意护理。Take care of the shaft during handling and assembly.

电镜分析SEM Analysis 
在起源的地方，断裂方式为脆性（沿晶）的过载特征（图4）。在源处有一个浅的变形；这是在开裂的之前或同
时发生的。由于未腐蚀，时间不能估计，只能说，在之前或者断裂之后有防护油保护。 The fracture mode at the origin 
area has brittle (intergranular) overload charactaristics (figure 4).  At the origin is a shallow deformation, which 
occurred prior to, or at the same time as the fracture.  Due to lack of corrosion, the timeframe cannot be estimated, other 
than to say it must have had some protective oil applied before or very soon after fracture.
