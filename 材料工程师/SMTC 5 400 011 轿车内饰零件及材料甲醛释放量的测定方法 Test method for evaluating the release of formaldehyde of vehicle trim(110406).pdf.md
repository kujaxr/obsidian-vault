3 测试原理及方法 
将特定质量与尺寸的试样固定于装在密封
的1 L 聚乙烯瓶中的去离子水之上，按规定时
间存放于恒温状态下。 然后，将瓶子冷却并确
定去离子水所吸收的甲醛量。所确定的甲醛量
同试样的干重（干料）有关，采用乙酰丙酮分
光光度法测定。在水溶液中，甲醛同乙酰丙酮
及铵离子起反应生成3,5-联乙酰-1,4-双氢卢
剔啶（3,5-diacetyl-1,4-dihydrolutidine 
(DDL)，合成物为黄色，波长为412 nm时，吸收
率最大。 
 
3  Test Principle and Method 
Samples of the given quality and size are 
placed in a sealed 1 liter polyethylene bottle 
with 50ml de-ionised water. This is then placed 
in an oven at constant temperature according 
to the stipulated time. The bottle is then cooled 
down, and the quantity of formaldehyde ab-
sorbed by the de-ionized water is determined. 
The quantity of formaldehyde is related to the 
dry weight of the samples, and determined 
using the acetyl acetone spectrophotometry 
method. In water solution, formaldehyde reacts 
with acetyl acetone and ammonium ion and 
generates 3, 5-diacetyl-1, 4-dihydrolutidine 
(DDL). The compound is yellow. When the 
wave length is 412nm, the absorption rate is 
maximised.

5 试样及其准备 
5  Samples and Their Preparations 
5.1 试样获取 
试样应在均匀分布在成型件上的适当的、
具有代表性的点上采集，但必须在距成型件的
边50 mm 处获取。 
5.1  Sample Pretreatment 
Samples should be collected at suitable 
and representative points evenly distributed on 
molded pieces, and the points should be 50 
mm away from edges of molded pieces.  
5.2 试样准备 
5.2  Sample Preparation 
5.2.1  每个试样的尺寸为40 mm×100 mm×样
品厚度，如材料厚度大于20 mm，应将试样的厚
度准确切割成20 mm厚。如因成型件的大小而无
法按此尺寸取样，或无法保证在距成型件边缘
50 mm 处取样，测试也可采用较小的试样进行。
取样时必须小心，保证所有试样的边缘切割完
好，为了固定试样，应在与钩子直径相应的中
心开孔，距顶边10 mm。 
 
5.2.1  The size of each sample should be 40 
mm×100 mm ×samples thickness. If the 
thickness of material is above 20 mm, samples 
should be cut into pieces of 20 mm thick ac-
curately. If samples of the size can not be 
made due to the size of molded pieces, or 
made at the points 50 mm away from their 
edges, test can also be conducted with smaller 
samples. Samples should be collected care-
fully so as to ensure that their edges are cut 
straightly. To fix samples, a hole should be 
opened at the center corresponding with the 
diameter of the hook, 10mm away from top 
edge.

5.2.2  每个成型件上至少要采集3 个试样，2 
个用于确定甲醛量，1 个用于确定含水量。如
果测定的甲醛释放量不小于限定值的75%，则应
再次进行测定。 
5.2.2
At least three samples should be col-
lected on each molded piece, with two for de-
termining the quantity of formaldehyde, and 
one for determining the water content. If the 
determined quantity of formaldehyde is not 
less than 75% of the limited value, it should be 
tested again.  
5.3 其他 
如试样的测试无法立即进行，应将其存放
于阴冷密封条件下，空气量应尽可能最小，存
放时间不得超过5 天。 
5.3  Others 
If samples can not be tested, they should 
be placed in the cold and sealed conditions. Air 
volume should be small as much as possible, 
and the placing time should not exceed 5 days.

6.2.1 试验方法 
6.2.1  Test  Method 
6.2.1.1  在分析开始前，试样应在精度为0.01 
g 的分析天平上称重，每个1 L 聚乙烯瓶中均用
吸量管加入50 ml 的去离子水。一旦试样挂在钩
子上，瓶子应密封并存放在（60±3）℃的加热
柜中3 h±15 min。 
 
6.2.1.1  Before the start of analysis, samples 
should be weighed on analysis scale with the 
precision being 0.01 g. De-ionized water is 
injected into each polyethylene bottle with pi-
pette. Once samples are fixed on the hook, the 
bottle should be sealed and placed in (60±3)℃ 
heating cabinet for 3 h±15 min. 
6.2.1.2  当达到测试时间后，将瓶子从加热柜
中取出，经过（60±10）min 后温度下降至23 ℃
±5 ℃，再将样品从瓶中取出，再进行一次无
试样的空白测试。 
6.2.1.2  When the test time is reached, the 
bottle is taken out from the heating cabinet, 
and the temperature is lowered to (23±5) ℃ 
after 60±10 min. And then, samples are taken 
out from the bottle, a blank test ( without sam-
ple) should also be performed.
