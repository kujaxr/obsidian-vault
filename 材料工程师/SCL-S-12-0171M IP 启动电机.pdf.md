Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）螺栓是由于载荷过大而断裂的。The bolt was broken because of overload. 
（2）失效件和抽样件螺牙底部过渡不好，并且抽样件底部有折叠。The transition of the teeth root is not good; there 
are folds on the teeth roots of the samples. The defect is not the direct cause for the failure. 
（3）与抽样对比，组织和硬度正常。The microstructure and hardness are normal, according to comparison samples. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查实际装配操作中是否可能存在扭矩过大或非正常的润滑。Check if it is possible a big torque or 
abnormal lubricant was applied during the assembly process.  
（2）在供应商那里，抽取10 件进行断裂扭矩的测试。Make a torque test for 10 samples in Valeo. 
（3）供应商应该维修模具，以确保螺牙尺寸和底部过渡良好，无折叠。Supplier should amend the die and 
ensure the dimension and a good transition on the teeth root and no folding.
