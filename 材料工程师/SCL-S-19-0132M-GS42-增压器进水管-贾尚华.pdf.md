Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  增压器进水管因疲劳开裂而失效，这与试验载荷较大有关。 
The intake pipe of supercharger fails due to fatigue cracking, which is related to the larger test load. 
2.  增压器进水管的材料成分、硬度满足要求，钎焊部分填充良好。 
The material composition and hardness of the intake pipe of the supercharger meet the requirements, and the brazing 
part is well filled. 
 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核增压器进水管的局部应力。Check the local stress of supercharger intake pipe.
