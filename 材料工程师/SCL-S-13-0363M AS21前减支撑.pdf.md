Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1.该失效件与之前失效案例相同：螺栓滚花凸台及支撑板上凸于橡胶面，与车身直接干涉、接触，导致其
发生疲劳断裂。The failed model was same with the former. Bolt knurling and the plate were above the rubber 
plane and contacted with the body, resulted in the fatigue fracture. 
2. 支撑内部骨架厚度符合要求；The thickness of steel plate met the requirement. 
3. 材料正常；The material of steel plate was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.优化设计，保证滚花凸台及支撑板低于橡胶平面；Optimized the design. Make sure the knurling and the supporting 
plate were below the rubber plane. 
2.支撑骨架材质DD13 为软钢，建议更改为高强度钢；The material of top mount DD13 was mild steel, suggesting 
changing to the high strength steel.
