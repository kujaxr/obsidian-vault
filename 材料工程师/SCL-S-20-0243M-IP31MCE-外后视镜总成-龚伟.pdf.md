Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件螺钉柱与金属支座间存在间隙，装配后根部过度拉伸，导致壳体开裂。 
There was a gap between the failure screw column and the metal support, and the root was 
excessively stretched after assembly, causing the shell to crack.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  短期措施：零件支座与螺钉柱件增加一1mm垫片。 
                    Short-term measures: add a 1mm spacer for the component support and the screw column. 
2.  长期措施：优化零件尺寸结构。 
Long-term measures: optimize the size and structure of parts.
