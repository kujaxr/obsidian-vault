Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 机油泵从动轴与齿由于发生粘着磨损而最终咬死，尤其润滑油口最为严重。The drive shaft and gear of oil pump 
adhesively worn and In particular, the lubrication oil passage was most serious. 
 
2. 从动轴成分、硬度、硬化层深度符合要求，组织正常；从动齿硬度、密度符合要求，组织正常。Chemical 
components of driven shaft, hardness, harden case met the requirement and microstructure was normal. Hardness, 
density of driven gear met the requirement and microstructure was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 排查系统的清洁度。Check the system cleanliness.  
2. 调查从动轴与齿孔的匹配间隙。Check the matching gap between the driven shaft and the gear.
