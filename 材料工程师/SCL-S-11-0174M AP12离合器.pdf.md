Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）由于摩擦产生高温导致内工作面和局部的外工作面出现高的综合内应力，导致表面裂纹的产生。High temperature  
is caused by friction and created high combined stress on the inside working surface and outside working surface, which caused the cracks. 
（2）其综合内应力与热应力，工作应力以及铸造内应力等有关。The combined stress arises from thermal stress, working stress  
, inner stress from casting and so on. 
（3）压盘的石墨形态和长度级别不满足技术要求，但是这不是导致压盘出现裂纹的直接原因。The graphite shape and 
length don’t meet the technology requirement of the pressure plate, but it didn’t lead to the cracks directly. 
（4）压盘的珠光体含量，渗碳体含量以及硬度满足产品的技术要求。The pearlite and cementite content and hardness meet the 
technology requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）建议供应商控制好工艺参数，改善材料的组织。Supplier must control the process well and improve the material microstructure.

工作面上裂纹的产生与材料所承受的应力有关。该应力与热应力，工作载荷应力以及铸造应力等有关。几种
应力的综合作用导致了工作面上裂纹的产生。The cracks are related to the total material stress, which is because of heat stress, working 
load stress, casting stress and so on. The combined stress led to the cracks on the working surface. 
工作面中间区域的综合应力相对较低，所以虽然工作面中间区域的组织过烧最严重，但是未出现裂纹。裂纹
出现在应力较大的内工作面以及外工作面。The combined stress on the middle region is lower than other ones. Although the microstructure 
on the middle region is seriously overheated, there are no cracks. All of the cracks are on the inside and outside working surface with big stress. 
 
说明：产品工程师已查明其根本原因是由于摩擦片材料的不匹配导致工作中滑动摩擦的产生，从而产生高温，引
起表面工作组织的变化，导致了最终裂纹的产生。与此同时，产品工程师已进行摩擦材料更改的验证性试验。鉴
于此，未对材料的成分进行检测。 
Note: The DRE has found the root reason that the material of the disc is not appropriate for the pressure plate which led to sliding movement during the 
working, high temperature and consequently made the plate crack. At the same time, the DRE has begun to carry out the experiment to validate the new 
material. Accordingly the chemical component of the material is not tested.
