Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）单向阀是由于疲劳而断裂；起源于内表面。The valve was broken because of fatigue; the fracture began from the 
inside surface.  
（2）硬度正常。The hardness is normal. 
（3）金相检测结果未见异常。The metallographic test result is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）请DRE 考虑试验过程是否正常。DRE should think if the test process is normal. 
（2）请DRE 对经过相同测试的单向阀进行检测，看是否有裂纹。Please check other unilateral valves undergoing the 
same experiment and tell if there were cracks.
