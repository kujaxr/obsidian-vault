1 
Scope 
This Technical Supply Specification defines the fuel requirements of diesel fuel. 
2 
Definition 
The diesel fuels described in the following consist of a distilled hydrocarbon base and contain 
additives which are soluble in hydrocarbons for quality improvement as well as, for certain types, 
fatty acid methyl ester (biodiesel) additives. 
3 
Description 
Description example for diesel fuel according to DIN EN 590, including additives, but without fatty 
acid methyl ester (biodiesel) components 
Diesel fuel acc. to TL 788-A 
4 
Requirements 
4.1 
General requirements 
Approval of first supply and changes according to Volkswagen standard VW 01155. 
Avoidance of hazardous substances according to VW 91101. 
At least 2,5 l are required for complete testing. 
Diesel fuels must comply with DIN EN 590. 
In as-received condition, the fuels shall look clear and free of turbidity when visually inspected. 
Furthermore, the requirements stated in Section 4 must be met. 
A manufacturer’s test certificate (see Appendix A) must be prepared for release. 
The English translation is believed to be accurate.In case of discrepancies the German version shall govern. 
Numerical notation according to ISO practice (see VW 01000).

4.3 
Types and standard part numbers 
For operation of vehicle diesel engines and for supply of in-plant gas stations and engine test 
benches with three different types: without index, A, B. 
For initial break-in operation in the plants building engines, with a separate type C. 
For factory filling of vehicles with diesel engines during vehicle production, with separate types: X, 
Y.

4.4 
Shelf life 
Shelf life in unopened original packaging between -20 °C and +30 °C shall be > 6 months. 
4.5 
Composition and properties 
4.5.1 
TL 788 
Diesel fuel according to DIN EN 590 (including additive). 
4.5.2 
TL 788-A 
Diesel fuel according to DIN EN 590 (including additive) without fatty acid methyl ester 
components. 
The maximum permissible fatty acid methyl ester content lies below 0,2 volume percent.

4.5.3 
TL 788-B 
Diesel fuel according to DIN EN 590 (including additive) with defined fatty acid methyl ester 
content. 
Required fatty acid methyl ester content is 5,0 volume percent. 
4.5.4 
TL 788-C 
Diesel fuel for the initial break-in operation of engines according to DIN EN 590 with additive and 
with improved lubricity of WSD < 400 µm according to DIN EN ISO 12156-1. Max. 5% (V/V) FAME.

Diesel fuel comprising pure hydrocarbons with defined cloud point and improved lubricity for factory

TL 788-Y 
For factory filling of vehicles with diesel engines during vehicle production similar to TL 788-X, but

4.6 
Additives 
Additives must be optimized for the following properties: 
─ 
cleaning effect on injectors 
─ 
corrosion protection 
─ 
prevention of foaming 
The value range of the silicon content shall be stated in the test certificate. 
4.7 
Special type “sulfur content < 350 mg/kg” 
In any case, the general "sulfur content < 50 mg/kg" type shall be requested from the supplier; this 
type is to be preferred. 
In plants outside the European Union the special type “sulfur content < 350 mg/kg” may be used or 
remain in use (corresponding to TL 788, 2004-10) after release by the Volkswagen Group 
Laboratory in Wolfsburg in special cases, i.e. if the general type is unavailable.
