Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 前副车架焊缝边缘上裂纹属于多源疲劳裂纹，裂纹从上片向下片方向扩展，裂纹位于零件的高应力区。The crack 
in the welding of front sub frame assembly was several resource crack. The crack propagated from the above plate to the 
low plate and it was in the high stress region 
2. 开裂部位上片焊缝熔深偏低，匹配间隙正常。Fusion in the crack region of the above plate is low and the matching  
gap is normal. 
3. 下板材料性能正常。The material property of the low plate is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核设计载荷，评估安全系数。Check the design load and evaluate the safety coefficient.  
2. 关注上片熔深。Pay attention to the fusion of the above plate.
