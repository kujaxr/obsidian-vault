Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，失效件的材料无异常。 
2. 装车前橡胶管受到外界异物刮擦导致开裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and the material test, it can be seen that the pipe was broken by an outside scratch 
before it is installed on vehicle. 
建议：防止卡箍和工具刮擦橡胶管表面。 
Suggestions：The scratches caused by the metal loop and assemble tool should be prohibited.

小结：经测试，失效件的内侧材料和外侧材料为CSM，材料符合设计要求。外层材料的力学性能满足设计要

Conclusion: The material of the rubber pipe is CSM which conforms to the design requirement. The mechanical 
property of exterior layer rubber conforms to the design requirement. And the tensile elongation of interior 
layer rubber is lower compared with that of requirement. But the different value is very small, so the 
mechanical property of interior layer rubber also conforms to the design requirement.
