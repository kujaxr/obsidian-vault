Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、装配密封面受力不均，使镀层剥落面，致使冷却油管漏油。 
 
   The leakage failure was due to uneven contact between sealing faces during assembly. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查装配工艺，确保密封面结合正确。 
Check Assembly proces, to ensure that the pipes are aligned correctly before tightening.
