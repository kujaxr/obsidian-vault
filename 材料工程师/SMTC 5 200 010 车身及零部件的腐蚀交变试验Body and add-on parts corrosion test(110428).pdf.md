板和带有不同耐腐蚀保护涂层的零部件的

特性和评定防腐保护措施。 
 
1  Scope  
This Specification is used for corrosion testing 
on completely painted bodies, body panels, 
assemblies 
and 
components 
with 
differing 
anti-corrosion coatings. 
The standard serves to monitor and evaluate 
corrosion 
behavior 
or 
corrosion 
protection 
measures of these parts when exposed to static 
load.

a combination of various climatic and/or corrosion 
loads. A test cycle consists of：

3.1.2  用此试验方法，评定静态负荷下通
过盐雾，湿度和温度交替变化的腐蚀特性。
在上述腐蚀条件下，能够对不同的腐蚀保护
涂层进行比较评定。  
 
3.1.2  Using this test method, corrosion behavior 
under static load resulting from salt, humidity and 
temperature is evaluated. Under these defined 
corrosion 
conditions, 
different 
anti-corrosion 
coatings can be comparatively assessed. 
3.1.3  由于在汽车上出现的损伤现象和腐
蚀过程是综合的，其腐蚀作用之间是相关联
系的，而非固定不变的，该试验并没有完全
掌握和反映出腐蚀的负荷现象。  
 
3.1.3  A correlation with the corrosion mechanism 
in actual driving operation is not necessarily 
possible, as the types of vehicle damage and 
corrosion curves are complex and are not 
completely covered and reflected by the changing 
corrosion load. 
3.1.4  允许为了检查和/或选择试样而使
试验有一个短暂中断。但不允许由于改变试
验程序而导致试验中断。 
3.1.4  A short interruption of the test for inspection 
purposes and/or the removal of test specimens is 
permitted. Interrupting the test by changing the test 
program is not permitted.

3.3 车身试验 
 
3.3  Body test 
试验过程符合第3.1节要求。在第3.6节
中有引用示例。为了进行车身空腔的一个特

台上的车身，在首次进入盐喷雾室内之前，

Testing is performed according to Section 3.1. 
Examples are given in Section 3.6. In order to 
carry out a specific evaluation of cavities, the body 
can be turned. Before being initially brought into 
the salt spray test chamber, the body, mounted on 
the pivoting frame, is preconditioned in the DIN 
50014–23/50-2 standard climate, and is then 
moved into the test chamber kept at a constant 
temperature.

3.3.1  Salt spray phase 
在采用旋转方式的试验过程时，进行每

继续旋转车身，以便依次进行以下角度位置
试验：0º，45º，135º，225º和315º。 
 
During the test sequence with rotation, before 
each salt spray cycle, the body is turned about its 
lengthwise axis so that it is positioned as in the 
following sequence: 0°, 45°, 135°, 225° and 315°.

3.4 零部件试验 
 
3.4  Component test 
一个完整试验至少需要5个零部件。零
部件或其截面依据ISO 9227。必须通过分离
/打开车身使得盐喷雾可以接触到空腔内
部。必要时在试验前必须对无保护措施的边
缘部分进行覆盖。  
 
The required minimum for complete testing is 
5 parts. The components or their sections are 
tested in accordance with ISO 9227. Cavities must 
be exposed to the salt spray fog by cutting/opening 
of parts. If necessary, Un-protected trim edges 
must be covered before testing. 
3.5 评价 
 
3.5  Evaluation 
3.5.1  一般在15/30/60和90次循环试验
后，试样的评定关系到：  
a)  腐蚀方式（覆盖层和/或母体金属
腐蚀）； 
b)  腐蚀类型（表面-或边缘生锈）；
c)  腐蚀出现的时间及发展， 以及腐
蚀保护层状态的其它变化，例如：
单边扩散，气泡等； 
d)  按照DIN EN ISO 4628-1中的表3 评
价有机涂敷层；  
e)  油漆钢板上的腐蚀扩散，可以采用
无损热成像方法对缺陷位置进行
定量的测定。  
3.5.1 After 15, 30, 60 and 90 cycles, the speci-
mens are evaluated with regard to 
a)  Type of corrosion (coating and/or base 
metal corrosion), 
b)  Form of corrosion (surface or edge corro-
sion), 
c)  Time of appearance and development of 
corrosion, as well as with regard to addi-
tional changes in the anti-corrosion coat-
ing such as loss of adhesion or blistering.
d)  Organic coatings can be evaluated ac-
cording to DIN EN ISO 4628-1 Table 3. 
e)  Signs of rust creep on painted sheets can 
be examined using a non-destructive 
thermographic method for quantitative . 
3.5.2  按照DIN EN ISO 4628-3的评价为
Ri5级或锈蚀穿孔后，试验结束。在试验记
录中必须说明：  
 
a)  试验零件（例如 油漆钢板）； 
b)  防腐蚀措施（例如 油漆（涂层厚
度：钢板中间测量值96 μm））；
c)  试验： 开始/结束； 
d)  评价； 
e)  基体金属腐蚀开始（例如 在经过
15次循环试验后：表面腐蚀Ri 2
级）； 
f)  气泡形成（例如：在经过18次循环
试验后：起泡，级别2级（S4））；
g)  单边扩散（例如：D＝3 mm）； 
h)  试验结束（例如：在30次循环试验
后）；  
i)  说明 / 注释。 
3.5.2  Evaluation of the development and spread 
of defects. The test is concluded when corrosion 
grade Ri5 according to DIN EN ISO 4628-3 or 
corrosion perforations occur. The following must 
be stated in the test report: 
a)  Parts examined : e.g., painted sheets; 
b)  Corrosion protection measures: e.g., paint 
finish (coating thickness: 96 μm meas-
ured in the sheet center); 
c)  Test: Start/end; 
d)  Evaluation; 
e)  Start of base metal corrosion: e.g., after 
15 cycles: surface corrosion Ri 2; 
f)  Blistering: e.g., after 18 cycles: blistering 
grade 2 (S4); 
g)  Adhesion loss: e.g., D = 3 mm; 
h)  End of test. e.g., after 30 cycles; 
i)  Notes / remarks.

4  Note on testing 
4.1 盐雾试验  
在已有防腐蚀保护的车身，车身钢板，
零部件上必须划线露出金属的基体材料。 
4.1 Salt spray fog test
The coated bodies, body panels, components 
and assemblies must be scribed through to the 
metallic base material.
