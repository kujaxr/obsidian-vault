able SMTC to effectively control material 
requirements of PC+ABS polymer, finished 
parts, and provide material basis for the com-

passenger cars of SMTC.

要求。

1  Scope  
The Standard stipulates material require-
ments of finished parts made of ABS + PC 
polymer blends. 
This standard applies to the material re-
quirements of finished parts made of ABS+PC 
polymer blends.

4  General requirements 
4.1 外观要求 
4.1
Appearance requirements
成品件表面与内部必须完全没有像流线、裂
纹、缩孔等类似空缺位置和制造缺陷。只有当凹
陷不影响功能与外观和/或可安装性时，才允许
在筋条与加强筋的安装范围内有凹陷。成品件必
须可以保障无缺陷安装。在仓储与使用后，不允
许出现非抗静电的塑料所特有的积尘现象。 
Surface and interior of the finished parts 
shall be free of flaws and processing defects 
such as flowlines, voids, cracks or similar 
faults. Sink marks at the base of ribs and 
reinforcements are permissible only if they do 
not adversely affect either the function or ap-
pearance and/or assembly of the part. The 
finished parts shall permit proper assembly. 
After storage and use, they shall not exhibit 
the dust accumulation which is characteristic 
for non-antistatic plastics.

6  Test requirements 
6.1 弯曲强度 
6.1
Flexural strength
确定在最大力矩上的弯曲应力按照DIN EN 
ISO 178，试件长度：（50 ± 1）mm；宽度：（6 ± 
0.2 ）mm；厚度符合产品厚度要求（最大4 mm）；
跨距：40 mm；接触半径（1.0～1.2）mm；试验
速度：（14 ± 1）mm/min。平放在试验设备的支
架上的已经过粗加工的一侧表面处理的成品必
须是表面处理的一侧，确保皮纹面受压。 
    Determination of the flexural stress at 
maximum load according to DIN EN ISO 178; 
deviating specimen length: (50 ± 1) mm, width: 
(6 ± 0.2) mm; thickness according to product 
thickness(up to max. 4 mm); support separa-
tion: 40 mm; support radius: 1.0 mm to 1.2 
mm; test rate:(14 ± 1) mm/min. Specimens 
taken from molded parts with one-sided 
graining shall be placed on the test machine 
support so that the grained side faces the 
pressure fin.

6.5  耐光照性能 
6.5
Light fastness
如果在图纸中对光照周期数目未作规定，则
适用于下列规定： 
– 在非阳光直接照射区域中的构件为三个
光照周期； 
– 在阳光直接照射区域中的构件为五个光
照周期（例如：车门胸区）； 
– 阳光强烈照射区域中的构件为十个光照
周期（例如：后搁板）。 
If the number of exposure periods is not 
defined in the drawing, the following rule ap-
plies: 
─  3 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to indirect sun radiation; 
─  5 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to direct sun radiation (e.g., 
door upper trim); 
─ 10 periods of exposure for components 
in areas subject to the highest sun radiation 
(e.g., rear shelf).
