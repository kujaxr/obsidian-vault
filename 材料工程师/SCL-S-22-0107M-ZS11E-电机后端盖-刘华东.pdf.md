Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、电机后端盖内螺纹孔滑牙是由于拧紧时轴向力过大引起。The tooth in the internal thread hole was caused by 
excessive axial force during tightening. 
2、电机后端盖硬度正常，螺栓摩擦系数符合要求。The hardness of the rear end cover of the motor is normal, and the 
friction coefficient of the bolts meets the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、排查装配过程是否有异常。Check whether the assembly process is abnormal.
