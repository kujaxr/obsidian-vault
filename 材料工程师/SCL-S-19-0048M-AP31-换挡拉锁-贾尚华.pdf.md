Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1. 换挡拉索受到较大载荷而发生过载断裂。 
    The overload fracture of the shift lock is caused by large load. 
2．材料的强度未见异常。The strength of the material is not abnormal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1．排查供应商的生产过程，并关注后续类似失效。Check the supplier's production process and pay attention to 
follow-up similar failures.
