Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 倒档惰轮与低速档同步器齿套啮合不良导致齿发生过载断裂。The gear asm-rev idler and the sleeve clench the 
teeth poorly, which results in the gears overload fracture. 
2、 倒档惰轮与低速档同步器齿套化学成分、硬度和金相组织符合图纸要求；倒档惰轮齿根非马组织不符合标准
要求。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure of the gear asm-rev idler and the sleeve meet the 
requirements of drawing; but the non-martensitic structure of gear asm-rev idler does not meet the standard. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、进一步排查齿轮啮合不良原因。Further investigate the reasons which cause the gears clench the teeth poorly.  
2、要求倒档惰轮供应商控制好齿轮非马组织。Urge the gear supplier to control the non-martensitic structure.
