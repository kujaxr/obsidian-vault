Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions: 
1、 由于燃烧室温度过高（如爆震燃烧）引起的火花塞断裂。 
The failure for the Spark plug due to the high temperature in the combustion chamber (such as the 
detonation combustion). 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1、 提供发动机完整的失效件进行失效分析。 
Provides complete engine failure parts for failure analysis. 
2、 检查、分析燃烧室温度过高的原因。 
Check and analysis of the combustion chamber temperature
