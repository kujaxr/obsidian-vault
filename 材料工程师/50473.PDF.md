PURPOSE
This Standard covers test procedures to determine gasoline resistance and film polymerization of paint
products applied as per Procurement Specification, on test panel (method A) or on the part itself (meth-
od B).
2
PROCEDURE
2.1
Test environment (unless otherwise specified)
Temperature :
23 ± 5 qC
Atmospheric pressure :
860 to 1060 mbar
Relative humidity :
45 to 70 %
2.2
Method “A” (by contact)
2.2.1
At 23 r 2qC place a wad of cotton wool soaked in gasoline (Std. 55511) on test panel in horizontal posi-
tion. Cover wad with a 100 c.c. beaker.After 30 min. remove beaker and wad and leave surface under
test to dry in air for 24 h. After this period, wash test panel with water, dry with filter paper and inspect
surface under test.
2.3
Method “B” (by rubbing)
2.3.1
At 23 r 2qC soak a wad of cotton wool in gasoline (Std. 55511) and rub on any visible painted area of
the part for ~ 10 s exerting light manual pressure of ~ 15 to 20 N. Inspect wad for any traces of color.
