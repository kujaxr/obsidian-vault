Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 该支架失效是由于加强板采用了不正确的原材料；使用的是低碳钢（约85HV），而不是不锈钢（约200HV）。
The bracket has failed due to incorrect material used for the reinforcement plate. Low-carbon steel (~85HV) was 
used instead of stainless steel (HV~200).   
2、 疲劳裂纹起源于强度低的加强板，然后扩展通过支架，引起失效。The low strength material allowed fatigue 
cracking to start in the reinforcement plate, which spread through the bracket, causing failure. 
3、 低碳钢是不适合这种应用，因为它的低强度和高温下的高氧化率。Low carbon steel is unsuitable for this 
application, due to it’s low strength and high oxidation rate at temperature. 
4、 支架材料符合图纸要求。The bracket material met the drawing requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 整个支架使用正确的材料。Use the correct specified material for the whole bracket.

金相分析Microstructure analysis 
图5 为通过裂纹源头点的横截面。腐蚀后，两个板材之间的金相组织的差异是明显的：加强板有铁素体的组织，
而不是正常的奥氏体晶粒结构。显微硬度测试结果显示：加强板的硬度只有约85HV1；支架材料的硬度约
200HV1。A cross section was taken through the fracture origin (figure 5).  On etching, the difference in microstructure 
between the two panels was clear, with the reinforcement plate having a ferritic microstructure, not the correct austenite 
grain structure.  Microhardness testing was then carried out, and revealed that the reinforcement plate had a hardness of 
only ~85HV1, compared to the ~200HV1 of the bracket material.

材料分析Material analysis 
支架材料化学成分和金相组织符合要求。硬度偏高，但要求的目的只是为了制造的，并不会对产品的性能产生负
面影响。加强板是普通的低碳钢，而不是所需的不锈钢。硬度（拉伸强度）相应地较低。 
The bracket material met the requirements of the drawing in terms of chemical composition and microstructure. 
Hardness was slightly high, however the requirement is only for the purpose of part manufacture, and did not negatively 
affect its performance. The reinforcement plate was found to be plain low carbon steel, not the required stainless steel.
