Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 尾门内板焊点和钣金开裂均为疲劳开裂；The cracking of the welding spot and metal palate is fatigue cracking. 
2、 发生开裂的焊点熔核直径符合要求，旁边四个焊点中有一点焊核直径偏小；The nugget diameter of the cracked 
spots meet the requirements, and the four welding spots have one is smaller. 
3、 内板化学成分符合要求，硬度金相正常。The chemical material of the plate meets the requirement; the hardness 
and microstructure are normal. 
  
建议Suggestions： 
1、调整焊接工艺，确保焊接正常；Adjust welding procedure to ensure spot welding normal. 
2、优化焊点布置，降低焊点位置应力。 Optimize the layouts of welding spots, and reduce the stress at the welding 
spots location.
