Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过宏观分析初步可以判定，异物进入金属管，造成金属管堵塞，转向系统不能及时卸压（正常工
作压力10 Bar），导致转向回油管内压力持续升高，当达到回油管的爆破极限值时（70~80 Bar），回油
管发生爆破。 
      2．接下来进行失效件的材料、力学性能测试和供应商装配现场工艺排查。 
Conclusions: 1. According to the macroscopic observation, an external metal piece are found in the metal pipe. 
During work, the external metal piece blocked the metal pipe. So the pressure of steering system can’t 
decrease in time (Normal working pressure is 10 Bar). The pipe was broken while the inner pressure reached 
the limited break pressure （70~80 Bar）.         
2. The mechanical property and material of the failure part and the manufacture process inspection of the pipe 
will be done later.
