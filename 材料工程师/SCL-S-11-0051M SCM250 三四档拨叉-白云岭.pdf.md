Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1,由于收到异常载荷导致了失效件过载断裂。The failure has occurred through overload, due to abnormal force during operation.  
2,端口附近有打磨痕迹，致使该部位应力集中。A notch was ground during manufacture, which is not according to drawing.  This 
has caused a stress concentration point and weakened the part. 
3,化学成分、球化率、基体组织符合技术要求。The chemical composition, graphite nodularity and microstructure meet the 
requirements. 
4,芯部硬度和珠光体含量不符合技术要求，但实测的结果不会影响零件的失效，请重新考虑芯部硬度和珠光体含量的技术要求。The 
core hardness & pearlite content do not meet the requirement. However, this has not affected the failure.  These limits are 
considered relatively low considering the application.  
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请进一步校核使用工况的受力情况。Check the forces applied during running. 
2、 优化设计。Improve machining of the failured part (remove notch). 
3、 试验优化后 请在图纸中明确金相组织和硬度的技术要求。It is recommended that the requirements for microstructure 
and hardness are changed on the drawing, after successful testing has been carried out.
