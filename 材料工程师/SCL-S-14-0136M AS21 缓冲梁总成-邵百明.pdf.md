Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）横梁在大的冲击下，局部承受大的载荷而发生剧烈变形，并导致横梁瞬间拉断。The front intrusion beam’s local 
bore a big load under a big impact and got a severe deformation, which made the beam broken fleetly under tension. 
（2）1#焊缝不符合要求，但不是导致横梁断裂的原因。The weld 1 didn’t meet the weld requirement, but this was not 
the failure reason. 
（3）横梁的材料成分，机械性能符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and mechanical properties met the 
requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
（4）封板的成分符合要求；强度和组织无异常。The chemical components meet the requirement; the strength and 
microstructure are normal.  
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化系统设计，以达到系统碰撞能量的合理分配与吸收。Optimize the system design to achieve the reasonable 
collision energy allocation and absorption. 
（2）弧焊会降低了热成型钢的局部性能，请在结构设计时加以考虑。Welding can reduce the local properties of hot 
forming steel; please pay attention to it during the structure design.
