2 断口分析（Analyze）： 
 
零件为多层开裂，可推断为材料强度下降导致。 
There are several cracks in the failure part, and we can conclude that this is caused by the decline of the material strength. 
 
零件断面是充实的，未发现气孔，杂质及胶料不均匀性分布等不良现象，可以判定零件开裂不是由于以上原因造成的。 
We did not discover the bubble, impurities and the uneven distribution of the material and etc undesirable phenomena on the 
failure part. The conclusion that the brake of the part should not be caused by such reason can be get. 
 
3 生产过程分析(Analyze of the production) 
生产现场发现零件脱模时, 如果工人用力过大，零件可能会弹到注射机的注射口挡板上。由于注射口温度设定约为180-200℃，
若对此环节忽视的情况下，零件将受到高温老化，验证实验结果如下： 
The finished part will fly to the plate above the injection port occasionally when the worker pull the part using too much force in the 
stripping procedure. Because the temperature is set between 180-200℃, the part is aged before assembly if this phenomena is ignored. The 
validation test is as follow:

试验结果可以发现，180℃×8h 老化后零件的拉断力明显下降，24h 后材料强度将继续降低。由此可以推断，失效件在装配前已
经过长时间的高温老化，导致强度下降，在使用过程中开裂。 
From the test result, the brake strength of the part is obviously declined after aged in 180℃×8h, which means the part is aged. The brake 
strength will declined continuously after 24h ageing. So we deduce that the finished part is aged for a long time before assembly and brake in 
the usage. 
建议在脱模口处增加挡板，杜绝零件在脱模时飞溅到注射头挡板上。 
We insist that the plate should be installed to prevent the finished part flying to the plate above the injection port.
