Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1．车架悬置管子由于硬度偏高，镦扩不到位，导致与车身发生线接触。Due to abnormal high hardness of 
Tube Assy-Body No.4 and head expanding didn’t meet the required dimension, tube touch the body. 
2．悬置管子与车身的干涉是车身底板开裂的主要原因之一。The interference between suspension tube and 
body is one of the main reason of body crack. 
建议Suggestions： 
1．加强控制悬置零件的尺寸与原材料质量。Suspension component material and dimension should be 
strictly monitored. 
2．校核车身悬置安装部位的应力分布与疲劳强度。Stress distribution and fatigue strength of suspension 
component mounting area should be analyzed and verified.
