Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：胶管材料符合要求。 
Conclusion: the material of the hose conforms to the standard. 
胶管内层和外层同时受到损伤，并在使用中导致胶管漏油。 
The cover and tube of the hose was damaged already and caused  the leaking of the hose during the usage.  
建议：考虑到胶管受伤处均在卡箍附近，故在安装环节中避免对胶管形成损伤，并确保衬管表面无毛刺。 
Suggestion: considering the damaged area of the hose is near the clap, so assure the hose prevented being damaged during the installation 
and the surface of the tube free of burr.
