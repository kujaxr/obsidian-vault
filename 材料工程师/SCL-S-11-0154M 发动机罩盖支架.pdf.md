Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 通过支架的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，支架与基体结合较好，异常外力导致支架在根部断裂。 
Conclusions: After testing, the material of the bracket conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic dimension observation and material test of the bracket, it can be seen that the connection 
between the bracket and engine cover is good and the break of bracket was caused by an abnormal external 
force.  
建议：避免发动机罩盖受异常外力作用。 
Suggestions：The excessive external force on the engine plastic cover should be avoided.

小结：经测试，支架失效件的材料符合设计要求。支架材料第一次熔融过程和第二次熔融过程中的熔融焓变
化不大，零件的注塑工艺参数较好。 
Conclusion: After the tests, the material of the bracket conforms to the design requirement. The change of ΔH 
between the first circle and the second circle of the bracket part is small, so the injection process of it is good.  
Ⅳ. 尺寸与宏观分析 Dimention and Macroscopic analysis 
 
图2 是断口整体形貌，可以看出支架在与基体连接处发生断裂（支架齐根断裂），断口表面没有空洞、夹杂等
缺陷，支架与基体连接较好。图3 是断口的局部放大图，图中有一明显的支架断裂后将基体材料撕扯掉的断口，
从而可以判定支架沿着图中箭头所示方向断裂，而正常状态支架只受垂直基体面方向的力。 
Fig 2 is the whole crack surface, it can be seen that the fracture of bracket occurred at the linking region (also is the 
bottom region of bracket) and no defects such as voids and including are found on the crack surface. Fig 3 is the 
magnified part of crack, a part of engine cover was torn off by the fracture of bracket which indicates the break direction 
of bracket as the arrow. At normal conditions, the bracket undertake a force which vertical to the engine cover.
