Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件受外力后断裂。 
The failure part was fractured by external force.  
                 2.  零件含水率低，未进行调湿工艺。 
                     Moisture content of the part is low, due to no humidity control in production process. 
                 3.  零件转角位置未设计倒角。 
                     There is no R-angle in corner position. 
4.  失效件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of failure part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  增加调湿工艺。                       2.  零件转角位置增加倒角设计。 
                 Humidity control should be added.             R-angle should be used in corner position.
