Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 排气管通过疲劳断裂。发现之前，已断裂了很长的时间。The exhaust pipe failed through fatigue, a significant time 
before the fracture was discovered.   
2. 发现了多个疲劳裂纹的起源点，然而由于氧化严重，因此不能判断这些起源点是否形成了主要的疲劳裂纹。
Fatigue crack origins were found, however, due to heavy oxidation, it cannot be said whether these formed the main 
fatigue crack. 
3. 材料和焊接合格。The materials and weld were acceptable. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查类似行驶里程的车辆，看此区域是否有裂纹。Inspect similar mileage vehicles for cracking in this position. 
2. 为了降低焊接处的受力，排气管法兰总成需要做尺寸或者涉及影响模态的改进。To reduce loading force on 
welding area, flange and pipe assemble design (dimension, shoulder position etc.) should be improved.

断口分析Fracture analysis 
由于高温和废气的冲刷，断口的整个表面被严重氧化。因为没有新鲜断口，很明显，管断裂的时间已经很
长，也许甚至几千公里。断裂处于焊接的区域，并且断口已氧化；这使得断口分析非常困难。图2 显示了
断口的全貌。 
The whole surface of the fracture was heavily oxidised, due to the high temperature and agressive exhaust gases.  
Since there was no fresh fracture, it is clear that the part had been fractured for a significant length of time, perhaps 
even several thousand kilometers. This oxidation, together with the weld characteristics, makes fracture analysis 
very difficult.  Figure 2 gives an overview of the fracture.
