Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions: 
1、火花塞属于外力过载引起的断裂失效，在火花塞断裂后断口表面受到高温引起局部过烧现象。 
The failure for the spark plug due to the overloading of the external force. The local over-burning phenomenon in 
fracture surface due to the high temperatures. 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1、 提供发动机完整的失效件进行失效分析。 
Provides complete engine failure parts for failure analysis. 
2、 检查、分析燃烧室温度过高的原因。 
Check and analysis of the combustion chamber temperature
