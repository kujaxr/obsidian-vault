Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件注塑冷却时间不足，强行脱模导致熔接痕位置壁厚偏薄，加剧熔融不良状态，形成
失效问题。 
Due to the insufficient cooling time of the failed parts, the wall thickness of the weld line is too 
thin due to forced demoulding, which aggravates the poor melting state and forms the failure 
problem.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商严格按工艺文件要求生产操作。 
                 The supplier shall operate in strict accordance with the process documents.
