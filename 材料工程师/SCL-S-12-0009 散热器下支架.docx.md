Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions

结论：LSJW26G988S002735的零件：胶管由于爆裂导致漏油。

Conclusions：Part of LSJW26G988S002735: the hose burst which leads to the leak of the brake oil.

     LSJW26G3295025022的零件：接头处裂纹导致制动液渗入编织层，使编织层同外层胶脱离，最终导致外层开裂漏油。

       Part of LSJW26G3295025022: the crack near the connector leads the brake oil penetrated in the reinforcement layer, which make the cover separated from the woven layer. And the cover braked at last.

建议：胶管供应商提高胶管耐疲劳性能。

Suggestions：the hose supplier should improver the fatique properties.

由于胶管走向苛刻，避免使用中在转向下高压制动的频次。

As the hose is arranged too harsh, avoid using the high pressure brake in the steering in high frequency.
