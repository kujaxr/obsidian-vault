Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 支架是在空气中的自然腐蚀。The Pipe –Bracket was the natural corrosion in the air. 
2、 螺钉和螺母是由于不正当的装配破坏了镀层引起的腐蚀。The wear failure for the bolt and nut failed due to the 
coating were damaged, caused by frequent assembly. 
 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
   1、提高该支架的防腐等级，SMTC 5100 001 盐雾试验要求：大于等于240 小时。Advance the corrosion level of the 
Aircondition Pipe –Bracket，minimum requirement for engine compartment is 240h NSS to red corrosion 
2、检查螺栓和螺母的使用及装配情况。Please check for the condition working and assembly .
