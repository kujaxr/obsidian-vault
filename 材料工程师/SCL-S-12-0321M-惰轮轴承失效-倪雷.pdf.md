Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、轴承由于内圈出现疲劳剥离（次表面起源型剥落）而失效的，钢球、外圈和保持架未失效。The bearing has failed 
due to fatigue（Sub-surface origin type peeling）of the inside race, the ball, outside race and cage are not failed.  
2、在内圈材料组织中，较多颗粒状碳化物呈带状聚集分布在回火马氏体基体上，局部有碳化物液析；In the inside 
race material microstructure, more granular carbide gathered distribution in the matrix of tempered martensite 
as banding, and local carbide liquation; 
3、钢球和外圈的材料组织和硬度符合要求，夹杂物水平正常；The microstructure and hardness of the ball and 
outside race meet the requirement, the inclusion contents are normal. 
4、轴承润滑良好，未见其它异常损伤。The lubrication is ok; there are not other abnormal damages. 
建议Suggestions：  
1、改善原材料质量，以提升轴承工作寿命。To improve the quality of raw materials, to improve the bearing 
working life. 
2、请NSK详细介绍白色组织剥落原理；NSK detailed white tissue peeling principle; 
3、请工程在ECU新版软件上对该零件进行进一步验证。Engineering shall further validation of the parts on the 
new version of the software in the ECU.

三、材料分析Material Analysis 
1）内圈的化学成分、硬度等均符合材料要求（如下表）；The chemical composition, hardness of the inside race 
all conform to the material requirements（Following Table）; 
2）内圈（失效件和新零件）材料组织不符合材料技术要求，较多颗粒状碳化物呈带状聚集分布在回火马氏体基体
上（见图7&12），组织中有较多碳化物液析（见图8&9）；The microstructure of the inside race（Failure Parts and 
New Parts）does not conform to the requirements of materials technology, more granular carbide gathered 
distribution in the matrix of tempered martensite as banding（see Fig.7&12）, more carbide liquation in the 
organization（see Fig. 8&9）; 
3）外圈和钢球的硬度、材料组织均符合材料要求（如下表和图14/16），夹杂物水平正常（如下表和图15/17）。
The hardness, microstructure of outside race and steel ball all conform to the material requirements（Following 
Table and Fig.14/16）, the inclusion contents are normal（Following Table and Fig.15/17）. 
 
结论：颗粒状碳化物聚集分布构成带状偏析，使钢的力学性能，尤其是疲劳性能显著下降。且其易成为疲劳
剥落的发源地。Conclusion: granular carbide aggregated distribution constitute ribbon segregation, so that the 
steel mechanical properties, particularly fatigue properties significantly decreased. And its easy to be the 
birthplace of fatigue spalling.
