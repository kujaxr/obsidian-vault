Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、两个失效件的开裂均是由于应力集中造成的疲劳开裂，疲劳源均位于螺母法兰面压痕的边缘。 
Two pieces cracking failures are due to stress concentration caused by fatigue cracking, fatigue source a re located 
in the edge of the nut flange surface indentation 
2、零件的材料性能未发现异常。 
The material properties of parts is not abnormal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请检查装配过程。 
Please check the assembly process. 
2、 校核支架实际所受的载荷。 
To check the actual load suffered for the bracket.
