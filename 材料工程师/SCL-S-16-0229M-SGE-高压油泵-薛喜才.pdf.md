Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 低压电磁阀压偏，导致泵体材料缺失，压装屑卡滞高压端，使阀球不能密闭。 
The deviation of low voltage solenoid valve result in pump body material loss, which make the valve ball not 
closed. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）进一步排查电磁阀压偏的原因。 
     Further investigate the deviation of the solenoid valve. 
（2）关注同批次或邻近批次零件，如出现高压油泵油压低，及时排查并更换零件。 
     Pay attention to the same batch or adjacent parts. If the fuel pump oil pressure is low, investigate the failed 
reason and replace the part.
