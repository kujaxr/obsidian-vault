Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. IS12 车架总成是由于多处发生疲劳开裂而失效。Frame assembly was broken off though fatigue at several points. 
2. 纵梁与NO3 横梁侧面焊缝搭接处(见图3b)没有100%熔透、纵梁与车身安装支架焊缝(见图5b)熔深偏低。No 
100% penetration was observed between the longitudinal beam and the cross beam, (side seam joint, shown in figure 
3b). The penetration was low between longitudinal and body mounting bracket (shown in figure 5b). 
3. NO3 横梁失效件、量产件及转向机横梁的材料化学成分基本一致，虽符合标准要求,但锰含量低于常见
SAPH370，抗拉强度低于常见的SAPH370。Chemical components of the failed parts and the production of the 
beam met the requirement and consistent, but content of manganese and tensile strength were lower than the normal. 
4. 车架纵梁及上摆臂支架的材料符合要求。The material of longitudinal girder and bracket met the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 对量产件进行风险评估，并勒令供应商进行整改。Do risk assessment of the producttion and ordered the supplier to 
carry out the rectification.  
2. 校核工作载荷，并评估目前试验工况下的安全系数。Check the active load and evaluate the safety factor. 
3. 加强焊缝质量控制。Control the welding process strictly to affirm the quality.

车身安装支架化学成分符合要求；硬度及组织正常。
