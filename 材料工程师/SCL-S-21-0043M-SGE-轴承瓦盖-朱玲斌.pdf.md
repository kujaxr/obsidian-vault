Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 轴承盖过载开裂失效，失效出现在烧结后的生产环节。The bearing cap was broken by overload which was 
occurs in the production link after sintering;  
（2） 轴承盖材料硬度和密度合格。The hardness and density are qualified. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 排查生产过程，重点关注整形过程。Check the production process and pay attention to the shaping process; 
（2） 对同批次零件进行筛查。Screen the parts in the same batch.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results 
泰国工厂反馈，在拆装发动机时，发现一例凸轮轴轴承盖开裂。 
According to the feedback from the Thai factory, a case of camshaft bearing cap cracking was found after uppack the 
engine. 
 
图1 为送检失效件，可以知道： 
 
轴承盖失效一侧螺栓装配孔区域多处开裂（图1a）； 
 
将失效件的孔剖开，发现开裂孔内侧接触痕迹不正常，有倾斜接触的迹象，未开裂孔接触痕迹正常（图
1b）；这些接触痕迹是在零件整形过程中形成的； 
 
同批次未失效的轴承盖孔（图1c）和未使用过的新轴承内孔剖面形貌（图1d）与失效件上未开裂一侧基
本相似，说明失效孔在生产过程中存在异常。 
The morphologies of failed parts are shown in figure 1, we can know: 
 
There is multiple cracks on one side of the failure bearing cap near the the bolt assembly hole area(Figure 1a); 
 
Dissection the failure area, it was found that the failure hole contact trace is abnormal which looks like an 
oblique contact; and the contact trace of no crack hole is normal(Figure 1b)；these contact marks are formed 
during the shaping of the parts; 
 
The contact trace of the same batch of bearing caps(Figure 1c) and new bearing cap(Figure 1d) is similar to the 
no crack side of the failure part which shows that the crack hole is abnormal in the production process.
