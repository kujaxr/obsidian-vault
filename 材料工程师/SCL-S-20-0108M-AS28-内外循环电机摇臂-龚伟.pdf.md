Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件材料严重降解，是失效的原因，同时，失效件材料与认可状态不一致。 
   The material of the failure part was seriously degraded, which was the cause of the failure. At the 
same time, the material of the failure part was inconsistent with the approved status.   
2. 零件存在注塑孔洞。 
   There are injection holes in the parts. 
建议Suggestions：1. 责令供应商对材料及工艺问题做出解释并整改。 
                 Let the supplier explain the material& craft problem and rectify.
