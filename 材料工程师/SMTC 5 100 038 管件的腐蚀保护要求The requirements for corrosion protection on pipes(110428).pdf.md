3 要求 
3  Requirements 
3.1   按照 SMTC 90008 标准避免有害物质。 
3.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances 
according to  SMTC 90008. 
3.2  环件及表面上与使用中流入管件的介质直接
接触的密封面、工作面不得含有有机涂层。必须经
相关测试部门认可公布才允许例外。 
 
3.2  Sealing surfaces, face surfaces of ring 
pieces and surfaces, which come in contact 
with the media flowing through the pipe during 
operation must not have organic coatings. 
Exceptions are possible only with a release 
from the appropriate test department. 
4 类型 
4  Type 
    双层系统包括一层锌合金或锌/镍合金涂
层、与之配套的转换处理或底漆处理，以及一层表
面有机涂层，如环氧树脂材料涂层配套的处理。 
注：涉及的涂布法以及涂层材料必须在测试之前经过
上海汽车中心实验室同意。 
   Duplex system consisting of a zinc alloy 
or zinc/nickel alloy coating, a conversion or 
primer treatment matched to this coating and 
an organic top coat, on, for example, an epoxy 
resin base. 
Note: The coating methods and materials to be 
applied must be agreed upon with the Shanghai Mo-
tor central laboratory prior to their use. 
5  涂层一般要求 
涂层上不得有瑕疵和多余的材料。只有压制管
套上的孤立的针眼或线状损伤是可以允许的，但不
允许出现工具碰撞造成的有机涂层剥落。涂层不得
对成品件的功能特性产生不利影响。在组装过程中
不得损伤涂层。 
5  General coating requirements 
The coating must be free of flaws and 
excess material. Isolated pinpoint or linear 
damage will be tolerated only on pressed 
sleeves, but flaking of the organic top coat as 
a result of contact with the tools cannot be 
tolerated. The coating must have no adverse 
effects on the functional characteristics of the 
finished part. No damage to the coating must 
occur during proper assembly. 
6  生产过程中的涂覆操作 
在所需的焊接操作完成之后，可以在生产过程
中的任何阶段进行涂盖操作。如果在涂盖操作之后
需要对管件进行弯曲加工，则一定要在涂层工序之
后立即进行，弯曲加工之后应直接进行测试，以确
保涂层不受损。必须根据第 8.2 小节说明进行腐
蚀测试，并提供无损伤证明文件；肉眼检查不足以
提供这样的证明。不允许在弯曲半径上出现涂层腐
蚀保护程度的减弱。对涂盖操作时间安排有影响、
或对涂层工序之后的计划有影响的生产过程中的
变动，必须注明，对于这样的变动需要重新进行首
次采样。 
6  Coating during the production process 
The coating can be applied at any point 
during the production process after all re-
quired soldering or welding operations are 
completely finished. If a pipe must be bent 
after the coating has been applied, this must 
be done immediately following the coating 
process, directly after which tests must be 
performed to ensure that the coating was not 
damaged. Documentation that there has been 
no damage must be provided by means of the 
corrosion test under section 8.2; a visual test 
is not sufficient. A decrease in the corrosion 
protection along the bending radius is not 
permitted.  Changes to the production 
process that affect the timing of the coating 
process or a different procedure after the 
coating must be indicated and will require a 
new first sampling.

7.1  涂盖零件 
焊有附件或支架的钢管，附加零件是指总成中
和管件一起使用的零件，包括环件、联管螺母、螺
纹销，以及各种托架，等等。 
7.1
Coating parts
Steel tube with accessories or brackets 
welded or brazed onto it, Add-on parts include 
ring pieces, union nuts, threaded pins and 
various brackets, among other things, which 
are used with the pipe sections in the ASSY. 
7.2  镀锌 
    符合 SMTC 5 100 002标准的电解镀锌，涂层
厚度不小于 8 μm。在获得特别许可的情况下，也
可以使用符合 SMTC 5 100 003 标准的锌镍涂层。
必须使用抛光剂含量最低的电解液进行操作，确保
最佳附着性。 
7.2  Zinc coating
Electrolytic zinc coating acc. to SMTC 5 
100 002, coating thickness ≥8 μm. A 
zinc-nickel coating acc. to SMTC 5 100 003 is 
also possible by special agreement. An elec-
trolyte with the lowest possible amount of 
brighteners must be used to ensure optimal 
adhesion.
7.3  预处理     
在镀锌操作之后涂层工序之前，必须进行锌磷
化处理或者等同的基底涂层处理，该操作也适用于
不能被有机涂层覆盖的表面。铬酸盐光泽处理会降
低涂层的附着能力，所以不得在预处理中使用。
（锌
镍镀层上可以进行该处理），预处理之后必须立即
进入涂层工序操作，光亮锌镀层上不允许直接覆盖
有机涂层。 
7.3  Pretreatment  
After the zinc coating and prior to the 
coating process, zinc phosphating or an 
equivalent primer treatment must be applied. 
This treatment may also be applied to sur-
faces that must remain free of organic coat-
ings. Chromating reduces the adhesion of the 
coating and is not permitted as a pretreat-
ment. (It is permissible with a zinc-nickel 
coating). The pretreatment must be applied 
immediately prior to the coating process. No 
direct organic coating of the bright zinc layer is 
permissible. 
7.4  有机表面涂层   
有机涂层厚度不小于20 μm，粉末涂层厚度不
小于50 μm，在烧进状态下可以含有氟化聚合物。
必须在每次操作中，都明确分析是否要在可能出现
的螺纹部位覆盖有机涂层。联管螺母附近管道涂层
不允许出现瑕疵。 
7.4  Organic top coat 
Organic coating thickness ≥20 μm, 
Powder paint thickness ≥50 μm in burnt-in 
state. Fluorinated polymer content is possible. 
It must be clarified on a case-by-case basis 
whether an organic coating may be applied to 
any threads that may be present. Flaws in the 
pipe coating around the union nuts are not 
permitted.
7.5  后续处理 
覆盖涂层之后，必须采取合适的措施，确保在
组装之前管道内部不会出现腐蚀现象。 
7.5
Post-treatment
After coating, suitable measures must be 
taken to ensure that no corrosion can form 
inside the pipe prior to assembly.

8.1.2 
胶带试验测试 
仅限于由于直径太小无法在 8.1.1 测试规定
中使用的管件。胶带试验测试中，表面涂层任何部
位不得粘附胶带。按下列步骤进行胶带试验测试：
在管件的平直部分刻出 X 形记号，使用粘附强度
为 3 N/cm 的胶带，在划痕上粘牢之后，以垂直于
试样表面的方向迅速撕掉胶带。 
8.1.2
Tape test
Permissible only for pipes on which the 
test found in section 8.1.1 cannot be per-
formed because their diameter is too small. 
No part of the top coat may adhere to the 
adhesive tape during the tape test. The tape 
test is performed as follows: after making an 
x-shaped cut on a straight section of pipe, a 
piece of adhesive tape with an adhesive 
strength of 3 N/cm of width is applied to the 
cut and removed with a jerk-like motion per-
pendicular to the specimen surface.
