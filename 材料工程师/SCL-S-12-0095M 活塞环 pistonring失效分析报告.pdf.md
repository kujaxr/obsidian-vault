Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 活塞油环是由于多源疲劳而失效的。 The piston ring has failed through fatigue. 
2. 未收到活塞，因此不可能确定疲劳失效的原因。由于仅仅油环的一部分具有疲劳裂纹，因此有可能活塞
槽的机加工误差或者其它缺陷导致振动的产生。Since the piston was not submitted for analysis, it’s not 
possible to say the reason for the fatigue; however since only one part of the ring displayed fatigue cracks, it’s 
possible a machining error or other defect in the piston ring groove led to vibration.  
3. 没有材料缺陷的迹象。硬度符合图纸要求。There was no indication for material defects. Hardness met the 
drawing requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商要加强质量控制过程。Supplier must strengthen quality control process.  
2. 委托失效分析，请提交有关相匹配的零部件。 Please always submit relevent adjoining components for failure 
analysis.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
收到的活塞环如图1 所示。不幸的是，活塞实物未提供，但提供了照片图2 所示，呈现出槽有异常的特征。  
活塞油环由三个环组成，其中平环断裂成5 部分；另两个环无任何异常。  
断裂环的照片如图2 和3 所示。环沿着现有的疲劳裂纹断裂。在整个区域（图4 及5）有广泛的疲劳裂纹。由于
裂纹很多，无法确定裂纹萌生的次序；同时还有发动机运行过程中带来的严重的磨损。  
The piston rings were received as shown in figure 1.  Unfortunately the remainder of the piston was not returned, 
although a photo was supplied as shown in figure 2, showing abnormal groove.  Of the three rings which comprise the 
oil control ring, one flat ring was broken into 5 pieces, whist the other two were completely free of any abnormality.   
Photos of the fractured ring are shown in figure 2 & 3.  The ring fractured along existing fatigue cracks.  There is 
extensive fatigue cracking over the whole area (figures 4 & 5).  It was not possible to establish a pattern of the crack 
initiation since there are so many cracks, and significant damage due to running in the engine.
