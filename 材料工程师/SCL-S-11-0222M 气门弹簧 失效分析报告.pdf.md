Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions：
  
 
气门弹簧通过多源疲劳断裂；源区处于钢丝的两个相对表面。  The valve spring has failed through multi-origin fatigue, 
originating from two opposing areas of the wire. 
 
弹簧严重变形；沿着弹簧钢丝的方向有线圈到线圈接触的痕迹，这表明装配误差导致弹簧过度压缩。这符合
低里程的特征。 The spring was heavily deformed, and signs of coil-coil contact were seen along most of the spring length, indicating 
an assembly error led to exessive compression of the spring.  This is consistent with the low mileage. 
 
失效与材料无关，虽然没有测试材料的残余应力。There is no material reason for the failure, although residual stress was not 
measured. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
确保发动部件的准确的安装。Ensure accurate installation of components during Engine build 
 
喷丸的质量无法检查，因为没有在规范中定义残余应力限制。工程应采取措施，评估和限制气门弹簧的残余
应力。 The quality of the shot peening could not be checked, since there is no residual stress limit defined in the specification.  
Engineering should take steps to evaluate and set limits for residual stress in valve springs.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
气门弹簧断裂成两部分，两者都有变形（图1）。检查线圈发现：大多数线圈出现线圈碰到线圈的接触痕迹，包括
断裂位置。由于磨损痕迹是连续的，并通过断裂的两部分。这是在失效之前产生的（图1b）。在线圈的两端, 线
圈碰到线圈的是正常的，但在中心不正常的。 
The valve spring was fractured into two pieces, both of which were deformed (figure 1).  On inspection of the 
coils, it was found that most coils showed trace from coil-coil impact, including the fractured area.  Since the 
wear trace was continuous over the fracture, this condition existed before the failure (figure 1b). Coil-coil 
impact is normal at the ends of the coil, but abnormal in the centre.

断口分析Fracture Analysis 
两个断口，情况不错，腐蚀少，这是由于得到了发动机油的保护。一些表面的磨损（图2 可见白色）是由于将断
裂面放在一起造成的。 
The two fracture surfaces were in fair condition, with little corrosion, due to protection from engine oil in the 
packaging.  Some surface wear (seen white in figure 2) is due to replacing the fracture faces together after 
fracture. 
 
断口的特点是在两个方向上存在几个不同的断裂模式。疲劳台阶表面：疲劳裂纹从线圈较低表面上的几个点开始
(见图2)。在扫描电镜下，发现：第二个疲劳裂纹源区在钢丝相对的另一面。中央三角区是抗剪超载断裂的，而相
邻外的区域是过载断裂的（见图3）。 
The fracture is characterised by several different crack modes in two directions.  Rachet marks point to one 
fatigue crack beginning at several origins on the lower surface of the wire in figure 2.  A second fatigue crack 
was identified through electron microscopy at the opposite surface of the wire. The central triangular area is 
overload fracture with shear characteristic, whilst the adjacent outer areas have brittle overload characteristics 
(see figure 3).

图 4 疲劳断口源区 
 
 
在同一位置出现两个疲劳裂纹是反常的。考虑上弹簧变形和线圈接触的痕迹，可以得出结论，装配误差，如安装
气门锁片差（见图5），导致弹簧承受不正常的载荷，随后出现疲劳断裂。 
The appearance of two fatigue cracks at the same location is unusual.  Together with the deformation and 
signs of coil-coil contact, the conclusion may be drawn that an assembly error such as poor installation of the 
valve cotters (see figure 5), resulted in unusual loading on the valve spring, and subsequent fatigue fracture.
