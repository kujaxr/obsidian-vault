Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1. 后横拉杆断裂是由于碰枪导致焊接不良引起的。调查发现：该后横拉杆为焊接调试件。 
Thrust rod breakage is caused by poor welding caused by hitting the gun. The investigation found that the thrust rod is a 
welding debugging piece. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 加强生产过程中调试件、不合格品的管控。 
Strengthen the control of debugging parts and substandard products in the production process.
