Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）铝箔表面发黑的物质主要为氧化铝，是与液体接触，腐蚀产生的。The material on the surface of aluminum foil 
is mainly aluminum oxide, which was caused by corrosion due to contraction with liquid. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）对生产过程进行调查，排查可能的液体来源，并加强过程控制。Investigate production and logistics processes, 
check possible sources of fluids, and strengthen process control.
