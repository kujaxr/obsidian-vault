Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、螺纹销拆解困难主要是由于内螺纹孔加工不良引起的螺纹装配咬死。The difficulty in disassembling bolts is 
mainly due to poor processing of internal threaded holes, which causes abnormal thread engagement. 
2、螺纹销硬度和金相符合技术要求，内螺纹强度满足要求。The hardness and microstructure of failure bolts meet the 
requirements, and the hardness of internal thread meet the requirement.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、改善内螺纹孔加工。Improve the processing of internal threaded holes. 
2、适当降低预拧扭矩，可以缓解螺纹咬死现象。Reducing the pre-tightening torque appropriately can alleviate the 
thread seizure.
