Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过失效件的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，塑料缓冲块的材料耐温性与图纸要求相比偏低，同时
减震器运行中，缸体内温度较高，导致缓冲块受热熔化变形。 
2. 经测试，失效件缓冲块材料为PA6+PA66，与图纸要求不符。 
Conclusions: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the damper, it can be seen that the high 
temperature resistance performance of the failed plastic cushion is lower than that of design requirement. 
During the damper was working, the plastic cushion occurred a deformation by the high temperature of the 
cylinder. After testing, the material of the plastic cushion is PA6+PA66 which can’t conform to the drawing 
requirement. 
建议：采用图纸要求的材料制造缓冲块。降低减振系统工作温度。  
Suggestions：The required material should be used in the production of the cushion. The temperature of damper 
system during working should be decreased.
