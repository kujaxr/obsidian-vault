Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 螺栓、螺母咬死，在外力作用下将螺栓扭断。引起螺栓、螺母咬死的诸多因素中，螺母牙高不齐、螺栓表
面大量漆层可能性较大。Outside force results in the bolt twisting off because of the seizure of bolt and nut. Tooth 
no-uniform of not and lot of oil paint are the possible failed reason.   
（2） 失效螺母牙高不齐；失效螺栓弯曲、牙型异常，螺牙表面粘附铁屑和大量油漆。Tooth height of failed nut isn’t 
uniform. Failed bolt is bending and tooth shape is abnormal. Scrap iron、lots of oil paint adhere to bolt tooth. 
（3） 新螺母牙型异常、牙高不齐。Tooth height of new nut isn’t uniform and tooth shape is abnormal.  
（4） 螺栓材料性能正常。Property of bolt is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 同批次螺母、螺栓全部隔离。Isolate the same batch bolt and nut.   
（2） 螺栓、螺母供应商提供分析整改报告。Supplier of bolt and nut provide the rectifying report. 
（3） 采取防护措施，避免螺栓被电泳。Take the protected measure to avoid the bolt electrophoresing.
