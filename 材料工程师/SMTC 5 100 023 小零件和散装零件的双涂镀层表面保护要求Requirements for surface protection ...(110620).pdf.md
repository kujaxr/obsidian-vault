1 范围 
本标准规定了根据SMTC 5 100 002规定的电
镀锌镀层和SMTC 5 100 003规定的锌/镍镀层，
附加以环氧树脂为基础（阴极浸涂料）的有机涂
层的要求。 
 
 
本标准适用于评价附加以环氧树脂为基础
的有机涂层的电镀锌镀层和锌/镍镀层。 
1  Scope 
This standard defines requirements for 
electrolytically deposited zinc coatings ac-
cording to SMTC 5 100 002 and zinc/nickel 
alloy coatings according to SMTC 5 100 003 
with an additional organic coating based on 
epoxy resins (cathodic dip paint). 
The standard is adopted to evaluate elec-
trolytically 
deposited 
zinc 
coatings 
and 
zinc/nickel alloy coatings with an additional 
organic coating based on epoxy resins.

3.2.5  Components with metric thread with 
this surface protection may only be used at 
ambient temperatures < 90 °C. This coating 
may not be applied to high-strength screws 
with Rm > 1000 MPa.

3.2.6  The H-zone must not be fallen below 
and the h-zone must not be exceeded for

3.2.7 对于挂镀件，悬挂点不允许存在大面积
未施涂的部分（大触点）。相反，独立的、几乎
肉眼看不见的小触点，因其并不损坏防腐性能，
是允许的。 
3.2.7
The large contact points at the suspen-
sion points that are typical of rack galvanized 
goods are not permitted. In contrast, isolated 
barely perceptible micro contact points, which 
do not, in particular, impair the corrosion re-
sistance, are permitted. 
3.2.8 尽管整个涂镀过程允许最长为24h，但
各施涂程序应环环相扣（联机程序），以最大限
度减少临时贮藏和/或运输的时间。安装时须小
心，镀层不应以功能减损和/或规定的防腐保护
性能降低为代价而受到破坏。 
 
3.2.8  The coating procedures should follow 
on directly from one another (online proce-
dure), so that interim storage and/or transport 
times are significantly reduced, although a 
maximum of 24 h is permissible. Given proper 
mounting, the coating shall not be damaged if 
this would lead to impairment of function 
and/or decrease of the specified corrosion 
protection.  
3.2.9 镀层能为连接组件提供非标表面保护。
如果螺纹接头将用于连接组件、并且其与要求的
润滑油结合需要，对螺纹接头的检验是必需的。
这样做是为了在装配条件下并将温度进行考虑，
测试螺钉、台面层以及待固定的零部件的摩擦性
能（螺钉实验台）。 
3.2.9  The coating provides no standard sur-
face protection for joining elements. If it is to be 
used on joining elements, if necessary in 
combination with required lubricants, an ex-
amination of the actual threaded joint is 
needed. This is done by testing the friction 
behavior (screw test bed) of the screw, the 
counter layer and the components to be fas-
tened under assembly conditions (screwing 
speed), taking into consideration the opera-
tional temperatures.  
3.2.10 镀层体系的总镀层厚度不应超过根据
标准 DIN EN ISO 4042 规定的镀层厚度。 
3.2.10  The total layer thickness of the coating 
system may not exceed the layer thicknesses 
according to DIN EN ISO 4042.  
3.2.11 根据Sp-s611、Sp-s617、Sp-s621 以及 
Sp-s627 的实验要求适用于螺母、螺栓的头部、
表面和/或平面以及适用于螺纹零件和相似零件
的平面。在3.6 节规定的降低后的要求适用于螺
栓杆和螺纹。 
3.2.11  The test requirements according to 
Sp-s611, Sp-s617, Sp-s621 and Sp-s627 apply 
only to the head, the face and/or the flat sur-
faces for nuts and bolts and to the face sur-
faces of threaded parts and similar molded 
parts. The reduced requirements specified in 
Section 3.6 apply to shank and thread.
