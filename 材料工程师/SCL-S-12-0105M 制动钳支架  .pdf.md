Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是在镀锌工序之前产生的原始裂纹（如打磨、物流磕碰等）导致零件断裂失效。 
The failure duo to the original crack before the coating process (such as casting polished, logistics bump) 
2、 硬度不符合技术要求。 
The hardness did not meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 加强生产过程质量控制 
       To strengthen the quality control of production processes.
