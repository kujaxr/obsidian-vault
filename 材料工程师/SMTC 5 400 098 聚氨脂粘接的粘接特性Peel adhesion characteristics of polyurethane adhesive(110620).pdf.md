1 范围 
本标准规定了聚氨酯胶粘剂的粘结特性及
试验步骤。 
本标准适用于评价粘结到各种底材上的聚
氨酯胶粘剂的粘结性。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the procedure for 
assessing the adhesion of polyurethane adhe-
sive to various substrates. 
This standard applies to procedure for 
assessing the adhesion of polyurethane adhe-
sive to various substrates. 
2 设备 
2  Apparatus 
2.1 干净的软抹布。 
2.1  Clean, lint free cloth.
2.2 挤出枪。 
2.2
Extrusion gun.
2.3 压板。 
2.3  Spatula.
2.4 解剖刀。 
2.4 Scalpel.
2.5 圆形有鼻老虎钳。 
2.5  Round nosed pliers.
3 试样准备 
3  Specimen preparation 
3.1 拿一个底材样品，底材用生产商推荐的溶
剂清洁剂或粘结促进剂湿润过的软抹布擦拭干
净。 
3.1  Take a sample of the substrate and wipe 
clean using a lint free cloth moistened with the 
manufacturers recommended solvent clean-
er/adhesion promoter. 
3.2 根据相关产品数据单的规定，允许有闪蒸
现象。注：一些粘结促进剂要求有“擦上-擦除”
程序。 
3.2  Allow to flash off in accordance with the 
relevant product data sheet. Note: Some ad-
hesion promoters require a ‘wipe on - wipe off’ 
application. 
3.3 用生产商推荐的涂覆器，涂覆一层连续的
薄膜（如果有要求）到底材上。根据相关产品数
据单的规定，允许有闪蒸现象。 
 
3.3 Using the manufacturers recommended 
applicator, apply a continuous film of the spe-
cified primer (if required) to the substrate and 
allow to ‘flash off in accordance with the rele-
vant product data sheet. 
3.4 涂覆3 片胶粘剂到指定的底材上，但是在
涂覆时应避免有空气孔存在。每片直径10 mm、
长度至少120 mm。 
3.4  Apply three beads of adhesive to the 
prepared substrate taking care to avoid air 
entrapment during application. Each bead 
shall be 10mm diameter and at least 120 mm 
long. 
3.5 用一个压板将胶粘剂的高度压平到4～5 
mm 的高度。 
3.5
Using a spatula flatten the beads to a
height of 4 to 5 mm. 
3.6 根据第4 条的规定，试验前，在（23±2）℃
和（65±5）％ RH 的湿度条件下，调节试样168 
h。 
3.6  Condition the prepared specimens at 
23 °C ± 2 °C and (65 ± 5)% RH for 168 h prior 
to testing in accordance with Clause 4. 
4 试验步骤 
4  Procedure of test 
4.1 在需要时，按指定的环境老化程序进行试
验样品的调节。 
4.1  Where relevant, subject the conditioned 
test specimens to the specified environmental 
ageing procedure. 
4.2 割下胶粘剂片的端部并保证割线是平行
的，并且和底材面有0.5 mm～1 mm 的距离。切
割的长度应保证胶粘剂片的末端有足够的长度
4.2
Cut the end of the adhesive bead en-
suring that the line of cut is parallel to, and 
within 0.5 mm ~ 1.0 mm from the substrate 
surface. The length of cut shall be sufficient to

5.1  The failure mode classification as deter-

sive product reference.

艺，面漆颜色等。 
5.4  Full details of the substrate on which the 
test was conducted, including: base. materials, 
ceramic coatings, galvanised coating, paint 
technologies, topcoat’ colours, etc.
