Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
 
1、 曲轴是由于外力过载引起的高应力低周期疲劳断裂，裂纹区域未发现原始材料缺陷。由曲轴运行时间很
短，说明曲轴受到了异常载荷，这些异常载荷可能来自于曲轴空间的不合理，相关零部件或其他引起过载的
情况。 
The Crankshaft has failed through high stress, low cycle fatigue. The fracture was initiated at several origins and no material defects 
were found.   
Since the part failed after such a short running time, this points to an abnormal loading condition which can arise from dimensional 
errors in the crankshaft, adjoining components or other overload situation. 
2、 曲轴的金相组织不合格，但不是引起断裂失效的根本原因。 
The microstructure does not meet the requirements and must be improved, however this has not caused the failure in this case. 
 
建议Suggestion: 
请检查发动机实验时的安装和载荷情况。 
Check the installation and loading conditions of engine test.
