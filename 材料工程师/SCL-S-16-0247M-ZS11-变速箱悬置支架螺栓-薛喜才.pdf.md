Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 螺栓属于扭断失效，断裂原因与试装过程中扭矩枪设定有关。Bolt is twist off and the reason is relate to the 
torque gun setting. 
（2） 螺栓材料成分、硬度及组织正常，螺牙无折叠，但表面存在氧化物。Chemical components、hardness and 
microstructure are normal. No folding defect is observed but there is oxide at surface. 
（3） 同批次螺栓摩擦系数符合要求。The same batch coefficient meets the technical requirement. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查扭矩枪设定。Check the torque gun setting.  
（2） 查找螺栓表面残留氧化层的原因，并评估其影响。Investigate the residual oxide and evaluate the influence.
