Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳开裂。Failure pieces belong to fatigue cracking. 
2、 失效件的焊缝存在未焊透现象，这会显著降低疲劳性能。The existence of the phenomenon of failure pieces 
incomplete penetration welds, which can significantly reduce the fatigue performance. 
3、 原材料化学成分符合技术要求。Chemical composition of the raw materials meet the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 提供整改方案，加强焊缝质量控制。Quality control of the welding process.  
2、 对焊缝的分布进行优化。Provide corrective programs to strengthen the weld quality contro.
