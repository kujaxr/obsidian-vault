Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该玻璃升降器卡夹-传动掣发生过载断裂，断裂面无明显缺陷。 
The glass regulator clip transmission lever experienced overload fracture, with no obvious defects on the fracture surface. 
（2）材料化学成分中Mn低于下限值，不符合要求，这不是引起本次失效的主要原因；硬度、金相组织正常。 
The chemical composition of Mn in the material was lower than the minimum limit value and did not meet the 
requirements, which was not main reason of this failure ; the hardness and metallographic structure were normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）排查过载原因。To check the cause of overload.
