Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，挡板和阻尼器材料与图纸要求一致。 
2. 通过分析可以判定，烟灰缸的弹簧挡板被锐器割断，烟灰缸的阻尼器受较大拉力而被拉断。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the materials of the supporting spring plate and damper are PC/ABS and PA66 
respectively, which conform to the drawing requirement. According to the macroscopic observation and 
material test of the ashtray, it can be seen that the supporting spring plate was cut by a sharp tool. The damper 
was broken by an excessive force. 
建议：避免阻尼器承受过大外力；避免锐器切割烟灰缸。 
Suggestions：The excessive outside force on the damper should be prevented. To avoid sharp tool cutting ashtray.
