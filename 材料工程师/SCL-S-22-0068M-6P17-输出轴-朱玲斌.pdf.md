Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）输出轴的齿在齿根应力较大处发生疲劳断裂失效。The output shaft tooth fatigue fractured originated from the 
root large stress area . 
（2）输出轴材料成分、表面硬度、芯部硬度、硬化层深、内氧化以及非马连片深度符合图纸要求，组织正常。
The output shaft material composition, surface hardness, core hardness, depth of hardened layer, internal oxidation and 
soft skin depth meet the drawing requirements, and the structure is normal. 
（3）非马深度略高于该类零件的一般要求（≤0.02mm）。The non martensite depth is slightly higher than the general 
requirements for such parts (≤ 0.02mm). 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）排查实验过程和实验规范。Check the test process and test specifications. 
（2）对另一台通过实验的输出轴进行材料检测。Carry out material inspection on the other output shaft passing the 
experiment.
