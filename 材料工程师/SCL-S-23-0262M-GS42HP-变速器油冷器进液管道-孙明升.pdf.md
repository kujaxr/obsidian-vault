Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）该变速器油冷器进液管发生过载断裂，断裂面无明显缺陷。 
Overload fracture occurred on the transmission oil cooler inlet pipe, no defects were found on the fracture surface. 
（2）材料金相组织和硬度正常。 
Material metallographic structure and hardness were normal.  
建议Suggestions：  
（1）排查和规范装配流程。 
Inspect and standardize the assembly process.
