Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 缺陷是表面氧化物，产生于原材料的加工过程。The defect is surface oxide, originating during raw material 
manufacture. 
2. 硬化层符合要求。The hardening process meets the requirement of the drawing. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 供应商要改善原材料和成品的检查。Supplier should improve inspection of raw material and finished product  
2. 原材料供应商应改进材料表面质量。Raw material supplier should improve surface quality of material

截面分析 Cross-section analysis 
从失效区域取样，横截面的照片显示片状的材料附着在表面上。片状材料的下面没有夹杂。这表明这种缺
陷是一种表面缺陷，如结疤，是在轧制过程中形成的（图5a）。腐蚀后，该金相组织正常（图5b）。 A cross 
section through the failed area, shows flakes of material on the surface.  There was no inclusion underneath the 
surface, showing that the defect is a surface effect, such as a scab, caused during the rolling process (figure 5a).  
When etched, the microstructure is normal (figure 5b).
