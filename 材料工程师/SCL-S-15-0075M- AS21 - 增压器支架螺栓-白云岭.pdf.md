Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 螺栓属疲劳断裂。The failure was the fatigue crack. 
2、 螺栓的材料性能符合技术要求。The materials meet the technical requirements. 
3、 螺栓有过重复装配标识。There were second assembly logo. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、检查螺栓的装配过程质量。 
To check assemble process quality. 
2、检查试验过程载荷。 
   To check the force during test.
