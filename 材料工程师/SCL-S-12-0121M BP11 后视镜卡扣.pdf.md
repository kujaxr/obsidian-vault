Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）弹簧夹是由于过载而断裂。The spring clip was broken because of overload from operation. 
（2）材料硬度高于上限，对其断裂有一定的影响，但不是直接原因。The hardness is higher than the top limit, which 
has a significant effect on the failure, but is not the main cause.  
（3） 成分和组织正常。The chemical composition and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）控制材料的硬度处于合适的位置。Control the hardness of the material.  
（2）装配中，避免产生过载的操作。During assembly, avoid overloading the clip.
