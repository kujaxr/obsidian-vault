Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、失效螺栓是由于硬度过高而发生脆性断裂。The failure bolts were brittle fracture due to the high hardness. 
2、失效螺栓芯部硬度和表面硬度均不符合标准要求；同批次螺栓硬度也不符合要求。The core hardness and surface 
hardness of failure bolt doesn’t meet the requirements. And the hardness of the same batch bolt doesn’t meet the 
requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、隔离该批次零件并对已装零件进行更换。Isolate the batch of bolts and replace the installed parts. 
2、切换该供应商。Change this supplier.
