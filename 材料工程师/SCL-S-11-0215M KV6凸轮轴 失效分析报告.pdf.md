Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 
因为凸轮轴、缸盖轴座表面和凸轮轴支架间异常咬合，导致进气凸轮轴未能通过扭转过载。 The Intake 
camshaft has failed through torsional overload, due to abnormal adhesion between the camshaft and the bearing surfaces of cylinder 
head and camshaft bracket. 
2. 
由于润滑不良致使粘连，剥落了承载面局部区域。测试中轴承系统润滑油的供应没有中断，所以堵塞局
部润滑油的是外来物质，如密封胶，铝颗粒或干油脂。The adhesion was caused by poor lubrication, isolated to a small 
area of the bearing. Since there was no disruption to the oil supply at this bearing, the oil was blocked by a foreign material such as 
sealant, aluminum particles or dried grease. 
3. 
凸轮轴材料化学成分和金相组织符合要求。凸轮轴非冷激的本体硬度不符合要求,但是没影响失效. The 
camshaft material composition & microstructure meet the requirements.  The camshaft hardness does not meet the specification, 
however this did not influence the failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
消除承载面区域外来物质的源头。Eliminate sources of foreign substances in the bearing areas.  
2. 
供应商提高凸轮轴硬度. Supplier to improve camshaft hardness.  
3. 
DRE 更新图纸: 改RES 21.FC.02 变成SMTC5 130 002.  DRE to update drawing: change RES 21.FC.02 to SMTC

凸轮轴 Camshaft 
 
凸轮轴的前端（驱动端）出现断裂（图2a）。断裂起源于内圆角处，以脆性方式（快速断裂）扩展；对于这
种材料，过载时，出现这种形式的断裂是正常的。未发现任何会直接导致失效的缺陷或显著疏松。 
图2b 中，箭头所指位置发现有很多铝的磨损残留物。这些物质来自于气缸盖和凸轮轴支架，因为局部粘着
磨损的发生。凸轮轴前轴颈向后的一部分表面良好，因此可以排除机油供应引起失效的可能性。 
The camshaft had fractured through the front end (driven end) (figure 2a).  Fracture was initiated at the internal radius and propagated in a brittle 
manner (fast fracture) which is normal for this type of material when overloaded.  There were no defects or significant porosity which would have 
directly led to failure. 
Heavy deposits of aluminium were present on the outer part of the front bearing journal (figure 2b).  This is material transfered from the cylinder 
head and camshaft bracket by adhesive wear process.  The rearward part of the front journal, and remaining journals on the camshaft were all in 
excellent condition, therefore a problem with the general oil supply can be excluded.

只有一半轴颈表面呈现粘连，因此不可能存在润滑油供应的问题。

断口分析Fracture Analysis 
 
断裂碎片大小均匀，与扭矩过载相一致。外表面有轻微的与带轮和油密封件接触的痕迹（图3）。The fractured pieces 
are of uniform size, consistent with torsional overload. Slight markings on the outer surfaces are consistent with contact with the belt wheel and oil 
seal (figure 3). 
电镜分析表明：断裂特征正常；裂纹通过石墨与金属界面传播；一些金属晶粒出现韧性断裂（图4）。SEM analysis 
revealed normal fracture characteristics, with crack propagation at graphite-metal interfaces, and some ductile fracture through the metal grains 
(figure 4).

气缸盖和凸轮轴支架 
Cylinder head and camshaft bracket 
 
缸盖进气凸轮轴和凸轮轴支架承载面末端区域都表现出严重的粘着磨损（图7）。缸盖材料去除的深度比凸轮轴支
架更多，这是由于较高的负荷情况（正常）。汽缸盖粘着磨损的区域延伸到支撑区域的边部，显示润滑不良（见图
7c 及7e）。 
 
磨损区域没有明显的外来材料（如有色金属，非金属颗粒等），但磨损非常严重，外来颗粒可能已经嵌入到材料中。
外来铝颗粒无法从磨损的残渣中分离出来 
 
轴承的向后部分是正常的，无磨损。油的渠道足够大，也不可能被粒子、密封胶等阻塞（图7d）。 
 
凸轮轴支撑面有干的黑色沉积物，可能是磨损高温引起变化的油脂（图7f）。 
 
如果是夹紧载荷导致其出现严重磨损，那么高夹紧载荷会使得承载面的磨损更均匀。 
 
The intake camshaft end bearing areas of cylinder head and camshaft bracket both showed heavy adhesive wear (figure 7).  The material 
removal from the cylinder head side was deeper than at the camshaft bracket, due to the higher loading situation (normal).  The cylinder head 
side also has an extended region of adhesive wear to the edge of the bearing area, indicating poor lubrication here (figures 7c & 7e).   
 
No obviously foreign materials were seen in the wear traces (eg ferrous metal, non-metallic particles etc), however the wear is heavy and 
particles may have been buried during material transfer.   Foreign particles of aluminium cannot be isolated from wear debris. 
 
The rearward part of the bearing was normal, without wear.  Oil channels were adequately big, and not blocked by particles, sealant etc (figure 
7d). 
 
The camshaft bracket side has deposits of a dry black material, possibly overworked grease (figure 7f).   
 
The influence of the clamping load was considered, however, it’s thought a too-high clamping load would have created more uniform wear over 
the bearing surface
