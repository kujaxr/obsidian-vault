Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺栓是由于高温引起涂层失效，导致拆解时过载断裂。The bolt is overload fracture occurs during disassembly due 
to abnormal high temperature. 
2、 失效螺栓金相组织和硬度符合标准要求。The microstructure and hardness of the failed bolt meet the standard. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、校核该位置温度是否有异常。Check the temperature of the position. 
2、更换耐高温涂层。Change the high-temperature resistant coating.
