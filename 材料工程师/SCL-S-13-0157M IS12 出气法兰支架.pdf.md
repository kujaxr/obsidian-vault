Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 支架是由于疲劳开裂的，主裂纹源产生于落料的边部，无缺陷。局部应力过大是导致其开裂的根部原因。
The bracket was broken by fatigue and the main crack was initiated at the cutting edge without detect. A big 
stress at the local is the root cause for the failure. 
（2） 材料的成分，强度，组织和厚度符合要求。The chemical composition, strength, microstructure and thickness 
meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核载荷；如果载荷过大，通过设计，降低其应力。Check the load. If the load is big, redesign to reduce 
the stress. 
（2） 了解失效车辆的路况以及驾驶员的习惯，检测该工况出现的振动载荷。Survey the road condition of 
the failed car and the drivers’ habit and check the vibration load under the condition. 
（3） 对售后类似里程数车辆的支架进行检查。Check the bracket of the customers’ car with the same 
mileage.
