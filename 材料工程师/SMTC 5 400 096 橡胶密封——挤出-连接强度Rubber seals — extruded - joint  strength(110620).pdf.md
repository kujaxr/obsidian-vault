1 范围 
    本标准规定了带有斜接角和斜接角、粗端或
嵌接结合端的膨胀和固态挤压橡胶密封条的测
试方法。 
本标准适用于带有斜接角和斜接角、粗端或
嵌接结合端的膨胀和固态挤压橡胶密封条的测
试。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies expanded and 
solid extruded rubber seals with mitred corners 
and mitred, butted or scarfed joints. 
This standard applies to expanded and 
solid extruded rubber seals with mitred corners 
and mitred, butted or scarfed joints. 
2 设备 
2  Apparatus 
2.1 剪刀和/或解剖刀。 
2.1  Scissors and/or scalpel.
2.2 适当的拉伸试验机，能提供50 mm/min 的
钳分离速度。 
2.2  Tensile testing machine having a jaw 
separation rate of 50 mm/min. 
3 试样准备 
3  Specimen Preparation 
3.1 裁取一个长为150 mm 的试样，并使粘结
处在长度的中间位置。 
3.1
Cut a test specimen 150 mm in length 
such that the joint is at the mid-length. 
3.2 在密封条粘结或结合在行李架的情况下，
用剪刀或解剖刀从行李架上剪掉橡胶部分，剪的
时候应尽可能的从靠近行李架的地方。 
3.2
In the case of seals adhered or bonded to 
a carrier, remove the rubber portion from the 
carrier by means of a scalpel or scissors, cut-
ting as close to the carrier as possible. 
4 步骤 
4  Procedure 
4.1 将试样夹持在拉伸试验机上，试验机每个
夹具的夹持的长度为25 mm。 
4.1  Position the test specimen in the tensile 
machine with 25 mm of the specimen in each 
of the jaws. 
4.2 按（50±5）mm/min 的速度进行试验来测
定粘结断裂时的载荷。 
 
4.2  With the tensile testing machine operat-
ing at a jaw separation rate of (50±5) mm/min, 
determine the load to break the bond. 
4.3 按以上程序进行另外两个试样的试验。 
 
4.3
Repeat the above procedure on two fur-
ther specimens. 
5 报告 
5  Report 
    报告平均断裂载荷和断裂的方式，（粘结或
橡胶），不考虑连接处的交叉部分。 
Report the mean breaking load and the 
mode of failure, (bond or rubber), irrespective 
of the cross section of the joint.
