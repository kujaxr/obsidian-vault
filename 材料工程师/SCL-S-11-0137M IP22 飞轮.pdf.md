Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 由于过载导致齿断裂。其原因无法从单个齿圈分析获得。 The gear teeth have failed due to overloading.  The reason for this cannot be 
determined from the ring gear alone.  
2、 一些齿部前表面的磨痕表明起动电机不容易咬合运转。Wear marks on the front surface of some teeth show that occasionally the starter 
motor cannot engage easily. 
3、 感应淬火工艺不当，使得淬火的组织不合格，从而降低了材料的强度，但这并不是导致齿圈失效的直接原因。The induction hardening 
process was incorrect, resulting in poor microstructure. Although this did not directly cause the failure, it did reduce the material strength. 
4、 上述损害汽车的原因，可能也对起动电机造成伤害，但这无法判断。The cause of the mentioned damage to the car, may have also caused 
damage to the starter motor, but this cannot be judged.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 SQE，QAL 和DRE 讨论超载可能的原因，并结合以往的个案。SQE, QAL and DRE to discuss possible reasons for overloading, considering 
previous cases. 
2、 请供应商改善热处理工艺和过程控制。Supplier must improve heat treatment technology and process control 
3、 如有可能，结合驱动齿轮，对失效原因作进一步的分析。Further failures should be accompanied by starter gear, if possible.
