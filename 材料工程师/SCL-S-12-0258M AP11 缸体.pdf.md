Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）缸体是由于过载而开裂，可能是右碰撞事故造成发动机局部应力过大所致。The engine block is cracked 
because of overload, which is possible led by a big local stress from the right collision accident. 
（2）材料满足图纸要求；无可能导致失效的缺陷。The material met the requirements of the drawing and there were no 
defects which could have led to the failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查与发动机相连接的底盘件。Check the chassis parts connected with the engine.
