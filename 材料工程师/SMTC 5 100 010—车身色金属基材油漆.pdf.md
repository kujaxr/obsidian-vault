自本标准实施之日起废除SMTC 5 100 010—2013

This standard is established for The surface 
protection requirement of exterior metal parts 
with car body color.
This standard is a revision for SMTC 5 100 
010—2013（V3）. This standard will replace 
SMTC 5 100 010—2013（V3） from the 
implementation date.
The main changes between this standard and 
SMTC 5 100 010—2013（V3）are as follows:
——In chapter 3.1.1,add the hazardous 
substances requirements for export parts
——adjust the structure of this standard
——change the method of Resistance to 
chemical to ISO 2812
——change the media of alkali-resisting to 5% 
sodium hydroxide solution
——change the media of acid-resisting to 10% 
sulfuric acid solution and 10% hydrochloric 
acid solution
——the requirement of light resistance change 
2000h to 2500KJ
——add Release and Revision section

本标准规定了多层漆的金属外饰件的防腐
保护要求。
本标准适用于多层漆的金属外饰件。
This standard specifies the requirements for 
decorative corrosion protection, which is 
applied onto visible external surfaces of metal 
parts.
This standard applies to visible external 
surfaces of metal parts.
