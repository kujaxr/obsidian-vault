1 范围 
本标准规定了汽车用连续热镀锌/锌铁合金钢
板及钢带的术语和定义、分类、代号、尺寸、外
形、技术要求和试验等。  
本标准适用于上海汽车股份有限公司乘用车
公司生产的车辆中使用的以冷连轧钢带为基板，
通过连续热镀工艺生产的热镀锌/锌铁合金钢板及
钢带，以下简称钢板。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the terms and 
definitions, 
classification, 
steel 
grades, 
dimension, shape, technical-requirements, 
inspection and test etc. of continuously 
hot-dip zinc (Z), zinc-iron alloy (ZF) coated 
steel sheet and strip for auto vehicle. 
This standard applies to the hot-dip zinc 
and zinc-iron alloy coated steel sheet and 
strip (hereinafter referred as steel sheet) 
which are manufactured from cold rolled 
steel strip used by SAIC MOTOR.

3 术语和定义 
3  Terms and definitions 
下列术语和定义适用于本标准。 
热镀纯锌镀层——热镀锌生产线上生产的由
纯锌组成的镀层。熔融锌液中锌含量应不小于
99%。  
For the purposes of this standard, 
following terms and definitions are applied 
for the standard. 
Hot dip zinc coating(Z)——The zinc 
coating is produced by hot dip zinc product 
line. And the zinc content of the melting liquid 
zinc is at least 99%.  
热镀锌铁合金镀层——在热镀锌生产线上，
将经过预处理的钢带浸入熔融锌液中所得到的镀
层。熔融锌液中锌含量应不小于99%。随后，通
过合金化处理工艺在整个镀层上形成锌铁合金
层，合金镀层中铁含量通常为7～15%。  
Hot dip zinc-iron alloy coating(ZF)  
— — Application of a zinc coating by 
immersing the prepared products in a molten 
bath containing a zinc content of at least 
99%. Subsequent annealing produces an 
iron-zinc coating with an iron content of 
normally 7% to 15%. 
无间隙原子钢——无间隙原子钢是在超低碳
钢中加入适量的钛或铌，使钢中的碳、氮间隙原
子完全被固定成碳、氮化物，钢中没有间隙原子
存在的一类钢。  
Interstitial free steel——Steel achieves 
interstitial free by adding some amount of 
titanium or niobium to the ultra low carbon 
steel that can change carbon and nitrogen to 
carbide and nitride. 
无间隙原子高强度钢——通过控制钢中的化
学成分来改善钢的塑性应变比（r值）和应变硬化
指数（n值）。由于钢中元素的固溶强化和无间隙
原子的微观结构，这种钢既具有高强度，又具有
非常好的冷成型性能，通常用来制作需要深冲压
的复杂部件。 
High strength interstitial free steel—
—R-value and n-value can be improved by 
controlling chemical components within the 
steel. This kind of steel has high strength and 
good 
formability 
as 
its 
solid 
solution 
strengthening elements is interstitial free 
atomic structure. 
高强度低合金钢——在低碳钢或超低碳钢
中，通过单一或复合添加铌，钛，钒等微合金元
素，形成碳氮化合物粒子析出进行强化，同时，
通过微合金元素的细化晶粒作用，以获得较高的
强度。 
High strength low alloy steel(HSLA)
——In the low carbon steel or extra low 
carbon steel, steel achieves the required 
strength level primarily by adding small 
amounts of micro-alloying elements such as 
niobium, titanium and/or vanadium, which 
contribute to fine carbide/nitride precipitation 
and grain size refining. 
烘烤硬化高强度钢——在低碳钢或超低碳钢
中保留一定量的固溶碳原子，必要时可通过添加
磷等固溶强化元素来提高强度，钢板成形后经涂
漆烘烤处理其强度会进一步提高，同时这种钢板
还具有较好的成形性能。 
Bake hardening high strength steel—
— An adequate amount of solid solution 
carbon and/or nitrogen remains in the low 
carbon steel or ultra low carbon steel, solid 
solution strengthening elements such as 
phosphorus may also be added to increase 
strength. The steel exhibits an additional 
increase in yield strength due to age 
hardening after forming which is accelerated 
by subsequent paint baking. Meanwhile, it 
has good formability. 
双相钢——钢的显微组织主要为铁素体和马
氏体，马氏体组织以岛状弥散分布在铁素体基体
上。双相钢无时效，具有低的屈强比和较高的加

higher 
strength 
and

strength. Its yield strength is higher than 
what is the same tensile strength as it. It has 
high energy absorbing property and high 
residual strain ability.  
相变诱导塑性钢——钢的显微组织为铁素
体、贝氏体和残余奥氏体，其中，残余奥氏体的
含量最少不低于5%。在成形过程中，残余奥氏体
可相变为马氏体组织，具有较高的加工硬化率、
均匀伸长率和抗拉强度。与同等抗拉强度的双相
钢相比，具有更高的延伸率。  
Transformation 
induced 
plasticity 
steel(TRIP) ——Steel consists mainly of a 
ferrite, bainite and remainder austenite. 
Austenite is not lower than 5%. In the 
forming process, remainder austenite can 
transform to martensite. This has a high work 
hardening rate, uniform elongation and 
tensile strength. Compared with the same 
tensile strength dual phase steel, it has 
higher elongation. 
拉伸应变痕——冷加工成形过程中，由于时
效的原因，钢板出现不均匀变形，导致钢板发生
局部塑性变形，最终会在钢板表面呈现与拉伸方
向成一定角度的一系列平行线状的褶皱或不规则
折线、不规则表面扭曲等有损表面外观质量的缺
陷。  
Stretcher strain marks——As aging 
during the steel cold processing, there may 
be  inhomogeneous deformation. It will 
result in partly plastic deformation. Bugs 
which influence surface quality will appear. 
These bugs include parallel drapes, irregular 
fold line, and irregular surface distortion.

5.1 化学成分 
钢板基板的化学成分按供货钢厂的标准规定
执行或由供需双方协商确定。 
烘烤硬化钢及磷固溶强化钢：C含量 + P含量
≤ 0.16%。 
5.1  Chemical composition 
The chemical composition of the steel should 
be in accordance with the steel factory’s 
standard or confirmed by both supplier and 
purchaser. 
For bake-hardening steels and phosphor 
solid solution strengthen steel: the content of 
carbon + content phosphor ≤ 0.16%.
