Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、连杆断裂成4 部分，断口均为过载断裂。 
Failure for the Connecing Rod belongs to overload fracture , the failure part broken into four parts. 
2、连杆的材料性能符合技术要求。 
The material properties for the Connecing Rod meet the technical requirements. 
   3、活塞的化学成分和硬度符合技术要求。 
      The chemical composition and hardness meet the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 建议系统排查该发动机的运行情况。 
To investigation the operation of the system of the engine.
