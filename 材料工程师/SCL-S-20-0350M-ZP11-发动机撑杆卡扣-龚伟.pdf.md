Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效件材料不符合技术要求，韧性差，装配时断裂。 
The performance of failure part material didn’t conform to requirement, with poor toughness, 
fractured occurs during assembly.

Order the supplier to explain and rectify the material problem.
