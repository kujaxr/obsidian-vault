Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论：1.大灯安装时孔对孔有错位，橡胶圈无法居中，顶在支架壁最薄处，导致失效件开裂； 
       2.零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions：1. The rubber loop nobbing the thinnest part of the bracket when the headlamp installation, made 
the parts cracked. 
             2.Material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议：1.检查结构与匹配 
Suggestions：1. Inspect the structure and matching
