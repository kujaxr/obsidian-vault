Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 零件受外力导致断裂。 
2．零件玻纤含量超过正常上限。 
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made part fracture. 
2. Glass content of part was beyond normal high-limit.        
建议： 1. 责令供应商解释材料问题。 
2. 加强对零件的保护。 
Suggestions: 1. Let supplier explain to the material problem. 
2. Protection to the part should be improved.
