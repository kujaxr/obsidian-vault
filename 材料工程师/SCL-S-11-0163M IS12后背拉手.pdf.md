Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 拉手卡钩受异常外力导致在根部发生快速断裂。 
2. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: After testing, the materials of the handle conform to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the handle, it can be seen that the fastener of handle was broken 
at the bottom region by an excessive outside force.   
建议： 增加卡钩根部圆角；采用强度高的材料制造拉手；避免对拉手施加过大外力。 
Suggestions：A round corner should be introduced at the bottom of the fastener. The material with higher strength 
should be used in the production of the handle. The excessive outside force on the handle should be avoided.
