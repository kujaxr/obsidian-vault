Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 导线受到较大振动应力在结构变形处疲劳断裂失效。The wire harness fatigue fractured in the structure 
deformation place due to large stress from vibration. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 对比国内国外车型差异，关注标定，油品差异等变化导致的工作应力差异。 Compare the differences between 
domestic and foreign models, pay attention to the differences in working stress caused by calibration, oil 
differences and other changes. 
（2） 对结构进行优化来减少振动强度，比如：增加护套、波纹管、固定点等。Optimize the structure to reduce the 
vibration intensity, such as increase the sheath, bellows, fixing point, etc.

不等，以长里程居多。送检其中1 根失效线束到实验室进行分析，该例售后行驶里程：6190Km。
