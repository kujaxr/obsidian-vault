Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1）铸件厚薄过渡区存在严重铸造缺陷-缩松，导致该部位强度下降，进而受冲击后发生自上
而下的脆性断裂。The casting thickness transition zone have serious casting 
defects-shrinkage, lead to decreased strength of the site, then occur brittle fracture 
top-down by impact. 
              2）铸件支臂表面的冷隔缺陷，导致该部位强度下降，受冲击后发生自上而下的脆性断 
裂。The casting arm surface have a cold shut defect, lead to decreased strength of  
the site, occur brittle fracture top-down by impact. 
建议Suggestions：1）改进压铸工艺，杜绝出现类似铸造缺陷，提高铸件质量。Improve the casting process, 
prevent similar casting defects, and improve the casting quality. 
                 2）加强对铸件的质量检查，如采用100%X 射线探伤等。Enhance the castings quality 
inspection, such as use 100% X-ray detection Etc. 
                 3）工程应在图纸技术要求中，将断裂处的铸件孔隙率要求提高至5%。DRE should 
technical requirements, the casting porosity requirement of the breaks to 5%.

III. 测试结果 Test Results 
1、失效零件宏观观察Macroscopic Observation of the Failure Parts 
（1）两件断裂的发动机悬置如图1、3所示，断裂在相同的位置。The failure parts have broken at the same 
position , shown in fig. 1 and 3. 
（2）1#失效件断口部位有一铸造冷隔缺陷，见图2。The No.1 fracture site have a casting defect-cold shut. 
（3）2#失效件断口表面存在一长28.5mm的铸造缩松缺陷，缩松处表面氧化严重，见图4。The No.2 fracture 
surface have a long 28.5mm casting defect- shrinkage, shrinkage surface oxidation serious, see Fig.4. 
（4）断裂面未发现任何塑性变形的痕迹，呈现脆性断裂形貌。Material deformation is not seen, brittle fracture 
morphology only.  
 
结论 result：铸件厚薄过渡区存在严重铸造缺陷-缩松和冷隔。The casting thickness transition zone have 
serious casting defects-shrinkage and cold shut.
