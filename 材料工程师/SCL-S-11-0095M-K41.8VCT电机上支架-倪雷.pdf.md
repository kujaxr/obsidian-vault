Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1) 
从失效模式可以确定，由于打磨导致裂源部位应力集中。在复杂工况下，该支架受到的力超过其疲
劳极限，致使在应力集中部位发生疲劳断裂。The failure mode was determined, as the grinding 
lead to stress concentration at the crack source area. In the complex conditions, the applied 
stress is higher than the fatigue limit of the bracket, causing fatigue fracture at the area of stress 
concentrate. 
2) 
零件的孔隙度不符合材料要求，在断面可见严重的铸造缺陷（缩孔及疏松），这导致材料强度的下
降。The Porosity isn’t conforming to material requirements, casting defect（shrinkage cavity and 
porosity）in the fracture surface, that reduced the material strength. 
3) 
材料的成分、硬度和金相等符合要求。The material composition, hardness and Microstructure 
meet the requirements.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
优化铸造工艺，控制铸造缺陷（包括飞边等，以避免打磨等工序）。Optimize the casting process, 
control casting defects（Include flash, etc., so as to avoid grinding processes）.
