Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）蜗壳在热应力和振动载荷共同作用下于应力较大的区域引发多源疲劳开裂。The turbocharger volute cracked by 
multisource fatigue at the local with a big stress which was caused by vibration and thermal stress. 
（2）材料的成分和硬度均符合要求；组织和孔隙未见异常。The chemical components and hardness meet the 
requirement and the microstructure and porosity are normal. 
（3）零件的壁厚符合要求。The thickness meets the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化局部结构，降低应力。Optimize the local structure and reduce the stress.
