Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：经测试，失效件的材料无异常。塑料基体断裂模式是装配时螺栓受到较大的侧向力，造成螺栓轴线偏
离，进而导致塑料基体开裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the plastic support, it can be seen that the axis of bolt has been 
deviated from the reference line by an excessive outside force during assembly. And then the plastic support 
is split by the bolt deviation. 
建议：装配时防止气枪扭力过大。 
Suggestions：The excessive torque of assemble tool should be prohibited during assembly of the start motor.

Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
通过图3 失效电机和图2 新电机对比可以看出，失效电机的螺栓轴线已经偏离正常基线，可知螺栓装配时受
到较大的侧向力；同时塑料基座在螺栓根部断裂，裂纹扩展方向与侧向力导致的开裂方向一致。 
From the comparison between the failure part and the new part, it can be seen that the axis of bolt has been deviated 
from the reference line by an excessive outside force during assembly. At the same time, the fracture of plastic support 
occurs at the bottom of the bolt and the propagation direction of the plastic support is the same as that caused by the 
outside force.

小结：通过失效电机塑料基体的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，螺栓装配时受到较大的侧向力，造成螺栓轴
线偏离，进而导致塑料基体开裂。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the plastic support, it can be seen that the 
axis of bolt has been deviated from the reference line by an excessive outside force during assembly. And 
then the plastic support is split by the bolt deviation.
