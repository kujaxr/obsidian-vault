Paint Coating on Exterior 
Plastic Parts Requirements 
1 范围 
本标准规定了依据配色表或特殊组合表的
塑料件（车身附件，如保险杠、轮罩饰板、后
导流板、行李箱盖及前翼板等等）上的底漆、
单层和多层漆的要求。 
本标准适用于涂装外饰件。 
1  Scope  
This 
technical 
supply 
specification  
specifies requirements for primer coat, single 
and multicoat painting acc. to color chart or 
special combination chart on plastic parts 
(body-mounted parts, e.g. bumper, wheel 
cover, rear spoiler, trunk lid, fender, etc).  
The standard apply to exterior plastic 
parts.

 外观和感触性能 
 
    零件整个表面外观匀称并满足图样要求和
符合认可样品的光泽和色调。不允许出现缩孔、
粗大裂纹以及其它影响外观要求的缺陷。色调
及光泽度的有关记录附于首次样品报告或开发
试验样品报告中。（参照SMTC 30513）。 
3.2 Appearance and haptic characteristics 
The parts shall exhibit a uniform appearance 
on their entire surface; they shall conform to 
the drawing requirements and to the released 
sample for gloss and color.  
Pores, coarse cracks, and other flaws which 
adversely affect the specified appearance are 
not permissible.  
Color 
and 
degree 
of 
glossiness 
are 
documented in the first-sample test report or 
development sample test report acc. to 
SMTC 30513. 
3.2 大气暴晒试验 
 
  所有涂装零件由上汽乘用车按照SMTC 5 
400 001 进行大气曝露试验（1 年湿热和1 年
干热）。由供货商负责解决试验过程中的质量问
题并改进涂装系统。 
3.3 Open-air weathering  
All painted components are tested by the 
SAIC Group according to SMTC 5 400 001 
(open-air weathering: 1 year in moist heat 
and 1 year in dry heat).  
The supplier is obligated to remove the cause 
for complaints occurring during this test and 
to improve the paint system.

3.3 涂装工序 
 
涂装工序由涂装方和油漆生产商共同调整。
双方确认首次样品试验报告，意味着双方对满
足要求共同负责。 
 
 
 
职责划分如下： 
 
---油漆生产商提供关于如何达到标准要
求的信息，制订工艺规程并在实施过程中提供
技术支持。 
3.4 Painting process  
  The painting process shall be coordinated 
by paint shop and paint manufacturer. Both 
parties will confirm the first-sample test 
report, meaning that both are responsible for 
fulfilling the specified requirements.  
Responsibilities are distributed as follows:  
   The

manufacturer 
provides

on

---涂装方提供基材信息（塑料种类、品

   The paint shop provides information about

3.4 预处理 
所有供试验用的样品发运前须经预置处
理：（18～28）°C室温中放置7 天或在50°C 中
放置48 h。 
依据DIN50014，在每次单项试验前须将零
件在18 至28°C中放置24h。 
 
3.5 Conditioning  
All samples delivered for testing shall be 
pre-conditioned:  
7 days at room temperature with narrow air 
temperature range (18 °C to 28 °C) or 48 h at 
50 °C.  
Prior to individual testing, the parts shall be 
conditioned for 24 h at 18 °C to 28 °C acc. to 
DIN 50 014.  
4 对结构和层厚的要求 
4 Requirements on structure and paint 
coat thickness  
4.1 概述 
仅在总体漆厚＜150μm 时允许一次性补
漆。 
通常允许就如下色调组合补漆： 
a) 同色补漆 
b)所有色调都允许的第一道漆：黑色、深
蓝色调、深灰色调(包括金属漆或者珠光漆)。 
其他色调组合补漆须征得该品牌相关部门的认
可。 
 
4.1 General  
Only one single re-painting job is permissible, 
considering the total paint coat thickness of < 
150 µm.  
In principle, the following color combinations 
are permissible for re-painting:  
a) first and second coat are of the same color 
b) permissible first coats for all colors: black, 
dark blue shades, dark gray shades (also 
metallic or pearlescent finishes) Other color 
combinations shall be agreed upon with the 
relevant release departments of each brand.
