Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 左前下控制臂在翻边、折弯过程中，由于材料相互挤堆、失稳而萌生原始裂纹，而后疲劳扩展。 
The original crack initiated because of the material extruding in the bending and flanging process. The crack 
propagated in the SD bench testing 
 
2. 零件成分、厚度符合要求，硬度及组织正常。 
Chemical components and thickness met the requirement. Microstructure and hardness were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 建议供应商优化零件的成型工艺。 
Optimize the forming process of the parts.
