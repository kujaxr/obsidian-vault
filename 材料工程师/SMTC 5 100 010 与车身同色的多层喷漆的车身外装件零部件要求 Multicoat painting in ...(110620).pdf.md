3.3  室外风化 
3.3  Open-air weathering 
在上海汽车集团，所有上漆的零部件按照
SMTC 5 400 001 进行实验（在湿热下2 年和在
干热下2 年）。供应商有责任消除在实验中产生
抱怨的原因并完善涂漆系统。 
All paint components are tested in the 
SAIC Motor according to SMTC 5 400 001 (2 
years in moist heat and 2 years in dry heat). 
The supplier is obligated to remove the cause 
for complaints occurring during this test and to 
improve the paint systems. 
 
3.4  施涂工艺 
施涂工艺应在涂漆车间与油漆厂家之间进
行协调。双方应共同确认首样实验报告，即双方
对完成规定的要求都负有责任。责任分配如下：
a) 油漆厂家提供完成SMTC 标准中规定要
求的信息以及关于油漆应用方法的支
持。 
b) 涂漆车间提供基材和预处理步骤的信
息，检查最早可能结合处的要求并请求
得到油漆应用方法的支持。 
c) 车身涂漆的工艺条件应由油漆厂家和
涂漆车间共同协商同意。 
d) 对于供应部件的涂漆，油漆厂家须提供
其加工指导方法，涂漆车间应确认对其
的粘附性。 
 
3.4
Paint process
The paint process shall be coordinated 
between paint shop and paint manufacturer. 
Both parties will confirm the first-sample test 
report, meaning that both are responsible for 
fulfilling the specified requirements. Respon-
sibilities are distributed as follows: 
a) 
The paint manufacturer provides in-
formation on the fulfillment of re-
quirements specified in the SMTC 
standards and provides support re-
garding the paint application me-
thods. 
b) 
The paint shop provides information 
on the substrate and pretreatment 
steps, checks the requirements at the 
earliest possible juncture and re-
quests support regarding the paint 
application methods. 
c) 
The processing conditions for body 
painting must be agreed mutually 
between the paint manufacturer and 
the paint shop. 
d) 
For the painting of supply parts, the 
paint manufacturer must make its 
processing guidelines available and 
the paint shop must confirm its ad-
herence to same.
