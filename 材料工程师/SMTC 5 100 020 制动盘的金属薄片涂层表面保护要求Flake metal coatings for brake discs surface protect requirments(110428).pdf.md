3.1 基本要求 
 
3.1  General requirements 
3.1.1  按照 SMTC 90008 避免有害物料。 
 
3.1.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances 
acc. to SMTC SMTC 90008. 
3.1.2  使用的镀层介质以及镀层必须不含Cr
6+
化合物。 
 
3.1.2  The coating media used as well as the 
coatings must contain no Cr（VI） compounds.
3.1.3  一次完整检验需要用到10个试样（视尺
度而定）。  
 
3.1.3  About 10 specimens are required for a 
complete test （depending on dimension）. 
3.2 外观与结构 
 
3.2  Appearance and structure 
3.2.1  工件整个表面必须符合表面保护特性的
规定要求。如果这些技术要求对某些部分表面不
适用，则应在图纸里标注。 
3.2.1  The whole surface of the work piece 
shall comply with the specified characteristic 
requirements for surface protection. If such 
technical requirements are inapplicable to par-
tial surface, it shall be marked in the drawing. 
3.2.2  不得存在会影响制动活塞外观、电镀层
粘附力和耐蚀性的气孔、粗裂纹或其他缺陷。经
电镀处理后的工件表面不得留有脏污或其他变
色现象。 
 
3.2.2  There shall be no pores, coarse cracks 
or other defects that may affect the brake piston 
appearance, the electroplated coating adhesion 
and the corrosion resistance. Work piece sur-
face after electroplating process shall not have 
smudge or other discoloration. 
3.2.3  通过专业化安装以及适合的运输条件，
需确保电镀层不会受到导致防护性能降低以及/
或者无法满足规定要求的损伤。 
3.2.3
Make sure that the electroplated coating 
will not be subject to damages that may impair 
its protective properties and/or prevent it from 
meeting the specified requirements by profes-
sional installation and proper transportation 
conditions. 
3.2.4  因使用或安装造成的轻微变形，不应导
致电镀层的剥落。 
 
3.2.4  Slight deformation resulted by use or 
installation shall not cause electroplated coating 
stripping. 
3.2.5  在制定相关的生产流程和控制流程时，
必须确保成品的应用性能和减震系统的功能不
受到负面影响。 
 
3.2.5
Relevant production process and control 
process shall not impair the application per-
formance and shock mitigation system of the 
finished parts.

3.3  Gas content

（沸点：240 ℃）脱气。不得过度加热，加热过
程中制动液不得接触加热板。试验制动液不能重
复使用。

brake fluid (boiling point: 240 
). Do not ove

制动液的耐热玻璃瓶里面。活塞孔必须朝下。在
瓶底部放入一块丝网，以防止活塞与玻璃瓶底相

3.3.3  A glass funnel with burette and stop 
valve shall be mounted on the upside of the 
bulb to capture the escaped gas. The lower 
margin of the glass funnel must be 20 mm lower 
than the brake fluid liquid level at least. Then, fill 
the whole funnel (including the burette) with 
brake fluid using a bulb tube, and fasten it up 
with the stop valve. Now, heat the whole set of 
device to 140 ± 10

20分钟。

piston surface, it is proved that the gas compo-

be shown by the readings on the burette. Start 
testing when reaching the lowest temperature

3.3.5  至少需对三个活塞进行试验。 
 
3.3.5  At least three pistons are required for 
testing. 
3.3.5.1  要求： 气体含量不超过每活塞0.1 
ml。 
 
3.3.5.1 Requirement: gas content ≤ 0.1 ml/ 
piston 
3.3.5.2  如果在生产过程中无法确保气体含量
低于或等于指标每活塞0.1 ml（即在测量中或者
个别情况下测出较高的气体含量），则制动活塞
必须进行在180 ℃保温15 min的热处理。 
 
3.3.5.2  If it cannot be guaranteed that the gas 
content is lower than or equal to the index of 0.1 
ml/piston during production (which means 
higher gas content is measured in the mea-
surement or in individual cases), then the brake 
piston must undergo thermal treatment for 15 
minutes at object temperature of 180 ℃.
