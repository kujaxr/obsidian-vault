Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 增压器隔热罩垫片是由于疲劳而开裂的，振动载荷较大是主要原因；The SHIELD ASM-TURBO HT was broken 
by fatigue and the main reason was a big quivering load. 
2. 材料成分、板材厚度符合要求，抗拉强度及组织未见异常；Chemical components and plate thickness met the 
requirement. Tensile strength and microstructure were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核载荷；Check the stress load.
