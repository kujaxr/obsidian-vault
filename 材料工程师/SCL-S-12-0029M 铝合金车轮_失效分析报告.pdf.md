Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于很严重的铸造缺陷缩松，铝轮辐条断裂。The wheel spoke fractured due to massive shrinkage porosity. 
2. 如此明显的缺陷，在产品检验时不可能没有发现。 It’s not possible that this defect could be missed during 
quality inspection. 
3. 填充物表示供应商已经发现了缺陷，并进行了浅表修补。Filler material shows that the supplier already found 
the defect, and carried out superficial “repair”. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 根据要求进行100%的X-Ray 质量检验。Carry out 100% X-Ray quality inspection according to requirement.
