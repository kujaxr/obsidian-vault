Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 舌片是在手柄装配前受到从外侧向内侧的外力而发生第一次的脆断，在手柄装配至总成后，在使用
过程中对舌片有一个从内侧向外侧的力，致使舌片发生第二次的脆断，此时舌片完全断裂；The part had first 
brittle failure caused by external forces from outside to the inside before handle assembled. After the handle assembled, 
the part had a complete rupture as a result of second brittle failure caused by forces from inside to the outside during use. 
2、 失效件材料符合技术要求；Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 在装配、物流等环节加强对零件的保护；Improve the protection of the part during the assembly and 
logistics. 
2、 加强对零件的检验。Improve the inspection of the part.
