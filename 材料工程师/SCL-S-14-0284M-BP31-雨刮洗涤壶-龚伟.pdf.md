Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受异常外力导致开裂。            
2. 失效件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The brittle fracture of the parts caused by abnormal force.

Suggestions:  1. Supplier improves the management and control of the trimming link.
