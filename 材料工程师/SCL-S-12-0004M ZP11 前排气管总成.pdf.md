Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）3#排气管的断裂是由于多源的双向疲劳所致；裂纹源主要产生于弯曲载荷较大的3#管底面；管材无法承受
其载荷而过早断裂失效。The 3# pipe break is cause by multi-origin reverse bending fatigue; the crack sources were 
mainly generated in the lower region of the pipe with big bending load which caused early fracture.  
（2）3#排气管与消音连接处大于75%的焊缝为虚焊。75% of the weld region connecting the pipe and silencer has no 
fusion. 
（3）材料正常。The material is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）DRE 和供应商对管材的承载应力进行确认。 DRE and supplier check the working stress of the pipe. 
（2）改善焊接质量，保证熔深。Improve the weld quality and assure the weld penetration.
