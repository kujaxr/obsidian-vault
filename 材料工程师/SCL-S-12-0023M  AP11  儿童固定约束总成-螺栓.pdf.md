Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
结论Conclusions： 
1. The failure reasons are as below: 
1#失效件： • 原材料原始裂纹引起的断裂。The bolt failed due to the original crack of the raw materials. 
• 芯部硬度不合格，高于技术要求。The hardness was higher than requirement (GB/T3098.1-2000). 
2#失效件： • 由于过载引起的断裂。The bolt failed due to overloading. 
• 热处理不合格，未经调质处理。The bolt was not quenched and tempered. 
• 硬度不合格，低于技术要求。The hardness was lower than requirement (GB/T3098.1-2000). 
• 螺牙有折叠缺陷。There were folding defects in threads. 
2. 图纸对螺栓的技术要求明确. The drawing specification is not correct. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、加强过程质量控制，如原材料、热处理、内部物流控制等。Strengthen the process of quality control, such as raw 
materials, heat treatment, logistics in supplier workshop. 
2、优化材料性能，如使用正规的冷镦钢。Optimization of material properties, such as using a regular cold heading steel. 
3、在图纸中明确螺栓的技术要求。Specify correct requirements on drawing.  Bolt must meet GB/T3098 Grade 8.8.
