Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属过载断裂。 
The failure due to overload. 
2、 失效件的铅含量不合格，其他材料性能符合图纸技术要求。 
The chemical composition for Pb did not meet the technical requirement. Other material properties meet the 
technical requirements.  
    
建议Suggestions： 
1、 建议系统检查零部件匹配情况和实际操控情况。 
 
Recommended to check the matching of system components and actual manipulation conditions.
