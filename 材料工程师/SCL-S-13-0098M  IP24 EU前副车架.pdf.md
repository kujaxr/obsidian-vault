Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）液压管侧虚焊是导致焊缝出现裂纹的根本原因。The weld has no fusion with the base material which led to the 
failure. 
（2）中塔与液压管间的匹配间隙过大，已导致出现焊接虚焊、熔深不良等焊接缺陷。Gap between midtower and tube 
is too big, easily cause infused and inadequate penetration. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）供应商必须优化匹配间隙，保证焊接质量。Supplier must improve the weld gap and insure the weld quality. 
（2）排查同批次零件质量，以确定是否存在疲劳风险，并采取相应措施。Trace the same batch of products and check 
the weld quality in order to determine whether there is a batch rick and then make corresponding measures.
