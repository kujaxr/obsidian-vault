Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）由于活塞的一端未装卡簧，活塞销向一侧滑动，导致连杆受到了异常的载荷，而发生断裂；连杆失效过程
中，连杆螺栓头部与缸体发生作用，出现剪切断裂。The piston pin moved to one side due to no circlip and made the 
rod bear a bending load and break; during the rod failure process, the bolt head suffered the load of the cylinder block 
and was broken by shear. 
（2）连杆的材料成分，硬度，密度以及脱碳层深度均符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components, hardness, 
density and decarburized depth meet the requirement and the microstructure is normal. 
（3）螺栓的硬度符合要求；无脱碳和螺牙缺陷；组织正常。The hardness of the bolt meets the requirement; no 
decarbonization and no defect of the screw thread were found and the microstructure is normal.  
建议Suggestions： 
（1）采取措施，管理装配过程，避免装配错误的发生。Take some measures and manage the assembly process to avoid 
the assembly error.
