Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 螺钉上垫片在固定孔边缘处产生的压伤变形导致该隔热罩发生疲劳断裂。The screw generated the deformation  
at the fixed holes of the thermal baffle, which result in the fatigue fracture. 
2. 隔热罩的成分，厚度符合要求；金相及硬度测试未发现异常。The chemical composition and thickness meet the  
requirement. Microstructure and hardness values were normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化设计，调整垫片与固定孔尺寸，避免边缘压伤。Optimizing the design and adjusting the dimension of the shim 
and the fixed hole to avoid the deformation.
