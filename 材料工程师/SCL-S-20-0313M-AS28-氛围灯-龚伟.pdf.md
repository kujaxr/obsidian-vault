Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 导光条表面存在点状压伤/划伤，在特殊角度下，伤痕被放大，形成类似裂纹状失效。 
There are crushing points /scratches on the surface of the light guide strip. Under special angles, 
the scars are enlarged, forming a crack-like failure. 
建议Suggestions：1.  排查均诺&华伟生产物流环节。 
                    Check Gennault & Huawei's production and logistics processes.
