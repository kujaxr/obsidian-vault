Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 排气管隔热罩是冲压过程中局部剧烈变形而开裂。The heat shield of exhaust pipe is cracked due to local severe 
deformation during punching process.  
2. 隔热罩的材料化学成分和金相组织符合要求。The material chemical composition and metallographic structure of 
the heat shield meet the requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 加强生产过程的管控。Strengthen the management and control of the heat shield in the the production process.
