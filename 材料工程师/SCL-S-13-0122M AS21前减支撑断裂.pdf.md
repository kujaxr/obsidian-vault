Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1.螺栓滚花凸台及支撑板上凸于橡胶面，与车身直接干涉、接触，导致疲劳断裂。The bolt knurling and the 
plate were above the rubber plane and contacted with the body, resulted in the fatigue fracture. 
2. 支撑板材质无异常。The material of steel plate was normal. 
3. 螺栓金相无异常，硬度、化学成分符合要求。Microstructure of bolt was normal, hardness and chemical components 
met the requirement.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
优化设计，保证滚花凸台及支撑板低于橡胶平面，确保其实际工况不与车身接触、干涉。Optimized the design. Make 
sure the knurling and the supporting plate were below the rubber plane and did not contact with the body。
