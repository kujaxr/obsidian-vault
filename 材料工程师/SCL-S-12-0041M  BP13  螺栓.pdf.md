Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、两个失效件均是由于过载引起的断裂。 
 
  Both failure due to overloading by assembly. 
     2、失效件和同批次零件质量符合技术要求。 
       The parts for the failure and same batch meet the requirements of drawing. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查装配工艺，并确保正确装配工艺实施（如多次装配、扭矩监控等）。 
Check the Assembly process, to ensure the correct assembly process implementation (such as repeatedly 
assembly,torque control,ect.)。 
2、 检查是否有润滑油污染。 
Check there is no contamination from lubricants.
