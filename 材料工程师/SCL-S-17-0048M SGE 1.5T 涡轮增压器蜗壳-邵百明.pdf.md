Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）涡轮增压器蜗壳在振动载荷和热应力共同作用下于应力较大的区域发生多源疲劳开裂。The turbocharger 
volute cracked by multisource fatigue at the local with a big stress which was caused by vibration and thermal stress. 
（2）材料的成分（除Nb 外）和硬度均符合要求；组织和孔隙未见异常。The chemical components (except for Nb) 
and hardness meet the requirement and the microstructure and porosity are normal. 
（3）零件的壁厚符合要求。The thickness meets the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化局部结构，降低应力。Optimize the local structure and reduce the stress.
