Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
 
（1） 材料使用错误导致制动卡钳支架过载断裂。The brake caliper bracket overload fractured due to the material 
erro. 
（2） 失效支架材料为灰铸铁，材料不符合图纸要求。The material of the failed bracket is gray cast iron, and the 
material does not meet the requirements of the drawing. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 抽检库存件了解卡钳材料状态。Spot check inventory to know the material status of calipers. 
（2） 评估材料使用不当的可能带来的风险。Assess the potential risks of improper use of materials. 
（3） 排查材料不当的原因。Reasons for improper investigation of materials.
