Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1）驻车制动支架焊缝开裂是由于焊缝熔合不良所致。 
The welding between the bracket and pipe was cracking because of bad fusion. 
（2）对同批次和不同批次零件焊缝抽检结果显示：支架侧均100%熔透、管子熔深正常。 
Sampling results show that: the bracket is 100%fusion in the same batch and different batches of parts and the tube 
penetration is normal.   
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 隔离该批次零件，并对已装车零件进行风险评估。 
Isolate the parts with the same batch and carry out the risk assessment of the loaded parts.   
（2） 供应商排查生产记录，提交产生焊接不良的分析报告。 
Suppliers check the production records and provide the analysis report.
