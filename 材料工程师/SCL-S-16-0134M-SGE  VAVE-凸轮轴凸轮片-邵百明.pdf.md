Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）失效凸轮片硬化层深度不均匀，局部淬透，且表面伴随着熔融的缺陷，从而形成了较大的材料内应力和缺口，
最终在装配应力的作用下发生脆性开裂。其中局部淬透和表面伴随的熔融缺陷是引起失效的主要因素；堵头装配
产生的装配应力是次要因素。The hardening of the failed cams is not uniform and the local was completely quenched and with 
melting defect, which formed a large internal stress and a material gap; finally the assembly stress caused the brittle fracture. The 
main factors are the local completely quenched and surface melted. The assembly stress generated y the plug assembly is a 
minor factor. 
（2）库存件和通过测试的拆机件均未发现表面熔融的痕迹和淬透的现象；个别零件端面硬化层深偏浅，不符合要
求。The stock parts and the disassemble parts passing the test were not found traces of the surface melting and completely 
quenching phenomenon. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）供应商应查找质量波动的原因，控制工艺过程，避免表面出现熔融的缺陷和淬透，确保硬化层深符合要求。
Supplier should find the reason for the quality fluctuation, control the process , avoid the defects of the surface melting and 
quenching to ensure the hardened layer.
