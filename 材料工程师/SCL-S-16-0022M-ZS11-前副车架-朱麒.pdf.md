Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1． 前副车架裂纹属于疲劳开裂，裂纹起源于上下片焊缝根部； 
The crack is belongs to tiredness, it is from the base of the upper and lower pieces; 
2． 零件开裂处的焊缝熔深符合要求，零件材料硬度未见异常； 
The part’s welding seam and part’s material thickness met the requirement; 
3． 未失效的零件（完成多通道1 件和单通道1 件）相同位置的焊缝符合要求，与失效件相比，无明显差异。 
The same welding of parts which are finished the testing met the requirement, it is close to the disabled part. 
建议Suggestions： 
1． 工程校对零件试验载荷； 
The engineer should collate the testing load; 
2． 优化焊接次序，并减小焊接匹配间隙； 
Optimize the welding process, left and right weld should be same, and reduce the welding space;  
3． 优化加强板形状和焊缝分布。 
Optimize the board’ form, and the welding distributing.
