Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：（1）下系杆支架为过载断裂，断裂面未见明显缺陷。The part of lower tie bracket is overload 
fracture，no obvious defects found on fracture surface. 
                （2）下系杆支架的成分、硬度、孔隙符合材料要求，金相组织正常。The chemical composition、
hardness and porosity of lower tie bracket parts are qualified, and the metallographic structure 
is normal.
