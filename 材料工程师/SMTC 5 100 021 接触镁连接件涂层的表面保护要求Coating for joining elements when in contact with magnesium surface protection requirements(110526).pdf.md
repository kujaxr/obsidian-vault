The standard specifies coating for joining 
elements when in contact with magnesium sur-
face protection requirements. 
The standard is adopted to joining elements 
when in contact with magnesium.

3.3 连接元件 
对于螺栓与螺母以及相关快速紧固元件，后面
提出的有关基体金属腐蚀的表面结构测试要求，仅
适用于头部和扳手面；在类似螺纹-和成形件(例
如，双头螺钉)情况下，仅适用于端面。对于连接
元件处与过程相关的涂层薄弱环节(例如，颈部与
螺纹)而言，为了达到应用目的，要依据5.2.2 条
降低要求。此外，关于锌电镀层最大可能厚度，要
考虑DIN EN ISO 4042 中的说明。这种较低的测试
要求，同样适用于快速紧固元件。按照 SMTC 7100 
013，进行摩擦系数测试。对于标准应用、带有公
制螺纹的连接元件而言，要涂覆组合有润滑剂的封
闭剂。 
3.3  Joining elements 
For screws, nuts and quick fastening ele-
ments, the test requirements with respect to base 
metal corrosion for the surface finishes listed be-
low apply only to the head and wrench bearing 
surfaces; in the case of similar threaded and 
shaped parts, such as e.g. tap end studs, only for 
the face surfaces. The reduced requirements ac-
cording to Section 5.2.2 apply to process-related 
weak spots of the coating on joining elements 
such as the shank and the thread. Furthermore, 
the specifications in DIN EN ISO 4042 concerning 
the maximum possible thickness of the electrop-
lated coating in the threaded profile shall be taken 
into consideration. These reduced requirements 
also apply to quick fastening elements. The coef-
ficients of friction are tested according to SMTC 
7100 013. For joining elements with metric thread, 
the seal with integrated sliding agent shall be 
used for standard applications.

4.1.2  电解液 
用弱酸性或者弱碱性电解液进行镀锌，其中不
允许由于铁渗入而污染镀锌层。 
4.1.2  Electrolyte 
Zinc coating shall be made from slightly 
acidic or alkaline electrolytes; the zinc layer must 
not be contaminated by incorporated iron. 
4.1.3  转化涂层 
为了改善耐腐蚀性，要进行厚层钝化处理。钝
化溶液不允许含有六价铬化合物，确保形成无六价
铬的转化涂层。 
4.1.3 Conversion layer
Thick layer passivation is used to improve the 
corrosion resistance. The passivation treatment 
solution must not contain any Cr(VI) compounds 
in order to ensure that the resultant conversion 
layers are likewise Cr(VI)-free.

4.1.4  封闭剂 
在钝化处理结束中，或在湿态中，或在4 h之
内涂覆封闭剂。借助于附加的封闭剂，可微量增加
涂层结构厚度，但不允许损害功能性。在用户视野
范围内，对零件来说，尤其不允许因剧烈色调变化
波动而损害零件的外观和彩虹状。通过后处理，决
不允许由于结晶或者薄膜的形成而在零件表面上
保留不美观的水滴状残留物。除了腐蚀防护，如果
要求其它功能性的表面性能(例如像可上漆性，媒
质相容性，滑移性能，可硫化性)，则必须对零件
进行特定测试或者功能性测试。 
4.1.4 Sealing
The seal shall be applied after passivation, 
either wet-on-wet or within 4 hours. The thickness 
of the layer structure can be increased slightly by 
the additional sealing, but this must not impair the 
functionality. Especially in those areas visible for 
the customer the outer appearance of the parts 
must not be impaired by strong color variations or 
iridescence. The post-treatments must not cause 
any impairments such as unsightly drop-like resi-
dues resulting from crystallization or formation of 
a film on the part surface. 
If besides the corrosion protection further 
functional surface characteristics (such as painta-
bility, compatibility with agents, sliding properties, 
vulcanizability) are required, part-specific tests or 
functional tests shall be performed.

5  Properties 
5.1 粘附强度 
依照DIN EN ISO 2819 进行热冲击测试。测试
部件在(220±10) ℃情况下存放30 min，随后把它
浸没在温度为15 ℃ 到25 ℃的水中。要求：锌镀
层没有气泡或不出现大面的剥离。 
5.1 Adhesive strength
Thermal shock test based on DIN EN ISO 
2819. The specimen part is aged for 30 minutes at 
(220 ± 10) °C and then dipped in water at a tem-
perature of 15 to 25 °C. Requirement: no 
large-scale or bubble-shaped stripping of the zinc 
coating. 
5.2 耐腐蚀性 
依照DIN 50 021-SS 进行测试，依照DIN EN 12 
329 进行评估。必须保证系统在交货状态以及120℃
热老化24 h 后的耐腐蚀性。这是在任何情形中都
要遵守的最低要求。

temperature of 120 °C. These are minimum re-
quirements and shall always be adhered to.
