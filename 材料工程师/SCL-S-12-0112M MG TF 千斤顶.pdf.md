Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）凸焊熔合区域不足，降低承载能力，从而导致千斤顶失效。 Not full fusion in the weld reduces the load ability 
and leads the failure of the jack.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）在零件图纸中，应明确界定焊接质量标准。The weld quality standard should be clearly defined in the part 
drawing. 
（2）采用ZP11 的千斤顶设计方案。Use the design of the ZP11 jack
