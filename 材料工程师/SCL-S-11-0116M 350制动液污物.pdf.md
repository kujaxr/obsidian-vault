Ⅳ. 宏观分析 Macroscopic observation 
图1 中的混浊制动液取自制动液壶（见图2）。图3 为制动液壶的剖面图，没有杂质沉积。 
The cloudy brake fluid shown in figure 1 was acquired from brake fluid pot (Figure 2). Figure 3 reveals the section 
of brake fluid pot. There was little feculence in it.  
    
      
 
图2 制动液壶                         图3 制动液壶剖面 
   Fig. 2 Brake fluid pot                    Fig. 3 Section of brake fluid pot 
 
将浑浊的制动液过滤，得到澄清的制动液如图4，得到的杂质如图5。 
 
Clear brake fluid and feculence were acquired by filtration and were shown in figure 4 and figure 5 respectively. 
       
 
 
  图4 过滤后的制动液                             图5 过滤后得到的杂质 
         Fig. 4 Brake fluid after filtration                    Fig. 5 Feculence acquired by filtration 
 
小结：制动液失效的原因是夹带了大量杂质。 
Conclusion: The large amount of feculence in brake fluid led to the failure.
