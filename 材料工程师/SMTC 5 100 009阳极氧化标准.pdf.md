To specify the Anodized coating requirement 
on aluminum parts we issued the standard.

009-2013 (V3), Anodized coating on aluminum 
parts requirements for surface protection.

from the implementation date of this standard.

vious version is described as follows:

1 范围Scope 
本标准规定了纯铝件和铝锻件上的阳极氧
化层的表面保护要求。 
本标准适用于装饰性的汽车用内外饰零件，
也可用于有表面功能性要求的汽车内外饰零件。 
 
   
This standard specifies requirements for anodic 
oxide coatings on pure aluminum and aluminum 
wrought alloy parts.  
 
The standard is suitable for decorative interior 
and exterior automobile components; however, 
they can also be applied for achieving functional 
surface properties.

5 氧化层性能Anodized Coating’s performance 
5.1 镀层厚度Layer thickness 
 
 
厚度要求为： 
a) Sp-WH10/WH20： 5-10µm；  
b) Sp-WH30/WH40： 8-15µm； 
c) Sp-WH60： 5-15µm.(如因功能性要求镀
层厚度超过15µm，在保证镀层不开裂的情况下
可与中性实验室进行协商) 
 
Thickness Requirements: 
a) Sp-WH10/WH20: 5-10µm; 
b) Sp-WH30/WH40: 8-15µm. 
c) Sp-WH60: 5-15µm(If the layer thickness 
is more than 15 m, it can be negotiated with the 
CENTER LAB in case the coating is not cracked) 
5.2  耐腐蚀性能Corrosion resistance 
根据ISO 9227-SS，盐雾试验480h。要求：
无表面可见腐蚀现象。 
 
Test: according to ISO 9227-SS for 480 h. Re-
quirements: no visual surface change due to 
corrosive effects. 
5.3 冷凝水试验 Moisture condensate alternating atmosphere test

对于Sp-WH10、Sp-WH20、Sp-WH30 和
Sp-WH40；要求按DIN 50 018-KFW2.0 S 进行
5 个周期的试验。要求：无表面可见腐蚀；如果

As to surface protection type Sp-WH10, WH20, 
WH30 and WH40, test according to DIN 50 
018-KFW 2.0 S for five cycles. Requirement: no 
change in the surface appearance caused by 
corrosion; if the slight stain occurred, if it can be 
deleted by polish cotton, then could be allowed. 
5.4  耐碱性Alkali resistance 
对于Sp-WH10、Sp-WH20、Sp-WH30 和
Sp-WH40；试验：把零件浸入18-20 ℃，由2 g/l 
Na3PO4 200 mg/l CI-；pH = 12.5±0.2 组成的
无铝混合试验液中（PH 值用NaOH 和H3PO4
调整）。 把零件约10cm 的阳极氧化表面浸入到
试验液中，浸置约10 min，然后用蒸馏水洗净，
并自然吹干。其后，把对该零件进行DIN 50 018 
KFW 2.0 SO2 测试，5 个周期，然后进行评价。
要求：用抛光剂，或蘸有氧化镁溶液的抛光棉进
行处理后，表面无变化。无明显浸入的边界痕迹。 
 
 
   
As to surface protection type Sp-WH10, WH20, 
WH30 and WH40,Test: The specimen shall be 
immersed in an aluminum-free test solution 
comprising 2 g/l Na3PO4, 200mg/l Cl-, pH = 
12.5±0.2 (achieved through NaOH and H3PO4) 
at a temperature of 18 °C to 20 °C.A length of 
approx. 10 cm of the anodically oxidized surface 
of the specimen shall be immersed for ten mi-
nutes in the test solvent. The surface is then 
rinsed with distilled (or fully deionised) water and 
dried in air. After the immersion process the spe-
cimen shall be subjected to moisture condensate 
alternating atmosphere DIN 50 018 KFW 2.0 SO2 
for 5 cycles and subsequently be assessed. Re-
quirements: No visual change after treatment of 
the surface with a polishing agent or with an 
aqueous suspension of magnesium oxide and 
polishing cotton. No visibly perceptible immersion 
line.
