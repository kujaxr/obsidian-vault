Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，失效件的材料无异常。 
2. 风门加强筋内存在空洞缺陷，造成该处强度降低。风门堵转时，风门受到持续的扭转力作用，在加
强筋缺陷处发生韧性疲劳开裂；裂纹扩展至风门本体时，本体发生快速脆断。 
Conclusions: 1. After the test, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement.  
2. According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the choke valve, it can be seen that the 
mechanical property of the hole region was decreased by the hole defect. A ductile crack firstly occurred near 
the defect region under a continuous stress when the valve was blocked. Then the matrix of valve was quickly 
broken when the crack propagated to the matrix shell of valve during it’s working. 
建议：改善注塑工艺，避免空洞等缺陷产生。 
Suggestions：The injection molding process of the choke valve should be optimized for the micro-defect avoidance.

小结：由于空调国际对风门板的原材料性能指标没有规定，因此采用SMTC22555 标准对风门板进行测试。
经测试，失效件的材料和冲击强度符合设计要求。失效件第一次熔融过程和第二次熔融过程中的熔融
焓变化不大，零件的注塑工艺参数较好。 
Conclusion: The Air International Shanghai Co.,Ltd don’t have the mechanical property requirement of the choke 
valve. So test standard of SMTC22555 is used here as the property requirement. After the tests, the material 
and unnotched impact strength of the part conform to the design requirement. The change of ΔH between the 
first circle and the second circle of the failure part is small, so the injection process of it is good.

小结：通过风门的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，风门加强筋内存在空洞缺陷，造成该处强度降低。风门堵
转时，风门受到持续的扭转力作用，首先在加强筋缺陷处发生韧性疲劳开裂；裂纹扩展至风门本体时，
本体发生快速脆断。 
Conclusion: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the choke valve, it can be seen that the 
mechanical property of the hole region was decreased by the hole defect. A ductile crack firstly occurred near 
the defect region under a continuous stress when the valve was blocked. Then the matrix of valve was quickly 
broken when the crack propagated to the matrix shell of valve during it’s working.
