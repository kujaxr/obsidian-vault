Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件内应力过大，导致开裂。 
Excessive stress in the failure part, resulted in cracking.   
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商排查冷模件流出原因。 
                 Suppliers check the cause of cold mold outflow. 
2. 优化现有注塑工艺，避免量产零件的内应力开裂。 
                 Optimize existing injection molding processes to avoid internal stress cracking in mass-produced 
parts.
