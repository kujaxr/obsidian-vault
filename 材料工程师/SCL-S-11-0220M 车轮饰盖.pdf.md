Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 车轮饰盖失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
2. 装配时，卡爪受外力超负荷导致断裂。 
Conclusions: 1. The material performance of failure wheel decorative cover conforms to requirements. 
           2. The plastic hook of wheel decorative cover was overloaded during assembly, which led to fracture. 
建议：1. 改进装配工艺。  
      2. 更改卡爪设计。 
Suggestions：1. The assembly technology should be improved.  
            2. The design might be changed.
