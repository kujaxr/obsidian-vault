Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是由于螺母焊接位置偏移引起螺栓与螺母之间的螺纹连接异常造成螺母和螺栓滑牙，致使被连接
件松旷。 
Failure is due to a nut welded pieces position offset caused. 
2、 材料性能未见异常。 
Material properties of the parts were normal.  
建议Suggestions： 
1、 供应商加强过程质量控制并提交分析整改报告。 
Suppliers to strengthen quality control and rectification report submitted to analysis.
