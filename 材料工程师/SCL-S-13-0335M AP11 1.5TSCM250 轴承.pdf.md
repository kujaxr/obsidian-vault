Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 断口已完全磨损，无法识别断裂类型；其它区域未发现裂纹和异常损伤。The fracture was completely worn 
and can’t be analysed; there are not crack and abnormal damaged on the other region. 
（2） 保持架的表面硬度，渗层深度符合要求。 The surface hardness and the hardening depth meet the requirement.  
（3） 断裂处和非断裂处的基体组织特征相同（局部晶粒长大，出现混晶）；硬度一致；在非翻边处，混晶区的
硬度与正常区域的硬度相当。The microstructure characters at the fracture and non fracture location are same 
(The grain of the local grew big and mixed grain microstructure happened); the hardness is similar; at the area 
without flanging, the hardness at the mixed grain microstructure and the normal region is equivalent. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 请供应商开展工艺试验，研究工艺参数的优化对晶粒长大的影响。Please supplier make an process 
experiment to research the process parameters affection on the grain growing. 
（2） 对其它试验后的保持架进行检查，看是否有裂纹和混晶。Check other cages after experiments and distinguish 
if there are cracks and mixed grain. 
（3） 对保持架的载荷进行校核。Check the load of the cage.
