Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  刹车盘过载开裂。The brake disc is cracked due to overload. 
2.  刹车盘壁厚正常，材料硬度、石墨片形状及大小正常满足要求。The brake disc wall thickness is normal, and the material 
hardness, graphite sheet shape and size meet the requirements.
