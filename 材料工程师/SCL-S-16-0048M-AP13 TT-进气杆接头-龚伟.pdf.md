Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件模具设计不合理，注塑不佳，导致零件内部存在大量孔洞。零件强度降低，受力后断
裂。 
The mold design of the parts is unreasonable with a poor injection molding process, leading to a 
large number of holes inside of the part. The strength of parts decreased, and fractured after 
forced.               
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
   Material performance of part conformed to requirements.   
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商修模或者新开模具。 
                   Supplier adjusts design of the mold or designs a new one.
