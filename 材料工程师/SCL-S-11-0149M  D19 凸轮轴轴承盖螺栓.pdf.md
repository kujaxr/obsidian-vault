Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是在装配过程中过载引起的断裂，引起过在的原因可能是扭矩过大或涂装了润滑剂。The bolt has failed due to 
overloading during tightening.  Possible causes are too high torque, or additional lubricant. 
2、失效件芯部硬度、螺牙缺陷、表面全脱碳层深度、基体组织均合格。 
     The components core hardness, thread defects, surface decarburization depth and microstructure meet the 
requriements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 螺栓的装配拧紧必须符合技术要求，如无特殊要求，附加的润滑是不被使用的。 
The bolt must be tightened according to the design torque & method.  No additional lubricant may be used 
if not specified.
