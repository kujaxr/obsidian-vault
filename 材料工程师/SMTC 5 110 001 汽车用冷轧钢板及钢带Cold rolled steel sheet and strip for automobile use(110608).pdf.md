和代号、尺寸、外形、技术要求和试验等。 
 
本标准适用于上海汽车集团股份有限公司乘
用车公司生产的车辆中使用的冷轧钢板及钢带，
以下简称钢板。 
 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the terms and 
definitions, 
classification 
and 
symbol, 
dimension, shape, technical-requirements, 
inspection and testing etc. of cold rolled steel 
sheet and strip for automobile. 
This standard applies to the cold rolled 
steel sheet and strip used by SAIC MOTOR.

3.1 无间隙原子钢——无间隙原子钢是在超低
碳钢中加入适量的钛或铌，使钢中的碳、氮间隙
原子完全被固定成碳、氮化物，钢中没有间隙原
子存在的一类钢。 
 
3.1  Interstitial free steel 
Steel 
achieves 
interstitial 
free 
by 
additions of titanium or niobium to the ultra 
low carbon steel that can change carbon and 
nitrogen to carbide and nitride. 
3.2 无间隙原子高强度钢——通过控制钢中的
化学成分来改善钢的塑性应变比（r 值）和应变硬
化指数（n 值）。由于钢中元素的固溶强化和无间
隙原子的微观结构，这种钢既具有高强度，又具
有非常好的冷成型性能，通常用来制作需要深冲
压的复杂部件。 
 
3.2  High strength interstitial free steel 
R-value and n-value can be improved by 
controlling chemical components within the 
steel. This kind of steel has high strength and 
good 
formability 
as 
its 
solid 
solution 
strengthening elements are an interstitial free 
atomic structure. 
3.3 高强度低合金钢——在低碳钢或超低碳钢
中，通过单一或复合添加铌，钛，钒等微合金元
素，形成碳氮化合物粒子析出进行强化，同时，
通过微合金元素的细化晶粒作用，以获得较高的
强度。 
 
3.3  High strength low alloy steel (HSLA) 
This 
steel 
achieves 
the 
required 
strength level primarily by adding small 
amounts of micro-alloying elements such as 
niobium, titanium and/or vanadium, which 
contribute to fine carbide/nitride precipitation 
and grain size refining. 
3.4 烘烤硬化钢——在低碳钢或超低碳钢中保
留一定量的固溶碳原子，必要时可通过添加磷等
固溶强化元素来提高强度，钢板成形后经涂漆烘
烤处理其强度会进一步提高，同时这种钢板还具
有较好的成形性能。 
 
3.4  Bake-hardening steel (BH) 
An adequate amount of solid solution 
carbon and/or nitrogen remains in the low 
carbon steel or ultra low carbon steel, solid 
solution strengthening elements such as 
phosphorus, etc. may also be added to 
increase strength. The steel exhibits an 
additional increase in yield strength due to 
age hardening after forming which is 
accelerated by subsequent paint baking. 
Meanwhile, it has good formability. 
3.5 碳锰钢——以碳、锰原子固溶为主要强化机
理的低碳结构钢。 
 
3.5  Carbon manganese steel 
It refers to the low carbon structural steel 
whose strengthening mechanism is carbon 
and manganese solution strengthening. 
3.6 双相钢——钢的显微组织主要为铁素体和
马氏体，马氏体组织以岛状弥散分布在铁素体基
体上。双相钢无时效，具有低的屈强比和较高的
加工硬化指数以及烘烤硬化值，是结构类零件首
选材料之一。 
 
3.6  Dual phase steel (DP) 
Steel with both higher strength and 
enhanced formability, consists of a ferritic 
matrix containing a hard martensitic second 
phase in the form of islands. Dual phase 
steel does not age. It has a low yield ratio, 
high 
work-hardening 
index 
and 
bake-hardening 
value. 
It 
is 
good 
for 
structural parts. 
3.7 马氏体钢——钢的显微组织几乎全部为马
氏体组织，马氏体钢具有较高的抗拉强度，一般
在1000MPa 以上。马氏体钢通常需进行回火处理
以改善其塑性，使其在如此高的强度下，仍具有
足够的成形性能。 
 
3.7  Martensitic steel (MS) 
Almost all of the microstructure of steel 
is martensite. Martensitic steel has higher 
tensile strength, generally above 1000 MPa. 
Martensitic steel usually needs tempering 
treatment to improve its plasticity and thus it 
still has enough forming properties with so 
high strength. 
3.8 相变诱导塑性钢——钢的显微组织为铁素
体、贝氏体和残余奥氏体，其中，残余奥氏体的
含量最少不低于5%。在成形过程中，残余奥氏体
可相变为马氏体组织，具有较高的加工硬化率、
均匀伸长率和抗拉强度。与同等抗拉强度的双相
钢相比，具有更高的延伸率。

steel (TRIP) 
Steel consists mainly of a ferrite, bainite 
and remainder austenite. Austenite is not 
lower than 5%. In the forming process,  the 
remaining 
austenite

will result in partly plastic deformation. 
Defects which influence surface quality will 
appear. These defects include paralled 
drapes, irregular fold line, and irregular 
surface distortion.
