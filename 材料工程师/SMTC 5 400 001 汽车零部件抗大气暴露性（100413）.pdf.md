1 范围 
本标准规定了安装状态的整车的乘员室零
部件、汽车蒙皮和外装件的抗大气暴露性试验。
 本标准适用于整车、零部件和材料试样的
开发和系列监测。应用该标准的目的是确保有足
够的抗大气暴露性。 
1 Scope  
This standard describes the testing of 
resistance 
to 
open 
air 
weathering 
of 
components of the passenger compartment, the 
vehicle skin and add-on parts in the vehicle in 
the installed condition. It must be applied in the 
development and series monitoring of vehicles, 
components 
and 
material 
samples. 
The 
application of this standard is intended to 
ensure 
sufficient 
resistance 
to 
open 
air 
weathering.

5.1.1 附加的抗大气暴露性 
2年抗大气暴露性的附加要求适用于下列零
部件/材料： 
― 油漆（外部） 
― 前大灯（配光镜） 
原则上下述也同样适用：2年的大气暴露之
后，无下列有害影响：材料崩解、分离（分层）、
开裂、断裂或孔洞，这些会显著地消弱零部件的
功能或导致其失效。 
5.1.1 Additional Resistance to Open Air 
Weathering  
The additional requirement of 2 years 
resistance to open air weathering applies to the 
following components/materials: 
― Paint (exterior) 
― Front headlights (lenses)

2 years open-air weathering, no detrimental 
effects

material 
disintegration,

5.2 有效性 
“按照SMTC 5 400 001，抗大气暴露性：
无有害影响”的要求适用于下列条目的图纸和技
术规范： 
― 零部件 
― 纺织品 
― 纤维材料 
― 多层结构材料 
― 涂层（保护涂层和/或装饰涂层） 
― 薄膜 
― 泡沫塑料 
― 等等 
和所有的金属，聚合物或天然材料（例如木
材、皮革）。 
发动机舱的零部件除外，因为环境循环试验
和腐蚀试验必须确保其功能。 
5.2 Validity 
The requirement "resistance to open air 
weathering per SMTC 5 400 001: no detrimental 
effects" applies to drawings and technical 
supply specifications of all  
― Components  
― Textiles  
― Fiber materials  
― Sandwich structures  
― Coatings (protective and/or decorative) 
― Films  
― Foamed plastics  
― etc. 
of metals, polymers or natural materials 
(e.g., wood, leather).  
Components of the engine compartment 
are exempt because their function must be 
sufficiently ensured by environmental cycle tests 
and corrosion tests.

5.3 Documentation of Resistance to Open 
Air Weathering 
Documentation shall be provided in the 
course of open-air weathering of the assemblies 
(ASSY) and the components in as-installed 
condition on vehicles at the open-air weathering 
proving grounds used by the SAIC Group.

6.1 试验场 
上汽集团目前使用下列试验场做老化试验：

6.1  Proving Grounds  
The SAIC Group currently uses the 
following grounds for aging: Kalahari (dry heat), 
Arizona (dry heat) and Florida (humid heat).  
6.1.1 气象数据 
试验场的工作者必须持续记录气象数据并

数据（上汽企业内部网，CD-ROM光盘或者纸质
版）。该数据以数字形式归档。 
需要记录的气象数据： 
― 气温 
― 全局辐射 
― 紫外辐射 
― 光照时间 
― 风速 
― 风向 
― 降雨量 
― 潮湿时间 
― 相对湿度 
6.1.1  Weather Data  
The operator of the proving grounds must 
continually record and document the weather 
data. The weather data must be made available 
to users of the grounds (SAIC intranet, 
CD-ROM, or in paper form). The data are 
archived in digital form.  
Weather data that must be recorded:  
― Air temperature  
― Global irradiation  
― UV radiation  
― Duration of sunshine  
― Wind velocity  
― Wind direction  
― Amount of precipitation  
― Wet time  
― Relative humidity

6.2.2.1 试验架 
标准老化试验可以用闭式架（与水平方向成
90°和5°）。该试验架用于汽车蒙皮和外装件的
大气暴露试验，应该选取零部件和材料试样的老
化类型以便辐射和温度载荷与其在整车上的状
态具有可比性（尽可能在零部件的底部加以绝
热）。必须借助于相当的温度测量方法来确保这
一点。 
地表面：草地（湿热），沙地（干热）。 
只有提供匹配性文件和咨询实验室负责部
门后，才能改变地表面。 
朝向：根据第6.2.1.2节。 
 
 
 
示例： 
― 90°试验架：轮装饰零部件，散热器隔栅，前
后字标，侧面转向信号灯 
― 5°试验架：塑料翼子板，前大灯，尾灯总成，
风窗玻璃刮水器 
注：高热负荷的零部件例如流水槽，侧面保护零件
或者水箱盖只能在整车上做试验（试验车辆），这是因
为试验架老化试验热负荷过低。

Closed shelves (90° and 5° to the 
horizontal) are available for standard aging 
tests. The shelves are for open-air weathering 
of skin and add-on parts. The aging type for 
components and material samples should be 
selected 
such 
that 
the 
radiation 
and 
temperature load is comparable to the situation 
on the vehicle (possibly by insulating the bottom 
of the component). This must be ensured by 
means 
of 
comparative 
temperature 
measurements.  
Surface: grass (humid heat), sand (dry 
heat).  
The surface may only be changed with 
documentation of suitability and in consultation 
with the Laboratory Department.  
Orientation: according to section 6.2.1.2  
Examples:  
― 90° shelves: wheel trim elements, radiator 
grilles, front and rear emblems, side turn 
signal lamps  
― 5° shelves: plastic fenders, headlights, 
taillamp assemblies, windshield wipers  
Notes: Components with high thermal load such as 
drip moldings, protective side moldings or water box 
covers should only be tested in the vehicle (test 
vehicles) since the thermal load is too low in shelf aging.
