Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 失效弹簧发生了疲劳断裂。The failed spring fatigue fractured which may be relation to the non-metallic 
inclusion.  
（2） 失效弹簧脱碳和夹杂物符合要求，硬度和组织正常。The detect area decarburized layer and non-metallic 
inclusion of the failed spring meet the requirement, the hardness and microstructure are normal. 
（3） 抽检同批次弹簧夹杂物符合要求。The non-metallic inclusion of the same batch spring meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 重新校核弹簧的实际工作载荷，横向对比NSE 不同机型间的差异。Recheck the actual working load of the 
spring and compare the difference between different models of NSE.
