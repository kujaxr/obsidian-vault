Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件安装孔装配时开裂。 
Failure parts crack in the installation hole during assembly process.  
2. 零件存在注塑孔洞。 
There are injection holes in the parts. 
3. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1. 排查装配环节异常。 
                 Troubleshoot assembly problems. 
2. 供应商优化注塑工艺以消除注塑孔洞。 
                 Supplier optimizes injection molding process to eliminate injection holes.
