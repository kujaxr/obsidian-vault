Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions: 
（1）扭杆发生了脆性断裂，与折弯半径偏小有关。The torsion bar was broken by brittle fracture, which was related to 
the smaller bending radius. 
（2）失效扭杆的成分符合要求；组织和硬度正常。The chemical components of the torsion bar met the requirements and 
the microstructure and hardness are normal. 
（3）抽检的同批次零件和新批次零件的强度略有偏低，但这不会引起脆断。The strength of the same batch parts and 
new batch parts is a little lower than the requirements, which can’t cause the brittle fracture.  
建议Suggestions： 
（1）查找折弯半径偏小，尺寸一致性较差的原因。Find out the reason that the bending radius is too small and the size 
consistency is poor. 
（2）确定内径的尺寸要求，并将内径尺寸作为实际产品的尺寸控制对象。Determine the size requirements of the inner 
diameter, and make the size of the internal diameter as the actual product control object. 
（3）对返回给供应商的不合格件进行报废处理。Discard the rejected parts returned to the suppliers. 
（4）加强原材料性能的控制。Strengthen the control of the material mechanical properties.
