Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1．失效件受内应力开裂。 2.滴塑后折弧工装定位销位置距离产品过近，logo 易过度弯折。 3.DAB 压合
工艺稳定性差，logo 易过度拉伸。  
Conclusions: 1. The brittle fracture of the parts caused by external forces.  
2. The logo excessive bending when arcing process. 
3. The logo excessive stretching when DAB process 
建议： 1. 优化定位销位置 2.DAB压合工装增加预压功能 
Suggestions: 1. Improve pin position.  2. Add pre-press function
