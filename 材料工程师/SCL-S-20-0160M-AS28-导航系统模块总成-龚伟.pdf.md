Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.模拟酒精存在于屏幕&保护膜之间，高温后形成花斑，与失效件一致。 
Simulated alcohol exists between the screen and the protective film, which will form mottling 
after high temperature, which is consistent with the failure parts.  
建议Suggestions：1.PMMA 材料零件表面应避免喷洒酒精类溶液。 
                   Avoid spraying alcohol solution on the surface of PMMA material parts.
