Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  失效位置轮眉内外板焊接无异常。 
The failure position of wheel arch welding was OK. 
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
    Material performance of the part conformed to requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  排查零件异常受力情况。 
Investigate the abnormal force of failure part. 
2.  调整焊接工装，避免虚焊问题。 
Adjust welding tools to avoid false welding. 
3.  定制焊点拉断力测试工装。 
Customized test tools of welding spot breaking force.
