Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
 
1. 发动机悬置属于疲劳开裂，在裂纹源处未发现疏松或机械损伤。 
Engine-mount was broken off by fatigue. No loosen and damage was observed in the crack region. 
2. 零件成分、硬度符合要求，金相组织及孔隙率正常； 
Chemical components and hardness met the technical requirement. Microstructure and porosity were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 对开裂车身进行检查，并判断发动机悬置与车身开裂的先后顺序和相互影响，从而确定悬置与车身开裂的 
根本原因。 
Check the crack body and further analyze the cracking order of the engine mount and the car body, so find out the 
failure season.
