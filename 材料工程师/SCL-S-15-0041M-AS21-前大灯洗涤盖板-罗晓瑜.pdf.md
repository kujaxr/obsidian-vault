Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 在喷嘴运动过程中，内外板存在分离的力，外板翻边长时间与支架碰撞，导致外板卡脚处断裂； 
There’s separated force between inner plate and outer plate during the nozzle moving. The impacting between 
outer plate and  support result in the fracture of clip.  
2、失效件材料符合技术要求；Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements 
3、零件有较大的内应力。The parts have large internal stress. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 优化结构，消除内外板间的分离力； 
Optimize the structure in order to remove the separated force between inner plate and outer plate. 
2、优化注塑工艺。Improve the injection mold.
