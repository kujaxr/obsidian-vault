Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 鼓风机失效件的材料符合质量要求。 
2. 鼓风机叶片受外力（异物进入）导致脆断。 
Conclusions: 1. The material performance of failure fan conforms to requirements. 
           2. The vane of fan fractured for external force. 
建议：1.避免外界异物进入鼓风机，增加保护罩。  
Suggestions：1. A protector should be added in order to avoid entrance of foreign bodies.
