Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件属于疲劳断裂。The failure for the part belong to fatigue fracture . 
2、 螺纹牙底形状不合格，是引起断裂失效的原因之一。The shape of the bottom of the thread did meet the 
technical requirement ,that is one of the reasons that caused the fracture failure. 
3、 零件材料性能符合技术要求。The parts material properties met the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 立即改进牙底形状。Immediately improved the shape of the bottom of the thread. 
2、 请检查螺栓试验时的受力载荷情况。Check the loading conditions during the testing. 
3、 请检查装配过程。Please check of the assembly process.
