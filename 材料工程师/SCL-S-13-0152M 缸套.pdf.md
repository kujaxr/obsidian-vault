Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 缸套是由于过载而开裂的。Cylinder liner was broken, duo to the overload. 
2. 缸套金相及硬度符合要求。Microstructure and hardness met the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 检查缸套装配和使用过程。Check the assembly and use of process. 
2. 校核缸套残余应力。Check the residuaal stress of parts.
