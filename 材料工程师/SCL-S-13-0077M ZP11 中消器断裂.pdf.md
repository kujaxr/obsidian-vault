Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）中消器内管严重锈蚀，而出现断裂；断裂的发生使得进气管与端盖焊接处承受的载荷增加，最后也出现断
裂。The inner tube seriously rusted and then was broken, which made the weld of the input tube and the cover bear 
bigger load and finally was broken. 
（2）内管组织和硬度符合要求；Cr 含量偏高，但不是导致失效的原因。The microstructure and hardness meet the 
requirement and the Cr content is on the high side, but this is not the failure cause. 
（3）进气管和端盖的材料成分，组织和硬度符合要求。The material chemical composition ,microstructure and 
hardness of the input tube and cover meet the requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）跟踪售后市场类似失效。Pay attention to the part after sale.  
（2） 换成耐腐性更好的材料，如SUS436L，SUS 432。Change to material with more anticorrosion，such as SUS436L，
SUS 432. 
（3）加强入厂检验，确保材料符合要求。Check the raw material well and ensure the material meets the requirement.

消音器部分与地面接触产生噪音。 
 
 
图1  失效的前排气管总成

厚度均匀，腐蚀相对较轻；应该是内管断裂后，进气管和端盖承受的载荷增加，而出现断裂的。
