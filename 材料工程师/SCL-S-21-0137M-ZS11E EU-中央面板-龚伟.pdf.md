Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 零件因屏幕溶胶浸蚀导致开裂。 
The parts cracked due to the screen sol etching. 
2. 失效件熔融指数异常。 
The melting index of failure parts was abnormal. 
建议Suggestions：1. 根据实验室验证方案，选用合适的硅胶膜。 
                   According to SCL verification scheme, select a suitable silica gel membrane. 
2.排查当前库存车是否存在如图2 所示的开裂零件。 
                   Check if there are cracked parts of current stock cars shown as Figure 2. 
3.供应商对材料问题做出解释并整改。 
                   Suppliers explain material problems and rectify.
