Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 驱动半轴属于过载断裂。Drive shaft was broken off because of the over load. 
 
2. 故障件局部表面硬度偏上限，其他材料性能正常。Partial surface hardness was near the upper limit and other 
property was normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 考核驱动半轴的设计强度。Check the design strength. 
2. 检查装配及试验过程。Check the assembly and testing process.
