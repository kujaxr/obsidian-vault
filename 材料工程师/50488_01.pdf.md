1
GENERAL
1.1
Subject
This Standard covers procedures (times, environmental conditions and equipment) for testing the re-
sistance of paint, enamel or protective films to chipping by road grit impact.
1.2
Scope
Accelerated simulation of the effect of gravel or other road grit striking paint and/or protective coatings
of a vehicle during service.
1.3
Application
The method is commonly used for vehicle exterior coatings consisting of paint, protective enamel and
similar.
2

4
TEST PROCEDURE
Test shall be carried out after conditioning test panels in cold room as per § 3.2, at 00.5 qC for 2 h and
at least 48 h after polymerization of coatings applied to steel test panels, or 5 days for coatings applied
to plastic test panels.
4.1
Place test panel in the associated holder and load hopper with 0.5 liter water---worn road gravel.
4.2
Open the air valve (previously adjusted to a pressure of 4.30.2 bar).
4.3
Remove test panel and apply adhesive tape as per § 3.4.
4.4
Rapidly pull the adhesive tape off by hand, applying the force down---up.
