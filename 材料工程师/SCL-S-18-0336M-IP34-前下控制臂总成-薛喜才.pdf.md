Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 焊料偏向套管侧造成本体侧焊缝厚度和强度变低，引发较早失效。 
   The welding material deviates to the pipe, resulting in the body strength, welding thickness reducing and 
cracking earlier.  
2. 套管与本体匹配间隙不均匀。  
   The matching dimension between the pipe and body isn’t uniform. 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 抽检同批次零件，评估风险。 
Check the same batchs quality and evaluate the risk.   
2. 供应商应加强焊接过程，确保焊接质量，同时控制本体尺寸。 
   Supply strenth controlling the weld process to make the quality and controll the body dimension.
