Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 失效件受力超负荷导致断裂。 
2. 失效件材料不符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1. Foreign force made failure part fracture. 
2. Material performance of failure part did not conform to requirements.

Suggestions: 1. Material and process should be rectified and improved.
