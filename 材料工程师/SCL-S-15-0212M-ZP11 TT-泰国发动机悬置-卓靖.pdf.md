Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论：橡胶缺料导致悬置主簧橡胶开裂。 
Conclusion: The cracks on main spring were caused by lack of rubber material. 
 
 
建议：加强生产过程控制，防止问题件外流。 
Suggestion: Strengthen production process control and prevent the outflow of defect parts.

IV. 测试结果 Test Results  
1 外观分析 Appearance analysis 
 
 
图1.发动机悬置 
Figure1. Engine mount 
如图1 可见发动机悬置主弹簧一侧主筋橡胶开裂，该部位位于主筋与内芯支架连接处。 
     As shown in Figure 1, the crack was on the main rib of the engine mount main spring, which was located at the 
joints between main rib and the inner core support.  
 
 
图2.发动机悬置开裂区域 
Figure2. The cracking surface of engine mount 
拆开总成件，去掉外部金属骨架（如图2），可见主簧橡胶缺料，骨架未被完全包覆而露出。两处开裂均发生
在缺料区域，这是因为橡胶在受力后易从这些区域发生撕裂，而这种情况是由制造原因导致。经特瑞堡确认由于
硫化模具进料孔内有上一模橡胶脚料没有清理干净，使得注料孔不通顺影响流入到模腔内的注胶量偏少，导致出
现橡胶缺料。接下来又由于员工检查工序的疏忽导致零件流出。 
     After separating the assembly and removing the external metal frame (As shown in Figure 2), we found the skeleton 
was not been covered completely by main spring rubber. Two cracks occurred in the areas with lack of material, because 
the rubber was easily torn apart by force from those areas, they caused by the manufacturing reasons. It was confirmed
