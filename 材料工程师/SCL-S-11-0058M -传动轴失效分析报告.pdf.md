结论与建议 Conclusions  
 
 传动轴是经由脆性超载断裂. 没有疲劳迹象, 破坏或缺陷. 材料属性符合要求. The driveshaft has failed through 
brittle overload.  There were no signs of fatigue, damage or material defects.  The material properties met the requirements. 
 
 断口原因相一致跟冲击从缚牢螺母过程. 所以任为传动轴被扭紧高度紧张了, 再说额外过度紧张从冲击导致失
效. The Fracture origin coincides with the impact made to fix the driveshaft nut.  It is therefore believed that the over-stressing of the shaft during 
torque tightening, and additional stressing due to the impact is the cause of the failure.

宏观观察 
传动轴是在车轮尽头花键的位置中断裂 (见图1). 断口源位置是被人字形花纹在断面上和缺乏变形找到了(见图
2) . 这是对脆性很特征. 也有人注意到，断裂的起源与所取得的影响恰逢(争取到轴螺母). 
 
The driveshaft had fractured through the splined area of the hub end (figure 1).  The fracture origin was located through observing chevron marks on the 
fracture surface, and lack of deformation at the fracture origin (figure 2).  These are both very typical signs of brittle overload fracture. 
It was also noticed that the fracture origin coincided with the impact made during vehicle assembly, to secure the nut to the shaft.
