Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions:. 
1. 镜子支架是由于过载失效。支架边缘的磨损痕迹表明，到另一个组件的紧密接触，这可能引起拆开比较难，
导致失效。 The mirror bracket has failed though overload.  Wear traces on the edges of the bracket suggest that there 
was a tight fit into another assembly, which might have made disassembly difficult, leading to the failure.  
 
建议Suggestions:  
1. 检查邻近的零件的尺寸。Check dimensions of mating assembly parts. 
2. 确认零件的强度复合要求。Test failure force meets requirements. 
3. 在组装或拆卸时不要使用武力。Do not use excessive force during assembly or disassembly 
4。供应商应采取措施以减少气孔。Supplier should take steps to reduce gas porosity.

材料因素Material factors 
被指定为压铸锌合金材料，没有进一步的规范。使用材料是按照JIS H5301-72。材料的硬度测量80HV10 
（~76HB 按照 ISO18265）。这是比82HB 在JIS H5301-72 的要求较低，但是孔隙影响测试结果。不必要检
查化学成分。 支架完整的截面被切断，孔隙度检查。虽然有一节中有气孔隙，不是在断裂区（图5）。 
  
The material is specified as Zinc Die Cast alloy, without further specification.  The used material is according to 
JIS H5301-72.  The hardness of the material was measured at 80HV10 (~76HB using ISO18265).  This is 
slightly lower than the requirement of 82HB in JIS H5301-72, but the test result was influenced by porosity. 
I was not necessary to check the chemical composition.  The intact side of the bracket was cut in cross-section to 
check for porosity.  Although there was some gas porosity in one section, this was not in the fracture area (figure 
5).
