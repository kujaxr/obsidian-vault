Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  尾门锁加强板由于局部应力高先发生疲劳开裂，后续加强板与内板焊点相继疲劳失效。 
    The reinforcing plate of tail door lock first crack by fatigue because of big stress. The welding point between 
the reinforcing plate and inner plate was cracking though fatigue later. 
2.  加强板和内板的材料成分、板材厚度满足要求，强度及组织均正常。 
Chemical、thickness of strengthening and inner plate meet the requirements. Strength and microstructure are normal.  
3.  焊核长度满足要求，开裂区域减薄率无异常。 
    The welding spot length meets the requirements and thinning rate at cracking zone wasn’t abnormal. 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化加强板结构设计，以降低应力、提高安全系数。 
Optimize the structure design of reinforcing plate to reduce the stress an improve the safety coefficient. 
2.  脱胶部位增加涂胶量，加强板与内板间涂结构胶。 
    Increases the amount of coating and sticks with glue between the plate and inner plate.
