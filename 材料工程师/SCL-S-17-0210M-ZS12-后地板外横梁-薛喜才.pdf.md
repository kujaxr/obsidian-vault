Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  局部应力较大引发外横梁连接板疲劳开裂。 
    Big stress results in crossmember bear floor outer cracking by fatigue.  
2.  板材成分满足SCR260Y 要求，材料性能、板厚正常。 
    Chemical components meet the technical requirement and material properties/thickness are normal.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  优化零件结构、降低应力如增大圆角。 
    Optimize structure to reduce the stress such as enlaege the angle.
