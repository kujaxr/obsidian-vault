Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 1#和2#消声器的环焊缝沿晶开裂失效，部分晶面及穿晶开裂区域有可见的疲劳断裂特征。The girth weld of 
1# and 2# middle muffler intergranular cracking failure, and some crystal faces and transgranular cracking 
regions have visible fatigue fracture characteristics. 
（2） 1#排气管材料成分合格，1#和2#失效焊缝的熔深、焊高和焊宽符合要求。The composition of 1# muffler is 
qualified, and the weld depth、weld height and weld width of 1# and 2# muffler meet the requirement.  
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 排查焊接工艺过程，关注焊后是否会出现异常快冷的情况。Check the welding process，pay attention to 
whether there will be abnormal fast cooling after welding. 
（2） 关注同批次零件的市场表现。Pay attention to the market performance of the same batch parts.
