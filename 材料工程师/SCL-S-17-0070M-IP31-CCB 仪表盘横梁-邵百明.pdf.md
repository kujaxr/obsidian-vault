Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）装配中，螺套在螺栓作用下，发生轴向移动而产生较大的膨胀张力，导致镁合金横梁装配孔在应力集中处发
生开裂。During assembly, the screw sleeve moves axially and produces a larger expansion tension, which results in the cracking of the assembly hole at the stress concentration. 
（2）失效件具有严重的气孔和疏松；这降低了材料强度和拉脱力，促进了螺套移动和开裂的发生概率。The failed parts 
are with serious porosity and loose, which reduce the strength and pull-out force and promote the occurrence probability of mobile screw sleeve and cracking. 
（3）抽检发现：当前产品的台阶面为直角，与认可状态不符（对拉脱力影响的研究实验在进行中）。The step surface of 
the current product is a right angle, and the state does not agree with approval status ( the test experiment for the effect analysis is in progress). 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）优化模具结构，降低局部孔隙，提升铸件质量。Optimize the mold structure to reduce the porosity and improve the casting quality. 
（2）研究影响螺套移动的关键因素，如匹配型面和尺寸，并优化结构和尺寸，提升产品的可靠性和稳定性。Studies 
the key factors affecting the movement of the screw sleeve, such as matching surface and size, and optimize the structure and size to improve the reliability and stability of the product. 
（3） 规范刀具管理，避免产品与设计出现偏差，确保机加工质量。Standardize tool management to avoid deviation between product and design 
to ensure machining quality.

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
南京装配车间在将转向管柱安装在仪表板横梁上时，横梁的安装孔出现开裂；据SQE 反馈发生了7 例。
2017 年4 月1 日下午，实验室收到第一例失效件，如图1 所示。由图可见：（1）左侧的螺套光杆部分完全
拉出，并涨大，在内侧匹配面形成裂纹，靠近凸台边部由于应力集中，而断开；（2）右侧的螺套装配中有轴
向移动，与端面完全平齐。
