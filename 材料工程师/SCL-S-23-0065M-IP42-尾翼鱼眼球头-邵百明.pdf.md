Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 鱼眼球头内部存在严重的收缩孔洞和疏松导致断裂。There are serious shrinkage holes and porosity inside the 
fisheye ball joint, leading to fracture. 
（2） 新件和通过测试零件的内部也存在严重的铸造收缩孔洞和疏松。There are also severe casting shrinkage holes 
and porosity in the interior of new and tested parts. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 更改零件铸造成型为热锻成型。Change the part molding process from casting molding to hot forging molding。 
（2） 基于零件的受力要求，对已装车零件进行风险评估。Based on the stress requirements of the parts, risk 
assessment is conducted on the parts of the whole vehicle.
