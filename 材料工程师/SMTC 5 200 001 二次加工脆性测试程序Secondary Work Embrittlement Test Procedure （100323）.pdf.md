1 范围 
本标准规定了二次加工脆性测试所需装备、
要求、程序及报告的内容。 
本标准适用于钢板的二次加工脆性温度的
测定（低温脆性转变温度），主要适用于无间隙
原子钢和加磷钢。 
 
1  Scope  
    This standard specifies the equipments, 
requests and reports of secondary work 
embrittlement test procedure. 
This procedure is used to determine the 
secondary work embrittlement temperature 
(also known as the cold work embrittlement 
temperature) for sheet steels. This test 
procedure is primarily applicable to interstitial 
free and rephosphorized steels.

4.2.1.1 圆形料片是从厚度为0.8±0.02mm 钢
板上冲出来的。料片尺寸大小为成型杯子冲模直
径的两倍，偏差±1mm（即Φ33mm 的冲模需要66
±1mm 的料片）。在测试之前对边缘进行毛刺清
理。 
注意：每次测试可能最多需要50 个料片 
 
4.2.1.1  Circular blanks are to be punched 
from 0.8 ± 0.02 mm thick sheet steel. The 
blank size will be within 1 mm of twice the 
diameter of the punch used to form the cups 
(i.e. a 33 mm punch will require 66 ± 1 mm 
blanks). The edges are to be deburred before 
testing. 
NOTE: As any as 50 blanks may be required 
for each test. 
4.2.1.2 利用拉深比为2.0：1，拉深料片形成
杯子。冲模侧翼半径为6.0±0.5mm。对于镀锌
板，在每次成型之后模具需要清洁。记录每次杯
子成型向下的压力。 
注意：为了减少起皱和降低模具磨损，可以
使用润滑剂。润滑剂为聚丙烯板，0.040±
0.005mm。 
 
4.2.1.2  Draw blanks to form a cup utilizing a 
draw ratio of 2.0:1.The punch shoulder radius 
shall be 6.0 ± 0.5 mm. For coated materials, 
the die is to be cleaned after each forming 
operation. The hold down pressure used to 
form the cups is to be recorded. 
NOTE: Lubrication can be used to eliminate 
wrinkling and reduce die wear. The lubricant 
will be a polypropylene sheet, 0.040 ± 0.005 
mm. 
4.2.1.3 利用车床修边，修边之后杯子的高度是
杯子直径的2/3±1.0mm（即一个33mm 的杯子修
边后高度为22±1mm）。包含缺陷如起皱、开裂
等的杯子在机械加工之后都不能使用。车床加工
遗留的任何毛刺在测试之前都需被去除。 
 
4.2.1.3  The edges shall then be machined 
utilizing a lathe to trim the edge. The height of 
the cup after trimming is to be 2/3 of the 
diameter of the cup ± 1.0 mm (i.e. a 33 mm cup 
would have a trimmed height of 22 ± 1 mm). 
Any cups containing defects such as wrinkles, 
splits, etc. after the machining operation are 
not to be used. Any burrs left from the lathe 
operation are to be removed before testing.

6  报告 
以下项目需包含在测试报告中：测试的材
料牌号、材料厚度、化学成分、基板金属标准所
需的力学性能、镀层的种类和质量（如果有镀层
的话）、制作杯子时所用的压力、润滑剂种类和
二次加工脆性温度。

A.1  落锤冲击测试仪 
落锤冲击测试仪的常用设计如图A.1 所示。
由4 个主要部件组成：底座、立柱、冲头和释放
机构。只要遵循测试程序的要求，每个部件的细
节是可以变化的。该要求是一个10kg 的物块从
1m 高的地方落下，1m 距离测量的是从冲头的底
部到固定杯子的底座之间的距离。落锤冲击冲头
的图纸可以从钢板专家小组那里获得。 
 
 
A.1  Drop Impact Tester.  
A currently used design for the drop impact 
tester is shown in Figure 1. It consists of 4 
main components: the base, the columns, the 
indenter, and the release mechanism. The 
details of each component can vary as long as 
the requirements of the test procedure are 
followed. The requirements are that a 10 kg 
mass be dropped from a height of 1 m. The 1 
m distance is measured from the bottom of the 
indenter to the base that holds the cup. 
Blueprints for a drop impact indenter can be 
obtained from the Sheet Metal Specialist 
Team.
