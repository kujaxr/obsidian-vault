Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是疲劳断裂，是试验过程中材料过热造成。 
The failure of the valve was fatigue fracture ,may be due to overheating during the testing. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、检查试验时的发动机排温情况。 
Check the engine exhaust gas temperature during the testing.. 
2、 请在实验前检查气门的相关尺寸。 
To check the valve size before the test 
3、 请考虑在锥面上进行堆焊强化。 
Please consider surfacing to strengthen the valve cone.
