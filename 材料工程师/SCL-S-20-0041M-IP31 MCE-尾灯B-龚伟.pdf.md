Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1. 失效件退火时间不足导致应力开裂。 
Annealing time of failure part was short, leading to stress crack.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
                 The performance of the parts material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1. 供应商明确退火要求，按规定执行。 
                 Suppliers clearly define annealing requirements and implement them as required. 
                2. 关注认可零件状态。 
                 Focus on approved part status during ESO.
