Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：密封圈材料符合要求，变形是因零件尺寸偏大，受压导致的。 
Conclusions: the material of o ring conforms to the requirement, and the reason of distortion of the o ring is  that the 
o ring is squeezed by the lid which is caused by the wrong dimension of the O ring. 
建议：保证密封圈的尺寸，壶盖安装时避免对密封圈挤压 
Suggestions：Assure the dimension of the o ring conforms to the requirement and avoid pressing the o ring when 
install the kettle lid.
