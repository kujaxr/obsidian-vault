Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 链条内链片局部在腐蚀介质作用下先发生应力腐蚀断裂，随后引起链片其他区域相继发生疲劳和过载断裂。
The local Timing chain fractured thought stress corrosion by corrosive medium, and then fractured by fatigue and 
tension fracture at last. 
2、 内链片硬度和金相组织正常。The hardness and the microstructure of the chain are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 进一步排查腐蚀介质来源。Further investigate the source of corrosive medium.
