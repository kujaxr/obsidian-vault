1 范围 
本标准规定了对汽车内饰零件材料的燃烧
特性的一般要求和试验方法。 
本标准适用于零件的表面直接接触乘客室
空间和距乘客室空间深度在13mm（约1/2英寸）
内的单一材料或复合材料。对于结构上相互接触
和整个表面不完全结合的零件，必须先去掉最靠
近室内空间的件，再重新考虑厚度为13mm（见
图1），直到层厚度大于13mm或总厚度达到
26mm（1英寸）为止。 
1  Scope 
This 
Technical 
Supply 
Specification 
defines requirements and test method for the 
burning behavior of materials / components in 
the passenger compartment. 
It pertains to every part of a single or 
composite material whose surface contacts the 
air space of the passenger compartment and 
that is located within a maximum depth of 13 
mm 
(1/2 
inch) 
from 
the 
passenger 
compartment surface. For parts that are joined 
over less than the full surface area and that 
succeed each other during assembly, the 
depth of 13 mm shall be taken into 
consideration again after removal of the part 
closest to the passenger compartment (see 
Figure 1), until a layer exists that is thicker than 
13 mm or an overall depth of 26 mm (1 inch) 
has been reached.

3 术语及定义 
汽车内饰材料/零件——座垫、座椅靠背、
安全带、顶盖衬板、活动式折叠车顶、扶手、所
有的护板、杂物盒、头枕、地板面层、遮阳板、
窗帘、百叶窗、座椅面套和所有其它在乘客室的
零件所使用的材料，包括撞车时吸收碰撞能量的
填料和缓冲装置用的材料。 
复合材料——由多层相同或不同的材料组
成，所有接触点（整个面）相互结合的零件（见
图1）。 
 
3  Terms and Definitions 
Materials/components 
of 
the 
passenger compartment: seat cushions, 
back-rests, seat belts, roof liners, folding tops 
of convertibles, arm rests, all trims, shelves, 
headrests, floor coverings, sun visors, curtains, 
blinds, seat coverings and all other materials 
used in the passenger compartment, including 
padding and elements that deploy in the event 
of a crash whose intended purpose is to 
absorb energy during a crash.  
Composite materials: Parts that are 
made up of multiple layers of the same 
material or different materials that are bonded 
together at all points of contact, i.e., over the 
full surface area (see Figure 1).

6 试验方法 
6  Notes on testing 
6.1 燃烧特性试验 
除以下要求外，试验仪器和方法根据机动车
不同的销售市场相应地按GB 8410、FMVSS 
571.302或DIN 75200。 
在做新材料测试时，如果结果的平均值和跨
度不可预知，通常至少需要做10个试样。 
当某一材料的特性众所周知，结果标准偏差
确定时，试样数量可以减少。应该沿着对试验结
果最不利的方向切割材料来制备试样。 
位于乘客室最近的材料表面必须朝下（放
置）。 
除试样宽度<51mm的试样之外，只有当试
样在火焰中（预检）弯曲、卷曲及由于燃烧不呈
水平状导致不规则燃烧的情况下，才允许用金属
丝支撑。 
对于试样长度<100mm的零件：支撑金属丝
间距10mm，第一个金属丝与开启的支撑尾端相
隔10mm。 
如果从计时开始，试样长时间缓慢燃烧，则
可以在试验计时20 min时中止试验，并记录燃烧
时间及燃烧距离。 
6.1  Testing of burning behavior 
Except for the following requirements, for 
different sale markets of the vehicle test 
equipment and method according to China GB 
8410, U.S. Regulation FMVSS 571.302 or DIN 
75200, correspondingly. 
In the case of initial testing of new 
materials when the mean and spread of results 
is unknown at least 10 samples will normally 
need to be tested. 
Where the behaviour of a particular 
material is well known and the normal 
deviation of results established the number of 
tests can be reduced. The specimen is 
produced by cutting the material in the 
direction that provides the most adverse test 
results. 
The material surface that is located 
closest to the air space of the passenger 
compartment shall face downward.  
Except for specimens < 51 mm in width, 
the use of support wires is not permitted for 
specimens that, when exposed to flame (in a 
preliminary test), deflect, roll up and exhibit 
irregular burning due to the fact that the 
burn-off is no longer horizontal. 
The following applies to parts with a 
specimen length < 100 mm: distance between 
support wires10 mm, first wire is 10 mm from 
the open end of the holder. 
If the specimen has been burning slowly 
for a long time from the start of timing, the test 
can be stopped when it has burned for 20 
minutes from the start of timing. The burn time 
and burn distance should be recorded.
