Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 2/6 档齿套的的齿磨损严重，已无断口形貌，断口无法分析，故只进行理化检测分析。2/6 speed gear sets of 
teeth badly worn ,the fracture can not be analyzed, it is only the physical and chemical testing analysis. 
2、 3/5 档齿套属疲劳断裂。3/5 speed gear set is a fatigue fracture 
3、 4/R 档齿套先断裂再磨损的。4 档从动齿已严重变形。4 / R file sets of teeth was first fracture and wear again. 
4、 所有失效件的表面硬度、硬化层深度、芯部硬度符合技术要求。Surface hardness, hardening depth, and core 
hardness meet to the technical requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核零部件的实际载荷和试验工况。Check parts of the actual load and test conditions. 
2、 请检查个零部件之间的配合情况。Check with the situation between the two parts.
