Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）扭转梁是由于疲劳而开裂；裂纹起源于横梁内侧边缘高应力区；该处有细小的磕碰凹坑。The axle was cracked 
by fatigue. The crack started from the inside edge of the beam with high stress and a small concave damage. 
（2）开裂及附近区域的边部存在原始损伤，不符合横梁原材料要求，但不是导致失效的原因。There are clear 
damage marks at the beam edge near to the crack，which don’t meet the requirement of the raw material，but this is not 
the cause. 
（3）扭转梁的材料成分，机械性能，组织以及板厚均符合要求；焊缝无异常。The chemical components, mechanical 
properties, microstructure and thickness meet the requirements; the weld is normal. 
（4）横梁的开口宽度，前束角和后倾角符合要求。The width, front toe angle and rear camber of the axle meet the 
requirements. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）调查该车辆的使用历史。Investigate the use history of the car. 
（2）基于该车辆的配置进行载荷分析并贴应变片进行实际应力校核。Analyse and test the load basing on the car’s 
condition. 
（3）控制原材料的表面质量，同时在生产中，避免横梁的磕碰伤。Control the raw material surface quality, and 
at the same time, avoid the damage of the beam during production.
