Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）机油泵电磁阀失效是由于阀芯锈蚀而出现卡滞引起的。The Oil control valve was stuck due to the corrosion of the 
spool. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）结合生产和物流过程，查找锈蚀的原因，并采取防范措施（如涂油等），加强过程控制。Combined with the 
production and logistics process, find out the cause of rust and take preventive measures, such as oiling and so on, and 
strengthen process control.
