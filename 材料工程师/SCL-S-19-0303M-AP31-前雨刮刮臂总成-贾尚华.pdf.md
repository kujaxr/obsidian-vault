Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  前雨刮刮臂总成由于装配倾斜发生了过载断裂。 
The front wiper arm assembly is broken due to overload due to assembly tilt. 
2.  同批次的雨刮刮臂-臂座尺寸满足图纸要求。 
    The size of wiper arm and arm seat of the same batch meets the drawing requirements. 
3.  失效刮臂的金相组织正常，孔隙率和硬度满足要求。 
The microstructure of the failure scraping arm is normal, and the porosity and hardness meet the requirements. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 对装配过程进行排查。 
Check the assembly process.
