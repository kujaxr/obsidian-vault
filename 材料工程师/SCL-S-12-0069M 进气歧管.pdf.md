Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 进气歧管内部产生巨大压力，超过强度较低的焊接区域和应力集中的转角区域的负荷，导致断裂。
断裂方向由内向外。 
       2. 失效件材料符合质量要求。 
Conclusions: 1. The great pressure at inside of induction made welding area and corner area fracture. The fracture 
direction was from inside to outerside. 
 
   2. The material performance of failure part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 避免进气歧管内部产生巨大压力。 
Suggestions：1. The great pressure at inside of induction manifold should be avoided.
