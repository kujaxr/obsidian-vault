Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  高压线束连接器-互锁针脚是受到异常外力导致的过载断裂。 
   High voltage wire harness connector-Interlock is overload fracture caused by abnormal external force. 
2.  材料的硬度满足要求，金相组织正常。 
   The hardness of the materials meet the requirements, and the metallographic structure is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 
关注物流和制造过程，避免针脚承受异常外力。 
Focus on logistics and manufacturing processes to avoid collision damage.
