Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承是由于内圈和钢球出现典型的疲劳剥离而失效的；依据剥离的程度来看，内圈是先失效的。The bearing 
was failed because of the typical fatigue peeling of the inside race and outside race; according to the fatigue peeling 
degree, the inside race was failed firstly. 
（2）轴承的内外圈，钢球的硬度，组织和碳化物符合要求；夹杂物水平正常。The hardness, microstructure and 
carbonization of the inside race, outside race and ball meet the requirement; the inclusion levels are normal.  
（3）内圈的成分符合要求。The chemical components of the inside race meets the requirement.  
（4）失效轴承的内圈和外圈滚道面半径符合要求。The raceway radius of the failed bearing meets the requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）检查变速箱其它部件是否有异常。Check if other parts of the transmission are normal. 
（2）通过设计优化，进一步提升轴承的承载能力和疲劳寿命。Improve the load capacity of the bearing by design.

后，发现轴承出现了剥离。ZP11 was on EOBD test in UK and after 123876Km, the bearing of the transmission 
noised. It was found that the bearing was failed after disassembling.

内圈剥离区和其它区域的形貌如图3 和4 所示。剥离区为典型的疲劳剥离；内圈的接触区处于偏离中
心的一侧区域，但未与挡边的边部接触，如图黄色的虚线所标识。该轴承承受着一定的轴向载荷，工作时
接触区会偏向一侧；接触区未达到挡边的边部，反应实际工况并不是很恶劣。The peeling zone and other zone 
were shown in fig. 3 and 4. The peeling is because of touching fatigue, which is a typical failed type for the 
bearing. The touching surface of the inside race is off the middle zone, but doesn’t touch the edge, which is shown 
as the yellow line. Because the bearing born an axle load, the touching zone was off the middle zone; according to 
the touching zone, the load is not very big, because the working condition is not very bad. 
接触区域的表面有细小的压痕。这是由于有异物进入工作区所致。这些异物主要来自于剥离的金属屑。
是否存在轴承以外其它外来物质进入滚道引起压痕，无法确定。未见硬颗粒的压痕和划伤。There are small 
marks on the raceway surface, which is because of some foreign matters on the working surface. The mattes are 
mainly from the metal peeling of the bearing. Of cause, it can’t tell if other matters from the other parts went into 
the working zone of the bearing.
