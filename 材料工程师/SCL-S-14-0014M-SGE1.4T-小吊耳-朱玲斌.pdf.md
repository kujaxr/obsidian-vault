Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 标识不当导致发动机吊钩支架发生疲劳开裂。The Bracket-eng lift is fatigue crack due to the unreasonable 
logo. 
（2） 失效件成分符合要求，硬度正常。The composition of failure part meets the requirement, the hardness is 
normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 标识应远离结构薄弱区域。Logo should be far away from the weak region of the structure.
