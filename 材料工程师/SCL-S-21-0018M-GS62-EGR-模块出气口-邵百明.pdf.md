Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）EGR 模块出气管路是由于受到尾气中含Cl 和N 的腐蚀性物质而引起腐蚀。The outlet pipeline of EGR module 
is corroded by corrosive substances containing Cl and N in tail gas. 
（2）材料的成分符合要求；金相组织和夹杂正常。The composition of the material meets the requirements; the 
microstructure and inclusions are normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）对油品进行检测，确认Cl 含量。Test the gasoline to confirm the Cl content. 
（2）基于油品的检测结果，评估采用抗腐蚀性更高材料（如SUS304L 或316L）的必要性。Based on the inspection 
results of gasoline, the necessity of using materials with higher corrosion resistance (such as SUS304L or 316L) was 
evaluated.

示；所含的元素相同，均含有较高含量的Cl 和一定量的N 和O 元素。其中Cl 和N 元素均具有腐蚀性，特别Cl
元素对不锈钢腐蚀性极强。Cl 应该来自于汽油。
