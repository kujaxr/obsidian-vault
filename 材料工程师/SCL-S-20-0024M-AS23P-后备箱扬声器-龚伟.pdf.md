Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 防尘帽断口为疲劳断口，与台架试验工况一致。 
The dust cap fracture is a fatigue fracture, which is consistent with the bench test conditions.   
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
                 The performance of the parts material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.关注扬声器箱体结构对失效的影响。 
                 Pay attention to the effect of speaker cabinet structure on failure.
