Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1.零件受外力导致脆断。2.原零件结构受滥用力强度不够。3.材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions: 1. Side crank brittle fracture caused by the external force. 
2. The original part of the structure strength by excessive force is not enough. 
3. The material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 新曲柄零件结构已加强，观察市场表现。 
Suggestions: 1. The new Side crank structure had been strengthened and observe the market feedback.
