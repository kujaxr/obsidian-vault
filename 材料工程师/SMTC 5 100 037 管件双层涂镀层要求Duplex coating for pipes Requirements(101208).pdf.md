1  范围 
本标准规定了以油、水、燃料等作为介质
的管件的腐蚀保护涂层的一般要求、涂盖操作、
涂层结构、物理属性和耐蚀性能。 
本标准适用于管件的双层系统，包括一层
锌合金或锌/镍合金镀层、与之配套的转换处理
或底漆处理，以及一层表面有机涂层，如环氧
树脂材料涂层配套的处理。 
 
注：环件及表面上与操作中流入管件的介质直接接
触的密封面、工作面不得含有有机涂层。必须经相关测
试部门认可公布才允许例外出现。 
1  Scope 
This 
standard 
specifies 
the 
general 
requirements, 
coating 
process, 
structure, 
physical property and corrosion resistance 
behavior of corrosion protection coating on 
pipes that carry such media as oil, water, fuel, 
etc.  
This standard adopts to the pipe duplex 
system which consist of a zinc alloy or 
zinc/nickel alloy coating, a conversion or primer 
treatment matched to this coating and an 
organic top coat, on, for example, an epoxy 
resin base. 
 
Note: Sealing surfaces, face surfaces of ring 
pieces and surfaces, which come in contact with the 
media flowing through the pipe during operation must 
not have organic coatings. Exceptions are possible only 
with a release from the appropriate test department.

4  涂层一般要求 
4  General coating requirements 
4.1  涂层上不得有瑕疵和剩余材料。 
4.1  The coating must be free of flaws and 
excess material. 
4.2  只有压制管套上的孤立的针眼或线状损
坏是可以允许的，但不允许出现工具碰撞造成
的有机涂层剥落。 
4.2  Isolated pinpoint or linear damage will be 
tolerated only on pressed sleeves, but flaking of 
the organic top coat as a result of contact with 
the tools cannot be tolerated. 
4.3  涂层不得对成品件的功能特性产生不利
影响。 
4.3  The coating must have no adverse effects 
on the functional characteristics of the finished 
part. 
4.4  在组装过程中不得损伤涂层。 
4.4  No damage to the coating must occur 
during proper assembly. 
5  生产过程中的涂盖操作 
5  Coating during the production process 
5.1  在所需的焊接操作完全完成之后，可以在
生产过程中的任何时候进行涂盖操作。 
 
 
5.1  The coating can be applied at any point 
during the production process after all required 
soldering or welding operations are completely 
finished. 
5.2  如果在涂盖操作之后需要对管件进行弯
曲加工，则一定要在涂层工序之后立即进行，
弯曲加工之后应直接进行测试，以确保涂层不
受损。必须根据第 6.2 说明进行腐蚀测试，并
提供无损伤证明文件；肉眼检查不足以提供这
样的证明。不允许在弯曲半径上出现涂层腐蚀
保护程度的减弱。 
 
 
5.2  If a pipe must be bent after the coating 
has 
been 
applied, 
this 
must 
be 
done 
immediately following the coating process, 
directly after which tests must be performed to 
ensure that the coating was not damaged. 
Documentation that there has been on damage 
must be provide by means of the corrosion test 
under section 6.2; a visual test is not sufficient. 
A decrease in the corrosion protection along 
the bending radius is not permitted. 
5.3  对涂盖操作时间安排有影响、或对涂层工
序之后的计划有影响的生产过程中的变动，必
须注明，对于这样的变动需要重新进行首次采
样。 
5.3  Changes to the production process that 
affect the timing of the coating process or a 
different procedure after the coating must be 
indicated and will require a new first sampling.

6.2 镀锌 
6.2  Zinc coating 
6.2.1  符合SMTC 21510 的电解镀锌，涂层厚
度不小于8μm。 
6.2.1  Electrolytic zinc coating acc. to SMTC 
21510, coating thickness ≥8μm. 
6.2.2  在获得上汽乘用车公司同意的情况下，
也可以使用符合SMTC21517 的锌镍涂层。 
6.2.2  A zinc-nickel coating acc. to SMTC21
517 is also possible by agreement with SM
PV. 
6.2.3  必须使用抛光剂含量最低的电解液进
行操作，确保最佳附着性。 
6.2.3  An electrolyte with the lowest possible 
amount of brighteners must be used to ensure 
optimal adhesion. 
6.3 预处理 
6.3  Pretreatment 
6.3.1  在镀锌操作之后涂层工序之前，必须进
行锌磷化或者等同的预处理，该操作也适用于
不能被有机涂层覆盖的表面。 
 
6.3.1  After the zinc coating and prior to the 
coating process, zinc phosphating or an 
equivalent primer treatment must be applied. 
This treatment may also be applied to surfaces 
that must remain free of organic coatings. 
6.3.2  铬酸盐光泽处理会降低涂层的附着能
力，所以不得在预处理中使用（锌镍镀层上可
以进行该处理）。 
6.3.2  Chromating reduces the adhesion of the 
coating and is not permitted as a pretreatment. 
(It is permissible with a zinc-nickel coating). 
6.3.3  预处理之后必须立即进入涂层工序操
作。 
6.3.3  The pretreatment must be applied 
immediately prior to the coating process. 
6.3.4  光亮锌镀层上不允许直接覆盖有机涂
层。 
6.3.4  No direct organic coating of the bright 
zinc layer is permissible. 
6.4  有机表面涂层 
6.4  Organic top coat 
6.4.1  有机涂层厚度不小于20μm。 
6.4.1  Organic coating    thickness≥20μm. 
6.4.2  粉末涂层厚度不小于50μm。 
6.4.2  Powder paint      thickness≥50μm. 
6.4.3  在烘烤状态下，可以含有氟化聚合物。
6.4.3  In burnt-in state. Fluorinated polymer 
content is possible. 
6.4.4  必须对每个零件明确分析，是否要在可
能出现螺纹的部位覆盖有机涂层。 
6.4.4  It must be clarified on a case-by-case 
basis whether an organic coating may be 
applied to any threads that may be present. 
6.4.5  管接螺母附近管道涂层不允许出现瑕
疵。 
6.4.5  Flaws in the pipe coating around the 
union nuts are not permitted. 
6.5  后续处理 
覆盖涂层之后，必须采取合适的措施，确
保在组装之前管道内部不会出现腐蚀现象。 
6.5  Post-treatment 
  After coating, suitable measures must be 
taken to ensure that no corrosion can form 
inside the pipe prior to assembly.

7.1  附着力和脆性 
7.1  Adhesive strength and brittleness 
7.1.1 划格测试 
按照DIN EN ISO 2409 的要求进行划格测
试，特征值必须为0。 
7.1.1  Crosscut test  
  Crosscut test acc. to DIN EN ISO 2409, the 
characteristic value 0 must be met. 
7.1.2  胶带测试 
7.1.2  Tape test 
7.1.2.1  仅限于由于直径太小无法在7.1.1 测
试规定中使用的管件。 
7.1.2.1  Permissible only for pipes on which 
the test found in section 7.1.1 cannot be 
performed because their diameter is too small. 
7.1.2.2  胶带测试中，表面涂层任何部位不得
粘附胶带。 
7.1.2.2  No part of the top coat may adhere to 
the adhesive tape during the tape test. 
7.1.2.3  按下列步骤进行带测试：在管件的平
直部分刻出×形记号，使用粘附强度为3N/cm
的胶带 ，在划痕上粘牢之后，以垂直于试样表
面的方向迅速撕走胶带。 
7.1.2.3  The tape test is performed as follows: 
after making an x-shaped cut on a straight 
section of pipe, a piece of adhesive tape with 
an adhesive strength of 3 N/cm of width  is 
applied to the cut and removed with a jerk-like 
motion perpendicular to the specimen surface.

7.3.12 其它要求 
对于使用特殊介质的汽油适用管件，必须
在高温下进行16h 的测试。测试温度必须为相
应介质的最高操作温度，由测试实验室和相关
测试部门一同对每次测试进行单独分析，并规
定其测试温度。如果未作规定，高温测试温度
即被认定为100° C。

with 
a 
special 
medium, 
for 
which 
the

be performed at elevated temperatures. The 
test temperature must correspond to the max. 
operating temperature of the medium and is 
defined on a case-by-case basis by the test 
laboratory together with the relevant test 
department. If none is specified, 100° C shall 
be used as the elevated test temperature.
