Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1. 局部应力较高导致前副车架与套管焊趾疲劳开裂。Front subframe crack by fatigue because of high local stress. 
2. 焊缝熔深、焊高满足要求，局部存在未熔合、夹渣缺陷，但其不是导致零件失效的主要原因。Welding fusion 
and welding thickness meet the requirements. There are some defects such as lack of fusion and slag inclusion, 
which aren’t the main reasons for the failure of parts. 
3. 材料成分、厚度满足要求，材料组织、强度正常。Chemical composition and thickness meet the requirements. 
Microstructure and strength are normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 将焊缝由满焊更改为两段焊，避开高应力区。Change full welding to two-stage welding to avoid high stress area. 
2. 建议供应商采购钢板材料时，要求厚度尺寸靠近上偏差。Suggest supply that the thickness dimension should be 
close to the upper deviation when purchasing steel plate materials.
