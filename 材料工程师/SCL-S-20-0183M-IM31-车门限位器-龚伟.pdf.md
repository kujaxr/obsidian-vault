Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 包塑件在工况下受挤压导致内凹变形。 
The plastic-coated was squeezed under working conditions, resulting in concave deformation.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement.
