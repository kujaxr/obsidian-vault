Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 螺柱脱落是由于过载引起的断裂，可能是受到异常外力引起。 
Failure for the part  is due to overloading , may be subjected to unusual external force caused 
2、 螺柱焊接的破坏扭矩试验共6 个，其中1 个扭矩低于技术要求，其余5 个合格。 
Destruction torque test for six the parts, a torque below the technical requirements, the remaining 
five qualified. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请校核装配工艺的适用性。 
Please checking the applicability of PAM. 
2、 检查装配过程质量。 
Check the quality of the assembly process.  
3、 供应商加强焊接质量监控。 
Suppliers to enhance welding quality monitoring.
