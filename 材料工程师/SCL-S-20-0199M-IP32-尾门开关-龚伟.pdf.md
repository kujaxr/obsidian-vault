Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件结构较为薄弱，客户反复使用后断裂。 
                    The part structure was relatively weak, broken after repeated use. 
                2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the failure part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  优化零件结构，提高受力位置强度。 
                    Optimize the structure of parts and improve the strength of the force position. 
2.  排查其他车型此处结构并同步优化。 
                    Check the structure of other models here and optimize simultaneously.
