Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 装配时零件受侧向外力后卡脚断裂。 
During assembly, the clamp foot was broken after the part was subjected to lateral external force.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions： 1. 跟踪零件后续装配表现。 
                  Follow up assembly performance of the parts.
