Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 由于承受较大的振动载荷，造成托盘多处发生疲劳开裂。Battery tray is cracking though the fatigue fracture 
because of the big vibration load. 
（2） 横梁型材硬度偏低，晶粒较为粗大，成分及板厚正常。Crossbeam：Hardness is low and the grain size is rude. 
Chemical and thickness are normal. 
（3） 加强块硬度偏低，晶粒较为粗大。Enhancing block: Hardness is low and the grain size is rude. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 评估设计载荷。Evaluate the design load. 
（2） 供应商加强生产工艺监控，确保零件质量。Inspect the productive process to make sure the quality.
