Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  前下摆臂本体中存在初始裂纹，路试过程中疲劳扩展，在受到较大冲击载荷发生过载断裂。 
    Primitive cracks exist in the front lower control arm and the crack propagated by fatigue in using. Finally the front 
lower control arm fractured suffering from the big impact. 
2.  材料成分、硬度满足要求，微观组织正常。 
Chemical components and hardness meet the requirements. Microstructure is normal. 
  
建议Suggestions： 
1.  隔离同批次零件并对已装车零件进行更换。 
Isolate the same batch parts and replace the parts with using in the car.  
2.  排查裂纹形成原因。 
Check the reason of the crack.
