Ⅱ. 结论与建议Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 
砂眼孔与钢板交界处有环状突出，砂眼系由外物压入钢板表面所致。钢板激光拼焊过程中的夹具或飞溅产生
砂眼的主要原因。Sand Hole was caused by foreign body indention, ring extrude was found at the connection area between 
sand hole and sheet plate. Clamping or spatter during laser spelling welding process may be the main reason. 
建议：供应商检查其焊接夹具，做好防飞溅措施。Supplier should check the welding fixture and improve the anti-spatter measure.
