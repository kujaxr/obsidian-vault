Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 零件内应力过大，导致零件开裂。   2. 零件存在注塑孔洞。              
3. 零件材料符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1. The internal stress is overload, which lead to fracture.  2. There are injection holes in the parts. 
3. Material performance of part conformed to requirements. 
建议： 1. 供应商根据调整后的工艺参数&新增2h退火工艺进行生产。 
       2. 供应商继续优化工艺参数，确认最佳工艺参数。 
Suggestions:  1. Suppliers adjust process parameters & 2h annealing process according to the adjustment. 
            2. Suppliers continue to optimize the process parameters to confirm the best.
