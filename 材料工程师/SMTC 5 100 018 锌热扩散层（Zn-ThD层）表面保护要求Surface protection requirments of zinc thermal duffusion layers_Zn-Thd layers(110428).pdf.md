1  Scope 
This standard defines requirements for 
Cr(VI)-free zinc alloy coatings applied to ferr-
ous materials by means of thermal diffusion. 
This procedure is described in Appendix A.1. 
The standard is adopted to evaluate 
Cr(VI)-free zinc alloy coatings applied to ferr-
ous materials by means of thermal diffusion.

3  Requirements 
3.1 表面保护类型 
3.1  Surface protection types 
3.1.1 应用列于表1 中的保护类型。 
3.1.1  The surface protection types listed in 
Table 1 apply. 
3.1.2 对于工作温度高达300 ℃（例如，在发
动机组件中或在刹车和排气系统区）以及腐蚀会
增加的组件，使用银灰色涂料作重型防腐蚀保护
（保护级别6）。由于本喷涂方法不会引起氢脆，
所以该防腐蚀保护也可用于抗拉强度值Rm>1000 
MPa 或表面硬度值大于320 HV 的硬化和高强度
钢部件。 
 
3.1.2
The silver gray coatings are used as 
heavy-duty corrosion protection (protection 
class 6) for components for which operation 
temperatures of up to 300 °C (e.g., in the en-
gine compartment or in the area of the brake 
and exhaust systems) as well as an increased 
corrosion load are to be expected. Since hy-
drogen embrittlement cannot be caused by this 
coating process, this corrosion protection can 
also be used for hardened and high-strength 
steel parts with tensile strength values Rm> 
1000 MPa or surface hardness values > 320 
HV. 
3.1.3 由于扩散区与实际涂层之间没有过渡，
所以该表面处理特别适合用于松散材料，因为表
面机械损伤不会明显损害防腐蚀保护。 
 
3.1.3  The absence of a transition between 
the diffusion zone and the actual coating 
makes this surface treatment particularly suit-
able for so-called bulk goods, since mechani-
cal damage to the surface will not significantly 
impair corrosion protection. 
3.1.4 本涂料不适合用于与镁有接触的情况。
 
3.1.4  The coating is not suitable for applica-
tions where it comes into contact with magne-
sium. 
3.1.5 如果应用上述涂料，带公制ISO 螺纹的
螺纹部件只可作为多用途部件使用。本涂料不得
用于标准部件。 
 
3.1.5  Threaded parts with metric ISO threads 
may only be used as multi-purpose parts if the 
above mentioned coating was applied. Appli-
cation of this coating to standard parts is not 
permissible. 
3.1.6  锌热扩散层不应在客户视觉范围内使
用。如果钝化处理损坏，则在相对较短的时间内
会发生局部红色腐蚀。虽然这种腐蚀不会对部件
功能造成严重影响，但会在客户视觉范围内损害
部件外观。 
3.1.6  Zinc thermal diffusion layers should not 
be used in the visual range of the customer. In 
case of a damaged passivation treatment, lo-
calized red corrosion will occur within a rela-
tively short period of time. Although such cor-

3.2 一般要求 
3.2  General requirements 
3.2.1 根据SMTC 90008，避免有害物质。 
 
3.2.1
Avoidance of hazardous substances 
according to SMTC 90008. 
3.2.2 要进行完整测试，要求10 个制成部件。
对于小部件初次样品发布，例如，螺纹紧固元件，
应执行大于50 公斤的测试生产。 
 
3.2.2  10 finished parts are required for com-
plete testing. For first-sample release of small 
parts, e.g., threaded fastening elements, a test 
production of > 50Kg shall be performed. 
3.2.3 根据规格，涂料在扩张或收缩（如弹簧）
过程中不得出现任何剥落或裂纹。 
 
3.2.3  The coating must not exhibit any flaking 
or cracks during expansion or contraction (e.g., 
of springs) according to specifications. 
3.2.4 涂料必须没有裂痕和多余材料损害部件
功能。 
 
3.2.4  The coating must be free of flaws and 
excess material impairing the components 
function. 
3.2.5 对于各个部件，制造商、喷涂方法和使
用位置必须根据上海汽车中心实验室和/或技术
中心进行商定。 
 
3.2.5  Manufacturer, coating method and lo-
cation of use shall be agreed upon for each 
component with the SAIC Motor Central La-
boratory and/or with SMTC. 
3.2.6 保护层不得有大孔隙、大裂缝、损坏和
其他损害防腐蚀保护和/或指定外观的缺陷。 
 
3.2.6  The protective layers must be free of 
large pores, large cracks, damages and other 
flaws impairing the corrosion protection and/or 
the specified appearance. 
3.2.7  生产流程的安排不得损害部件的功能特
性。 
3.2.7  The layout of the production process 
shall not impair the functional characteristics of 
the part. 
3.3 带公制ISO 螺纹的螺纹零件 
 
3.3  Threaded parts with metric ISO 
threads 
3.3.1 本喷涂过程不应损坏连接件指定的机械
和物理属性。因此，制造商应核实喷涂公司指定
的喷涂温度是否适合用于待喷涂零件。 
 
3.3.1
The coating process must not impair 
the mechanical and physical properties speci-
fied for joining elements. Therefore, the man-
ufacturer shall verify whether the treatment 
temperatures specified by the coating compa-
ny are suitable for the parts to be coated. 
3.3.2 对于螺钉，下面列出的测试规范只适用
于头部和/或扳手轴承表面。对于螺母，测试规
范只适用于正面表面和/或扳手轴承表面。对于
螺纹及螺纹状成型部件，如双头螺栓，测试要求
只适用于正面表面。 
3.3.2 In the case of screws, the test specifi-
cations listed below only apply to the head 
and/or the wrench bearing surfaces; in the 
case of nuts they apply only to the face sur-
faces and/or wrench bearing surfaces. For 
threaded and thread-like shaped parts, such 
as tap end studs, the test requirements only 
apply to the face surfaces. 
3.3.3 在喷涂之前，螺纹零件应具有涂层厚度
对应的公差、符合上海汽车对带外部螺纹以及内
部螺纹的公制螺纹零件的相关公差要求。 
3.3.3  Prior to coating, threaded parts shall 
exhibit tolerances corresponding to the coating 
thickness; tolerances for metric threaded parts

3.4  Structure 
3.4.1 基材

3.4.1  Base material
For component base material see draw-

3.4.2 层结构 
a) 锌铁扩散层（约2-90％的锌，越靠近边
缘锌含量越高，表面允许最大10-20％
铁(Fe)）； 
b) 钝化处理层。 
3.4.2  Layer structure
a) 
Zinc-iron diffusion layer (approx-
imately 2 to 90% Zn, Zn content in-
creasing towards the edge, max. 10 
to 20% Fe permissible at the surface);
b) 
Passivation treatment layer. 
3.4.3 钝化处理层 
3.4.3  Passivation treatment layer
3.4.3.1 为提高在盐水和冷凝水中的抗腐蚀性
能，要求在钝化处理溶液中进行后期处理。 
 
3.4.3.1  In order to improve corrosion resis-
tance to salt water and condensed water, a 
post-treatment in passivation treatment solu-
tions is required. 
3.4.3.2  本流程中使用的钝化处理溶液不得含
有任何Cr
6+化合物，以确保所产生的钝化层同样
不含Cr
6+。 
3.4.3.2  The passivation treatment solution 
used in this process must not contain any 
Cr(VI) compounds in order to ensure that the 
resulting 
passivation layers 
are likewise 
Cr(VI)-free. 
3.4.4 顶部涂层/润滑剂 
3.4.4  Top coats/lubricants 
3.4.4.1 指定使用额外浸泡式润滑剂，以确保
公制螺纹连接件上达到定义的摩擦指数。在这种
情况下，应符合上海汽车公制 ISO 螺纹机械连
接件相关标准规定的摩擦系数。 
 
3.4.4.1
The use of additional immersion lu-
bricants is specified in order to ensure defined 
friction parameters on joining elements with 
metric thread. In this case, the coefficients of 
friction according to limit values for coefficients 
of friction of mechanical joining elements with 
metric iso thread of SAIC Motor shall be ad-
hered to. 
3.4.4.2  如果除防腐蚀保护之外，还要求其他
功能表面特性，如再喷涂性、与其他试剂的兼容
性、特定摩擦属性、特定螺纹特性、可硫化性或
特定温度特性的保护，则需执行针对特定零件的
测试或功能测试。 
3.4.4.2  If further functional surface characte-
ristics such as re-paintability, compatibility with 
other agents, specific friction properties, spe-
cific thread behavior, vulcanizability or specific 
temperature behavior are required besides 
corrosion protection, part-specific tests or 
functional tests must be performed. 
3.4.5 层厚度 
锌铁扩散层的厚度根据DIN EN ISO 1463（要
求请参看表2）使用切片测定。在标准生产过程
中，也可根据DIN EN ISO 3497用X射线荧光法测
量Zn/Fe含量来测量涂层厚度（例如，Helmut 
Fischer或Rˆntgenanalytik的Fischerscope测
量仪）。这要求使用一种合适的标准以及与切片
进行比较。（相关性测定取决于层厚度） 
 
3.4.5  Layer thicknesses 
The thickness of the zinc-iron diffusion 
layer is determined using a microsection ac-
cording to DIN EN ISO 1463 (see Table 2 for 
requirements). Layer thickness measurements 
during standard production may also be per-
formed with the help of the x-ray fluorescence 
method according to DIN EN ISO 3497 using 
the Zn/Fe measuring task (Fischerscope 
measuring device from Helmut Fischer or 
Röntgenanalytik, for example). This requires 
the use of a suitable standard as well as the 
comparison with a microsection (determination 
of the correlation dependent on the layer 
thickness).

3.5 倾向性 
3.5 Liability
3.5.1 钣金件的测试：使用1 mm 厚的钣金进行
弯曲测试，进行半径为2 mm 的90°弯曲。要求：
变形区不得发生碎裂。 
3.5.1  Test for sheet metal parts: Bending test 
using 1-mm-thick sheet metal, 90° bend with a 
radius of approx. 2 mm. Requirement: No 
chipping must occur in the deformed area. 
3.5.2  以下适用于连接件：根据规定，作为松
散材料不得因运输发生碎裂，在随后的组装中也
不得出现断裂。 
3.5.2  The following applies to joining ele-
ments: Chipping must neither occur as a result 
of transportation as bulk goods nor during the 
subsequent assembly according to specifica-
tions. 
3.6 防腐蚀行为 
3.6  Corrosion behavior 
3.6.1 对于螺纹紧固件，测试要求只适用于头
部和/或扳手轴承表面。以下适用于带螺纹的快
速紧固元件的柄部和螺纹区的评估：根据DIN EN 
ISO 9227 使用NSS（中性盐雾）测试方法，经过 
480 小时测试后不允许出现基底金属腐蚀。 
3.6.1 In the case of threaded fasteners, the 
test requirements apply only to head and/or 
wrench bearing surfaces. The following applies 
to the evaluation of the shank and thread 
areas of threaded and quick fastening ele-
ments: no base metal corrosion permissible 
after a test duration of 480 h using the NSS 
(neutral salt spray) test method according to 
DIN EN ISO 9227. 
3.6.2 在高温(120±5 ℃)下经过24 小时老化
实验后，防腐蚀保护不得被破坏。 
3.6.2  The corrosion protection must not be 
impaired by 24-hour aging at elevated tem-
perature (120 ± 5 ℃).

A.1  系统描述 
A.1  System description 
A.1.1  预处理 
要喷涂的零件采用磨料喷砂（颗粒大小为 
30-70微米）进行机械清洁处理。喷砂必须与要
喷涂的材料相匹配。喷砂之前要先清除涂油零件
的油脂。 
 
A.1.1  Pretreatment 
Parts to be coated are mechanically 
cleaned by means of abrasive blasting (30 to 
70 µm grain size). The blast material must be 
matched to the material to be coated. Oiled 
parts shall be degreased prior to blasting. 
A.1.2  喷涂流程 
A.1.2  Coating process 
A.1.2.1  这些部件放在喷涂容量达1000公斤的
转鼓中，然后将最高含有90%锌粉的粉体加入转
鼓中，添加量与部件大小成固定比例（约1-3％，
具体取决于所要求的涂层厚度）。 
 
A.1.2.1
The components are placed in drums 
with a capacity of up to 1,000 kg for coating. A 
powder consisting of up to 90 % zinc dust is 
then added to the drum in a fixed ratio to the 
component size (approx. 1 to 3 %, depending 
on the required layer thickness). 
A.1.2.2  除锌（颗粒大小<5微米）之外，粉体
还可包含金属和非金属添加剂以及加速和控制
实际喷涂流程的添加剂。 
 
A.1.2.2
In addition to the zinc (particle size < 
5 µm), the powder can also contain metallic 
and non-metallic additives as well as additives 
facilitating and controlling the actual coating 
process. 
A.1.2.3  转鼓灌装后关闭，并放置在一个室炉
中。 
A.1.2.3
The drum is closed after filling and 
placed in a chamber oven. 
A.1.2.4  一旦达到处理温度，在空气环境下，
扩散处理需要1.5-3个小时。 
A.1.2.4
The diffusion treatment takes 1,5 to 3 
hours under air atmosphere once the treat-
ment temperature is reached. 
A.1.2.5  在温度为330-400 ℃时（温度随材料
不同而不同），转动鼓会产生恒定厚度的扩散层，
以及导致整个部件表面的顶层稍微开裂。 
 
A.1.2.5
With a material-dependent tempera-
ture of 330 to 400 °C, turning the drum will 
result in a diffusion layer of constant thickness 
as well as a slightly cracked top layer across 
the entire component surface. 
A.1.2.6  扩散处理后，将该容器从室炉中移除，
冷却到室温，然后倒空。喷涂的部件没有粉末残
留，并使用超声波清洗，然后钝化，烘干。 
A.1.2.6
After the diffusion treatment, the 
container is removed from the chamber oven, 
cooled to room temperature and then emptied. 
The coated parts are freed from powder resi-
dues, cleaned using ultrasonics, passivated 
and dried.
