Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） A 档拨叉双向疲劳断裂失效，这应该与拨叉实际工作载荷较大有关。The A shift fork biaxial fatigue fractured 
which should be relation to the large working load.  
（2） 拨叉材料成分合格，硬度和金相组织正常。The chemical compositions of the fork is qualified, the hardness and 
microstructure are normal 
 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 调查拨叉载荷异常的原因。To investigation the cause of abnormal load of fork.
