Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 卡箍是由于应力腐蚀而开裂的；这可能是受到含S 腐蚀物作用的结果。The spring clamp was broken by SCC. 
Maybe this is because the corrosive thing including S touched the spring clamp. 
（2） 材料的成分，硬度和组织符合要求。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure meet the 
requirement. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 生产和储存过程中，避免腐蚀性物质与卡箍和胶管的接触。During the production and storage, it 
should be avoided that the corrosive thing touches with the spring clamp and rubber pipe. 
（2） 关注相同批次的卡箍。Pay attention to the same batch of the spring clamp.
