Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 失效件是过载引起的快速断裂。The thread failed due to overloading during tightening. 
2、 失效件和相关零件未发现异常。The failure part and related parts did not found abnormalities. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 校核扭矩标准，如维修、维护时的扭矩要求。Follow standard procedures, such as torque specification, when 
performing maintenance. 
2、请提供全部的失效件，以便更全面的分析。Please return all parts of the failed assembly for full analysis.
