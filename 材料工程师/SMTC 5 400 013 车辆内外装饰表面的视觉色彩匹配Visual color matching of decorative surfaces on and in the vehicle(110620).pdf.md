1 范围 
本标准规定了根据 DIN EN ISO 3668对车辆
内外装饰表面进行视觉色彩匹配的测试方法。 
本标准适用于上海汽车乘用车公司生产的
车辆内外的所有装饰表面。 
 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the test method of 
visual color matching of decorative surfaces on 
and in the vehicle according to DIN EN ISO 
3668. 
This standard applies to all decorative 
surfaces on and in the vehicle manufactured 
by SMC.

根据 DIN EN ISO 3668，按视觉匹配表面的
色彩。此外，需遵循以下规范。 
3  Test method 
The color of surfaces is visually matched 
according to DIN EN ISO 3668. In addition, the 
following specifications are to be observed. 
3.1 内外测试光类型 
在 D65 类（平均人工日光）和 F11 类（人
工暖白光）测试光中执行测试。如果有需要并经
相关测试站同意，可使用更多种类的测试光。 
3.1  Types of test lights for the interior and 
the exterior 
The test is performed in the test light types 
D65 (average artificial daylight) and F11 (artifi-
cial warm white light). If required and agreed 
upon by the test stations involved, more types 
of test lights may be used. 
3.2 匹配外表面 
3.2  Matching exterior surfaces 
3.2.1 实验室条件下的个别组件和测试板 
 
3.2.1 
Individual components and test 
plates under laboratory conditions 
3.2.1.1  评估效果色彩时，需变换观察和照明
方向。对于评估，要根据 DIN 6175-2 对准照明
测试角度和观察方向。具体如下： 
    a)  接近光泽度：对应约 25°的效果角；
b)  平面图：对应约 45°的效果角； 
c)  成角图：对应约 75°的效果角。 
3.2.1.1  When evaluating effect colors, the 
observation and lighting direction is to be va-
ried. For the evaluation, the test angle of the 
lighting and observation directions is to be 
aligned based on DIN 6175-2.  
a)  Close to glossiness corresponds to an 
effect angle of approx. 25°; 
b)  Plan view corresponds to an effect 
angle of approx. 45°;

3.3 匹配内表面 
3.3  Matching interior surfaces 
3.3.1 实验室条件下的个别组件和测试板 
为测试测得的色差的合理性，将试样放置在 
45°的测试角。对于效果色彩的评估，要遵循第 
3.2.1 节中的规范。当评估对擦拭敏感的材料
（例如丝绒地毯）时，在均匀的擦拭方向上评估
试样。 
3.3.1 
Individual components and test 
plates under laboratory conditions 
In order to test the plausibility of measured 
color deviations, the specimens are to be ar-
ranged at a test angle of 45°. For the evalua-
tion of effect colors the specifications of sec-
tion 3.2.1 are to be adhered to. When eva-
luating materials sensitive to brushing (e. g. 
velour carpet) the specimens are to be eva-
luated in uniform brushing direction. 
3.3.2 整车 
可在有或无车窗的条件下执行评估。建议在
有车窗的条件下执行评估（根据客户需求情况而
定）。如果有必要，相关测试站可能会同意在车
内按规定的照明和观察方向安排测试。 
3.3.2 Complete vehicle
The evaluation may be performed with 
and without vehicle glazing. Preferably, the 
evaluation is to be performed with vehicle 
glazing (customer’s situation). If required, the 
test stations involved may agree on a test ar-
rangement in the interior of the vehicle with 
defined lighting and observation directions. 
3.4 测试报告 
根据表 1 评估色差（根据 DIN EN ISO 3668 
的固定评估量表）。得到特定角度的色彩效果后，
