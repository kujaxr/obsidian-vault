Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）前缓冲梁定位孔处的断口形貌为脆性的沿晶与穿晶断口；这是在电泳的前处理过程中形成的。The front buffer 
beam’s location hole was cracked by intergranular and transgranular type; The brittle fracture happened during the 
pretreatment of the electrophoretic painting. 
（2）材料的成分和硬度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and hardness meet the requirements and the 
microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）调查泰国电泳工艺过程，以进一步确定缓冲梁开裂原因，并检查和评估已生产零件的风险。Investigate the 
electrophoresis process in Thailand to further research the root cause and at the same time check and evaluate the risk of 
the treated parts. 
（2）避免使用酸性介质进行清洗。 Avoid using the acid medium to clean the parts.

综合分析 Comprehensive Analysis 
 
前缓冲梁开裂位置处于热成型过程变形不是很剧烈的位置，不容易开裂；此外，该孔是通过激光切割形成，
不是冲切得到的，因此孔的加工不容易出现边部开裂。基于上述两点：可以排除该裂纹是在热冲压制造过程
中形成的可能性。The crack location of the buffer beam is without acutely deforming during hot stamping and it is 
not easy to cracked and at the same time, the hole was got by the laser cutting and not by stamping, so the hole 
machining can’t make the edge crack. Base on the two points: the possibility that the crack is formed in the process 
of hot stamping is eliminated. 
 
断口微观形貌为脆性的沿晶断口+穿晶断口；明显不同于人为过载形成的韧窝型断口。由此可以排除：热成
型完成后，受到外力导致孔的边部出现过载开裂的可能性。The cracks were intergranular and transgranular. The 
morphology is different with the man-made fracture surface. So it can be ruled out that the crack was caused by 
external force.  
 
该零件在国内是随车电泳，采用碱溶液进行清洗，并未出现类似的开裂；而在国外是零件单独电泳。如果采
用酸进行清洗，有可能导致零件变脆并出现开裂，特别是对于此类高强度零件。The parts’ electrophoretic 
painting in China is with the white car bodies and the cleaning liquid is alkaline liquid; no cracks have been found. 
In Thailand, the parts’ electrophoretic painting is made solely. During the painting, if the cleaning liquid is acidic 
liquid, the material will become brittle, especially for the high strength steel. 
 
基于上述分析来看：前缓冲梁出现脆性的沿晶和穿晶断口，不可能来自零件的制造过程；应该产生于电泳前
处理的清洗过程；这有可能是使用了酸性介质导致材料变脆所致。Base on the above analysis: the front buffer 
beam cracked by intergranular and transgranular type, which is not generated during forming and should happen in 
the pretreatment cleaning; maybe this is because the cleaning by acidic liquid made the material brittle.
