Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 前副车架与橡胶支撑的连接焊缝开裂是由于虚焊所致。The weld between the subframe and mounting rubber was 
cracked because of cold solder joint. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 追踪同批次的产品，抽检焊缝质量。Tracking the same batch of products, testing the weld quality. 
2. 供应商加强生产过程监控，确保产品质量。Supplier control the process strictly and make sure the quality.
