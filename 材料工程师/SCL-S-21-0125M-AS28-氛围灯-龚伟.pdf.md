Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 导光条亮度不均系表面附着有异物，异物来源为与对手件（支架）异常磨损所致。 
The uneven brightness of the light guide strip is due to the unknown material attached on the 
surface, which is caused by the abnormal abrasion of the hand piece (bracket). 
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  排查零件异常磨损原因并整改。 
                    Check the cause of abnormal wear and rectify.
