Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 前下控制臂内球头是由于多源疲劳而断裂的，断口形貌(见图3)显示其断裂前所受应力载荷较大。The ball stud 
was broken by fatigue and the fracture morphology (Figure 3) shows that the active stress load was big. 
2. 在部分裂纹源表面发现腐蚀坑，但无法确定其是在球头开裂前还是在开裂后形成的；The fractured source was 
found corrosion pits, but it was difficult to determine whether the corrosion exist before cracking. 
3. 左下控制臂内球头的化学成分，抗拉强度符合图纸要求，但其抗拉强度值处于临界点；Chemical components, 
tensile strength met the requirement, but the tensile strength was at critical point. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 校核内球头开裂处应力载荷；Check the active stress load at crack region. 
2. 调查内球头表面腐蚀物的来源；Investigate the source of corrosion.
