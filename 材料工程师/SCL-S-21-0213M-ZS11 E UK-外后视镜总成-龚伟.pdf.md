Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件被钢砂膏等物质污染，光照后，零件表面发白。 
The failed part is polluted by steel sand pastes, and turns white after illumination.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions： 1.  避免钢砂膏类物质对零件的污染。 
                    Avoid the pollution of steel sand paste on parts.
