Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件生产过程中混入了过量脱模剂或混入其他油脂，导致材料融合不良，形成鼓泡。 
Excessive release agent or other grease was mixed during the production process of failure part, 
resulting in poor fusion of materials and formation of blisters.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.排查生产环节异常，避免此类问题再次发生。 
                   Troubleshoot abnormal production processes to prevent such problems from recurring.
