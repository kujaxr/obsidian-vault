Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过O 型圈的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，装配过程中O 型圈受到挤压，拆卸时O 型圈在被挤
压损坏部位被撕裂。 
2. 经测试，O 型圈的材料符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the O ring, it can be seen that the O ring 
was impressed during assembly and the O ring was broken at the impressed region during disassembly. After 
the tests, the material of failure part conforms to the drawing requirement. 
建议：装配时保证O 型圈预安装到位；保证O 型圈硬度。 
Suggestions：The O ring should be pre-installed in place during assembly. The hardness of O ring should be 
guaranteed.
