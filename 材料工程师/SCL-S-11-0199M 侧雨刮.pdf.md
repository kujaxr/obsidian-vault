Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：雨刮胶条上许多各类损伤导致刮不净。 
Conclusions: A lot of scathes were observed at the edge of rubber strip which led to the failure of side wiper. 
建议：新雨刮在使用前妥善保存，避免受损。  
Suggestions：New wiper should be carefully preserved before use.
