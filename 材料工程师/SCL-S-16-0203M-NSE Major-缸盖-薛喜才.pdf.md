Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
（1） 铸件内部存在几乎贯穿性冷隔缺陷，造成零件泄漏。   
Cold shuts exist in the casting, which cause the leakage of the parts.  
 
（2） 材料成分满足要求，组织及硬度正常。 
Chemical components meet the technical requirement. Microstructure and hardness are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）供应商加强生产过程监控，查找产生冷隔的原因。 
Suppliers strengthen the production process monitoring and find the cause of the cold.
