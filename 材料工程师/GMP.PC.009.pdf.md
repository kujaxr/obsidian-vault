POLYCARBONATE — EXTERIOR LENS
GMP.PC.009
1
SCOPE.
This specification covers the properties of an
ultraviolet stabilized, transparent, injection molding grade pel-
let form, polycarbonate.
1.1 PAINTABILITY.
Approved paint systems that can be
used on this material are listed in the GM Approved Paint On
Plastic Systems (APOPS) database.
1.2 APPLICATIONS.
Typical applications include the fol-
lowing: lenses for automotive lighting equipment.
1.3 RESTRICTED AND REPORTABLE CHEMICALS
(GM1000M).
All materials supplied to this specification must
comply with the requirements listed in GM1000M, Restricted
and Reportable Chemicals.

3
TEST PROCEDURES.
Tests shall be conducted
using the latest issue of the test methods indicated. Suitable
precautions should be taken to ensure that applicable safe limits
are not exceeded when conducting test procedures, processes,
and/or handling the materials required by this specification.
3.1
Test specimens shall be injection molded according to
ASTM D1897. Test specimens shall be 3.2 ± 0.2 mm thick
unless otherwise specified, in this GMP specification. Izod
specimens shall be cut from the centers of the 127 x 12.7 x 3.2
mm bars.
3.2
Tests shall be conducted 40 h min after molding and
immediately following a conditioning period of 24 h min @ 22
± 3˚C and 50 ± 5% RH, unless otherwise specified. All testing
shall be done on unannealed samples.
