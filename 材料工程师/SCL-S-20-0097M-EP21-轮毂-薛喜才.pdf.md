Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 轮毂受到冲击、从表面铸造细小缺陷处萌生疲劳裂纹。 
   Wheel spider cracked by fatigue from tiny casting defect because of shock absorber. 
2. 轮毂材料成分以及硬度满足要求，组织无异常。 
   Chemical components and hardness a of wheel spider meet the requirement. Microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
1. 建议工程与供应商重新评估轮毂结构强度。 
   Suggest that the engineer and supplier estimate the structural strength.  
2. 继续关注该型轮毂售后失效情况。 
  Continue to pay attention to the after-sales failure of this type of wheel spider.
