Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1、失效件受外力导致脆断；2、失效件材料符合技术要求；3、失效件注塑工艺良好；4、金属件
防锈油对塑料件材料性能无明显影响；5、金属件边缘不圆润有毛刺，塑料件在失效位置未倒角。 
Conclusions：1、The brittle fracture of the parts caused by extrenal force. 2、Material performance of the 
failure parts conformed to requirements. 3、The injection molding process of the part is good. 4、The rust oil of the 
metal had no significant effect on the material properties of plastics part. 5、The metal edges are not rounded burr, 
and the plastic didn’t chamfered at the failure position. 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 检查装配与物流环节；Check the assembly and logistics process.  
2、 金属件边缘应圆润无毛刺，塑料件在失效位置应倒圆角分散应力。 The edge of metal part should be 
rounded, and the plastic should be chamfered at the failure position in order to disperse stress.
