Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  前稳定杆支架一侧螺栓先发生脱落，引发另一侧受力异常而快速疲劳断裂。 
    One side bolt falling out caused the other side suffering abnormal force and fast fractured by fatigue. 
2.  前稳定杆支架材料成分、强度满足QStE500TM 技术要求，板材厚度正常。 
Chemical components and strength meet the technical requirement of QStE500TM. Plate thickness is normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
1. 排查螺栓装配过程，查找螺栓脱落的原因。 
   Check bolt assembly process to find the loosing reason.
