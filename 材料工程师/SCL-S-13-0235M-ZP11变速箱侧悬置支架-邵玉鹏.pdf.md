Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 因在压铸过程中螺栓温度较低且螺栓头部沾有脱模剂，导致铸件成型过程中螺栓头部与铝液不能有效结合，
铸件与螺栓间隙过大导致螺栓松动；Because of the temperature is low and the bolt head stained with lubricating agent, causing the 
bolt can’t combined with molten aluminum effectively in the casting process, bolt is loosen by way of the gap is too large; 
（2）铸件与螺栓头部法兰边角结合处有裂纹，不符合图纸要求，裂纹均分布在螺栓头部尖角处，对铸件强度不利，
但不是螺栓松动的直接原因；There is crack in casting joint with bolt flange, which is don’t not conform to the requirements of the 
drawings, cracks are located in the sharp corner of bolt, disadvantage to the casting strength, but it is not the direct reason for loosening; 
（3）铸件和螺栓化学成分、硬度、金相组织符合要求，但铸件孔隙度明显偏大。The chemical composition, hardness, 
microstructure castings and bolts conform to requirements, but casting porosity is large significantly. 
建议 Suggestions：  
（1）生产过程中螺栓避免接触脱模剂，优化螺栓头部法兰面R角设计或优化压铸工艺（压铸前对螺栓预热），避
免热裂纹的产生；Avoid contacting with lubricating agent of bolt in production process, optimize R corner design or 
improving process avoiding heat cracks (preheat the bolt before casting); 
（2）图纸中增加铸件与螺栓结合处孔隙度要求。Add porosity requirement at casting and bolts junction in 
drawings.

角结合处有裂纹，2 个正常未松动件（以下简称正常件）在同样部位有裂纹，且均分布在上边缘无R 角处（见
图1～图7），这是由于尖角处存在应力集中，易产生热裂纹。
