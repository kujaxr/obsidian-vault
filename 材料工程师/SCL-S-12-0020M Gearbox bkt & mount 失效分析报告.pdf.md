Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 定位销由于过载而失效；该失效发生在装配或拆装过程中。The locating pins failed through overload, during 
either assembly or disassembly. 
2. 由于材料不足，变速箱悬置的铆钉在平面的上表面形成薄弱点。钢板和变速箱侧悬置的配合有小的
间隙，阻止铆钉完全穿透钢板，实现正常的铆接。The rivets of the transmission mount are weak at the 
plate top surface due to insufficient material.  The fit between steel plate and transmission mount inner 
leaves a small gap, preventing full penetration of the rivet.  
3. 螺栓固定位置无相对运动的迹象，表明夹紧力是充分的。The bolt fixing positions show no sign of relative 
movement, indicating that clamping load was sufficient 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 在装配和拆卸中，注意使用正确的扭矩。Take care during assembly and disassembly, use correct torque. 
2. 提高铆接质量（增加材料或改善配合）。Improve rivet quality (increase material or improve fit)

Gearbox Bracket  
The gearbox bracket is cast from alloy A380 (ASTM B85-09).  There was no suspicion of incorrect material, so this 
was not tested. 
Two fracture mechanisms were seen on the fracture surface, overload toward the bolt, and shear fracture, which indicated 
direction from left to right as seen in figure 3.  Since the overload fracture was adjacent to the shear fracture, it’s likely 
that this was a pre-crack, caused by knocking the pin (from direction of bolt).  The reduced-strength pin then sheared 
during further assembly.

The two rivet fractures showed different characteristics.  The left hand rivet in figure 8a has fractured at the top of the 
steel plate, fracture mechanism is uniaxial overload.  It’s noticable that there is little material at this point, so the rivet is 
weakened. The right hand rivet fractured after the left side.  Fracture is partially overload, changing to shear, with clear 
left-right direction. 
It is noted that the rivets are loaded in tension during torque tightening, if the steel plate does not fit well with the 
aluminium casting.
