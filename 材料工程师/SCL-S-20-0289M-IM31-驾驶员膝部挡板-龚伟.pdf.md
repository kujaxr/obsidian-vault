Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件断裂方向与使用时拉出方向一致。 
The broken direction of the failure part was the same as the pulling direction during using.  
2．失效件材料实测与分供方定义不一致。 
The material of the failure part is inconsistent with the definition of the sub-supplier.   
建议Suggestions： 1. 供应商解释材料异常原因并整改。 
                    The suppliers explain the material problem and rectify. 
2. 供应商优化零件结构。 
                    The suppliers improve parts’ structure.
