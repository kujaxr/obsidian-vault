Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions 
1.  发动机箱悬置断裂是由于悬置支架与车架发生干涉，产生较大的弯曲载荷所致。 
   The fracture of the chassis mounting is caused by the interference between the suspension bracket and the frame, 
resulting in a larger bending load. 
2.  材料的成分和硬度符合要求；孔隙和组织正常。 
   The composition and hardness of the materials meet the requirements, and the pores and tissues are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  加强悬置和车架关键匹配尺寸的控制。 
    Strengthening the control of the key matching dimensions of the suspension and frame.
