Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1. 变速箱悬置支架安装孔内存在双螺纹，导致螺栓滑牙。 
   Double screw thread was found in the transmission suspension, resulting in the bolt screw loose. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1.  隔离同批次零件。 
   Isolate the same batch parts. 
2.  对已装车零件进行风险评估。 
   Evaluate the risk of assembly parts.
