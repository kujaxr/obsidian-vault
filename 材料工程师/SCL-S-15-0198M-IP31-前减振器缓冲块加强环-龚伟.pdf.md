Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力沿熔接痕位置断裂，零件注塑工艺不佳。   
2. 零件结构设计不合理，导致存在较大缩孔，且此缩孔无法避免。3．零件材料符合技术要求。 
Conclusions:  1. The failure part fractured by excessive force along the weld line, and the injection process is poor. 
2. Structure design of the parts is unreasonable, leading to large shrinkage hole, which can’t be avoided. 
3. Material performance of the parts conformed to requirements.  
建议： 1. 以增加溢料井等形式来优化注塑熔接痕问题。    2．供应商更改结构设计以消除注塑孔洞。 
Suggestions:  1. Add overflow hole or other forms to optimize the injection molding welding line. 
            2．Supplier improves structure designs to eliminate the injection hole.
