Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）轴承由于局部接触应力过大而发生疲劳剥落失效。The bearing was failed by fatigue because of a big touching 
stress at the local. 
（2）相对通过试验的零件，失效件的沟槽较宽。这导致滚道面宽度变小，接触应力增大，对疲劳剥落失效起到了
促进作用。The groove width was wider than the one of the part passing the test, which reduced the width of work surface, 
increased the touching stress and promoted the fatigue. 
（3）失效轴承和通过试验轴承的材料无明显差异。其成分和硬度均符合要求；组织和夹杂物水平正常。The materials 
of the failed parts and the part passing the test are without difference and the chemical components and hardness meet the 
requirements; the microstructure and inclusion are normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）校核载荷，评估设计的安全系数。Check the load and evaluate the safety parameter.  
（2）增大滚道面宽度，同时在生产中加以控制。Increase the work surface width and at the same time control it during 
the production.
