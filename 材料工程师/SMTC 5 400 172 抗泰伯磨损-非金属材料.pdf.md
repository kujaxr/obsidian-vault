1  范围 
本标准规定了织物、皮革、塑料件等非金
属材料抗泰伯磨损的试验方法。 
本标准适用于测试一定的压力和砂轮下的
织物、皮革、塑料件等非金属材料的抗泰伯磨
损性能。 
                                          
 
1  Scope  
The 
standard 
specifies 
Taber 
abrasion 
resistance for non metallic materials such as 
fabric, leather and plastic. 
 
The Standard is suitable for determining the 
Taber abrasion resistance of non-metallic 
materials(such as fabric, leather and plastic) 
under controlled conditions of pressure and 
abrasive action.

4.1  状态调节 
 
4.1  Conditioning 
试验前，样品必须在ISO 291-23/50 的标
准气候下至少放置24h。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at 
least 24h before test.

4.4  试验过程 
 
4.4  Implementation of the test 
4.4.1  选择材料技术要求中规定的砂轮并安
装在仪器上,每次使用砂轮前的准备按ISO 
5470-1 第7.2 的规定。 
 
4.4.1  Select the specified types of wheels and 
installed on the instrument.Preparation before 
each use according to ISO 5470-1,clause 7.2. 
4.4.2  将试样测试面朝上安装在试样架的橡
胶垫上，并固定试样。试样表面张紧，没有褶
皱和扭曲。 
 
4.4.2  Locate the test specimen, with the 
surface to be tested uppermost, over the rubber 
mat on the specimen holder and secure, taking 
care that the test surface is taut and free from 
wrinkles and distortion. 
4.4.3  施加规定的载荷，启动试验，使砂轮接
触试样，并按规定的循环次数和速度进行试验。
同时打开吸尘装置，去除磨掉的碎屑。 
 
4.4.3 Apply the specified load, start the 
specimen platform, lower the wheels on to the 
specimen and run for the specified number of 
cycles. The vacuum cleaner is open at the 
same time to remove the worn debris. 
4.4.4  试验完成后移去试样，对仪器和样品进
行清理，并按照相关材料或产品标准的要求进
行检查。 
 
4.4.4  Remove the specimen, clean with a soft 
brush and examine in accordance with the 
requirements of the relevant material or product 
standard.
