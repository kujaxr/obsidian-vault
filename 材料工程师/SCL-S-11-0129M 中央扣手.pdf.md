Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，失效件材料符合图纸要求，但与新件材料牌号不一致。 
2. 通过拉手的材料分析和宏观分析可以判定，扶手扣手正常使用时受过大外力，导致扣手转轴快速断
裂。 
Conclusions: After the tests, the material of the plastic buckle conforms to the drawing requirement, but its grade is 
not the same as that of new part. According to the macroscopic observation and material test of the plastic 
handle, it can be seen that the shaft of buckle was broken by an excessive force quickly. 
建议： 采用图纸要求牌号制造扣手；避免扣手承受过大外力。 
Suggestions：The required material grade should be used in the production of buckle. The outside excessive force on 
the buckle should be avoided.

为了进一步确定失效件断口特征，进行了不同条件的弯折模拟试验。图3、图4 和图5 分别是快速弯折无缺
口模拟断口、快速弯折有缺口模拟断口和慢速弯折无缺口模拟断口。从图中可以看出撕扯纹方向与断裂方向一致，
在初始变形区域存在明显折痕，在有缺口模拟断口观察到横纹存在。所以根据模拟实验的结果，可以确定失效件
的断裂方向由图6 箭头所示。   
In order to know the kinds of crack features, simulation tests have been done at different conditions. Fig 3, 4 and 5 
are the crack surfaces at fast bending test, fast bending with a notch test and slow bending test, respectively. From the 
pictures, it can be seen that the direction of ductile tearing cracks are the same as that of fracture direction, the bending 
lines also appeared at the beginning deformation region, several cross crack traces are found the crack surface of fast 
bending with a notch test. According to the simulation test crack feature, it can be seen that the fracture direction of failure 
part is as arrow shown in Fig 6.
