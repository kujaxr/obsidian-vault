1 范围 Scope 
 
 
本标准规定了对汽车内饰件的涂装要求。 
本标准适用于安装于汽车内部的油漆、胶头印
刷、热压、模内转印和水转印方法涂覆的（内部装
饰）金属和非金属材料。本标准将内饰涂装零件分
为I类与II类： 
a) I类零件：中级轿车及以上车型的内饰涂装
件（B级车及B级以上车型）与出口欧洲的
车型； 
b) II类零件：中级轿车以下车型的内饰涂装
件（B级车以下车型）。 
 
The 
standard 
specifies 
the 
paint 
coating 
requirement on vehicle interior trim. 
 
This Technical Supply Specification applies to 
painted, 
pad-printed, 
heat-sealed, 
in-mold 
decorated (IMD) and cubic-printed metallic and 
non-metallic materials, which are used in the 
vehicle interior. This standard classifies the 
interior trim parts into Type I and Type II. 
a) Type I: interior trim parts in B grade cars 
and above, cars exported to European 
b) Type II: interior trim parts in cars below 
grade B

4  漆层性能 The performance of the coating 
4.1  预处理Conditioning 
    所有有待检验的样品必须进行预置：在空气温
度范围23±5°C内存放7 天或在60±2℃环境内存
放48h。 每个单项检验后零件必须预置30 min，以
便进行目视评定。按照第4.3 、4.4 条检验必须在
23±5℃预置1 h。纤维增强、滚珠增强或发泡零件
必须在23±5℃下的预置24 h后进行检验。 
 
  
All 
samples 
before 
testing 
must 
be 
pre-conditioned at least 7 days at room 
temperature 23±5°C or 48 h at 60±2 °C. Parts 
must be preset 30 min after each individual test, 
for visual evaluation. Testing acc. to Sections 4.3 
and 4.4 shall be performed after 1 h of 
conditioning at 23±5°C. Fiber-, ball-reinforced or 
foamed parts shall be tested after 24 h of 
conditioning at 23±5°C.  
4.2  涂层厚度Coating thickness 
    涂层厚度应满足油漆供应商提供的TDS要求。
如果因某种原因无法达到（如外观效果冲突），而
需要调整膜厚，则必须得到中心实验室的同意。 
 
Normally, the coating thickness of the parts 
(including 
the 
testing 
sample 
and 
mass 
production parts) should be in the TDS scope. If 
limited in certain factor (for example, conflict with 
the appearance), the modification process must 
be approval by SCL. 
4.3  附着力 Adhesion 
  
按照ISO 2409 ，用粘贴的胶粘带突然剥离进
行试验。胶带牌号：德莎4657。特征值要求：≤1
级。如果受到零件表面结构或大小的限制无法进行
该试验，可以用十字切割试验替代，要求：无油漆
颗粒黏附在胶带上。 
 
Test according to ISO 2409, tape: TESA 4657. 
Requirement: Characteristic value≤ 1. If limited 
by the parts structure or size, crosscut test can be 
used to replace with ISO 2409. Requirements: no

4.4  耐刮性能Scratch resistance 
    用型号318 的Erichsen-硬度试棒，10N 负荷，

验。要求：涂层无油漆的开裂现象，但是由负荷造
成的基材有一个凹陷口是允许的。

with 10 N load and a test needle (point: 0,75 mm) 
according to SMTC 5 400 014. Requirement: The

allowed.

4.13.2  液滴试验Drop test

10min持续作用后，在60℃ 循环空气中干燥30 
min。表面评定应在处理后24h进行。要求：无油漆
色调，光泽度和漆层的改变；试验后会有一定光亮
度改变，只有当湿布不能够抹去表面痕迹时视为缺
陷。 
 
 
Test: Drop 0.1 mL droplets on the surface. After 
10 min, it is allowed to dry for 30 min at 60 °C. 
The surface should be assessed after 24h.  
Requirements: No change in color, degree of 
glossiness or paint-finish structure; Changes in 
degree of glossiness are to be stated only if they 
cannot be removed with a wet cloth.
