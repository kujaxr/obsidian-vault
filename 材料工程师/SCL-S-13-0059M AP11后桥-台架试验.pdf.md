Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）加强板与横梁的接连焊缝开裂是由于焊缝熔合不良所致。The weld between the reinforcing plate and the beam 
was cracked because of bad fusion. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）追溯同批次的产品，抽检焊缝质量，以确定是否存在批量风险，并采取相应的措施。Trace the same batch of 
products and check the weld quality in order to determine whether there is a batch risk and then make corresponding 
measures. 
（2）审核供应商的生产过程记录，查明焊接不良的原因。Audit the record about the produce and find out the root 
cause for bad fusion. 
（3）供应商应执行严格的生产过程监控，确保产品的质量。The supplier should control the produce according to the 
strict process rule and ensure the quality.
