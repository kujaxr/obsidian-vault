橡胶软管组合件管接头 
拔脱负荷 
End fitting pull off load of 
rubber hose assemblies 
1 范围 
    本标准规定了测量高压软管组合件接头拔
脱力的测试方法。 
本标准适用于具有通过扣压或者挤压方式
永久连接管接头的高压软管组合件。 
 
                                       
1  Scope  
This standard specifies the method which 
is applicable to high-pressure hose assemblies 
and measure the load required to separate 
these end fittings from the hose. 
This standard applies to the hose assem-
blies having end fittings permanently attached 
by crimping or swaging. 
2 设备 
2  Apparatus 
  配备记录器并可以承受25 mm/min 的十字
头速度的拉伸试验机。 
   Tensile testing machine equipped with a 
recorder and capable of crosshead speeds of 
25 mm/min. 
3 特定要求 
3 
Specific requirements 
软管应在试验前在23 ℃ ± 2 ℃ 的温
度、65% ± 5%的相对湿度下调节24 h。 
   Hose shall be conditioned for 24 h at 
23 °C ± 2 °C and 65% ± 5% relative humidity 
prior to test. 
 
4 程序/试验 
4  Procedure 
4.1 将管接头插入拉伸试验机夹具内，确保夹
持的管接头超出软管组合件的橡胶部分。 
4.1  Insert the end fittings into the grips of the 
tensile machine ensuring the grips are set to 
hold the ends beyond the rubber section of the 
hose assembly. 
4.2 以设置为25 mm/min 的十字头速度，开动
试验机并做好记录，直至发生故障。 
4.2  With the crosshead speed set at 25 
mm/min, start the machine and recorder and 
run until failure occurs. 
4.3 记录破坏载荷，单位为牛顿。 
4.3  Record the failure load in Newtons. 
4.4 对另外两个试样重复第5.1 至5.3 的程
序。 
4.4
Repeat clauses 5.1 to 5.3 on two further 
samples. 
5 报告 
5  Report 
在三次测定中，记录故障种类和最大拉伸载
荷的算术平均值。 
   Report the mode of failure and the arith-
metic mean of the maximum tensile loads for 
each of the three determinations.
