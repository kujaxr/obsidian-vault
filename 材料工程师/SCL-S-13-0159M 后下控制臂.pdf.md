Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 后控制臂是由于疲劳而开裂的，开裂处位于控制臂焊接处，裂纹起源于边部。The rear lower arm was fatigue 
broken, the crack location was at the welding, and the crack propagated from the wedge of the parts. 
2. 零件微观组织无异常，化学成分、厚度符合要求。Microstructure was normal, the chemical components and 
thickness met the requirement 
. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 优化支架的焊接位置或更改支架的连接方式，禁止在控制臂边部进行焊接。Optimized the connecting of 
bracket or changed the welding location, prohibited welding at the wedge of rear lower arm.
