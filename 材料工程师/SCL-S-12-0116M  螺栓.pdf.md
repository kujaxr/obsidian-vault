Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 由于螺纹收尾处的牙底圆角不合格，产生应力集中致使螺栓疲劳断裂。 The bolt has failed through fatigue 
fracture due to stress concentration at a sharp radius at the thread end. 
2、化学成份不符合图纸技术要求要求。 The chemical composition did not meet to the requirements of the 
drawing. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 改进工装模具。Improve the dies. 
2、 加强过程质量控制。strengthen the process of quality control.
