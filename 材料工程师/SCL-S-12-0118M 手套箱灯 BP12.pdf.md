Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 零件注塑工艺不佳。 
      2. 零件材料灰分偏高。 
Conclusions:  1. The injection process of this part was bad. 
 
  2. The dash content of this part is higher than requirement. 
建议： 1. 责令供应商对零件材料和工艺问题进行解释和整改。 
Suggestions：1. Let supplier explain to the material and injection process problems and rectify them.
