Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 输入轴齿表面烧伤导致强度降低，使用后疲劳失效。The input shaft gear surface burns lead to strength reduced, 
the gear fatigue fractured after use； 
（2） 输入轴材料成分和芯部硬度合格，表面硬度由于烧伤偏低。The composition and core hardness are qualified, 
the surface hardness is low due to burns. 
. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 排查齿加工过程，确认烧伤原因。Check the gear processing process to check the cause of burn .
