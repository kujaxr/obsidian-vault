范围Scope  
本标准规定了氟橡胶（FKM）材料要求和试
验方法。 
本标准适用于静态密封类氟橡胶材料，例如
O型圈，密封圈等零件，不适用于动态密封类密
封件材料要求。 
 
This standard specifies the material require- 
ments and test methods of fluoroelastomer 
(FKM). 
This standard applies to the FKM rubbers used 
in the static seal parts, such as the O-rings, 
sealing, etc., and is not applicable for dynamic 
seal parts.

test sheet of 2mm thick is only used when the 
dimension of the part cannot meet the 
specimen preparation.

试验要求Notes on Testing 
4.1  硬度测试Hardness Test  
硬度要求在图纸上或双方协议规定，硬度公
差一般为 ± 5。O 型圈及类似密封件的硬度采用
国际微型硬度(IRHD)进行表征，其余零件硬度
采用邵氏硬度。如无特殊要求，邵氏硬度测试依
据ISO 48-4，测试时间为3 秒，IRHD 硬度测试
依据ISO 48-2，测试时间为30 秒。 
 
The hardness is specified on the drawing or in 
the agreement of the both side. The tolerance 
is preferred as ±5. The hardness of O-Ring or 
similar parts is examined by IRHD hardness, 
and Shore hardness is used for other part. The 
test period of Shore hardness (ISO 48-4) is 3s, 
and that of the IRHD hardness (ISO 48-2) is 
30s if there is no special requirement.

4.2  热空气老化Heat Aging Resistance 
测试样条存放在符合 ISO 188 要求的鼓
风式烘箱内，试验完成后取出样条 30 分钟后
进行测试。 
 
Test specimens are aged in a mechanical 
circulation oven according to ISO 188. All Tests 
are performed 30 minutes after specimen 
removal. 
4.3  油品兼容性Oil Resistance 
依据ISO 1817，测试试样存放在装有油品
的容器内，需使用零件实际工作中接触的油品
（机油、变速箱油或其他），液体体积至少为试
样体积的80 倍。试样在介质中冷却至室温，从
介质中取出样条30 分钟后进行测试。 
 
According to ISO 1817, test specimens are 
aged in a bottle with the actual oil used in the 
car (Engine Oil, Transmission Fluid and other 
oil contact with the part in use) and the volume 
of the oil is at least 80 times of that of the 
specimens. The specimens remained in the oil 
until cooled down to the ambient, and all tests 
shall be performed 30 minutes after removal 
from test liquid. 
4.4  耐燃油老化Fuel Resistance 
依据SMTC 5 320 041 中4.3 的要求进行耐
燃油老化试验。测试在试样从液体中取出15 分
钟后进行试验。 
 
The test is conducted according to the item 4.3 
in SMTC 5 320 041. Evaluation is performed 
15 min after removal from the test fluid. 
4.5  耐燃油老化后干燥 Dry After Fuel Aging 
依据SMTC 5 320 041 中4.4 的要求进行耐
燃油老化后干燥试验。干燥温度为150℃。 
 
The test is conducted according to the item 4.4 
of SMTC 5 320 041. The temperature of drying 
is 150℃. 
4.6  耐曲轴箱介质循环Crankcase Gases Aging Cycle
