Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 离合器由于受到了异常载荷从而发生压盘断裂以及壳体开裂。The clutch born a abnormal load, which 
caused the platen broken and the shell of the clutch cracked. 
（2） 压盘边部区域的石墨为E 型和D 型，不符合要求；其中E 型石墨会增加材料的脆性，使得压盘在大载荷
下开裂的可能性增加。The graphite shape of the platen close to the edge is E and D type, which doesn’t meet 
the requirement. The graphite of the E type could increase the brittle property and caused the cracking 
possibility of platen increasing under a big load.  
（3） 压盘的成分，硬度以及基体组织符合要求。The chemical components, hardness and the matrix structure of the 
pressure platen met the requirement. 
（4） 离合器壳体的成分和强度符合要求；组织正常。The chemical components and strength of the clutch housing 
meet the requirements and the microstructure is normal. 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 加强铸造工艺过程控制，确保压盘的石墨形态符合要求。 Control the casting process well and ensure the 
graphite shape meets the requirement. 
（2） 售后服务部门了解客户的驾驶操作。The sale service department knows the customer’s driving.
