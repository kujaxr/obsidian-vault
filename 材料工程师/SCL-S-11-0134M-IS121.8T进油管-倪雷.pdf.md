Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1) 
该断裂是钢管在冷拔过程中产生的延性断裂，同时在断面上发现一48.3um 的夹杂物。The fracture 
is a ductile fracture in the steel cold-drawn, and found a 48.3um inclusion in the fracture. 
2) 
零件的金相组织为针状魏氏组织（由钎焊导致，详见报告SCL-L-11-0464M），不符合材料要求，
这将导致材料韧性差。同时表面有明显脱碳降低了表面力学性能。The Microstructure is acicular 
widmanstatten structure（By the brazing result, see the report SCL-L-11-0464M）, don’t conform 
to the material requirements, this will result in poor toughness. And the significant 
decarburization that reduced the mechanical property at the surface. 
3) 
材料的成分、和力学性能等符合要求。The material composition and mechanical property meet 
the requirements.  
建议Suggestions： 
1） 零件供应商应加强原材料夹杂物质量检验。Parts suppliers shall strengthen the quality inspection 
of raw materials inclusion.  
2） 零件供应商改进钎焊工艺，以避免零件材料出现针状魏氏组织，严格控制气密性试验。Parts 
suppliers must improve the brazing process, avoid the acicular widmanstatten structure of parts, 
and strictly control the air-tightness test materials.
