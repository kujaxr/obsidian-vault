1 应用范围 
本标准规定了对锌镍合金镀层中的锌、镍
含量的测定方法。 
本标准适用于对锌镍合金镀层中的锌、镍
含量的测定。 
1  Scope  
This standard specifies a method to determine 
the content of nickel in the zinc-nickel alloy coatings. 
This standard is suitable to determine the con-
tent of nickel in the zinc-nickel alloy coatings. 
2 测试 
2  Test 
2.1  设备 
250 ml 烧杯、玻璃漏斗、500 ml 容量瓶和
100 ml 容量瓶、吸量管、量筒、热板、通风柜、
原子吸收分光光度计。 
2.1  Equipment 
250 ml beaker, glass funnel, 500 ml volumetric 
flask and 100 ml volumetric flask, pipettes, graduated 
cylinder, hot plate, fume hood, atomic absorption 
spectrophotometer. 
  
2.2  化学品 
 
浓盐酸、浓硝酸、蒸馏水或去离子水、三
氯化铁、标准镍溶液：每千毫升含镍 1 g。 
2.2  Chemicals 
Hydrochloric acid concentrated, nitric acid con-
centrated, distilled or de-ionized water, iron（lll）
chloride, standard nickel solution: 1 g nickel in 1000 
ml. 
2.3. 试样制备 
将试样截面或部分（例如紧固件）放置在 
250 ml 烧杯内，然后在烧杯内加入 20 ml 的浓
盐酸和 1 ml 的浓硝酸。加热后镀层会溶解，同
时产生大量气体。待镀层完全溶解后，将溶液
倒入 500 ml 容量瓶。待其冷却后，倒入蒸馏水，
直到装满到刻度处。应使用该储备液配制所需
的稀释剂。 
 
2.3  Specimen Preparation 
    The specimen section or part(such as a fastener) 
shall be placed in a 250 ml beaker, to which 20 ml 
concentrated HCl and 1 ml concentrated H2NO3 shall 
be added. The coating will dissolve when heated, and 
there will be vigorous generation of gas. After the 
coating has dissolved completely, the solution shall 
be poured into a 500 ml volumetric flask, and when it 
has cooled it shall be filled to the mark with distilled 
water.  The required dilution shall be produced from 
this stock solution.   
2.4  校准溶液和空白溶液 
配制校准溶液时, 配液量应与样品溶液的
量相等。校准溶液的浓度应在锌镍线性测量范
围的上三分之一内。锌的活动呈线性变化，直
到达到 1.0 mg/L的浓度；镍的活动呈线性变化，
直到达到 5.0 mg/L的浓度。校准和空白溶液中
的盐酸或硝酸浓度应与样品溶液中的浓度相
符。此外，应通过滴入盐酸三氯化铁溶液按颜
色调节校准溶液中的铁浓度。 
2.4  Calibrating solution and blank solution 
The same volume shall be used with the sample 
solution, especially when preparing the calibrating 
solution. The concentration in the calibrating solution 
should be in the upper third of the linear measuring 
range for zinc and nickel. Zinc behaves linearly until a 
concentration of 1.0 mg/L is reached, whereas nickel 
has linear behavior until a concentration of 5.0 mg/L 
is reached.  The hydrochloric acid or nitric acid 
concentration of the calibrating and blank solutions 
should correspond to that of the sample solution.  In 
addition, the iron concentration in the calibrating so-
lution shall be adjusted colorwise by adding drops of 
hydrochloric-acid FeCl3 solution. 
2.5  测量 
应采用原子吸收分光光度法确定样品溶液
中的锌和/或镍浓度。 
 
2.5  Measurement  
The zinc and/or nickel concentrations of the 
sample solution shall be determined by atomic ab-
sorption spectrophotometry.
