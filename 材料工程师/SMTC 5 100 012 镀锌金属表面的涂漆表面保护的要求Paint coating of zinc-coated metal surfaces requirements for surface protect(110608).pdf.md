1 范围 
本标准规定了在镀锌部件上施涂各种表面
类型的有机涂层（单层液体漆和阴极电泳漆）的
表面保护要求。 
本标准中Sp-x632 适用于有镀锌工序的零
部件，Sp-x633 适用于半成品的镀锌件。 
1  Scope  
This standard defines the surface protection 
requirements for organic coatings (single-layer 
liquid paint or cathodic dip paint) on zinc-coated 
parts with the protection types. 
Sp-x632 for zinc coating of individual parts 
and Sp-x633 for parts made of zinc-coated semi 
finished products.

3.2  预处理 
如果使用风干涂层系统和/或该油漆系统并
非在高温下（大于60 ℃）烘干，则零部件应于
试验前在60 ℃下预处理24 h，然后冷却至室温
（18～28）℃。 
3.2
Conditioning
If air-drying coating systems are used and/or 
the paint system is not burned in at elevated tem-
peratures (T>60°C), the components shall be 
conditioned for 24 hours at 60 °C before the test 
and then cooled to room temperature (18 to 
28) °C.
