Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论：零件材料性能符合要求。断口分析可得，暖风箱回水管受到意外损伤，导致破裂漏油。  
Conclusions: The material perfomance of the hose conforms to the requirments.Throuth the fracture analysis, 
we can get the conclusion that the hose was injured accidently by external damage which caused the leakage. 
 
建议：在胶管安装过程中，注意胶管不受外界损伤。 
Suggestion: Avoid external damage on the installation process of the hose.

2 断口分析（Analyze）： 
 
对LSJZ14E38BS067837 零件胶管的裂纹进行分析： 
 
断口表面的橡胶基体平整，无杂质、气泡等异常现象。异常裂纹纹路笔直，结合故障公里数，可排除材料问题引起的破裂。 
The fracture surface of the rubber is smooth. There are no impurities, bubbles and other anomalies on the fracture. Considering 
the car mileage of the failure and the straight abnormal crack, we can get that the damage dose not caused by the material. 
 
 
胶管外层裂纹口处有明显凹陷和破损，且与异常裂纹相对应，证实此裂纹内外贯通。说明外界有尖锐物刺穿胶管，导致胶管破
裂，出现液体泄漏。 
There is significant depression and damage on the outside of the tube, and it corresponds to the abnormal crack. The 
abnormal crack runs through the hose. So we can get the conclusion that some sharp objects pierced the tube which caused the 
damage and leakage.
