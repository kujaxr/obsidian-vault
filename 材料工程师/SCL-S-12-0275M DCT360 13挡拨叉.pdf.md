Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）拨叉是通过多源疲劳而开裂。局部应力过大，导致裂纹在焊缝处产生，然后逐渐扩展。The shift fork is cracked 
through fatigue, Big local stress caused the crack to begin from multiple origins of the weld and then gradually expand. 
（2）材料的成分和强度符合要求；组织正常；焊缝的深度满足图纸要求，无焊接缺陷。The chemical compositions 
and strength meet the requirement; the microstructure is normal; the penetration meet the requirement and no defect is 
found in the weld. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化设计，降低局部应力。Optimize the design and reduce the local stress.
