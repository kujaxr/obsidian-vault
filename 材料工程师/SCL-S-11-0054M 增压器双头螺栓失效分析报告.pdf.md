Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 
失效件是由夹紧力降低引起的弯曲疲劳断裂。这种情况是由于螺栓紧固顺序不合理引起的，在紧固其他螺栓
的螺母时，第一个紧固的螺栓夹紧力降低了，使不同螺栓之间的夹紧力不一致。 The part has failed through bending fatigue due 
to loss of clamping load. Such case can arise from not following a correct tightening sequence, so that the part is not aligned during tightening. Tightening of the 
other nuts then realigns the joint and the clamping load in the first bolt is lost. 
2、 
硬度、基体组织符合技术要求。化学成分不合格（Mo 低于技术要求），对失效无影响,。但是供应商明知道材
料不合格，还是使用了。 The hardness and microstructure conform to the requirements. The chemical composition (Mo) does not meet the 
requirement.  Although this did not affect the failure, the supplier knew about the result and did not reject the material. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 螺母安装顺序必须遵守。 先把三螺母本身夹紧, 再到扭转要求(62±3),才能保证紧密的连接。 装配时候，应该确
认零部件不受热。  Assembly tightening sequence must be followed.  Snug tightening followed by torque tightening to 62±3 is recommended to 
ensure tight joint.  Engineering dept must confirm that the components are cold when assembled.  
2、 请检查垫片材料情况。 Material and condition of the gasket should also be checked

三  安装分析(紧固扭矩)  Assembly consideration 
1. PAM 安装扭矩是62±3Nm, 摩擦系数是0.12-0.18. 经计算的夹紧力是15-40kN.  
The specified assembly torque is 62±3Nm, Coefficient of friction µ=0.12-0.18. From calculation, the achieved clamping load is 15-40kN. 
2. 螺栓是M10，10.9 级。失效件的硬度是38HRC，经换算可得其抗拉强度为1195MPa。 经计算可得螺栓的破坏载
荷是69kN。 
The bolt is M10 grade 10.9.  Hardness was measured at = 38HRC = 1195MPa. From calculation, the force required to break the bolt = 69kN. 
3. 即使摩擦系数=0.18, 夹紧力最低(15kN), 需要最少20%螺栓截面支承所的夹紧力，但是疲劳裂纹占据95%的螺栓
截面. Even if µ= 0.18 and clamping load is at lowest value (15kN), then at least 20% of the bolt is required to support the clamping load.  But the fatigue 
crack has taken 95% of the bolt area.   
 
结论：夹紧力降低引起的疲劳断裂。 
Therefore, there was no clamping load remaining in the joint during the fatigue failure.
