1   范围Scope 
 
 
本标准规定了POM聚合物 
成品件材料要求。 
本标准适用于POM 聚合物的成品件的材料
性能。 
本标准要求范围不适用于增韧、增强以及其
他种类的改性POM。 
 
This standard specifies the material require-
ments of finished parts made of POM. 
 
This standard applies to the material require-
ments of finished parts made of POM. 
 
This standard can not be applied to the high 
impact or reinforced or other kinds of modified 
POM

3  要求Requirements 
 
 
3.1 样品和预处理要求Sampling and conditioning requirements 
3.1.1 样品要求Sampling requirements 
用于本标准所规定的测试试样，应当从
成品上取样。若遇到无法从成品上取样时，
其样品制备方法应当与SMPV中心实验室协
商确定。为完成试验，需要多个成品零件。 
 
The test samples stipulated in the Standard. 
Samples should be made from finished products. 
If samples can’t be made from finished products, 
sample preparation method should be determined 
through the negotiation with SMPV SCL. Several 
parts are required for complete testing. 
3.1.2 预处理Conditioning 
 
 
试验前，测试样品必须在ISO 291标准
气候条件23℃,50 %RH下至少放置48 h。 
 
The test specimens should be conditioned at 
23 °C，50%RH according to the ISO 291 at least 
48h before test.

3.2.7 光照性能Light fastness 
 
 
本要求仅针对材料应用于内饰产品。试
验方法按照SMTC 5 400 003，要求灰色牢度
等级  4，按照ISO105-A02，目视外观不得
出现缺陷和颜色等明显变化。光照周期按照
下列规定： 
a) 在非阳光直接照射区域中的构件 
为三个光照周期； 
b) 在阳光直接照射区域中的构件为 
五个光照周期（例如：车门胸区）； 
c) 阳光强烈照射区域中的构件为十 
个光照周期（例如：后搁板） 
 
Light fastness test only for the materials used in 
the interiors products. The test is according to 
SMTC 5 400 003. Requirements: Gray-scale level 
≥ 4 according to ISO105-A02. No significant 
changes in color and other defects. And the test 
periodicity defined as below: 
a)  3 periods of exposure for components in 
areas subject to indirect sun radiation; 
b)  5 periods of exposure for components in 
areas subject to direct sun radiation (e.g., 
door upper trim); 
c)  10 periods of exposure for components in 
areas subject to the highest sun radiation 
(e.g., rear shelf).

验和要求。测试方法为采用显微镜检测受力
区域或CT 无损检测，测试要求结合零件的
性能验证进行定义。 
 
For POM parts， the porosity should test and 
defined. Either microscope or CT detection can 
be adopted for porosity test. The requirements 
can be defined according to the part ADV.
