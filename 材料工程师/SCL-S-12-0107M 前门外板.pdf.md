Ⅱ. 结论与建议Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 
    前门外板用钢板内外表面“黑点”缺陷为钢板表面砂眼，系由外物压入钢板表面所致，属于制造过程缺陷。
具体结果见后页。“Black point” of Front door outer plate surface is the Sand Hole defect which was caused by foreign 
body indention. It is happened during sheet manufacturing. 
建议： 
供应商检查其钢板镀锌至卷取过程，做好防护措施。Supplier should check the manufacturing process and improve the 
protect measure.
