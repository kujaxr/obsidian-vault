Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）P14 轴承的内圈，外圈和钢球均发生了疲劳剥落失效；这很可能是由于接触应力较大所致。The inner ring, outer 
ring and steel ball of the bearing were failed by fatigue spalling, maybe which was because of big contact stress. 
（2）内圈，外圈和钢球的硬度符合要求；组织正常；未见明显的夹杂物。The hardness of the inner ring, outer ring and 
steel ball meet the requirements; the microstructure is normal; there is no obvious inclusion. 
（3）内圈和外圈的成分正常。The components of the inner ring and outer ring are normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）检查变速箱标定，校核轴承的载荷，并优化设计，提高疲劳寿命。Check the transmission calibration and the load 
of the bearing, and at the same time optimize the design to improve the fatigue life. 
（2）检查可能增加载荷的相关变速箱尺寸。Inspection the relative sizes of the gear box which increase the load.
