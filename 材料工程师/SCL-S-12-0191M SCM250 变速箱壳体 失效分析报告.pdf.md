Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 离合器壳体发生失效，疲劳裂纹起始于表面孔隙。由于大量的疏松呈互连特性，在材料空隙间小区域的发生
过载，裂纹易发生扩展。The part has failed through fatigue, which was was initiated at surface porosity.  Due to a 
significant amount of interconnecting shrinkage porosity, the crack spread easily, through overload of the small areas 
of material between the voids.   
2. 发现一个次生的疲劳裂纹，在尖角处发起。在该点附件没有发现明显的缺陷。 A secondary fatigue crack was 
found, initiated at a sharp corner.  No obvious defects were found near to this origin. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 改进铸造工艺，以消除这些缺陷, 尤其在表面。Improve casting technique to eliminate porosity, especially at 
surface. 
2. 使用CAE 分析来评估该区域的受力量。Use CAE to evaluate the load at this area. 
3. 要求图纸增加孔隙率限制。Add porosity limit requirement to the drawing. 
4. 添加一个圆弧在第二裂纹的位置。Add a radius at the secondary crack location.

裂纹1 Crack 1 
 
疲劳裂纹开始于外表面缩孔区。然后通过超载扩展。此断口表面的主要特征是：宽广的显微缩孔横跨的整个截面
（图2＆3），其严重削弱截面强度。 
This crack was initiated as a fatigue crack from an area of shrinkage porosity at the outer surface.  The crack then 
propagated through overload.  The main feature of this fracture surface is the extensive micro-shrinkage porosity over 
the whole area (figures 2 & 3) which severely weakened the section.
