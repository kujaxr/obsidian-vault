Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1） 失效弹簧受到较大载荷发生疲劳断裂。The failed spring fatigue fractured due to large load.  
（2） 失效弹簧化学成分、脱碳、夹杂物和残余应力符合要求，硬度和组织正常。The composition、detect area 
decarburized layer、non-metallic inclusion and residual stress of the failed spring meet the requirement, the 
hardness and microstructure are normal. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1） 校核弹簧的实际工作载荷。Recheck the actual working load of the spring and compare the difference between 
different models of NSE.
