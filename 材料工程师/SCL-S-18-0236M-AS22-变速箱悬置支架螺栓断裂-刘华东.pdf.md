Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
1、 螺栓为过载断裂。The bolt is brittle fracture due to the high hardness; 
2、 螺栓化学成分，硬度，金相组织均符合要求。The chemical composition, hardness and microstructure of bolt meet 
the requirements.   
 
建议Suggestions：  
1、继续关注该零件装配并及时保留拧紧曲线。Continue to pay attention to the assembly of the bolt and keep the 
tightening curve in time.
