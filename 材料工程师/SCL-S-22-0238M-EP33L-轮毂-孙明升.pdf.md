Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：  
（1） 该轮毂发生疲劳开裂，无明显缺陷。 
Fatigue crack occurred on wheel hub, and no obvious defect was found. 
（2） 材料化学成分、硬度要求，金相组织正常。 
The chemical composition of the material and hardness met the requirements, the metallographic structure was 
normal. 
建议Suggestions：  
（1） 采用铸旋工艺，提高轮毂性能和可靠性。 
The casting and spinning process was adopted to improve the performance and reliability of the wheel hub.
