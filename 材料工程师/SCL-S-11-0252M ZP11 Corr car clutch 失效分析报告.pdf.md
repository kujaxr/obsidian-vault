Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
 
离合器因离合器分离轴的腐蚀和磨损而失效。The clutch has failed due to corrosion and wear of the clutch release shaft.   
 
失效机理是轴和袖子之间集聚磨料颗粒（被油脂保持），从而导致锌涂层遭受高磨损，然后轴开始腐蚀，
使情况变得更糟。The failure mechanism is build up of abrasive particles in the gap between the shaft and sleeves (kept there by 
grease), which caused high wear of the zinc coating.  Corrosion of the shaft then took place, making the situation worse. 
 
锌层厚度符合图纸的要求，然而，由于低耐磨性，锌层不是适合这种应用的涂层。The zinc thickness met the 
requirements of the drawing, however, due to low wear resistance, zinc is not an appropriate coating for this application. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
 
更改轴的表面保护层。对氮化层或硬铬层的耐腐蚀性和耐磨性进行评估。Change the shaft coating.  Nitrided layer 
or Hard chrome layer should be evaluated for corrosion and wear resistance. 
 
在其它离合器的投诉中，考虑腐蚀对转动轴转动载荷的影响。In other cases of clutch complaint, consider the 
influence of corrosion (measure load to turn shaft).

III. 调查结果 Investigation Results  
 
测试之后，按踏板无法操作离合器。离合器臂无法旋转（图1a）。拆开后离合器壳体，内部表现出严重的腐蚀，
尤其是离合器轴和轴承。由于隔套腐蚀很严重，轴无法旋转 (见图3)。 
After test, the clutch could not be operated by pressing the pedal.  The clutch arm could not be rotated (figure 1a).  After disassembly the inside 
of the clutch housing showed heavy corrosion, especially the clutch shaft and bearing assembly.  The shaft could not be rotated due to heavy 
corrosion at the bushes (see figure 3).
