Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
(1) 材料的成分不满足材料要求，硬度明显偏低，强度不足。这是导致配重块过早断裂的主要原因。The chemical 
components don’t meet the material requirement; the hardness is very low, so the strength is not enough, which is the main reason of the 
failure. 
建议Suggestions： 
(1) 追溯不合格品的批次号，对不合格品做出相应的处理。Find batch number of unqualified parts and make a relevant treatment. 
(2) 督促供应商对问题进行分析，整改过程控制中的漏洞；做好生产过程控制以及质量检测。Require the supplier  
to analyze the reason for the process fault; control the process and test the quality. 
(3) 产品的技术要求中，需要明确规定可接受和不可接受的铸造缺陷，如孔隙率，以便质量的控制，特别关注直
角处的铸造质量。如果采用现有模具和工艺无法保证关键部位的质量，需要从产品的结构方面考虑，以便获
得质量可靠的产品。The part requirement needs to define the acceptable and inacceptable defect level for quality control，such as 
porosity, especially regarding the quality of the right angle region. If the die and the process can’t assure the quality of the key region, need 
to resolve the problem from the part design to get the qualified parts.
