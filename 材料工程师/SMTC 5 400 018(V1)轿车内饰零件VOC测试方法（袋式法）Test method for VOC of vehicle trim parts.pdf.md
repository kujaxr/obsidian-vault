3.1  挥发性有机物（VOC） 
 
3.1  Volatile organic compounds(VOC) 
在常压条件下沸点或初馏点低于或等于
250℃，且在常温常压下能自发挥发的有机液体
或固体。 
 
Under the normal boiling point and initial dis-

and at normal temperature and normal pres-

liquids or solids.

4 
测试原理 
 
4  Principle 
将待测零部件放入密封的采样袋，充入适量
氮气后，将采样袋在65℃温度加热2小时，使零
部件或材料中的挥发性有机物散发到袋内气体
中。加热结束后，用Tenax管采集苯类物质，用
热脱附仪-气相色谱质谱联用仪（ATD-GCMS）进
行定性和定量分析；用DNPH管采集醛类物质，用
高效液相色谱仪（HPLC）进行定性和定量分析。 
 
 
Put the test parts in sealed sample bags, filled 
with the appropriate amount of nitrogen, the 
sample bag is heated to 65℃ for 2 hour，so 
that parts or materials of volatile organic 
compounds released into the bag gas.After the 
end of the heating, benzene material collected 
with Tenax tubes,using thermal desorption 
analyzer - gas chromatography mass spec-
trometry (ATD-GCMS) for qualitative and 
quantitative analysis ；using DNPH tube to 
collect the aldehydes, using high performance 
liquid chromatography (HPLC) to carry out 
qualitative and quantitative analysis.

6.3.1  捕集完的TENAX 管应立即进行定量分

6.3.1  Trapping finished TENAX tube should 
be immediately quantitatively analysed for the 
most accurate results . If the gas can not be 
analyzed immediately , the application of seal 
card to seal tube at both ends , and then alu-
minum foil wrap the trap tube , and the tube 
must be stored between 4-10℃ for a maxi-
mum of one week . Benzene, toluene, xylene, 
ethylbenzene, styrene analysis using the 
ATD-GC-MS，and analytical methods refer to 
HJ/T 400-2007. 
6.3.2  捕集完的DNPH 管应立即用乙腈洗脱，
若不能及时洗脱，应用铝箔纸包好在4-10℃环
境下保存（保存期限最多7 天）。乙腈洗脱出来
的溶液定容到10mL 溶液瓶里，应尽快分析，在
4-10℃环境下保存（保存期限最多7 天）。DNPH
管捕集的甲醛、乙醛、丙烯醛等醛酮类物质的分
析使用HPLC，分析方法参考HJ/T 400-2007。 
 
6.3.2  Trap after DNPH tube should be im-
mediately analysed with acetonitrile elution , If 
the gas can not be analyzed immediately , the 
application of seal card to seal tube at both 
ends , and then aluminum foil wrap the trap 
tube , for a maximum of one week . The ace-
tonitrile elution out of the solution given should 
be dissolved into 10ml solution bottle and 
analyzed as soon as possible , if this not 
possible it can be stored between 4-10℃ for a 
maximum of  one week . Formaldehyde, 
acetaldehyde and acrolein analyzed using 
HPLC , analysis method reference HJ/T 
400-2007.
