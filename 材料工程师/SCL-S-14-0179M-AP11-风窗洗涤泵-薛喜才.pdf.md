Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 由于液体冲刷、腐蚀造成风窗洗涤泵电机主轴直径逐渐变细，最后在扭转力的作用下发生断裂；The detergent 
erosion and corrosion resulted in the spindle thinning and finally fractured under the torsional force. 
2. 主轴材料成分符合要求，金相及硬度正常；Chemical components met the requirement. Microstructure and hardness 
were normal. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 请使用正确的洗涤剂；Use the correct detergent.
