Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1）由于螺栓拧入时发生偏置（头部受损严重），致使螺丝孔受损（见图8）。Because bias 
occurs at bolts stocking（head badly damaged）, resulting in damage to the screw 
hole（see Figure 8）. 
2）由于线束夹头与螺栓的配合间隙较大，导致线束夹头与螺栓丝套端面配合不均匀（有
偏移），在螺栓丝套端面形成一压痕。多因素叠加致使螺栓丝套自上而下发生胀裂。As 
the wiring harness clamp and bolt with a larger gap, lead harness clamp and bolt 
wire sets face with uneven（and a shift）, at the bolt wire sets face form a indentation. 
Superposition of multiple factors leading to the bolt wire sets from top to bottom 
place splitting. 
建议Suggestions：1）优化装配工艺，首先进行手工预紧拧入，然后再使用规定工具进行紧固。Optimize the 
assembly process, first manual tightening screw, and then use the provided tools for 
fastening. 
             2）优化线束夹头固定孔设计，避免在装配中出现偏置现象。Optimize the fixed hole 
design of the harness clamp, avoid bias phenomenon occurs in the assembly.

III. 测试结果 Test Results 
1、失效零件宏观观察Macroscopic Observation of the Failure Parts 
（1）机油泵一侧固定线束螺丝孔开裂，见图1、2。One side fixed-wire screw hole of the oil pump cracking, see 
fig1 and 2. 
（2）整个内螺纹受挤压严重变形并有明显纵向开裂现象（见图6），与该螺丝孔连接的螺栓表面及头部均有严重
损伤（见图5和6）。The severely deformed by compression within the thread and significant longitudinal 
cracking（see Figure 6）,the surface bolt and bolt head all have a serious injury which connected with the 
screw holes（see Figure 5 and 6）. 
（3）螺纹孔上端面有一明显压痕（见图4），螺栓、螺丝孔和线束夹头尺寸（见图3），尺寸设计不太合理，装
配中造成配合不到位。Threaded holes on the face with a clear indentation（see Figure 4）, bolt, screw holes 
and wiring harness Chuck size （see Figure 3）, size of the design is not reasonable, create with the assembly 
in place. 
结论 result：机油泵螺栓丝套螺牙受损严重，发生纵向开裂。The oil pump bolt wire sets thread badly damaged, 
Longitudinal cracking occurred.
