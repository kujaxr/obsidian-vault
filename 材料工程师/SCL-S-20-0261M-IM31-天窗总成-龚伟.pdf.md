Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 遮阳帘滑脚与铝边条过盈配合，装配后滑脚受挤压开裂。 
The slider is in interference fit with the aluminum edge strip, cracked after assembly.  
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions：  1. 供应商调整模具，增加塑料件失效位置间隙&改善零件翘曲情况。 
                    Supplier adjusts the mold to increase the gap of the failure position & improve part’s warpage. 
2. 供应商优化注塑工艺改善零件翘曲情况。 
                    Supplier optimizes injection molding process to improve part’s warpage.
