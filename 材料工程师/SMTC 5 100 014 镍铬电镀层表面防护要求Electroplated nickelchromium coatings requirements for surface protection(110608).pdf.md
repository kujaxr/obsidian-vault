铸件上装饰用镍/铬电镀层。 
1  Scope  
This standard defines requirements for de-
corative, electrolytically deposited Ni-Cr coatings 
on parts of steel, copper and aluminum materials.
This standard is suitable to raise require-
ments for decorative, electrolytically  deposited 
Ni-Cr coatings on parts of steel, copper and alu-
minum materials.

4.1 一般要求 
4.1
General requirements 
4.1.1  对于一次完整的试验大约需要10 个零件
（取决于尺寸）。 
 
4.1.1  Approx. 10 parts (depending on size) are 
required for a complete test. 
 
4.1.2  以下其它数据在首样试验报告中给出： 
a) 零件编号； 
b) 镀层公司、镀层系统； 
c) 电镀日期； 
d) 处理顺序（处理时间、电流密度、温度、化
学制品供应商）； 
e) 零件在挂具上的位置和排列形式。 
4.1.2  The following additional data shall be 
given in the first-sample test report: 
a)  part number; 
b)  coating company, coating system; 
c)  electroplating date; 
d)  process sequence (process times, cur-
rent densities, temperatures, chemical 
suppliers); 
e)  position / arrangement on the rack. 
4.2  表面加工 
4.2  Surface finish 
4.2.1  保护层不应包含任何气孔、粗糙裂纹、或其
他缺陷，以致会影响规定的表面要求和/或附着力，
或防腐性能。电镀部件不应有污点和变色。 
 
 
4.2.1
The protective coating shall not contain 
any pores, coarse cracks, damage or other flaws 
impairing specified appearance and/or adhesion 
or corrosion protection. The electroplated com-
ponents shall be free of spots and discolorations. 
4.2.2  零件表面（光泽度和表面结构）应符合首样。
4.2.2  The appearance of the parts (degree of 
glossiness and surface structure) shall corres-
pond to the initial sample. 
4.2.3  当零件处于合理恰当的安装及运输的条件
下，则其电镀层性能不应受到影响或降低它特定要
求的损害。使用和安装时的轻微变形不应导致电镀
层的附着力丧失。 
 
4.2.3  Given proper mounting and transportation 
conditions, the coating must not be damaged in a 
way that would lead to impairment of function 
and/or decrease in the specified requirements. 
Slight deformations due to use and mounting 
shall not lead to loss of adhesion of the electro-
lytically deposited coatings. 
4.2.4  电镀接触位置将经过协商达成一致。电镀层
的要求通常适用于部件的所有区域，除非图纸中强
调了具体的重要区域（例如客户可视的区域或不可
视的区域）。 
4.2.4  The electroplating contact positions shall 
be agreed upon. The requirements of the elec-
trolytically deposited coating generally apply to all 
areas of the component unless specific important 
areas (e.g., area visible for the customer or areas 
that are not visible) are emphasized in the draw-
ing.

4.4 附着强度 
 
4.4 Adhesive strength
 
4.4.1  依照 DIN EN ISO 2819，须使用以下任一方
法（至少要进行温度循环试验和一次下述的机械试
验）测试镀层结合强度。 
 
4.4.1  The adhesive strength of the coating shall 
be tested according to DIN EN ISO 2819 using 
one of the following methods (at least the tem-
perature cycle test and one of the mechanical 
tests listed below are to be carried out). 
4.4.2  锯切试验：使用金属切削带锯，自镀层的反
面锯部件（22 个锯齿/25mm；移动速度40 到45 
m/min）。依照 DIN EN ISO 2409 (3 mm)进行交叉切
割试验，但是要使用切割工具，例如BLB Omnilab
生产的Edding Cutter M9（另外按照交叉切割试验
进行操作（圣安德鲁十字））。要求：Gt 0。 
 
4.4.2 
Saw-cut test: The component is sawed 
from the reverse side toward the coating with a 
metal-cutting band saw (22 teeth/25 mm; traverse 
speed 40 to 45 m/min).Requirement: No stripping 
of the coating.Crosscut test according to DIN EN 
ISO 2409 (3 mm), but with a cutter, e.g., Edding 
Cutter M9 by BLB Omnilab (alternatively carried 
out as a crosscut test (St. Andrew’s cross)). 
Requirement: Gt 0. 
 
4.4.3  锉刀试验：以45°的角度，从镀层的反面
锉镀层部件。要求：镀层之间不可分离或镀层不可
从基材上剥落。 
 
4.4.3  File test: Filing of a coated component 
from the reverse side toward the coating at an 
angle of 45°.Requirement: No separation of the 
coating layers or stripping of the coating from the 
base material.

4.6 电化学性能 
4.6 Electrochemical behavior
4.6.1  测试：测试镍层之间的电压差，例如使用
Helmut Fischer GmbH
生产的库伦测厚仪
（Couloscope CMS Step）。要求：半光亮镍对于光
亮镍的电位差至少大于100 mV。 
 
4.6.1  Test: test of the voltage differences be-
tween the nickel layers, e.g., using a Couloscope 
CMS Step from Helmut Fischer GmbH. Re-
quirements: The semi-gloss nickel layer must be 
at least 100 mV nobler than the glossy nickel 
layer. 
4.6.2  对于三重全光亮镍体系，对于半光亮镍和光
亮镍之间的镍层（它包含了大量硫），光亮镍相对于
它的电位差至少大于15 mV。另外，直接位于铬层
下面的多孔镍，其相对于光亮镍的电位差至少大于
20 mV。 
 
4.6.2  For triple glossy nickel layers, the Ni layer 
between the semi-gloss and glossy nickel layers, 
which contains an especially high amount of sul-
fur, must be at least 15 mV less noble than the 
glossy nickel layer. An additional thin micropor-
ous Ni layer directly below the chromium coating 
must be at least 20 mV nobler than the glossy 
nickel layer.

a)  按 10A/dm
2，阴极除油持续1 到3 并在10%

a) 33.0 g/l CuSO4 + 5 H2O 
b) 16.0 g/l H2SO4 (96%) 
c) 2.3 g/l KCl 
d) 0.1 ml/l 润湿剂,例如Enthone GmbH 
-生产的润湿剂（Wetting Agent DL-S） 
c)  极化顺序： 
1)  在1000mV 时，阳极极化30s 
2) 不提供电压达30s 
3) 在400mV 时，阴极极化30s

使用Enthone 公司的霍尔曼测试，确定多
微孔铬镀层中的气孔数量。

1)  30 s anodically at 1,000 mV; 
2)  30 s no voltage supplied; 
3)  30 s cathodically at 400 mV.
