Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1、 断口属弯曲疲劳断裂，断裂源于两角位。The fracture belongs to bending fatigue fracture, origins are at opposite corners. 
2、 材料的硬度、金相组织符合该牌号材料的基本性能。The hardness& microstructure meet the requirements of the material 
specification. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1、 请进一步校核使用工况的受力情况。Check the forces applied during running.
