Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件装配时受玻璃冲击断裂。 
Failure parts were broken by glass impact during assembly.  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions：1.  合理装配，避免玻璃直接落下，对传动掣产生过大的冲击力。 
                    Reasonably assemble to prevent the glass from falling directly and causing excessive impact on 
the transmission switch. 
2.  请工程评估是否有必要提高零件强度。 
                    DRE evaluates if to increase the strength of parts.
