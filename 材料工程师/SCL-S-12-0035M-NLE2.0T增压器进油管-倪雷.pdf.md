Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 
1. 增压器进油管是由于疲劳发生的断裂。Turbocharger Inlet Pipe fractured due to fatigue. 
2. 因素：a）异常的疲劳载荷，振动等；b）严重的带状组织会造成各向异性，导致横向的抗拉强度抗疲劳性力学
性能均减弱，在横向受力的条件下，会导致产生早期疲劳失效，寿命减短。 
The contributing factors are:  
a）Abnormal fatigue loading, vibration etc  
b）Strongly banded structure will cause anisotropy, lead to the transverse tensile strength of anti-fatigue mechanical 
properties are weakened, under conditions of the transverse force, would result in early fatigue failure. 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 调查疲劳载荷产生的原因。Investigate the cause of the fatigue load. 
2. 改善原材料热处理状态，杜绝带状组织。Improve the heat treatment condition of the raw materials, eliminate the 
banded structure.
