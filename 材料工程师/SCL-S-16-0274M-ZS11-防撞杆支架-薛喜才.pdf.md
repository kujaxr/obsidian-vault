Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
1.  左、右焊点沿热影响发生双向疲劳开裂。Left/right welding reqion crack by fatigue along the heat affect area. 
 
2.  焊缝质量正常。Welding quality is normal. 
 
3.  车门内板材料符合要求。Body material meets the requirement. 
 
 
建议Suggestions： 
1. 结合工件实际结构如开裂部位存在焊点，使用CAE 分析应力分布。Evaluate the active stress distribution using 
CAE,based on the structure.
