Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受异常外力导致脆断。  
2. 零件材料符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1.The brittle fracture of the part caused by external force.   
2. Material performance of the failure parts conformed to requirements.     
建议： 1.优化设计，增加倒角，分散应力。  
Suggestions:  1.The design of part should be optimized, increasing chamfering and scattering stress.
