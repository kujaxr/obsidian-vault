Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions： 1. 失效件卡脚断裂系翘起之前的拆卸所致。 
The broken buckle occurred by the disassembly before the warping. 
2．零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of material conformed to requirement.   
建议Suggestions：1.  排查PDI检查前零件是否有拆卸情况，并确认拆卸过程异常。 
Check if the parts have been disassembled before PDI, and confirm the process.
