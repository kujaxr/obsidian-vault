Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions 
1.  车辆快速经过颠簸桥面，变速箱悬置支架受到冲击发生过载断裂。 
    Bracket of transmission suspension fractured though bumping bridge because of seriously shock load.   
2.  材料成分、硬度、孔隙及组织无异常，局部组织中存在冷隔(见图5d)。 
Chemical、hardness、porosity and microstructure are normal. Cold shot is observed near fracture surface 6.  
建议Suggestions： 
1.  继续关注售后车辆悬置支架的失效情况。 
    Concern the failed situation of bracket of transmission suspension. 
2.  优化工艺参数，提高铸件质量、避免冷隔。 
Optimize the process to improve the casting quality and avoid the cold shot.
