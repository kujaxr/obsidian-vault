Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions： 
（1）隔热板是由于振动产生较大的载荷而出现开裂的。The thermal baffles were cracked because of a big load caused 
by vibration. 
（2）材料的成分和板厚符合要求；硬度正常；金相检验未发现异常。The chemical composition and thickness meet 
the requirement; the hardness is normal; no abnormality was found during metallographic examination.  
 
建议Suggestions： 
（1）优化设计，降低应力。Optimize the design and reduce the stress.

未见过载的韧性断口特征。

纹，由此可见：该开裂为疲劳开裂。
