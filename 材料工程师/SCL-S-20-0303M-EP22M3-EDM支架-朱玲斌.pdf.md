Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
 
结论Conclusions：  
（1）EDM支架由于振动强度较大发生疲劳断裂。The EDM bracket fatigue fractured due to high vibration intensity. 
（2）支架材料成分和硬度合格，孔隙和金相组织正常。The composition and hardness of the bracket are qualified, the 
porosity and microstructure are normal. 
 
建议Suggestions：  
（1）对EDM 支架进行强化。Reinforcement of EDM bracket.
