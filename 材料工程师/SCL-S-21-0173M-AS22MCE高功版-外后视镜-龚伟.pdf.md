Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  酒精及风窗洗涤液不会导致零件发白（工况），丙酮等腐蚀性较强的溶剂/液会导致此种失效。 
Alcohol and windshield washer fluid won’t lead to white in working conditions, but acetone and  
other highly corrosive solvents / solutions will lead to such kind of failure.

    Material performance of part conformed to requirements.
