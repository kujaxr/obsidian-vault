1  范围Scope 
本标准规定了钢制件上电镀锌的表面保护
要求，但不针对与镁接触的零件。 
本标准适用于金属零件镀锌层的评价。不适
合使用环境温度持续大于100℃的镀锌层。 
 
This standard specifies the requirements for 
electrolytically deposited zinc coatings on steel 
parts, but not for contact with magnesium. The 
standard is adopted to evaluate the zinc coat-
ings on steel parts，but not for those working 
condition over 100 ℃.

3  基本要求General requirements 
3.1  避免有害物质按SMTC 5 500 001。对于出
口零件有害物质按SMTC 5 500 002满足REACH法
规标准。完成完整的试验需要10 个零件。零件
表面不允许有可见的外观缺陷。加工及装配过
程，不允许损害零件的防腐性能。 
 
3.1  Avoidance of hazardous substances ac-
cording to SMTC 5 500 001. For export parts, 
hazardous substances according to SMTC 5 
500 002.Ten parts are needed for a complete 
test. No visible defects are permitted on the 
surface of the parts. In the assembling pro-
cess, no damage which will lead to decreasing 
the anticorrosion performance is allowed.  
3.2 除非在图纸上指出零件的某些部分不需要
表面保护，否则图纸上标注的类型适用于整个零
件表面。 
 
3.2  If one certain surface protection type is 
defined on the drawing ,that means the whole 
part should treated by this type, unless some 
areas is defined as an exception.

3.3  对于紧固件，适用于端部、头部等非螺纹
部分。对于螺纹部分的考核适当降低要求。 
 
3.3  As to the fasten parts, only the head , the 
wrench bearing surface is evaluated according 
to one certain surface protection type which is 
defined on the drawing. The threaded areas 
usually relatively decrease the requirement. 
3.4  为避免氢脆，必须迅速在电镀后（最多不
超过电镀后的4小时），进行热处理。由于氢脆
的风险，高强度钢制件（Rm > 1000 N/mm
2）的
表面保护不允许使用电镀锌层。 
 
3.4  In order to avoid hydrogen-induced em-
brittling, heat treatment is need with four hours 
after the zinc-plated process(the sooner the 
better).in addition, electrolutically deposited 
zinc treatment is not allowed on the parts 
which Rm is more than  1000 N/mm
2.. 
3.5  镀锌后没有直接进行表面处理的半成品的
电镀镀锌层（例如从镀锌车间运输到油漆车间），
按照Sp-CM10,不允许进行钝化处理和磷化处理。 
 
 
3.5 Semi-finished parts that needed be coated 
after the zinc-plated (for example, transporting 
from zinc-plating plant to the painting plant), 
according to Sp-CM10, should have no post 
treatment, such as passivation or phosphate.   
4  镀层性能Layer performance 
4.1  镀层厚度Layer thickness 
镀层厚度要求： 
a) Sp-CMXX：对于普通零件8～35μm；对于
焊接件8～14 μm 
b) Sp-CHXX：对于普通零件15～35μm；对
于焊接件15～23 μm 
 
Requirements for layer thickness: 
a) Sp-CMXX: 8～35μm for general com-
ponents ; 8～14 μm for welded parts;  
b) Sp-CHXX: 15 ～35μm for general 
components ; 15 ～23 μm for welded 
parts; 
4.2  附着力Adhesion 
按照ISO 2819进行热冲击试验：样件在（220
±10）℃下存放30 min，紧接着浸入室温的水中。
要求：镀锌层没有大面积或者气泡状的脱落. 
 
 
Test according to ISO 2819 thermal shock 
test ：place the specimen is the oven 
（220±10）℃/30min, than quickly put it in the 
water under room temperature. Requirement: 
no large scale zinc layer peeling off from the 
base metal, and no blister on the surface also. 
4.3  耐腐蚀性Corrosion Resistance  
按照DIN EN ISO 9227-NSS进行试验，试验
时间和要求详见表格1。以下两种状态样品必须
均满足试验要求：a)正常供货态样品； b) 经过
120 ℃高温处理24h的样品。 
 
Testing according to ISO 9227-NSS, the test 
time and requirement refer to the table one. 
Two conditions specimens must be tested 
separately, and both should meet the re-
quirements in table 1: a) as-received speci-
mens, b) specimens that have been aged at 
120℃ for 24h.
