Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论： 1. 失效件受外力导致断裂。  
2. 失效件材料不符合技术要求。  
Conclusions:  1.The brittle fracture of the part caused by external force.   
2. Material performance of part conformed to requirements.     
建议： 1. 责令供应商解释材料问题并整改。 
        2．优化倒角结构。 
Suggestions:  1. Let supplier explain to material problem and rectify. 
            2. Optimize chamfer structure.
