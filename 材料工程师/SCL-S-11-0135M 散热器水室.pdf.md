Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 经测试，零件材料符合图纸要求。 
2. 水室台阶变形和O 型圈老化、回弹性能下降，导致水室与O 型存在间隙，进而水室泄露。 
Conclusions: After testing, the materials of the water chamber conform to the drawing requirement. According to the 
macroscopic observation and material test of the water chamber, it can be seen that a gap exists between the 
connector step and O ring due to the bending deformation of chamber step and the decreased recovery 
performance of the O ring during aging process. Then the chamber was leaking. 
建议：避免水室台阶发生变形；提高O 型圈的耐老化性能。 
Suggestions：The deformation of the connector should be prevented. The aging resistance of the O ring should be 
improved.

Ⅳ. 尺寸与宏观分析 Dimention and Macroscopic analysis 
 
图2 和图3 分别是水室接头和O 型圈的关键图纸尺寸，水室与弯管的接触间隙为1.9 mm，间隙处放置O 型
圈，O 型圈的直径为2.4±0.1 mm。 
图4 是水室失效件的接头，分别对1-4 点进行了测量，测量结果见表1。图5 是水室接头台阶尺寸，正常台
阶高度为1.9 mm。通过表1 的台阶测量尺寸发现，不同点的台阶尺寸明显不一致，测量点3 处达到2.06 mm，说
明水室接头已经发生一定的变形。 
表2 是失效O 型圈的厚度，平均厚度1.93 mm，该O 型圈永久变形已经达到92.6%，回弹性能基本消失。该
O 型圈最大厚度为2.0 mm，已经小于水室台阶测量3 处的高度（2.06 mm），说明无论O 型圈与水室台阶间隙如何
匹配，都会存在最小0.06 mm 的间隙。 
Fig 2 and Fig 3 are the dimensions of chamber connector and O ring, respectively. The gap between the chamber and 
the bend pipe is 1.9 mm which is assembly position of the O ring. The diameter of O ring is 2.4±0.1 mm. 
Fig 4 is the measure positions on the connector, the results as shown in Table 1. The normal step height is 1.9 mm 
which as shown in Fig 5. From the step heights in Table 1, it can be found that there is a clear difference between the 
measured results which indicates the connector occurred bending deformation. The maximum step height appeared at 
position 3 which is 2.06 mm.  
The average thickness of the O ring is 1.93 mm as shown in Table 2 which indicates the recovery performance of the 
O ring decreased seriously. The maximum thickness of the O ring is 2.0 mm which is smaller than the maximum step 
height of chamber position 3. So, no matter how we rotate the O ring in the assembly gap, a small gap always existed 
between the connector step and the O ring.
