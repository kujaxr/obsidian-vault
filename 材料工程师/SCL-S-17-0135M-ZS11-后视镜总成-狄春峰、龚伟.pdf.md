Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and Suggestions 
结论Conclusions：1.  零件在装卸工装或装配时开裂，导致失效。 
The part was failure because of improper operating in loading &unloading equipment or 
assembly process.  
2.  零件材料符合技术要求。 
The performance of the part material conformed to requirement. 
建议Suggestions： 1.  供应商优化喷漆工装结构，固定点避开三个失效卡脚。 
Supplier should improve the structure of painting tool, with the fixed points avoiding the failure 
position. 
                 2．改善装配工艺。 
                    Improve the assembly process. 
                 3．优化零件结构，失效卡脚根部增加倒角。 
Improve the structure of part, with R-angle in root of pins.

始位置呈韧断形式，后期发展为脆断；断口④⑤分别自两侧向中间汇合。
