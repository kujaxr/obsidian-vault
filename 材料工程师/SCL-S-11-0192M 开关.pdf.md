Ⅱ. 结论与建议 Conclusions and suggestions 
结论：1. 通过开关的材料分析和模拟分析可以判定，正常工艺制造的开关在正常使用状态下不会发生断裂；
该稀释剂对PC 材料开关质量影响较大，是导致开关开裂的可能原因。 
2. 经测试，零件材料是PC，符合图纸要求。 
Conclusions: According to the simulation analysis and material test of the switch, it can be seen that the break can’t 
appeared at normal using conditions and by normal manufacture process. But the paint thinner has significant 
effect on the quality of PC switch, so the possible reason for the switch break is the influence of paint thinner. 
The material of failure part conforms to the draw requirement. 
建议：供应商选有合适的开关油漆，推荐底漆使用佩特VPCH04723，面漆使用佩特VPCH01251-1。 
Suggestions：The recommended base coat of PETER VPCH04723 and top coat of PETER VPCH01251-1 should be 
chose for the PC switch.
