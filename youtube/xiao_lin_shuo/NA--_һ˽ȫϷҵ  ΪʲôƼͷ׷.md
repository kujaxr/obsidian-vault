On the surface, it is a
form of entertainment that satisfies people's sensory stimulation.
But in fact,
it is a product of the integration of technology
and it also highly overlaps with the direction of future hard technology,
so when you develop games
it actually drives the iteration of the surrounding technology
and these iterations will in turn drive games.
It has great social energy
and is a spiraling super digital scene.
So you see that games are for playing
but the game industry may pave the way for the future.
